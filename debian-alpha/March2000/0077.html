On Wednesday, 15 Mar, Waite, Michael wrote:
> Where are the latest floppies that will work for EV6?

The boot floppies have appeared on ftp.debian.org and mirrors in
debian/dists/potato/main/disks-alpha/current. The disk images you are
looking for are images-1.44/rescue.bin and images-1.44/root.bin. There is
also an unreleased and untested TFTP/BOOTP image (which probably contains
a slightly older version of the installation system) at
ftp://genie.ucd.ie/pub/alpha/test/tftpboot.img .
