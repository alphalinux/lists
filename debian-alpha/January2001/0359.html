To all folks who have been helping me with problem before,
but with the v2.4.1 kernel, I just tried the v 2.4.2 kernel and it got further.

The system actually booted this time. The following is evidence of the
fact that it booted:

Linux fea6 2.4.2 #1 Thu Feb 22 15:02:26 EST 2001 alpha unknown

However, it did not stay up long. It locked up solid. I suspect that
the auto-mount of a drive from a remote machine might have
done it, but I am not sure.

Also, it has been suggested to me to try the "generic" insteady of
"pc164" Alpha type. The problem there is that the
"CONFIG_ALPHA_SRM=y" cannot be set for generic kernels
(at least from make menuconfig).

Any ideas on what could be wrong or what to try are appreciated.
