An update to the xdm_4.0.2-1 peculiarity I reported previously: a file
with the unprintable name <50 78 03 20 01 0A> (hexadecimal) has
magically appeared in both the / and /root directories:

# find / -xdev -name 'Px^C ^A' -exec ls -l {} \;
-rw------- 1 root root 89 Feb 20 06:37 /root/Px? ?
-rw------- 1 root root 89 Feb 24 00:15 /Px? ?

The files are similar, but not identical:

# od -A n -t c 'Px^C ^A'
 377 377 \0 \0 \0 \0 \0 023 X D M - A U T H
   O R I Z A T I O N - 1 \0 020 ` 023 0
   e 317 260 ; 211 \0 355 l 016 351 e ~ L 377 377 \0
  \0 \0 \0 \0 022 M I T - M A G I C - C
   O O K I E - 1 \0 020 \0 E { 257 - 203 205
 376 317 t 9 a 225 v 370 X
