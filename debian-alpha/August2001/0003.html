Is someone willing to do an NMU of php3-imap in unstable? It depends
on libc6 rather than libc6.1 on Alpha (bug #105811) and its
non-installability is preventing installing new versions of imp, and
in particular will (when Debian package is released) probably prevent
installation of imp 2.2.6, which is a remote-exploit security update
(bug #106179).
