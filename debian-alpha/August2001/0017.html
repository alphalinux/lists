>
> I was told that Nautilus and Jensen are the systems where MILO does
> not work, the others are supposed to prepare a MILO-disk. The
> MILO-images will be stored right next to the boot-disk-images on the
> FTP-server/on the CDs.

There is no MILO for the Nautilus, it originally shipped with AlphaBIOS
and APB (Alpha Processor Bootloader). Since then SRM has been released for
it, so it can't be booted just like most the other Alphas out there.

AlphaBios and APB development are no more, so what's out there is it.
