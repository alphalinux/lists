Friends of Alpha:

I just did a quick poll of some of my favorite bookmarks for the Alpha.
I was displeased to find:

1) www.alphalinux.org has been down for several days
2) www.alphanews.net has not seen a post since 7/31
3) the UNH peer-to-peer support pages for Compaq/Linux products appears
to be no longer in existance

These are not good signs...
