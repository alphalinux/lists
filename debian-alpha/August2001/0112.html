Anthony Towns just posted a method on debian-boot:

/usr/sbin/debootstrap --download-only --arch alpha woody woody-alpha http://ftp.debian.org/debian/
(cd woody-alpha && tar cf ../basedebs_3.0_alpha.tar *)
rm -rf woody-alpha
