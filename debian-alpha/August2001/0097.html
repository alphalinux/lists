> Aug 10 11:49:02 hot init: PANIC: segmentation violation! sleeping for 30 seconds.
> Aug 10 11:49:32 hot init: PANIC: segmentation violation! sleeping for 30 seconds.
> Aug 10 11:50:32 hot last message repeated 2 times
> Aug 10 11:51:32 hot last message repeated 2 times
> Aug 10 11:52:32 hot last message repeated 2 times
> Aug 10 11:53:32 hot last message repeated 2 times
> Aug 10 11:54:32 hot last message repeated 2 times
>
> I don't understand where the problem might come from...
>

This is *not* healthy.
What might have happened is that a memory page got corrupted somehow,
it's happened to me once. A reboot fixed it.

If, however, the binary is corrupted, then the system won't really come
up in any useable state... I recommend reinstalling the sysvinit .deb
via "dpkg -i sysvinit_2.81-1_alpha.deb" before rebooting.
