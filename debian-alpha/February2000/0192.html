The problem is: the autobuilder has already uploaded e.g. foo 1.2-3 to
woody, and you're trying to upload foo 1.2-3 to potato AND woody. The
installer finds that >= 1.2-3 is already in woody, and rejects the
entire foo upload. Yes, it's not expected behaviour, but there _is_
some logic in it :-)

So by editing the *.changes file to remove "unstable" from the
"Distribution:" line (and resigning it) you can get the upload to work.
