No. Just replace the milo and try again. You can try potato's rescue
disk, where the milo is newer, or download milo by ftp from genie.ucd.ie.
There are also 2.2-based milos at http://www.stefan-reinauer.de/Milo/, but
I'm not sure they are ready for non-ev56 platforms yet.

The whole story is quite strange though, since Avanti has been the
primary milo testing platform for years.
