On Thu 27 Jan 2000, Ron Farrer wrote:

> I tried to install the resently released netscape rpm and here is what
> happens:
>
> # rpm -i netscape-4.7-1.alpha.rpm
> error: failed dependencies:
> /bin/sh is needed by netscape-4.7-1
>
> # ls -ls /bin/sh*
> 0 lrwxrwxrwx 1 root root 4 Jan 15 11:56 /bin/sh ->
> bash

rpm doesn't know about deb, hence doesn't know that /bin/sh exists.
Why that silly dependency is in there is a mystery to me (you can be
fairly sure that your system won't be doing much without /bin/sh
installed anyway).

I used some force flag on rpm, i.e. rpm -i -<something> ...
I forget what it was but it's in the manpage.

> P.S. Has anyone installed it yet? How well does it work?
