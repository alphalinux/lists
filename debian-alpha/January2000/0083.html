>I´ve got an LX with Alpha BIOS. Can I use the MILO from RedHat as well
>for Debian or should I prepare a small DOS partition on the new
>harddisk as well?

Yes, you can share MILO. In fact, with a recent-enough MILO, you can
have an /etc/milo.conf with the two different systems defined in it
and pick which one you want at boot.
