dont stress about these errors.. alignment problems are corrected by the kernel..
its just warning you... The best way I've found to reduce the annoyance is to
type
'dmesg -n 1' which will still log the messages to /var/log/messages but wont spam
the console.. else you can make a small change to the kernel and eliminate the
warnings all together.. I find the above easier since I don't have to deal with
unclean patch/upgrades of the kernel.
