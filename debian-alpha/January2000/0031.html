Matthew R. Pavlovich (mpav@purdue.edu) wrote:

> I have a alpha PC164 w/ a 21164PC 500Mhz processor. I am confused as to
> which sub-arch I should use.
>
> Is the kernel-image-pci164 the correct sub-arch? I have read and heard
> from others to use the sx164 and the pc164..

The PC164 and the 164SX are different systems/boards. The PC164 uses
normal 21164 processors up to 500MHz. The 164SX uses only 533MHz 21164PC
processors.

If you have a 500MHz processor then you have a PC164.
