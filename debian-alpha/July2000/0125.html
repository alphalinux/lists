I had the same problem ... and Jay Estabrook helped to me, and possibly you.

ftp://gatekeeper.dec.com/pub/DEC/Linux-Alpha/TESTING/up-2.2.17-pre12-15jul/

you want to compile your own kernel ...

I think, this will help :-)
