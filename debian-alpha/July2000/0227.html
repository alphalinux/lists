> Could you elaborate on this? X on Alpha certainly has ZPixmaps, and I
> get the same extensions listed as on an i386. What exactly is missing?

PEX5 support. The extension's #defines are all in the headers, but the
extension shared lib never compiles (it's purposely omitted from alpha
since it's not 64-bit clean...even in 4.0.x). Technically, we have
ZPixmaps, but the SHM support is limited.
