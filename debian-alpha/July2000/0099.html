Hi folks,

I'm running potato and 1/2 on my (t)rusty SX here, but I have
always been having troubles with gnomeicu (0.90b, 0.92, and
0.93). When I login in invisible, occupied or dnd mode,
everything works fine. However as soon as I switch to some other
status (online, occupied, busy, etc...) gnomeicu bombs out with
the following error:

Gdk-ERROR **: BadDrawable (invalid Pixmap or Window parameter)
  serial 9 error_code 9 request_code 134 minor_code 1

Now, I haven't had this behaviour when I was running redhat a
couple of months ago and I haven't seen this on x86 boxen so I
guess this is debian specific. I've used the gnome-libraries from
potato, woody and now helix, but they don't make any difference.
I've also tried to debug this beast, but I haven't traced down
the problem yet as I am unable to find out where exactly t
his happens. (Jeremy I'm CC'ing this to you, because you may have
an idea how to handle this)
Has anybody out there seen the same problems?
