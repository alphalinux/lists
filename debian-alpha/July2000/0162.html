Actually I tried powercycling just the device but it didn't help. That's
why I finally rebooted into ARC (would be SRM if it'd be friendly with
my Matrox card...) to see if ARC could see the device or not (which it
did not). From there I powercycled the system checked to see if it could
see it and it did NOT. So I powered the system, TZ87, and scanner off.
Powered the system, the scanner then the TZ87 back on and ARC saw them.
So I booted Debian and was happy to see Linux finding it at boot.
