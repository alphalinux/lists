On Thu, Sep 09, 1999 at 08:24:21AM -0700, Ron Farrer wrote:

> I had a look at non-us.debain.org and found no alpha-binary directory!?!
> WTF? Is there not one? Or am I missing the big picture?

There isn't one, due to a muxip when slink was frozen. You'll need to
build the source packages yourself (get all the files from the source
section of the archive, unpack them with "dpkg-source -x <dsc-file>"
then cd into the "package-version" directory created and say
"dpkg-buildpackage -b -us -uc".
