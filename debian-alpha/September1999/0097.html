On Mon, 20 Sep 1999, Peter S Galbraith wrote:

> Looks like perhaps I should turn off Symbios NVRAM detection and

It's possible. That is an extremely odd problem and one I've never seen
(have a Tekram 390F with two Barracudas and a CD-ROM). Turning off the
NVRAM detection *might* work, but YMMV. Have you booted the card on a
i386 and do anything strange during the setup? With mine, the defaults
were fine, but again YMMV.

> README.ncr53c8xx says to pass the argument `ncr53c8xx=nvram:n' at

Keep me (and the list) posted on this. It's the first time I've ever seen
this kind of problem with an 875-based board.
