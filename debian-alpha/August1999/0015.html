        Hi George,
AFAIK , those boards do use a standard AT power supply. Use the second
connector from the edge.
the other one is for supplying 3.3 V for PCI cards that need this.(maybe
your video card does ?)
 I did not have to put anything there, just used a normat AT PSU and that
worked for me.

If you or anyone is interested: I have a pdf version of the AXPpci33
Designer Guide. This guide
is intended for those who like to design a system using this MB and has
all technical info about these boards,
including all jumper setting , all connector pinnouts , power
requirements, and things like sizes , heat dissipation and so on.
I can send it to you , but it is a 800 kb file. Just let me
know.......

HTH,
Jaap
