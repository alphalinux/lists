I and two friends have had extremely unpleasant time with the Avanti/SRM
boots - namely, we reached EXACTLY the stage you describe here and got
stuck on SCSI timeouts.

We, in the end, got around the problem by using (either of) the
following solutions:

        1. ARC boot.
        2. Network boot of MILO

The AS200 (at least those _we_ have) have both SRM and ARC.

The network boot is accomplished as follows -

        0. issue "show dev" and note the MAC address.
        1. issue "boot -protocol bootp ewa0" at the SRM prompt.
        2. Have a bootd on another machine serve the Avanti MILO image.
        3. Continue as normal.
