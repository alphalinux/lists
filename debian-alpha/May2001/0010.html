Ron Farrer (rbf@debian.org) wrote:

> I'm looking for a few people to help with creating boot-floppies for
> Alpha. Right now a lot of work needs to be done in a very short amount
> of time. We need to get the woody boot-floppies created and tested or
> we'll probably end up not having a woody release for Alpha. So I'm
> looking for a few people who are willing to work as a small team on
> it. Do I have any volunteers?

I've gotten many responses (thanks!) Here is what we need to do first:

1) Those of you who are currently Debian Developers and are willing to
help with coding on boot-floppies, please contact me and let me know
that you ARE a DD.

2) Those of you who want to become Debian Developers and also want to
help with coding on the boot-floppies, please contact me and let me
know that you WANT to become a DD.

3) Those of you who are not Debian Developers and don't want to become
one, you can still help with testing! Those of you who have already
emailed me, I still have your name/address so you don't need to do
anything but watch here for an announcement when were are ready to
start testing.

Big thanks to everyone who has offered to help!

Ron
