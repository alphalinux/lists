Hi,

As you may or may not know, I've been building kernel-image packages and
boot-floppies for potato. I tried to upload 2.2.17 but it was
unceremoniously rejected. So we will not have 2.2.17 proper for 2.2r1.

Unfortunately I no longer have direct access to any Alphas, including the
potato machine which I built boot-floppies on. So if future updates are

to an Alpha and a bunch of extra stuff - if you volunteer I can certainly
answer any questions)

While I can still build kernel-image packages on lully, I'd prefer for
someone with their own Alpha (i.e. someone who can test them) to take
over maintenance of these packages.

Any volunteers?
