On Fri 19 Nov 1999, simonst@WellsFargo.COM wrote:
>
> Is tulip.o the correct module for the Dec AlphaServer 1000/4?

I'm guessing it's probably one of the DEC cards. In that case,
you may try de4x5 as the module. Tulip should also work, but I
always have better luck with the de4x5 module.

You can check /proc/pci to see what devices are listed, that should
help you find out what NIC you have.
