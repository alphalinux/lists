Could we please at least put something on the website documenting floppy
based installs? At present, the installation instructions are
completely incorrect and are at best unhelpful when one is trying to
install. Something like "Install MILO using the instructions in the MILO
HOWTO, then burn rescue and root floppies and in MILO say whatever" (that's
the procedure I use) would go a long way. I guess something similar is
needed for other media.

have to install systems and have never touched an Alpha before.
