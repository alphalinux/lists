> Ummm, yeah :P replace the battery.. tis quite easy to do, take a look

> > Anyone have any suggestions about how I can get it working again?

        I was able to get such a replacement battery from Radio Shack, but
they had to special order it (~1 week time to deliver), and ending up
costing about $20US. Just take in your current battery and they should be
able to fix you up!

        PS. Make sure to write/mark/remember which direction the battery
is plugged into the motherboard. There is no labeling on the board where
the Black or Red wires go!!!
