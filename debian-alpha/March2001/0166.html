>
> I am running snort 1.5.1 on my Ruffian 600mhz running kernel 2.2.18pre21
> I downloaded the deb last night, but I am getting the same error
> repeatadly on tty1 and and in /var/log/messages:
>
> snort(8725): unaligned trap at 0000000120009bbc: 00000001201c1e1c 29 2

Did you get these with the previous version of snort? 1.7-4.alpha.1.
I had patched that version to work around these unaligned accesses,
and I got a report that my patches were put into the new release;
perhaps this is a new one... I'll look into it.

> Is this a known bug? Is it fixable?

No, and probably.
It only costs a bit of performance and the logging may be a nuisance,
but that could be fixed through judicial editing of /etc/syslog.conf
