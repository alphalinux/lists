There was a problem a while ago, with PYXIS support (MIATA/LX/SX)
being limited to 1GB of RAM. Not a problem on LX/SX, since they have
only 4 DIMM slots, but MIATA has 6... :-\

This was fixed... :-)

better, 2.2.19.
