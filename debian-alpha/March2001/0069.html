Hello Sun Un!

What is the processor type of your machine? Older EV4 based machines like
the jensen need patches for traps.c as the traping in 2.2 seríes kernels
is broken for those machines. The effect is that once the machine traps
it never comes back but the offending application will eat all processor
time there is without responding any further.
