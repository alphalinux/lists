> Depends: gnome-bin (>= 1.0.50-0.2) but it is not installed

You are missing the "gnome-bin" and "gnome-libs-data"
packages. Normaly doing "apt-get -f install" fixes that, but you said
it didn´t in your last mail.

Try "apt-get -f install gnome-bin gnome-libs-data" or download them
manually and do "dpkg -i gnome-bin*.deb gnome-libs-data*.deb".

Also, please allways include the errors given when something goes
wrong (except when it is more than a couple of lines)
