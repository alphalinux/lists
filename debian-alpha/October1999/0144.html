a.bottemanne@nl.ibm.com writes:

> Hi,
>
> When configurating X (svga) for the Millennium II (4 MB)
> graphics card I fill out all the data needed for configuration
> also the monitor data etc. When I start X, I get the following
> error message:
>
> fatal sevrer error:
> No valid modes found.
>
> I configured 640x480, 800x600, 1024x768 and 1280x1024. Also I tried
> only 640x480 (just to be safe...) but still the same eror. What can I do
> to get X working ?

You need modes that work for your card. Without any infrmation what
you did and what errors come up I cannot say whats wrong so you have
to elaborate a bit.

You should use XF86Setup (its its own package) to configure X and you
should look at the ouput given by startx when it doesn´t work.

If you see something about modes getting deleted you have the cause
why you have no modes. Probably you set the timings to high or to low.
