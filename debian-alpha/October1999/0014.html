Ian Johannesen <ian@advice.dk> writes:

> Well can't I just deactivate the use of the root disk under installation?

You can, but you can´t.

You can change the medium used. If you have another system, you can
partition the harddrive for the alpha and install root.bin onto a
small partition (e.g. what shall be swap later) and use that as root
device with "root=/dev/sdaX".
