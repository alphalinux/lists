Sure; works fine. I have my AS200 and my dual P166 hooked up to the same
MAG MXP17F monitor. The P166 has a Millenium I with the VGA15-BNC5 cable
(with the 5 color-coded cables hooked into the monitor) and the AS200 is
connected with a standard VGA cable. A switch on the monitor allows me to
toggle between the two.
    The only hard part was finding the BNC5 cable, but there are good places
on the net where such things can be ordered.
