> Apr 5 18:38:24 slinky (Server)[19029]: long: Get_spool_control: cannot

1) Are you running rpc.(k)statd? (It's needed for locking, available in the knfs package)
2) Does the server support locking? (i.e. is it running something other
that the normal Linux userlevel nfsd, as it doesn't support locking)
