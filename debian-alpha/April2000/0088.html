Does linload.exe work on the ruffian (UX) board? I tried
it yesterday, and it really wasn't happy at all. It wouldn't
do anything, and when I pressed a key, I got a prompt to
go back to ARCSBIOS. In the end, I grabbed ldmilo.exe from

from ruffian to milo).

www.debian.org/~dhd/, as it wouldn't work for me... I suspect
the download may have been dodgy.. but when I recreated
the root disk on a loop back floopy image, and wrote that,
it booted okay...
