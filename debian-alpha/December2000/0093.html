installed apache-common and apache-ssl. The ssl module does not work.
Apache-ssl complains thus when started:
  Starting web server: apache-ssl.
  Syntax error on line 50 of /etc/apache-ssl/httpd.conf:
  Cannot load /usr/lib/apache/1.3/libssl.so into server: undefined symbol:
  RAND_egd_bytes
  /usr/sbin/apache-sslctl start: httpsd could not be started

Here are the versions I have:
ii apache-common 1.3.14-2
ii apache-ssl 1.3.14.2+1.42-1
ii libapache-mod-perl 1.24.01-2

I noticed that /usr/lib/apache/1.3/libssl.so is stripped; I hope this is not
the dreaded module-stripping problem that has caused so much trouble for
XFree86 4.0.1!
