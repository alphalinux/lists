> 2. I'm assuming that the VT must be set to 8/1/none
> to work correctly.
>
yes. this is good. it is usually the default too :)

> 3. What /etc/inittab entry must I use to get a login
> prompt?
>
standard entry. it's commented out in /etc/inittab :)
just after
# Example how to put a getty on a serial line (for a terminal)
T0:23:respawn:/sbin/getty -L ttyS0 9600 vt100

remove the # from the front of the line, and then kill -HUP 1 will restart
init. hist enter a couple of times on your vt teminal and a login should
appear.
