Unfortunately, the make clean also removes the vmlinux.lds. In consequence,
your correct order should be
 make dep
 make-kpkg clean
 make archdep
 make-kpkg --revision=custom.X.X kernel_image
 dpkg -i ../kernel-image***.deb
