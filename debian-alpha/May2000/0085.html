> I believe that I've managed to get the sound set up on my Multia
> (/dev/sndstat looks good, and throwing files at /dev/dsp makes
> noise), but CD players don't make any noise. ISTR that a CD-ROM
> drive needs a dedicated line to the sound card, but the PDF manual
> is light on details about the MSS (unless I missed something), so
> I was wondering whether (a) there is a connector for such a line,

> and then do something useful with it.

I guess you could conceivably use cdparanoia to rip the data and play
it in real-time, but I think the discman would be the better move.
