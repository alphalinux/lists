This is sparc port bug of w3m-ssl. Could someone fix this?
i386 and arm port is ok, but alpha has same bug of this,
becase alpha libc6.1 is 2.1.3-18.

Are other binaries in 2.2r3 ok?

pandora% pwd
/org/non-us.debian.org/ftp/dists/stable/non-US/main
pandora% dpkg -f binary-alpha/w3m-ssl_0.1.10+0.1.11pre+kokb23-1.deb depends
libc6.1 (>= 2.1.97), libgc5, libgpmg1 (>= 1.14-16), libncurses5, libssl09, w3m

attached mail follows:

The new stable version of w3m-ssl depends on an unstable version of libc6 (2.2)
and on an unavailable library (libssl096).
As such, it's not installable on a fresh system and cannot be upgraded on an
already installed system.
