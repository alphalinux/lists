Actually, the redhat 7.0 alpha disk should start up without any -file or -fl
switches.. Just put the Redhat disk in and type boot dka400..
Assuming your cdrom is dka400. Like Greg said, "do a show config" to see
what the cdrom is.

Also download Jump start from Compaq. This will help load up your secondary
loader into your system.

http://opensource.compaq.com/sourceforge/project/?group_id=3

Follow instructions.

If you would like to get from SRM to ARC, on my systems I just type ARC at
the SRM prompt..
>>>ARC

Good Luck
