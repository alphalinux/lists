Chris,

>Does anyone know how I can get SRM on my Alpha server to see my Mylex card
>which is on bios version 3.51?

What model system are you using? I know 5.8 firmware on most machines can
see Mylex (non OEM) controllers as valid devices, but older (5.6ish)
firmware on many machines only see "StorageWorks" or "Digital" branded OEM
Mylex controllers with the OEM variant (level 2.x) of Mylex firmware. I'm
not sure there is a solution for you other than to look for Digital OEMed
firmeware and remove the EEPROM plug-in chips you already have and replace
them to take on the Digital "personality."

Good luck,
