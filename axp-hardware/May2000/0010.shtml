> Does anyone remember what the memory bus width (bits) was on the

> size/speed memory. Anyone remember for sure?

Hello Ron:

        Looking at the OEM Design Guide on pg 2-17 and 2-18 each simm
socket has 36 data lines, 4 for parity, and 32 for data. Since we bank
them in groups of two, it looks like the data path is 64 bits. I did not
see any mention of a 'wide' mode of 128 bits. I think the wide data paths
are in mobo's that lie beyond our humble AXPpci33's. I hope I'm wrong. Any
corrections out there?

Andrew
