Looks like a nice box, Exactly what kind of SCSI card is it?
Do you happen to know which chipset that graphics card of yours
has? If it's one of the more exotic ones it's probably not
supported by X windows.
 
> System ALPHA PL 164LX, ALPHA BIOS VERSION 5.62-1A, DIGITAL ALPHA 21164
> PROCESSOR

You may want to consider switching your console to SRM.

 ftp://ftp.digital.com/pub/DEC/Alpha/firmware/v5.9/alphapc164lx/

Though be sure that you have a supported SCSI card, otherwise
you wont be able to boot off it.
