I finally figured out why I can't boot Milo from the hard drive. The
problem is this:
I am using an AlphaStation 400 4/233 with the latest ARC firmware. However,
the firmware in unable to properly initialize the scsi bus. Even though, I
get the OK message at bootup, I know something is wrong since when listing
the devices on the bus, the list is wrong. For example, I have 1 hard
drive, 1 SyQuest drive and 1 cdrom drive on the bus, but under the
supplemental menu, the hard drive is listed twice or sometimes three times
(The first time it is correct with the right number of partitions). The
result of this is that I can't boot from the hard drive or the CDROM drive.
At the moment, I am running RedHat Linux on the machine and the Milo
firmware is able to properly initialize and detect all devices, but I can
only load Milo from the floppy. Anyone have an answer?
