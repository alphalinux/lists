On Mon, 16 Apr 2001, Steve Ruzila wrote:

> I have a 164LX alpha and it has a full size Digital (DEC) video card in it.
> X doesn't recognize the card. The guy I bought the box from told me that it
> a 16MB Oxygen video card. I replaced it with a generic Trident PCI 8MB video
> card. I do not get any video feed when I use the Trident card. The monitor
> stays in sleep mode. I have video with the Oxygen card. The motherboard has
> 2 32bit and 2 64bit PCI slots along with 2 ISA slots. The card was in the
> first 64 bit slot but has a 32bit connector (if that's the right word). I'm
> attempting to get Suse Linux 7 to work. Does anyone know a good PCI card to
> use or why mine won't work. I'm using SRM. Also when I boot I only get the
> command line. X doesn't seem to want to come up but I have a feeling it's
> because of the card and not anything else. I just used the suse auto install
> not the expert mode.

I'd guess the Oxygen card isn't supported by XFree86. As for the trident
card not coming up, which version of SRM do you have? I'd say put on V5.8
which is the latest, you can get it from Compaq's website. As for PCI
slots it really shouldn't matter in an LX, there's only 1 PCI bus there,
though trying different slots won't hurt.
