Oh, you've got tons of luck;)

> but it turned out that these chips were to narrow to fit the right sockets.

My tip here is: Buy IC sockets (fitting in those wider sockets on
the PCB) and lead your too narrow ICs on these sockets. That doesn't
cost a lot of money, but has some advantages (electronical connection
to the PCB is better, ...).
