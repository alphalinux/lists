>
> well, my question:
>
> I have a Multia (UDB) Noname 166, running Suse 6.1/Kernel 2.2.9
> and I'm trying to get a AVM Fritz PCI ISDN card running.
>
> Whem I'm tryiun to load hisax though
> (with 'modprobe hisax type=5 protocol=2 irq=15 io=[9000|64000]')

use modprobe hisax type=27

do _not_ specify irq or io-port for PCI cards.

type 5 is an ISA card
type 27 is the PNP and PCI variants

> the kernellog says that that at the given IO-port the HSCX-Version could
> not be found.
>
> here my questions:
>
> + does anybody have this working ?

yes, Fritz!PCI on a AXPpci33 'noname'

> + does anybody know about valid IO-Ports in this combination ?

don't specify any - the PCI subsystem will find out suitable values (actually,
it will decide on the address and _tell_ the card the address it has to use)
