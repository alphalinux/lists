On Fri, Jun 23, 2000 at 02:22:24PM +0200, Jan-Benedict Glaw wrote:
> 2.4.0test2-pre9 (and -pre11 as well) fail to mount the root device for
> me on AXPpci33. 2.4.0-test1-ac19 was fine, so it broke somewhere in
> between. Is this a known problem of shall I track this down?

It's due to Wildfire change first appeared in ac21 (now in test2).
Kernel start address was moved from 3Mb to 8Mb to leave the space
for a huge Wildfire console.
So we must upgrade our bootloaders (to aboot-0.7) and say goodbye
to the 5Mb of RAM :-/

Ivan.
