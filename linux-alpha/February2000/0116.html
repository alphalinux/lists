Hi!

When building RTC as a module, it is unuseable because the module cannot
get IRQ8. This is caused by:

[./include/asm-alpha/hw_irq.h]
     30 #define RTC_IRQ 8
     31 #ifdef CONFIG_RTC
     32 #define TIMER_IRQ 0 /* timer is the pit */
     33 #else
     34 #define TIMER_IRQ RTC_IRQ /* timer is the rtc */
     35 #endif

I notices this because "hwclock --hctosys" produces strangest times and
dates if there is no RTC driver present on my old AXPpci33 board. Deleting
the above section (well, I let "#define RTC_IRQ 8" and "#define TIMER_IRQ 0"
remain, of course...) ans loading the rtc.o module before executing hwclock
works perfectly for me.

Why was this strange IRQ scrambling added? Do I miss something?

MfG, JBG
