On my box the system stops completely. CTRL-ALT-BACKSPACE, SYSRQ-[RSUB]
and CTRL-ALT-DEL do not work at this state. I also recognizes that the
OS is no loner running, cause no filesystem-syncs are done anymore.

But there is sometimes an exception in the behaviour where CTRL-ALT-DEL
succeeds to reboot the machine, but then milo hangs on startup. Then i
still have to reset the box.
