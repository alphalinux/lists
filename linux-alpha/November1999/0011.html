On Mon, 8 Nov 1999, Daniel Tryba wrote:

> > With few tweaks, I was able to compile SVGATextMode on pc164. Works well
> > with the Diamond Stealth 64 (s3 trio64) I have in alpha.
>
> Could you make your version available to the rest of the world? A long
> time ago I tried to compile it myself and didn't succeed. I (and

 The package available at:

ftp://ftp.ds2.pg.gda.pl/pub/mirror/ftp.amg.gda.pl/macro/SRPMS/SVGATextMode-1.8-2.src.rpm

should compile and work out of the box. A binary Alpha package used to be
available at ftp.amg.gda.pl but that site is not operative anymore.

 Note that the package is almost two years old and was not tested on a
glibc 2.1 system. I believe it should compile against glibc 2.1 but it
was only compiled against glibc 2.0.
