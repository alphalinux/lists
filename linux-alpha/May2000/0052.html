AlphaNews.net up and running.

Thursday, May 25nd 2000.
By Ron Farrer and Rich Payne

AlphaNews.net is an Alpha news site devoted to all things Alpha. All news
related to the awesome 64-bit processor known as the Alpha is welcome.

The site was created by members of the Alpha Linux Organization to
provide a place to read and discuss Alpha news. AlphaNews.net is believed
to be the first Alpha-related news discussion site. Using the Scoop
software allows users to submit articles and vote which of them should be
posted.
