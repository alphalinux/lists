> Now, my question:
> I have SX164 box with AlphaBios (v5.66). When the power fails and then
> gets back, the box stays off. I have to toggle the power switch to get it
> back up. Is there a way to change this so that the box would come back up
> automatically after the power gets beck?
>
> I know that this is ATX-related and most of Intel mother boards act
> similarly. However, some of them have an option in BIOS that changes
> behaviour. I wonder if there's such thing in AlphaBios.

I'm not sure if you can change this within AlphaBIOS, don't remember
seeing that option. One thing you could do (and I don't think this is a
'supported' option) is to jumper the soft power switch, then you should be
able to control the power by the power supply. I don't know if this will
cause any long term damage to the boards...I just know it worked for me on
an LX.
