What's Cooking at MandrakeSoft?
----------------------------------------
A new web page has been posted that explains how to buy MandrakeSoft
shares on the Euronext Paris "Marche Libre".
http://www.mandrakesoft.com/company/investors/trade

The most important information required by a broker is:
1) The code for the share, which is 4477 (or 4477.PA, MAKE.PA)
2) Is it a "buy" or "sell" order?
3) Always include a "limit" order when conducting trades on the Marche
Libre.
"limit" orders safeguard you from buying shares at too high a price or
selling too low on this market.

Finally, check out
http://www.boursorama.com/cours33.phtml?symbole=1r04477-OTC
http://uk.finance.yahoo.com/q?s=4477.PA&d=t
to keep up-to-date on the latest share prices.

The Adelaide Institute of Technical and Further Education in Australia
uses 144 Mandrake Linux workstations to teach students networking &
