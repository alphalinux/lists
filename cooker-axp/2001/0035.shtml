Product News
----------------------------------------
Mandrake Linux 8.0 ProSuite Edition now available at MandrakeStore.
Designed specifically for small and medium-sized businesses, the
Mandrake Linux 8.0 ProSuite Edition provides all the tools and
applications required for today's demanding computing environment. Built
upon the award winning Mandrake Linux operating system,
the ProSuite Edition includes 7 CDs (plus two free update CDs), 90 days
of MandrakeExpert installation support (48 hr. response time)
and two manuals.

The ProSuite Edition scales easily for large enterprise deployment. A
short list of features include: easy to use configuration tools; the
Apache Advanced Extranet WebServer; Mandrake Control Center;
PHPGroupware and PHP-Nuke community management systems;
reliable and proven email servers Postfix, Sendmail, POP3 and IMAP; LDAP
server to maintain and administer user data; firewall/router
to protect your network; Samba for providing file & print sharing to
Windows PCs; Web Administration with Webmin; securely store your
data with the MySQL or PostgreSQL databases.

Mandrake Linux 8.0 ProSuite Edition is available for purchase at
MandrakeStore:
http://www.mandrakestore.com

MandrakeExpert contest has been announced
----------------------------------------
The "MandrakeExpert of the Month" and "MandrakeExpert of the Year"
contests will award the top Experts from the MandrakeExpert
website. The "MandrakeExpert of the Month" contest will award prizes at
the end of July, August, September, October, November and
December 2001. The "MandrakeExpert of the Year" contest will award the
overall top expert during the six-month period of
July-December 2001.

How to get involved? Simple! Just sign yourself up as an expert at
MandrakeExpert and answer the Linux questions that people will ask
you! Good luck to all participants!
http://www.mandrakeexpert.com/contest.html

Business Case of the Week
----------------------------------------
Services: Hotel Jan III Sobieski.
The Jan III Sobieski Hotel is one of the biggest, most luxurious and
prestigious hotel facilities in Poland. The hotel offers a wide variety
of services ranging from suite rentals, restaurants and hosting
conferences. They use Mandrake Linux for their POS workstations,
servers and office PCs. "The implementation was one of the few stories
of success in the Polish computer industry. I would rather not
make any references to major system failures experienced by large
companies, but we tried to steer clear of the common errors."
http://www.mandrakebizcases.com/article.php?sid=44

Hidden Treasures: Fun With Columns.

a quick lesson about formatting text into columns.
http://www.mandrakeforum.com/article.php?sid=1056?=en

New feature in Cooker! Now it's possible to use USB network adapters to
perform installations.
A new boot image, called usbnet.img, supports USB Controllers and common
USB Network Adapters. This is a cool feature, especially
considering new batches of laptops without pcmcia extensions, and
because most if not all today's computers have USB connectivity.
http://www.mandrakeforum.com/article.php?sid=1055?=en&mode=thread&order=0&thold=0

First 1000 Articles.
Time to celebrate -- this is MandrakeForum article number 1000! More
