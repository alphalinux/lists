On Monday, 4 Dec, Lorenzo Corradini wrote:
>
> My system: Alpha XL 300 (without IDE, only a floppy disk and a SCSI
> connector)
> 64MB RAM (8 SIMMs x 8MB)
> 4 SCSI internal disks (ID=0,1,2,3)
> 1 SCSI external CDROM (ID=4)
> On board integrated SCSI controller and Ethernet
> PowerStorm 4D30T on PCI2 (the second PCI socket starting from
> the CPU board,
> the first is empty)
[...]
>
> Swapping to PALcode at 0x80000....
>
> AND HERE THE SYSTEM HANGS!!!

Perhaps MILO cannot initialise the video card? Can you connect a terminal
to COM1 to see whether MILO is running or dead?
I don't know how many PCI buses there are in XL 300, but if there is more
than one, make sure the video card is installed in a slot on the bus 0.
