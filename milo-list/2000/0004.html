Salut Etienne -

One thing you might try, just a small suggestion: did you change the
power supply? I once heard that the Pyxis chipset used in LX
motherboards is sensitive to bad power supplies. The message you
have is an NMI from the motherboard - typically an ECC error on PCI
bus or main memory.

Regards,
Stig Telfer
