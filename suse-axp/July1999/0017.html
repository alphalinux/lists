I have finaly installed the SuSE-Linux for AXP 6.1 on my Multia (UDB)
Machine last Sunday. It runs. I missed the Setup for generating the Milo- and
Installdisk. But after formatting two disk's (MS-DOS-Format) and copying the
Files "install" and "noname-tga", with the dd-command, at least I can do the
complete installation. The X-Server runs (crawls --) ) also fine with KDE
1.1.1 .
So don't worry, with this version (SuSE-Linux for AXP 6.1) the Multia will
run.
