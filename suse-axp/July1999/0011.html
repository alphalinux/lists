Michael,

> I just had a look on the system requirements for SuSE Linux 6.1 Alpha-AXP.
> Why is the 21064 processor not in the list of supported processors? I have
> a 21064 Multia System with 128 MB RAM and 5 GB disk that is currently
> running RedHat. Is this type of system definitely not working with SuSE
> Linux 6.1 Alpha-AXP or is it just not tested or not recommended because of
> performance reasons? I really would like to have the SuSE distribution on
> the Intel and the Alpha systems.

I haven't got the same amount of RAM nor HD, but my 4 UDB's at home are
running SuSE AXP since the beta came here. The problem is in the TGA card,
or at least that's the main problem I got. Now, as you already have RH,
like me then, you can recompile a 2.2 kernel on that box, and make sure
you've got the TGA support included, possibly also use the right NIC and
SCSI (or do you have IDE ?) drivers.

Something extra: I bet you won't even notice, but 48 MB or plus was needed
to do an FTP-install, even after removing some options I guess (or no,
then 32 was OK)
