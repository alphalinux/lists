Hallo,

I am not sure if everything works with 6.4. I successfully installed SuSe
6.3 on a 233 MHz Multia, however, it was not easy, because the standard
aboot of 6.3 does not support the TGA grahics of the Multia. in addition,
KDE does not work. I think these things have been fixed with 6.4, but I did
not try myself.

BTW, the Multia runs much better with Linux than it did with NT. And
another good operating system to run on the Multia is OpenVMS.
