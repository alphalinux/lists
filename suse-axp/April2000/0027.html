I spent quite a time to find a working ghostscript rpm package
which would be installed on SuSE-6.3 for Alpha.
Unfortunately, the redhat-derived Ghostscript rpms cannot be
installed due to conflicts. All rpm versions derived from SuSE
did not work. The symptom in my case is that the gray-scale gets
black instead of gray. This makes ghostscript unusable in most
circumstances. Both gs versions 5.10 and 5.50 showed the same
behavior.

BTW, this does not happen on SuSE-6.3 for i386.

Someone suggested to hand-compile ghostscript-6.0. Well, I would
just access one of Intel linux machines in the network.
