I had diffuculties as well with fdisk of SuSE 6.3 AXP (SRM on an AS255) - Yast
was not able to "see" the BSD-Partitions I created. To solve this problem I
created the partitions with SuSE 6.2 AXP and installed 6.3. The swap was not
recocnised - I created it manuallay after successful instalation with mkswap or
something like that.
