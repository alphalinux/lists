I used Larry's suggestion to just blow away the old partiitons with dd
if=/dev/zero of=/dev/sdx bs=1024k count=4 and it worked like a charm.
I did have to re-init the system so yast could see that the disks were blank
though.
On another note when do we think that "swriteboot" will work properly on
SuSE Linux?

I made the incorrect assumption that it worked and ended up having to
reinstall again doing it manually.
