Hey folks!

I have got an alpha 21164 SX properly working with kernel 2.2.13. My printer is
a HP Laserjet 5L which printed on SuSE 6.1 but since I graded up to SuSE 6.3,
it doesn't print anymore:

When I try to print with "lpr file.ps", dmesg tells me:

gs(2706): unaligned trap at 0000000120048bfc: 0000000120306c7a 28 1
gs(2706): unaligned trap at 00000001200499d4: 0000000120306c7a 28 1
gs(2706): unaligned trap at 0000000120049d28: 0000000120306c7a 28 3
gs(2706): unaligned trap at 0000000120062480: 0000000120306cea 28 4

When I try to print with " echo hello > /dev/lp0", I even don't get any error
messages. There is no light blinking, so I know that the printer doesn't get
any signals! The cable is connected (on WinNT 4 I can print). The printer is
the only device connected to parallel interface.

The modules parport_pc, parport and lp are loaded on every printing request.
My printcap is configured with yast:

ascii|lp1|ljet4-a4-ascii-mono-300|ljet4 a4 ascii mono 300:\
        :lp=/dev/lp0:\
        :sd=/var/spool/lpd/ljet4-a4-ascii-mono-300:\
        :lf=/var/spool/lpd/ljet4-a4-ascii-mono-300/log:\
        :af=/var/spool/lpd/ljet4-a4-ascii-mono-300/acct:\
        :if=/var/lib/apsfilter/bin/ljet4-a4-ascii-mono-300:\
        :la@:mx#0:\
        :tr=:cl:sh:sf:
#
lp|lp2|ljet4-a4-auto-mono-300|ljet4 a4 auto mono 300:\
        :lp=/dev/lp0:\
        :sd=/var/spool/lpd/ljet4-a4-auto-mono-300:\
        :lf=/var/spool/lpd/ljet4-a4-auto-mono-300/log:\
        :af=/var/spool/lpd/ljet4-a4-auto-mono-300/acct:\
        :if=/var/lib/apsfilter/bin/ljet4-a4-auto-mono-300:\
        :la@:mx#0:\
        :tr=:cl:sh:sf:
#
raw|lp3|ljet4-a4-raw|ljet4 a4 raw:\
        :lp=/dev/lp0:\
        :sd=/var/spool/lpd/ljet4-a4-raw:\
        :lf=/var/spool/lpd/ljet4-a4-raw/log:\
        :af=/var/spool/lpd/ljet4-a4-raw/acct:\
        :if=/var/lib/apsfilter/bin/ljet4-a4-raw:\
        :la@:mx#0:\
        :tr=:cl:sh:sf:

conf.modules is configured:

alias parport_lowlevel parport_pc
options parport_pc io=0x378 irq=none

Has anybody a hint for me why my printer doesn't work???
