Hi Lennart, hi all!

Yes, there is Applixware for AXP (version 4.4.1 at least). I managed to
order it about a year ago online. It has import filters for M$ Office 97
which do work for _not_ _too_ _big/complicated_ documents (I had only 2-3
excel sheets which were empty...).

No, I did not find an upgrade offer 4.4.1 to 4.4.2 or any 5.0 beta for
AXP :-(

There is an online store on http://store.applixware.com/ telling me the
price is still $99 for 4.4.1 AXP and $189 for 4.4.1 AXP
"developer". "developer" has some additional printed manuals for learning
the embeded macro language.

Hope this helps ;-)
