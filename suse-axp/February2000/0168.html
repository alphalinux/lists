I have had a date problem where I could boot my Alphastation. I don't know
about a Digital server but there is a discrepancy between the way ARC and
SRM see dates. Setting the data via the OS or in one firmware will confuse
the other.

My solution was this (I don't know if it was the best or the quickest) I
took my battery out. Which left me with all my defaults (boot information
etc.) lost and I was stuck in the SRM console I could not even switch to
ARC. I did a firmware upgrade (which I think effectively installed ARC)
then I was allowed to change the date again without getting any errors.

If you use SRM you may not even have to bother doing the frimware install
unless you want the latest and greatest.

Hacking from a point of extreme ignorance sometimes works!

Justin
