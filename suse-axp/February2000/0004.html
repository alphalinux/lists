>
> /dev/sda1 boot (blocks 1-2)
> /dev/sda2 /swap
> /dev/sda3 /
> /dev/sda4 /boot
>
If you have this partition table then I'm guessing your kernel is in sda1 or
sda4?
Then you should be trying to boot from partition 1 or 4?

so your aboot string should look like this:

0:[1 or 4]/vmlinuz ro root=/dev/sda2
