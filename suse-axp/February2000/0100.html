> The Matrox G200 is working nicely in my Miata. You may even be able
> to get the Voodoo 3 PCI cards to work as well.

G200 is good, just about any Permedia II cards (Elsa Gloria Synergy,
3DLabs Oxygen ACX, there's a diaomnd also I think). Voodoo3 will work but
you'll probably need an XFree86 4.0 pre-release (which I might add is
very stable for me). Stay away from anything TNT2 based.
