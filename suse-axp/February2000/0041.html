Well, IMHO, it's infinitely better to have Netscape 4.7 on AlphaLinux now
than Mozilla at an unspecified time in the future. I will certainly switch
to Mozilla once it is mature, but for the moment, NS is the answer to my
prayers.

It's the same old story as in AlphaNT vs. x86NT: How are you going to get
people to adopt the Alpha, an undisputably better technology, when even the
most basic desktop applications, like a decent browser, isn't available. In
the AlphaNT case, it was the lack of Office compatibility which killed it.

Please, note also that the really good thing with Netscape for AlphaLinux
being released by Compaq, is not that we now can run Netscape, but that we
have more binary compatibility with Tru64 since we got necessary libraries,
too.
