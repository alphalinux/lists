Yes, that did it! I booted NT and copied LINLOAD and milo from the boot
floppy to sdc1: After that the following changes to the boot selections
worked:

OSLOADER=scsi(0)disk(6)rdisk(0)partition(1)\LINLOAD
OSLOADOPTIONS=boot sdc1:vmlinuz root=/dev/sdc3
