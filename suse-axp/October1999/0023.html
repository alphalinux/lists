Are the softlinks to the driver set? I had to manually change the existing
softling to the tga driver.

I don't remember which one I had to change but currently, my links are:

/usr/X11/bin/X -> /var/X11R6/bin/X -> /usr/X11R6/bin/XF86_TGA

(And in case you're such a newbie as I am (absolutely no offence intended!);
softlinks are maintained with the "ln -s" command, as in "ln -s
/var/X11R6/bin/X /usr/X11R6/bin/XF86_TGA". Do "ln --help". I had to hunt for
that little piece of knowledge...)
