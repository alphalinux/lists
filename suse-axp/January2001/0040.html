Hi to all,

just for your information:

I upgraded an Alpha Station 200 4/166 ("Avanti") from kernel
2.2.9 to 2.2.16 (SuSE 6.1 -> 7.0).
The machine has a "Digital DC21040 Tulip" ethernet card.

I could not access the ethernet at all using the new kernel.

I then changed the configuration to use the "old" version
of the driver ("CONFIG_DEC_ELCP_OLD"), now it works.

Regards and success to all,
