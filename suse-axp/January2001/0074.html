Hello,

we have NO experience with the Promise FastTrak 100 IDE-RAID.
The Promise FastTrak 100 IDE-RAID is *NOT* a Hardware RAID-Controller,
controller, but a software-RAID. Perhaps you ca use it as a
UDMA-Controller, but for the RAID function you need drivers which do not
exist for LINUX and twotimes not exist for ALPHA LINUX.
