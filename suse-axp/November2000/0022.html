I've been working with 4 that we got back from a loaner program and are
trying to see if we can make them work with Linux. So far, I have been
able to persuade 1 of the 4 to install RH 6.2, but haven't managed to get
a GUI working on it yet (2 of them appear to have power supply or other
hardware issues, and the last I haven't figured out just yet). Make sure
your SRM is updated to the latest version for the 2100; I found 5.3B at:

ftp://ftp.digital.com/pub/DEC/Alpha/firmware/v5.8/alpha2100/

One of the files should be right for your system; the file 'fwreadme.txt'
will help you figure it out. Feel free to email me if you like; I'll do
what I can to help.
