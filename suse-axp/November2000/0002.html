> I'm trying to install SUSE 6.3 (over RH 6.0) on an 164SX.
>
Your problem is trying to install SUSE 6.3 on an Alpha -- as if it was a PC.

Boot off the 6.3 CD-rom from the SRM, and installation takes off merilly.

X works fine, depending upon what graphics cards you have. If you have a
PowerStorm card, you're out of luck. There are no Linux Drivers for the
cards. Evans and Sutherland evidently won't allow them to be written even
though Compaq has offered to port the Tru64 drivers over to Alpha Linux.
{I suspect primarily because the PowerStorm cards are seriously obsolete.
E&S Current cards are based on a totally new chipset.)

Also, check out the Compaq "Alpha Jump Start" cd. You can download it and
burn your own or wait about 4-8 weeks to get one (free) in the mail.

http://www.compaq.com/alphaserver/linux/
