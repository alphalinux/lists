> I'm trying to install SUSE 6.3 (over RH 6.0) on an 164SX.
>
> I can boot the floppy and load MILO. At this point I can, "MILO> hdb:".
> I can then look at my CDROM and see the files on it (this alone took
> hours to figure out).
>
> I have tried the following:
> 1. MILO>boot hdb:/etc/vmlinux.gz root=/dev/fd0 fake_initrd
> ---asks for "boot disk"

You have to create a disk with the image /disks/install from the 1. CD,
insert it when asked for the bootdisk.
(The bootprocedure differs between RH and SuSE at this point: RH mounts
the CD to get the installation System, SuSE installs from a RAM disk)

The other oprtion (if available) is to boot from SRM-Console.
