> Isn't DRI a Intel-only technology?

No, DRI currently has support for Intel, PPC, and Alpha,
though the Alpha port at least is not heavily tested. It's
not a matter of if it can be done, it's a matter of who is
motivated enough to contribute. :-) I wish I had the time to
work on DRI on Alpha but thankfully there are a few folks out
there working on it.
