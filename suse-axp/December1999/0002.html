This is what I do. All of our new servers get shipped with that combo card
(specifically DS20s and ES40s). It is a good card.
Take out the card. Put in a Qlogic then do the install. After you do the
install build a kernel and make sure you put in support for the Symbios
53C875. Power down the system and put the combo card back in. That is, in my
opinion, the easiest way to get the system up and running.

The new kernels that we have don't have this problem but the generic kernel
that ships on the cdrom does not have the correct support. that is why you
get stuck in that loop.
