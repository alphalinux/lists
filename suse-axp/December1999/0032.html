I want to configure an Xterminal via bootp! So I did the following entries in inetd.conf:

tftp dgram udp wait root /usr/sbin/tcpd in.tftpd
bootps dgram udp wait root /usr/sbin/bootpd bootpd -i

and bootptab:
 
xter:sm=255.255.255.0:\
        :hd=/usr/local/terminal/ncd:bf=Xncd15r:\
 :ht=ether:\
 :ha=0000A700B547:\
 :ip=192.168.2.2:\

(In the /usr/local/terminal/ncd directory is the Bootfile and other configuration-files of the x-terminal. my system: SuSE-Axp 6.1, Kernel 2.2.9

When I turn on the X-Terminal, "tail /var/log/messages" writes:
Dec 9 11:45:10 cerebrum login[174]: ROOT LOGIN on `tty1'
Dec 9 11:53:44 cerebrum bootpd[409]: version 2.4.3
Dec 9 11:53:44 cerebrum bootpd[409]: ioctl SIOCSARP: Invalid argument

Is this an error of bootp under alpha linux, (I read that ioctl is not machine independent) or did I forget something to configure??

Thanx in advance Rudi
