> the CD runs aboot, but it gets an "Invalid compressed format"
[...]
> Any suggestions other than suicide (I've already considered that one) are

Have you compiled aboot yourself? Maybe you need an aboot with all the
patches. aboot on 6.1 should have all these. If you use our aboot and
get this message, the last resort may be to uncompress the kernel and
compress it without the "-9" flag to gzip which is used when you do a
make boot in the kernel tree.
