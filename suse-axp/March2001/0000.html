SuSE 7.0 Axp system.
sendmail is used as the SMTP-system on localhost which manages outgoing
mail with my ISP. Sendmail is started with the typical ISP-settings "-bd
-om" (I think it was) and called explicid by "sendmail -q" whenever
there's mail to deliver.
This works fine as long as i use the commandline <mail -s subject
