> This is actually no problem. Have a look at www.alphalinux.org under docs
> jensen you will find updated instructins to get things going. In addition
> to that the SuSE 6.4 cd even is bootable on the jensen. IF (and only
> if) your cd-rom is jumpered for 512 byte blocks instead of the more common
> 2048 byte blocks. You only have to get into SRM and type 'boot dkXN00',
> where X is a letter standing for the scsi host adapter, and N a number
> which equals the scsi-id of your cd-rom drive. (If in daubt 'show dev' is
> your friend).
Once you are in aboot just type '3' and there you go...
(maybe 'boot dkXN00 -fl 3' in SRM will also get you into the installer)
