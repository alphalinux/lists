SuSE offers installation via ftp. Just download and create the boot floppy,
boot it and select FTP installation. You'll have to enter your IP
configuration by hand, and look up the ip-address of ftp.suse.com
beforehand, though.

Whether this is better or worse than an ISO image is, of course, a matter of
opinion. You'll propably end up downloading a lot less than a full ISO
image, but on the hand if you need to do it more than once you might
eventually have downloaded more, and the full contents of the 7.0
distribution covers 6 CDs...

It is of course the ONLY way to go if you don't have a CD burner handy.
