The pdksh.rpm on the 6.1 Beta installs a ksh
that segfaults immediately when you try executing it.

I pulled pdksh-5.2.8 off of metalab/sunsite, and
compiled it. Same problem. I took a look at where
it was crashing, and made the following change:

In config.h (generated by ./configure script):
old:
#define HAVE_SYS_SIGLIST 1
new:
#undef HAVE_SYS_SIGLIST

[And I'm sure that you're not supposed to edit it
by hand, but it was a quick way to see what was happening.]

Then I rebuilt it, and got a working ksh out of the deal.
Well, at least it doesn't die while starting up :-)

Until the problem between pdksh and the system siglist
is resolved, you might want to compile pdksh this way
(or your favorite variant :-)...
