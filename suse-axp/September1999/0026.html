Dear colleagues,

in the meanwhile I did some more research regarding the ghostscript printing
problem with SuSE 6.1 AXP. What I did last was to get the ghostscript 5.50
version and the SuSE patches from the SuSE FTP server. I applied the SuSE
patches, compiled and installed the whole package hoping this would give better
results than the package from the distribution CDs. The result is: the behaviour
did not change at all. gs still crashes. At least I made one new observation. gs
works for low resolutions and has only problems with high resolutions. This
means: Viewing a PS-file with ghostscript works but when I open a window to zoom
part of the text it will not work due to the increased output resolution. If I
define my cdj550 printer with a resolution of 100 dpi it works, but if I
increase the resolution to 150 or more ghostscript will crash and my print job
disappears.

I will continue searching for the cause but meanwhile I think about setting up
an additional old Intel box only for printing.

best regards

  Michael
