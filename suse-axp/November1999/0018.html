Excuse me if I'm checking the obvious, but:

You need 2 diskettes to boot. The 1:st should be the "aboot" diskette when
booting from SRM (or "avanti" when booting from ARC), the 2:nd the "install"
diskette.

(I have both documentation and CDs at home, so I can't verify the exact
names of the diskettes right now; I'm quoting from my own unreliable
memory!)

(I have seen the behaviour you describe when I accidentally forget changing
to the 2:nd diskette before hitting a key to load the kernel.)

Otherwise try recreating the diskettes using new, fresh, unused diskettes.
Perhaps you have a bad spot somewhere.
