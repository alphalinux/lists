> Are you saying that ccc generated code with a 60% LOSS of performance over
> gcc? What were the compiler switches you used? Sounds like you are
> comparing an optimized version to a debug.

No optimization nor debug. The thing was that without any tuning or optim.
gcc was faster. But gcc didn't, even at highest opt. level, produce a
faster binary. All it did was ruin the float instructions. ccc created a
63% faster bin. After that I didn't have time to try anymore. I just have
to get a precompiled bin to test the correctness of the output. If it's
good then the Linux Alpha have a much faster way to create mp3s...
