I had a CMI8330 ISA PnP-card, that worked with isapnp and the
kernelmodules.
I just took the configuration from pnpdump, edited conf.modules
and it worked.
But I tried some other soundcards eg. ES1370 that crashed my Alpha
(Ruffian)
