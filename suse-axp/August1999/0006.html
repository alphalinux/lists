Hi to all!

I'm a Newbi to sound on linux -- I had troubles with all systems I tried
to install sound drivers... :(

My system is a LX164 with a AWE64 (ISA-PnP). The card is configured
properly and activated. Kernel is compiled as described in
/usr/src/linux/Documentation/sound/AWE32. Modules are configured in
/etc/conf.modules as:
------------------------8<-----------------------------
alias sound sb
alias midi awe_wave
post-install awe_wave /usr/bin/sfxload /usr/synthfm.sbk
options sb io=0x220 irq=5 dma=1 dma16=5 mpu_io=0x330
------------------------8<-----------------------------
with the last line showing my /etc/isapnp.conf for the AWE64.

And now a 'modprobe sound' gives me:
------------------------8<-----------------------------
/lib/modules/2.2.9/misc/sound.o: invalid parameter parm_io
/lib/modules/2.2.9/misc/sound.o: insmod /lib/modules/2.2.9/misc/sound.o
failed
/lib/modules/2.2.9/misc/sound.o: insmod sound failed
------------------------8<-----------------------------

What am I missing ?
