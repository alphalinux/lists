On Tue, 16 May 2000, G. Hugh Song wrote:

>
> Yes, I had that exact trouble with Cheeta. Those new LVD hard disks
> do not have the termination jumper. According to SCSI gurus, it
> needs a separate termination device at the end of the internal
> cable. I have not seen such a thing unfortunately. I was able
> to use the Cheeta on the single-ended connector. So, my idea is
> just an opinion at the moment.
Hi,

termination for LVD was the first thought that crossed my mind
when I heard of all these troubles. I'm not sure about extra
LVD-terminators, but here in Germany they sell SCSI-LVD cables
with 3 or 7 connectors and a LVD terminator fixed at one end.
Works pretty reliable on one of my machines (dual-x86 with
an AIC7890 onboard)
