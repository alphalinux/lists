Yes, we've seen this as well.
 
> I am about to order an ITI card for my UP2000. Exactly, ITI6101-U2.
> Before the final decision, I would like to confirm that this specific
> model works with UP2000 and my Cheeta.
>
> If you have ever used ITI6101-U2 on a UP2000 or UP1000, please tell
> us about it.

I haven't tried a 6101-U2 specifically but I've used a 6100L and 6200L and
they seem to work OK.

> When the ITI6101-U2 is put on a PCI slot, does the on-board Adaptec
> get disabled? How does the machine select the boot disk?

No, the adaptec stays enabled. The machine doesn't select the boot disk,
you do via the bootdef_dev SRM variable.
