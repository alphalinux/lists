Yes, I had that exact trouble with Cheeta. Those new LVD hard disks
do not have the termination jumper. According to SCSI gurus, it
needs a separate termination device at the end of the internal
cable. I have not seen such a thing unfortunately. I was able
to use the Cheeta on the single-ended connector. So, my idea is
just an opinion at the moment.
