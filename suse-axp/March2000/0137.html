Today, I had my ethernet checked by the school technician.
I found that the Tulip driver malfunctions with 2.2.15pre15 kernel for
the recent hardware from Digital. I think mine uses 21143, the
newest chip from Compaq, Etherwork something.
The symptom of the malfunction is losing the ethernet connection
at peak load or something.

He checked this with his Fluke Test hardware. The Test hardware
found the trouble even when the load is not high. So, I recompiled
the kernel choosing the DE-500 driver instead of the Tulip. Then,
everything became OK.

The problem with the SuSE-6.3 installer appears to be due to the fact
that if it finds the Digital's Ethernet hardware, it tries to use the
Tulip driver by default.

I'm gonna retry installing SuSE-6.3 after swapping the ethernet
card with my old one.
