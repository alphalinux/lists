At first I've got to say that I'm not experienced with Alphas at all.

I just tried to install SuSE on an AS250/4.
Therefore I created a "aboot" disk with rawrite and the file "aboot_xx" from
ftp.suse.com.

To boot the disk I enter at the SRM console:
boot dva0 -f vmlinuz.gz -flags "root=/dev/fd0 load_ramdisk=1
/bootdevice=/dev/fd0"
