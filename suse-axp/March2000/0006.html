hi,

according to the compaq support pages on
http://www.iol.unh.edu/linux/fortran/cfal.html,
the cfalrtl_rh52-1.0-2 package should be used for non
red-hat systems.

we are using this version on our ds10 with suse 6.3 without any
problems.

i could not find any exact information concerning differences
of the two cfalrtl packages. anyone out there with more luck?
