> Hi,
>
> On Thu, 9 Mar 2000, Wolfgang Kutscherauer wrote:
>
> > In our department we hav two old Alpha-Server AS1000 4/266 with
> > MIKASA-Boards, NCR 53810 SCSI and Mylex DAC960 Raid-Controller (PCI).
> >
> > I tried to install a SuSE 6.1 AXP on it. I was able to boot from
> > Floppy, but i failed when i tried to load the kernelmodule for the
> > Mylex DAC 960. Must i insert parameters für IRQ and/or I/O-Adress? I
> > think there is no need for parameters for PCI-cards.
> >
> > Have anyone experiences in installing a AS1000 with Mylex DAC 960 Raid?
>
> Hmm, I am not sure about this, but did 6.1 already support the Mylex
> Controller? You might want to use SuSE Linux 6.3 (AXP) instead.
The module could not be loaded on alpha because of a kernel-problem on 6.1.
Loading fails with "unresolved symbol ioremap_nocache" which was not
defined in arch/alpha at that time. It should be fixed in 2.2.13 and
chances are the driver works on 6.3.
