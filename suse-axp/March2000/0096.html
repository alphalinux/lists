The flash on axppci33 is small, so your SRM is limited in its
capabilities
to destroy itself. Change the jumpers on the motherboard (read MILO
HOWTO,
it has a good description) and use 'failsafe boot' process to reinstall
other
bootloader image in flash from floppy.
