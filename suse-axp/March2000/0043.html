I am not really certain what you are looking for. There is no such beast to
my knowledge, SRM is SRM is SRM. However the installation guides provided
by Compaq for our supported machines (http://www.compaq.com/linux) do
provide some information on SRM installations. (The DPW is fairly close to
the XP) After those the Distro manuals seem to be fairly complete on older
hardware.

If you are looking for information on the PCI bus you have 2 options.
1. Open the machine and be able to read the chips and wires.
2. Use srm's show config,dev,etc. (help show) to look at the system
configuration and available devices.
