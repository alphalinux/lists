On Mon, 27 Oct 1997, Keith Griffith wrote:

> I have a 166mhz UDB ( soldered on cache and processor ) running RHL 4.2
> with X and want to get 800 X 600 hi color out of my TGA video. It was
> preset ( I guess, because this is the only resolution it works in ) 640
> X 480 256 color. I have scoured the FAQ's and archives, but can't find
> any pictures or explanations of locations for the video setting jumpers.
> I have multiple FAQ's regarding what jumper goes where for what
> resolution, but I have 2 separate groups of jumpers on my mlb, neither
> of which are labeled. I dunno what to change!

There should not be any jumpers you need to change, just change
the modeline uncommented in the XF86Config file. I have run the UDB's
video resolution in X all the way up to 1600x1200 with no problems. Though
it is impossible to get more than 256 colors (8bit) out of the card at any
resolution. Apparently the card was just plainly never designed to handle
such things. Hope this helps. If you want a copy of my XF86Config, just
ask.
