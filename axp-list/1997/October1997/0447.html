hi.
i have the same machine, and achieved 800x600 by trial and error with
XF86Config. just commented out all the modes and modelines, uncommenting
one at a time the modes for 800x600 until it came up correctly. i did not
need to change any jumpers on the board. unfortunately i cant tell you the
settings that worked for me cause the box is down at the moment and i am
not there to bring it up and find out...
apparently the card does not support anything but 256 colors, but i did
get the higher resolution out of it.
