It appears that the problem is *NOT* inherent in IDE or the LX164, but can
be encountered on (at least) a few other Alpha platforms as well (PC164 and
CABRIO are two I've personal experience with).

It's an installation-related problem, that seems to occur whenever you use
a large IDE disk and partition/mke2fs it DURING INSTALLATION.

The workaround is to partition and mke2fs BEFORE installation, and then
during installation DO NEITHER.

Note that the problem does not occur with RH 4.9.1's "Disk Druid"... :-)
