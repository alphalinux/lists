> My recommendation is to never use anything higher than -O for

As I run into examples which were creating problems with -O but
not with -O2 or higher (I do no remember them at the moment but
they were for real) I do not think that this is good recommendation.
Also a code which is using 'inline' function may create problems
below -O2. Driving slowly does not allways mean driving safely. :-)

Also below -O2 you do not really have much of an optimization with
gcc. This means that you may be crippling your machine in a vast
majority of cases for naught.
