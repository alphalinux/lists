Uh, this is confusing, but the original post concerned the XL-233 or XL-266,
not what we now call the XLT (XL-300 and XL-366). The latter have no such
limitations.

And yes, the builtin Ethernet is a DEC TULIP chip; use the DE4X5 driver.
