*Please* if you ever get subscribed to a mailing list and you want to
leave it, but don't know how to, just post a message to it saying
"I have been subscribed to this list by someone else, and I don't know
how to unsubscribe; can someone tell me how".

back to the mailing list and wasting lots of bandwidth.
