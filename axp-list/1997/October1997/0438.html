Dave Wreski writes:
> Just to let you know I'm not disputing whether or not tar can backup over a
> network. I just know that not only have I experienced incidents of not being
> able to get an archive off a tape, because I wrote it with tar, and it was
> corrupt 1/3rd the way in..

When this has happened to me, I just seek past the bad section & start
tar from there. If you don't seek to the beginning of a file from the
tar archive, then GNU tar will print some message about that, and will
read until it sees something which looks like the start of a file.

In any case, I think this has gotten pretty far off topic...
