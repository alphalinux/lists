>I'm looking for a backup utility that can backup across the network to a
>single Colorado Jumbo 1400. Basically what I have is 3 UDB's with
>3 hard drives totaling 4.5 GB running Redhat 4.2 (2.0.30) and 1 Intel PC

We've been using Amanda to backup lots of Unix boxes (Ultrix, Digital Unix,
AIX) for about 4 years. I've not yet backed up any Linux boxes but I'm lead
to believe that there are lots of users out there doing it. (My problem is
that the version of dump I have doesn't like the version of ext2fs that I
run...).
