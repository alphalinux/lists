At 11:54 AM 10/9/97 -0400, you wrote:
>Hi! When I install on my udb, everything goes ok, but after the install,
>I reboot from the milo floppy and try to boot from the hard drive from milo
>< milo: boot sda2:vmlinux.gz > I get a cannot load kernel message. What
>am I doing wrong?
>
>TIA
>
>Keith Griffith

I had the same problem on my latest install and apparently, somewhere along
the line, the Kernel was never copied to the hard drive. I booted as:

boot fd0:vmlinux.gz root=/dev/sda3

and from there compiled a new kernel. Of course, you could just use the
original kernel if it suits you.
