Ok, here's what I know:

Well, not much. =) The chipset is a 21172 (21172BA and 21172CA chips), the
CPU is a 500Mhz. There are 8 SIMM slots, 4 PCI slots, 2 ISA slots if I
recall. Does anybody know which precompiled milo/kernel set I should be
using? I have no clue what kind of motherboard it is or anything, as my
company purchased it before I came into this position. We're trashing NT
and I've been working (read: fighting) with it for a week now. I've
successfully booted with the MILO-2031-PC164 and PC164-2031-test.gz
precompiled images that I got (I think) from gatekeeper. I ftp'd the
entire alpha subdirectory from redhat.com (pub/redhat/RedHat-4.2/alpha),

stripped kernel, and cp vmlinux.gz /dev/fd0.
