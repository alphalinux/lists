Unfortunately, this is true. In fact, CNN aired a segment on it. Because
of the ongoing infringement argument between the two entities, it seems
like this is the only way for the two to settle (albeit, not the best way
IMO). Rumour has it also that Intel may incorporate some of the Alpha
technology into it's future generation Pentium lines.

Personally, I think this is a disgrace and agree that the Justice Dept
should be analysing this "deal", but until it happens, there isn't
anything to scrutinise.

What I'm wondering is if Intel is even going to pretend to help the Alpha
processor family along for awhile. If not, then alot of the work that
I've done porting software will be in vain :( Also, even if they do help
the processor family along, then I hope theydon't end up discontinuing it
outright (which they probably will, but we can always hope, can't we).
