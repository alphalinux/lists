On Thu, 23 Oct 1997, Christopher Young wrote:

> I have no idea who you folks are, but quit sending me your junk mail.
>
> I got home yesterday and found 25 messages in my mail box.
>
> I compained individually to each of you. The response? 60 messages over
> night.
>
> What is this? Revenge?

Well, the list server is probably not sending you any more messages than
any one else on the list, so I think personal revenge is out of the
question... You simply got subscribed to a list unintentionally.

> I have never signed up to be on your listserver.
>
> Who ever is the list master, please delete me from your list. I would also
> like a personal explanation from you as to why/how I got stuck with you.
>
> I'm sure you serve a purpose. However my interests are elsewhere.

Naturally you should not be placed on a list you have not asked for, and I
have no idea why that has happened, nor am I responsible for maintaining
this list. But there is no reason to be upset about this, simply follow
the instructions about unsubscription following each and every mail on
this list:

--
To unsubscribe: send e-mail to axp-list-request@redhat.com with
'unsubscribe' as the subject.  Do not send it to axp-list@redhat.com


It actually says exactly what to do if you don't want to receive any more
"junk mail" as you put it. I see no reason to reply to every mail you get
that you don't want it when there are clear instructions on how to get off
the mailing list. For each mail you send, every innocent participant on
this list will receive a copy, and we are not any more interrested in
your problems than you are in this list. So please follow these 
instructions and stop flooding the list with any more unneeded mails.
