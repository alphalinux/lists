Hi all,

Well, I found the problem preventing me from being able to ftp
to my UDB. <Drum roll please> - it was a couple of people suggested PAM.
In the /etc/pam.d/ftp file I played with some of the lines and found
only the first line needed to be removed for it to work:

#%PAM-1.0
#auth required /lib/security/pam_listfile.so item=user sense=deny
file=/etc/ftpusers onerr=succeed
auth required /lib/security/pam_pwdb.so shadow nullok
auth required /lib/security/pam_shells.so
account required /lib/security/pam_pwdb.so
session required /lib/security/pam_pwdb.so

So now I'm wondering what options on the first auth line might be causing
pam to deny all ftp users access?
