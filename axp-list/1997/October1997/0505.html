>any [performace] comparisons to the 533MHz from microway/aspsys
>mentioned recently (when taking different clock rates into account;
>would virtual XL533 and Aspen533 or XL300 and downclocked Aspen300
>be the about the same?? if not, why?) ?

The pc164LX-533Mhz and the new 164SX(211PC)-533Mhz is easily twice as fast.
Benchmark tests we have run have shown the new SX to be about 10~20% faster
than the PC164-500Mhz and the LX is even faster due to 2mb 3rd level cache.
In addition the 211PC processor has only 8kb 2nd level cache and the 21164
series has 96kb.

The XL-300 is about even with a Pentium Pro 200Mhz on interger related work
but faster on floating point intensive jobs, but depending on what your
using it for you milage may vary.
