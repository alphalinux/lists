I am using IP masquerading, on my Linux-Alpha box. Everything seems to
work fine for the 2 Win95 boxes that are being masqueraded.

Today I wanted to try modules (ip_masq_quake in particular).

I have the following lines in my rc.local file.

# ip masquerading modules
/sbin/depmod -a
/sbin/modprobe ip_masq_ftp
/sbin/modprobe ip_masq_quake

After rebooting, quake doesn't seem to work, so I tried loading the module
manually using...

[root@palm030 /root]# modprobe ip_masq_quake
[root@palm030 /root]# modprobe -c | grep masq
[root@palm030 /root]#

As you can see, 'modprobe -c' doesn't show any modules having to do with
masq.

Can anyone help me with this? Why isn't this working? This shows that I
have made and installed the modules (I think).

[root@palm030 /]# dir /lib/modules/2.0.30/ipv4/
ip_masq_cuseeme.o ip_masq_irc.o ip_masq_raudio.o
ip_masq_ftp.o ip_masq_quake.o ip_masq_vdolive.o
