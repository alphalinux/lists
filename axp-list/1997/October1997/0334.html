>Hello !

> I have actually a big problem on a Multia 166/24Mg with an extern 3.2G
SCSI harddisk Ibm fireball. On this I have redhat 4.2. Everything is going
well for one hour or two, I can do everything I want, the disk is in
read-write mode. But after this period, the disk change ??? to read-only
mode and I can't do anything else ! even Cron can't write in /var and I not
neither reboot ! I have to stop the multia by hand. It is wery
unconfortable ! I can just work for I hour or two ! Do someone know where
it can come from ? I need help.
>
>Thak you very much in advance,

If you just need to get to the hard drive to update some files so things
work correctly, you can try: mount -n -o remount rw /dev/sda2

You may need to change the device, but this is a common one used with the UDB.
