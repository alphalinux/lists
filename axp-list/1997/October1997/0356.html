IMPORTANT NOTE: there's a single line patch you must put into 076 (081 is
similar), which will keep "unaligned accesses" to a minimum; change the
obvious line to:

#define SKBUFF_RX_COPYBREAK 16384

There's a whole thread of messages giving a bit more details about this
in the axp-list archives from early this past September; search "tulip.c".
