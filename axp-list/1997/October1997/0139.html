I have the utmost respect for you Jon and your position at Digital.
However, Bob Palmer has made his numbers quarter after quarter by either
firing people or selling yet another part of the company. It is not out
of the scope of what Digital has done to put its own technology on the
table for sale. The last people to acknowledge it would be those in the
know. Case in point, CohesionWorx. I was in a government program that
had signed up to use (I might add on my recommendation) the CohesionWorx
IDE for their CM. We were up one level under Palmer with the deal. The
ink had not dried on the deal when we read in a computer rag (actually
the customer gave it to us) that the product had been sold to a third
party. We were all red faced that it took us another week through
"official" channels to find out that the story was correct. I know you
truly believe what you are saying, but I am just as sure that Bob will
do anything to make the numbers. I also know that more the Journal has
reported this "rumor" and it seems to have some factual basis that you,
nor I, am probably aware.
