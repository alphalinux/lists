I am running v4.2 under a HD that has 95 sectore/track. I noticed that
this was a problem when fdisk only accepted numbers from 1 to 63 for the
number of sectors. I tried implementing the work around found on the
RedHat site (fudge the geometry of the disk but just make sure that the
total number of sectors is less than or equal to the actual number) but I
still suffered from inode map corruption.

Has anybody else encountered thsi problem and found a way around it?
