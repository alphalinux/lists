I have been using my 3C509B since I made the driver work with linux/alpha
more than a year ago, and the trick is to put the card in an intel machine
and disable the PnP there, setting up the IO/IRQ so that it fits in your
machine. The program isapnp might enable you to use PnP cards, but don't
use it unless you have to.
