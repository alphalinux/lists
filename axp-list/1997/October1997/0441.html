non PnP cards are required for the alpha, since they don't have a PnP bios, and
since the isapnptools kit for alpha systems is still under development. my best
suggestion is one of the folowing. a WSS (windows sound system) software setable
card.. it's not PnP, but has a EEPROM. and is built in to the multia systems..
so the driver has had some development. (i think) #2 an old SB16, easy compatab
ility, and what not. you can also try GUS cards

WSS cards are mostly made by Aztech, but may have packard bell, or revel stamped
over the aztech chip.. they arn't the geatest cards ever made, but they work wel
l, and have high quality crystal DAC.. you can find them on the surplus market f
or ~$30
