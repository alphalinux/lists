I've just put out the binaries for em86-0.2-2 on my internal site, and
they should appear at ftp.digital.com real soon.

Fixes:

- em86 now autodetects whether you're running a properly-patched
kernel or not, and exits with an error message if you aren't.

- I fixed some holes in getsockopt/setsockopt.

- A new kernel patch fixes the seg-fault problem

- The sources are now released in both SRPM and tar.gz format

New files in ftp://ftp.digital.com/pub/DEC/Linux/Alpha/em86/v0.2:

RPMS/em86-0,2-2.alpha.rpm
SRPMS/em86-0.2-2.src.rpm
SOURCES/em86-0.2.tar.gz
SOURCES/patch-em86-0.2-2.gz
kernel-patches/alpha-patches-2.0.30-0.2a.gz

Would somebody please try this with raplayer and tell me if it works
over the net now? That was what the sockopt fixes were supposed to
address, but since I'm behind a firewall I can't test it...
