Paul,

You will note that I did not either confirm nor deny that the rumor is true.
Quite frankly, I do not know.

However negotiations in the suit are still underway and any conjecture
brought about by unconfirmed news stories are bound to be incomplete.

Right now the Alpha chip is still the world's fastest single-chip computer,
and for the foreseeable future Digital believes it will be unless one of our
competitors makes a step-function jump. Despite (differing) news accounts
of our market penetration (some say 1%, some say 9%) into the RISC CPU
marketplace, it is the core of our system business. Last year we sold $1.7
Billion in Digital Unix Alpha system sales, which was more than our OpenVMS and
WNT business combined.

I would rather wait on this one to hear what the ink says on the contract
and not on the newspaper.
