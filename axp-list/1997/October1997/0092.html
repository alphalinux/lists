On Mon, 29 Sep 1997, Gert van der Knokke wrote:

> I compiled it with
>
> make linux-devel
>
> removed the DEBUG option in the makefile for linux-devel and voila,

Ok, you must've removed -m486 too..

> it runs faster. I think it uses tables instead of realtime calc's
> that way. On the P100 it went from 75% to 50% on the AMD K5-PR100 from
> 95 to 55% and on the Alpha from 80 to 30%...

Yeah, well in which way does this indicate that newer mpg123:s are slower?

I compiled mpg123-0.59f with linux-devel and compared it to my
mpg123-0.59k, and here are the results:

ajo@wopr ~/tmp/audio/mpg123-0.59f >time ./mpg123 -s Topgun.mp3 > /dev/null
<...>
[4:11] Decoding of /music/mp3/film/Topgun.mp3 finished.
58.098u 0.638s 0:59.99 97.8% 0+0k 0+0io 113pf+0w
ajo@wopr ~/tmp/audio/mpg123-0.59f >time ./mpg123 -s Topgun.mp3 > /dev/null
<...>
[4:11] Decoding of /music/mp3/film/Topgun.mp3 finished.
60.444u 1.166s 1:04.05 96.1% 0+0k 0+0io 143pf+0w
ajo@wopr ~/tmp/audio/mpg123-0.59f >time mpg123 -s Topgun.mp3 > /dev/null
<...>
[4:11] Decoding of Topgun.mp3 finished.
59.206u 4.788s 1:05.29 97.9% 0+0k 0+0io 166pf+0w
ajo@wopr ~/tmp/audio/mpg123-0.59f >time mpg123 -s Topgun.mp3 > /dev/null
<...>
[4:11] Decoding of Topgun.mp3 finished.
60.549u 6.580s 1:08.07 98.6% 0+0k 0+0io 148pf+0w

./mpg123 is mpg123-0.59f and mpg123 is mpg123-0.59k

I'm not seeing the great speed diferrence here. Maybe you can get
mpg123-0.59f to run slower if you use decode.o and dct64.o but I seriously
doubt that you can make it go any quicker than the newer versions.
Please correct me if I'm wrong.
