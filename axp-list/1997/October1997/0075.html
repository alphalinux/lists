The enclosed patch fixes the unaligned traps in dhcpcd-0.65, and for at
least 2 people made dhcpcd work where it had not worked at all. This patch
may make dhcpcd work with sparclinux (and maybe other arch's too), but I
have no way of testing that. I'd appreciate it if anyone using dhcpcd on
alpha would test this, and if anybody knows where I can send it so it can
get tested on other arch's, please let me know. I have sent this patch to
the author of dhcpcd, but it got there too late for incorporation into the
next version (0.70).

Andrew Kieschnick
