Ken Griffith,

Thank you for your response.

It sounds like you are unable to accept criticism of any sort.

Why is my response "whiney"?

Like I said in my messages to your individuals in your group, "I have no
idea who you are." I am sure you serve a legitimate purpose to those of
you with similar interests. I belong to only one listserver (Not Yours)
and know the value of these things.

However when you enter my domain uninvited (I never joined yours) with 25
messages all of a sudden, and when I awake this morning with 60, it seems
that my annoyance is justified. These are not "infantile musings". It is
you and your group who is "trashing my inbox", not the other way around.

Besides, I sent my "whiney drivel" back to the individuals, not to your
total list. However this message is being sent to all your colleagues so
that they can get a taste of what kind of person you really are.

I did not subscribe to your group. Please advise me when I first showed up
on your list of subscribers.

Because your interests are not mine, why should I bother to read any of the
messages? I did not invite you in.

Furthermore your threat of retribution of 1000 responses is irresponsible.
Now that you have made your threat public, if you carry it out, you could
legitimately be hauled up for conspiracy.

You suggest that I change my e-mail address. Why should I do that? What
have I done wrong to deserve this? What could I have done differently?

Keith, if you are the listmaster, surely you have the power to delete names
from your own list. If so, please remove mine. Then please let me know
privately that you have done so. That would be the mature thing to do.

Your group seems designed to help one another in the computer area (I don't
understand your jargon). The list I belong to does the same thing, except
in 18 months we have yet to flame anyone. Lets trust that you can achieve
the same level of netiquet.

I have no desire to get into a slinging match with anyone. You stay in
your domaine and I'll happily stay in mine.
