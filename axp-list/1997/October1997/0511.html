> Hello,
>
> Are kernel modules supported for Alpha? It seems, at least, that they are
> not fully supported. Certainly, the RedHat 4.2 documentation says that you
> must build a monolithic kernel, but things do not seem quite that bad. Does
> anyone have the story on this?

Yes they are, BUT you must have newer binutils which contain
fixes for assembler, and linker problems which cause stock
RH 4.2 AXP not to be successfull. (And recompile modutils
as well as kernel with those newer tools.)

You may get them from RedHat updates from their ftp server.
