>>> saswato said:
> The main problem at the moment is that the on-board ethernetcard
> (DEC434?) just doesn't seem to perform well.

Is there any chance that you're using the "tulip.c" driver instead of the
"de4x5.c" driver? The latter is preferred for Alphas with DEC TULIP cards,
except for the 21142/21143 based controllers (DE500-BA, or MIATA builtin).

Be aware that an unpatched "tulip.c" driver, even with the latest revisions
from Donald Becker, will cause *massive* amounts of "unaligned access"
events/messages, which will cripple performance.
