I'm trying to decide where to purchase some alphas with Linux
preinstalled. I want to make sure I purchase from a reputable dealer
with lots of Linux experience, good technical support, etc.

To help decide, I'd like to hear about peoples' experiences with their
vendors, expecially very good and very bad experiences.

So please email me with your stories. I'll send one big post back to
the list with everyone's messages. If you don't want to publicly
flame your vendor, then please mention at the top of your message that
you want me to keep the email confidential, and I won't include it
when I post everyone's experiences.
