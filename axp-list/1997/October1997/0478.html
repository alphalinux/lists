To be honest, the board and CEO do not care about the stockholders. They
care about short term profit and their income based on that short term
profit.

Simplistically, there are few ways to create profit. One is to slowly sell
parts of a company off. This is an easy way to generate profit and thus,
increase the wages of the people in charge.

Secondly, you can slowly grow a company. This is a much more difficult
approach. It requires more competent people in charge, and thus less
desirable to do. It has long term pay offs for the people in charge, the
company, and for communities. Unfortunately, the way CEO compensations are
structured, there is not much of a benefit for CEOs to do such things.

Of course, there are very many other ways, such as buying up companies, going for the
BIG ONE, etc., but you get the gist of my rant!!!!
