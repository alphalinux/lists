Leo,

Having lived thru the naming period at Digital as they were bringing Alpha
to market, I can tell you that AXP is not an acronym for anything
particular. It was just part of the name - Alpha AXP. It is no longer
used, but it did generate a lot of speculation among the Digital user
community about its possible meaning. Now, if you are really curious ask
about the meaning of EV as in EV4 or EV5, which is the unofficial numbering
scheme for the Alpha chip (along with the official 21064 and 21164,
respectively). There's an amusing story behind it.
