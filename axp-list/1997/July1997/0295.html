In article <Pine.SOL.3.96.970710183011.22287B-100000@spot6> you write:
>On Thu, 10 Jul 1997, Greg Rumple wrote:
>| I have a Multia 166 and would like to change the console size from 80x25
>| to something else but have find no way to do this.
>|
>Try SVGATextMode 1.5. It sould do the trick.

I don't think it would work on a multia. Those ones have no VGA compatible
cards, and therefore no text mode. The text console is emulated in
graphic mode (but really quick nevertheless). I think it should require
something more advanced than a few video card registers accesses.

There is always the possibility to find a PCI S3 video card and stick it
in the multia; then disable the TGA support in the kernel, and you will
get something much similar to an Intel linux box (although it has a much
better look and hacking value). In particular, SVGATextMode is then likely
to work.
