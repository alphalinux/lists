> Ok, I read throught the PDF manual and it looks like the internal 3.5"
> drive that ships with some UDBs is an IDE. From what I can tell, it
> connects to the motherboard, not the PCI riser card. Can someone
> confirm this?

No, the 3.5" drive is SCSI and connects to the riser card. The 2.5" drive is
also SCSI but connects to a small (non-standard?) connector also on the riser
card. Of the limited number of UDBs that I have seen (4), none had anything
connected to the IDE connector.
