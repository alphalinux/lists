>Could someone please tell me what micropal is meant to do?
>micropal.S is part of the milo source tree,
>and mentions Huffman decompression.
>
>What is it decompressing? the osfpal, the compressed kernel,
>or maybe a compressed part of milo?
>
>thanks,
>
>!!Dean
It's simular to having a compressed executable using pkzip.
Milo is compressed and merged with micropal so when the
program is started the micropal portion decompresses the milo
image and starts it running. This is done to save space in the
boot ROM or where ever you have milo loaded.
