> Another problem I ran into, and it might be related to the above, is

Same here. It got so anoyying that I hat to reboot up to ten
times until it detected the correct irq.
Now I added "ether=5,0x300,0,0,eth0" to my startup-line (where 5 ist the
irq and 0x300 is the io-address in hex).

Since then I never had a problem anymore.
