This is Jennifer Ridder, borrowing my Dad's account again.

This is not that off-topic as you may think.

Mr. Wayne Irvine is running an OS survey. He has included Linux
as the operating system of choice in the survey.

We need to get the word out about not just Linux, but the other
UN*X look-a-likes *BSD, and Hurd.

This is not a call to spam the survey, please repond in a professional
manner, and given well thought out reasons as to why you run an os
other than microsloth virii, and obviously the MacOS.

As you will see below the current survey is slightly one-sided. This
is given since he posted his request to the 'macway mailing list' from
apple.com.

Please pass this one to other mailing lists, and news groups that would
have a valid interest. Please do not spam mailing list, or news groups
which would have no interest in the OS survey.

> This action item is from:
>
> Wayne Irvine, <waynei@ws1.byteserve.com.au>
>
> A couple of my customers are constantly impressed by what I can do with
> my Macintosh. From designing and hosting websites to editing video.
> Unfortunately, whenever the question arises as to which computer to buy
> next it seems no amount of persuasion will convince them to look at the
> Macintosh.
>
> One of my larger customers has challenged me to prove the benefits of the
> Macintosh and I need your help.
>
> I've set up a survey on my website. It asks which operating system you
> prefer and why. I will be promoting it here and to a number of Wintel
> oriented lists and newsgroups. I'm interested in numbers, but I'm really
> interested in real life examples.
>
> <http://www.byteserve.com.au/ossurvey>
>
> From the results I will compile a list strengths of each operating system
> and distribute the results to all participants and my client base.
>
> My hope is that, on the strength of this survey, we will win at least one
> more battle for Macintosh, if not more.
>
> Your help is greatly appreciated.
