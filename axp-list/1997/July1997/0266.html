>>> Lo Sheng said:
>
> Just when I thought I have finished setting up RH4.2 on the Miata (433a)
> machine, I discovered another unaligned traps during boot-up:
>
> kernel: unaligned trap at fffffc00003f6118: fffffc000000a4d9 29 16
> kernel: unaligned trap at fffffc00003f614c: fffffc000000a4d9 29 16
>
> I must apologise for my lack of experience in the area, and would like to
> seek the advice of fellow list members what on earth are these messages.

The "unaligned trap" messages are WARNINGS ONLY! The kernel will fix them
up to get the "right" results.

These two messages, I believe, are caused by certain code in the TULIP
driver. It accesses some fields of a *packed* data structure on boundaries
not supported by the Alpha chips, which can used only 4 or 8 byte boundaries.

Bottom line: ignore them.
