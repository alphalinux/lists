I've just spent a day getting a Millennium card working with RH4.2 on my
XL300. The final "fix" was to upgrade to a later version of the kernel. I
think that the RH4.2 default kernel was 2.0.18 and upgrading to 2.0.30 got
it all working. Well, this is a slight exaggeration, I still can't get a
stable image on my IBM 6091 fixed frequency monitor, but at least x starts...
