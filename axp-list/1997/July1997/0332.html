micropal.S has the task of getting the machine into the right state
(addressing mode) and then moving itself to the right place in memory.
Don't forget that MILO can be booted a variety of ways (WNT ARC, SRM,
Debug Monitor, flash).

It does not decompress anything, but the image that it jumps to is,
in fact, a compressed MILO image. I think that I wrote a good description
of the hop skip and jump in the MILO HOWTO. Or did I?
