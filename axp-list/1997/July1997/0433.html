>OK, a quick question to get things started:
>
>How does linux compare with Dec's OSF? Pros/Cons?

I've used both. Our machine running Linux/Alpha is
much less stable, but the person in charge of the machine
is unwilling to work on the machine to apply the known
fixes. The Linux/Alpha machine seems to make better use
of memory, and also didn't suffer from the $2k-odd surcharge
DEC imposes for Digital Unix.

NFS on the Linux/Alpha machine seems much worse than NFS on
Intel/Alpha machines. I am not sure why, or if it is
connected to the other problems. I have had NFS exported
directories get completely hozed.

The Linux/Alpha machine can run Digital Unix binaries.
