Hello,

I'm quite proud to report that the port of Linux to
the DeskStation Technology Ruffian RPX-162 motherboard
is functional!

On one of our prototypes I was able to do a RedHat install
and run Xwindows on this pyxis chipset based motherboard.

I compiled in the most recent NCR53C8XX driver for the onboard
SYM53C875 UW scsi chip. I'm still hunting for a DC21143 10/100
ethernet driver.

Now I'll be cleaning up the mess I made to the source tree
(poor little irq.c was gutted and left for dead)
and send my diffs to the linux guys.

Special thanks go to Jay++ for giving me tips
when the times seemed bleak.
And I'd like to thank my parents and respective omniscient diety for....

share and enjoy,

!!Dean
