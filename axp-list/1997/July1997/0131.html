At 12:21 PM 7/4/97 -0500, you wrote:
> Does anyone know if these monitors will support 1280x1024?

This is what I have for the specs; I think that a max of 66KHz is not
enough to do 1280x1024, or if it is, at a low (60Hz) refresh rate.
If you enter these specs, XFree86 servers will protect the monitor from
any out-of-range Modelines...

>Section "Monitor"
> Identifier "PCXBV-KA/KB"
> VendorName "DEC"
> ModelName "PCXBV-KA/KB"
> HorizSync 30-66
> VertRefresh 50-130
>EndSection

Good luck.
