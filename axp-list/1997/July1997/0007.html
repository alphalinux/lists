Hi!
I'm currently trying to build a cross gcc from SunOS to Alpha Linux (ELF) in
order to send it to Mark Podlipec (for the proprietary codecs for xanim).
First I had to find out that the current gcc-2.7.2-2 has no Alpha Linux
patches installed (WHY???). So I grabbed the SRPM from RedHat-4.2 and tried
this one. After installing all included patches I could configure GCC for the
desired platforms but the compile failed. In line 1229 of regclass.c some
defined variable named 'U' is obiously not declared. Any ideas?

You may ask why I'm doing this.
The reason is simple: For a project here at our university we need the
proprietary video codecs for cinepac and others in our xanim binary. And
since Mark Podlipec, the author of xanim, is so kind to provide those in
object form when one can supply him with a cross compiler SunOS -> Linux-???
I wanted to do so.
Or has someone already sent him one for Alpha-Linux?
Or is there any way to convert Mark's OSF-1 COFF objects to Linux ELF?

Any help is REALLY appreciated!
