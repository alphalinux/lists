>>> "Bert Kortenbosch" said:
>
> Video Adapter Cirrus CL-GD5424 512Kb Ram (checked with SuperProbe)
>
> - This makes my AlphaStation 500 / 266 run in terminal mode.
> I tried to start the XFree XTga driver from the X directory
> of the RedHat 4.1 CD.
> X -probeonly gives no errors but when I do startx the monitor goes black
> and I have to use the reset button to get the SRM prompt back.

What you have is a TGA2, aka Powerstorm. Its the successor to the old TGA,
and, unfortunately, incompatible. There's a VGA on board (the Cirrus 5424)
for text-mode console support, but it's apparently unusable for X, due to
the limited amount of memory, or some quirk of the circuitry, not to mention
that the Cirrus support in XFree86 hasn't been ported to the Alphas.

> Secondly I was unable to compile the Tulip driver into
> a new kernel, due to missing symbols at link time.

Hmm, haven't heard of this problem; did you use the DE4X5 driver or the
TULIP (ELCP) one? The former supports the older chipsets a little better,
while the latter is necesary for the very latest ones (21142 and 21143).

> At the moment I have given up to try to get this running, because I
> get the feeling that the "resently" released 4.2 might do better.

This *may* solve the TULIP problem, but won't solve the TGA2 problem.
