>
> I got RH 4.1 installed about 2 months ago, many things work fine on it,
> except... gcc.

Without knowing details of your problems one can only conclude that
something in your gcc installation is corrupted. A more remote
possibility is that you have hardware (memory, the most likely, or
SCSI bus?) problems. gcc drives these systems pretty hard, but you
should notice troubles with other programs as well.

After I compiled many 'serious' programs on various alphas (including
many different kernels and custom milos) I can't even guess what
is wrong in your case. It is true that there is a lot of broken
source out there with implicit, incorrect, assumptions about relative
sizes of various basic C types - but this is normally fixable with
a smaller or bigger amount of work.

Did you try to reinstall gcc and/or libraries?
