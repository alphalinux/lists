On Fri, Jul 11, 1997 at 04:55:11PM +0200, HAIMO@wisconsin.cern.ch wrote:

> I too have observed strange time behaviour on a Miata 433a.

The '2017' problem is common on the alpha platform and can be solved by
inserting "ARC=true" into /etc/sysconfig/clock (and rebooting, or issuing
"clock -A -s")

The "running too fast" problem seems to be unique to the Miata. I solved
it, but I don't have any idea how... it just disappeared some day :-/
