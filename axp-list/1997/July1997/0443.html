If you think RH is broken, then stay far away from Craftworks! I waited
and waited for Craftworks to ship a CD, once I received it the install was
fine but it seemed to be mixed with ECOFF and ELF binaries and libraries.
Many of the binaries would dump core, even gdb (so much for finding the
problem). The particular version was supposed to be entirely ELF. It was
really a disaster, they offered to return my money and send me a
complimentary copy of the "fixed" cd. I received my money but never
another CD. They were very nice on the phone but reminded me of the old
days when I used Microshaft viruses, put out a product and have customers
test it.
