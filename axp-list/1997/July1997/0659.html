patches, etc to comp.os.linux.alpha and then cc them to axp-list
instead of mailing it to axp-list only? I for one am no longer
subscribing to axp-list and would much prefer receiving such important
information on comp.os.linux.alpha.

Note: I'm not suggesting to make axp-list go away. I am suggesting
that we treat axp-list and comp.os.linux.alpha equally so the choice
of mailing list versus newsgroup is up to the end-user.
