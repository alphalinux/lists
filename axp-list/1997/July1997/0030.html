On Jul 1, Adam Biskobing wrote:

> I have not had experience with 100mbit with axp, but with x86 linux the
> Intel card works beautifully and the 3com card works alright, I have not
> had any problem with the stability of the driver

but *many* other people have severe problems with 3C900/905 cards and
stability of this drivers (even the lastest greatest alpha-test-drivers).
things wont get better too fast IMHO because 3COM still didn't release
docs for these chips...

all these "success" reports that 3C900/905 works fine are mostly from
very light-loaded networks (mostly only 10mbit) with no attempt to
try/get high throughput or net traffic...

we returned all our 3com cards and now are happily running TULIP based cards
(which have been cheaper too, but more important all docs are available online!)
