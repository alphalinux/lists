> Can anyone into libc explain to me where things like this come from?
> Do I have incompatible headers/libs installed?

Yes, you installed a 2.0.x kernel without the axp-diffs patch.

Three options:

1) Reinstall the kernel-includes rpm and do your kernel builds
somewhere other than /usr/src/linux. This is preferred.

2) Apply the axp-diffs to your 2.0.x kernel.

3) Install a development series (2.1.x) kernel. Unless you are
prepared to deal with the development series this is not
recommended.
