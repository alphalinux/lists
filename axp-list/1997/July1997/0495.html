> steve@icarus.icarus.com said:
> > I tried to get my Linux/alpha machine to support 2 ethernet cards
> > and I ran into some difficulties.

anti@spin.de said:
> I don't have multiple ethercards, but I'm experiencing the same sort
> of trouble with aliased net-devices (eth0:1, eth0:2 ...);

I found the problem. Upgrade your initscripts rpm. The problem is that the
network interfaces are configured out of order. The later versions are
changed so that the loopback device is always configured first, thus
curing most problems of this nature. Worked for me.
