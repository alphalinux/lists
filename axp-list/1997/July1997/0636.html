On 28-Jul-97 Paul N. wrote:
>i have a 83megabyte kcore file in /proc...does anyone know what this file
>is; why i can't chmod it; and why it keeps increasing in size?

Yes, /proc is not a real filesystem, it's a window on the kernel internals
(more or less) :) /proc/kcore is an image of your system's memory. It's not
really taking up space on your disk, btw :)

As to why you can't chmod it, proc can't be modified in that manner.

And finally, I think that it's increasing in size as you go into swap space (?)
