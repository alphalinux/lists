>The PCI bus on PC164 uses 3,3 V
>
>Hope this helps
>
>wolfgang Dreye

>From the Technical Reference Manual -

"While both the 32-bit and the 64-bit slots use the standard 5-V PCI connector
and pinout, +3.3 V is supplied for those boards that require it."

Do not go out and buy 3.3 volt PCI cards (are any available?) because
they will not fit into the connectors. PCI specifies a different connector
layout for 3.3 volt bus cards. 3.3 volts is available for internal use by the
cards but the interface signals must 5 volt compatible.
