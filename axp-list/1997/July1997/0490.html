Hi,

We are trying to get the MD driver (raid0) to work on our Alpha machine.
We want to use MD because we want to enlarge our news spool file-system.

The machine is a dedicated newsserver and it has taken a full news feed
for several months without a single problem. This setup used the 4 GB
disk as spool (see below).

After we added the new disk (the 6 GB) and configured the MD stuff we
got some crashes and funny kernel messages like:

T2 machine check: OS-specific PAL bugcheck
+ 0 0000000000000220 000001a000000110
+ 10 000000010000008a 0000000000000000
+ 20 000006f800000004 0000000000000000
.
.
.

We are currently thinking of:

1. a hardware problem with the new SCSI setup
2. a problem with the MD code on the (64 bit Alpha)

We are investigating nr 1. but we are also *very* interested in
success/failure stories about the combination of Alpha and MD !
