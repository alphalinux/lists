> > so i am here asking if anyone that has a UDB 166 or 233 would care to
> > comment on the performance and suitibility for particular tasks

I have a UDB/Multia 166MHz with 40Mb RAM and 1 gig external scsi hard drive.
I'm using the built in 2BM ram TGA video.

The things makes and incredible base machine. Its not as blazingly fast as
the 500Mhgz Alpha I got to play with at the Atlatna Linux Showcase but its
not bad for a starter machine. Compiles are slow. Especially the finally
linking phase. Its definitely faster than a P90 for everything that I've
done.

Displaying graphics isn't quite fast enough but I use it to render POV
scenes and its very quick for doing 640x480 images even when they do lots
of reflections and such. It does like to have lots of RAM though. RISC
boxes tend to take about a third more disk space and RAM than CISC boxen.

The real bottleneck on the thing seems to be the memory path and the cache.
I hear that a similar motherboard with the same chip with 1MB of cache
outperforms the 233Mhz UDBs overclocked to 266. I think DCG was selling
them in this configuration.
