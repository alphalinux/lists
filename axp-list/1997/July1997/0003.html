>Hi Kay,
>
>we had a testing-maschine with a pc164 and 128MB. It was very unstable, if we
>set memsize to 128MB, BUT then we set memsize (in milo!!) to 127MB and the
>pc164 was stable again... almost as stable as the intel-version ;-).
>We came to this setting, because the kernel-log shows, that some pages or
>pointers seems to be in the nirvana above everything!
>
>So the answer to your question is, that the current kernel supports more than
>64MB!
>
>(Another BUT: I heard from some SMP-linux-users, that a 512MB-SMP-intel-machine
>should run stable, when they set memsize to 508MB. The problems occure just on
>some boards with really heavy load, but perhaps something isn't correct in the
>kernel-page-handling...)
