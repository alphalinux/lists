>>> Don_Munroe@ns.pcp.ca said:
>
> I currently own a multia/UDB 166 with 96mb ram and noticed that there are
> some Alphastation 200's available at onsale. They are asking $1310 for
> these units.
>
> Questions
>
> o Will run linux without problems?

Yes; use the AVANTI or XL MILO and kernel.

> o How much faster is this than my UDB?

Prolly twice as fast for a kernel compile, maybe more. It uses the EV4 CPU,
not the LCA4, so the cache bottlenecks are not present.

> o Is this a good price?

Some say that the Digital UNIX license alone is worth $1200, but only if
you're not intending to use it... :-)

It's not a bad price; the Mach64 is well supported by XFree86, the sound
card is MSS compatible (like the UDB one).

However, I'd heard of some XL-300's being blown out for less than $2K,
which box is at least twice as fast as the AS200. So, look around on the
net before you commit...

Good luck.
