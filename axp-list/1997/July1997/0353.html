Hi!

Build a new kernel. In make config, enable Iomega Zip Drive (somewhere in
the SCSI stuff). Disable printer support.

Add ppa=0x3bc at the end of your LOADOPTIONS in the boot chain.

This works fine for my PC164 running from an IDE drive.

Good luck,

Jo
