I don't know what you paid, but current prices on 4M36 - 60ns - tin lead
SIMMs is $95
If you see a price substantially below that I doubt that you are getting
anything you can trust. If, OTOH, you pay much more, you are getting gyp'ed.

If it's a reliable part number you are looking for, the Kingston part
number for the "right stuff" is :
KTM2x36L-60T (8MB SIMM)
KTM4x36L-60T (16MB SIMM)
KTM8x36L-60T (32MB SIMM)

But these, like most Kingston parts, are pretty expensive. Last quote I got
on Kingston puts the 16MB unit at about $ 129.
You can get good memory cheaper than this, but as Rick said " Buyer beware".
