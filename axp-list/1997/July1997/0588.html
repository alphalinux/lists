Hopefully thi is not a FAQ,

but I am trying to port the new experimental console part of GGI to the
Alpha and have problems with modules/modutils.

I am running a 2.0.29 kernel (which has been pre-installed on my system),
and got the modules-2.1.34 RPM from redhat, as the installed modutils didn't
work right with respect to exporting names.

The installed modutils exported symbols with the leading _, so stacked
modules were impossible (as they tried to resole foo and found only _foo
in the table).

However I still have strange problems: The modules insert fine, and from the
reaction of the screen (well - they are console-drivers ;-) I can tell that
the init-code runs.

However printks do not work. I see a strange message like <scr (0,0,0)> at
insertion time plus a few bytes of garbage, which could originate from the
printk.

Moreover I suspect inter-module-linkage to be wrong, too, as the setup-module
cannot create instances of registered drivers.

Is this a known problem ? Maybe I really have to install 2.1.34 for the
modutils to work right ? Any other hints ?
