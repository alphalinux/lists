The following message is a courtesy copy of an article
that has been posted as well.

You may remember that I was looking for somebody else to maintain the
Linux/Alpha FAQ. I'm happy to announce that Geerten Kuiper has
volunteered to take care of the FAQ for the time being. He already
produced an updated version of the FAQ (v1.04) and from what I hear,
he's planing on many neat improvements for the future. Please welcome
Geerten in his new position! Also, I'm quite sure he would appreciate
any help he could get in maintaining the FAQ. Geerten's new version
of the FAQ can be found at:

http://www.azstarnet.com/~axplinux/FAQ.html

StarNet, my ISP, graciously agreed to continue hosting the Linux/Alpha
web and ftp site for the forseeable future. In the long run, it would
be nice to have a more appropriate domain name (alpha.linux.org or
some such), but as long as that doesn't happen, I think it's good if
we can keep the old place (who knows how many printed pages and
bookmark files point to azstarnet.com/~axplinux...).

--david
