Hi all, a question which it seems must have a simple
answer -- can anyone help?

PC164, originally RH 4.1, now with a few packages from
RH 4.2. I'm trying to get the kaffe appletviewer to
work. I upgraded to kaffe-0.8.3-6 and kaffe-bissawt-
0.8.3-6 from their rpm files at ftp.redhat.com. I
ran ldconfig. I set environment variables as follows:

CLASSPATH .:/usr/share/kaffe/classes.zip:/usr/share/kaffe/biss.zip
KAFFEHOME /usr/share/kaffe
LD_LIBRARY_PATH /usr/lib

that doesn't use the AWT seems to work fine. When I
try to run the appletviewer I get:

at biss/awt/kernel/NativeLib.<clinit>(line unknown, pc 0x1202ed958)
at biss/awt/kernel/Toolkit.<init>(line unknown, pc 0x1202eb09c)
at biss/awt/kernel/Toolkit.<init>(line unknown, pc 0x120253230)
at java/awt/Toolkit.getDefaultToolkit(190)
at sun/applet/AppletCopyright.<init>(33)
at sun/applet/AppletViewer.mainInit(861)
at sun/applet/AppletViewer.main(870)
