First off, thanks to everybody voting regarding comp.os.linux.alpha.
I was a bit worried that there wouldn't be enough votes, but, boy, was
I wrong! (The final vote count was around 680 yes against 9 no.)

Now to the FAQ: as you may have noticed, I haven't had much time
recently to keep up the FAQ. I'm in the middle of graduating and will
be moving away from Tucson sometime during August (and I'll be plenty
busy before and after that). Thus, I think it would in the best
interest of everybody involved if someone else would take over
maintaining the FAQ. I think there is a chance that we can convince
StarNet (my current ISP), to continue providing web space for
Linux/Alpha, if that's desired. So, all that's really needed right
now is a motivated person.

Please let me know if you're interested.
