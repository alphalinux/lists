For everyone's edification, my problems with Milo not understanding the
partition information on /dev/sdb were due to my not having partitioned it
initially with NT arcinst.exe.

If the initial partition is done with the NT tool (then re-arranged with
Linux fdisk) all is well.

Perhaps this bears looking into?
