I am using the Dec 434/5 Combo on 10Mbit with out any trouble on my udp.  
Performance seems better than the 3c509 and is not prone to lockups.
The driver was smart enough to detect that I had switch from coax to
100Mbit port with out any intervention 2 months ago.  I have been using the 
desktop both for my network connection and as an NFS server to Intel Box.
Both are currently running Red Hat v4.1.  Uptime has been 43 days.  This
will probably change as soon as I upgrade to v4.2.
