On Wed, 2 Jul 1997, Britt Bolen wrote:

> What is the possibility of using my UDB with a PC Card modem to handle IP
> masquerading for a pair of PCs and a PowerMac? The How-To I saw needed
> modules, and I'm not sure how stables modules were under 2.0.30 or if
> masquerading worked on the alpha.

You could use a socks proxy host (haven't tried compiling it yet) instead
of masquerading.

Also, if you get the alphabits update of 2.1.42 modutils and fix the
following:

SPECIAL NOTE
============

On an Alpha, if you are using a binutils before 2.7.0.9, define
AXP_BROKEN_GAS in Makeconfig.

alphabits can be found at (among other places)

ftp://ftp.varesearch.com/pub/mirrors/redhat/alphabits

Then also do something with the PCMCIA (there are two patches that may be
needed), the PCMCIA, and kerneld and the rest of the module stuff works.

I haven't tried the fancy IP stuff on the alpha.
