I think that your problem is due to not telling MILO where your / partition
is. Currently, Alpha kernels default to sda2 as being /. If it's not, you
have to specifically tell it by typing: boot sda2:vmlinux.gz root=/dev/sdb2
(if, for instance, you have /boot on sda2 and / on sdb2)
