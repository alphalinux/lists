Terry,

Thanks for working so hard on the Alpha/Linux/POV thing.
With its reputation as an excellent graphics platform, I had
hoped that the Alpha would be the perfect POV tool. I hope
I wasn't mistaken. I don't know whether the problem is with
Alpha or with POVRay, but either way it's the same: nobody
has managed to render the graphic on an Alpha. Or not at
800 x 600, anyway. God bless him, Matthew Economou (from
this list) has put a lot of work into finding the problem,
and others as well, but no luck so far.

I can't duplicate the problem, because I don't have an
Alpha. As things stand, bummer, it doesn't look as if I
_will_ have one. If it chokes on POVRay, it's no good to
me.

Maybe other listmembers will have a go. If it's a POV bug,
I'll happily report it to the developers. OTOH, if it's an
Alpha/Linux thing, that's worth knowing, too. This list
includes the people most interested in finding the problem,
and most competent to find it. For anyone who doesn't know,
the image source is at:

http://homepages.packet.net/schaeff/povmen.html

Once again, Terry, thanks for putting in the time. What
resolution are you using, and on what scanline does POV
choke?
