On Mon, 24 Mar 1997 vaughan@Zeus.itdc.edu wrote:

> I came in to the UDB game kind of late and would like a pointer to
> the 2017 problem/solution. I am running RH4.0 and it seems that the
> boot causes the machine to initialize to 2017. Sorry to ask for a
> solution, but I didn't see it in the FAQ.

I used to have this problem as well, until once, when I had to reinstall
RH4.0 (one of the many times, I might add..<g>), I noticed that I was
doing something very dumb...remember how the installer asks you for your
timezone? well, up at the top there is a selection to have the clock come
through the ARC console...choose that, choose your timezone, and then set
the ARC clock to GMT and your problem should go away.
