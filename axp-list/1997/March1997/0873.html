Steve,

You do not need the kernel patches for the latest kernels. You can compile a
proper axp kernel with an unpatched 2.0.29 source tree. Just edit
the base Makefile to define the alpha architecture (remember to comment out any
SMP defines), and link include/asm-linux to include/asm.

On another note, I notice that you are involved with trying to port mysql to
linux/alpha. I am just starting work on building binutils-2.7.11, gcclib.a,
and glibc-2.0.2 to get a working linuxthreads on alpha. Have you managed to
get anywhere yet on mysql? Just curious, since I am about to go down the same
path.

David
