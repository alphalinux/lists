> Is there any known problems with the Adaptec?

On Linux Kernel list Richard B. Johnson, who is apparently an engineer
sitting up to his ears in electrical problems of a SCSI bus, claims
that transceivers in Adaptec 2940 series are designed improperly and
do not follow SCSI specs. Apparently one can work around by providing
cables with characteristics which will make this OK, but the point is
that supposedly you should not have to go to such lengths.

I have really no way to confirm or refute such claim (beyond the fact that
with higher transfer rates cabling and termination issues are becoming
crucial). On the other hand, on this list a while ago Erik Troan dreamed
loudly about running a dumpster truck over a pile of Adaptec boards. :-)
