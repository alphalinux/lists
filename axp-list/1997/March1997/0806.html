> Given that D-UX and Linux/Alpha are binary compatible, can
> I mix .o files compiled on D-UX boxes and Linux/Alpha boxes and
> expect them to work?

No. Even if you are using ECOFF on Linux as well, there's a
matter of the libc ABI. They don't match. So you have to use
the libc that corresponds to the header files the object was
built with.
