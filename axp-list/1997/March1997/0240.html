> Can anyone tell me which 4MB video cards fit in the UDB? I was looking
> at Stealth 64 and Stealth 3D cards this weekend. These looked too big
> to fit (at least by the picture on the box). If anyone has had any luck
> jamming one of these boards into the UDB I'd like to hear about it.
> Please specify the model number (I seem to remember lots of 2000/3000
> models but am not sure at this point what the differences were).
>

Yes, you do have to worry about size, not much space in the UDB case.
Another thing to worry about is if it will work without an intel CPU.
I ready someplace that a big problem with a lot of these cards on the
alpha is that they contain some intel code in their rom that is used
to initialize the card. Without an intel cpu this code can not be
interpreted.

I would also be very interested in seeing what other video cards would
work.
