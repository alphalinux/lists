All,

Am I the only site running Alpha Linux 2.1.28 whose networking is totally
hosed? My UDB installation runs just fine under either 2.0.27 or 2.1.24,
but with the newest kernel:

- Responds to ping from other boxes on the network.
- Shows loopback and eth0 on ifconfig.
- Hangs when:

running "route"
attempting to telnet in or out.
attempting NFS mount
you name it...

Basically it's numb. No log messages. No screen messages. Everything
else seems fine, but zero network function.

A patch recently posted to this list by Phillip made no difference either
way.
