>>> Bryan Liles said:
> I have a AXPpci33 motherboard and a Matrox Milllenium PCI card. When I
> power the machine up, it takes anywhere from 2 minutes to 10 minutes to
> boot. When it finally does boot, the screen looks like it is out of sync.
> I have an ISA video card that works just fine. I am using SRM console. I
> want a machine that can dual boot netbsd and linux, but netbsd won't boot
> without a PCI video card. Is there a fix for this, or should I just get
> another video card.

Unfortunately, the BIOS code on the Millennium is a bit beyond the abilities
of some of the earlier revs of the DEC BIOS emulator.

See if you can find the latest rev of SRM console for the NONAME on:

gatekeeper.dec.com:pub/DEC/Alpha/firmware/v3.9/decaxppci33

I'm not sure exactly where the docs for doing the update are, however,
but you can probably get the idea hunting around in that directory tree...

Good luck.
