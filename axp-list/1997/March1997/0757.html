bought a 233mHz Multia w/24MB RAM, 500MB tiny HD, and 512k cache. I
connected it to an external scsi enclosure containing a CDROM and a 3GB

$1800 including the RedHat 4.1 CDROM. It went together really easily and
works like a charm.
