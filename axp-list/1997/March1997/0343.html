There is an updated amd package for 4.1 that supposedly fixes the bootup
problem.

As far as sendmail on an network without a namewerver, you may want to
rebuild sendmail without DNS support. Sendmail assumes you have working DNS
on your site. I found the no_DNS feature in a custom cf file with
the precompiled Sendmail does not work well for me. I have a network with a
intermittant dialup to an ISP, and without a local nameserver. My sendmail
clients are compiled without DNS support so they can forward mail to my
local hub just using host table lookups. Otherwise, the mail
just sits in the queue on the individual hosts until the dialup network
(and DNS) is up.
