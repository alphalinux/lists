You are looking for a file called:
decaxppci33_v1_6.exe

You put it on a floppy, rename it to:
fwupdate.exe
Put the floppy in the machine, and from ARC menu choose "update firmware"
Then after a while you wil be at a new prompt.
Type " update arc"
respond with "y"
then "verify arc"
after it says it's completed you reboot the machine.

One warning: You may have to temporarily put in a different video card to
do it. I have found that the updater itself only supports certain video
cards. For example I had a noname with a S3 vision 968 video card in it (
Diamond 3240XL ) and it reset to a blank screen. I had tp temporarily put
in a S3 Trio64+ based card to be able to do this.

If any further help is needed, please send me private email and I will do
what I can.
