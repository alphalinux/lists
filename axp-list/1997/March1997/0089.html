Hi.

We are currently running RH4.1 on a DEC Alpha Mikasa box. The kernel
version is 2.0.28. Our local network consists of the Alpha, an NIS
master (an AIX box), and miscellaneous other machines.

We have the following problems:

(1) Whenever we run ypbind on the Alpha, it jams the ethernet.

(2) When another machine on the net does a file transfer from the
Alpha (be it tftp, ftp, or cp of an NFS-mounted directory), it is
glacially slow (about 5Kb/sec). Transfers in the other direction
and amongst other hosts are OK.

Suggestions?
