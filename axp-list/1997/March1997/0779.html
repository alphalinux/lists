On Mon, 27 Mar 2017, Fade wrote:

> The UDB can send through my gateway (Cisco 1004) fine, but sending to any
> other machine on my net causes (DUP!) packets. The netstat -r looks fine -
> it's in line with the setups on my other redhat boxes... I can't find the
> culprit here. Has anybody had a similar experience? Any ideas where I
> should be looking?

It sounds like you have some machine in promiscuous mode that's
re-forwarding your packets. You don't say exactly how you observe these
duplicate packets, so it's difficult to be much more precise.

Use tcpdump -e, and look carefully to see if any packets turn up with the
wrong link-layer source address.
