>
> This sounds like quite an interesting deal. I've got quite a hardware
> closet at home, so it would turn out to be quite inexpensive. I do have
> a few questions. What CPUs can be used in the ZIF socket? I've heard
> about the upcoming 21164PC chips which sound like great performers for a
> low price. Would they work? Why is Digital dumping these boards? What's
> the downside?

These motherboards use the 21066(A) processors ONLY. That is _2_
generations removed from the 21164. Here's a short chronology:

21066 - 166/233 MHz - 64/64 bit, 2 instructions/cycle
21064A - 275 MHz - 64/64(128?), 2 instructions/cycle... Improved internal
archetecture... FASTER.
21164 - 300Mhz+ 64/128 bit, _4_ instructions/cycle. WOW!
