Actually, shouldn't you be saying " Has anyone asked Adaptec about this?"
Or, alternatively and probably more usefully:
"Has anyone volunteered to look ath the driver and see if they can track it
down and fix this?"
Or even: "Has anyone sent the author of the driver a bug report about this?"
I really can't see what this has to do with Digital.
Remeber, this is _FREE_ software we are talking about here. It's up to _US_
to fix it if there is something wrong. If you want a commercially supported
64 bit operating system, then buy one. If you want to run a free OS then be
prepared to participate, and in the end you will get results. Look at the
bright side: If you _were_ to make that call to Adaptec, what chances do
you think you would have in getting a useful answer? Even on what they call
a "supported" operating system, I have never yet seen them give anyone a
straight answer like, "Really, a bug? We'll fix that right away and call
you back when the fix is ready". Unless maybe you are Microsoft, anyway.
