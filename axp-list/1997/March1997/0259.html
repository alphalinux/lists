Phil,

I have found that networking is hosed with 2.1.28 (and 2.1.29) on the UDB. X
locks up on me within a few seconds. On 2.1.28, incoming tcp (telnet,
rlogin, and ftp) would be delayed for close to a minute. 2.1.26
is the last kernel that has more-or-less worked for me.

Unfortunately, the kernel does not do anything spectacular like "oops", so
there is little to diagnose, beside the observation that certain networking
things do not work properly.

I have not observed load problems with 2.1.26.

David
