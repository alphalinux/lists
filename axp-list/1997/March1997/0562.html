> stage1/crtend.o(.text+0x94): relocation truncated to fit: LITERAL $41

Whee! .got segment overflow. There are two solutions:

(1) Get binutils 2.7.0.9, which includes the multi-got ld patches.

(2) Compile with -fno-exceptions, or edit toplev.c to default them off,
as they don't work anyway. And they triple the size of the .got.

I actually recommend both. ;-)

Oh, one other thing. You'll need to edit config/alpha/elf.h to change
ld-gnu.so.1 to ld.so.1; a change that I forgot to fold back to Kenner.
