To all interested,

I just received the 2M upgrade module for the Diamond Stealth Video 64 VRAM
card (3240) installed in my VX42 UDB. Sadly, I found that the floppy drive
is in the way of the installed module, with about 3-4mm of interference. So
this does not appear to be a viable way to get a 4M video card, unless you
are prepared to give up the floppy drive.

In the event that I am unable to return the memory module, anyone who is
interested in a 2M VRAM module for the above mentioned card, please feel
free to contact me.
