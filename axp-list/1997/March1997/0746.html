Unverified sources claim that Paul Wouters wrote:
>
> Has anyone found out the problems here? Is it just a matter of
> nuking amd? I've heard people blame amd for lots of networking problems,
> but is that relaly the culprit?

I've been running 4.1 on my UDB for almost two weeks now (hectic work
schedule kept me from doing it when I received the CD) and I haven't had
any problems, 'cept for xplaycd and it's brethern seg faulting. I'm not
running amd, but I am using NFS mounted filesystems.

When 4.1 was released and everybody started having tons of problems,
it startled me to say the least. After watching this list for a few days
it became obvious that amd was indeed causing much heartburn. So, when
I tried to upgrade 4.0 to 4.1, I made a note to stay away from amd. Of
course, when the upgrade got to the MAKEDEV package it promptly hosed
the install, so I just wiped the machine and did a clean install. Most
everything has been working great.

I know that this doesn't really help, but I'd suggest trying things
without amd to see if they make a difference.
