Below is a message the I recieved from the NT alpha mailing list.
I thought it may be of interest to this list. I have no expierence with
this company so I cannot agree or disagree, but I do know that there have
been some compatibility problems that have surfaced in the NT mailing
list.
------------------------message from NT list---------------------------
To: ALPHANT@beauty.mke.ra.rockwell.com
Subject: RE: Alpha clones feedback needed

We bought two Alpha Clones from Polywell Computers.
We planned to run both Windows NT and Digital UNIX
on the computers.

We could not run certain software subsets (UNIX Clusters) that run
with Digital UNIX and that we were promised would work.

The applicable information for you folks is that it has been
a complete nightmare dealing with these folks.

We spent three months working with them to try to get the
UNIX Cluster software to work on the systems and couldn't.
When we tried to send the equipment back they said that we couldn't because
it was longer than 30 days after we bought them!

We sent the systems back anyways. Now it is ten months later (from
initial purchase) and Polywell has both the computers and my money!!

Take my advice and stay as far away from Polywell Systems as
you can.
