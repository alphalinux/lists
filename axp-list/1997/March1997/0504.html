  • In reply to: geerten kuiper: "PC164 clock"
>I've got a PC164 running at 366MHz (I think). This evening i had a look at
>the jumper settings. IRQ0-IRQ3 are the ones used to set the speed. The
>Users Manual says:

>IRQ0 IRQ1 IRQ2 IRQ3
>out in in out 300
>in out in out 333
>out out in out 366
>in in out out 400
>out in out out 433
>All other combinations reserved

That table looks right, and it even makes sense. Replace "in" with 0
and "out" with 1, flip the table from left to right, and you have
binary values for (clock speed)/33.33 in each row.

>I expected to find IRQ2 in, but it's really IRQ1. Have I secretely been
>running at 433MHz all the time ? (ARC -> Display Hardware Configuration
>says 366MHz) Or is there a mixup in the Manual ?

Maybe you or they got the jumpers in reverse order ;)
