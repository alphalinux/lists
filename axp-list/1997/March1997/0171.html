> At the risk of sounding silly, why is there no difference (other than a
> silly little patch to the filter part of lpd.c) with lpr-0.13?
> In fact, when one extracts the src.rpm, one gets lpr-0.13.tar.gz !
> Not only that, but it doesn't build properly (and I am not only saying
> it is not a clean build - it actually bombs).

I was also surprised to find a lpr-0.13.tar.gz with the filter-patch in the src.rpm, but
on my system (EB164, RH4.0, 2.0.29) it was possible to compile everything (with a lot of
warnings, too), and the new lpd does not show the remote printing errors any more.
I guess that it would also work with the lpr-0.13.tar.gz without filter-patch (but I didn't
try) and that David Mosberger-Tang just used the newest version for his patch.
The only difference between our systems seems to be the kernel version, so perhaps you should
try the 2.0.29. If you want, I can also send you the lpd binary.
