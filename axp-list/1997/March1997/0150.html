David Mosberger-Tang wrote:

> If you're interested in trading Alpha hw for a better
> gcc, you might want to take a look at:
>
> http://www.goof.com/pcg/
>
> Note: I do not know much about this project, but since
> they're explicitly asking for an Alpha machine to improve
> gcc support for Alpha, it might be worth looking into.

I hope I do not sound like belittling the effort these guys are
into, but - just reading the first 150 K of ChangeLog changes in
their 2.7 Mbyte diff - I get the impression that they simply use an
up to date snapshot of the gcc backend. Of course that will bring
you enhanced performance, at the expense of being at the cutting
edge of technology (because it is unreleased material).

I have my own thoughts on the release schedule employed by the FSF,
but it seems unwise to me to step into this *unless you're very
well aware of the risks*.

Hope this helps,
Toon.
