>
> So anyway, I'm running it at 1280x1024 in 16 bit mode at 76Hz just fine.
> When you do startx though, it defaults to 8 bit mode, which sucks. So I
> have to do startx -- -bpp 16 when I start it.. but big deal.

'startx' is just a shell script and there is nothing sacred about
it. You may rewrite it to do something else. Or you may put something
like that in your .profile (assuming you run bash):

startx () {
( /usr/X11/bin/startx -- -bpp 16 ; sleep 2 ) &
}

or whatever else you fancy, and you will default to 16 bit mode.

There is also few other ways to do that.
