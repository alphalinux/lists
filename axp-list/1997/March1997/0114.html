Well, I cannot answer the first very well, it is my understanding that a
native port of JDK 1.0.1 was made, but the current JDK is not ported as
of yet.

As to NT, dual booting on an UDB is a piece of cake. These boxes are
designed to run NT, and it is very easy to setup the ARC menu to permit
booting between NT and Linux.
