Or from milo:

MILO> boot sda1:vmlinux.gz root=/dev/sda1 init=/bin/sh

Of course, replace sda1 with whatever your particular HD is. Realize
that you will need to remount any of your filesystems rw as this
will bring them up in ro mode.

mount -n /dev/sda1 / -o remount,rw
