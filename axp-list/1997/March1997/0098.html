Hi,

I've got a PC164 running at 366MHz (I think). This evening i had a look at
the jumper settings. IRQ0-IRQ3 are the ones used to set the speed. The
Users Manual says:

IRQ0 IRQ1 IRQ2 IRQ3
out in in out 300
in out in out 333
out out in out 366
in in out out 400
out in out out 433
All other combinations reserved

I expected to find IRQ2 in, but it's really IRQ1. Have I secretely been
running at 433MHz all the time ? (ARC -> Display Hardware Configuration
says 366MHz) Or is there a mixup in the Manual ?
If the Manual is wrong, what is the correct setting for 400MHz ?
And finally: is it safe/recommended/etc. to overclock from 366 to 400 ? Has
anybody tried this ? 433 even ?
How difficult and how costly is it to replace the CPU ?
