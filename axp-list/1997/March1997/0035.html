On Mon, 3 Mar 1997, Edwin Koshimoto wrote:

>
> I would like to purchase an Alpha to run Linux.
> I'm looking at SWT and Aspen Systems at the moment.
> Would anyone care to comment with personal
> opinions about either company? I would also
> appreciate it if anyone has other OEMs they can
> recommend.
>

We've had the best luck with DCG Inc. (www.dcginc.com) and SW Technologies
(www.swtech.com). So far, we've been less than satisfied with Aspen in
several areas, tech support being the biggest problem. We've had one
EB164 from Aspen that hasn't run for more than 10 days at a shot, with the
average being about 7 hrs. After several attempts to fix the problem,
both from here and through Aspen, they are now saying that it is a bad
power-supply.

Steve Gaudet at DCG has always been helpful and prompt in responding to
requests.
