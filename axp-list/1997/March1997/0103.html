Is it related to heavy PCI bus activity such as disk I/O? Depending
on your configuration, starting X can generate a lot of disk I/O to
startup all the clients. If it's related to heavy i/o activity, it's
definitively a good idea to try a MILO with the latency fixes
(actually, just make sure that /proc/pci reports Latency values that
are > 30 or so for master-capable controllers (if you boot Linux
through ARC and then MILO, I'd think that the latency settings should
still be OK).
