bought it as a bit of an impulse buy and now I might be regretting it.

buy some sort of cases to turn these into external devices or if I've
wasted a lot of money.

My best guess is to turn my other PC into an NFS server and make the UDB
a diskless workstation, but I'm not sure how that would even be done.
(Instructions are minimal.)

If anyone has immediate ideas on making the HD or CD-ROM external
devices or how to easily devise a diskless workstation approach, I'd
really appreciate it.
