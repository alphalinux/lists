Hello Eliot

>I just upgraded my EB164 machine to 256M of DRAM and started
>to get kernel messages "memory violation" and also "unable
>to handle kernel paging request" from arch/alpha/mm/fault.c
>I'm using the 2.0.29 kernel.
>
>I suspected bad SIMMs. So I ran the memtest command from the
>EB164 debug monitor... No problems were found! So, the bug
>must be in the kernel...
>
>Next I tried to repeat the problems by lying to linux about
>how much memory was installed (via "setenv MEMORY_SIZE 64" in
>MILO) and thereby running with 64M of memory. The problems
>went away. The problems seem to remain when running with 128M
>of memory.
>
>Anyone had similar experiences with > 64M of memory?
>Anyone have any ideas about a fix?

I have two, new U12 upgrade to 3EEF, with new 4.49-7 firmware. Second, don't
use anything above 2.0.27, just not stable.
