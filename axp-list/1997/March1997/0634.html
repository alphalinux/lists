On Fri, 21 Mar 1997, Alexey Zilber wrote:
> Speaking of IDE.. Does anyone know of any external IDE enclosures? If
> that's the case with the UDB, I'd stick the 5gig IDE drive on it (if it were
> externalized).

I don't think you could. Not safely, anyway. IDE has a really small cable
length limit, something like 20 inches or some such nonsense. That usually
isn't a problem, since IDE devices are always internal and you never have more
then two devices on the chain. Or maybe that's a cause rather then an effect.
:-) I suppose you could run the IDE ribbon cable out the front and into a
drive enclosure, if you put the enclosure right up against the system unit,
but that's ugly at best.
