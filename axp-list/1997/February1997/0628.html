> I am running RH4.1 Linux on a DEC Alpha. This has the new glibc, so
> it should support NIS. I would like to get NIS running.
>
> I didn't find any mention of nistools-1.0 or ypbind-3.0. Where did
> you find them?
>
> According to the RH manual, you don't need ypbind running, and that
> the info in the /etc/yp.conf file should suffice. Is this really
> true?

OK, I got the nistools and ypbind from
http://www-vt.uni-paderborn.de/~kukuk/linux/linux.html

You must apply the ypbind patch in the nistools distribution
to the ypbind directory. Then just compile "ypbind", the rest
of the tools in that directory won't work (at least on my
system.)

I was not able to get the glibc internal NSS stuff to work,
(basically, how do you turn it on?) but ypbind has been working
OK.

Good luck,
