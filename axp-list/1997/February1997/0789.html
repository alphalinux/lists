I have a couple of old HP monitors & would like to use them with
my Multia (Universal Desktop Box, whatever). One is an HP 98782A
(marked Sony GDM 1902 on the back) with 5 BNC connectors (RGB,
Horiz, Vert). The other is an HP 98752A (made by Sony but no
Sony part number I can find) with 3 BNCs (RGB).

The 5-BNC monitor can most likely be used outright, with a cable
that brings out R,G,B,H and V signals to separate BNCs (I made one
from a cable ripped out from a defunct VGA monitor).

The 3-BNC monitor can be used via an adapter; one source is Griffin
(http://www.nashville.net/griffin).
