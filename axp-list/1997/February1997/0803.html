Hubs are incredibly cheap these days. I picked up an 8-port hub for $99, with
the cables (for a 5-host network) the total came to $125. The nice thing
about TP is adding hosts on the fly (like a laptop), and just plain solid
reliability. Thinnet is cheap initially (no hub), but cables cost more
than pre-terminated lengths of Cat5 TP, and is far less reliable. Recommended.
