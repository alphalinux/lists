I recently compiled the 2.0.27 kernel on my system (AlphaStation 200
4/233), and it all seems to work perfectly, except that my PPP account
no longer seems to work. The PPP and serial modules are being loaded
correctly, but when I ping something, it tells me the network is
unreachable, i.e. my nameservers don't work, etc, and I can't do
anything. Has anyone else had this problem? I have tried this both
with and without the ipv6 module, which I thought might have caused some
sort of incompatibility problem between my ISP and my box, but still the
same result. Is this just a bug in the kernel, or am I doing something
wrong?
