Yawlp... I _just_ figgered this one out, too. seems ye gotta hack the
Makefile in the sound directory. look for the "ld" line and add the "-dc"
like it asks ye to. Here's my line:

sound.o: local.h $(FIXEDOBJS) sound.a
-rm -f sound.o
$(LD) -dc -r -o sound.o $(FIXEDOBJS) sound.a

Hope that works! Now if I can only get rid of that whine on my UDB, I'd
