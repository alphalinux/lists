modules-2.0.0 never supported the Alpha. You can try modutils-2.1.23.

To build it under a 2.0.x kernel you need to grab include/asm-alpha/unistd.h
from the 2.1.23 kernel, as it needs to know a new syscall number even when
it won't use it under 2.0.x.
