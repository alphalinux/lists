If you want permanently connected Internet connection, go get
a leased line. Your telephone network is not built for it.

The telephone system is designed with a certain degree of over-
committing trunc capacity in comparison of interfaces. The factors
are fairly large on assuption that most of the calls are within
the neighbourghood (the same switch) anyway, and/or of rather
short duration. Severe dose of "Internet calls" makes those
commitment factors obsolete, and building new capacity to handle
these new long duration calls needs money, thus I see it just
right to put the bill on those that cause it.
