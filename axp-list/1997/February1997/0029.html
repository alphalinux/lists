Hello all,

I see on this mailing list a great deal of questions that are
continuously resent, although they have received an accurate answer
(and even a digest). IMHO, this is due to the very high volume in the
list : I fear the search engine at RedHat is not efficient enough. On
the other hand, I understand from my experience with the AXP port FAQ
that maintaining a comprehensive FAQ of the mailing list would be a
really huge amount of work, and not psychologically rewarding at all
since the once critical questions quickly fade out due to software
development.

So I suggest that people emitting a digest such as myself, Gerald
Anderson (with the Idiot's guide to Netscape), Steven Hirsch (with his
not-so-quite HOWTO for glibc upgrading) and others post their summary
or announcement once a month as long as it remains relevant, with a
subject line beginning with "[FAQ]", to ease the search at
www.redhat.com. What about this ?

Cheers,
