Fixes are now available for shadow passwords and xdm on Red Hat/Alpha 4.1
from ftp.redhat.com:/updates/4.1.

Upgrade to the new XFree86 version for a working xdm and the new glibc
for shadow support. Running 'pwconv5' after installing the new glibc should
yield a working shadow implementation.

These items will appear on the Errata shortly.
