This sounds very much like an updated-for-the-Internet version of the
old urban legend about the FCC planning to charge per-minute fees for
use of modems.

I would be very dubious of the story; it sounds virtually identical
to the old "modem tax" proposal that kept getting "spammed" to BBS
conferences. To the point of involving an "FCC tax." The *sole*
difference is that it mentions "the Internet" rather than "modems."

There is, of course, the question of what formula they'd apply to
intranets; they may, or may not, connect to "the Internet," based on
time of day or peoples' notions.
