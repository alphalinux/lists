Przemek Klosowski <przemek@rrdjazz.nist.gov> wrote:
> PLEASE!! This phone tax thing raised its head once again. It is a hoax
> dating back to the eighties---wasn't even true then.

The FCC really did have such a thing as a "modem tax" proposal back then,
but it was quickly killed. It did spawn an urban legend though. (Who says
urban legends can't have some basis in fact?)

But unfortunately this lastest scare is real. If you don't believe it,
check out the Internet Access & Information Service Provider NOI, dated
December 23, 1996:
http://www.fcc.gov/isp.html

While the Red Hat AXP list is *not* an appropriate forum for this
discussion, you shouldn't go telling people to ignore it and that it is
a hoax. But please make sure that you include a date in any message
your circulate, so that this *doesn't* turn into a perpetual modem tax
urban legend.

Let's please end the disussion here, or move it somewhere else.
comp.dcom.telecom (moderated) or alt.dcom.telecom would seem appropriate.
