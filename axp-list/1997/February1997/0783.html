On 25-Feb-97 Adam Biskobing wrote:
> Well, I have finally gotten my UDB up and running and am alarmed
>by the amount of ECC memory errors that occur(even though it says they
>are corrected). Last time I booted I counted over 20 memory errors. Is
>this normal??

Perhaps you have a bad SIMM.

>Another thing I have noticed is that after the machine
>starts to get hot it will stop booting right after fsck with the message
>eth0: media is EXT SIA. Since it happens after heating up it makes me
>want to believe it is hardware anyone had this problem??

The ethernet adapter is not sensing a connection. Are you
connected to a network? I had a similar problem connected via BNC, it seems
that the center socket in the BNC connector on the UDB may have been expanded
and did not make proper contact with the pin in the BNC tee. I could twist the
tee and it would make contact and all would be fine, but just not very
reliable. When I rewired my network to TP, I had no further problems.
