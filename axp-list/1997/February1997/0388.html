For those of you with a spare 486 about and a couple of network cards,
Applixware works great via remote X. I have it installed on a
"headless" 486DX4-100 that is a server for Applixware, Netscape, and other
Intel/Linux apps to my VX-42 UDB, using NFS mounted home directories. It
is an effective and not-too-expensive workaround for the lack of Alpha/Linux
applications.

David
