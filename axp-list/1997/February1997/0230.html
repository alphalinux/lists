ZACK@MPS.OHIO-STATE.EDU wrote:
> Sorry I have to post this again but I desperately need help on
> this. I have a UDB 266MHz and NT3.5 on it. I use a NEC Multisync 3FGe.
> I tried EVERY POSSIBLE COMBO of the W3-6 jumpers but the
> only readable thing I get on the screen is the ARC phase during
> booting. Then NT loads and I get a fuzzy colourful snowy screen.

You're not going to get much help with NT on a Linux list. But in general
what you need to do is borrow a monitor that can handle the higher scan rate,
log in to NT, and run the Control Panel or Setup or something to tell NT
about your monitor.

The SRM console is the only thing that pays any attention to W3-W6. ARC,
NT, the Linux kernel, and XFree86 all totally ignore them.
