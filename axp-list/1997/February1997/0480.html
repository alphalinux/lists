I bought a UDB with no memory or HD from Starship about 2 months ago.

absorb all the RedHat pearls I could, and reading bazillions of
messages about compile problems, hardware glitches, incompatibilities

plunge.

Therefore, I have a UDB (from starship) 166 MHz no memory, no HD. I
also have 64MB of parity RAM (not installed yet) and some other parts
