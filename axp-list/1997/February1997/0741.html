> What was the final result of the various suggestions as to how to fix
> the RH4.1 amd hangup on boot? (Or is there more to it than amd?)

I found and fixed the bug in amd. I posted the fix in this mailing list
and sent the fix directly to RedHat. The problem is the glibc htonl()
function sign-extends the result, causing amd (which assumes that the
value is unsigned) to go haywire in its setup phase.

If the fix is not forthcomming from RedHat or the mailing list searches,
I can post the fix again.
