>
> The RH User's Guide says that the following command will produce ascii:
>
> man at | groff -mandoc -Tascii

This is a bit baroque. 'man at | cat' (or 'man at > file' if you are
saving results) will have an identical effect. As results of 'man at'
do not have any troff commands then 'groff -mandoc -Tascii' (same as
'nroff -mandoc', BTW) above is a pretty expensive equivalent of 'cat'.

>
> However, I get the following:
>
> N^HNA^HAM^HME^HE

Pipe it through 'col -b'; 'man col' for details.
'less' actually will interpret the above in a more readable manner. :-)
