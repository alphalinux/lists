At 08:27 AM 02-05-97 -0500, you wrote:
>> I have had experiences with these DE500's switching back to 100T on a
>> similar machine running Digital UNIX as well. These cards seem to want to
>> revert back to 100T. Never did try to fix it, just put in a DE450 instead...
>
>I found the other day (after 2 hours of tracking it down :-<) that there
>is an environment variable in the SRM console that controls this. I would
>tell you what it is, but I would have to reboot my computer. If you are
>using SRM, do a printenv and look at what is set. Then set it to
>Twisted-Pair and things will be happier.
Yes, Jim. I ran into this too. Something like "set ewa0 twisted" as I recall
offhand. This puzzles me, though, as it seemed to still NOT work on these
boxes ( Cabriolet style Alpine baords and EB164's). No matter what I set, it
still reset itself to 100T. As I said, simplest solution was to change to
DE450 for 10T needs.
Interestingly enough If I use the DE500 on an EB164 with NT4 it seems to be
possible to do the settings and they work.
