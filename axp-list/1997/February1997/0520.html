On Fri, 14 Feb 1997, Chris RIley wrote:

> I'm tring to install Red Hat 4.0 on a Alpha that has an external NEC-511
> (4x SCSI) cd-rom attached. During the boot process the CD is identified
> by the system , and during the installation once I select local scsi
> cd-rom it ask me for the type. The NEC is not in the list box. but
> there is 2 EATA choices (I read on another page that EATA was the
> correct choice for NEC). WHen I choose either EATA devices I get an
> error screen that says:
> /bin/insmod not found..

You shouldn't have that happen on an Alpha at all -- it means your your
SCSI driver wasn't detected during bootup, which is bad. What kind of
SCSI card do you have? If the answer is Adaptec 2940, try one of the
boot images from the Red Hat 4.1 distribution (it'll work fine with 4.0).
