On Mon, 17 Feb 1997, Chris Adams wrote:

> Will that include support for long passwords? One of the systems I run
> has long, shadowed passwords, and it is rather a pain to upgrade
> anything on the system. I thought that I had read that RedHat 4.1 (at
> least on Intel) support shadow passwords and long passwords, and I was
> looking to upgrade and get with a more "standard" system.

It will support md5 style long passwords but probably not the old
shadow style long passwords (which are less secure then normal
short passwords anyway). The Intel and SPARC versions both support
md5 passwords already.
