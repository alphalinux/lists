Jeff Odom <zeus@jhu.edu> wrote:
> It was compiled w/o rsaref2 - are there still export restrictions on the
> non-rsaref2 version?

There are export restrictions on anything with a key size larger than
40 bits. It doesn't matter what crypto library you use.

It is now much easier to get export licenses for software with key sizes
up to 56 bits, but you do still need to get the licenses.
