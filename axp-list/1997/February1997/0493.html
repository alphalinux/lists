> Hello,
>
> Upon upgrading to 4.1, my UDB started hanging at startup. The
> problem turned out to be amd. Starting it by hand seems to do the
> same thing. I'm not entirely sure what it is waiting for, but
> unless I force
> it into the background w/ "&", I end up waiting forever. Also, I was
> attempting to poll its status w/ amq, but amq isn't registered in the RPC
> database... how can I correct this? (I put the registration # that amq's
> man page gave into the /etc/rpc file, but I don't know what else needs done).
> Amd worked fine before my upgrade.

Install the amd source RPM and make sure you apply the patches
(including
amd-nonis.patch and amd-upl102-glibc.patch). If you need NIS go ahead
and apply
the amd-nonis.patch and than edit amd-upl102/config/Makefile.linux to
and around line 52 uncomment the -DHAS_NIS_MAPS -DHAS_NIS_RELOAD flags.

Than edit config/Makefile.local.linux and remove the
-DNEW_TOPLVL_READDIR
from it.

Everything should now compile and work.
