> After failing in my attempt to compile xemacs from source i gave the
> dec-osf binary version a try.. It looks like i works quite
> nice.. But..
>
> When starting xemacs complains abaout no /etc/svc.conf file. This, I
> belive, is the reson for xemacs to fail when looking up hostnames to..
>
> Now, is the format of /etc/svc.conf copyrighted and a well kept secret
> of Digital, or could there be a sample lying araound somewhere?
>

The svc.conf file is plastered with a Digital copyright notice, so I will
not post it here raw. However, it's really quite simple and serves the
same function as nsswitch.conf (glibc).

Just list database and service in pairs:

database=service,service, etc..

e.g.:

hosts=local,bind,yp

Your next problem will be the fact that xemacs undoubtably expects the
Digital libraries and dynamic loader to be available as well. This I
cannot help with, but for $1500 or so DEC will be glad to sell you a
license!
