In message dated: Tue, 11 Feb 1997 14:14:58 EST, you wrote:
>
>Hi folks, I've just installed Redhat 4.1 on my PC164 system,
>and I find that, while it has NIS support in glibc (supposedly),
>it does not have RPMS for the YP-client daemons, etc.
>
>Has anyone successfully got NIS working on an alpha system
>yet? If so, how?

Well, you should configure /etc/nsswitch.conf and /etc/yp.conf in the
right way. You don't need to run ypbind with glibc.

In /etc/nsswitch.conf put:

passwd files nis
group files nis

....

And in /etc/yp.conf:

domain yournisdomain
ypserver yourypserver
