Okay, managed to figure out upgrading to RH4.1 on my UDB - but it
didn't solve the problem we were having with RH4.0.

Symptom: If I telnet in to the box from elsewhere on the network and
beat on the keyboard into a catted output (cat > blahfoo), eventually
the connection locks up, but only for that connection - other
connections can come in immediately after it locked up in one telnet.

It happens around the 250th character that I enter, within about 10
characters on either side. (checks something else) It also happens
with regular typing - I can type a longish sentence in repeatedly and
it'll lock in the same point.

Before it became my problem, the previous handler of this box had
noticed in one of the logs that it was switching between the TP
connection, the BNC connection, and the other ethernet connection,
trying to find the network again. Of course, now that it's my
problem, I can't find what he's talking about. (smirk)

This is a limited case - it doesn't seem to have a problem accepting
data from a lynx connection, and you can receive tremendous files
through FTP without a glitch, but we're somewhat worried about using
this box as a production unit if the network connection can be made to
die quite that easily.

Am I pushing the network driver? Overflowing some form of output
buffer? Or are we looking at faulty hardware, and is it replaceable?
