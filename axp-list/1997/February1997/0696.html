We have had four crashes in the last two weeks. Three of them have
resulted in entries in /var/log/messages (one of these was lost due to
misconfiguration).

The setup: Cabriolet (300MHz 21064A), 256M RAM, two DE500 ethernet
cards (one at 10Mbit/s, one at 100Mbit/s), Mach 64 video card. The
machine has worked pretty reliably under Digital Unix for more than a
year.

Linux-2.0.28 with axpdiffs-2.0.21, with NCR 8xx and DE4x5 drivers.

Two of the crashes were apparently caused by Sicstus Prolog test runs,
but could not be repeated with a later, recompiled binary. One was
apparently caused by trying to log in from 25 X-Terminals in short
succession (the crash happened about a minute after the last login). One
crash had no identifyable cause.
