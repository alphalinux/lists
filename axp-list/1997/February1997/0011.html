The machine is probably booting, but leaving you at
the SRM console. You should have a prompt looking like this:

>>>

If this is what you see, enter "arc" at the prompt, this will
switch you back to the ARC console.

If you cannot see a prompt from a fresh boot (probably due to display
resolution problems), then after turning on the system, walk away for a few
minutes as firmware performs its diagnostics, then type "arc" and return.
