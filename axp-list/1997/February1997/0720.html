> Could I get you to go ahead and send me your fixes. If not, I guess the
> primary question I have is how the heck do you tell ld to look at a specific
> lib for a specific app as opposed to looking at just the latest??

LD_LIBRARY_PATH is an environment variable in the same format as
path that tells non-setuid programs where to look for shared
libraries. What I do is have a copy of glibc-1.98 in the quake
directory and run

LD_LIBRARY_PATH=. ./xquake
