You will need to copy /bin/ls to the ~/ftp/bin directory and set the
proper permissions (111) and ownership.

cp /bin/ls ~/ftp/bin
chmod 0111 ~/ftp/bin/ls
chown root:wheel ~/ftp/bin/ls
