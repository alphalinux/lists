Paul Wouters <paul@xtdnet.nl> wrote:
> Could anyone tell me what the ide looking connector is on the main
> board just after the 2 1/2 rack where the floppy and hd is mounted?

It's an IDE port as you surmised.

> I'd like to hook up a 2 1/2 IDE drive i managed to get a hold
> on, and remove the floppy drive.

If you mean to do this in order to have a second internal hard drive,
I'd strongly recommend against. The power supply was not designed
to handle multiple drives, and there is not sufficient airflow for
multiple drives.

If you don't already have an internal drive, or if you plan to replace a
2.5" SCSI drive with a higher-capacity 2.5" IDE drive, you won't have to
remove the floppy drive.
