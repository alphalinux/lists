If you haven't already done so, look for the ARC/AlphaBIOS for the AS255
at www.windowsnt.digitial.com, and the SRM at:

www.digital.com/pub/DEC/Alpha/firmware (or some such).

You should be able to go back and forth, but the FLASH on the AS255 is
(usually) only big enough to contain one at a time. Choose which is more
comfortable... :-)
