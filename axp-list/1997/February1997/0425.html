I have 4.1 installed, and for the most part it's been working quite
well. I've had a few weird memory problems similar to what you
described (unable .. paging request), and I think they were heat
related. The errors happened most when I tried to work on it on a
weekend. My office tends to get hot on the weekends because the air is
turned off. It sounds like you have an internal drive. If so,
unfortunately, that's probably your problem. The UDB seems to overheat
very easily. I would at least try pulling the disk out as a test.

- Brian
