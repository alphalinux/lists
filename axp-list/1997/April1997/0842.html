I forgot to add some ftp statistics for comparison:

ftp tests over FDDI:
local: Linux
remote: DEC Unix

ftp> get 100Mb /dev/null 5.9Mb/sec 1st time
8.2Mb/sec subsequently
due to file-caching at remote

ftp> get 100Mb 100Mb 1.7Mb/sec repeatable
limited by local disk write-rate probably

ftp> put 100Mb /dev/null 2.3Mb/sec 1st time
9.4Mb/sec subsequently
due to file caching at local
