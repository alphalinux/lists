I bought two AXPPCI33 (Noname) boards with no cache on them (the famous
$129 special) and I seem to be having a lot of problems with them.

Right off the bat they did not want to work with my ATI Mach 64 VT cards.
This was remedied by temporarily replacing the video cards with different
ones and upgrading the firmware.

Now that I'm actually to the point where I can install Linux, I get random
machine checks claiming that ECC had to be used to fix an error. Both of
the boards I have don't seem to like either pair of SIMMs I try using in
them. I have one pair of 16 MB SIMMs and one pair of 32 MB SIMMs.

The obvious answer to my problem is that I have bad SIMMs, but I'm not
sure this is the case. I also have a 233 MHz UDB that I used the pair of
32 MB SIMMs in and I never got a single machine check with it.

Any idea what's going on here and how to remedy the situation?
