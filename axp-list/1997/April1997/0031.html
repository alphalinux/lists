Before we go and hang these folks let's think this through for a second, OK?

This is a company (Parasoft) that heard that someone had tried their FREE
downloadable evaluation software, and upon discovering that the user had
some problems spent the time, effort and costs to contact the individual
and try and help them in getting better results. The person who posted
about this stated that there were some improvements, although not enough to
satisfy him.

While it might have been bad timing and perhaps they didn't use perfect
etiquette, I can only conclude that they certainly are going the extra
distance in trying to satisfy their (potential) customers.

I can only wish that a few other companies would be this concerned with the
needs of users and devote this kind of effort in trying to help...
