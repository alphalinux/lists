>>> Franz Figl said:
>
> is there anybody out there who can run XFree with Matrox Millenium and a
> Philips 21A monitor on Alpha XL 266?
> I tried with Kernel 2.0.27 and 2.1.26. The modelines are from the handbook.
> I get a hung of the machine with vertical stripes. There are no error
> messages from XFree.

What version of the SVGA server are you using?

1. XF86_SVGA from Red Hat 4.1 is based on the 3.2 XFree86 release version,
and is quite stable but not super-quick.

2. XF86_SVGA from the XFree86 3.2A beta binaries is *very* quick but may be
somewhat fragile. Try adding:

Option "noaccel"

to the Device Section, and see if it works better. It'll be slower but it
may be more stable.
