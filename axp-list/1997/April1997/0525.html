Currently I'm running the stock RH4.1 2.0.27 kernel and the modutils, etc.
that came with it (and the updates). I downloaded the 2.8.23 PCMCIA source
from hyper.stanford.edu. It did not make at first, failing on the Xircom and
one other module. It looked like duplicate includes/headers but since I
didn't need either of the two drivers, I edited them out of the makefile.
After I did the make, I did the install and went to the
/lib/modules/2.0.27/pcmcia and did the strip -x *.o trick. I then edited the
/etc/sysconfig/pcmcia and make PCMCIA=YES and PCIC=i82385 (although that
might automagically get done with one of the make options). Also, the pcmcia
makefile points to the wrong place for the xforms libraries for RedHat if
you want to use the cardinfo utility under X. Beyond that, it works OK (once
you know how to fix all the boo-boos...:)

FYI, I'm using a modem card in my UDB.
