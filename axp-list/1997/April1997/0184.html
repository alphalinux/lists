Yes, I think this is it primarily: in my posting to comp.emacs.xemacs
I already wrote which additional patches were necessary. I add the
diff's against XEmacs-19.15 at the end of this message.

Just to be sure that I don't forget anything I will enumerate the
additional requirements here:
o steal unexelf1.c from FSF Emacs
o make shared libraries from liblw.a and libcompface.a (this
is why I thought that patches are a bit useless...)
o add "-rpath=/usr/libexec/xemacs-19.15/alpha-linux" to
ldflags in src/Makefile
o place the two shared libaries in the directory mentioned
above

Many thanks again for your help in getting XEmacs-19.15 to run on
Linux Alpha again. I'm really happy about this.

Have a nice weekend,
