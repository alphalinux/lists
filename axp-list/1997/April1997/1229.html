The concept behind multi-level marketing is simple, but very
powerfull. The majority of sales made are not made directly by you,
but by your customers, who in turn sell your product. To understand
the potential of MLM methods, lets examine how it works as applied
to the product you will be marketing.

You will be marketing four "MLM Reports", numbered one through
four at a cost of $5 each. The reports discuss MLM issues such as how
to best make use of MLM methods, evaluation of MLM techniques, and
how corporations are making use of MLM. You will start by marketing
report number one. Suppose you start small and only sell 20 reports.
In turn, each of these 20 people will then sell report one to, let's
say, 20 more people each, for a total of 20x20=400 reports. You will
see no revenue from these 400 reports sold, but each of those 400
people will need a copy of report two, which you will provide.
Continuing in this manner, those 400 people will each sell report one
to 20 more people, for a total 400x20=8000 copies of the third report
sold by you. Finally, those 8000 people will sell to 20 more people,
meaning you will sell 8000x20=160000 copies of report four. So in
total you will sell 168420 reports, at $5 per report, for a total
revenue of $842100.00! How does that sound for financial stability?

How do you find 20 people to purchase a copy of your report?
By sending a copy of this email message to as many people as possible
you can easilly achieve 20 sales. Suppose only 1% of the people you
email purchase a report (that means 99 out of 100 people do nothing).
Then you only need to email 2000 people. As it turns out, purchase
rates of the reports are much higher than 1% and we'll show you how to
easilly obtain over 250000 email addresses! Imagine you get only a
0.1% purchase rate (999 out of every 1000 people do nothing) but you

of report one. If we assume each customer then only sells 20 reports,
as we did above, then you can expect to make a total of 2105250
sales which translates to over 10 million dollars!!! What could you
