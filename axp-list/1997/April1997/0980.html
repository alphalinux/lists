After having recompiled XFree, xpm and libgr from redhat-4.1,
just compiled Xemacs 19.15p2 sucessfully at my alpha-box against glibc-2.0.3.

i used gcc-2.7.2.1 and binutils-2.7.0.16 -- my self-compiled versions, not the new rpm's from
ftp.redhat.com:/pub/alphabits. The Xfree recompile needed some changes
to xterm.c to get the right macros. xpm and libgr rebuilt out of the box.

The complete xemacs-patch, including the needed unexelf1 from stock emacs 19.34,
is at:

fabserver1.zarm.uni-bremen.de:/pub/axp/xemacs-19.15p2.patch.gz
