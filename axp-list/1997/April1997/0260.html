Hi.

I bought one from Starship Computer (via www.onsale.com). I think they
cost about $400 from Starship. You also need 70ns parity RAM (64M),
external SCSI drive bay and drives (disk and CD-ROM), plus a monitor and
keyboard.

Anyway, I'm running NT 4.0 Server on it, and it seems pretty decent with
native code. FP-intensive stuff seems faster than my P133. Haven't really
benchmarked it though. Intel apps are tolerable using the FX!32 emu.

My bitches are that the video is hard-wired at 256 colors and the internal
2.5 IDE hard drive connection doesn't seem to work. I have a spiffy 4M
CL546X 3D PCI accellerator that does VGA in the PCI slot, but not NT AXP
drivers. Bummer.

I'd like to get another one to run Linux on, but the RAM has gone sky-high
recently.
