For specifics, the Xircom module and the SMC module do not compile from
pcmcia-cs-2.9.4 with 2.0.30+axp-diffs. I don't use either so I took them out
of my makefile. After that, during the compiles, there were several messages
about redefining various functions and pointer casting to different sizes.
Beyond that, I had to do a strip -x *.o in the completed modules (otherwise
there's the stupid length messages with subsequent failure to load). In
addition, ds.o fails with something like "undefined reference:
__bad_user_length" (or something like that) upon loading. This may have to
do with one of the compile time messages about "__get_user" being redefined...

Also, I found (much to my chagrin) that I could not just go back to 2.0.27
kernel. The 2.9.4 install does something to the install and/or load scripts
(or config files) that prevents the previous 2.0.27 (pcmcia-cs-2.8.23)
modules from loading properly. I had to go back and re-compile/re-install
the previous version (probably scripts/config files) to resurrect pcmcia.
