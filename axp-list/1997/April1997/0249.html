>>> Eric Jones said:
>
> I'm trying to get the /dev/sequencer stuff to work on my UDB. Has anyone
> managed to get these critters to play midi files right?

They can't do MIDI via the FM synthesizer, as there is no FM sythesizer
in the UDB.

> Card config:
> MS Sound System at 0x530 irq 9 drq 3
> (OPL-2/OPL-3 FM at 0x388 drq 0)

The parentheses are the give-away: no OPL2/OPL3 was found.

My advice: look into "timidity", it uses GUS samples and sythesizes the
appropriate waveform and pushes it out via /dev/dsp (IIRC), and sounds
*much* better than FM synthesis. IMHO, of course. Try looking at:

http://www.clinet.fi/~toivonen/timidity/
