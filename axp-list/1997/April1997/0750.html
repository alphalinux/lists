To All;
I bought a 300Mhz DEC Alpha clone from Polywell Computers recently.
I had them install RH Linux 4.0 (kernel 2.0.18 and alphaBIOS 32.0.3)
at the factory. After firmware initialization, but prior to booting,
the hardware configuration output under the supplementary menu does
not list an lp port (everything else is present, including 2 serial
ports). During booting, lp_init is not run, and after booting /proc/
devices does not list any lp devices. I have tried a new motherboard
(from Polywell) with no change, and I have switched the orientation of
the internal parallel port cable (it looked backwards initially), again
with no luck. Should I expect to see an lp device after firmware
initialization? Are there any mother board subtleties (e.g. jumpers)
that could cause the problem? If worse comes to worse, can I use the
RH HP color printer drivers with a serial port, and is there anything
(hardware or software), other than a parallel/serital converter, that
I need to have to run the printer in serial mode.
TIA,
greg pollak
