>>> Thomas Peter Burg said:
>
> I have a DEC Personal Workstation 433a equipped with the AlphaBios 5.25
> and I've been trying to get milo running on it for at least 20h and it
> just doesn't work. I've tried out about every milo that is around out
> there including mikasa, alcor, xlt, eb164, and several others. I'm using
> linload 1.5 which is said to be suited to the AlphaBios. The only thing
> I always get when running milo over the OS BootLoader is the following
> output:
>
> Swapping to PALcode at 0x80000
>
> and this is where my machine hangs. If anybody knows what I could try
> next, I'd really appreciate his reply.

I'm assuming that you *first* tried the appropriately named image, ie:

miata-milo-2028

found in gatekeeper.dec.com:pub/DEC/Linux-Alpha/Kernels. The latest
linload.exe, version 1.5, you seem to already know about needing for
booting from AlphaBIOS.

So, what does the machine configuration look like? If you have a graphics
adapter that's unsupported by MILO, that's what you might see. Try hooking up
a terminal or comm line to COM1 and see what comes out when booting; MILO
does echo more messages to COM1 than to the screen for debug help.

Good luck.
