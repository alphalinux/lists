I bought a UDB a while back and managed to get it up and running very nicely.
I've noticed in the last few months that lots of folks are purchasing these
machines to run Linux/Alpha and WinNT on them. I figured that since so many
folks are doing this that its time we created a FAQ for the UDBs running
Linux Alpha in particular that handles the cases that the Linux Alpha FAQ
doesn't. To get the initiative started, I'm going to volunteer to act as
the primary contact point.

Please send in descriptions of problems and *solutions* that you have
incurred while getting your UDB to work with Linux/Alpha. I'll take
information relating to WinNT as well as long as it involves Linux/Alpha
ins some way as well.
