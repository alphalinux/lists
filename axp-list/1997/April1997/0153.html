You will need to compile a kernel with sound. It *may* be possible to load a
module for it, but the kernel would need to be compiled with sound as a module,
which I am not sure that it is for alpha.

As a practice, RedHat does not ship
with sound drivers on in their kernels. I don't know if this is from licencing
reasons from the developers of OSSlite, or just due to the significant
compilication of reliably detecting the vast array of sound hardware, DMA
channels, IRQ's and IO ports potentially in use during installation.
