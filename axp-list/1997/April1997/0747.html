>>> "Steven J. Curtis" said:
> I have an AXPpci33 based system (aka noname, UDB) and I am trying
> to add another ethernet interface to the system. I am having all kinds
> of problems. Whenever the 'ether=IRQ,ADDR,eth1' line is added to the
> boot sequence; the card is recognized, but the system never finishes booting.

If you boot it into "single" mode, can you then do the appropriate
"ifconfig" and "route" by hand and have them both come up, either
separately or together?

It's possible that the added card is being configured as "eth0", and if so,
may not be on the appropriate network for the way you set-up the old one.
If you have a NONAME, try swapping the two cards in their PCI slots, making
sure you keep the appropriate network plugged into the appropriate card.

Good luck.
