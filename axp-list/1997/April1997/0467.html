Hello folks!

We have a PC164 with a WDAC33100H IDE drive and an Matsushita CR-584 ATAPI
cdrom drive from Vobis for testing and try to install RH4.1 at the moment.
Both occupy the IDE connectors of the board (I assume there are two IDE
ports on the board).

We have MILO up and running and already booted into the ramdisk. After the
dialog "partitioning disks" RH tries to check the filesystems. Everything
is o.k. for the build-in harddisk, but RH tries to do a HDIO_GETGEO ioctl
on the ATAPI cdrom, recognizes a disklabeled harddisk and hangs after trying
to run fdisk on it.
The HD is being recognized as /dev/hda, the cdrom as /dev/hdc.

We don't know how to get any further, we need some help with this.
