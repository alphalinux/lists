I followed your suggestion that my problem might related to the interrupt handling.
I had checked the hardware spec. sheets of my AS500 and found that the IRQ of
SCSI Controller(PCI Slot 9) is set to 12. But from the log messages of MILO:
> qlogicisp: now isp 1020 revision ID(2).
> scsi0: QLogic ISP1020 SCSI on PCI bus 0 device 9 irq 28 base 0xb000.
> scsi: 1 host. "
it said that the IRQ is 28.

Is it possible that my problem is the MILO's wrong IRQ setting? So, there was
"dev_config: don't know to set hostid of SCSI device (1077:1020) " error message
on MILO loading process too. If this is the case:

1. How can I tell MILO the correct IRQ of the SCSI Controller?
2. Is there other way to fix this kind of problem?

I really need urgent helps, thanks in advance for any suggestions.
