Hello all,
About a week ago there was some discussion of problems running povray
under Linux/Alpha. Since I can not run Linux/Alpha on the machine I have
currently, I decided to try to debug the problem under Digital Unix.
I was getting an FPE just like the people running Linux were so I assumed
that I was seeing the same problem. Well you know what they say about
assuming :-) It seems that I have uncovered a bug in the Digital Unix
libc function F_pow() which is used in place of pow() when certain optimizer
flags are passed to the compiler. Its nice to know that we are not the
only ones who have bugs. After I sent off a bug report to DEC,
I recompiled povray with less agressive optimization and it runs fine.

I really don't know what all the output means, though it does generate a
nice image I can look at in xv. I am posting it here in case anyone is
interested.

Thanks,

Jim
