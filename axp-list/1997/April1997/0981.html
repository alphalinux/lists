I got a quote last November on the OSF-BASE license (DEC UNIX comes with
a two-user OSF-USER license). OSF-BASE is needed to run the operating
system. Without it, only root can log in and only on the console.

The quote I got last November came from the DEC sales group at
1-800-344-4825. The list price for the OSF-BASE license for my EB164
was $3,381. I also called Aspen Systems, who would sell it for
somewhere in the upper $2K's. It was too expensive for me! With that
money, I could buy another Alpha!

Note that this doesn't include licenses for any other products, but I
have access to those through the CSLG program. (The CSLG program does
NOT include the OSF-BASE license. It comes with your Alpha if you buy
it from DEC or if you buy a 3rd party machine with DEC UNIX on it.)
