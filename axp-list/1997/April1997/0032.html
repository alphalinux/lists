> The problem is MILO says "Swapping to PALcode at 0x0" and halts the
> system. Please help.

Did you use the XLT version of MILO? This is important, as the XL version
will NOT work on the XL366 because of the different CPU and such.

XL's come it two flavors, XL-233 and XL-266 have EV45 CPU, while the
XL-300 and XL-366 (the Turbo boxes, hence the T in XLT :-) use EV5/EV56.

Sorry if this is confusing...

If you *are* using the correct MILO, what kind of graphics is in the box?
If it's a VGA or TGA of some flavor, you should be OK, but a higher-end
graphics card may not work.
