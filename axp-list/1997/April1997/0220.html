Does the gfx directory exist? If not you'll have use 'unpak' to
generate the required files. It's been awhile since I did this.
IIRC you can find lots of 'unpak'ing utilities at:

ftp://ftp.cdrom.com/pub/idgames2/utils/bsp_pak_tools

I installed quake on my dos partition, unpak'ed everything, then
tar'ed up the whole mess and moved it to my linux partition.
