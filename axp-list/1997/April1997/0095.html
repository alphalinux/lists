On Wed, 2 Apr 1997, Adam Biskobing wrote:

> this is probably easily answered. How do I allow root to login remotely?

I woudn't advice this. The easest way to allow root access to a machine
is to log into a regular users account and use qsu, or simply do a su
root.

The ability to remotely log in as root, or allow people to remotely open
X-windows from root is a VERY VERY VERY VERY bad idea from a security
standpoint. I'd suggest getting qsu working.
