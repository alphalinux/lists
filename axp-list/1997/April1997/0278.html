Hallow <hallow@hallow.erols.com> writes:
> 2) I've heard conflicting reports as to performance. I've heard
> everything from P5-133 type to P6-200 type performance for the
> 21066 166Mhz. How comparable is it really to x86, and how would
> the 21066A 233 do, and would it be worth the price jump?

I believe that the 166 is _realistically_ equivalent to slightly more
than a 60Mhz Pentium. The 233 would probably be about a 90.

Given how much you have to spend, you might want to go with something
faster---I believe there are some vendors reading this list.

> 3) Withe the 2MB TGA card, what color depths are supported at 1024x768?

The TGA card in the UDB only does 8-bit.
