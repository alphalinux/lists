The following is a copy of a letter to my credit card company regarding
a purchase I made from RedHat Software.
Draw your own conclusions!!!

I placed an order via RedHat Softwares' web site, 10/21/96, for the
Applix software suite.
A colleague advised me that he did not think that this package would
run on an Alpha processor. A telephone call, 10/22/96, to Redhat
confirmed that this was so.
I requested that the order be cancelled and was told that it would be.
The package was delivered to me 10/28/96.
I attempted to contact Redhat by telephone several times that day with
no success.
The package was returned to Redhat,10/28/96, via UPS.
UPS records show this package was received, at Redhat's 480 Bonham
Ave., facility ( only return address on packaging ) and signed for by a
'Prinz'
UPS tracking no 1Z 961 454 08 9995 312 8

I have spent a great deal of time, since November 1996, and effort
multiple e-Mail messages, voice mail messages and two postal letters
trying to get Redhat to refund $199 cost plus shipping of $6.95 each
way, total of $212.90.

Redhat did advise me that a refund would be issued if they considered it
justified!!! That was some time ago.

As they obviously have no intention of refunding my money please credit
my account with $212.95 and take the appropriate action to have Redhat
Software reimburse you.

Yours Truly

David W. Morton
