mouse (you will see if you open your mouse 8-)) In Linux this works for
me:

console (from /etc/sysconfig/mouse):
MOUSETYPE="PS/2"
XEMU3=no

X11 (from /etc(X11/XF86Config):
Section "Pointer"
Protocol "PS/2"
Device "/dev/mouse"
EndSection

The console confugration results in gpm being run with the '-t PS/2'
option.
Linux reqognizes the three buttons both in console and X11.
