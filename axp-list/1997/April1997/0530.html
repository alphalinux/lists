Scott Locklin <locklin@gabor.lbl.gov> writes:
> I must reconfigure elm for the default "respond-to" -this is the third or
> fourth time I have replied to the wrong place.

It's the mailing list servers fault, because it inserts a Reply-To:
header to the list. To avoid this behaviour, include your own Reply-To:
header on outbound mail, and use a .procmail entry like this

:0:
* ^(from|to|cc|sender):.*axp(-?|-kernel-)list
* ^(from|to|cc|sender):.*redhat.com
| egrep -v "^Reply-To: axp-(kernel-)?list@redhat.com$" >>lo-pri.spool

to filter incoming mail.
