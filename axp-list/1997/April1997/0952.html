Scott D. Webster wrote:
> Y'know, I'm really suprised Linus didn't hack the UFS driver to
> work w/ DU. Didn't he start Linux/AXP development w/ a DU/Linux hybrid
> machine?

Yes he did, but he (and I, for that matter) used the ext2 filesystem
from day one. What I did was write some utilities in the spirit of
mtools which let you read/write ext2 filesystems from Digital UNIX.

As a matter of fact, when he started the Linux/Alpha port there
WAS no UFS filesystem for Linux. That didn't come until later, as
a byproduct of the SPARC Linux work...
