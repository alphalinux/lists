I just got off the horn with a guy at the IDL company about them cooking
up an IDL port. He was asking me all kinds of unanswerable questions about
what kind of market is out there for Interactive Data Language for
Linux-AXP. I know there are people here at LBL who would like this done,
and I would imagine there are folks elsewhere.

They have a port for Digital Unix, as well as Linux-x86, so it would seem
none too difficult.

Perhaps folks who are interested in this could send me some email, or
bother the IDL people about it.

(Incidentally, he told me that Digital actually bribed them to port IDL to
WinNT-AXP; hint hint).
