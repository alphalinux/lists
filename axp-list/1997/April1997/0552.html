> Some people offered me Noname 0K cache with 166 Mhz or 233 Mhz fow less
> than a Pentium.
> What do you think ? Is it too old or is it still good ?

Its a good deal but be aware that the 166Mhz processor system even with a
1MB of cache isn't going to be anywhere near as comparable to a Pentium
compatible system at the same speed. You have to go to the higher end of the
Mhz spectrum to get the serious performance boost. Also, alpha's tend to
need much more cache to get real performance (not to mean memory
bandwidth).
