Hi All,

I've been trying to recompile the redhat source distribution of
glibc-2.0.1 with linuxthreads, since going through the
binutils/gcc/glibc-2.0.2 upgrade path didn't work out very well.

I assumed that the standard distribution would compile, but have run in
to a few problems. The first two were pretty minor, and I just modified
the headers to fix what it was looking for. The resolv subdir seems to
have some problems, with the ipv6 handling though:
