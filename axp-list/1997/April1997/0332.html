Our lab recently purchased a PC164 with a 3com Fast EtherLink XL card.
After compiling a kernel with the 3c59x driver, the kernel will report the
cards ethernet address, its IRQ as 16, as well as giving some
information about the internel state of the card. After mounting the hard
drives, the kernel hangs after a eth0: vortex_open() message.

I have also tried the updated driver at http://cesdis/gsfc/nasa.gov/linux/
but that driver also hangs the kernel.

Has anyone gotten the 3905-TX to work under Linux/Alpha? Or can someone
tell me of a card thats known to work?
