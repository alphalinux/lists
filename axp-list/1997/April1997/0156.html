Hello Jim;

This is very good to know about the OSF compiler.
Would you have the time to download the latest POV version 3.01 and
try that version under OSF?

We have found the POV version 3.00b does work under Linux/Alpha, but
POV version 3.01 does not work.

Also just out of curiosity what Alpha are you running?

My son and I have run several tests on this test scene and the best
time we have so far is 5 hours 28 minutes on a PC164 433Mhz , 128Mb,
2Mb, Linux 2.0.27, and gcc 2.7.2.1.

> Hello all,
> About a week ago there was some discussion of problems running povray
> under Linux/Alpha. Since I can not run Linux/Alpha on the machine I have

> that I was seeing the same problem. Well you know what they say about

> flags are passed to the compiler. Its nice to know that we are not the
> only ones who have bugs. After I sent off a bug report to DEC,
> I recompiled povray with less agressive optimization and it runs fine.
