On Thu, 10 Apr 1997, Alvin Starr wrote:

> The problem will only show itself on systems where sizeof(long unsigned)
> != 4. You should be safe on an intel platform.
> The problem is that the person who wrote the code took advantage of the
> fact that a unsigned long was 4 bytes in size. This is not true on all
> platforms.
>
> Alvin Starr || voice: (416)493-3325

I have hed the same proble and here is the solution:
struct tcphdr *tcp=(struct tcphdr *)((__u32 *)ip+ip->ihl);
struct udphdr *udp=(struct udphdr *)((__u32 *)ip+ip->ihl);
struct icmphdr *icmp=(struct icmphdr *)((__u32 *)ip+ip->ihl);

This will work at x86 too :)
