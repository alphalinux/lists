Hello all!

As stated yesterday we have a PC164 board from VOBIS for testing purpose.
After installing RH4.1 (not as easy as we thought of before...) we have another
problem. Whenever compiling the kernel or doing other things that cause some
load the system hangs after some minutes. Many hundred lines of the form

+ 0x0007457800

are dumped to the console and it finishes with saying something about "CIA
hardware monitoring". After reboot the ARC console boots into a hardware
monitor debug message and you have to reboot again.

The VOBIS compute is being shipped with an IDE harddisk and ATAPI cdrom.

Thanks,
Stephan
