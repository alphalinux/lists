Maurice,

Before we have too much confusion on the list, let's make
clear that the post you quote below is NOT by Terry, who had
the run-in with Parasoft, but by myself. I think Terry's
post is a model of restraint and decorum, and I confess that
mine is not. When it comes to situations such as Terry
described, I don't do decorum. I think Parasoft's conduct
in this case is absolutely inexcusable, and while I'll mince
words here on the list, I certainly wouldn't if I had
Parasoft on the phone.

For a company rep to cold-call someone and *reprimand* him
for a posting to the net is the height of bad manners, not
to say idiocy. But to call three times? Monumentally
stupid. Any employee of mine who did that would be looking
for a job in one big f*cking hurry.

Then again, I *AM* a hothead, Maurice, and saner people than
I might react differently. <shrug> All a matter of style, I
guess, and if Parasoft and I, or you and I, don't agree on
this one, well, so be it.
