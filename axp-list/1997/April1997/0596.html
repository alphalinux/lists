You won't be able to install DUNIX onto alpha PCxxx machines, because
no SRM has be developped for them (and that's why you couldn't find it). This
is intended (I believe) to avoid having people buying low priced WNT systems,
and then install DUNIX onto them. I also believe it's for the very same reason
that one's cannot have SRM and AlphaBios at the same time onto an AS255. sight
...
As for the fuses blown into the processor according to the OS type, I
do not know for sure, but I doubt *very* strongly this is true. Check and cross check your
sources of information.
