Hello;

On last Saturday I posted a message describing my experience with Insure++
under Linux/Intel. I had downloaded the evaluation copy from the Parasoft
ftp site.

Today I had someone from Parasoft telephoned and IMHO begin to chew me out
for posting that message. He mentioned that someone from this mailing list
'forwarded' him a copy. I do not understand why someone on this list felt
it necessary to forward that mail to Parasoft. I do not appreciate having
any vendor telephone me three times in one day, implying that I do not know
what I am doing with Insure++. I did speak with Parasoft's tech support
and explained why I felt it was slow, and in the case I was evaluating
unacceptably slow. I went along with his suggestions and the while the
program did have somewhat better performance the increase was nothing
great.

I do stand by what I stated in the Saturday posting that Insure++ is
slow, and after spending the Easter weekend working with it, I find it
unacceptably slow.

Given Parasoft's actions today in telephoning me at home after explaining
to the first caller that I have a broken shoulder and had been up all night
from pain, I see no reason to have any further dealings with this Company.

This is my opinion only, your mileage may vary. ;-)

I would ask that the person who felt that he has to forward my original
posting to Parasoft, please contact me via private e-mail and explain yourself.
What happen to the common courtesy of asking me first?

I have CC'ed Parasoft a copy of this message already so there is no need
to forward them another.
