At 10:05 21/04/97 +1000, you wrote:
>
>Well I seem to have a bit of a problem. Recently somebody was 'playing' around
>with the ARC console on my udb (VX40B-F2) and now I don't get any video. My
>monitor reports that there is no signal. The monitor works faultlessly on
>my PC.
>

I found the problem and thought I should post the solution here for anybody else
who may encounter the problem in the future.

As it turns out the console was switched from ARC to the SRM console, and it
appears the
SRM console does not directly support the TGA video card (ie - There is no
'Sync' signal
when running SRM). This lead to additional confusion when neither console
echoes
to the serial port if a keyboard is plugged into the keyboard socket.

Unplugging the keyboard and installing a dumb terminal did the trick. When
I switched
back to the ARC console (command >>> set os_type NT) the TGA video was revived.

Is this normal ?
Do other people have problems using the onboard video adapter with the SRM
console ?

Cheers
