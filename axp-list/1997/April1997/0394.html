>>> Britt Bolen said:
>
> What is the fastest of the TGA Xservers? and what is the difference
> between the EV4 and EV5 Xservers? Thanks

Fastest servers are the 3.1.2-based ones; most stable is the 3.2 one from
XFree86, which allows toggling to VCs and use of XDM, unlike the former.

Choose version EV4 to run on platforms with EV4/EV45/LCA4/LCA45 CPUs, such
as UDB, NONAME, AS2xx/AS400, Cabriolet, EB64+, EB66(+). Choose the EV5
version for AS500/AS600 (ALCOR), EB164, PC164, Alpha-XLT (300/366).
