AFAIK "some" stands for "all" in this case (which is still "some",
strictly speaking :-). In a private correspondence Erik Troan indicated
that he will look at this, some day.

For now do this (after setting your clock properly):

touch /tmp/now
find / -newer /tmp/now | xargs touch
rm /tmp/now

You may want to be a bit more selective in a list of directories
for 'find' to operate. This will still leave your symbolic links
with dates "back to the future".
