>Questions: what's a good swap size if you have 64M? And does anyone
>bother comparing BogoMips anymore?
>
For Linux/x86 twice the memsize is recommended for swap. I believe that
this is generally assumed to be valid for axp as well, but I doubt if
anyone ever investigated this. Much depends on your workload.

As for BogoMips: you will findyour BM's to be much the same as your MHz.
Interestingly, this does not depend on the type of Alpha you use, which
goes to show that BM's is _not_ a very good performance indicator.
