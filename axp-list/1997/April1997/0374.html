Dave,

Yes, a standard VGA monitor will work on your UDB. I share the Sony
monitor on my Gateway P5-150 with my UDB through a dumb switchbox. It
works just fine. Don't try to use a dumb switchbox to do the same with
mouse and keyboard though. It seems that the UDB requires a constant
flow of electrons to and from the keyboard and mouse. The flow would be
cut off with one of the inexpensive switchboxes. There are switchboxes
out there that would work (keep the flow going) but they cost more than
the UDB! Figures, huh?? ;->

Enjoy . . . .
