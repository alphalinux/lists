On Mon, 7 Apr 1997, Dirk Grunwald wrote:

> I'm trying to get Digital Unix shared libraries working on a Linux/Alpha
> system. Suspend your concerns with licenses for the moment, I first want
> to know if this is possible & will address the other issues later.

[...]

> 5685:./foo: /sbin/loader: Fatal Error: set_program_attributes failed to
> set heap start [miata-24]

This is usually a sign that you're using libraries/loader from DU 4.0 rather
than DU 3.2. I believe one of the newer 2.1.x kernels adds support for DU
4.0 libraries/loader. Otherwise try the libraries/loader from 3.2.

Good luck.
