> I just upgraded to glibc-0.961212-3 via rpm and anything that uses
> crypt() is broken. What do I have to do to get everything working,
> including logging into the system!

it seems that crypt has been moved out of libc and into libcrypt. the
only problem is that a number of programs that dynamicly load libs and
expect libc to contain crypt will break. Perl is a prime example of this
I ended up changing the SRPM and rebuilding/reinstalling the package to
get to work. I have included my spec file for all those interesetd
