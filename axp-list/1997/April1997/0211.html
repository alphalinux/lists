> Linux developers early access to the software, to aid in the
> verification of software packages, and to provide feedback and bug

Congratulations on an excellent and worthwhile project. I'm amazed at how
well the emulator works, even with my little 166 MHz Multia with only 24 MB
of memory. Netscape3 is definitely usable even on my Multia. (X Server is
on another machine) I have only begun to try a few programs, most with
sucess.

Is there an established method for bug reporting yet? I seem to have trouble
resolving hostnames with Web browsers. Netscape and Amaya both run great
under the emulator, but they cannot seem to resolve hostnames. No problem
using
telnet, ping, whatever to resolve hostnames. Additionally, the GIMP fails
when loading some graphic formats (haven't gone past that yet.)

Thanks to Digital for their support of this project!

Chris
