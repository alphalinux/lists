On Mon, 28 Apr 1997, Rob van Nieuwkerk wrote:

> > Ever since I installed 4.1 if I try to run netstat I get these
> > messages in my /var/log/messages:
> >
> > Apr 27 18:19:17 chomper kernel: netstat(937): unaligned trap at 00000155556ba83c: 000000011ffff4ac 2d 10
> > Apr 27 18:19:17 chomper kernel: netstat(937): unaligned trap at 00000155556ba83c: 000000011ffff4bc 2d 10
>
> Yep, me too. And netstat prints some rubbish too.

Can you (and anybody else similarly afflicted) try this patch, please?
It's against net-tools-970422, though it ought to work with any reasonably
recent version.
