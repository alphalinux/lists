These seem to come from the tcp part of the tcp/ip protocol. with netsat
udp shows up fine but the errors are with tcp. ? ? ?
Has anyone else seen this problem. I am using a eb164 board. 2.0.30,
tulip v .74

I had these same problems on our alpha PC164. It is due to the IP
header not being word aligned within the ethernet packet. I worked
around it by changing

#define SKBUFF_RX_COPYBREAK 200

to 1600 at the top of tulip.c, which forces all packets to be copied.
