I to was having the same problems that other people on the list was
having. I tired all the hints that people gave and none of them seemed to
work for me. Here are the things that I tried.

1. I grabed linux-2.0.30.tar.gz from ftp.cs.helsinki.fi and applied the
axp diff from 2.0.21. When I tried compiling it I got the same error that
other people got.

2. Next, using the same linux-2.0.30.tar.gz, I applied then axp diff from
2.0.21, and then applied a patch that someone on the list sent out. This
didn't work either. It puked the compile at the same spot.

3. I saw people on the list saying that they got 2.0.30 to compile without
any diffs at all... so I tried that. This time I got that include file
error (timesomething.h). That didn't work...

4. I don't know why I thought this might work, but here's what I tried
next. I had the linux-2.0.28.tar.gz. I untarted that, applied the patch to
bring it up to linux 2.0.29. Then I applied the axp diff patch. Then I
applied the patch to 2.0.30, along with the em86 patches. And guess what?
This time it worked!

So if nothing anyone else has sugested worked, try this... worked for me..
