Try to compile yourself a milo which uses "53c8xx" driver (generally
distributed images rely on "7,8" driver). I had to do recently for
a card which was not recognized at all by "normal" milo images and
it worked fine afterwards. This milo image is available from us,
but we have it only for pc164 (nothing else was needed at the moment :-).

Don't know if this will work, but it worth a try. Oh, milo also checks
if it deals with a "known" NCR card type. As my hardware did not fall
into that range I simply commented out this check in milo sources
(which was hardly correct, but was simple and fast :-). It does not
seem to be the problem in your case, as your milo works after
initialization was done by NT.
