On Apr 14, Steven Kokinos wrote:
>
> 2.0.2 doesn't give the same error, but it bombs out in the sunrpc section
> right near the end. Apparently this is due to a bug in binutils. If
> there is a workaround to upgrading binutils I'm all ears. I've heard
> there might be, but can't remember what it was.
>

I don't remember the way I did my upgrades, but I never experienced
problems like these. You may consider getting precompiled binaries
(e.g., just for bootstrapping purposes if you want to build your own
gcc/binutils/glibc) - I have some at ftp://genie.ucd.ie/pub/alpha
(no Trojan horses). These binaries seem to work fine - I built X11R6.3
and a lot of other stuff which appears to function properly.
(Except for linuxthreads.)

I wouldn't recommend upgrading to binutils-2.7.0.11 or later (at least
up to .14), since ld segfaults there; I use 2.7.0.10 with Richard
Henderson's PLT (or whatever it is called) patch.
