>Anyone know of current support or work under way for Iomega Zip/Jaz
>drives for Linux/Alpha? TIA.

What kind of support? My Jaz is recognized as a fixed disk, but
works as well on my alpha as on my i386 linux. (But it crashes
Windows. :-/)

I would watch for problems when swapping to a cartridge with a
different partition table without rebooting, but to date I've
been happy with either one fdisk partition or one SMD-UFS
partition.

There's a handy program called jaztool that toggles ro/rw status
and can eject the cartridge. Look at sunsite. I believe it
compiles without change on the alpha, but I'm not entirely sure
since I changed it anyway for other reasons.
