> How can I do a complete reinstall with a VGA card? There must be a way
> to do this using the vmlinux.gz kernel on my hard drive partition. Any
> MILO experts out there?

You don't need a MILO expert. MILO will let you pass any parameter to the
kernel - read the documentation on the ramdisk driver to find out what you
need to say. "load_ramdisk=1" may be part of it, but I suspect you need
something else to get the prompt.
