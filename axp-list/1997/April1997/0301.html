set_program_attributes syscall is not implemented in 2.0.x kernels,
because (as far as I understand) it was not used in DU 3.2 binaries and
libraries. It is implemented in 2.1.x together with a couple of other new DU
syscalls. One is supposed either to upgrade to 2.1.32, or to apply a patch
(ftp://genie.ucd.ie/pub/alpha/kernel/osf_43.diff) for 2.0.29 (the patch
only affects set_program_attributes, so Netscape does not seem to run
anyway).
