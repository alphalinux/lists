Umm, S3 is way better...

AlphaStation 255 seems to have some PCI weirdness I haven't investigated
yet. For example, my machine works OK (with 3.1.2) when the video card
is in slot 3 and hangs when it is in slot 1. Strange? Yes.
If I have my video card in slot 3 and some other PCI card in slot 1, X
server seems to work but occasionally hangs the system - it may have
worked for several hours and then suddenly hang.

The moral is:
1) Trident may not be a soultion as well (although most probably it is);
2) you may eventually succeed if you try playing with 3.1.2 server and
putting your video card into different slots (as a temporary measure
while the problem is under investigation by Harald Koenig and
AlphaStation 255 owners' community).
