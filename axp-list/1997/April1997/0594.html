> P.S. No I don't have a DEC UNIX licence, it is to exspensive for my.

Then send email to one of the nice Digital staff that post mail on this
mailing list and ask them if they'll send you a "free, pirated" copy of the
Digital UNIX libraries that you want. Tell them that their products are
drastically overpriced, and that you're a good guy and ought to get copies of
their software for free. No doubt they will consider the "drastically
overpriced" bit to be a term of endearment. They may even send you a free
Alpha box. (Or suggest that you have a chat with either of the Finnish police
or a Sun Microsystems representative. :-) )

Or find an institution in Finland that has Digital UNIX-based machines and
pull out the lock-pick tools...

In short, if you're not willing to pay the money for Digital UNIX licenses,
then you have two choices:
a) Try to steal what you want, and risk legal sanction. This can have very
bad side-effects. Not recommended.
b) Do without.

I'll just note that this is probably the most stupid place to make requests
for pirated Digital "warez" as there are considerable numbers of Digital
employees represented on this mailing list.
