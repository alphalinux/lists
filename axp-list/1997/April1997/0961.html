> On 21-Apr-97 Nils Faerber wrote:
> |> GUS in DSP mode works fine but when using the native GUS mode all samples
> |>are much TOO fast! Using a mod player like gmod a song normally taking
> In xgmod 3.0.5 there's an option to run at a 50Hz clock frequency, have you
> tried this?
I tried nearly all switches and also tried other MOD players. But
everything with the same effects.

> |>about five minutes finishes in 5 seconds!
> |>Is this an AXP problem?
> |>Or is the Voxware driver broken for Alpha?
> It may be that GUS + Alpha support isn't concrete yet...perhaps you should try
> the Linux Ultrasound Project's drivers; that might fix it :) (they've been
> tested on a noname, so hopefully they'll work on a jensen as well).
Oh oh ... I got the latest version from their web site but it did not
compile cleanly at all! I had to patch several files that are indeed
intended for other architectures. But in the end I had no success because
of Jensens very own I/O interface: insmod fails with missing symbols
_bus_inb and _bus_outb.

I also fiddled with the sources of all players I tested but could not find
any dependencies to HZ at all. I even grep-ped through the sources for
"100" and "50" but could not identify any timing critical sections.
So in the end I exchanged cards with another AMD486 I have: GUS into the
AMD Linux box and a SB16 into the Alpha. Now both are happy (but I'm not
completely ... it was intended vice versa).
