This is a much debated issue. Most say [faq's and all] that the
UDB on 166Mhz compairs at around the speed of a 60-75 Mhz Pentium..
and there are others that say that the speed of such a machine
varies according to the applications your using.. and all.. and
that it could vary from 60-100 Mhz.. speed of a Pentium processor.

I'm not such an expert on this.. lets here what they have to say.
BTW, heard that the limited cache [on the standard issue of 166Mhz
UDB/multia] is pretty small and there for puts a big strain on the
overall system.
