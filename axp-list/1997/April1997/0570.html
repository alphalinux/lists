At 09:25 AM 04-12-97 -0400, you wrote:
>I am using 2 seagate barracuda ultra wide scsi hard disks. my hdc is an
>ultra wide controler
>
You may want to consider your termination setup. As these drives are wide,
they have both a low and a high termiantion byte. Make sure these are
terminated, and that there are no terminators enabled except at the ends of
the SCSI bus. Many times I have seen a wide card misconfigured, especially
if it is managing narrow devices too. Can you tell me which card, and what
is hooked up where? i.e.: which port are HD's on, what is their
termination, what about your CDROM drive?

Thanks.
