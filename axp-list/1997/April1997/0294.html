Why is everyone still looking at UDBs? There are now a bunch of
AlphaStation 200s (21064) and AlphaStation XL (21164) machines that
are hitting the market. The nice thing about these machines is that
they are desktop boxes so that you can put more than one PCI card in
them. The also have a couple of ISA slots for misc stuff like modems
and so forth.

For about the price of a UDB, you can now get a AlphaStation 200
fully configured. Take a look:
