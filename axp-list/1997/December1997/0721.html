On Sun, 21 Dec 1997, Keith Baker wrote:

> ok... I have been trying to boot the RH 5.0 installer. I got the Noname
> image and the ramdisk image. How do I get them too boot!!! I can boot
> the kernel and it mounts my RH 4.0 partition and keeps going. I tried
> telling it to use root=/dev/fd0 but it asks me for the disk I instert it
> and it panics. What am I doing wrong.

You need to pass "root=/dev/fd0 load_ramdisk=1 prompt_ramdisk=1" at
boot time.
