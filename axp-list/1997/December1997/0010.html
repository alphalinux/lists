Correct, but sda2 in only an example and not a requirement, of course,
and you may want to add:

boot sda2:/vmlinux-2.0.30-my-very-own.gz root=/dev/sd<whatever>

in case your root does not live on a default partition sda2.
Various other parameters, like 'single' :-), can be also attached
to this command line.
