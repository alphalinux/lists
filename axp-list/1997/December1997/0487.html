With regards to your message at 06:54 AM 12-12-97 -0800, Ladislav Zejda.
Where you stated:
> Dear all,
>
> I have seen on the market 2 kinds of SDRAMs - both are named as
>
>10 ns, unbuffered, 3.3 V, 168 pin SDRAMs
>
>but one group has addition "EPROM, works with Intel Natoma 440LX and later
>chipsets".
>
>Does anybody know if both kinds of SDRAMs will work with PC164SX board?
No, the EPROM is NOT what you want.

A list of qualified parts, from Alpha Powered lists reads:

AlphaPC164SX- non ECC

ATP 128 x64 AMU16V64E8S4G0
Kingston 16 x64 KTV164SX/16
Kingston 32 x64 KTV164SX/32
Kingston 64 x64 KTV164SX/64
Kingston 128 x64 KTV164SX/128
Viking 32 x64 VE4641U4SN3-DC01
Viking 64 x64 VE8641U4SN3-DC01
Viking 128 x64 VE16642U4SN3-DC01

This is not meant to a complete list of what WILL run, but rather what has
been tested and confirmed to be good from a tester at DEC.

I also know that Corsair has RAM that works.

Hope this might help.
