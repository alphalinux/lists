As an aside, I noticed that some of my AXP boxes got these
messages, and some did not. Upon further investigation, I discovered that
the apparent source of the 'unaligned trap' messages was the fact that I
had kerneld enabled in the kernel(i.e. autoload of modules). I had read
that the axp kernels must be monolithic and when I recompiled on those
machines giving the errors and excluded kerneld, the 'unaligned trap'
messages went away..

Anyone else have this experience, or know why my errors went
away???
