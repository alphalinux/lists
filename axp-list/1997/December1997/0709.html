Sorry I abused those ISDN terms, please don't shoot me.
FYI, I usually refer to ISDN "modems" always in quotes, and this refers to
a TA that is either an interrnal card, or an external box that connects
to a computer Via serial cable (Motorola Bitsurfer) This usually avoids
confusion with a lot of people that are not well versed with ISDN. After
helping several network admins install ISDN connections, sometimes the
simplest language is the best when explaining things *grin*

I hope I haven't offended TOO much :)
