>>>>> Erik Troan writes:

> A new version of ppp, ppp-2.2.0f-6, is now available for Red Hat 5.0
> on Alpha's. It should fix all of the problems with setting up routes
> the original package had. Please let me know if it doesn't.

> Erik

Erik, could you please explain why RedHat does not use the 2.3.2 version
of pppd? I just compiled it on a fresh RH5.0 and it worked (apart from
the infamous routing problem...) after i changed all occurances of
"#include <linux/[...]>" to "#include <net/[...]>". Ah and i had to rename
"#include <linux/if_ether.h>" to "#include <net/ethernet.h>".

Because ppp-2.3.2 offers some significant improvements over ppp-2.2.0f,
i was just wondering why RedHat is still sticking to 2.2.0f, especially
since pppd needs to be patched anyway.

BTW. "/usr/games/atc" of bsd-games does not work on the Alpha. The screen
is screwed up.
