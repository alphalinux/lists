> I just installed RedHat 5.0 (2.0.30) onto a AS255/300 whithout too much

> support the DEFPA fddi card. On boot I now see:
> Everything works fine, until I try to ifconfig the card. At that

I had this same problem, running a defpa card on x86-linux and freebsd.
It displayed the same symptoms on both platforms. :( The solution for my
x86 with a defpa was to replace the crap Tyan Tomcat with a jolly new ASUS
board. there seemed to have been some silly compatibility problems
between the tyan board and the defpa pci bioses... I'd look at a rom
upgrade for the as255 as a possibility, if nothing else seems to work.
