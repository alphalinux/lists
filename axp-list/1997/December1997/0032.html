The fact that you have to ask me shows the need. What you are proposing
to do is to take a set of software that you have no work invested in,
other than burning a CD, and selling that work for a profit. You have
not built this product, nor spent nights endlessly programming and you
intend to see none of the proceeds of your work to go to the orginators
of the work. At least you make no mention of what proper renumeration
would be to those who have *ACTUALLY* done the work and put the sweat
into it. So in the end you wish to personally profit off of a product
that you have no equity in, looking for a way not to give any
renumeration to those who have sweated to make the product and then ask
me why I see the need for you to learn about morals and ethics? If it
is not apparent then you will probably not benefit much from such a
class anyway.
