On Sat, 6 Dec 1997, Joakim Roubert wrote:

> Hmm.. I get error in /usr/include/resourcebits.h here too... Strange. That
> file is the main problem, I reckon. I'll se if I can get another one.

The patched kernel sources that I have do not define RLIMIT_CPU,
RLIMIT_FSIZE, etc. in <asm/resource.h>. Instead, they are defined as
__KERNEL_RLIMIT_CPU, __KERNEL_RLIMIT_FSIZE, and so on, which caused any
piece of source written to use <sys/resource.h> to puke. I modified
<resourcebits.h> to use the __KERNEL_ definitions instead. This may or
may not be the right thing to do, but it worked fine on my system, and
programs that use <sys/resource.h> compile cleanly.

You might be running into a similar issue.
