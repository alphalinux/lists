Dave Gilbert <gilbertd@treblig.org> writes:
> OK - I've finally got PPP and mail going on my LX; here are some
> soloutions and some problems I hope people can help me with:
>
> 1) PPP - if your PPP is stuck upgrade your kernel! I built a 2.1.63
> kernel from standard unpatched 2.1.63 sources and it works fine.
> (Its doing this!!). My old PPP scripts worked with very minor
> tweaking using the RH5.0 distribution pppd/chat.

That's interesting. Well, up to know I didn't look into the 2.1.x
kernels, Because I was quite happy with 2.0.30. Anyway, when you mean
upgrading to 2.1.63, have you used another 2.1.x kernel, or also
2.0.x?

> 5) Soundcard - I have a soundblaster 16 value; true creative labs -
> works fine on Linux/x86 - how do I get it to work on Alpha? I tried
> building the sound drivers (in 2.0.30) in the same way as for intel
> and one of my EIDE channels stopped working - presumably due to
> an interrupt screw up??? How to ISA interrupts map to the interrupts
> and addresses seen by the kernel??

Which exact model you got? I got the ct-4170 with the Vibra chip. It
lacks an 16-bit DMA channel (=> set the 16-BIT DMA CHANNEL entry to 1
during kernel configuration), but otherwise it works fine. I am using
Interrupt 5, because somewhere I read that using interrupt 7 caused
some problems on some machines, also apperently that worked on my
Jensen as well.

Did you initalize the card correctly, using the Isapnp-tools? You could
check with 'cat /proc/{interrupts,dma}' which resources are already
used.
