I run Linux on my Alphaserver 1000 4/266 with no problems.
You will see this machine identified as MIKASA family in this newsgroup.

The Alpahserver 1000A has dual a pci bus and is reportedly NOT
supported.

The integrated video adapter is rather useless for Xwindows. I disabled
it and installed a S3 968 based PCI video adapter and now enjoy Xwindows
at resolutions up to 1600x1200.
