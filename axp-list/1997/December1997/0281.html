> What's the experience on tape drives on Alphas? The SCSI howto
> says that all SCSI-compatible tape drives should work. Is this true?

So far this was the case for all tape SCSI tape drives I had an
opportunity to try. I do not see any reasons why this actually should
be otherwise.

> Can the same be said for IDE connected tape drives?

No idea. Pay close attention to the fact that IDE specifies a total
length of cables not exceeding 50 cm (18").
