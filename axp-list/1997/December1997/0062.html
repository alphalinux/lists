You might have better luck with CBB, as it should not require compilation,
being written in Tcl and Perl. It is the most mature of the personal finance
packages available for Linux at this time.

See links at: <http://www.hex.net/~cbbrowne/finances.html>

I would suggest that XACC is probably not the right option for you at this
time; it is really in "alpha" state, with much development still to be done.
People should use it at this point if they want to do development work.
