On Fri, Dec 12, 1997 at 11:37:43AM -0700, Aspen Systems Technical Support wrote:
> In an attempt to stabilize an SX running RH 5.0, I have encountered the
> following error messages via the serial console. Does anyone have
> experience fixing these errors, or even know what they mean?
>
> swap_free: swap-space map bad (entry 710d0000000000)
[...]
> swap_duplicate: trying to duplicate unused page

This might indicate either you've mounted a swap partition without
configuring it with mkswap (unlikely) or you've encountered actual
bad blocks. I don't know if RH runs mkswap -c to check for those,
but you might just get the system up and installed without swap and
investigate those problems later.

You could also run mkswap -c by hand from VC2 during the installation.
