With regards to my comments concerning the Opera browser. Some people
feel it is not appropriate to discuss this on the mailing list. If that
is the case, that is your opinion and I respect that, but I was responding
to a mail I recieved from the list. As for the comments themselves, I
went to there site and read the fine print. I still feel it is the
developers responsibility to create a viable product before asking for
money. In other words, perform the work required and then ask for the
dough. Maybe I came off as cranky or something in my other mail.
Perhaps, that is true =) Nevertheless if there is a need for a decent
browser native to the alpha, I am willing to help in writing one. Is
there a current project now writing a GNU browser for the alpha? If not,
why not? I am a person of little pocket, but of much work ethic. Let's
write one. I felt alot of negativity from replies to my post, maybe you
misread me and thought I was hostile. I merely question anyone who
expects to be paid before they do the work. That is my "starving student"
mind-set. I re-iterate is anyone interested in writing a browser for the
alpha?

And yes, finals are getting to me. =)
