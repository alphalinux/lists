It is possible. Imperfections in removable media plus known
"touchiness" of Adapatec may play together to give what you see.

> (Anyone else had trouble with Adaptec + Zip).

There is one Alpha machine around here with Adaptec and Jazz. It had
serious problems. After using "abort/reset" patches from Doug Ledford
situation greatly improved although it is not perfect. Doug has
some suspicions but I could not investigate any further so far.

AFAIK these patches are not available in 2.1.xx series and are
incorporated into the current 2.0.33. They make Adaptec driver less
prone into going in a vicious cycle if infinite resets.
