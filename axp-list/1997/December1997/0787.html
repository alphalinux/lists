As a possible answer/dumb comment:

I used to get unaligned trap errors all the time on my AXP boxes.
However, once I started compiling kernels with NO kerneld support for
loadable modules (i.e. A monolithic approach) the unaligned trap errors
went away and have NOT come back.

Cheers!
