I have an AlphaServer 1000 and purchased off the shelf 36bit 60ns memory
(HITACHI i think?) and everything has worked fine. Be advised that at
least on the AlphaServer 1000 series you must populate a bank of 5 simms
at a time, 1 is used for ECC memory purposes. For me to have 128 MB ram
I had to purchase 192 MB (5 modules).
