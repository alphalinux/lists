People brave enough to try on their Alphas fvwm2 window manager, version
2.0.45 - to be found on many servers and CDs, may retrieve patches via
anonymous ftp from ellpspace.math.ualberta.ca.

These patches are designed mostly to ensure an unventful compilation,
although they caught a bug or two as well, and they are equally valid
on Alpha and other platforms. I avoided even obvious corrections (like
using size_t instead of int in contexts where obviously strings lengths
were meant) in order to keep sources as close to the original as possible.
Try that out. If they are getting on your nerves you may reduce compiler
complaints by removing -W... options from flags (see Fvwm.tmpl).

In immortal words: "It works for me!"

Michal
