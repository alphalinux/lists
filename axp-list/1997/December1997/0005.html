Carlos Carvalho <carlos@fisica.ufpr.br> writes:

>
> Harvey J. Stein (hjstein@bfr.co.il) wrote on 25 November 1997 16:36:
> > 14. reboot with /vmlinux-2.0.30-my-very-own.gz.
>
> Sorry, how do I do this? I'm new to linux-alpha... All the other steps
> I understand.

Look at the MILO HowTo, or the SRM HowTo, or the ALPHA HowTo, or ...
In brief, assuming you're using milo, and assuming you're booting off
of sda2, and assuming you don't need to pass the kernel any boot
parameters, then at the MILO> prompt you'd give the command:

help boot

Then, you'd follow the instructions. I think the command would be:

boot sda2:/vmlinux-2.0.30-my-very-own.gz

but I'm not sure.
