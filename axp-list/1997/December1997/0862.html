Michael W. Chalkley wrote:
>
> Hello:
>
> Trying to remote print from Solaris 2.5.1 to a RedHat Linux Alpha 4.2
> box running lpd. It doesn't seem to work. Anybody have remote printing
> through lpd working?

I'm printing from a 2.5.1 machine to a RedHat 4.1 Intel machine and it
works just fine. The RH box is running the standard printing setup with
Ghostscript. I told the Solaris machine that the printer was a Panasonic
KXP-5400, since I knew that printer would just take a straight
Postscript Dump. The printer on the RH machine is actually an LJ-2 but
that didn't work since RH treats the printer as a PS device.

There may be some Alpha-specific problem, the only ones I can think of
are the fact that the parallel printer on an Alpha is usually /dev/lp0
and not the lp1 of most PCs, or you may not have parallel printer
support compiled into your kernel or loaded as a module.
