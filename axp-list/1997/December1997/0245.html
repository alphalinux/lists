Why does everyone want to run IDE on good hardware? IDE is by it's design
bad hardware and consumes valuable resources from your system.

Even some dutch magazine was finally getting the picture and advised NOT
to use IDE on servers or other high performance hardware.

So even if some Alpha system DO have IDE (or EIDE) interfaces I would
recommend you do NOT use them.

Hugo.
