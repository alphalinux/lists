The origin of the GPL had nothing to do with bloodsuckers like
CheapBytes being in business. It was intended that others would take
the work, add their own value and then be able to distribute the work
without royalties (in the industry much is made of what would be called
derived works). Everyone who is selling these CD's is making mney off a
product that they themselves have no work invested, usually without any
thought to paying for the sweat equity. This is both immoral and
unethical. If I add some value (and I do not see the value os simply
burning a CD) to a product and then wish to distribute the product and
make a profit, at least I have done some work for which I am being paid
for. But I guess this diatribe digresses from the topics of this
list.....
