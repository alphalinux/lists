On my machine, which runs 2.0.32 with the latest axp-diffs from Jay Eastbrook,
<asm/resource.h> no longer defines RLIMIT_CPU, RLIMIT_FSIZE, etc. as it does
under i386. Instead, it defines __KERNEL_RLIMIT_CPU, __KERNEL_RLIMIT_FSIZE,
etc. It seemed logical to modify <resourcebits.h> to use the __KERNEL
definitions instead. This may or may not be the right thing to do, but I havent had any
problems since.

You can find my modified glibc-2.0.5c <resourcebits.h> at
ftp://ftp.linuxdot.com/pub/patches/glibc/glibc-2.0.5c-resource.h
