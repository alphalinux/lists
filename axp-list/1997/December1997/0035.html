> anyone know off-hand if it is legal to download the
> ftp tree from a redhat mirror and burn it to cd and
> sell it for profit?
>
> thanks
> nick

Not only is it legal, but under the GPL (which much or Linux falls under)
it is encouraged. Note, however, that you may *NOT* distribute on CD the
items in the non-free directory. Everything under the redhat-5.0 directory
(and contrib, I believe) is fair game, though, as it contains none of the
commercial software on the RedHat boxed set version.
