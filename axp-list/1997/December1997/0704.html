> Also, the Digital Unix compilers and assemblers perform code scheduling.

> say it) NT, the NT assembler is a Digital Assembler with scheduling, but

Remember the MIPS assembler and let it be a lesson -- smart assemblers
are annoying. Better that the compiler know about these things. Anyone
writing assembly themselves doesn't want the assembler getting in the way.
