On Fri, 19 Dec 1997, Dominik Kubla wrote:

> Erik, could you please explain why RedHat does not use the 2.3.2 version
> of pppd? I just compiled it on a fresh RH5.0 and it worked (apart from
> the infamous routing problem...) after i changed all occurances of
> "#include <linux/[...]>" to "#include <net/[...]>". Ah and i had to rename
> "#include <linux/if_ether.h>" to "#include <net/ethernet.h>".

1) It wasn't available when we shupped 5.0
2) As a general rule, we don't change to new versions of packages when
bugs need to be fixed. There are exceptions to this, but ppp didn't
warrant recommending a new vesrion. Remember that the Intel platform
did not need a ppp bug fix at all.
