On Tue, 16 Dec 1997, Andrew J. Scott wrote:

> Mostly from curiosity, the udb isn't that slow, you should see how long it
> takes to compile on '386sx/16 with 8 megs of ram and 32 meg swap! It will
> compile, but I used to do it overnight.

Try a 386SX/12 w/ 4mb + 16 swap... Yes, you read correctly 12,
offbrand clone, 12mhz, 8 bit slots only and 120ns sipps. :)
even the old (small) kernels (pre 1.0, the machine was thrown out around
.99pl12) took a full day to compile. Now that was a slow machine.

Getting back on topic, the UDB is not nearly that slow, I replaced
12 486s with 2 UDBs. They may have a slow memory bus, but they are very
nice little machines.
