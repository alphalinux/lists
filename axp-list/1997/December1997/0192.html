On Sat, 6 Dec 1997, Joakim Roubert wrote:

> Hi!
>
> When using a 2.1-kernel, I can use my UART at full speed, 57600, and not
^^^^^^^^^^
> 38400, which is the highest for me working rate in 2.0.30.
>
> The problem is, when connecting to my ISP and switching info, I get this
> message:
>
> Dec 6 02:14:17 octavia pppd[187]: ioctl(SIOCADDRT) device route: Operation
> not supported by device
>
> It also appears a sign about this when booting. What is it, and how can I
> solve it?

The 2.1 kernels are a bit pickier on how routing should be done, e.g.
device routing doesn't work anymore:

route add default dev eth0

fails on 2.1.

IIRC, all you have to do is recompile the ppp srpm.
