stable. I'm experiencing intermittent errors during compilation of for
example a kernel. Sometimes gcc exits with an internal error. (2)

and start some heavy copying the SCSI bus locks up and the system is
dead (kernel message: ncr died ?) With a cdrom connected I experience

this system.

Anyone familiar with these problems ?
