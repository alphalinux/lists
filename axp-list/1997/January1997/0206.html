That is the one. I believe the most recent one is
relative to gas 961211.

Alternately, I understand HJ Liu included the patch
in binutils 2.7.0.5 released earlier this week, though
I havn't seen or compiled it myself.

I'll repeat the warning in the original beta announcement:

There was a bug in the old assembler that was exposed by
the new linker that affects all local symbol relocations.
You must recompile libgcc.a, crt*.o, and any other static
library or object file you plan on using. Old shared
libraries are fine.
