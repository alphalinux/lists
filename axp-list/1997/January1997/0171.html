I am using the NCR52c8XX driver and have had generally trouble-free
performance. This driver is tunable, and it is possible that the settings
for one of the kernel configuration options (disconnects perhaps) may be a
contributing factor. How is your cabling and termination (active is
recommended)?

I did have some problems at one time with a defective CDROM drive and
with some bad cabling that caused similar lockups for me. However, both the
CDROM drive and cable were replaced, and I have had very reliable performance
since.

It could be that the UDB is unusually sensitive to SCSI bus quality.

David
