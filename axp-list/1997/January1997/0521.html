Out of interest, which version of XFree are you running. I had a number of
glitches running XFree-3.1.2, but since I upgraded to XFree-3.2, the display
has been rock solid. This is with a 2M Diamond Stealth 64 Video VRAM in a
266MHz Multia.

It could also be your modeline. You may be close to the upper limit of your
monitor. I have seen similar distortions trying to achieve a high refresh rate,
and backing off seemed to help.
