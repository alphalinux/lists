Please look at the mailing list archives on RedHat.
A few weeks ago I had the exact same
problem, and it was kindly pointed out to me that the pc164.img file on
the RedHat disk does not have built-in support for the Adaptec 2940.
You must make a boot disk with a kernel that supports the 2940 without
having to load a module. After that you can boot and install 4.0
in the normal manner. I suggest you go to your friend's machine,
make a kernel with 2940 support, and put it in your boot floppy. Then
you should be home free.

Daniel
