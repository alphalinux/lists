>A related question: do I need a special cable to connect two of those
>cards directly,
>or is it the same cable as the one used to connect a card to a hub ?

You need a "crossover" cable to directly connect two 100-base-T (or
10-base-T) network cards as opposed to the "straight-through cable"
normally used to connect one of these cards to a hub.

If you are making the cable yourself using the "flat" type wire and
modular connectors the crossover is accomplished by flipping over one of
the connectors before crimping it on so that the wire order is reversed.
