> crypt is not in unistd.h under alpha.

A random sampling of other Unix vendors showed only about half
had crypt in unistd.h, while most had crypt.h as well. As glibc
(like everyone else in this country) is forced to distribute the
crypt functions separately, we decided to keep things clean and
leave it out of unistd.h.

> /tmp/cca081481.o: In function `main':
> filetest.c++(.text+0x8c): undefined reference to `crypt(char const *, char
> const *)'

There are two potential problems here: first your version of crypt.h
doesn't do the C++ extern "C" protection bits, so the external name
of the function is mangled.

Second, did you remember to include -lcrypt on the link line?
