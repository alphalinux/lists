On Tue, 14 Jan 1997, Pablo Manalastas wrote:

> Regarding the compilation of the linux kernel on an Alpha
> Yash wrote:
>
> > grab the kernel source - the latest stable release i know of is:
> > ftp://sunsite.unc.edu/pub/Linux/kernel/v2.0/linux-2.0.27.tar.gz
> >
> > you'll need the patch for axp-diffs. grab:
> > ftp://ftp.azstarnet.com/pub/linux/axp/sys/axp-diffs-2.0.21.gz
> >
> > in /usr/src/linux, do a:
> > patch -p1 < axp-diffs-2.0.21
> >
> > do a make config;make dep;make clean;make boot;
> >
> > yash
>
> In addition to the above, in the case of a Jensen alpha, the build
> fails because of the undefined symbol 'asmlinkage' in /usr/src/linux/
> arch/alpha/kernel/bios32.c. I remedied this problem by adding the line
> #include <linux/linkage.h> somewhere in bios32.c.
>
> I got only warning messages, such as 'this variable not initialized',
> and 'wrong integer type', or some similar warnings. The generated
> kernel works properly though.
>
