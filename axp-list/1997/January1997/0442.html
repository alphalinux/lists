At the time I upgraded, I pulled down the SRPMS, except where it was obvious
that no changes were made in the source (same release number), and rebuilt all
I could. I did pull all the libraries, binutils, NetKit, ld, gcc. etc, as
binary RPMs and installed prior to rebuilding other packages. My observation
was that almost all the X apps compiled fine, but I had problems with many
other packages. Some big packages such as Emacs and Tetex compiled fine as
well. I did notice that many packages were still *.axp.rpm instead of the new
*.alpha.rpm. I mainly had problems with these. I wonder if these were even
rebuilt under the new libc.

I finally pulled the entire 4.1 release down.
