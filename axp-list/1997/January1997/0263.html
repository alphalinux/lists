I have a 233 UDB running redhat 4.0. It has the 2 12 meg digital simms
that came with it and 2 32 meg simms that I bought for it. The 32 meg
simms are parity simms. It seems to work fine, for a while. When the UDB
has been running for a while, it starts scrolling errors accrossing the
console and the machine is gone after that. I can't get the exact address
cause it goes pretty fast, but all these problems go away when I move the
12 meg simms from the first 2 simm slots to the second 2 slots and put the
32 meg simms first. Everything works fine then, only I have only 80 meg
instead of 88.

Has anyone had this problem too? Maybe this is a problem with the weird
digital 12 meg simms. Or could it be the 32 meg simms are parity and not
ECC? If anyone has an idea I am open to it. I have tried reseating the
simms, switching the order, and other things that have nothing to do with
simms with no success. Any clues lets hear them.

kevin
