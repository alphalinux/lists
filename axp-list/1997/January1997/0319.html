I've been having problems with my UDB and external 1.2GB Quantum Fireball.
Recently it's spontaneously decided to do a SCSI reset, but nothing ever
happens after that. This has happened a few times, somewhat
unpredictably, most recently while fscking after the last time it crashed.
Sometimes (and possibly related), it decides that it will accept
incoming telnet sessions, and send out /etc/issue.net, but never display
the login prompt. (slogin in doesn't work in these situations.) And the
load average goes sky-high, and opening a new xterm crashes the machine
hard. (Or at least the X server, which just freezes.)

Does anyone have any idea what may be wrong? The system stayed up for
about two weeks over break, and in the last few days it's decided to be
quite obnoxious.

Thanks for any help, or even just for reading this far.
