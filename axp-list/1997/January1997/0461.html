>What video card do you have?

The video card is a DEC 8-plane TGA, aka ZLXp-E1, which is supposed to work
(and does) under Linux (which has lots of choices for video) and OSF (which has
very few video alternatives that are not expensive). One hassle with this card
is that the OSF TGA driver only supports 8 bit servers (even though the card
could do more, go figure).
