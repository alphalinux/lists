http://www.brouhaha.com/~eric/computers/udb.html

* Documentation
* The UDB only comes with a few sheets of drool-proof paper showing
* how to plug in the cables. More technical information is available
* in the 116-page manual "Multia MultiClient Desktop Service Information",
* DEC part number EK-MULTS-IN.C01. This manual is fairly pricey (about $85
* in the US).

* Fortunately it is now available in machine-readable form these have
* identical contents. Please don't download more than one. It would
* just be a waste of your time.

* 1. Adobe PDF format for Acrobat, 2.3 megabytes << available for
download
* 2. gzipped PostScript, 5 megabytes << available for
download
* 3. PostScript in a ZIP file, 5 megabytes << available for
download

I printed out the ALPHA pages from the PDF version (which also includes
Intel info.)

Contents Include:

System overview
Troubleshooting
Console Commands (Alpha)
Removing & replacing FRUs
Restoring Multia Desktops to initial software state
Part numbers and pinouts

Keep in mind that this is a hardware service manual and not a
LINUX/UNIX/software guide...
