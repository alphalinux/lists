who says you're stuck with the FAT? If you load fmu.gz instead of your
favorite linux kernel, you'll get the flash management utility. Flash
that happy MILO image into your PROM, and viola, no more FAT.

within MILO (type "setenv" for all the juicy stuff
you can do) you can even get it to auto-boot for
you. this _is_ assuming you have a flash. :)
