> i'm trying to compile kernel v2.0.27 (fetched from sunsite.unc.edu)
> in order to make use of features not included in the kernel
> accompanying the rhl/axp v4.0 distribution. as i understand the
> issue, glibc is required for compiling the kernel. i fetched
> glibc v1.99 along with glibc-crypt and glibc-linuxthreads from
> ftp.redhat.com and placed the latter two in the glibc directory.

For what it's worth, I have compiled and am using kernel 2.0.27
on rhl/axp 4.0. You need the 2.0.27 sources, patched with
axp-diffs-2.0.21. After patching you must copy/move the files
that were not previously in the distribution but were created
by the patch program in the base directory, to their proper
location. Then you can do the usual make:
make mrproper
make dep
make clean
make
make boot
make modules
make modules_install
And I must say that this kernel has been working stably for several
days now. I have not remade glibc or anything, but I have installed
some of the recommended new rpm's from the errata list at Red Hat
http://www.redhat.com/support/docs/rhl/rh40-errata-general.html
