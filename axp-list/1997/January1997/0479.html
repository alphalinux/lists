> "conversion from pointer to integer of different type."
^^^^ size?

> I guess this has to do with the fact that Alpha's pointers are
> 8 bytes but ints are 4 bytes only. Can I safely ignore these
> warnings (my machine is only 96MB anyway) or must I correct
> the ints to longs?

Depending on what is going on, this can mean you are loosing the
top 64-bits of your pointer, or that someone was trying to be
clever and not use a union and packed ints into a void* and is
not unpacking them again.

The fact that you only have 96M is immaterial. All programs are
mapped beginning at 0x120000000 in order to detect 32/64 bit
problems right away.
