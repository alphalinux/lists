Since the local bookstore I normally get all my Linux-related stuff from
was unable to get _any_ Redhat product for weeks back in November, I also
decided to order the Yggdrasil package. I've ordered the product by mail,
they sent it by "Global Priority Mail" (whatever the heck that is...) and
I got the package ONLY FIVE DAYS AFTER I'D PLACED MY ORDER! (please note
that I live in Europe and Yggdrasil is a US company!)

I really wish Redhat would have a Sales Division as good as that... :-(

The following packages are not included in the Yggdrasil CD-ROM. (Please
note that Yggdrasil excludes _any_ package that is not explicitely
declared as "free of charge". So you can get all these packages by FTP
from any Redhat mirror.)

Here is the list of files missing from the Redhat/Alpha Distribution:

getty_ps, ncftp, doom, procmail, rxvt, seyon, spice, xanim, xearth, xfig,
xgalaga

On the other hand, you also get all the Linux/Alpha related stuff from
azstarnet with that CD too.

hoping to have more luck in getting the next release of
Redhat Linux/AXP directly from Redhat...

Christian

BTW: I did a NFS install on my UDB using the Yggdrasil CD-ROM. Everything
