Haven't seen this behavior yet, but pine on the alpha is known to have
some trouble not seen in the Intel/SPARC platforms.

(It seems to have real trouble with child processes - often the editor
for new or replied mail will hang or the sendmail thats trying to actually
send the mail will go zombie.) Just viewing mail and other actions that
don't involve child processes seem to work fine.

Since this didn't happen in pine 3.93 (which was shipped with Red Hat 3.0.3),
my guess is either 3.95 has a 64 bit problem or something with the glibc
library on the alpha. (I am NOT necessarily indicating the library is at
fault, merely that some difference btwn it and the libc used on intel/SPARC
is causing pine trouble.)

(If theres a pine hacker out there that could give me a hint or two where
the trouble might be, I have all the sources but haven't had time to paw
through them yet.)
