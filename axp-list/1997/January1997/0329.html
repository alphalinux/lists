Well, I've recompiled a new kernel with just some minimum components and
found out that the main mistake was that I had put in 10 Mb/sec
transferrate. After setting this to 5 Mb/sec and sync negotiation on,
the whole system runs stable now. Even with CD-ROM and jaz drive
attached. Moved massively files from and to the jaz without hickups.
After this I dared to compile 2.1.20 and it seems to run fine, sound and
networking, SCSI etc is OK now!

Does anybody know if this 'old' 53c7xx,53c8xx' driver is still needed
anyway, otherwise it might be a good idea to remove it completely from
the kernel tree....
