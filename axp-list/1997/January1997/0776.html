There are a number of vendors that are selling UDB's. Typical price for a
166MHz VX-40 w/24M memory + 340M disk is about $500. I have seen the 233MHz
VX-42s w/24M memory + 540M disk for around $800.

A good place to find vendors selling these machines are the following
newsgroups:
comp.sys.dec
misc.forsale.computers.workstation

You may also want to check your local *forsale* newsgroups.

Most of the vendors seem to be in the surplus market, rather than established
DEC Alpha dealers. As such, you may get limited post-sale technical support.
I purchased a VX-40 in September '96 from StarShip Computer Guys through
www.onsale.com, who have been blowing these things out (now without memory or
HD). Although the problems I had initially were resolved to my satisfaction
(I was shipped a defective machine), I cannot really recommend them except as a
source of last resort. I recently purchaced a VX-42 from DCG Computers
(www.dcginc.com), a well established Alpha vendor, and was happy with the
purchase and support.

Good Luck

David
