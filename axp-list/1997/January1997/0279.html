On both the VX40 and the VX42s there is in fact an onboard IDE controller
just before the floppy and hard drive mounting bracket. We used it and it
does work. However, finding a IDE disk with a cable at one time was a large
pain in the $#@ so we never went further.

FYI, have to build a kernel and milo for the system to see it.
