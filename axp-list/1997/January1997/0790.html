> I get to the console and boot from the floppy. Tells me this Milo is built
> for 2.0.18. I quess this is correct. I'm assuming Redhat put compatible
> Milo and Kernels on the CD-ROM. At the milo prompt, I place the boot floppy
> in and boot it. Milo responds with VFS: Disk change detected on device 2/0

I think at this point you need to boot like this

boot sda1:vmlinux.gz root=/dev/sda1

If /dev/sda1 was your root partition and also where you had your kernel.
You can do a "ls sda1" to see what files are present as well.

In any event, you might be able to boot your RH 3.0.3 system this way.
