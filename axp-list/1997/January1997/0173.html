On Tue, 07 Jan 1997 00:46:21 +0100 Gert van der Knokke <gertk@xs4all.nl> wrote:

>Als the SCSI system looks flaky, when I connect a jaz drive to the UDB
>and start some heavy copying the SCSI bus locks up and the system is
>dead (kernel message: ncr died ?) With a cdrom connected I experience
>these problems seldom but SCSI bus lockups do occur somtimes then.

>Anyone familiar with these problems ?

It sounds like my Alpine. Intense disk activity would cause
the "ncr died" message or "SCSI reset" and it might or might not recover.
Adding an external drive to the chain improved the situation, so I replaced
the passive terminator with an active one. That appears to have fixed it.

Hope this helps,
