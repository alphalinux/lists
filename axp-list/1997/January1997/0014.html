> The most likely reason is that your SCSI bus termination is not really
> very healthy. I am guessing here, but I have seen too many problems
> caused by that not to suspect this. These Alphas are really sensitive
> on problems in that area. Active termination may help. Better and
> shorted cables inside, good connectors are also important.

Well, I double-checked the connections and termination settings, and they
seem to be ok:

The Adaptec 2940UW is set to automatic termination, and I have only
internal devices: Properly terminated wide disk, and narrow cdrom
and the Exabyte, with termination only on the Exabyte (at the end of
that chain).

Besides, I get no problems reading the cd or from general disk activity,
only from the tape.

I am in the process of installing the 2.0.27 kernel and RedHat 4.0, and
will have something new to tell after that (hopefully it will be better
news!).
