I don't think this really applies to the AXP version of RH :) In the Intel
version, you get such things as Red Baron, Grail, and Metro-X which come
with licenses of their own (as opposed to being covered under the GPL).
None of these have yet been ported to Linux/AXP, however. Sooooo...about
all the official RedHat version means for RedHat is that you can get
support direct from RedHat, and you get a warm fuzzy feeling -- knowing
that you supported RedHat Linux ;)
