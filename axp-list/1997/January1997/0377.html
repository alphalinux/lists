>>> David Bernstein - Account expires 8/31/97 said:
> I'm trying to install a new kernel on my 200 4/233. Compiling
> the kernel went fine and now I need to build a new MILO to boot it
> (the old one wouldn't do it). Is there a howto or other source I can look
> at? (Sorry but the Redhat manual that came with the 4.0 distribution

Don't bother to build it, just grab the appropriate one from:

gatekeeper.dec.com:pub/DEC/Linux-Alpha/Miniloader/V2.0/2.0.27/XL/milo

(Avanti) and AS250/255.
