Howie,

To build an application from the RPM, I do the following:

rpm -i <pkg.src.rpm>

unpacks the src.rpm under /usr/src/redhat/, sources and
patches go in /usr/src/redhat/SOURCES and .spec files go in
/usr/src/redhat/SPECS

rpm -bb /usr/src/redhat/SPECS/pkg.spec

builds the package and puts a axp.rpm under
/usr/src/redhat/RPMS/axp/ (on my machine)

rpm -i /usr/src/redhat/RPMS/axp/pkg.axp.rpm

installs the package; yay!

There's probably easier ways to do the same stuff, but this works well
for me.
