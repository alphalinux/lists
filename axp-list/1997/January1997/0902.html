>Depending on the model they may or may not come with SRM, the Digital UNIX

This is not exactly true, for Digital UNIX your suppose to have the proper
parts as follows:

The following is supported for NT and Linux only.

21A04-B0 PC164 Motherb.
21164-P4 300 Mhz
21164-P5 366 MHz
21164-P6 433 MHz
21164-P7 500 MHz


This motherboard comes with the SRM and the processors listed are the ONLY
ones supported for DEC UNIX.

21A04-B2 PC164 Motherb. with SRM and 2 user base license.
21164-DB 333 Mhz
21164-EB 366 MHz
21164-FB 400 MHz
21164-HB 433 MHz
21164-IB 466 MHz
21164-JB 500 MHz

Digital has different pricing for the NT/Linux mbs vs. the UNIX
motherboards. It USE to be a hugh price difference, however, someone within
DEC saw the light and made a major drop.

Therefore, if you want the proper supported parts you need whats listed
above and you'll receive the 2 user base license with it. Keep in mind the
two user base license USE to have a list at $3400.00.

So if you need Digital UNIX my suggestion is order the proper parts and
avoid any problems later.
