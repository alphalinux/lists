On Sun, 26 Jan 1997, Gregory Boduch wrote:
> I'm looking for information on where to buy a high-end, studio-quality
> sound board (with S/PDIF or AES/EBU digital I/O) that is supported under
> Alpha/Linux. Please direct me to the information. Digital's pre-sales
> support people are clueless on the subject of digital I/O.

Linux/Alpha uses the same soundriver (OSS, http://www.4front-tech.com/)
as Linux/x86 so most cards that works on intel machines have a good
chance of working on alphas also. I've never heard of any supported
card that has digital io, but you might have 2 chances. I know
several ppl that has soldered directly onto UltraSound cards to use
an exernal dac. Or I think the awe64 _pro_ that will be released soon
will have digital io, and if you are lucky the oss drivers supports that.
