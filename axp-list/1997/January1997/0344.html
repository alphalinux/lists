On Mon, 13 Jan 1997, dayv wrote:

> I asked one before but didn't get any replies.. Has anyone gotten
> ircd to work under RedHat/axp? It compiles clean (as clean as the intel
> version) but when clients try to connect to it, it just sits there (it
> makes the first connection and moves the cursor to the bottom of the
> screen) but from there you could wait up to a good 10 minutes before you
> actually are on the server. anyone got any ideas?

I got it to compile under Linux 2.1.18 by adding '-Wall' to the CFLAGS= in
the Makefile and then going and fixing the resulting typecast errors.
Most of the problems are network functions where the there are numbers
defined as int but should in fact be size_t. The other big catch is that
there is already a variable named local in the include files so I changed
all references to local to say irc_local.

I did this for version 2.9.2. If you want the changed source and want to
test it (I didn't feel like throwing together an ircd.conf), send me
e-mail.
