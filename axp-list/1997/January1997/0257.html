1. Did you link linux to linux-2.1.20?

2. Did you check the symlinks from /usr/include/asm to
./src/linux/include/asm-alpha, /usr/include/linux to
./src/linux/include/linux, and /usr/include/scsi to ../src/linux/include/scsi

3. make config for your hardware, make dep, make clean, make boot

4. Do you have glibc-1.99 installed? (required for kernels above 2.1.14)
