>A related question: do I need a special cable to connect two of those cards
directly,
>or is it the same cable as the one used to connect a card to a hub ?

You need a "crossover cable" for direct connect. pinouts are a full null,
that is they are:
1--------3 TX+ to RX+
2--------6 TX- to RX-
3--------1 RX+ to TX+
4 nc 4
5 nc 5
6--------2 RX- to TX-
7 nc 7
8 nc 8

Use Category 5 100-ohm unshielded or screened (ScTP) twisted-pair UTP office
cable ( 8 conductor, 4-twisted pair).
