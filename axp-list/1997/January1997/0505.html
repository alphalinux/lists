Yes, use Qlogic, Adaptec or another controller (most cases NCR based) which
the Digital ARC supports. We will be releasing a new NCR controller using
the NCR 53C875 chip which is UltraWide. The good news is its supported
under Linux, NT and Digital UNIX.

FYI:Digital does not at this time have a UltraWide controller that runs DEC
UNIX, this does.
