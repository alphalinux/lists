> I hope this is the right place to post this. I have 2 of the UDB 166 models
> with PCI slots. I want to install a video card that can serve me better than
> the integrated TGA.
> What are my options given the limited space available? Does anyone have a
> preference and why? I want to run the X-server if this makes a difference.

My preference is the builtin ZLXp-E1 video. It gives you a very
high-performance, high-quality display at high refresh/resolution, and 256
colors. The 21030 chip can do e.g. hardware dithering, for truecolor 8bit
displays, and also hardware scanline color interpolation, for some fast gouraud
shading. I want to start working on a X server that would make some justice to
the abilities of the 21030 chip. (That would be a True-color X server).

I don't want to spend the precious PCI slot on some stupid PC VGA peripheral.
And TGA is faster at 2-D than Matrox Millennium :)
