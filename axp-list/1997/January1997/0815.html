On 26 Jan 97 at 23:50, traveler@Neosoft.Com wrote:

> Question 2.) Under my Slackware for Intel I have the nice little colour-ls -
> Can anyone tell me what I need to do to get it going on my Alpha?

This one I feel confident answering: (and I know this is in a RedHat
FAQ somewheres...)

'ls --color=tty' does the trick. So.. add a line to /etc/bashrc:

alias ls="ls --color==tty -F"

BTW: I just the the '-F' option... :) This is one of the first
things I do whenever I install RedHat...
