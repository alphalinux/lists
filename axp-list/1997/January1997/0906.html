> - how do I configure the mmeserver to use the MSS in the Multia ?

No idea how to do this under DU; it *requires* iobase 0x530, irq 9, dma 3,
in order to work, as these are hardwired into the motherboard.

> - how do I change the screenmode for X ? I'd like 1280x1024 instead of
> 1024x768

Well, there's a set of 4 jumpers on the motherboard that correspond to the
4 DIP switches that are on a TGA card. If you have the DU docs on the TGA,
it should tell you which switches select which resolution/refresh rate.

Now the jumpers on the motherboard: with the CPU chip at the top left of
the board, look at the lower middle, and just above the two rows of 4 VRAM
chips is a set of 4 jumpers (with bit values assigned) arranged like this:

8 o-o o-o 1
4 o-o o-o 2

They are probably set to 6, right? Well, here's your choices:

TGA Switch Settings and Monitor Values
======================================

switch pixel monitor refresh
setting freq (MHz) resolution rate (Hz)
--------------------------------------------------------

0 130 1280x1024 72
1 119 1280x1024 66
2 108 1280x1024 60
3 104 1152x900 72
4 93 1152x900 66
5 75 1024x768 70
6 74 1024x768 72
7 69 1024x864 60
8 65 1024x768 60
9 50 800x600 72
10 40 800x600 60
11 32 640x480 72
12 25 640x480 60

Make *sure* your monitor is capable of your selection!!!

Good luck.
