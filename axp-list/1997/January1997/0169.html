> Als the SCSI system looks flaky, when I connect a jaz drive to the UDB

> Should I suspect my memory expansion (I used 2 8Mb simms from my P100

It sounds like you are being bitten by the NCR53C7,8 driver! I spent
most of last Saturday rebooting a locked system while attempting to
build glibc. In frustration, I downloaded the latest version of the
FreeBSD driver port. Bingo. Absolutely _no_ problems since :-).

The configuration options for the NCR family of controllers are
confusing, at best. Be sure that you install only the NCR53C80XX
driver, configured for:

Tagged Queueing
IO-Mapped I/O
Forced Sync Negotiation
5-MHz Transfer Rate

Good luck, and you'll find the driver at

ftp://tsx-11.mit.edu/pub/linux/ALPHA/scsi
