Following the hints from this list, I got pcmcia-2.8.23 compiled and
installed under RedHat 4.0 w/ 2.0.21. It kind of worked but not
without problems.

1) My Grid 2M SRAM card works fine. I can now backup my Zoomer
easily. BTW, does anyone know how do I get the card recognized
under DOS (actually GEOS?)

2) A Thomas Conrad 5411 10baseT card (I paid $25 new at a liquidation
a couple of years ago) worked fine on an Intel laptop, but failed on
my UDB. (problem loading modules?)

3) The default pcmcia script behaves weirdly. I have to disable it at
boot time, otherwise the UDB ethernet doesn't get setup. However,
pcmcia start works just fine.
