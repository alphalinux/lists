> For Red Hat 4.0 it is much more of a pain in the posterior, because 4.0/Alpha
> doesn't come with module support, and the PCMCIA package won't build properly.

I do not know about building PCMCIA package (I did not try, but I do not
expect real problems there), but I know that "4.0 doesn't come with module
support" is simply not true. I used it extensively on UDB and PC164.
Admittedly there is a trick to it. One has to do 'strip --strip-debug'
on module objects before insmod will load them. Yes, this is a bug but not
a "show-stopper". Add that action to your Makefile on 'modules_install'
target and you are set.

Why would anyone want to use 2.0.18 with problems when 2.0.28 is out is
another question. :-)
