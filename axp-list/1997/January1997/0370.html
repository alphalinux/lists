> 1. i'd like to print on a HP LaserJet4
> (queue_on_hp) connected to a Netware network.
> what do i need to download to make this work?

If that HP is on the network via a HP JetDirect card, give the printer
an IP address and use the standard Unix lpd remote printer protocols to
talk to it. HP printers are multilingual.

It will be a lot less painfull than trying to get IPX and the appropiate
netware support programs running on the AXP i would guess.
