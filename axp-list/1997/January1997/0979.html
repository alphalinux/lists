ftp.cs.indiana.edu and I couldn't find a C-code version of a scheme
interpreter or compiler, at ALL.

in C/C++, and then finish it in scheme b/c C/C++ is more widely used under
UNIX) but I'm looking to get scheme for my UDB (taking a class this
semester, and part of it is to learn/use scheme heavily).
