Instead of fighting with Netscape I gave a try to grail (found on Python
sites). It is slooow on a low end UDB, but actually not bad at all on
275 MHz Alpha (EV4).

with X which does run Netscape, then there is another option. It is
not hard to arrange things that way, that you click on an icon on
your monitor and remote Netscape opens its window on your display.
If a network and other host are not overloaded this works rather well. :-)
