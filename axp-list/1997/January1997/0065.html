I am using a Diamond Stealth 64 Video VRAM (3240, S3-968) with 2M VRAM
successfully in a 233MHz UDB. It was recognized immediately by the hardware.
I have had great success with the XFree 3.2 S3 server, running at
1024x768X16bbp. I am getting unaligned traps, but far less than with the 3.1.2
server, and have not had a server crash under XFree 3.2.
