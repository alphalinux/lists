At 10:05 hrs. 01-06-97 -0600, you wrote:
>
>Simple question, does Red Hat 4.0 for alpha's support the 2940UW card? Or
>should I just get a Buslogic 958?
Either card works. The Buslogic is probably a better bet, as Adaptec has a
nasty habit of changing their hardware without telling people. Also
performance has been less than stellar IMHO. You amy also want to consider
some of the new careds using the NCR 53C875 chip. This is a wide/ultra
implemantation of the familiar NCR product, which is very well supported
under Linux/Alpha. ASUS variant of this costs under $170 and works with the
53c8xx drivers (but not the 53c7xx/8xx driver).
All three sport fairly similar performance, all need minor configuration and
option setting in kernel build for use... Kernels and Milo that work are
avaiable at Digital site:
ftp://gatekeeper.dec.com/pub/DEC/Linux-Alpha/
