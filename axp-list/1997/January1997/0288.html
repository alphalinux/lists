On Fri, 10 Jan 1997, Liam Irish (UND) wrote:

> I am working on putting a mud on my multia, has this been done yet? Does
> anyone have some good porting advice, I've read the FAQ, but maybe someone
> has some specific pitfalls not mentioned in it that they can enlighten me
> about. From my testing on diku, it seems to be mostly pointer and int
> size problems, and some stuff that is different in the muds files and

MudOS was been ported to the Linux/Alpha by Beek. Use version v22.1a13
or up. See http://www.imaginary.com/~beek/mudos (I think ;)

It's on ftp.imaginary.com and mirrors in /pub/LPC/servers/MudOS.

In fact, porting is a big word. It only needed to figure out you have
a machine that has __linux as well as __alpha defined. It was already
64 bit clean.
