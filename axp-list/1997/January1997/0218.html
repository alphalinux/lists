I would definitely do a "complete" make, i.e. mrproper, config, dep, clean,
make, modules, modules_install, boot.

Also, make sure you have all the libraries installed, as well as the proper
links for the header files (/usr/include/asm, /usr/include/scsi,
/usr/include/linux) pointing to your /usr/src/linux devel. tree.
