At 11:14 hrs. 01-05-97 -0500, you wrote:
>hi folks,
>
>i'm curious about ther performance of the internal udb scsi bus...
>could i put in an adaptec scsi card with pci bus (i'm thinking
>about using the aha2940)?
>
>is this theoretically possible, or has someone had sucess with
>this?
You could, but it would not be any faster than the NCR53C810 chip that's
already there. If you want Wide/Ultra you might try the 2940UW or the
BusLogic BT958C or one of many cards using the NCR53C875 chip. They work,
with appropriate kernel and Milo builds.
You will probably gain more performance with more memory if your machine is
under 48MB, tho'. A good fast disk helps _lots_ too. It's not the SCSI
interface that is slow in the UDB, just the disk...
