You must have glibc-1.93, which has /usr/include/timespec.h, the content
of which is the linux/timespecbits.h dependency. It appears that timespec.h is
not in glibc-1.99 (the most recent glibc for AXP), so I suspect that the latest
line of development kernels require glibc-1.99 to compile on Alpha. Just a
guess on my part, I am running 2.0.27 myself (which compiles fine with
glibc-1.93).
