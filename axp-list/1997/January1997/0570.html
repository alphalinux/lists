On saturday, Februari 1st, the HCC UNIX and C ug will be having a meeting
again.

The meeting will take place at the Zalen Centrum St. Jan, Dorpstraat 58,
Vreeswijk (Nieuwegein), The Netherlands.

The meeting will start at 10.30 hours

Theme of this meeting will be Linux/Alpha.

Several Linux Alpha systems will be present during the meeting, and also the
possibility to install Linux on Digital Alpha Multia systems.

At 12 o'clock a lecture will be held, with Linux/Alpha as subject, by Wim
van Dorst:

Being a user of Minix and Linux for quite some years, Wim van
Dorst will give a presentation of his more recent experience
with Linux on his Alpha. In the lecture, several Linux/Alpha
points will be presented, such as:

- Which Alphas are available? How fast are they? What do they cost?
- What is the advantage of a 64-bits environments?
- In which phase is the Linux/Alpha development? Which software
is available?

Basically it will be Wims personal experience, with additional
general information directly off the Internet.

The meeting will end at 16.00 hours
