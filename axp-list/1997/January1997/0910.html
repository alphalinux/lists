I noticed a new problem while upgrading to 2.0.28:
when I do lots of I/O, I get the message

Whoops: async_io complete on unlocked page

Specifically, I have a PC164 with '8xx SCSI controller,
two disks. I copied a large file (about 200MB) from sda2 to sdb2.
Another time, I copied something smaller but another task was
writing on sda2 at the same time. These messages are very difficult
to reproduce.

cannot be accessed without a reboot, and the disks can't be
unmounted, remounted readonly or anything.
