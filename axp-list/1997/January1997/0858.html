I have found similar problems between f2c+gcc and DEC f77, that
DEC f77 is 2x as fast as the converted code.
I believe that there is something terribly slow in the
libf77 that comes with f2c, because people in our group
have benchmarked gcc vs DEC cc on numerical C-codes, and found
only a 10-20% slowdown with gcc.

Eagerly awaiting g77 version 0.5.20, which should have Alpha
support...
