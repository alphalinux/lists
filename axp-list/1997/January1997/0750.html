Another nice drawing program is Tgif, which you can get at

http://bourbon.cs.columbia.edu:8001/tgif/

Last time I checked the source was not 64-bit clean, i.e., there were
some problems with integer->pointer conversion. They were all flagged
by the compiler, though, and were easy to fix. As I recall, the main
culprit was missing malloc declarations.
