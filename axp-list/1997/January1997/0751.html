On Sat, 25 Jan 1997, Tony Scalzitti wrote:

> I only just put on afterstep, and not having time to look at it any deeper,
> but my xterm comes up "blank" or maybe black on black?
>
> Has anyone seen this before?

You've probably run out of colors --what color depth are you running at?
The purty icons grabbed up all your colormap real estate befor your xterm
got a chance to start, so there was nothing left for its foreground color.
If you've got a very good memory and faultless typing, you can use the
text-less xterm just fine, but I'd recommend that you either increase
your default colordepth or make sure the colors that your xterm wants to
use are the same as some in the piggish icons.
