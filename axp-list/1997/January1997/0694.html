Robert Williams <bob@bob.usuf2.usuhs.mil> wrote:
> PCI.
> It appears now that the PC164 ARC Console shows SCSI device 7
> (the card) has IRQ 4, but a check of the card on an Intel system
> shows that it is set to IRQ 9.

PCI cards don't have fixed IRQ assignments; the assignment is
platform-specific. If ARC shows it at IRQ 4, that is really where the
PC164 hardware and/or firmware put it. The IRQ 9 from the Intel system
is irrelevant, and in fact a different Intel system (or different BIOS
settings on the same system) might show a different IRQ.
