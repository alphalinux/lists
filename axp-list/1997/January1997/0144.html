the signal handler for SIGCHLD doesn't reset itself on POSIX (in this
case, Linux) systems. as a consequence, many zombies result with the
redhat 4.0 cron. curiously, this behaviour just manifested itself with
a recent glibc snapshot. i guess somebody fixed signal behaviour. just
thought people should know about this.
