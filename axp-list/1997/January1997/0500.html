> It seems that there are a couple files missing

'Imake.tmpl' (not 'imake.tmpl') and few other '.tmpl' files, and a pile
of other stuff, are in /usr/X11R6/lib/X11/config; 'locate' would tell
you that in no time at all.

Some particular program could supply its own '.tmpl' files, but most
of them don't.
