Just as a data point, I'm experiencing the same symptoms here. When users
log out from remote hosts using rlogin or ssh (both seem to cause problems),
the utmp does not get updated correctly. It doesn't happen on every login;
looking at the wtmp, it looks like about 50% of logins leave 'ghosts' lying
around.

I haven't noticed any problems with logins from localhost, but that could
be because nobody logs in from localhost.

My install is bog-standard RH 4.0, with most of the updates from
ftp.redhat.com:/pub/redhat/updates, and ssh 1.2.17.
