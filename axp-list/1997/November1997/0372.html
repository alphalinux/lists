>>> "Jim. Shipman" said:
>
> I have a PC164LX with AlphaBIOS installed. I need to change the console to
> SRM. I've got lx164srm.rom and fwupdate, but when I try to update using
> the menu in AlphaBIOS it doesn't seem to work. The system operates the
> floppy drive and then restarts what looks like an older version of
> AlphaBIOS instead of SRM. When I boot again it reverts to the AlphaBIOS
> which was originally installed.

I think all you need to do is rename "fwupdate.exe" to "foo.exe", or
something else, and then the current AlphaBIOS will attempt to update
itself, rather than use that program off the floppy.

After you select the "Update FW" menu item, it should give you a list of
the available images on the floppy; from there, it's just follow the
directions...

Good luck.
