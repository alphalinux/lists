Ronny,

There are various references to Enet cards that work on this list.
Real DEC adapters work. Various tulip clone cards work. I use
4 different brands of tulip cards and all work with the tulip.c
driver. Linksys, Netgear, Bestdata, Cardware labs are the brands
I know work. Anywhere from $40-$75. Check Donald Becker's info
at: http://cesdis.gsfc.nasa.gov/pub/linux/linux.html

Look in the the archives for more info.

Wes
