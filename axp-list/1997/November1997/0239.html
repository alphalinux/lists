On Wed, 12 Nov 1997, Benjamin J. Greslick wrote:

> When the machine is turned on (cold boot) it goes through milo fine, but
> on a warmboot of any type (including the reset button) I get the similar
> MILO screen. I need to be able to do remote re-boots and this does not
> help things at all..
> This serial port problem may have some link to my next EMail problem
> (random system crashes)...

If you're using a stock MILO from gatekeeper, I believe that they are all
compiled with some type of "serial console" support, meaning, they echo
debugging output to the serial ports. The modem is problem answering the
echo'ed stuff and might be causing the problem you describe. I don't have
a modem on my Alpha (or a PC164, for that matter), but I did notice the
serial option when I last compiled my own MILO not too long ago.
