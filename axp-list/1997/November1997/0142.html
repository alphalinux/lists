Andreas Welbers wrote:

> At last I tried a DEC-Card with the TULIP-Chipset and now the systems
> stops while entering runlevel 3 (after sendmail is executed and the
> network card has been set to 10Mbit/s) with an error message!

I run various tulip cards in both PPros and ALphas, and have no
problems with them. I use RH4.2, the standard tulip.c driver (0.76??).

Watch out for DExxx drivers. They attempt to configure tulip cards
but I have not had good luck with those drivers. They are built
into the RH4.2 kernel so I had to remove them and pull in the
tulip.c driver and build a new kernel. (100Mb mode) I believe the
DExxx.c drivers work with real DEC adapters, but tulip.c works
better with clone cards (2114x chipset).

When you solve the YP problems, please post, I plan on using YP
in the next 60 days.
