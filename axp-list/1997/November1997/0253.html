>Has anyone had any problems with the intraserver card and DEC
>storageworks
>drives with numbers ending in "VW" (i.e. RZ29B-VW)? I seem to be
>having a
>problem with these drives not being detected at all in a 1000A drive
>rack,
>while other drives (ending in "VA") and cdroms seem to be working
>fine.

>Any ideas appriciated.

>S.

The -VW drives are fast wide scsi implementation. They are expecting a
wide bus and controller. Most wide drives have jumper settings that tell
them they are on a narrow bus and not to look for traffic down the
expanded bus.
