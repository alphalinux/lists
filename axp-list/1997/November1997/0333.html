Wes wrote
Anyone know of a web site for CMD??

I'd like to find a manual on the 646 to see how it differs from
Triton. My main interest is DMA mode. PIO is just a CPU pig...
Also, even in PIO mode, the CMD646 is slower than the triton
chip, so just fixing that would be worth it.

http://www.cmd.com/graphical/semicondct/prodsemiprodselection.cfm?Class=119&Category=54

They have comprehensive manuals for PCI0646 (Enhanced IDE to PCI bus
master interface chip (5.0 volt)) and PCI0646U/643U (PCI to Ultra ATA
Controller Chip (5.0V and 3.3V)). They require signing NDA to get the
second manual, but the first one, which I believe is used on Alpha
motherboards, is freely available.
