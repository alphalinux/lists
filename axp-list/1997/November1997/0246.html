At 12:25 12/11/97 -0500, Benjamin Greslick wrote:
>
> i have 2 USR modems attached to a DEC ALPHA PC164 500mHz machine with
>RedHat 4.2 loaded with most of the errata patches. I've seen a few posts
>like the following, but I have not seen any fixes posted besides "move the
>modem". Are there any fixes for this?
> When the machine is turned on (cold boot) it goes through milo fine, but
>on a warmboot of any type (including the reset button) I get the similar
>MILO screen. I need to be able to do remote re-boots and this does not
>help things at all..
> This serial port problem may have some link to my next EMail problem
>(random system crashes)...
> -Ben
>
By default Milo is built echoing all console output to the serial port. In
your situation this may result in some Babel babble between modem and Milo.
Solution: build your own Milo with serial port support turned off.
