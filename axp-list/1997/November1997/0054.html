[snip]
>Is their anyone out there that has got the XF86Config for UDB
>with setting for a 1024x768 8-bit display? Can you please email
>it to me, or better yet, to the list. Alot of people could use
>this configuration file, it seems.

I'm running my UDB at 1152x900 (the Sun standard resolution) using a
XFree86 configuation that was posted to the list early this year.
1280x1024 is just too small on a 15" monitor. I've included the
relevant sections below.

Of course, you need to make sure that your monitor supports this resolution
before using this configuration. Virtually all modern multisync monitors
should support it no problem, but don't blame me if it smokes your monitor.

-andy

------snip-----

#1152x900 66Hz 61.79kHz 92.98Mhz
#The standard SUN mode and probably the best choice for the UDB
Modeline "1152x900" 92.98 1152 1240 1416 1504 900 909 927 936

Section "Device"
Identifier "TGA"
VendorName "DEC"
BoardName "ZLXp-E1"
EndSection

Section "Screen"
Driver "accel"
Device "TGA"
Monitor "15sx"
Subsection "Display"
Depth 8
Modes "1152x900"
ViewPort 0 0
EndSubsection
EndSection
