I'm lookig for suggestions on why/how to find out why a DEC Alpha is
crashing.

The previously mentioned DEC Alpha PC164 500MhZ SCSI system is
experiencing random system crashes. It will crash every 2 to 15 days. I
have seen it crash under normal usage (11am on a school day, 20 users
using pine, with a load average of .4) and under almost no usage (2am, 2
users, load average of like .04).
We have one 10M DEC ethernet card in it, and 2 external modems hanging
off COM1 and COM2. I'm running 'mgetty -s 38400 ttyS0' to allow people to
dial in. This machine is also an EMail/WWW/DNS/bootp server for about 1000
users and 300 workstations.
ANY ideas, or suggestions on how to find out what is crashing the
server? the syslog files just end with normal usage (EMail or login),and
the screen is blank (screensaver,I'm changing that now) so no errors are
found. When it locks, everything is locked. no keys will unblank the
screen, and ctrl-alt-delete will NOT work, the machine is not even
pingable.
