For systems not based on the LX motherboard (like the XLT300) you need the
firmware upgrade which corresponds to your system. Digital has a complete
list of system firmware available at the following URL:

ftp://ftp.digital.com/pub/DEC/Alpha/firmware/index.html

Or contact the indivudual from whom you purchased your system for a
firmware update.
