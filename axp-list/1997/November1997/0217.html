On Tue, 11 Nov 1997, nm wrote:

> As far as I can tell, Digital Unix does not officially support
> AT keyboards or serial mice, I however have installed Digital
> Unix on an AXPPCI33 based system that had only an AT keyboard.

Do you really have an AT (84-key) keyboard attached to an Alpha? As far
as I know, they have a different code (with a limited functionality) in
the microcontroller than the newer PS/2 keyboards. Thay have also three
modes of work, of which the third one is used by DU and the AT keyboard
implements only one mode, which is compatible with the second mode of PS/2
keyboards.
