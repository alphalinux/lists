Yue,

I have a 533 PC164LX from Microway and also a PPro 200.
With the latest g77 compiler from the egcs project, my
DSP code is 3.5 times faster on Alphas than PPro.
It is important to use the latest g77 snapshot. With
old versions, the Alpha was about 1.7 times faster than
the PPro. The other choice is to buy a compiler from
Microway or someone else. I was seriously considering
that until the new g77 appeared last week.
