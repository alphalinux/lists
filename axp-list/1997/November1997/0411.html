>So if you don't have any internal device, and you terminate the external

>'810 controller and the smaller (2mm?) connector.

>I'm considering taking my HD out into a case. I wonder if it makes sense

>connector. This way I'd virtually eliminate all stubs on the bus.

WRT the termination of the internal HD, you can get bit on the butt if you
had a non-DEC HD put in. For example I had a 1Gig drive installed and it
came terminated, so anytime I tried to do the Right Thing and terminated
the end of any decent length external chain the system would barf. So you
might wanna check your own drive.

As for the way the internal scheme works, If the internal drive is _not_
terminated, the card is, and the external bus is terminated at the end (if
thats on the back of the machine, so be it). Everything should work fine.
