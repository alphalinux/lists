Roman,

I'm using a snapshot of the egcs group which is attempting to
combine the various GNU compilers into a single package.

They release weekly snapshots of their work. I don't
recommend that you replace your current compiler with what
they have but if you're interested, take the snapshot and
build it in another location and test it out.

http://www.cygnus.com/egcs/

They just added some performance tweaks to the g77 Alpha
implementation and it tripled performance in some of my
tests. Think they finally got the Alpha to start pipeline
operation.

Wes
