I just lost an EIDE disk AGAIN. Decided to upgrade to a decent
scsi subsystem. I'm running intel-linux but am hoping to upgrade to
alpha-linux in the not to distant future i.e.:
DCGINC 533 cpu+motherboard+case for $1025

15 companies announced 164pc machines today btw:
http://www.techweb.com/wire/news/1997/11/1117pc.html
Or:
http://www.digital.com/semiconductor/news/press17nov-a.htm

In any case there are lotsa nice scsi solutions. I want one with
maximum stability, performance without too high a price. I.e. under $300.

My personal favorite is the Buslogic 958, reliable, fast, low cpu usage,
cheap ($220) etc.

Unfortunately I think you can't use this controller as a boot device
under the lx motherboards... correct?

So do I have any choice besides the adaptec 2940? Not that adaptec
is bad. Seems a little slower, less stable, doesn't handle bus resets
as well, more sensitive to external cables etc. Oh and a good
bit more expensive. ($280 vs $220).

Anyone know exactly which controllers will boot on the LX motherboards?
The SX motherboards? (with linux of course)

Any info appreciated.
