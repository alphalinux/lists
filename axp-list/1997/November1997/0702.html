Hi Steve.
Look closely at the crystal on these. To run on Digital UNIX the 3140U
needs an 80MHz crystal. For NT and Linux it needs 40.
This is supposed to change with 4.0d UNIX.
If you look at the 4140 (SCSI/Ether combo) they even have a jumper on the
card to make the switch. So there IS a separate UNIX 3140 (for now).
