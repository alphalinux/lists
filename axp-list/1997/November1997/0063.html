On Sat, 1 Nov 1997 coder@ibm.net wrote:
>
> What resolutions are possible with a UDB's TGA? I'm running 1024x768x256
> at the moment. Can I go higher without dropping the colour depth?

If I remember correctly, the max resolution is 1600x1200 and the
max color depth is 256 colors or 8bits for the standard on board video
card in the UDB. Or at least it is for my 166MHz UDB.
Attached is my XF86Config for 800x600x256, though I was able to go
higher by just uncommenting the appropriate ModeLine. Though with
Digital's TGA server for Linux/Alpha, only one modeline could be
uncommented at a time. That may have changed as I have a rather old (3+
months) version of the Xwindows TGA server. Hope this helps everyone.
