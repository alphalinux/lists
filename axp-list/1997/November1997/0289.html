>>> nm said:
>
> i have a sound blaster 16 and i think i have included
> all the pertinant options/drivers in the sound config
> but here is what happens
>
> Card config:
> (Sound Blaster at 0x220 irq 5 drq 1,5)
> (SB MPU-401 at 0x330 irq 1 drq 0)
> (OPL-2/OPL-3 FM at 0x388 drq 0)

The parentheses indicate that the hardware wasn't found.

Any chance this is a PnP card? If so, review recent postings to axp-list
WRT how to do PnP cards; ie must do a module for the sound drivers, and *not*
load it until *after* you have a chance to run the "isapnptools" app to do
the actual card setup, etc, etc.

If it's *not* A PnP, then it has jumpers; make sure your driver settings
match the jumper settings.

BTW, the settings you show above *are* the normal ones for such cards; I've
a couple I use that way all the time, though I usually choose IRQ 9 or 11
to avoid my internal modem setting at IRQ 5... :-)

Good luck.
