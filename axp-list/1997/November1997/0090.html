* Re: your mailing list problems

* Please read this, and take the relevant action

You appear to have been subscribed to a mailing list without your
knowledge, and do not know how to unsubscribe. Instead you are
bouncing all the mails you receive back to the list.

This is extremely anti-social to the members of the list, who then
have to pay to download all your bounced messages (which we have seen
before). Please do not do this. [In addition, you risk being
reported to your Internet Provider]

You could perhaps have sent a polite message to the list, stating the
problem, and asking for advice on what to do.

You should do the following to be removed from the list:

* Send an e-mail to axp-list-request@redhat.com, with the subject
"unsubscribe" (without the quote marks).
