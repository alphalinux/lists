With regards to your message at 08:26 PM 11-08-97 -0800, Peter S. Halpern.
Where you stated:
>Is there a way to upgrade to 32k colours or better? Possibly with another
>video card?
>
Other video cards are good. MAtrox Mystique or Millenium, S3 based cards
like from Number 9 and Diamond, etc.. Take physical measurements first
because space is limited...

>What is the max external SCSI CD-ROM I can run on this box? The place I
>got it (Starship Computer) says 4x, others say 8x. Is it one of these or
>even higher?
Any speed you want.

>Which modems work on these boxes? I would like to get at least 33k, but a
>k56flex would be preferable.
Again, anything. K56 is a waste of money IMHO.

>Can you put in 60ns memory chips or is the DEC system pretty finicky about
>70ns chips?
Doesn't help. Like putting high-test gasoline in a car that runs fine on
regular.
If buying RAM be careful, it doesn't take garden-variety RAM.
