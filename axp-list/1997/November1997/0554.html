Bewate of the Zip drives they'll fuck your head.

Do you remember my last post? It was about the following SCSI chain:
UDB (internal hdisk ID 0) + external box (hdisk ID 1 + CDROM ID 5) +
ZIP (ID 6). Turning on UDB + ebox it worked, turning on UDB + ZIP also
worked, but turning on UDB + ebox + ZIP didn't work. Extrange and anoying.
You gave me the "right" solutions (thanks everybody): removing termination
from SCSI controler, adding active terminator, ..., but ... what made it
work was just moving CD form ID 5 to ID 4 (Arrrrgh!).
Now the question for the Iomega people: the Zip drive instructions
say you can choose ID 5 or ID 6 for the drive (by changing the switch on
drive's back), the drive can use any of them. But experience says: you can
choose whatever ID 5 or ID 6, the drive will use both. :-(
Is it me or these people spend too much time making stuff for
windo!"·$%& ?
