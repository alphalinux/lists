I have a multa, the 155, 340 meg scsi, 24 mb, etc (original dec
config), under Redhat 4.2 with 2.0.30 + alpha patches. Been up for 102
days now under load. A few 'Couldn't get a free page.....' messages, but
no core dumps, no downtime. In fact other than those few messages
nothing. For a < 500$ box I am very pleased. A pair of em repleced 6
486DX2/66 units.

I must say there is a steeper learning curve with Linux/Alpha
today than with Linux/Intel, but no harder than Linux/Intel was say 2
years ago. I had more trouble with Redhat (a roll it yourself type until
slackware came alone (anyone remember the old SLS distribs? with the non
functional installer? Scared me so much I just rolled my own system
running .99pl12. Now that's when Linux was a challenge :) ) than I did
with learning to deal with an alpha.
