> Hmm.. Not sure what my XLT-300 would be, but its longer than 4 minutes.. The
> point is that it greatly depends on what kernel options you have configured.
>
That's quite true. I.e. on my XLT-300 with my config, 'make boot' takes
7:01 but 'make modules' takes 3:36 more (numbers from one test run,
kernel sources 2.1.63). And on my UDB (233MHz), this takes about
31 minutes (boot) and 18 minutes (modules).

So, it is quite useful to know which parts are compiled as modules
and whether that time is included in the total. And of course,
which parts are included in the first place (a kernel without net,
filesystems or such might be quite quickly made but also quite useless).
