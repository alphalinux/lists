I informed at Request automation services B.V (www.request.nl) in the
Netherlands
(where I live) for a 21164PC chip (533Mhz) and the PC164SX mainboard. The
prices
I got for them were really absurd. They said that the costs were very high
if they
have to order one board + CPU from Digital.

For example:

PC164SX: NLG 2194,- ex. VAT.
21164PC 533Mhz: NLG 2356,- ex. VAT.

The 21164 was even worse:

PC164LX: NLG 3744,- ex. VAT
21164 500Mhz: NLG 1980,- ex. VAT.

The VAT in the Netherlands is 17.5%.
US$ 1,- = (+/-) NLG 2,-

This would be quite an expensive replacement for my UDB ;-)

When are Samsung / DEC going to start shipping their components
(mainbaords & CPU's) for more reasonable prices in Europe? At them
moment it cheaper to buy a whole system at Vobis (PC164SX, 21164PC
533Mhz, 64MB, IDE...) for less than DM 4000,- than to buy the
mainboard and CPU...

Request is also selling XL's. The basic 21164 300Mhz version is priced
at about NLG 3000,- ex. VAT. is this cheap??
