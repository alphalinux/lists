Yes, wide drives should autodetect a narrow bus. However, in Sheer's
case they apparently did not. Autodetection is implemented in both
drives and controllers bios. Also some of DEC's bays are "smart" which
means they have firmware in them. I am not familar with the 1000A or
the RZ29B-VW. However, I remember reading on DEC's web pages
about some bays and controllers not supporting the wide drives. DEC's
pages for StorageWorks (bays, drives, controllers) start at:

http://www.storage.digital.com

Also note that the part number scheme has changed. The RZ29B-VW is
old part # and is replaced by new # DS-RZ1CB-VW. The list for drives is
at

http://www.storage.digital.com/swrks/guide/pg_ddrv.htm
