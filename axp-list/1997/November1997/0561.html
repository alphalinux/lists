I recently e-mailed Zoid (Dave Kirsch) at id software and asked him
very similar questions (Zoid is contracted by id to port their games
to Linux). He said that presently no Linux/Alpha ports are planned
for squake QuakeWorld or Quake II because he doesn't own an Alpha.

This is indeed a Major Total Bummer. I for one do not want to waste
valuable disk space on an NT partition just to play Quake.

Probably the only way we'll see additional Linux/Alpha Quake ports is
if Digital or some other philanthropist entices Zoid by donating some
hardware (hint-hint).
