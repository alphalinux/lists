On Wed, 12 Nov 1997, yue-feng sun wrote:

You seem very confused on what is available. First off, be more
careful with the labels Pentium, PPro and PII (aka Pentium II), they are
all very different, a PII has nothing to do with a Pentuim, it is a PPro
takeoff.

Also, while a Quad Pentium, or PPro is possable, the PII is limited to a
Dual proc system. So, you end up with these options

Quad PPro 200/512K (or 200/1024K if available)
Dual PII 300/512K (PII is limited to 2 CPUs)

Alpha :)

Also, the PII has a feature in its cache module that limits cacheable
memory to 512MB, an issue for servers.

My suggestion is to go get a hot Alpha.
