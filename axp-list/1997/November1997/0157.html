On Sun, 9 Nov 1997, Bryan W. Headley wrote:
> >
> > Which modems work on these boxes? I would like to get at least 33k, but a
> > k56flex would be preferable.
>
> I suppose there's no issue here. Obviously get an external unit. Be aware that
> there's special cabling off of the serial port. Dec decided to wire both ports
> into a single connector, and you have to make a "Y" cable to go to the two
> external devices. Wiring diagrams in the archives.

Actually it is the original standard. The db25 layout allowed for
2 sync/async ports within the same connector. There is a dupe of
everything including transmit and receive clock lines (for sync).

It has been to long since I have read the standards, but if I
remember correctly the EIA232D when later grabbed as rs232 for the PC
world carried over all the signal lines.

Besides, a UDB is not a PC, it is a small workstation. Expecting it to be
exactly like a PC would be silly.
