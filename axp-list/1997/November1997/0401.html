> Have you or anyone else gotten a Matrox Millenium to actually work on a

I have tried a Matrox Mystique 220 with nil success; Stephen Gaudet of DCG
tried a Millennium in a UDB they had in stock with limited success (color maps
were not working well, and it took a long time for the system to boot up, just
to name two issues).

S3V (86c325) provided *limited* success; it would lock up the machine
irretrievably after several minutes in 8 bitplane mode, and shorter times in
16/24 bpp.

I have since installed a Diamond Stealth "Video 3200", using the S3 968
chipset with 2MB of RAM. That has been working quite successfully.

I would be interested in hearing any reports of actual success at using any
chipsets other than the S3 968 with the UDB/Multia; I have heard no
indications of decent results with any other chipsets.
