> My searching for an Alpha is not finished yet. I want to
>know if there is a limit for Adaptec 2940UW or Intraserver
>Ultra SCSI controller to drive a =>9GB hard disk and how many such
>disks (>=9GB) can be attached to the card. Does this depen upon the
>card or drivers on Linux? Sorry for the ingorance on this.

It depends on the SCSI controller. The Adaptec 2940UW can handle up to 15
devices, so you could have up to 15 devices.

Though if youre going to have 15 devices, it might be best to split it up onto
seperate SCSI controllers or get a multiple-PCI bus cards, like the 3940UW
which has 2 wide connectors (two seperate buses), with 15 devices for each
bus.
