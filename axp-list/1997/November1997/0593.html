On Fri, 21 Nov 1997, Brian Lund wrote:

>
> I've been trying to get both a 3c595 and a 3c905 to work in my AXPpci33
> box, without much luck though - it transmits - but doesn't recieve
> anything.
>
> it's connected w/ crossed TP to a linux box with a 3c905, wich sees the
> packages comming from the alpha, and responds to them (after I set the
> HWadd manually with arp -s etc..) but the alpha never sees anything
> comming from the other box.

I would suspect it's something to do with the cable. If you have access
to a cable tester you might try to use that to eliminate the cable as a
possible source of the problem.

You could also try out the network interface cards in known good machines
to eliminate them as well, but since the two cards demonstrate the same
behavior it would tend to point to the cable as being the problem.
