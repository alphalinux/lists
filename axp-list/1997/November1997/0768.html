my 3c509 works - it uses twisted pair, as for me the coax cableing was a
pain, not reliable, and hard to figure out where it went wrong. u do
have to set it up on a i386, and turn off the PNP ( if it has that
feature ), and set an addr that the kern ( yes the kern only knows of a
few card address's ). When the sys comes up, there should be a
recognition of that card ( u can see in /var/log/messages ). if the card
isn't seen, then change addr of card, or let the kern know ( edit the
table) where it is. if its seen, then does ifconfig see , and or
configure the card?. did u do a route add?
once u do this once, u sortta forget bec u dont do it again
