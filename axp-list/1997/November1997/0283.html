You don't want to write your own routine (reinvent the wheel) If you did,
the most efficient would be a table lookup for a seed to a Newton=Raphson
loop. But how are you going to inline it? with F77 this wont fit into an
arithmetic statement function, with C you could build macro, but this
is a regressive computing technique. Its the compiler! Find a copiler
that does the inlining. I haven't looked at it but I would bet that DEC
Fortran does this, I think they used to on the VAX.
