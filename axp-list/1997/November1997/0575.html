>>> "Chi Ming Hung" said:
> Hi, we're currently trying out milo-2029-pc164 on one of our alpha
> machines which has 128MB of RAM. As indicated in the document, the
> following parameter should be given to MILO:
>
> MILO> set MEMORY_SIZE=120

See previous postings discussing this; but simply, no, it doesn't appear
to be possible to coerce ARC or AlphaBIOS to allow multiple MILO commands\
on the OSLOADOPTIONS line.

However, you may find that a later MILO from:

gatekeeper.dec.com:/pub/DEC/Linux-Alpha/Kernels/Test-Milos-2.0.30+/

may remove the need for the MEMORY_SIZE setting.
