>>> Henty Waker said:
>
> Last time I heard (from a FAQ I think) Milo would not work for a Digital
> personal workstation. Support for the built in sound card and display card
> was also not available (but that's not as NB as Milo).
>
> Is there any news on when personal workstation might arrive ?

Well, it *arrived* with RH 4.2 (there's MILO and kernel on the CD); it just
arrived without any fanfare... :-}

Builtin sound is supported (SB Pro equiv), using io=220, irq=5, drq=1.

Builtin Ethernet (DC21142 or DC21143) is also supported, but at the moment
only by the "tulip.c" driver of Donald Becker. Be sure to get the latest
rev of his driver (082?) from:

http://cesdis.gsfc.nasa.gov/linux/drivers/tulip.html

Still not supported even for text mode are the higher-end versions of the
Powerstorm cards, ie 4D40/50/60. Text-mode only support for 4D20 and 3D30
works, I believe, but YMMV. No plans for XFree86 support for any of them.
A popular option that *is* well supported by XFree86 is the Matrox Millennium.
