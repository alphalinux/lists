On Wed, 19 Nov 1997, Paul Wouters wrote:

> Are the patches send back to the authors of the software which was patched?
> I've noticed on several occasions that there would be a 'fixed' redhat
> version while I'd personally would rather see the original being fixed.
> (I am not sure but i think diald was such an example)

Most of the time. It depends on how dirty the fixes are. If the fixes
are clean, they get back into the sources.
