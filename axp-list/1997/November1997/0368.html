Digital repair depots will swap them for ~$130, at least that is what
the Dallas office quoted. Are you sure it is the supply? I have two
UDBs, both of them had similar connector failures within a few weeks
of each other. They were both on the green 3.3 volt chip feed line
where the p/s plugs onto the m/b. Snipping off the connector and
splicing a new one one fixed the problem and was orders of magnitude
cheaper than two new p/s.
