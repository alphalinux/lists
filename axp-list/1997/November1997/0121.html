Hi all,

my axp server gets busier and I bounced into "too many open files" for
the second time today. This caused several processes to hang, resulting
in more and more filedescriptors beeing needed and so on...

I looked around a bit and found the limit of 1024 open files in
'cat /proc/sys/kernel/file-max'.

A search in some newsgroups and mailinglist-archives found suggestions
to "echo my_max > /proc/sys/kernel/file-max" in some rc-file.

Is this in your opinion a Safe Thing To Do [tm]?

What are my alternatives if not? And what might be unwanted side effects?
How can I find out what value of my_max is appropriate in my case?
