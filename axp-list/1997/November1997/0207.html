Amer Mallah <snare@jhu.edu> wrote:
> I'm running RedHat Linux (kernel 2.0.30).
> I try to compile SSH 1.2.20 and this is what I get:
>
> # make
(snip)
> *** [sshd] Error 1

Did you use ssh-1.2.20-3.src.rpm (you can get it from
ftp://ftp.pcuf.fi/linux/security/) to compile? If you rpm -ihv it,
you can get following patches:

ssh-1.2.20-config.patch
ssh-1.2.20-security.patch

I applied those patches to ssh-1.2.20 sources then compiled them
successfully, and now sshd works fine on my RedHat 4.2 (kernel 2.0.30)
system.
