Rick,

I'm sorry my message isn't likely to be very constructive, but I thought I'd give you some info.

I spent time last week trying to get milo compiled and had one hell of a time with it.
I'm not convinced that many people even bother to compile milo from source, because
it's so unclean. I ended up regressing to a 2.0.27 kernel, then compiled the 2.0.27-ELF
milo. I had no problem then booting my 2.0.30 kernel on the built 2.0.27-ELF milo.

I didn't get the error you see below, but got similiar errors at one point, and ended up
renaming the function calls that conflict. I have to believe that Dave Rusling's development
machine is a custom setup, and that he doesn't test his source with RedHat distributions or
anything else, because every version I've tried has had varying degrees of problems.

I sent David e-mail and never received a response. Quite frustrating.

If you get any helpful information or figure out a clean solution, I'd love to hear about it.

cheers,
