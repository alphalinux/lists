> Hello,
> If I ctl-alt-f2 out of my X session; how do I get back? ctl-alt-f1
> simply gets me to the character portion of virtual terminal 1. How do I
> get X to grab the screen again?

Head to the after-the-last virtual console.

If you're not sure, then it's probable that you haven't fiddled with the
virtual consoles, and the one you want is #7.

Hit Alt-F7. It should get you back to X.
