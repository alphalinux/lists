"Brian E.W. Wood" <beww@intac.com> wrote:
> Actually I have a scanner at the end of the chain, and all 5 of the
> external devices on the chain are configured to supply term power, as
> does the UDB itself. Even if the DAT drive (second unit off the UDB) was
> not providing power I think that there is sufficient DC available at the
> end.

Make sure the scanner is supplying terminator power then. Even if all the
other devices supply terminator power, if it all has to go through one
narrow-gauge conductor for several feet to your scanner, you are asking
for trouble. This is one of those things where everyone seems to think
that it should work in theory, but the reality is sometimes different. I've
actually looked at the terminator power under the conditions I've
described, and it ain't pretty.

Eric
