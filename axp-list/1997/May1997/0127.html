Most of the major disk drive manufacturers these days have put their
drive specs online... so even if you're hacking away at 3AM you can
find out everything you need to know about that drive you're trying
to install 8-) [of course, you need a second computer with a web
connection to do this, but isn't that what laptops are for? 8-) ]

Anyhow, everything you need to know about the 7345 can be found at:

http://ftp.maxtor.com/ftp/pub/scsi/7345s.txt

[btw - I betcha that what you thought was "73455" was in fact 7345S]
