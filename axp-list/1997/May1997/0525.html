On Thu, 15 May 1997, Sam Ockman wrote:

> I hate to reply to myself...but, every milo except for 2.0.30 has two
> versions, one being what I assume is an a.out version, and one being an
> elf version...2.0.30 on the other hand also has two versions, one being an
> ecoff version, and the other being what? An a.out version? An elf
> version? In the readme it says all of the elf ones are marked with -elf,
> but the .30 versions aren't...

Sam, if you ever re-compile the kernel you'll notice that ECOFF is
a.out. So the other version is ELF. I tried an ECOFF version of
2.0.27 and it didn't work right out of the box, and since my ELF
version did, I left it alone. I'm not sure why they have two
version of MILO.
