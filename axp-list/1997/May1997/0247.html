thomas.e.mccune wrote:
> Perhaps the 400 MHZ CPU is being run in this model at the 300 MHZ
> clock (underclocking) by another person who claims to being using
> 400 MHZ chips in his Multias.

Can we put this 400MHz bit to REST already??!!

There *IS* no such animal. There can *never* be such an animal,
because Digital has *NEVER* released a 21066 chip that runs at
more than 233MHz, and we never will. There is no other Alpha
chip that's pin-compatible with the 21066 either, so it's not
possible that somebody put some other 400MHz chip in the machine,
unless they designed one friggin' dandy of a daughtercard, which
I seriously doubt.
