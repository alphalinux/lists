On Mon, 12 May 1997, Kirk Saban wrote:
> Alpha with an IDE bus? I have an AlphaPC164 Motherboard and it has 2
> onboard IDE controllers. I have installed a Mitsumi 4X CD-ROM (IDE)
> and a Samsung HDD (IDE), however the system keeps hanging on me during
> the installation procedure when it tries to scan the hard drives.

Make sure that you have the ide's configured either salve/master or
standalone, depending on how you plugged them in. The easiest is to have
the cdrom drive as slave, harddrive as master, and plug them both into
one of the ide controllers.
