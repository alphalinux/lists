On Sat, 24 May 1997, Torsten Zirzlaff wrote:

> Hi.
>
> This maybe already discussed matter, but I have the problem in
> rebuilding a MILO 2.0.27. Neither the ELf nor the non-ELF version will
> build. The ELF sources stucks at linking and complains that the DEC
> bios86.a has no symbols, please ranlib. Well,after that it would not
> work. I have a Noname RH4.1 + patches, Kernel 2.0.27 and the RH 4.1
> MILO running.
>
> Anysuggestions what might the problem and how to work around? Is the
> only solution to switch to the 2.1 Kernel including MILO?

The Digital BIOS emulation only works with ECOFF. If you don't have the
gcc-ecoff you can't build it. So you have to say NO to the Digital BIOS
emulation (the second config question) and MILO will build just fine.

If you don't have an S3 based video card, the ELF MILO will not work. It
will not initialize the video card BIOS. Digital is working on the port
of the BIOS routines from ECOFF to ELF; but they are not ready yet.
