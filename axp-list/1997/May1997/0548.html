I have a UDB with a TGA card & I'm having problems getting X up.

Here's what I've done so far.

I run Xconfigurator
Choose Unlisted Card
Choose TGA
Choose DEC PCXBV-KA/KB as Monitor
2048 for video memory
Choose No RAMDAC Setting
Choose No Clockchip Setting
Choose "640x480" at the leftmost column for Video mode

I startx
I get a blank screen for about 5 seconds
and get the following message:

Fatal server error:
Cannot open mouse (Operation not supported by device)

_X11TransSocketUNIXConnect: Can't connect: errno = 61
giving up

xinit: No such process (errno 3): Server error.
