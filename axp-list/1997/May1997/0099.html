On Sun, 4 May 1997, Britt Bolen wrote:

> I know this is more of a general unix question... but how would i reboot
> from telnet when my UDB's keyboard/mouse freeze on me? logging in a then
> doing an su root doesn't let me access shutdown

do a "su - root" (and then a simple shutdown) or just enter the full path
(presumably /sbin/shutdown).

The reason is that a simple "su" doesn't mimic a full login so it's
possible that some environment variables (most notably $PATH) are not set
as they are when you login or do a "su -".
