> Now all my files have timestamps in the year 2017. Hum... :(
>
> Is there an easy way to fix this?

dirlist="/bin /boot /dev .... <all stuff you want to fix here>"
touch /tmp/now_is_the_time
find $dirlist -newer /tmp/now_is_the_time | xargs touch
rm /tmp/now_is_the_time

This will not change dates on symbolic links. They can be redone as well,
but require more care because they are used with libraries. Left as an
exercise to the reader (careful!). Also you may set date to some fancy
value, if you feel so inclined. See 'man touch'.

Fixing this dates is a very good idea; among other things your
makefiles, from time to time, will be recompiling things which they
really should not.
