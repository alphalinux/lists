Thanks to everyone who responed. I got a lot of very useful
information.

As it turned out, it was jestabro@amt.tay1.dec.com's question about the
video type that led to the solution.

Like a novice, it didn't even occur to me that this machine didn't have
a video card. Here I was, staring at a VT510 plugged into the serial
port and I didn't think to check to see if a video card actually existed
in the machine.

Well, as the story goes, there was NOT a video card in the box, so I dug
into the closet of misfit toys and pulled out a Realtek VGA card. I
plugged in the keyboard and a monitor, loaded up the SRM version of the
kernel, and away we went.

As of right now, we are doing a bad block check on the RAID system we
have on it, and everything looks good.

Thanks again to everyone for their patience and assistance.
