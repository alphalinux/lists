>I haven't had any problems with my Noname system and SCSI since I changed
>over to the 53c8xx driver. I've written and read several GB to/from my
>DAT tape without problem. Also, I routinely make disk to disk partition
>backups of 1 GB. I am running RedHat 4.1 with the 2.0.27 kernel.

I am running the 53c8xx driver and I had these problems with the Exabyte 8200,
which, interestingly enough, has similar problems on the Linux/Intel with the
same SCSI card (53c8xx). I have had Exabyte problems with that controller
since my first Linux box, the Exabyte 8200 works fine with other controllers.
One would have hoped that it would be fixed at some point but alas...

In any case the DU SCSI subsystem does not suffer from this, it works just fine
on the 53c8xx.
