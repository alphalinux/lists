brian -

seriously, try switching around the order of the devices. my scanner will
only come up at scsi id 1 and when the dat drive is in the center of the
chain. don't ask me why... scsi has a mind of its own at times. there is
always the chance that you've got some funky dat drive, but generally any
dat device should be recoginzed by a kernel with tape support (provided it
doesnt have some bizzare chipset-dependent scsi options compiled in). i
don't believe that tape drives are subject to the same block-size
specifications that cd-rom drives are subject to in order to come up on a
sun, although i may be wrong.
