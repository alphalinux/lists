Matt Hamilton wrote:
>
> Hello All,
> I have just purchased a 166Mhz UDB and while I wait for it to arrive I
> have a couple of questions and I couldn't find the answers online
> anywhere.
>
> 1) What type of SCSI controller does it have? Wide/Narrow/Ultra?

It is an NCR 53c810, which is a fast SCSI II (narrow) not wide and not
Ultra.

>
> 2) Will a Matrox Millenium card fit inside the PCI slot?

I have heard that it will (just barely). This assumes that you have one
of the models with a 2.5" drive as the 3.5" ones take up the PCI slot's
space.
