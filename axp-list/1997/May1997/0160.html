> Make sure the scanner is supplying terminator power then. Even if all the
> other devices supply terminator power, if it all has to go through one
> narrow-gauge conductor for several feet to your scanner, you are asking

And that means not only checking the jumpers on the drives, but to also
make sure the fuse is not blown - the SCSI standard asks for current
limiting on TERMPWR, and most devices use a fuse around 1A for this
purpose - which is easy to blow, and which may go unnoticed as long as
another device provides enough power.
