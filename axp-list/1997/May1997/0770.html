> After finally geting a kernel that would link again on my noname (2.1.40)
> I find that even with pretty well every driver possible removed, milo
> will still not boot the kernel. The kernel is much smaller than before but

Hmm; last one I've actually tried is 2.1.38 on a PC164, and it worked.

With that "size", I don't think its a MILO problem, but I could be wrong...

> Ive have gone back to SRM on my noname just to see if its a kernel build
> problem only ive got fdisk partitioning and no space for aboot....

Um, remember, on a NONAME/UDB, AVANTI, or P2K, the same kernel is bootable
from ARC/MILO and SRM. That's because the PALcode in MILO delivers interrupts
in identical fashion to the SRM PALcode. So this shouldn't make a difference.

If it *does*, PLEASE let us know!
