Hi.

I have made net-tools 1.40 available. Compared to the old 1.3x net-tools,
this release adds support for IPv6 and cosmetic improvements, and fixes
some problems to do with 64-bit machines.

You can get the net-tools at

ftp://ftp.london.uk.eu.org/pub/ipv6/net-tools-1.40.tar.gz
http://www.tazenda.demon.co.uk/net-tools-1.40.tar.gz
ftp://ftp.cs-ipv6.lancs.ac.uk/pub/Code/Linux/Net_Tools/net-tools-1.40.tar.gz

Please report any problems to me.
