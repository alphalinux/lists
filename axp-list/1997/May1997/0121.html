> Hello,
>
> I'm considering buying one of the 366Mhz Dec ALPHA systems (on the PC16x
> board I believe), with a SCSI disk system.
>
> Does anybody have any strong opinions for/against such a setup? I'm
> tired of intel-based systems and am looking to get out.
>
> I'm trying to find out as much as possible, but the FAQ doesn't really
> say how fast these systems are:
>
> Will it be faster then my P166 in terms of float, int?

It depends on what you're doing with it. Pentia run gcc awfully fast,
but a 366MHz 21164 based machine is probably 2x faster on floating
point code than a P6-200. (It is for my application anyway.) Which
is probably 3x faster than your P166 for floating point. Unless
you're computing square roots all the time.
