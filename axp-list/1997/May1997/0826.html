On Wed, 28 May 1997, Jeff Odom wrote:

> The reason that it doesn't find /lib/modules/2.0.30/modules.dep is that
> most likely on your system, it doesn't exist. RH4.2 doesn't include
> (at least, its mirrors don't) kernel-modules (check your rpm database.
> chances are you prob.. have an rpm from your previous version which can
> be erased).

Red Hat has said that modules are still not working on the Alpha version
of RH 4.2. It would be best to comment out the depmod -a and kerneld
lines in rc.sysinit. The file also has an error because it checks for
/proc/ksyms without the -n so it will always be true. The other problem
is Red Hat always makes dummy.o net driver a module so /proc/ksyms will
always exist of you don't re-compile. So I suggest you comment out those
two programs.
