> I've a little problem with "usercfg". It doesn't start any more:

I do not know that for sure, but from my investigation of similar
problems (grail, glint) this probably caused by broken tcl.
By itself it is written pretty badly and, when compiled from
"real" source or Red Hat distributed source rpms, it does not pass
its own test; this is true when even a "patch" which makes
grail somehow work on DU and Linux/alpha was applied. This "patch"
is not much of a help for glint, BTW.

The above information was checked on RH 4.1 but from reports that
grail still refuses to work as distributed with RH 4.2 I strongly
suspect that this is still the case; it seems that Red Hat did not
even bother to plug that known hole in tcl, not mentioning more
extensive tcl rewrites which would be required for a correct
operation.
