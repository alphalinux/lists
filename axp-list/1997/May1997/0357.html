I'm very interested in pvm. We use a set of DNA sequence-searching
programs to search large and ever-growing databases. One particular
run of this is expected to take 1 month cpu on one of our present
275MHz Alphas. There is a pvm version of this code and I aim to
get this down to a couple of days using a bunch of 500MHz PC164s.

Thanks to those who replied to my pvm queries.

Regarding cost-effectiveness of Alpha versus Pentium, we have a
PPro system with 128Mb and 4Gb SCSI, cost us nearly 2K pounds.
The similiarly-configured PC164/500MHz cost 3.5K pounds and
goes approx 5 x faster for much of our fp-intensive code.

Cheers,
Terry Horsnell.
