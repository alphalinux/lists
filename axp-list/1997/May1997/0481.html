I think these are all pertinent places to ask...

I cannot get ppp-2.2.0f compiled against glibc-2.0.3 to establish a
connection. Chat dials, login using PAP is fine, but when it comes
time to bring the link up, nothing happens.

With 'debug' in the options, I get a simple 'sifaddr failed' in my
syslog.

Investigating further, I recompiled ppp with -DDEBUGALL, and now get
the rather more specific 'ioctl(SIOCADDRT) device route: Network is
unreachable'. However I can't find anything obviously wrong with the
code---and it works fine on non-glibc system.

To further complicate matters, I'm running on an Alpha, but in looking
at the archives of the axp-list, I see nothing like this mentioned.
Of course, almost all of those systems are using RedHat-4.1. I'm not,
so my configuration is significantly different.

Unfortunately, I haven't been reading linux-ppp for a long time, so I
don't know if this has come up recently, and I can't find any
accessible archives. Nor am I on the list presently, so please cc: me
so I won't miss anything before I have a chance to sign up.

I certainly appreciate any suggestions.
