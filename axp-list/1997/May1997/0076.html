At 07:33 hrs. 05-03-97 -0400, you wrote:
>brian -
>
>seriously, try switching around the order of the devices. my scanner will
>only come up at scsi id 1 and when the dat drive is in the center of the
>chain. don't ask me why... scsi has a mind of its own at times.

In cases like this, the issue/problem is almost always termination. Just
having a terminator in the right spots doesn't necessarily ensure a good
termination scenario on a SCSI bus. Replacing the passive terminator on the
far end of the device chain with an active one usually clears these matters
up pretty quickly.
