> Does anyone know a *good* word processor for linux/axp. I just tried to
> run applixware thru em86 emulator with my Multia 233MHz, and it was a real
> pain :). Therefore I wonder if there's any word processor which I can run
> on this multia box without having to wait N+1 hours w/ it.

LYX compiles clean, works rather well, and is much, much nicer than any
word processor I have ever used for writing papers with lots of equations
in them. It even generates LaTeX and postscript output.

I'm in nerd-heaven; the Wintel and Apple boxen here at work will never be
soiled by my greasy little fingers again.

Granted it isn't as feature-laden as something like Applixware, but
frankly most of those features would probably get in my way in any case.

http://www-pu.informatik.uni-tuebingen.de/users/ettrich/lyx.html

Don't forget to D/L the xforms libs if you don't have 'em.
