At 11:11 hrs. 05-26-97 -0400, you wrote:
>
>Helollo..I am just wondering if anybody can give me a hand with something..
>I recently got ahold of an Alpha-NoName Board that I put RedHat on. I'm
having one problem
>How in the heck can I use a normal serial mouse with this board? I
noticed that
>i has got rs423 ports on it...what is needed to convert these babies to
232? I tried
>mapping a normal 9-pin 232 configuration to a 423 and then making a patch
cable that I could
>use to convert the 423 ports to 232...it didn't quite work..so whats the
secret here
>?...I am really looking forward to using X with this box..but I really
can't with out
>a mouse hehe..
Better solution is to get a PS/2 mouse, such as the Logitech Mouseman PS/2
or A4 Tech and plug it inot the mouse port provided on the board. If you do
not have a mouse port, then you have the type AA noname board, and it HAS
RS232 serial ports.
