I have a udb with rhl 4.1 on it and a pc also running linux.
I want the pc to masquerade for the udb. I think I have everything setup
correctly, but as soon as I do:
/sbin/route add default gw 172.16.0.1 (to make my pc the gateway for the
udb) the udb decides to lookup EVERYTHING through the nameserver...
It won't use the hosts file, and I can't figure out why. It even looks up
127.0.0.0. I can connect to computers on the net from the udb, so the
masquerading is working, I just can't connect to my pc from the udb or
from the udb to itself.

Anybody know how the heck to fix this?

On my pc I run:
/sbin/ifconfig eth0 172.16.0.1
/sbin/route add -net 172.16.0.0 eth0
/sbin/ipfwadm -F -p deny
/sbin/ipfwadm -F -a m -S 172.16.0.0/24 -D 0.0.0.0/0

On my udb I run:
/sbin/ifconfig eth0 172.16.0.2
/sbin/route add -net 172.16.0.0 eth0
/sbin/route add default gw 172.16.0.1

as far as I can tell, all the config files in /etc are correct.

thanks,
