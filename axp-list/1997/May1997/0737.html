Hello,
Well now that I've spent the entire evening upgrading and building
a kernel with modules, I've come to find I'm no better off than I was when
I simply used the modutils from the non-Red Hat distribution.
File system modules totally fail to function in 4.2 on a UDB. The
dependancies don't work with kerneld, or hand loaded insmod in the proper
order. I insmod fat, then msdos or vfat and the latter two complain that
all of the fat dependancies fail, even though the fat module is loaded and
initialized. And eql.o fails on the depmod -a.
I'm going to purchase not only the i386 4.2 but now the Alpha so I
can get the support and get this fixed! :-) Maybe I'll just purchase the
Alpha set and leave the (it works) i386 as non-supported.
Has anyone tried the module support on 4.2 yet? Anyone have and
ideas?

Thanks, Rick.
