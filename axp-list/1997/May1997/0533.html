>>> Rick Windham said:
>
> If you ever re-compile the kernel you'll notice that ECOFF is
> a.out. So the other version is ELF. I tried an ECOFF version of
> 2.0.27 and it didn't work right out of the box, and since my ELF
> version did, I left it alone. I'm not sure why they have two
> version of MILO.

The ECOFF version has superior BIOS emulation; its the same BIOS emulator
that appears as part of ARC/SRM/AlphaBIOS. But its only available as object
files, due to proprietary technology, and the only objects we have for it
are in ECOFF format.

This *will* change in the future, but for now, if you need the superior
BIOS emulation (and you do if you have an ATI or Matrox VGA, for sure),
you must build MILO under the ECOFF compilation system.
