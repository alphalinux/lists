Hello

>> You CAN load it into the flash rom. However, now your stuck with that image
>> and you can't do a dual boot between NT and Linux. Also when it comes to
>> putting something into the flash we only flash in Digital ARC consoles or
>> SRM consoles.
>
>In a similar vein, is there a clean way to triple boot between Digital
>Unix, NT, and Linux?

There is however, this option is only available with certain products. The
problem is the SIZE of the rom itself. The older PC64, a.k.a. Cabriolet and
EB164 could hold SRM and ARC together allowing you to boot Linux/NT from ARC
and Digital UNIX with the SRM. This also applies for the UDB, a.k.a. Multia.
I know because I sold about 100 such options to ISVs.

Now the bad news, the PC164 can only hold one image.
