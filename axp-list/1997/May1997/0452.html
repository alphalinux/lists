Hi,

You should take the TGA server (last option in menu), the 'other' video
card, and select no specific options for the video card settings (default). For
the display, take the DEC entry in the corresponding menu.
This worked for me. FYI, after typing startx, the screen will turn
blank, then gray and will stay like this for a long time, while all the init
scripts are executing, just wait ... this may take up to 2mn.
Hope this helps
