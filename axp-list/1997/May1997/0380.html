> I created a 2 MB 16-Bit MSDOS <32M partition (type=4 in fdisk) on
> /dev/sda1 and now I'm stumped as to formatting that little guy.

mformat, from mtools suite, will do that - but you need a fairly
recent mtools for mformat to work on a hard disk and you possibly
need to edit your /etc/mtools.conf file; you need a line like this
drive c: file="/dev/sda1"
and you format 'mformat c:'.
