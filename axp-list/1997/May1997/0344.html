On Fri, 9 May 1997, Lagodzinski, Andrew wrote:
> Can anyone tell me the difference in performance between 233 and 266MHz?

more bogomips and more heat. a lot of speed tests will say the machine is
faster, but in terms of real world experience, i saw no real difference
between 233, 266 and even 300 MHz.

without a larger cache and a faster bus, the udb isnt going to go very
much faster no matter how many MHz your cpu runs at.
