At 10:23 AM 05-07-97 -0400, you wrote:
>On Wed, 7 May 1997, Stephen J. Gaudet wrote:
>
>> You CAN load it into the flash rom. However, now your stuck with that
image
>> and you can't do a dual boot between NT and Linux. Also when it comes to
>> putting something into the flash we only flash in Digital ARC consoles or
>> SRM consoles.
>
>In a similar vein, is there a clean way to triple boot between Digital
>Unix, NT, and Linux?
>
Clean and safe wya, Paul is to use 2 hard drives. One for the NT and Linux,
and another for the UNIX. Depending on the macine you may or may not have
to reload firmware when going between SRM and ARC consoles.
NT can only be loaded from ARC
Linux can be loaded from either, although it is more complex from SRM
UNIX can only be loaded from SRM.

Disk labelling and filesystems for UNIX make it extremely difficult (I
hesitate to say impossible) to put it on the same disk as NT and Linux.
