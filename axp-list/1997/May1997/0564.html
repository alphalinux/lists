> In the course of an upgrade from RH4.0 to RH4.1 on

> to get stuck with the last line of the boot saying
> INIT: Entering runlevel 3
> .
> .
> .
> Mounting remote file systems

This is a known bug with RedHat 4.1. If you don't need amd, then you can
just remove the link from the "/etc/rc.d/rc3.d/" directory. Otherwise, RedHat
has a patch out; you'll find it under "http://www.redhat.com/updates".

Hope this helps!
