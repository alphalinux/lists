Hello Paul

>Why did you create the 1440K DOS partition for Milo and linload.exe?? I
>thought those things get loaded into the FlashROM.
>
>What gives??

You CAN load it into the flash rom. However, now your stuck with that image
and you can't do a dual boot between NT and Linux. Also when it comes to
putting something into the flash we only flash in Digital ARC consoles or
SRM consoles.

Past experiences with anything else has resulted in customers sending
systems back because they flashed the milo in the wrong address and blew the
rom. If you ever build a rom image from scratch you'd agree that keeping the
ARC intact is the only way to go.
