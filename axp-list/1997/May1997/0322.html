Hello folks,

I'm trying to make a case for replacing about 16 Pentium/SCO systems with
ALPHA/LINUX boxen. I believe that I can build my case using price/performance
as a major selling point, but I have to demonstrate that these systems will
handle the load over the next year's growth. I've been calling around to
some local vendors trying to get a decent eval unit to run in parallel
with one of the old systems and to port some of our home-grown utilities.
I've gotten some interest, but the only vendor that has been willing to
loan me a system gave me an old Cabriolet that freezes up whenever stressed.
I've spent several days trying to get around it's problems and making no
positive progress on the evaluation.

We're looking for;
300MHz or better 21164, 64-128M, UltraWide SCSI, 4G disk, PCI 10 BaseT,
reasonable graphics card (1024x768)

Can anyone point me at a reliable vendor who will do an eval for at least
two weeks with a system roughly equivalent to what I want to purchase ?
