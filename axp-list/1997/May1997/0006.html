I still have my memory problem though. I am beginning to think that the
Alpha motherboards are picky about what memory they will accept. I have
put IBM memory in both our jensen and cabriolet with no problem. placing
noname memory in the cab just will not work. I have also seen some
problems with the noname memory in our nonames( no pun intended). The
nonames will some times get ECC errors while booting up. These ECC
problems dissapear as soon as the system is warm. The noname ram sim's
are based using Hitachi HM5117400S6 and LGS GM71C400CJ60 devices. I
assume since the devices are 60ns that speed is not a problem. I expect
that the problem may lie in either CAS/RAS timing or possibly capicitive
loading.

I would really like to get this one nailed down. becuase the price
difference between noname ram and IBM ram is about $100. Does anybody
have a known list of known good alternate sources.
