Another related item which I don't like in the default configurations
of most mailinglist management systems is the tendency to STRIP AWAY
the "Received:" headers. For email professionals like me, it would
make life extremely simple to have the full message trace always
visible in case of fradulent/abusive messages make it to the list.

At VGER we found out that Majordomo will do stripping when the message
injection program is run with "-R" option. -- which is the default
configuration...

I hope the QMAIL based lists are also parametrizable in similar manner.
