On Wed, 21 May 1997, Ryan Garcia wrote:

> I'm having a problem installing redhat 4.1 on my multia. MILO thinks I have
> 2.88 meg floppies and it's trying to read past thed 1.44 megs that is available.
> Short of hunting down 2.88 disks how can I "tell" milo the correct floppy size?
> I'm loading it via the arc firmware BTW.

Add 'floppy=0,4,cmos' to your list of parameters for the linux kernel.
It is not vital to do this, the floppy will work anyway, just remember to
use the right device (e.g. /dev/fd0H1440) when formatting the disk.
