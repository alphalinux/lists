This makes, roughly-speaking, no sense.

The web page in question does seem to list some people that are involved in
"anti-spam" operations, but is not complete at all. It also doesn't make any
commentary indicating any value judgements beyond titles like "Mailing List
Whiners."

No doubt the email addresses are available in far more interesting places.

This is all a good argument for dropping a couple of email addresses for folks
that are tasked with investigating fraudulent activity into one's .signature
file... This way they most quickly get integrated into the email address
harvesters' lists, and they automagically send email about their fraudulent
schemes to the authorities tasked with busting 'em. Isn't that beautiful...
