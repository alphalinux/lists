Glad to hear it. I made mine myself from the docs in the manual that came
with my UDB (Thanks Steve).

I have been yelled at in the past for advocating rolling your own. It
seems most folks do not have the parts and/or the ability to do so, and
seem to resent those who do.

Accordingly, I offered a good cheap source for the cable, assuming that
those who can make one will do so anyway, and those who can't might
benefit :-)

There is a Sun part number (X985A or 530-1869/C9 - available from
SunExpress) which is a suitable "Y" cable as well, needing only a "gender
bender" at the UDB end.) The RS-232 spec is for 2 serial ports (async) on
the same DB-25 and both DEC and Sun adhere to it.
