Do you have /usr/bin/aumix on your system? It is from aumix-1.6.1-1.

Create a file of .aumixrc in your home dir with the following:

Pcm :36:36:P
Spkr :0:0:P
Mic :0:0:R
Mix :0:0:P
IGain:0:0:P
Line1:0:0:P
Line2:0:0:P
Line3:0:0:P

Then do:

aumix -L

to load the values from this file. These seem to be reasonable values for me.
