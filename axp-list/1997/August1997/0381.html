Hi there!

Here's an update on my progress with the sound card in the Cheap Vobis PC.
I tried the suggestions I got from the list - isapnptools is okay now. It
looks a bit complicated to me, so I tried the alternative by booting WNT
first to wake the card. This actually does work.

I haven't been able to get a sound out of the thing yet, tho. Testing several
different configurations in the sound support part of kernel comp. I managed
to see ADLIB/OPL-3 and MPU-401 in 'cat /dev/sndstat'.

But no /dev/audio in there. From what WNT tells me I can't decide if MSS/WSS
or whatever should be chosen. Here's the data (from WNT):

IO 0130-013F This must be the basic stuff
IRQ 05
DMA 01
DMA 03

IO 0330-0331 MPU-401
IRQ 07

IO 0388-038B ADLIB/OPL-3

Except using isapnptool, anyone got a suggestion how to correctly
configure sound support with this?

Thanx,
