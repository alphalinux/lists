> They are also shown as ZLXP-E2 under console mode, and only the ZLXP-E1 cards
> are supported (on multia/UDB and AS200, eg.).

Not completely accurate; yes, the 8-plane TGA is the only one with Xservers.
But the following is how the cards are named and what they have for hardware.

ZLXp-E1 8-plane TGA
ZLXp-E2 24-plane TGA
ZLXp-E3 24-plane plus Z-buffer TGA

Note that part numbers for the original TGA's are: PBXGA-XX.

The following are sold under the Powerstorm name:
3D20 S3 Trio64
3D30 8-plane TGA2
4D20 24-plane TGA2
4D40/50/60 24-plane+Zbuffer+texture memory, not sure what accel.

Note that part numbers for the Powerstorm's are: PBXGB-XX.
