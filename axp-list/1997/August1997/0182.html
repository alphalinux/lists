> (unknow instruction).
The itof and ftoi instructions were recently added and are not available on most of the alpha chips currently in the field. I believe that these are available in the 21164PC, and will be available in the 21264 series. These instructions simply move the contents of a floating point register to an integer register and vice-versa:

The itoft instaruction is equivalent to (assuming
you want to move register $1 to register $f1.

stq $1, 0($sp) # store on stack.
ldt $f1, 0($sp) # retrieve from stack
