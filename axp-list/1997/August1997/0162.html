Hi,
I'm facing a funny situation with one of my Miata's(I've got two).
Both machines run RH4.1 with milo-2.0.29. The problem is in the way the
machines initialise the Ethernet interface.

At bootup both machines identify eth0 fine. They then proceed upto the
"kernel : unaligned trap..." message where they pause for a minute or so.
Thereafter one machine continues to bootup & is fully functional(network & all)
while the other waits here for 25 minutes or so & boots up without network.
"ifconfig" has all the numbers in place as does "netstat"....loopback interface
works, but not ethernet.

I've checked for cable continuity etc. All is OK. The ethernet interface works
perfectly on NT. I'm puzzled.

Any help appreciated.
