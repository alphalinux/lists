On Tue, 19 Aug 1997, Nikhil Nair wrote:
> I need to order an Alpha this week. Budget restrictions mean I either get
> a 21164 366MHz / 1MB cache with 128MB SDRAM, or get a slightly faster
> machine with 64MB SDRAM.

It depends on where your bottleneck is. It sounds a lot like the slow down
is happening simply because the IBM has more RAM and thus can fit the entire
DB into file cache memory, while the Pentium cannot. RAM is many, many times
faster then disk, and having enough RAM to cache your entire database far
outweighs any benefit you'll get from the faster CPU. Go with the memory.
