jestabro@amt.tay1.dec.com wrote:
>
> >>> saswato said:
> >
> > I installed em-86 and tried to run netscape but it stops
> > with the message "received signal 7"
> > anybody who knows what that might mean?
>
> Yes it means you have to:
>
> 1. get the file:
>
> gatekeeper.dec.com:/pub/DEC/Linux-Alpha/Kernels/gnumalloc.so
>
> and install it in /usr/x86/lib.
>
> 2. in your .bashrc put the line:
>
> export X86_LD_PRELOAD=/usr/x86/lib/gnumalloc.so
>
> 3. rerun netscape from a bash which has the X86_LD_PRELOAD defined.
>
> --Jay++
>
>
