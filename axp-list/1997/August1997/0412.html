As an FYI, I like to burden each Attorney General of the state in
which the spam originates. California has a law on the books
which prohibits the practice and so far I have gotten a positive
response from Washington State AG. There is also a federal law
that might be applied to such crap as well that prohibits the electronic
distribution of "junk". It was written for fax machines, but to
my none-JD'd eyes might be broad enough to apply to electronic mail
as well.
