Another thought, do you have parallel printer support compiled into your
kernel? As I remember the kernel defaults, this is disabled and you need
to explicitly enable it during make config. This is only a
problem if you have built a custom kernel, since I believe the kernel
that comes with Redhat has parallel printer support enabled. Also, check
your cabling, etc.
