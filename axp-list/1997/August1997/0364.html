>Any thing I should really watch out for with this kind of configuration or
>in general?

Well, just don't expect blinding speed from a 21066 CPU; they're definitely the
low end of the Alpha totem pole. Also, not everything runs on Alphas that runs
on Intels; you'll sometimes have to live without some hot app (such as
Netscape, altho em86 does a decent job)

to be shipping with it already.
