Hi there!

Here's something the experts might be able to answer: AFAIK, on most
Intel boxes, *writing to* an external PCI card is fast, ie burst mode
*is* supported with a max. thruput 132 MB/s. But *reading from* that
same card is not possible in burst mode.

Is the burst mode read *from* external cards supported by Alpha machines,
specifically on PC164s?

This is rather important as one of our work group members developped a
multichannel-dsp PCI board to shove around lotsa data (from multichannel
measurements of spike signals of mammalian retinae, for those that're
really interested). Intel boxes really hurt the card due to slow reading,
so maybe an Alpha...
