| Alpha: On the Alpha if I have a screen with the contents of an ls
| on it and I vi a file, when I exit my screen contents previous to the vi
| are restored.
|
| i386: In the same situation as above I simply get the last page of
| the vi session and a prompt at the buttom of the screen. (This is how I
| am used to Unix working; but the other behaviour is preferred).
|
| Does anyone know why this occurs? I haven't found anything in the man
| pages yet.

Copying termcap across should make it work the way you want.
