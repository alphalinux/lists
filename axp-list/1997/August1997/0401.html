From: <ftp://ftp.redhat.com/updates/4.2/00README.errata>

[*] Package: lpr

Updated: 21-May-1997

Problem:
+ (21-May-1997) The lpr binary which shipped with Red Hat
Linux/Alpha and Red Hat Linux/SPARC is an Intel binary.

Solution:
+ Intel: This problem doesn't affect Red Hat Linux/Intel
+ Alpha: Upgrade to lpr-0.19-1.alpha.rpm
+ SPARC: Upgrade to lpr-0.19-1.sparc.rpm

The updated RPMs are in <ftp://ftp.redhat.com/updates/4.2/alpha/>
