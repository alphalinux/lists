> There's no technical reason why a Parallel ZIP couldn't be made to work
> on an Alpha machine that has a PC-style parallel port (like the UDB).
> However, it might be the case that the PPA3 driver doesn't currently
> support the Alpha.

I should point out that ARC console won't be able to boot from a parallel
ZIP, so even if it worked with Linux/Alpha, it would be useless for a
recovery disk unless you created separate Milo and kernel floppies to go
with it.
