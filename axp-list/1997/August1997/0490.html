tshrider@cs.indiana.edu (Todd M Shrider) writes:

> Is there a patch for the Gimp that lets you get around the seg faulting
> problems found on startup?

Yes, just get the latest "all-gimp-patches-in-one-file" patch and
apply it to 0.99.10. The rumors that GIMP isn't 64 bit clean are
(mostly) false. 0.99.10 with the patches applied runs on the Alpha
about as well as on any other platform. I don't think all plug-ins
are 64 bit clean yet, but GIMP itself runs nicely and most plug-ins
do, too.

See http://copper.ucs.indiana.edu/~sgoehrin/gimp/patches/ for the GIMP
patches.
