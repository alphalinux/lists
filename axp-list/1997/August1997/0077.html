Two network cards work just fine on a UDB. You pass the same boot
parameter as lilo to milo. Also, the card in the PCI slot gets detected
on boot as eth0, and the onboard as eth1. I currently have one ethernet
to my internal lan, and the other to my cable modem. Works great.
