>>> "Thaddeus A. Wilson" said:
>
> ** we now have a more current issue pertaining to the stealth graphics
> card Trio64 and our X server
>
> every time we try to run startx we get hung up. jay had suggested adding
> the following "option" to the "device" section of the /etc/X11/XF86Config:
>
> Option "no_pci_disconnect"
>
> however, this is not recognized as a valid option. We also cannot use the
> XF86Setup command, but by using either Xconfigure or XF86Configure we can
> set up our system.

Sorry, I guess that option may only be valid in release 3.3 or later of
XFree86, and you're running 3.2...

This is what the "Device" section for my S3-968 looks like, as I saw a
few instances of similar lock-up:

> Section "Device"
> Identifier "My Video Card"
> VendorName "Unknown"
> BoardName "Unknown"
> Option "no_pci_disconnect"
> s3RefClk 24
> MemBase 0x18000000
> EndSection

You may also want to put in a "MemBase" similar to the above; I think that
the Trio64 code wants the framebuffer on a 256Mb boundary on the PCI bus,
so interrogate your "cat /proc/pci" and find a spot to put it. It's better,
I think to give it an address than to allow it to make one for itself, as it
will do that expecting an INTEL environment and not ALPHA...

Good luck.
