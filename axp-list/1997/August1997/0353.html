I had to use the XLT milo (or was that the XL milo -- I've forgot
that, sorry) for our AlphaStation 255/233. I'm using the NT ARC
console quite happily -- the only exception was the partitioning
procedure. You'll need one FAT partition with at least 50 Megs to
boot from, or you'll loose after installing Linux. Otherwise the ARC
console won't let you boot your milo from disk.

Maybe this helps you.
