> What I was wondering Is before I fixed milo my clock ran at half speed

> bogomips have droped to around 600. This really makes no sense to me.

Yes, of course, I *always* have ideas, not always right, but hey... :-)

BogoMIPS calculations need the ticks/second to be accurate. It though that
ticks/sec (HZ) was 1024, when it was only 512, so it found it could do twice
as much stuff in (what it thought was) the same time, ie 1 tick. Hence the
inflated value of BogoMIPS (sorry to burst your bogo... um, bubble... :-).
