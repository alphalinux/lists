attempt to write to a scsi dds2 or dds3 tape drive (with tar, v1.11.8)
the kernel wedges after a small amount of data has been written to the
tape. I have a Adaptec2940 scsi controller.

I see this behavior with kernels 2.0.27, 2.0.30, and 2.1.47. Any
ideas? Has anyone else had similar problems? Has anyone else been
