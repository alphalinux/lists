Thanks to Mike Donahue (michael.donahue@nist.gov) who provided me with a
patch (and more importantly a change to the makefile) which allowed me to
get lpd to work (finally).

In the RedHat 4.2 SRPMS the patch has already been applied, but you need to
add the following definition to the Makefiles. Add the text
"-Dgetline=get_line" to the top level makefile (I just added it to the
CFLAGS definition, but you could also define RPM_OPT_FLAGS appropriately).
After a compile and reboot I can now print to the HP DJ400 attached to my
UDB from my NT laptop. (I need the laptop parallel port for my
videoconferencing camera!).
