Rick,

> Thanks for the info. I pulled my manual and found J30.
> Does this mean I need to play with the jumpers or is the Adaptec not the
> right controller?

My PC164 w/21165@500MHz works fine and stable with the 2940UW ever since I
corrected the J30 setting (No jumper plugged in at all).

There is really not much room for playing around, as you can see from the
user's manual. If you *really* need it, see what happens at the different
possible settings. 366MHz definitely won't work (at least it didn't for me).

Actually, I borrowed an NCR 810 a while ago, and it did work! So my configuration
PC164 w/21164@500MHz, 64megs RAM, 3.8GB Western Digital Caviar IDE disk, 8x IDE
CD-ROM, Elsa Winner 2000AVI/3D graphics, 4.1GB IBM dcas 34330 SCSI disk on the
NCR could take 45MHz bus clock.

I wouldn't be sure if other combinations do. Maybe talking to the vendors will
help to find out about specs?

Hope this helps!
