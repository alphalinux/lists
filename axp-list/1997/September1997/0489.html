On Wed, 24 Sep 1997, Scott Black wrote:

> Has anyone managed to compile Livingstons radius in a DEC/Redhat environment?
>
> Specifics - DEC Multia 133
> - Redhat 4.1
>
> Right now I use radius 1.6 on Intel/Redhat for primary authentication.
> This will be backup authentication for PM2s and PM3s.

Add -D__alpha and -D__osf__ to CFLAGS in your Makefile. You will also
need to add -lcrypt to LIBS.

There will also be a couple of places you will need to change an int to a
size_t, but I can't remember exactly where. It should be obvious when you
do a make.
