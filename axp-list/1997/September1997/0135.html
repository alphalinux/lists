Subject: UDB Temp / Powerup Problems

I've followed the UDB temperature thread with some interest. I've got two
UDBs that are both exhibiting the same characteristics that may be heat
related, but I can't make sense of all the symptoms.

At times the room is around 80-82F, which is hard to avoid since this is
in Texas on the southwest corner with a fairly large south facing window.
At times when it gets warm the machines get flakey and I need to restart
them, but they won't even get to the point of loading ARC. What is wierd
is that they won't restart at times even with the cover off and fan
blowing directly on the entire box. The CPU chip fins are just barely
warm to the touch, and it still won't restart, which makes me wonder if it
is a heat related problem or something else.

I just now noticed a new characteristic when it won't restart. After
powerup I hear the internal disk reset as normal, but nothing further
happens. There is then a barely perceptible click coming from the
speaker; you must be very near it to hear it at all. It is in the 120-180
beats/minute range, and the ethernet light blinks in sync with the click.

Anybody have any idea what that click/blink thing means? I ask because it
may be a clue to the problem.
