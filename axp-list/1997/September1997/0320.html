>UDB's don't go higher then 233 cpu's and there are no 1mb cache for
>them. I've checked the DEC parts lists, and they don't exist...

They may no longer be listed by DEC but they do exist - I have a DEC part
number from the UDB service manual, which was produced by DEC. On page 87
of the DEC service manual, in the "Recommend Spares" list it shows three
different Cache Simms:

VX41M-AA 256K Cache SIMM
VX41M-AB 512K Cache SIMM
VX41M-AC 1MB Cache SIMM

Now I guess its possible that DEC never actually produced the VX41M-AC, but
then why would they assign it a part number?

So the part Im looking for is a VX41M-AC 1MB Cache, though I would settle
for the 512K Cache SIMM
