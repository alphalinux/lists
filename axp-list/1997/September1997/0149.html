On Wed, 10 Sep 1997 08:59:14 -0500, Rob Zook wrote:

>Hi all,
>
>I have a UDB 133Mhz, running RH 1.1. I did the base installation,
>and added a couple of multimedia packages. One thing has never worked
>and now it's become a serious annoyance. I can anonymously ftp to
>the UDB, but I cannot ftp from my PC or another UNIX box to the UDB
>using a login on the UDB.

This may be a bit simplistic but all I did to get a ftp service running was
to grab the ftpd binary RPM (wu-ftpd-2.4.2b12-6.alpha.rpm) off of
ftp.redhat.com in the "/pub/redhat/redhat-4.2/alpha/RedHat/RPMS" directory.

I installed it with rpm -Uvh.

I did reboot after installing....but every thing works great.

Hope this helps
