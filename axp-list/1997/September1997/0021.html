The SCSI drive is partitioned in a fairly conventional manner: sda1,
a wee DOS partition containing MILO and linload.exe; sda2, /; sda3,
/usr; and sda4, swap.

When I attempt to add a boot selection from the ARC console, it only
offers:

SCSI Bus 0 Hard Disk 0 Partition 2

Is your DOS partition initialized with a DOS filesystem yet? It wouldn't
be just after fdisk'ing it---you can only set up ARC after you are done
installing and configuring Linux (including DOS format of sda1 and placing
linload/milo there). You probably took care of that, but I thought I'd ask
just in case.
