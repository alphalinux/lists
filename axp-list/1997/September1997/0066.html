On Fri, 5 Sep 1997, Matti Aarnio wrote:

>
> Running TCPDUMP shows weird junk coming in, which means the
> expected data is not coming it... Anyway the TCP does back
> off, and the throughput drops..

keep in mind that tcpdump doesn't get a copy of the packet, it gets a
pointer to the packet. so if the thing is processed before tcpdump gets
around to displaying it you might see garbage. on high speed nets you
might be running into this. alan posted the appropriate patch that copies
the packet instead of the pointer.. you do take performance hit, 'course.

just something to remember when debugging with tcpdump..
