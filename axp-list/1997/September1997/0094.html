Hello Steve:
The disk is a DEC RZ28D, 2007 MB, 4110480 sectors. The CHS values
given by bsdlabel are 2595/16/99. This values match those I found
factoring 4110480. Since fdisk doesnt allow you to set sectors higher than
63, I swaped the heads and sectors values, and divided the cylinders by 3
(just to keep it below 1024..), multiplying heads by the same values,
which gives 865/99/48 for CHS. This is the geometry I'm using.
