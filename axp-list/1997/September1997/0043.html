>>>> "Bielecki, Andrew P. CS/SCB" said:
>>
>> I'm working right now to get D-Link DFE500TX (21140 chip with tulip
>> driver) to work on my PC164's. Actually, they are working fine with
>> about 5.5MB/s transfer rate between to alphas (PC164-500MHz) but kernel
>> spits out bunch of unaligned traps and I don't know yet how to fix it.
>
>If you're using the tulip.c driver of Donald Becker, change:
>
> #define SKBUFF_RX_COPYBREAK 200
>to
> #define SKBUFF_RX_COPYBREAK 16384
>
>to force copies of the buffers to take place; this has the side effect of
>aligning some of the headers so that unaligned accesses don't occur.
>
>Or you could try using the de4x5.c driver, which already does the copies;
>however, this driver is known to not work as well with some non-DEC cards.
>YMMV... :-)
