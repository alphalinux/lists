Vlad,

My UDB came equipped with a 2.5 inch toshiba SCSI drive. The original 2.5
inch was a Toshiba 540 MB SCSI - Model # MK1924FBV. It died and has been
shipped to Toshiba for a warrenty replacement. Im currently using an IBM
2.5 inch scsi (340MB) right now. Power is supplied through the ribbon cable
that goes from the onboard SCSI connector to the drive. The cable should
already exist inside the case.

Note some UDBs come equipped with a mount for an internal 3.5 inch SCSI
drive. From what I can tell those that have the 3.5 mount do not have a PCI
slot available.

You may want to get hold of the UDB service manual....I believe you can find
an electronic copy of the manual at:

http://www.brouhaha.com/~eric/computers/udb.html

Hope this helps.
