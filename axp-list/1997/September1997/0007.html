I had a discussion with some of the alpha cpu designers about
4 years ago, essentially criticizing them for omitting byte
and 16bit integer instructions ( c = a+ b; where all entities
are 16bit took 19 clock ticks) but it took the power of Oracle
to get 4 additional instructions into the set (sign extended moves)
because without these Oracle ran like Molasses.

You may wish to check that the compiler you are using supports
these instructions. In fact you should probably check that the
instructions are indeed supported by your cpu.
