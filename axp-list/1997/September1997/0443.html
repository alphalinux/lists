Hi,

This card is the default SCSI card in the UDBs (Multia), and it is *much*
cheaper then the adaptec xxx (price / 4).
I have no information regarding performance, nor do I know if the system can
stand multiple interfaces (I'd think yes).
BTW, this card is bootable (ie, you can boot on devices managed by this card),
while some (never had the problem) are said non-bootable.
