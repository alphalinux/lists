> Anyone using the Toshiba 2.5" SCSI drive in their UDB? I'd love to get
> rid of the external drive case on my Linux box. It's hard to tell from
> pictures how this drive gets power - more importantly how I'd get power to
> it from my UDB. If anyone has experience and would send me
> e-mail... Thanks.

I installed a brand new 770MB Toshiba in my 166Mhz UDB last week. Works
like a charm. The standard 2.5" scsi cable supplies both power and data
connections. I will note that with the drive in the machine, it runs a lot
hotter (for obvious reasons) and I found that the machine runs cooler if
its mounted vertically in its stand rather than horizontally. Then again,
it could have been the fact that I had a 14" monitor sitting on top of it.
