Hello All,

I just thought I would report on my attempts to get a soundcard to work
on my AXP box.

Sound card: Turtle Beach Tropez(Not the PNP version)

I Decided to use kernel 2.1.55 because it is the most recent. I also
went and got the sound download file off of my intel box to intialize
the card. I had to change directory to linux/drivers/sound and type
make config to the process to work correctly. The new global make config
dosen't work correctly.

And it worked with no problems. After rebooting I was playing mods. and after
changing an u_long to an unsigned int in vplay I had wav files going.
(Strange why do people thing longs are anything but 64 bits)

Well the questions for the day.
- Anyone got a midi player?
- Anyone know of a wave player that can handle PCM encoded files?
- Is there such a thing as a 128 bit double in AXP linux? (yes it's off
the subject but a question :) )
