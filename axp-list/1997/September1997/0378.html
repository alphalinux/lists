try the ramdisk.img found at
ftp://ftp.kernel.org/pub/linux/distributions/redhat/redhat/redhat-4.2/alpha/images/

use 'dd' or rawwrite to cat it onto the floppy. the pseudobootdisk will
be used at the end of the installation.. (after it grabs all the crap off the
cd, it will ask you to "insert boot-disk"..that's where you will use the
pseudoboot disk.
