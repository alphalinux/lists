I figured I'd post this in case anyone else is having problems with sync,
reboot etc. These were hanging my shell and when I logged out, a new getty
would not come up.

The suggested fix was to make sure MILO and the Kernel were the same version.
This was not the fix for me.

Here's what I sent to a guy who has helped me get my Alpha up...

Thanks to all who helped me out!!!!
----
I got it fixed. It was NOT related to the MILO and kernel version. It
was related to some zombie processes relating to the getty?? or something
else. I cleaned up my rc.d scripts to run only what I wanted and the problem
seemed to go away. My guess is that is related to Yellow Pages when the network
was not up (I took out all the YP stuff). I really don't feel like tracking it
down however and screwing up my HD.
