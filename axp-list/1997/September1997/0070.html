On Wed, 3 Sep 1997, Ronny Ranerup wrote:

>
> Does anybody have a working xntpd? I compiled and ran xntp3-5.90.3
> but get the following from strace shortly after the fork.
>
> mlockall(0x6000 <unfinished ...>
> +++ killed by SIGSEGV +++

For a quick fix, edit config.h and undefine the bit that says that the
system has mlockall after running configure:

/* Define if you have the mlockall function. */
/* #define HAVE_MLOCKALL 1 */

Sure, you won't get an xntpd that is immune to paging, but depending on
your applicaiton, that might not matter too much.
