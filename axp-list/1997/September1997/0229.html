Putting on my cynical hat, I suspect Digital will try to suppress or not
acknowledge this. They dumped so many UDBs that the last thing they want
is some kind of massive returns for an engineering defect.

I would love to be proven wrong on this.

> On the matter or internal heat buildup, I have been considering adding an
> additional fan of the type that fits into expansion slot. Only thing is
> there are no PC type power connectors in the UDB. I recall seeing a
> reference to the schematic of the leads coming off of the power supply, but
> I cannot find it.

On the VX42s there is a 4 pin floppy type power tap on the SCSI riser;
I've not looked to see what voltages are there.

I measured the air temp on the fan exhaust of a UDB and got 115F, and it
runs hotter than that inside. I suspect the elevated temp could
accelerate corrosion. I'm going to spray the power connectors with some
Red Goo that a h/w engineer friend recommended, and I may well pull the
machines down every 3 months or so for PM on those connectors.
