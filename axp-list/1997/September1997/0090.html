OK. This is my setup:
Alpha PC164 (Durango) with D-LINK DFE 500-TX-B1 10/100MB/s Ethernet
Card.
Running RH 4.2 (2.0.30) with Milo 2.0.30 (from digital site) using Donald
Becker ( big thanks to him!) tulip.c version 0.76.
Switched for full duplex and copy break switch to 16384. Both Alphas are
connected to Cabletron MMAC + Blade switch setup for 100MB/s full duplex
( I was getting lots of collisions on the switch when I had it half
duplex). As far as performance I can't complain since I was getting
11MB/s transfer rate for small files (30-70MB) and 6.5MB/s for (200MB+)
but I think this is due to the hard drive slow access time.
