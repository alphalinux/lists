What did you do to your kernel headers?

> So I wrote -I options into the Makeconfig file...I know it's not a
> proper action but I did it, then the errors above appeared.

Well, duh, don't do that.

While building libc, the only headers outside the libc package
itself that are used are from the kernel. It very well should
be finding them in /usr/include/{linux,asm}.

If it isn't, or if you've mucked about with /usr/src/linux
(in such ways as installing new kernel sources on top of the
old that aren't the right version or have the right patches),
chances are you'll run into problems.
