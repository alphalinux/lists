the jumper is J7

MHz pin 1-2 pin 3-4 pin 5-6
--- ------- ------- -------
66 out out out
100 out out in
133 out in out
166 out in in
200 in out out
233 in out in
266 in in out
300 in in in

be careful though, my board was not labeled as to which pin is 1, so look
up your current clock speed so you can tell which pins are which
