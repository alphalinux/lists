Yes, well, actually, the problem *will* be fixed by a new MILO, but it's not
ready yet.

Seems that you have to use extra caution with some of the propagation delays
through the core logic chipset on MIATA (and LX164, for that matter); if you
don't, its possible to take a second interrupt from the timer before the
previous interrupt clear has made its way through the circuitry, thus resulting
in counting some ticks more than once... :-(

A problem with a known solution, but at the moment we can't build the PALcode
(where the fix is) from source under LINUX, so until we *can*, you'll have to
live with this problem.

I promise an announcement on axp-list when the MILO is ready.
