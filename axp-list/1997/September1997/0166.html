Hi all!

After some frustration trying to get bootparamd to work, I decided
to try a brute force approach to software maintainance. I grabbed the
latest Slackware source for it (just because it was handy), and did a
search-and-replace to convert all integers to 32 bits. Well... It works
now. Previously, bootparamd would give errors like "whoami failed!" and
the Sun 3 would report "no bootparamd responding..."

Anyway, just thought I'd mention that in case anybody else wanted
to boot their old Sun 3's etc. off of Linux/Alpha but gave up. Maybe
later this week I'll actually get a chance to look in detail at the source
and see where the bug guts actually are ;-)

Cheers!
-Aaron
