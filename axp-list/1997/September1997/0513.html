Rho Epsilon ARC - Alpha Chapter wrote:
>
> Howdy...
>
> Ever since I got X Windows going, I've had this strange xterm
> problem. All of the Control-<mouse button> menus appear and function
> properly, but the text in them is not displayed (in otherwords, they show
> up as empty white boxes)... any ideas? It's just plain wierd.
>

This may be a color problem:
Try out a XTerm*fontMenu*foreground: NavyBlue
in your ~/.Xdefaults or ./Xresources file and play
around with colors for fore- and background of the
mainMenu, vtMenu, fontMenu and tekMenu widgets.

Notes:
You must restart X to actualize your settings.
The name of the widgets used in xterm are described
in the xterm manpage.
You will find valid colors in /usr/X11R6/lib/X11/rgb.txt.
