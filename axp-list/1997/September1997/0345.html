>>> Jo Hausmann said:
>
> I was wondering if anyone knows what to do with clock-pc164-rh4.2,
> which is at gatekeeper in /pub/DEC/Linux-Alpha/Kernels.

You use it to replace /sbin/clock, on PC164s that show a random problem with
the clock.

If you do, on a PC164 (booted from ARC in this case):

while true; do clock -r -A; done

and notice that every now and then you'll get a bogus date, do the replace.

includes my original problem explanation:
