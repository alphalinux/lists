>>> Stefan Schroepfer said:
>
> There are two drivers for ncr53c875 based SCSI host adapters
> included in Linux: "ncr53c8xx.c" and "53c7,8xx.c".
>
> Which one is better (stability, performance) for Linux/Alpha?
>
> Am I right that none of these drivers supports Ultra-SCSI?

AFAIK, the 53c7,8xx.c version does not support Ultra. Also, its use has
been deprecated in favor of the later and more stable ncr53c8xx.c one.

The ncr53c8xx.c one *does* support Ultra, but only if you upgrade to a later
version than is currently in 2.0.30 (actually, the pre-patch-2.0.31's have
had 1.18i, which is fine for Ultra).
