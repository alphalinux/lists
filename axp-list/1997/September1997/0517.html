> Are there any special requirements for setting up 2 ethernet cards on an
> Alpha with 4.1?
>
> We have successfully done this on Intel, but are having a problem on our
> Alpha. Specifically, the kernel sees both cards, but locks up when it
> tries to go into runlevel 3.
>
> We have a PC164 based 500mhz alpha running 4.1, kernel 2.0.30.

You will propably need to replace the drivers/net/tulip.c with a newer
version from: http://cesdis.gsfc.nasa.gov/linux/drivers/tulip-drivers/
directory. (The lattest version was 0.79, when I recently looked for it.)
