If I remember correctly, when I was compiling the kernel, and first specified
CONFIG_YM3812=y and CONFIG_ADLIB=n (it means some generic FM support), the
compiler could not link the objects because of some missing function, so in
your case it had to be set to y in order to compile at all. On the other hand,
the sound chip in UDB, as far as I understand, does not anyway support FM, so
one can disable both options.

What surprises me is that you seem to be able to record, since I cannot at all.
Few days ago I posted a question about this, and got a response from somebody
who had the same problem like me. Do you have the CS 4... or CS 4...A chip inside,
or did you do something special in order to be able to record ?
