Armin Ollig <armin@data.tops.net> writes:

> /sbin/modprobe sound
> /lib/modules/2.0.30/misc/sound.o: init_module: Device or resource busy
>
> i recompiled the kernel about 10 times, with and without module support,
> downloaded and patched a new source-tree but i just can not get this
> sound card to work. the card is a sound-blaster 16 Plug'nPray (genuine
> creative) model which did work without a problem in my pentium box with
> linux and FreeBSD (220/5/1/5). But in the alpha it refuses to work.
>
> so my question is if someone has already managed to get the PnP-card to
> work ?

Yes. The fact that it worked on your pentium probably means your
pentium box have a PNP bios.

Is there any Alphas with PNP suport in bios?

> or do i need an old hard-jumper-type-card, since the PC-164 does not
> support Plug'nPlay (AFAIK) ?
No.

You need the isapnp utilities with some extra Alpha patches. (I got it
from this list some time ago)

If you like, I can email the patches to you.

It might be of intrest for you to know that i do not compile the sound
driver as a module. And remember that the sound card needs to be
initialized by isapnp before the sounddriver can find the card.
