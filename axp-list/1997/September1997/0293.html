> I just put it back to 233 for I will get really efficient cpu coolers

My APXpci33-233 has been running at 266 for a year now without problems,
although I did bolt a fan on top of the CPU heatsink just to make sure.
My 21066 was equiped with the 'High Profile' heatsink as well, so there is
plenty of area for the fan to blow air over. The UDB's probably have the
low profile heatsink.

My Noname is also mount in a PC tower case, with an additional fan, so
there is plenty of air movement over other components as well - the RAM and
the ram drivers get pretty warm to.
