>>> nm said:
> can anyone help me out with some info on how i can go back to arc after
> flashing with srm?

It'd be helpful if we knew the platform you were on, because some SRM
consoles, especially on the newer boxes, simply let you type "fwupdate"
with the appropriate floppy files available.

On www.windowsnt.digital.com, you'll find an Alpha drivers/firmware area,
which has most platforms' ARC or AlphaBIOS updates.

If your platform doesn't support the "fwupdate" command, then you'll have
to look on gatekeeper.dec.com:/pub/DEC/Alpha/firmware for some of the tools
used for updating older platforms, that help build a bootable floppy image
for SRM.

Good luck.
