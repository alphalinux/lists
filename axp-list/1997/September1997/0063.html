Hello. I have a problem of installing pvm(pvm3.4.beta3.tar.gz) on
my EB164 and PC164 under Linux/Alpha 2.0.30 which was developed by
Mr. Goto and is called StataboWare in linux-alpha-jp ML.

The problem is: host machine of linux/alpha cannot add the other
machine under pvm console. I succeeded in adding linux/alpha machine
if other OS machine, such as Solaris, would be a host of pvm. But
if case of linux/alpha I cannot add not only linux/alpha machine but
also other OS machines.

Additionally, I had the information that linux/alpha under RedHat 3.0.3
(though rather old) environment could compile pvm3.3.11.tar.gz easily
and worked fine for adding other machines in pvm console.

Is there any information about this phenomena? Or, if someone has the
experience of installing pvm on linux/alpha whose kernel is around
2.0.30, please let me know. Thanks.
