>I was perturbed to find out that a high profile diagnostic and
>administration tool like "netstat" was giving these unaligned trap errors,
>and surprised to find out it got through RedHat's normally superb
>QA. On examination of one of the patches in Redhat's source rpm, I found

It's been fixed in net-tools since version 1.40 or so. You can currently get
the latest version at

ftp://ftp.cs-ipv6.lancs.ac.uk/pub/Code/Linux/Net_Tools

The unaligned traps are harmless in themselves, and netstat isn't precisely
performance-critical so it ought not to be a big deal. However, the
particular bug that was in netstat was a bit worse than that because it could
actually cause a crash under some circumstances.
