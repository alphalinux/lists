The best method of booting via MILO in the first instance is to use a floppy.

the noname milo and linload.exe on a DOS floppy. See the MILO howto - it
talks you through
the whole thing. Once you have installed linux, you can use linux to set up a
DOS partition
which can hold linload and MILO.
