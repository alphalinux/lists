Hello Jim

>I have a PC164 system with an Adaptec 2940uw which doesn't seem to be
>recognized by SRM. The ARC recognizes it fine. Does anyone know which
>SCSI host adapters are supported by SRM?

The only SCSI controllers I've seen work are NCR 53C810, the Qlogic QA 1020
Fast & Wide, Qlogic has an UltraWide don't know the part number and the
Mylex 960 series. The last I know of and I consider the best is from
IntraServer they have a whole line up that supports, Linux, Digital UNIX,
OVMS and NT.

>While we're at it; How about ethernet cards?

On ethernet cards I've tried many and still only seen Digital's own to work,
ie: DE435, DE450, and DE500.

Hope this helps.
