witticism:

*reasonable* resolution, and I've been having no joy with attempts
to use a 4MB S3V card in my UDB.

highly appropriate to link into a UDB FAQ.

And particularly for Harald's consumption...

no better success than I had with the 3.3.1 binaries from ftp.xfree86.org.

what I got from the copy "straight from xfree86.org."
