I know this is not the right forum for this message but when I looked
around I found that I could find no better.

So could all please direct any responses/flames to me personaly.

I spoke to one of the DEC people about the AltaVista tunnel about support
for other OS's. They currently support NT, DUNIX, and BSD but they are in
the process of dropping BSD. When I asked about Linux the reacation I got
was that Linux would never be a supported OS.

It seems that the people at DEC believe that:
1) Linux is a fringe OS.
2) Linux is not used for real appilcatioins.
3) Linux users/developers will not pay money for 3rd party products.

My question is: Is there any way to raise the visibility of Linux to the
people at DEC.
