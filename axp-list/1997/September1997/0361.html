I'm sorry that I'm not answering your original question about IP
firewalling, but I just wanted to say that I had the same problem with SCSI
resets. We just bought 2 new Alpha systems (both from NekoTech). One had
a 500MB Drive, one a 1GB Drive. Both drives are SCSI. We had a few
clients on a UDB, so we decided to move the drive we had on the UDB to one
of the new (and faster) machines (running RH 4.0). The drives from
NekoTech came pre-installed with RH 4.2. I reclaimed the UDB for my
development machine and when I attempted to boot with the pre-built milo
and pre-installed RH 4.2 drive, I ran into the SCSI resets.

My solution was probably an easy one, although not what most would have
done. I simply re-installed Linux and havn't had problem since.

I would be interested if there is a better solution to this if we run into
the same problem in the future. Any ideas?
