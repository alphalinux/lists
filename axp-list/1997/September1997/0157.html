>>> Sheer Panic said:
>
> Can someone send me the list of patches to m ake the stablist possible
> kernel with intraserver card support?

Last time I built MILOs that had ITI adapter support, I did:

1. unpack linux-2.0.30.tar.gz

2. patch the above with pre-patch-2.0.31-3 (-9 is now available :-) in the
normal way

3. patch the above with alpha-patches-2.0.30-0.1 in the normal way

Seemed to work OK, no messages from "patch" indicating any problems, but that
may have changed between -3 and -9 of the pre-patches.

Note that -3 had patches for the ncr53c8xx driver up to 1.18i; that was enough
to get the 875 support, but the latest specific patches for that go to 2.4 and
beyond, IIRC...

Good luck, let me know of any problems with the above scenario.
