>>> "Jim. Shipman" said:
>
> I have a PC164 system with an Adaptec 2940uw which doesn't seem to be
> recognized by SRM. The ARC recognizes it fine. Does anyone know which
> SCSI host adapters are supported by SRM?

I suspect its NCR810, maybe some of the higher ones (825? 875?), plus the
QLogic 1020-ISP, perhaps even the 1040 (ULTRA) but I don't know for sure.
The latter, AFAIK, doesn't have a LINUX driver yet?

> While we're at it; How about ethernet cards?

I think you'll find its prolly only the DEC cards: DE435/450/500, and if
you're lucky, it may work with the clones of those, using the DEC chips,
ie some of the ones from SMC, maybe others.
