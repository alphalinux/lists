> What did you do about need monitor/keyboard/mouse cables from the
> computers to the switchbox??
>

I ordered a couple of cable kits from the same place I got the switch,
however, inorder to use the PS/2 type mouse, you will need male-male
6 pin mini-din cables to connect from the switch to your motherboard.

I had to get these from a local electronics store (CompUSA, Best Buy,
etc. only carry the male-female extender type cables.)

> Will a PS2 2-button mouse work OK with the UDBs??
>
Sure, you can configure XFreee86 to emulate 3-Button.

> Will my Gateway 2000 Keyboard work ok with the UDBs??
>
Don't know.

> Also, I am getting a used, refurbished 21" Sun Monitor. Will this big
> baby work??
>
As long as your resolution settting and refresh rate don't require a
dot clock higher than 100MHz and if it uses a standard PC type
connector, you should be ok.
