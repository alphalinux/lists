Thomas Pornin wrote:

> I have a UDB, and a lonesome 3.5" 1 Gig scsi harddisk.
> I once read that it is possible to plug it inside the UDB (but this prevents
> from using the PCI connector). How is this done ? I see no suitable
> connector, and no fixation either. The only web pages about this I found
> were in Chinese language. Anybody has done it ?
>
> --Thomas Pornin

There's a standard 50 pin internal SCSI connector in there, that
is now (in an off-the-shelf UDB) connected to the existing hard
drive. You have to take out the one that is already in there
and connect your larger capacity one instead.

A comprehensive listing of the connectors in a UDB and what can
be wired to them belings, IMO, in the evercongealing UDB FAQ.
