Hi there,
I've just about got my AS 255 up & running after facing a host of
similar problems. I used milo, avanti.img, ramdisk.img from the RH 4.1
CDROM. Worked fine.

The only problem I faced was with fdisk/minlabel. Apparently SRM does not
understand the boot tags of fdisk...you cannot get SRM to boot the OS
directly. A milo boot floppy is what I'm using right now. I'm planning to
move on to minlabel in a day or so.

Be glad to help further if you feel the need.
