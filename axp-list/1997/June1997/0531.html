I have made a MILO floppy containing the files:

Linload.exe
Milo

I have set up the boot selection as follows:
LOADIDENTIFIER Linux
SYSTEMPARTITION multi(0)disk(0)fdisk(0)
OSLOADER multi(0)disk(0)fdisk(0)linload.exe
OSLOADPARTITION multi(0)disk(0)fdisk(0)
OSLOADFILENAME \milo
OSLOADOPTIONS nodebug

The system accesses the floppy disk drive and responds with:

Opening file multi(0)disk(0)fdisk(0)\milo . . .
Loading file at 0x80000000 . . .
Getting firmware parameters . . .
. . . Memory size is 0x2000000
setting up arguments for Milo @ 801FFC00
setting up memory size for Milo @ 801FFE00
setting up memory size for Milo @ 801FFF00
Swapping to PALcode at 0x0 . . .

Then it hangs.
