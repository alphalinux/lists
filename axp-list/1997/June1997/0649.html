I've never tried to use PCMCIA (alpha/i386 or other) but I know two things
that will help you.

1) Modules don't work or don't work well in 4.1 (I don't remember which)
Compiling in PCMCIA support statically is the only way it will work.

2) To use rpm to erase a package, you don't give it version numbers.
"rpm -e modules" will work properly.
