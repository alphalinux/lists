Having trouble getting the cogent EM110 (DEC 21140) cards to
initialize with our PC164's.

We're setting up 4 systems consisting of the following
components:

PC164 500MHz
256M ram (256bit wide mem bus, j1 jumper removed)
1M bcache
Adaptec 2940 UW (ultra scsi enabled) + UW disks
Matrox Millenum 4M
PS/2 mouse

RedHat 4.1 (upgraded)
linux 2.0.29 milo / 2.0.30 kernel

using the cogent Em110 TX 10/100 ethernet adapter as eth0,
connected to 10-base-T local net.

we've got stable operation under very heavy loads with networking
disabled...e.g. i can get one of these boxes to reliably and
simultaneously build 3 independent source trees of the kernel
on three different disks, while handling a bunch of X graphics
and lots of input from the console...no hangs, no CIA checks,
no refused kernel page requests.

what we can't get is the network to start, either under the
/etc/sysconfig scripts at boot or ifconfig after a 'single' boot.

using the de4x5 driver we get an indication of 100 MHz startup
and an apparent hang...although we can still page the console
screen with shift-pgup shift-pgdn. the hub the pc164 is connected
to shows continuous activity (collision leds continuously lit)....
otherwise the pc164 ignores the world.

under the tulip driver we get an indication of 10 MHz startup
followed by a CIA check and total hang (no activity on our hub):

CIA machine check: reason for machine-check unknown (0x311588)
+ 0 0---------0 0---------10.

any input on this problem is vastly appreciated.
