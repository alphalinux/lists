At 02:00 hrs. 06-13-97 -0600, you wrote:
>
>I'm trying to get my noname board to work but I can't seem to get the
>serial ports on the board to work with anything (a mouse or a modem).
>The manual says that they are RS-423 instead of RS-232 but they have the
>same signal lines as far as I can tell. Anyone have any ideas on this
>one? When I plug a modem into the serial port the RD and SD
>(send/receive) lights are on constantly (so something is wired wrong).
Hello Greg. It sounds to me that the cables you have that hook the internal
connections to the external DB connectors are wired wrong. The correct type
is often referred to as DTK standard. The ones that produce behaviour like
you are seeing are often referred to as Acer type. The difference is in the
pinout setup. If you need more info, there is an attachment with this
email. It is in Microsoft Word 7 format. Hopefully you get it and can use
it. If not I will respond to private email on this subject.

Regards,
