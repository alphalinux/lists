> I am very astonished to hear this. For Linux/x86 Xcdroast works very well
> and as it is only a TCL/TK/wish frontend to cdwrite, I am astonished it can

We spent over a month trying to get a Philips CDD521 working on our
AlphaStation running Linux. cdwrite, Xcdroast, and TCL/TK all seem to have
problems on the Alpha. They compile but screw up when run. I would guess a
sizeof(long) problem, as that seems to be everyone's favorite Alpha bug.
Possibly problems with the SCSI drivers as well. We gave up and switched to
an Intel/Linux box.

(such as the error messages we got, kernel config we're using) if you think it
will help.
