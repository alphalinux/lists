I figured this will be useful info to someone out there:
Bought a Nomai 540 Meg removable disk for my UDB (Cheap backup; $130 for the
internal gizmo; access times faster than a Jazz drive).
Was nonplussed by all kinds of SCSI bus errors & system lockups upon big file
transfers. Noticed it worked just duckie on my PPro box at work (which had
the same SCSI card; NCR538XX with 7/8 driver).

So, after noodling around with cabling and termination, I recompiled without
"Disconnect" support & have transferred several Gigabytes back & forth quite
happily & without complaint ever since.
In case anyone is thinking about buying one; I give it a hearty thumbs up so
far. Just compile your kernels without disconnect support.

-Scott
