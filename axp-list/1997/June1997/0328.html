Woo, this is a FAQ :)

The architecture for Alpha machines was changed from .axp.rpm to .alpha.rpm;
you need to update RPM to a semi-recent version using the --ignorearch
command. Then, it will recognize .alpha.rpm's as being the correct
architecture for your machine.
