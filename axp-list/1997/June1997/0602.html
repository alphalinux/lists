Ok, here we go:
dpw433a with 256 MByte RAM, AHA2740UW with an DEC RZ1BA and a Seagate
ST32550W, and an additional KZPDA-AA (Qlogic ISP) to hold two Fujitsu
M2949SYU and two Pioneer DRM-624X. The ethernet chip identifies as a
21142 and is connected to a thinwire ethernet cable.

Situation:
I've tried ALL the kernels mentioned above, even the DONT-USE-FW-UPG, but
to the moment my network (10base2) won't work. The symptoms are the same
for miata-kernel-2030-test.gz, miata-kernel.gz and
miata-kernel-2029-complete.gz - the systems works, but spends A LOT OF
TIME in ifconfig upping the ethernet interface. After booting up, ifconfig
shows 0 for all counters, except TX errors.

I've tried different other ethernet cards (3c509, PCI-NE2000-clone,
SMC-Etherpower). The interesting one is the SMC card (DEC 21041 chip),
when put into the PCI bus, MILO will hang the systems on initializing the
AHA2740, putting out something along the lines "additional RAM detected".

Needless to say that a webserver without network interface doesn't make
much sense ? :-)
