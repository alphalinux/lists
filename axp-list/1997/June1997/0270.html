Roman Gollent wrote:
> I haven't solved this problem, but for what it's worth, the solution
> does not require you to remove any RAM. Simply
>
> setenv MEMORY_SIZE 64
Problem was: Kernel gets paging errors on UDB with 2x32M+2x8M.

I came upon another solution:
change to 2x32M+2x4M and everything is happy.

Funny how the 2x8M+2x4M works fine. I guess the SROM or whatsit
doesn't like putting 32s and 8s together. The UDB manual
http://www.brouhaha.com/~eric/computers/udb-man.pdf says
you can do it. Magic to me.
