On Jun 19, D. Unger wrote:

> > I am considering to buy a NE2000 card for my pc164 alpha (RH4.2).
> > Does anybody know which NE2000 ISA ethernet card runs,
> > what settings address and interupt must be set, etc

an article about the VOBIS pc164 based AXP box in c't 7/97 mentions
that NE2000 didn't work for them...

> After fighting with an NE2000 comp. (10Mbit) as well as an 3c905 10/100 Mbit
> card for a few days now, I plugged in an SMC card with the DEC 21041 Chip
> an hour ago and it just works with the PC164 IMMEDIATELY and without any work
> necessary! If you want to, I can give you a reference where to buy it (approx.
> 130.- +MWST)

LANTRONIX 10mbit (DEC 2104x) DM 70 +MwSt
LANTRONIX 10/100mbit (DEC 21140-AE) DM 110 +MwSt
form http://www.transtec.de/

we just bought the 10/100mbit cards for ix86 boxes and needed a new tulip driver from
http://cesdis.gsfc.nasa.gov/linux/drivers/tulip.html or pre-2.0.31,
but now they work like a charm...
