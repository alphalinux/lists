> This is a hot button for a few people. It is being handled by

More to the point, it is a rather delicate and tangled legal situation.
I talked to MadDog at great length about this, and we believe we're
on our way to a resolution which will not disappoint the Linux
community.

HOWEVER, given the nature of legal affairs, I believe it's vitally
important that we *not* discuss this case in an open forum such as
this until a final resolution is reached. Offhand comments do have
a way of biting you where and when you least expect it...
