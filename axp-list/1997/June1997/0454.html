I'm having some trouble locating a source of adequate power
supplies for the PC164.
Assuming these requirements:

Power requirements
------------------
+3.3 +5 +12 -5 -12 Volts
----------------------------------------------------------------------------
500MHz 21164 11.5 Amps
PC164 5 10 1 - 0.1
128Mb Mem (4 x 32Mbyte) - 4 - - -
+PCI cards - ? ?
+peripherals - ? ?
----------------------------------------------------------------------
Total: 16.5 14 1 - 0.1
(55W) (70W)
\___________/
125W
----------------------------------------------------------------------

The only ATX-compatible supplies I can locate so far, give:

200Wpsu: 14 20 8 0.5 0.8
\________/ \_________/
125W max 9.6W max

300Wpsu: 15 25 14 0.5 0.5
\________/
125W max

Neither of these is enough for the 3.3V rail, and the limit of
125W for the combined 3.3+5V supply means I cant have > 128Mb memory,
and with PCI cards and disk will probably exceed this limit anyway.
Does anyone know of a supplier of PSUs with really beefy 3.3 and 5V
rails? How are you guys with 1Gbyte of memory managing?

TIA,
