All right! There have been two rather dramatic developments! First,
off, we now have pledges for exactly $1500 in cash! Given the other
pledges (hw and providing a machine at cost), we definitely met the
original goal of funding half a machine for Craig. Congratulations!
It really shows that this is a great community to be in!

Now, the other dramatic development: in response to this campaign,
somebody stepped forward and offered a complete machine to Craig
(366MHz with plenty RAM and disks and all). This is great news, but
also opens the question of what we should do with the donations. I'd
like to propose the following: lets wait for a few days or so until we
know for sure whether Craig gets that free machine or not. If he
doesn't get it (for whatever reason), our pledge still stands and we
can start collecting the money. If he does get it, then I'd say it's
up to each individual to decide whether (s)he still wants to send the
check to Craig. Of course, you may want to keep in mind that Craig
depends on donations to keep going with g77 so I'm sure he would
appreciate your donation in either case.

Sounds reasonable?

Anyways, thanks to everybody who offered a donation!
