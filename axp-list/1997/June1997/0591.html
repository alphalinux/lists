Not sure if this will help or not. I had the same problem with
an AS250 4/266 with a DEC ZLXp-E3 (step 3) video card. Jay Estabrook
gave me some suggestions that led me to believe it could possibly be
a video card incompatibility. Here is what I did to be able to boot
milo:

1) Switch to SRM console ">>>"
2) set console serial
3) Get V2.0.28 of milo (.dd file) and rawrite or dd it to
a floppy.
4) boot dva0
5) At the milo> prompt boot RH 4.2 images.

I can't get any version later that 2.0.28 to work with this process
and I can't get the process to work from ARC console or from the
graphics console with SRM. Therefore I think there is a problem
with initializing the graphics device and instead of hanging
at Swapping to PALcode at 0x0 it just is unable to display the
next prompt to the console graphics device.

I'd be interested if anyone else has further ideas as I would
rather not have to connect a serial port on my alpha to boot.

Hope this helps.
