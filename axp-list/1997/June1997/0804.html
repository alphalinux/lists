Eric wrote about a monitor with

50.2khz horiz sync
60hz vert sync

I don't know if the values are OK, but assuming they are:

We tryed various modeline in this range
ModeLine "1024x768" 66 1024 1054 1304 1334 808 810 815 836 -vsync
ModeLine "1024x768" 64 1024 1054 1250 1280 768 770 778 806

Your first modeline results in 49.48 kHz / 59.18 Hz ;
the second gives 50 kHz / 62 Hz; both are slightly off...
I would try decreasing the vertical sync rate of the second modeline,
by using, say,

ModeLine "1024x768" 64 1024 1054 1250 1280 768 770 778 820
^^ ^^^^ ^^^
PixClk HorSync VertSync

BTW, you calculate the horizontal sync frequency by dividing the pixel
clock (in MHz) by horizontal sync period; to get vertical sync
frequency, you divide horizontal sync frequency by vertical sync period.

Good luck
przemek
