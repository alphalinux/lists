Hello:

I have a DEC Multia (UDB) which runs Red Hat Linux/Alpha 4.2
just fine with the on-board TGA video out. Wanting to run at
more than 8 bpp, and having the PCI slot free, I installed
a Number Nine 771 video card in the slot.

When I try to boot, the card seems to get properly initialized
by the DEC BIOS emulator, MILO runs, happily detects all the scsi
devices and such, and just after MILO
announces that the Linux kernel is about to load, the screen
output stops. Now, the system actually boots (I can telnet to it
just fine), I just can't see anything on the console.

Since I know that some folks out there have successfully gotten
PCI video cards to run in the Multia's PCI slot, I would greatly
appreciate any and all suggestions, ideas, and feedback. Thanks in
advance,

-M. Gilbert
