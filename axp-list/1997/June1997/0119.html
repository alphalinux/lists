>
> Finally getting to play with our new pws-433a running redhat-4.2 a bit.
>
> The EIDE performance on the quantum bigfoot seems REALLY slow. I.e.
> 800-1000 k/sec read/write.
>
> Cpu performance, memory performance, and general interactive performance
> all seem great.
>
> Any ideas if this is a temporary hack to support the new PXYIS chipset's
> EIDE which should be fixed soon? Or should I suggest paying the
> significant upgrade to scsi? (6.4 gb eide = $380, 4gb scsi+controller+

>
> Ideas? Suggestions?

for some bigfoot drives we bought here more than doubled their
speed. Have you tried tinkering with hdparm? What IDE chipset do the
Miata machines use, anyway?
