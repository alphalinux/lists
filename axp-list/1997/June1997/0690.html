I had exactly the same problem with my XL266. I think the milo image on the
4.2 CD is not quite okay (although I am not sure). But I used the milo file
from the previous distribution (4.1) with the new linload.exe and it works
just fine. My RedHat 4.2 Linux is now up and running just fine.

You can get the milo that came with the 4.1 CD from the net (most probably
from ftp://ftp.redhat.com/) or just email me and I'll send it to you.

Hope this helps,
