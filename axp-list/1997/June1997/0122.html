> Well, depending on how often the "unaligned trap" message appears,
> it may or may not be better to fix the unaligned accesses.

If it were just IPX, you would be right. As I understand it, at
the time we have to pull the header out of the ethernet frame we
don't know it is IPX, and copying things out and aligning them would
slow everything else down -- primarily IP.

So it is, I think, once per IPX packet, but not once per packet
in general. In any case, Alan and Linus refuse to muck things up
just for the sake of IPX.
