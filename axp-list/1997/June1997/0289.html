> Just had a look at the vobis machines.... but no info on what type of

The Vobis Alphas are PC164 based.

The differences between the EIDE and the SCSI systems are
- additional SCSI controller (Adaptec 2940),
- different HD and CDROM drives,
- different graphics card.

It is cheaper to buy an EIDE system and install SCSI components
yourself --- the graphics card of the SCSI system is not useable
with Linux, the EIDE implementation (I think of the PC164 MB itself)
is slow.
