Hey folks,
I have the good old 166 udb runing rh41. I have been useing it
with a 14 inch vga monitor (aaarrggh). Recently I was given an old
monitor that useto belong to an apollo domain 3000.
model number 008398-001 config #002
A friend got an old fixed freq monitor working by editing the modline for
X. We tryed to figure what it would be for this old beast with no luck.
One vid card vender gave me what he thought were the specs on the monitor
50.2khz horiz sync
60hz vert sync

We tryed various modeline in this range
ModeLine "1024x768" 66 1024 1054 1304 1334 808 810 815 836 -vsync
ModeLine "1024x768" 64 1024 1054 1250 1280 768 770 778 806

There was little to no horiz movement but there was alont of vertical
shaking usualy as much as 2 inches.

Anyone out there ever get one of these to work? or anything like it?
Please help me beat this beast of a sun god.
