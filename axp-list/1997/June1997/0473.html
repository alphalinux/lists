>>> m. capel said:
> we're running adaptec 2940UW's in some PC164's, connected to
> micropolis 3391WS ultra wide scsi drives. i've set the adaptec
> firmare to negotiate up to 40 MHz/sec...as the drives are capable
> of this speed according to their documentation. when the aic7xxx
> driver negotiates speed, during boot, we only get an indication of
> 20 MHz...is something set non-ptimally or is this the best we can expect?

The 2940UWs I've seen come from the factory with ULTRA disabled, which is
what you probably have encountered.

There's an Adaptec utility (acu.exe) that'll run directly from ARC that
offers *all* the setup possiblities for the 2940UW; look for it at:

www.windowsnt.digital.com

in the Drivers and Firmware file download pages.
