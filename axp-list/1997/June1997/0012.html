The quake core dump is not a kernel problem but a libc problem.

Something changed in libc between RH4.0 and RH4.1 that quake
doesn't like. I have no idea what. And at this point I'd rather
get Linus to build another quake binary against glibc 2.0 than
grovel around in 9 month old snapshots.

But if you can find one, a libc.so.6 from RH4.0, loaded specially
for quake with LD_LIBRARY_PATH, will make it work.
