Ok, I figured it out. The documentation that comes with the
motherboard (The part number is something ending in -B01) is wrong
(its actually not wrong, its deceptive) w.r.t. the serial pinout on
the motherboard. The new documentation that is available from dec's
ftp site is correct or at least clearer (The same part number, but
ending in -C01).

The -B01 and -C01 both IMPLY that the pin out is:

unused o o pin 5
pin 9 o o pin 4
pin 8 o o pin 3
pin 7 o o pin 2
pin 6 o o pin 1

It drawn in the document as:

5
o o
o o
o o
o o
o o
1

The -C01 document describes it that way, and it appears to work.

The -B01 document describes it as:

unused o o pin 9
pin 8 o o pin 7
pin 6 o o pin 5
pin 4 o o pin 3
pin 2 o o pin 1

(One of our techs sais the -B01 way is actually how this type of
connector is supposed to be numbered. The strong implication
otherwise is the problem.)
