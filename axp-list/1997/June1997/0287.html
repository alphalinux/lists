> >does anybody know if the soundcard which comes with the Vobis Alpha
> >is supported by Linux? And if yes, how do I have to configure the kernel?
> AFAIK, Vobis uses "SoundBoostar" with the Alpha. The "SoundBoostar" was made
> around the CreativeLabs Vibra16 chipset. If the Alpha BIOS or Linux configures

> the kernel).
