>> Jo Hausmann schrieb am Thu, 12 Jun 1997 15:07:29 +0100:

> Hi there! Has anyone got XEmacs-19.15 or better running on a
> PC164 machine? Maybe an rpm or a spec file? Patches for the
> XEmacs-20.2.src.rpm?

I have some binaries on
ftp://franck.pc.uni-koeln.de/pub

Since the XEmacs binaries are very sensitive to the used shared
libraries be sure to get the exact version which is suitable for
your settings (RH4.1 / RH4.2 + XFree-3.2 / RH4.2 + XFree-3.3).

Compilation of XEmacs-20.0 failed so far due to the bug in the Red Hat
supplied linker in respect to huge binaries.
