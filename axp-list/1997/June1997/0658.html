On 20-Jun-97 Terry Carroll wrote:
>My questions are 1) is there anything I should check at time of purchase
>to make sure the monitor will work with the UDB? and 2) does TGA support

I would try to get a multisync monitor as opposed to a fixed sync monitor.
Multisync monitors are just much less of a hassle, IMHO.

>SVGA, or should I just be looking for a VGA?

Sure, it supports SVGA, but only in 256 colors. I currently have mine
running at 1280x<whatever>. I haven't really checked, but I think that you
can rev them up to 1600x<whatever> if your monitor can take it :)
