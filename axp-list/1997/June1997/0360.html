The Alpha NT assembler (asaxp), which is available on the Alpha NT
Archive site (http://dutlbcz.lr.tudelft.nl/alphant/) comes with a
Windows help file as well as a readme.
The Alpha AXP Architecture reference Manual (second Edition) by Richard
Sites and Richard Witek (Digital Press) is about the best reference.
Another excellent reference is the Digital Unix Assembly Language
Programmers Guide. This is available online from the Digital web site.
The GAS assembler is a bit different than the DU or NT assemblers, but
most of the syntax is common.
(http://www.UNIX.digital.com/faqs/publications/base_doc/DOCUMENTATION/H
TML/AA-PS31D-TET1_html/TITLE.html)
