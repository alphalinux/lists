On Sun, 8 Jun 1997, Volker Barthelmann wrote:

> does anybody know if the soundcard which comes with the Vobis Alpha
> is supported by Linux? And if yes, how do I have to configure the kernel?

Can't you go and check their Windows installed Demo computer (I am sure
they have one in any Vobis store) and see what kind of settings/type it is.
The only Alpha soundcard I know is from the Multia. For Linux, you need
to enable Microsoft Sound system (Windows sound system) on io 530, irq 9
and dma 3,3 (single dma).
