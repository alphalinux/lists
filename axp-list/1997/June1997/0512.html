Kendrick,
I would try using three disks during the installation and then use the
ftp network installation(works very well). I would put the cabriolet
milo and linload.exe on one disk, the cab.img on another disk, and
ramdisk.img on another. Setup the srm and boot with the
milo/linload.exe, when you get to the milo prompt. stick in the cab.img
disk and type out your command line(yours is correct), then when
prompted insert the ramdisk.
If this fails try the cab-s.img instead of the cab.img.
good luck
