>
> Does anyone know of a way to control the frount panel LCD displays on the
> 1000A, 2100, and other assorted alpha systems?

On the 2000/2100, I think you can simply type in (to the SRM console):

>>> set ocp_text "your text here"

with a 16-character limit to the string length.

I suspect that other boxes are similar; do (under SRM):

>>> show | more

and see if you can find an envvar that contains what you see *now*...

Under ARC or AlphaBIOS, it may be similar but hidden away under CMOS
settings or some such...

Have fun!
