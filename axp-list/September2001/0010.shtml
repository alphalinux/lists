So begins the dark times of HPTC. Yeah, Intel will be weaning out new CPUs
and performace jumps to satisfy their revenue stream. Maximun profits for
them and minimun value for us. Oh and the hobbiest like me? What hobbiest?
Well you can always buy a Sun or an IBM. Though they don't have nearly as
much of a prescense in the Linux community on their native hardware. So
you'd most likely be running their OS. I'm talking about hardware we afford
too, not this S/390 crap. So, Does it really matter anymore what your code
runs on? I love Alphas, They're alot of fun and do a great job. After such
as decline what we've seen with Alpha (starting back in 97/98) I've learned
to never get attached to a hardware platform again. After all, they're only
machines. It'll still be one of my favorite hobbies :-).
