kernel on a FAT partition. I didn't have any problems with 6.2 reading
from the ext2. I wiped and installed 7.1 fresh and had that problem until
I moved the kernel that I wanted to boot from onto the FAT partition. Is
it known how to avoid this? I would rather not have to have a small FAT
partition on the drive. RH7.1 seemed to leave me no alternative.
