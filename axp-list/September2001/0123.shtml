That's a very good question. How do you measure how much stack you need? When I
first started to learn sysadmin, I was told - "Our software will crash unless
stacksize is set to unlimited". I just accepted that as fact. I do know that on
the alpha with 2.2.16 running, a stacksize of 32768 was enough to not cause the
machine to lock up, but it wasn't enough to prevent the software from crashing if
it required more than 128MB of RAM.

Ken
