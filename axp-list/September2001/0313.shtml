Hello there,

On Wed, 31 Oct 2001, hans van der heide wrote:

> I'm trying to install RH 7.1 on an AlphaServer 1000 4/266 (Mikasa EV45).
> Can somebody say what kind of SVGA-Controller I have to define in order to
> get graphics running.
        
        Maybe VGA16 might work, the chip is actually a cirrus one.

> I suppose I have to disable the onboard video-ctrl and plug in a
> graphics-card (what type do you suggest?)

        In my AS 1000 5/300 (EV5) a matrox millenium II was fine (and
much speedier than the elsa gloria synergy which replaced it. But SRM
support for the ELSA might not be there for EV45.) If you plug in a
graphics card be sure to use the appropriate jumper to disable the
on-board graphic.
