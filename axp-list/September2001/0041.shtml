On Fri, Sep 07, 2001 at 03:58:37PM -0400, 'Bryce' wrote:
> HappyRascal@aol.com wrote:
>
> > If I were to grab a new video card, any recommendations for decent perf?
> > Not gaming, just really want an MS alternative...and wish I had gotten
> > into Alpha long before the corporate technology slaughtering began.
>
> I get good milage out of voodoo3 cards even if the DRI interface for it will
> only work in 16bit and not 24.

Yes, 3DFx Voodoo3 2000/3000 PCI are good choices. I believe that Voodoo4
4500 PCI may also work, but I've not tested it.

> Other interesting options that will operate with DRI would be most mga cards
> (Matrox) and radeon boards. The ATI rage 128 should also work.
>
> DRI support is a BIG win. Try and get a card that supports it

MGA does DRI (3D) only on AGP cards. However, Millenniums and G200/400 PCI
are fine choices for 2D work.

ATI Rage 128 and Radeon PCI versions do work quite well, but you have
to be careful - many older boxes come with SRM console BIOS emulators
that will not work with the BIOS code on those cards. I think PWS 500au
should work with Rage 128, but may not with Radeon... ???

Good luck.
