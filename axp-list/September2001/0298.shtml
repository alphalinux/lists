ext3 is supposed to be included in the kernel "real soon now". Check out
your favorite kernel archives for the discussion. FYI ext3 is included in
the RH 7.2 and is the default install. If you backdate to an older kernel
that doesn't have ext3 support it should still be able to mount your ext3
partitions, it simply sees it as a ext2 partition :)

Here is the "ext3 homepage"
http://www.uow.edu.au/~andrewm/linux/ext3/ext3-usage.html

Information is available there and bugs are discussed.
