On Mon, Oct 22, 2001 at 11:21:30AM +0200, Peter Bech wrote:
>
> When trying to boot the CD on AS800, you get a message saying something like
> "error sending read to DKA400" and then "error reading DK400". It retires som
> 10 times and then goes back to SRM with "Boot failed".
>
> Standardwise, the CD is DKA400 on the AS800.

Is this a CD that has worked already on other machines? If not, maybe it
is bad. Or possibly the CDROM drive in the AS800 doesn't like CDRWs.

One possible workaround is to do the floppy boot-and-RAMdisk-load instead
of trying to access the CD from the console...
