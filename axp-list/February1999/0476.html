Hi,

I'm trying to export a filesystem from one axp to another axp and to x86,
using nfs.

Everything seems to be OK, I can mount on the remote (client) computer the
filesystem which is on the local (server). However, I have problems with
the ownership of the various files. The "translation" from server_users
to client_users is not correct. I think I understood that the translation
is based on the users id contained on the /etc/passwd file.

To be more specific, the server /etc/passwd has...

cavallo: passwd :507:507:Luigi Cavallo:/home/cavallo:/bin/tcsh

the client /etc/passwd has...

cavallo: passwd :500:500:Luigi Cavallo:/charlie/cavallo:/bin/tcsh
pino: passwd :507:507:Pino Milano:/home/pino:/bin/tcsh

The files that belong to cavallo on the server, they belong to pino on the
client.

Clearly I'm missing something in the setup. I went through the NFS-HOWTO,
but I didn't find something useful, or I missed it...

I know that probably is not a axp related problem, but there are several
gurus out there, which can help a non-guru like me.
