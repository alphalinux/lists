> Plan B: Let's say I was to compile and statically link our program on a Digital
> UNIX box. Would the Digital license place any restrictions or obligations on
> copying and selling the resulting executable on another platform?

Maddog asserts that yes, the fact that libc contains code from AT&T
Unix which is licensed in a funny way, you can only execute that
binary on a system which has a Digital Unix license. In order to get
around this, someone could port glibc2 to Digital Unix...
