Great, thanks for answering.
So, you have one drive, it is conencted to the inside port on the card.
As the drive is at one end of the chain, and the card at the other end you
turn on termination, both for low and high bytes.

If you plug in an external device you turn off one or both depending on if
the external device is a narrow or wide SCSI drive.
