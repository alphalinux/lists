There should be a safe boot/safe image jumper on the motherboard (check
your manual) that will get you to some type of BIOS (SRM, ARC or
AlphaBIOS - I don't remember) from there you can flash it again. If that
doesn't work you'll most likely have to call your local Compaq/DEC shop
and have them replace the ROM chip or whatever they recommend.

HTH,

Ron
