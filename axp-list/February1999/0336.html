Bernard Chew, Lai Wan wrote:
>
> Are there any special procedures I need to follow to get more than 1
> network card to work on an alpha?

A document, which you likely have on your disk, named BootPrompt-HOWTO
says:

  The most common use of this parameter is to force probing for a second
  ethercard, as the default is to only probe for one. This can be
  accomplished with a simple:

  ______________________________________________________________________
          ether=0,0,eth1
  ______________________________________________________________________

and some other details follow.

The other simple way to achieve your goal is to load card drivers as
modules and to add corresponding aliases in /etc/modules.conf

  Michal
