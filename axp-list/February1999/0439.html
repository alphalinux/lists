This isn't particularly useful to you, but I thought I'd mention it while we're
on the topic in case someone else is interested.

I haven't tested this with any GUI apps, because when I attempted to use it
as a Servlet JVM it went ballistic, eating 80% - 95% of the CPU time on my
SX164 533MHz -- while idle. This is not George's fault, but a known bug
with the green_threads port of the Sun JDK that goes way back. The solution
is to use, you guessed it, a native threads version or to turn off async GC.

So, I'm sticking with 1.1.7 until the (hopefully but not assuredly) more
stable native threads blackdown port is done.
