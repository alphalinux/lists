>>> "John C. Fremont" said:
>
> During boot the kernel does not recognize the PCI card for my DEC ALpha
> Workstation 600/533, ALCOR, 21164. When I run SuperProbe, it says I
> have a Cirrus Logic CLGD5424 Chipset. The pci.c and the pci.h does not
> contain an entry for this card/chipset. When I execute startx, it says
> "Fatal Server Error" "no screens found".

This is most likely a POwerstorm card of some flavor; at the SRM console
prompt, type "show config" and look for a card that identifies itself as
a TGA2 or Powerstorm 3D30 or 4D20. Unfortunately, there's no Xserver at
this time for the TGA2/3D30/4D20. The Cirrus 5424 VGA is there strictly to
provide text mode console support, and will run only the monochrome Xserver.
