Why don you just use the milo/images/udb-noname.img ?

Put this on a disk with help of dosutils/rawrite.exe .
Then Boot your Multia and add a boot menu entry with
OSLOADER \linload.exe on your floppy drive.

Then you show get a milo prompt (available commandos will be shown be
typing help) and be able to boot the kernel disk too.
