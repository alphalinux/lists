That'll be great, break backward compatibility. Maybe this would be
similar to Motorola's Alta-Vec, or the way KNI should have been. These
tend to improve operations that are media intensive, DSP, audio, video, 3D
& animation, as those tend to be the easiest to parallel operations.

For some or many of the claimed features of IA-64, the Alpha teams claim
is simple to implement on Alpha, such as lock-step and predication, and
the many of the compiler tricks to get performance out of IA-64 should
also improve Alpha.

Again, I do not know the gritty details of what I wrote here, so I could
be totally wrong. I am drawing from my CE/EE education, the little info
that I can get, and my own reasoning.
