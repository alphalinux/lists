The UDB takes a standard 4pin 4.5volt PC bios battery.
I beleive the pinout is (+5/null/null/ground) with one
of the center pins blocked out for alignment.

For additional info about "field-servicing" a udb,
download http://www.op.net/~rbowles/axp/udb-man.pdf
