On Wed, 24 Feb 1999, Tom Browder wrote:

> I. too, have stability problems with PC164--attributed I believe
> to the Adaptec 2940UW. I cannot use kernels 2.0.35 or 2.0.36.

If you can, try 2.2.1. It's sweet.

> How much is the Intraserver 3140 and where do you recommend
> getting one?

Not sure how much it cost, as my dealer swapped out the 2940UW for the
3140 even-up. Check IntraServer's site (www.intraserver.com) for a
list of dealers. While you're there, you might also want to look at
the 4000 series - they combine UW-SCSI with 10/100 ethernet. I've got
one of these (bought at a local (Calgary) shop) in my dual-PII. I've
yet to have any problems with either of these cards - colour me a fan.
