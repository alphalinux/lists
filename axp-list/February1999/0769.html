It's a "well known" fact that a 7200rpm drive will lead to problems in the
Multia, it has to do with long-term heat buildup shortening the life of the
motherboard.... With this in mind, I actually traded my 7200rpm drive for a
5400rpm one just in case; the guy I traded with was glad to get a faster
drive and I get a "guarantee" that it won't mess up my computer.

My $.02

One question on the RAM. Where did you get it and how much did it cost? I
need to add RAM to my other Multia and don't want to spend $99 per 32mb SIMM
at Starship Corp if I can help it.
