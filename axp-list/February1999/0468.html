8 is the major number. Two is the minor. minor/16 + 'a' will give you the
drive letter. minor%16 will give you the partition. Make sure that of the
two disks you show in the messages that sda *is* in fact the disk you
have linux is on. There's also a possibility (very small) that the command
line can get scrogged between milo or srm- you can edit
sys/arch/alpha/kernel/setup.s to change 0x802 to 0x8XX for whichever you
want the default root device to be.
