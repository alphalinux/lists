Your original compiler is probably /usr/bin/gcc. The new egcs is probably
/usr/local/bin/gcc. Type "which gcc" to see the one that is your default
gcc. Type "gcc -v" to see your version number. You can change the sequence
of your PATH variable, to force the new one to become your default. So in
your .profile (.bash_profile, whatever):

PATH=/usr/local/bin:$PATH
export PATH

Hope that's what you need.
