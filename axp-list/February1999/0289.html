I have to agree...

I had similar problems in the beginning with my 3940UW... I remember
going into the SCSI BIOS and changing settings and all of sudden bus
resets didn't freeze the system. It did this during installation, so I
had to find a way to get around it/fix it early on... I just can't
remember exactly the changes I made.. but I do remember turning off all
'auto' type settings and doing everything manually. Same on the SCSI
devices, hard set SCSI IDs and termination. I just don't trust "auto"
things.... I also have another 2940UW in another machine,... which works
beautifully. By the way, I too have mixed devices on one channel.

>I think that many of you may benefit by running the configuration

rate.

set

> If you have ARC, AlphaBIOS, or similar BIOS types, you can get the

>In my experience, on almost any machine, with mixed drives, on any OS,
