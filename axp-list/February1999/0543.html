Alvin Starr <alvin@iplink.net> writes:

> I am trying to figure out what does not work in DU under linux. Is there
> any body who has had some experiance with moving a applications from DU to
> linux using the DU libs.

Do you just want a list of applications? I can provide a list of ones
that I have tried. If you want a list of which DU features are not
supported (so applications that use these features will break),
somebody who knows more about the Linux kernel than me will have to
reply.

These responses apply to Linux 2.2.1 and DU 4.0D libs:

Netscape 4.5 mostly works (the browser part works as well or better
        than under DU, but the mail portion does not seem to work)
Adobe Acrobate 3 works
xemacs 20.4 works (I have not banged on it hard yet, though)
various small command line Fortran and C number crunching programs
        work
SAS for DU does not work
