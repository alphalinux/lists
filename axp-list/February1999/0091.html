On Tue, 2 Feb 1999, Gavin wrote:

> Hi ,
> any one know howto configure shadow passwords with RH 5.0 .. i have seen
> an rpm , named like that ., but the howto , is the question ..

If you install the shadow-utils RPM you are ready to go. All you have to
do then is read the man page for pwconv and grpconv; those commands will
convert your standard password and group files (the latter of which is
usually not necessary to convert) to shadow ones. You shouldn't need to do
much else, other than make sure you check any other documentation in
/usr/doc/shadow-utils-version.

Hope this helps,
