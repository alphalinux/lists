> > > >Ok.. I just looked at the specs for the 21264... why is it still a
> > > >.35micron chip? Almost everyone has gone below that.. and towards the
> > > >.18micron.... can you imagine the kind of heat a 0.35 micron chip can
> > > >generate at 600Mhz? I don't get it... does Samsung lack the technology?
        Well, my 0.35um 21164a at 500Mhz does not throw off a lot of heat.
Granted, DEC did a great job with the cooling system in the PWS series, but
it still is quite cool considering the speed. I always make a funny face
colleagues tell me that their 400 or 450Mhz x86 systems require three fans
and a peltier cooler to run stably without heat-induced problems.
> > > >Can they not license it from someone like AMD? Or are they just slow in
> > > >upgrading their fabs?
        Perhaps because Alpha Processor, Inc. does not feel pressure bearing
down to convert their current fab plants to a smaller process? I certainly
hope Compaq will move Alpha more and more into the consumer market - with
that strategic move, perhaps they will be motivated to get with the times,
so to speak.
