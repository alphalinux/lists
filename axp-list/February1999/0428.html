> Is it possible to compile sources on the Alpha and get Intel-Binarys as result ?

You may be able to build gcc as a cross compiler Alpha->i386 but look
at the makefiles and configure scripts of the stuff you want to compile.
Sometimes they do things like "uname -m" or effectively sizeof(int) at
compile time. So even with a working crosscompiler you will have a
_lot_ of makefile hacking to do. You will also need the i386 "dot H"
files. Would be a lot of work but in theroy, I think possible
