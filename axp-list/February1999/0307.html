Hi Tom --
If the card thinks it needs to apply a terminator, you need to convince
it otherwise, given your h/w config. You should have the last internal
device terminated and the last external likewise. The host adapter
should certainly not do it. The ctrl-A stuff is probably x86-based.
If you've got jumpers available, go for it. I'd get deeper, but I
only have experience with generic scsi and not with your card.
Good luck,
-ls-
