I found there's a missing "#include <linux/delay.h>" in sb_ess.c in
2.2.1. Otherwise udelay doesn't get defined, and when you try to load
the soundblaster stuff as a module it complains about unresolved
symbols.

Sound now works again with my AWE64 and 2.2.1. I haven't seen this in
any patches yet, is it somewhere?

-Wes.
