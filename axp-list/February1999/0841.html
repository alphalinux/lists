I have bttv working on my alpha much more so than I did on a pc. Try
compiling xawtv without the X extensions stuff. When you get xawtv going,
try grabdisplay mode rather than overlay from one of the menus. It should
be easy enough to get overlay working with the correct videomem= option. I
dont know about sound, as I dont believe it is supported for my card.
