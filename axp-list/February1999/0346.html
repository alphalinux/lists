Sure. The choices in XServers for Alpha are very limited in XFree86 for Alpha.
They include:
ATI MAch 64, Rage II and so forht
SVGA - Matrox Millenium, Mystique, G200 and so on.
3DLabs - Permedia 2 stuff
S3 - Includes Trio, 765,86x,96x, NOT Virge, there is *some* driver for the
Virge.
TGA 1 ( I heard of something for TGA2, but I don't know much on it..)

That is about it.

Of course you could buy a server from Metrolink or XiG.
They support quite a few cards.
