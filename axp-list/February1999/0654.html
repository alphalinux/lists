The parallel Mono-QuickCam and Color-QuickCam _might_ work,
though I've never tested it or heard of anyone who has...
Good luck finding one, I don't think they're in production.
This driver does _not_ work with the new QuickCam-II or QuickCam-VC
and no developers are even considering support for them.

Does your AlphaStation have a free pci-slot?
If so, grab any bt848-chipset card/camera any you should
be flying fine. These cards allow (much) better frame rates
that parallel-port solutions. For more information start at:
        "/usr/src/linux/Documentation/video4linux/bttv/"

I've heard good reports about the "Panasonic EggCam",
and my "USRobotics-BigPicture" works fine in one of my
UDB's (except it is a little flaky about initializing).
