        Another great choice is a Permedia-2 based card. I purchased my
Diamond FireGL 1000 Pro w/8M for $140 - prices have fallen since then,
I'm certain. The side-benefits of the Permedia2 are that XF86 supports
it just fine, and that chipset will be supported in both Metrolink's
Extreme3D product and XiG's OpenGL hardware-accel X server.
