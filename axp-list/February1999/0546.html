> > When I power it on, it only gives me six beeps (and a black screen). Six
> > beeps are supposed to mean 'Checksum error detected when image was read

when i installed my system at first, i also had a problem with beeps.
my millenium 2 graphics card or one of the other pci cards caused the
problem, so i removed every card except for an old s3 based graphics
cards and made a firmware update to v5.66. afterwards i reinserted all
card and everything works fine since then :-)
