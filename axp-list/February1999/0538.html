> I'v downloaded the patchs concerning linux-axp at linux.applixware.com,
> compiled iodbc and a postgres odbc driver, but i'v a "connection refused"
> error when connecting to the database. strace on axnet tel me that it try
> to connect on port number 5492 (if i recall right), but there is no

For one thing, you need to make sure you have a line for axnet in your
/etc/services file...

        axnet 5492/tcp

I'm not certain if that's your problem, as you're the first person I've
heard of who's working on using Data and PostgreSQL on Alpha. You may also
want to subscribe to applixware-list@applix.com to find some other folks
who've done quite a bit more tinkering with Applix Data on the x86
platform. To subscribe, send "subscribe" in the body of an e-mail to
applixware-list-request@applix.com.
