Given that I am die-hard vi(m) hacker, saving a file should be easily to
explain:
vi has two modes, a command mode and a text entry mode.
You probably have discovered the text entry mode on your own.
To save a file, hit <Esc> (enters command mode), <:>, hit <w><q><Enter>
I am bracketing the keys, so "<w>" simply means you hit the 'w' key.
