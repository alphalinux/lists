> Perhaps because Alpha Processor, Inc. does not feel pressure bearing

> hope Compaq will move Alpha more and more into the consumer market - with
> that strategic move, perhaps they will be motivated to get with the times,

Can we please stop speculating without bothering to refer to the
facts? I believe that it was announced a while ago that the EV57 --
which is a 21164 produced on the next-smaller-than-0.35 process -- was
going to be the cheap processor for the consumer market. At the same
time, the EV6 is so late that the EV67 will be out fairly soon. This
is the 21264a shrunk down from the original 21264.

Now I don't know if you want to believe the dates that were
announced. But the strategy is clear, so there's no reason to be
posting dozens of emails to this list speculating about what's already
known.
