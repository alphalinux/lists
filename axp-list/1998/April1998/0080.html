Are you using, by any chance, 'make menuconfig'? It is not recommended
as it will probably do something bad. You have a better chances with
'make xconfig', but the really working at this moment is really only
'make config' and 'make oldconfig'.

Especially the later one is the way to go if you are doing updates
or small changes (edit .config and run 'make oldconfig'). You will
be hardly asked for anything but new options, which were not present
in your old .config, will elicit questions.
