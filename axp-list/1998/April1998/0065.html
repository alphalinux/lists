Finally my Jensen boots using aboot... But the problems are not finished
:-(((

I used minlabel utility to partidion /dev/sda....
Here is my partition table:

/dev/sda1 -> 8MB (boot)
/dev/sda2 -> 450MB(root)
/dev/sda3 -> 32MB(swap)

I used copyboot exe to copy the jensen.img to /dev/sda1
I swiched to OSF and at the aboot prompt I typed:
1/vmlinux.gz root=/dev/fd0 load_ramdisk=1
The kernel boots and after loading the ramdisk I jump to RH5.0 Install..

I tried to install it from ftp, the network card (DE202) is properly
recognized and the distribution is on a local ftp server... So I go
trough the rest of the installation as usually without repartitioning my

I am not really sure where is the problem... Anyone knows???
