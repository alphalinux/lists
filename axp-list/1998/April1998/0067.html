Steve Williams wrote:
>
> > And how did you managed to include a reference
> > to an old loader in your executables?
>
> *I* didn't do it. These dependencies are there. And that's not the only
> program so confused. I did notice that there was a ld.so.1 in /lib that
> was a link to ld-2.0.6.so, even after I upgraded to 2.0.7.

That is exactly the reason why I wrote that you should link
'ln -s ld-linux.so.2 ld.so.1'. Then you will be a minor version
independent. Now you will switch to 2.0.8, or back to 2.0.6, and
you will have the same trouble as before.

I wonder what created this link in the first place. I do not recall
having it after an installation "from scratch" of 5.0.

> Adding a symbolic link seems to fix it for now.

Well, so indeed new linker will work with older libraries. :-)
