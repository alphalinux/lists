> >> Could the 233MHz 21066A be dropped into a Multia? (Speaking of Onsale)
> >
> > If you're good with a soldering iron. :)
>
> No matter how good you are with soldering the VX-40 MB does not have the CPU
> speed selection jumpers.

If you are good enough to resolder the entire CPU, the ADDING the jumpers
shouldn't be a problem for you. But it isn't worth the effort anyways.
There is too much chance to screw up and weighing the cost of the new chip
vs. the small potential performance increase vs. the chance of totally
messing up the main board, and the hundreds of pins that must be
desoldered perfectly, all suggest a 'no'.

Oh, the ONSALE chip noted can't be worth half the asking price. It
evidently isn't worth the present asking price to anyone - no one has
bought it, after being on the market for a month (I think). It just may
be the same one offered on eBay for 200$, now it is at 150$
