Hummm . . . personally, I really don't care if the man acts like an ass or
not. It's a free country and he has the right to be what he is. I prefer
to see if the "proof is in the pudding" . . . meaning, if the product can
do as advertised, under stress, then I'm happy. My goal is the smooth
operation of my services, not engaging in flame-wars.

Currently, sendmail *is* the ranking king of Internet e-mail services.
It's old, tested, tried, and true. Eric, the creator of sendmail, is also
working on better security and functionality for the product all the time.
It may be a bit hard to configure sometimes, depending on what you want
it to do, but you can't get that kind of flexibility without having a
certain amount of complication. Sendmail Pro will probably come, someday,
with a GUI interface to compile, install, configure, and maintain it. I
wish Eric luck in this endeavor.

Bottom line: Make my life easier and you're the greatest thing since
sliced bread. Make it more complicated? I won't remember your name.
Simple.

Just my $0.02.

Enjoy!
