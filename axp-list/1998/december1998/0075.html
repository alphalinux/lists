On Sat, Nov 07, 1998 at 02:34:18PM -0600, Bibek Sahu wrote:
> While working on the ggi kernel stuff, I've run into a little
> problem working in the ggi stuff. The kernel's trying to page address
> 0xa000000, which is actually the address of the framebuffer of my video
> card.

Actually, the kernel isn't literally trying to page that address,
it just means that that addresss isn't mapped in that process.

It is highly likely that you forgot that bus addresses are NOT the
same as memory addresses, and so are missing a readl/writel.
