When I was putting my Cabriolet together I asked the same question. The
manual is a bit vague about combinations. Although I have 8 8M true
parity simms and have not tried to insatall say 8's in one bank and 16's
in another, most people I asked on this list felt it would be ok to use
different sizes in each bank.

The motherboard manual can be found here:
http://ftp.digital.com/pub/Digital/info/semiconductor/literature/archives.html
along with other *.pdf you might wish to view.
