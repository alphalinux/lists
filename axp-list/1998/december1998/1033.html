>Nope. The problem is that it uses assembler to grab the parallel port
>status (assumption: the compile bombs because of asm directives).
>
>Anyhow, it'll take someone with experience in Alpha assembler to get
>this one ported; which just put the whole thing out of my grasp.

If it just uses inb and outb instructions then the thing to do is to convert
it to use <sys/io.h> and the corresponding C functions (of the same name...) -
that will work on Alpha and probably most other platforms too.

For 2.1 kernels there is also a driver available to let you poke at the
parallel port from user space without having to know the details of the
hardware.
