Have you tried:
        find / -exec grep nxterm {}\; -print
where nxterm is the startup program you are looking for? Oh yeah don't
try this without having something else to do while it runs ;) If your
version of find is broken, you may need to do something like:
        touch /tmp/empty.file
        find ./ -exec grep nxterm {} /tmp/empty.file \; -print
