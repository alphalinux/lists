Here's the listed "Trio" cards I see in the Xfree database. Is your card
one of these?

299 S3 86C764 (generic) S3 Trio64
300 S3 86C765 (generic) S3 Trio64V+
301 S3 86C775 (generic) S3 Trio64V2
302 S3 86C785 (generic) S3 Trio64V2

They pretty much all use the SVGA X server. Are you running XConfigurator
or xf86config to configure the card?

As long as your card is listed, and you know your monitor settings, this
should work.
