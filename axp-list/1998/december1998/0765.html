Both milo and the kernel wine when i have auto termination on my 2940UW.
Just set terminiation to high on both ends and be sure the terminating
resistor is activated on you last drive. My setup is:
on the ultra bus:
scsi id 0: 4.4 UW HD
scsi id 6: zip external
On the scsi-2 bus
scsi id 2: 32x cdrom

The terminating resistor is enabled on the cdrom closing up the scsi-2
bus
and the zip has termination on also. I have to leave it on all the time
too.
