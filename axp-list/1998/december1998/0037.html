Just as a data point, I paid $890 for my XL300, new, with WinNT 4.0. Came
with the usual stuff--i.e. 1GB Narrow Fireball, Millennium I/2MB, Tulip
10-base-T, 2X SCSI cdrom, explosion-proof case, etc. Great Lakes
Computers, www.glcomp.com, ask for Kathy.
