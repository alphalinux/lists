Hi,

I'm using the 2.1.130 for approx. 2 days, now on an SX164.
I haven't had any problems, yet.

One strange thing concerning the last stable kernels:

As the SX owners probably know, the stock kernel has problems with the floppy
driver (you can't access the floppy). Jay fixed it in his patches (for the
2.0.35), but I had to patch the 2.0.36, myself. The strange thing is, that the
2.1.xx kernels I have tried so far (126-130) all have a working floppy driver.
