[AS200 and sound]

> I read section G of my Alpha manual (which talks about the sound card)
> but I did not find a clue. I am running RH 5.2 with the stock Avanti
> kernel, boot from ARC, and use KDE 1.0.

Does the "stock" RH 5.2 kernel even support modules?

Unless you've changed some jumpers in your AS200 (or are using
another), your sound card is a Windows Sound System compatible card,
at 0x530 with IRQ 9. I forget where the MIDI ioport is (because I
don't use it and don't have the Alpha manual handy).

> In addition, I have installed sndconfig but the once I launch it gives me
> no sound card model options! I saw all the setting configurations in the
> next menu but I have no idea what to change to make the card work.

Unless they've finally decided to support Alphas with sndconfig in RH
5.2, it won't do you any good (I use 5.1 and am not sure about the
status of everything in 5.2).

Your best bet is probably to recompile yourself a custom kernel with
the wss driver and the correct ioport and IRQ settings.
