Yes, I am a long-time user of Applix on Solaris and befor that
on SunOS. I have it on Linux/i386. The macro language is called
"elf". Our company has written quite a bit of ELF code. It can
do just about anyting as you can "exec" a UNIX process from within
ELF. We have some buttons on the grapics and spreadsheet buttom
bars that fire off a DBMS report writter and import the result
back into Applix where some charts get automatically generated.

One good thing about Applix is that it is mature. It was been
around for years and grew up under UNIX from the start. At $99
it is a steal. We've paid over $1K per seat.
