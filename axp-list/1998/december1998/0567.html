Gang,

You know, I would love to see someone write and unbiased (yeah, right!
;-> ) white paper on the strengths and weaknesses of all the known MTAs.
The paper would include such information as:

1. Mail deliveries per hour or per day using the same OS, hardware, and
queue.
2. A check list of features in tabular format.
3. A list of known bugs for each MTA
4. A list of known security issues for each MTA.
5. etc . . .

I guess I'm looking for a document to let people, who are not already
religious about their MTA, make sound choices based on features and and
abilities.

Anyone know of anything even close to this?
