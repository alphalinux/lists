There is hardly any room inside a Multia/UDB for anything, let alone a
daughter card with a processor on it...

DEC did make desktop systems that could take AXP CPU daughter
cards, but they were servers/desktop systems.

DEC made an Intel Pentium-based Multia, but it had an IDE drive, an
S3 video subsystem, and a few other differences, take a look at the
Multia Handbook, it describes the various systems pretty well - I think
they are VX50 models...

Hope this helps,
