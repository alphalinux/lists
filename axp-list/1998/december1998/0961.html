Red Hat has moved some of X11's files/directories. It has been my experience
that *on some* files/directories critical links are not made. Have you
checked /usr/X11R6/lib/X11? I found a directory there (fvwm2) that
should have been a link back to /etc/X11.

As I see you are using xdm, the line of execution is as follows (from
the login):

1. GiveConsole
2. Xsetup_0
3. Xsession

You will see that Xsession executes ~/.xsession which is linked to
~/.Xclients. This is where your window manager *should* normally be
executed (the *last* item).

I hope this helps. I am fairly certain you have a missing link or else
your configuration is totally screwed up (not necessarily your fault).
