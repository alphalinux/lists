> IF there is some partition table on a media you put in "sdb" then this
> means that your drive does not read a disk inside. All these removables
> have this annoying property that sometimes what looks like a properly
> inserted cartridge is not recognized as such by a drive. Try to
> push it gently, or wiggle it a bit, or eject and reinsert once again.
> Sometimes there is a light on a drive which helps with that by providing
> a visual feedback; other times something has to "click". Otherwise try
> with a different cartridge. Sounds like a mechanical/electrical problem
> not directly associated with OS but if media are unreadable then you
> will get an error message like you qouted.
