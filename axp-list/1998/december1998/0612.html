Hi,

Today one of our Alphas (PC 164 board, 500 MHz, 128 MB RAM, RH 4.1/2.0.27)
lost network access. I could still log in at the console,
and found the following messages in the messages file:

kernel: Couldn't get a free page.....
kernel: eth0: Insufficient memory; nuking packet.

I did a ps auxw and found that four processes were showing 99.9 %
CPU usage: init, kflushd, kswapd, and /usr/bin/X11R6/X. Doing a shutdown
just hung, presumably due to the CPU being maxed out. I know I've heard
about these things before (I checked the archive but the search
feature isn't working), and am just wondering what might have
happened and/or what I can do short of resetting the system to
recover from this.
