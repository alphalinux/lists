I just posted this on alphalinux.org and thought I'd send it to
axp-also. If anbody is interested in helping, let us know.

"In what could be one of the biggest AlphaLinux news items of the year,
PATMOS and
the AlphaLinux community are working together to build a 256 node
AlphaLinux Cluster. Details are still being sketched out
at this point. More information will be made available at the PATMOS
(www.patmos-universal.com) and
alphalinux.org (www.alphalinux.org) websites. If you are interested in
helping build the worlds largest AlphaLinux cluster you can send mail to
either (rdp@cheshire.net) myself or to (rescatex@dmv.com)
Bill Anderson. A few pictures of the incoming hardware are available at
the (www.patmos-universal.com) PATMOS website, follow the link for
Parallel Systems."
