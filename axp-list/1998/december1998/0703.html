>>> Peter Petrakis said:
> Correct me if i'm wrong but doesn't debian alpha use SRM exclusivly
> without the use of milo or am I mixing this up with NetBSD.The theory
> being if you can boot linux with just SRM you could get rid of milo
> altogether?

You're wrong; at least NetBSD (don't know about the other *BSDs)
requires SRM, and cannot boot via MILO, as far as I know. I believe
it's a matter of dependence on some SRM support facilities that MILO
doesn't have.

Debian has built some MILOs, but they have also built some kernels
that boot from SRM, so they are not exclusivley one or the other.
