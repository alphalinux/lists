Hi,

Just thought I'd share my experiences.. I also tried using the rawhide
RPMs for egcs... it ruined my system. I don't recall exactly anymore,
but there were some new and odd dependencies on those RPMs... and I
started getting a lot of errors. Eventually, I had to find out where all
the files were installed, and delete them manually, as RPM decided to
think that egcs1.1.1-2 was not installed. I then went back to egcs
1.0.3a got the source for egcs 1.1.1 release, and recompiled myself.
After that, it's been working fine.

Plus, recompiling yourself, you get to pick your own optimizations for
your platform... I typically add the following for my PWS 500a:

-mcpu=ev56 -Wa,-m21164a

To be totally off topic.... has anyone here gotten SSH2 to work nicely
on Alpha/Linux??? I just compiled it.. few minor warnings, but it went
well. However, when i run sshd2, I get unalignment traps, or
occasionally a crash with no core.
