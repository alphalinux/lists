I have an AS 200 4/233 with the stock sound card, and even though I can
play music CDs and read CD ROMs I cannot play system sounds (.wav files).
Although the alarm bell beeps, I cannot attach nor test .wav files to
system actions (I am using KDE which lets me attach sounds to system
actions). Furthermore, I frequently read the following error message when
I logout from an X session:

Starting audioserver with talk id 2817
maudio: cannot open audio device

Moreover, the kaudioserver occasionally bombs the system with the
following error message:

kaudioserver: memory violation at pc=1555672aa60 rp=155558fca54 (bad
address = 00000000)

I read section G of my Alpha manual (which talks about the sound card)
but I did not find a clue. I am running RH 5.2 with the stock Avanti
kernel, boot from ARC, and use KDE 1.0. These are my conf.modules (/etc/)
configurations:

options io=0x220 irq=5 dma=0 mpu_io=0x330
alias sound sb
alias midi opl3
options opl3 io=0x388
options sb io=0x220 irq=5 dma=1 mpu_io=0x330 acer=1

In addition, I have installed sndconfig but the once I launch it gives me
no sound card model options! I saw all the setting configurations in the
next menu but I have no idea what to change to make the card work.

Could someone give me an idea about what to do, or to point me in the
right direction? TIA.
