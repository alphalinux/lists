I would be glad to receive suggestions for a good and stable graphic
card. I know I can check XFree 3.3.3 for the supported ones. But this is
not the question. I would avoid to have several "small" pains, that often
comes with supported but not STABLE pieces.

At the moment, I experienced with a S3_Virge, but I found it quite
unstable. X-applications (netscape 4.5 most noticeably) totally hang the
box. The sw_mouse and no_pci_disconnect options didn't help too much.

With a MilleniumII (borrowed from a PC for tests), I'v been much more
happy, but I still didn't find the right XF86 configuration to have a
fixed frequencies IBM monitor to work. (With the S3 it has been quite
simple).

What I need, is not a Super-Duper card. Something in the price range of
the Millenium, or G200, would be OK. I would like to have the card
working with KDE, no particularly heavy X-applications.
