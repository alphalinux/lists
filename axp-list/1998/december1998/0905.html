I don't think so. I went to 96 megs without having to change anything
on my AS200.

> The next mission, I suppose, is to find a web browser that will work. I
> don't have access to any DEC Unix libraries at the moment. Is there another
> working solution?

Lynx and kfm are the most stable options (kfm = the file manager /
browser that comes with KDE). The 100898 sources of Mozilla will
compile and run with Lesstif, though it's not particularly stable.
(The latest Mozilla sources will compile with GTK, but will segfault
immediately upon startup - no fun).

I typically use the aofrementioned Mozilla/lesstif combination for
browsing on my AS200, as it handles most of what I need in a browser.
Running x86 Netscape under em86 might also be an option, though I
haven't had time to experiment with that solution yet.
