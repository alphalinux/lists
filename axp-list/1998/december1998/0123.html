> hi!
> i've got some problems getting my NE*000 ISA-Clone
> working with kernels >2.1.105 (<=2.1.105 and 2.0.xx kernels work).
>
> the error message sounds like:
> ne.c ................
> not found.........(invalid signature 43 53)
>
> and additional:
> ..........ne.o: init_module: Device or resource busy
>
> i tried
> * compiling as a module and not
> * egcs 1.0.3a and pre-release 2
> * with/without the SUPPORT_NE_BAD_CLONES option in ne.c

Did you try to specify the IO port and interrupt in /etc/conf.modules (if you
compiled it as a module). If you compile it directlt into the kernel I suppose
you will have to specify IO and IRQ at the MILO prompt.

If everything else fails:

" 7.2. Diagnostic Programs for Ethernet Cards
[...]
NE2000 cards -- look for the file /pub/linux/diag/ne2k.c on
  cesdis.gsfc.nasa.gov. There is also a PCI version for the now common
  NE2000-PCI clones."
(quoted from the Ethernet-HOWTO)

That program helped me detect my NE2000 on my i486.
