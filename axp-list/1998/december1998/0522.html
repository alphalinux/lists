Agreed. I use sendmail for a reason. It's high stress and high volume
tested. It's usually not sendmail that breaks under heavy load. It's
usually either the OS (seldom) or the hardware (besacue the clients are
beating it to death!). When qmail has an equal share of the Internet

have beaten on it to work out the major and minor bugs.
