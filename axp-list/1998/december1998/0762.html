I don't know if this is related to your problem, but I have made some
alpha-specific patches to mpg123-0.59o. You can find them at
ftp://draal.physics.wisc.edu/pub/mp3/.

The development on mpg123 seems to be at a standstill (the maintainer hasn't
updated it since Feb, and doesn't respond to my mail -- though I know he
retreived my patch). Try my version of mpg123, and if it still doesn't
work, the problem probably isn't with mpg123. ;)
