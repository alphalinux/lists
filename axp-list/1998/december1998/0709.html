The reference to the filling of all 8 slots to get wider memory access is
for the
 PCI164, not the PCI64.
    ^ Note that one little important character!!
The AlphaPC164 was the first ATX motherboard running the 21164 chips. The
PCI64 is the older EB64 or Cabriolet board running a 21064A chip.
Anyway, you can fill either or both banks of 4 slots with SIMMs.
