Like it's been said. Connect two cables only to your SCSI host adapter.
Either two inside or one inside and one outside. Terminate both ends of
the bus, preferrably with active termination. You can use an adapter for
the SCSI cdrom drive. Or you can wait til january (if the ship date doesn't
slip) and be the first to own one of Plextor's UltraWide scsi cdrom drives.

40MBs. Their rating it at 14x-40x. But like all Plextor's drives you pay the
price of their name. $$$
