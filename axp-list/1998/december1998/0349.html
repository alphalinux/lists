Randy Carpenter wrote:
>
> SPeaking of cd writer's... has anyone found a good cd writer program that
> actually allows you to make direct scsi-scsi copies of cd's ? I need the
> ability to copy cd's with multiple data and/or music tracks. This works
> great on my windoze box... Oh, and it certainly needs to work on alpha :)

xcdroast does this. The author warns however that the reader be
at least 2X the writter's speed. I found out he was right and
wasted some blanks. So for direct copies I now slow down the CDR
to 1x as my reader is only 3x. This works fine.
