It doesn't hurt to be patient.

CPUMicromart is selling about 200-500 of these multias a week. The
salesperson I spoke to was very helpful and nice. They don't have a lot of
info on these machines, because they just picked up about 9000 of them in a
DEC FRU closeout. So, if you want a Multia cheap, be patient, deal with
the problems and get one.

I got two. They didn't double bill me, the order took 4 days longer than I
was told, but who cares?! It's the holiday season and I got Multias for $59!
