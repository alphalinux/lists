Jon,

I don't know if you solved your DNS problem yet, but I thought I would
drop you a note.

I had the 'same' problem with many versions of netscape (3 - 4.5) with
both the DU 3.2 and 4.0 libraries. I finally solved the problem by
compiling 2.1.129 under rh5.2 and _painstakingly_ removing all soft links
from ALL of the DEC libraries (4.0) and placing them all directly in
/usr/shlib. Magically after this, I rebooted and Netscape finally worked
(no mail though, even with the "touch /etc/sia" trick). I haven't
experienced any /sbin/loader errors with this configuration, although
prior to removing some of the softlinks with the 3.2 libs I did. Have
faith, it works.

Good luck.
