Hello Luigi,

I have an ELsa Winner 2000 in my UDB, driving an 6091/19, using the following
"Monitor"-section in XF86Config:

    Section "Monitor"
        Identifier "6091 19"
        VendorName "IBM"
        ModelName "IBM 6091 19"
        
        Bandwidth 120
        HorizSync 60-85
        VertRefresh 55-80

    ModeLine "1280x1024a" 108 1280 1284 1348 1724 1024 1025 1028 1054 -hsync -vs
ync
    ModeLine "1280x1024b" 108 1280 1328 1512 1712 1024 1025 1028 1054 -hsync -vs
ync

    EndSection

This drives the monitor a bit out of spec, but otherwise works GREAT.

'Hope this helps....

ms
