doing 140 kph on I95 and all. You are correct about "shutting down"
the device about to be "unscrewed," but that doesn't change the fact
that your critical chunk of code/data (the kernel) is getting tweaked.

to the down time (and overhead) incurred as a result of kernel modules.

Of course a kernel compile for me takes under 2 minutes on my dual box.
