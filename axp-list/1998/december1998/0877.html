>
> What does 'make mrproper' do? When it wiped out my previous setup I
> decided not to use it. Does it clean the kernel up to its condition when
> it was unzipped?

That's pretty much its purpose...make clean wipes out leftover .o's,
.a's, and such, but leaves your config info intact.

BTW, use make config for kernel builds. It's a bit of a
pain if you're not used to it, but it does what you tell it to do
(and ONLY what you tell it to do).
