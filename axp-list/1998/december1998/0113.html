Hi!

>I am trying to connect an old 6091-019 IBM monitor to my 600-LX
>RH 5.1 AXP equipped with a Matrox Millennium II. Up to now, I only
>have been able to have garbage on the screen, both in text mode as well
>as I launch startx. The problem, I think, is that the monitor only
>works with fixed high refresh rates.
>
>According to the monitor manual, it can do
>
>Geometry 1280x1024
>Pixel (?Dot?) Clock Freq. 120.0 Mhz
>Horiz Line Freq 70.75 Khz
>Vert Frame Rate 60 hz (N/I)
>
>There's a way I can teach the graphic card to cope with this monitor ?
>
>I "tried" to work on the XF86Config file, unsuccesfully. However, that way
>I could solve my problems when I start the X stuff, while the monitor does
>not
>work properly also in "text" mode.
>
>Any hints or suggestions out there ???

I have a fixed frequency monitor from DEC (VRT19-HA) and a Matrox
millenium 1 card. With a terminal connection to my Alpha I created a
proper modeline for X and changed the runlevel to 6. That way it will
start X and request a login name and password. Fixing text mode to work
with these type of monitors is, AFAIK, not possible.
