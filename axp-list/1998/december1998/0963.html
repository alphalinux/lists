I have a vt220 and a sportster modem on my UDB. I put the vt220 on cua0
because otherwise, when milo attempts to echo to cua0, the modem responds and
confuses the hell out of it. If you want to put the modem on cua0, then you
have to turn it off during the boot, or somehow disable the echoing to cua0
that the default milo configuration does.

I have the Y-cable from Starship Computers. The vt220 is connected using a
null modem adaptor and a gender changer.
