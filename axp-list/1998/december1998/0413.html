> Check out the ICP Vortex cards. That may be an option... Otherwise,
> SCSI-SCSI will work, but it will not be nearly as fast as controller-based
> RAID.

Hmm.. I am thinking that by SCSI-SCSI, he meant an external controller
that acts as a scsi disk to the host machine, but uses multiple disks as
its own array. I fail to see how this would be slower than anything else,
provided you have a good host controller in the system (UltraWide). The
ability to go to enormous caches (256 or 512Mb) makes a huge difference in
performance, and in drive longevity.
