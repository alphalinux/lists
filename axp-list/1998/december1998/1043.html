> Can anyone comment on the compatibility of Applixware with Excel97 and
> Word97 ? We had version 4.1.x of Applixware, and it did only a mediocre
> job of importing and exporting... Also, is there some deal availabel if I
> want to buy both Intel and Alpha versions?

        I have the SuSE "version" of Applix 4.4.1 for the Intel machine, and
SuSE has posted patches for the Office97 import files on its site -- apparently
updates that appeared after their Linux Office Suite 99 hit the streets. So far,
it's done an excellent job of pulling both Office97 / Mac Office98 and earlier
Word and Excel files. I haven't done things with any of the Microsoft products
that are particularly "on the edge" so there may be glitches out there
somewhere, but I can live with that. We only recently got Microsoft to make the
Mac version compatible with the Winders version, so what the heck...
        www.linuxmall.com sells the SuSE Linux Office Suite 99 (which includes
Applix, KDE, Communicator, SuSE 5.3, AdabasD, Arkeia, GIMP, GNOME, Xemacs, LaTeX
and who knows what else) for less than they sell the Applix 4.4.1 package
(though I'm betting that the documentation is different) by itself. They will be
selling the Applix for Alpha version as well (ask them about availability) and
they usually sell at some kind of discount price. In addition, if you order from
their website, they'll take another 5% off most orders.
