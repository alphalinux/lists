With regards to your message at 11:24 PM 12-08-98 -0500, Peter Petrakis.
Where you stated:
>Hi Guys,
> I just saw on the alphalinux homepage that there are abunch of 275
>mhz cabrolets for sale at $150. I just have two questions. What kind of
>ram does it take? What sort of bios does it use like the arc or
>alphabios? It looks like this thing performs like a P200 MMX. Oh one
>more question. How much does the ram cost and were can I find a good
>supplier? Thanks alot.
>
>Alpha Powered,
>Peter Petrakis

These take 36 bit SIMMs ( 72 pin, tin lead) in banks of 4 pieces.
It has ARC or SRM BIOS.
You also need a power supply with the extra 2 connectors providing discrete
3.3V.
