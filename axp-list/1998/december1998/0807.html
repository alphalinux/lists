        hmm, I use /dev/st0 which seems to work fine.

        do a 'grep tape /var/log/dmesg' to see what the kernel assigned at
boot time.. You shoudl see an entry there for a scsi tape, and if you do
not, your tape drive is either not properly setup hardware wise, or you do
not have tape support in the kernel (in which case you will have to
compile a kernel and include scsi tape support).

        Chris
