I remember reading about Alphas having some sort
of problem with larger disk drives. The limit may
have been 2GB but it was broken. I don't know if it was
ARC/AlphaBios or milo that had the problem but it did
exist sometime in the past year or so. Also, older
milos can't load larger kernels. When I installed
RH5.0, I had to use a new milo in order to get it
to boot the kernel. So, if you get bad combinations,
things won't work. Getting the latest stuff worked
for my systems...
