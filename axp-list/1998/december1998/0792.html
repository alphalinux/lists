The easiiest way to get ISDN is to use a router like a baynetorks RT328
which you can find from $250-$300. Then all you need is a net card,
configure your router, tell linux where the default gateway is , and the
name servers and you're all set. Some isp's sometimes provide a router
for a small setup fee. Look around some.
