Just wanted to add my .02 worth...

The cache is pretty standard on these boards, I even had a 486 MB that
had
the *exact* mfg/chip/part number that was specified in the manual as the
proper "certified" parts. These chips brought me up to 256K cache, the
manual indicated that 1 Meg was *possible*, but the chips needed were a
bit hard to find, and if you could find them, were about $15US each... I
am sure they would have made a difference, but I think at those prices
you
are looking at $250US (give or take, it's been a while) for the cache!

I bought my board from Maurice w/o cache, and at the time I got mine the
parts for the 1 Meg cache were *very* hard to find...

Hope this helps...
