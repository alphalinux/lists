With regards to your message at 09:27 PM 12-09-98 -0500,
axp-list@duct.ml.org. Where you stated:
>How does the Cabriolet fit in in performance ranges... A Pentium Pro 200
or so..
>Does it take non-parity 72 meg sims? Nope. Needs banks of 4 72 pin Parity
SIMMs.
>What does it have on board...
        floppy, IDE(slow) 2x serial, 1x parallel, PS/2 mouse and keyboard,
>What quarks should I know about?
Need an Intraserver or QLogic SCSI card. Adaptec 2940 and generic Symbios
53C875s don't work. Neither does BT958.

> Keith
Good quality units, very stable. We have some of these in Oracle servers at
a few customers sites. Only downtime in 2+ years is when we shut them down
for hardware maintenance and upgrades, maybe 2 times a year. Other than
that pure uptime.
