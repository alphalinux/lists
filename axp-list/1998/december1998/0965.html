Onsale has Y-cables (lot# 1455004), and I'm sure either Maurice or Steve
could help you out there (and forgive me if I forgot someone else).

These cables provide a DTE interface to whatever you connect them to.
The connection to the modem on ttyS0 would be direct. The terminal
would need a null modem adapter on ttyS1.
