Hi, same problem here. However, I found that it also depends on the card.

One S3Virge I tried, is more or less "stable". It hangs after 4-5 hours,
if I work on the "graphic side" of my K desktop. I mean, if I try to
change the font size of the terminal window by using the mouse, it hangs,
if I work with ALT-keys, no problems.

Another S3Virge just drove my crazy. It hangs the box after 2 minutes at
most. Sometime, even at the startx boot.

I also tried with sw_mouse and no_pci_disconnect, but it doesn't help too
much.

The solution ? Probably to go for a more stable one. Peoples in this list
once wrote that alpha & S3 is not a stable cocktail.
