>>> "B. James Phillippe" said:
>
> 1.) the floppy drive is always reported as 2.88MB under Linux. Although I
> have no factual information to back this, I suspect it is not the case
> under NT or Digital UNIX (chime in here folks and lay down the slap of
> reason if I am mistaken). We all know it works properly; it should
> therefore be able to be detected (cosmetically) properly.

I'm not sure, but believe that the floppy type is determined, on Intel, by
consulting the "CMOS"; such does not exist on Alpha, so a different technique
has been used: hardwiring... ;-}

If you look at floppy.h from 2.1.131, you'll notice the default type is set
to 6 (2.88M); this could just as easily be set to 4 (1.44M), but it could
adversely affect folks who actually *have* a 2.88M drive (some Jensens?),
*and* who actually attemp to use ED.

I think this is why it has remained set to 2.88M; anyone know different?
Anyone (else) care? ;-}
