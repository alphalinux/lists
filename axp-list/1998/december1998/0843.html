This is nontrivial, but possible, I think.

alpha binutils do support a.out format, and here's how you can compile
and link: gcc -static -Xlinker -m -Xlinker alpha

However, the problem is that ELF glibc on alpha can't be linked
into a.out, and you will need to rebuild glibc with a.out and alpha
defined.

I just don't understand why go through the trouble, gcc/egcs sucks so much
compared to DEC cc. :)

-alex
