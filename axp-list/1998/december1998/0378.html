The blame is nothing to do with "trying to emulate PC BIOS" and mostly to
do with a loader called AlphaBIOS which was designed to load NT.
AlphaBIOS expects to find the boot information in the first partition, that
it be a FAT16 partition, and so forth. As far as I have seen it is hardcoed
to that first partition.
You could also use the SRM firmware and aboot, but SRM is a licensed
software product, which bears an additional cost to you.. However it would
give you a more flexible booting solution. It still means backing up and
re-installing your files, however..
