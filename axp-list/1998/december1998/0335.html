>Hi!

>I thought it could be nice to get myself a CD-writer.
>So I got a couple of questions. I have an AS 200 4/166 (w/ linux, of

>* What brands and models work well?
>* Is there good software?

>I have a normal cdrom already. If I still have this attached, will I be

We have used the Panasonic/Matsushita 7502, The Sony 948U and the Yamaha
4260 ( CDR/RW)
All worked just fine with cdrecord, mkisofs and XCDRoast. Of the 3 I like
the Sony best, because it uses a caddy (which gives better dust control).
Disks made with all three worked well for various uses, although the Sony
made the best games disks, and the Yamaha seemed to do the best audio
disks. The differences are small, however, and I doubt you would be
disappointed with any of them.
