For what it's worth, neither the 6091 19 nor the 6091 19i are supported
with
a gxt120p in RS6000 land. This card is a matrox mga 2 meg. I suspect
that
the display would work in X as long as the resolution and refresh were
set
appropriately, but I doubt that you can make it work in text mode.
Either display
will handle X at 1280x1024 @ 60 Hz as supplied by a gxt110p or E15 (both
S3
chipset cards), but is completely out of sync on both of these cards in
text
mode. AFAIK, you will not be able to adjust the frequency of most cards
for
text mode.

Just my 2 cents,
        Jeff
