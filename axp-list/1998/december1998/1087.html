One can successfully build/link MILO *ONLY* under RH 4.2, unless you want
to use the free x86 BIOS emulator (not recommended for most VGA cards).

The DEC BIOS emulator is written in C++ and is the cause of this limitation.
The current binary of it was compiled under RH 4.2, and it seems to have some
problem when linked under any later release. When compiled under a RH 5.2
beta, it built and linked fine, but when executed caused my monitor to shut
itself off, indicating (to me) that the emulation was flawed in some way.
I haven't (yet) had the time to revisit this.
