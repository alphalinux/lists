  On another note, I thought the documentation included with the packages
was a bit lightweight, and now I see why!

You guys are selling the manuals for a separate $99 !!
This is REALLY annoying..

I would have gladly paid that to get it with it originally, if it was made
clear what the difference was between the "regular" and the "developer"
versions.
 Now I get to wait another couple of weeks, and pay yet another exorbitant
freight charge.

I think a little more effort in attempting to be more up-front about this
would have been nice. Here is what I mean: To find out what the version
with documentation you click on the "developer" bundle button. Which takes
you to a page via a "Buy It" button. What if I just wanted to "read about
it" rather than "Buy it"?

Anyway, we wanted more copies anyway, so after we get our first ones
installed ( we bought both Alpha and X86 Linux versions) I guess we will be

to being a "windows free" environment in our office. We will still need to

few other items. Does anybody know of a contact management package along
the lines of Goldmine or Act ?
