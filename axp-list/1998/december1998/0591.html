> I believe that xdm respawns itself when this is done. ctrl-r killed it on
> the implementation I've seen.

Or ctr-alt-F6 will switch you to a text console without killing xdm.
This comes handy when you completely messed up your xconfig. You can
either repair your config file, return to the X console (usely alt-F7)
and respawn it (ctrl-alt-backspace), or you can kill xdm with one of
te command posted before.
