I guess I'll take this one. From previous threads and other sources, I
get the impression that that board will take sets of four, but if you have
a set of eight, you get a 256 bit wide bus. Don't take my word for it,
look up dejanews and search on the board. I think it needs true parity,
but confirm elsewhere.

You could call a DEC rep, and I think links to documentation was submitted
in previous threads.

The potential bus width is insane, but I think the bus clock rate isn't
that impressive.
