>I'd definitely like to see a FAQ on Alpha Assembly techniques and a

Second.

>... why x86 just sucks :).

Despite saying x86, you probably never programmed an 8086 (or 8088) in
assembly. You want suck? You are in a maze of special purpose registers,
expensive branches, bizarre memory addressing, and of course, no OS worth
mentioning. The 80386 was the first x86 chip that actually worked right.
(And don't get me started on MASM.)
