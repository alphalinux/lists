Hi people,

some news concerning the lapack/blas/fastmath rpms.

1. I botched up Makefiles in fastmath, sorry. This led to no symbols
in libffm.so. I fixed this. I also renamed the rpm to fastmath-0.28 to
reflect the libffm currently in use. I put up the new source rpm on
Sat Dec 12 in the SRPMS directory.

2. I assumed that people have "." in their PATH, which is not a good
thing. The building of the rpms may fail because calling of
executables during the testing phase fails. The Makefiles for LAPACK
and the BLAS are now modified accordingly. There's a new
blas-alpha.tar and a new lapack source rpm (dated Sat Dec 12), which
have the modified patches. No other changes, tho, so if you've already
succeeded, don't worry ;-)

3. I forgot to bring along the patch for LAPACK. If you already have
the source rpm, please try to get the patch tomorrow (you don't need
the whole thing - just the patch).

Terribly sorry about this mess - things are a little hectic at work
these days. But I'll strive hard to get things running correctly!

One more thing - please tell me if you encounter problems, especially
FPEs or failures of the LAPACK tests.
