Definitly. Call up your bell. If it's bell atlantic call 1-800-get-isdn
and get loop qualified before you buy anything. also check around to see
if cable modems are available too.

Be sure you find an isp like mine (www.wn.net) that does data over voice
connects. This way you don't get any per minute charges and get 56K of
BW per channel. I pay around $80 for my isp and ISDN line with unlimited
internet and unlimted local calling. Funny when i got my first bill they
(bell at.) had measured my time instead of doing the unlimited and it
came out to $700!! They fixed it real quick, took about 3 minutes.
