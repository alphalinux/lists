Ric,

It looks like you are forgetting to initialize the modem before dialing.

This is a line in your chat script:

        OK ATDT$TELEPHONE \

This is a line in mine:

        OK ATE1Q0S0=0M0DT$TELEPHONE \

I added the M0 piece because I don't like to hear the modem dial or
connect. Hope this helps . . . . :-)
