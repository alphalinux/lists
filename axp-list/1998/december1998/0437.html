> How does the Cabriolet fit in in performance ranges...
By today's standards, slow.

> Does it take non-parity 72 meg sims?
No, it needs true parity, two banks of four.

> What does it have on board...
1 IDE, 1 floppy, 2 serial, one parallel, PS/2 key and mouse

> What quarks should I know about?
The keyboard and mouse ports don't line up on standard AT cases. You need
an AT power supply with two additional motherboard connectors (3.3v).
