hmm...
 if it's anything like the one I built from the december snapshot then
what
you need to do is something like this...
(from mozilla top level directory)
cd dist/Linux2.1.131_alpha_DBG.OBJ/bin (subst for your version of linux)
./moz-export or ./mozilla-export

At this point mine trundle off and collapse with a floating point
exception.
*However* if you haven't built it the way that the documentation
currently
recommends (i.e. building nspr library first, then the engine) and build
it in the
old fashion (one big long make from the top), I actually got it to work.

Occasionally does an abrupt crash for no apparant (or reproducible)
reason,
but good enough to make it worth your while.
