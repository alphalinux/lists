On Wed, 23 Dec 1998, Brian Guarraci wrote:

> Greetings,
>
> Has anyone gotten Postgres 6.4 to run properly. While running
> initdb, I get a fatal spin-lock timeout. I'm not sure what would cause
> this sort of thing. Does this sound like a Linux/alpha problem or
> Postgres?

        Yea, that is a known problem. It should be fixed in 6.4.1, or at
the very least in the latest snapshot from ftp.postgresql.org. I located
the problem a month or two back (think I even posted the patch to this
list), and posted a patch to the pgsql listed that was applied to the
pgsql CVS tree. I don't remember off the top of my head the reason of the
failure, but something to do with optimization if I remember correctly...
        Wait, the data just made it back from the far recesses of my
mind... It was due to a optimizer bug in gcc. Either get a newer version
of gcc, or don't use -O2. That should solve the problem.
