> /dev/st0 and /dev/nst0
>
> after accessing via /dev/st0, the drive will automatically rewind the
> tape. while accessing via /dev/nst0 (non-rewinding, thus the 'n'), the
> tape will not be rewound.
>
> usually access is through /dev/nst0
>
> then you can explicitly rewind the tape with:
>
> mt -f /dev/nst0 rewind
>
> i recommend reading the 'mt' man page for a basic understanding of your to
> manipulate the drive manually.
>
> matt
