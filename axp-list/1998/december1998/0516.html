This is an ongoing war of sorts - yet another mta is scheduled for delivery
into the fray soon enough (Vmailer). Sendmail isnt really all *that* hard to
set up - unless you're planning on doing something wacky with it. The line
that says qmail is more "secure" is more or less true depending on who you
talk to (latest revs of sendmail being more secure than past versions).

(come to think of it, this discussion is probably not "on topic" for this
forum. you might post your questions to the relevant qmail/sendmail
newsgroups/mailinglists.)

between you and me (oh yeah, and the rest of the list), I'm really not
impressed with qmail's documentation or source code, despite the fact that I
use qmail on several machines that I'm responsible for.

I bet that the latest versions of sendmail and qpopper will work just fine for
what you're trying to do. its when you get into the millions of mailboxes and
virtual domains that things get a little more hairy :)
