Hi Keith,

is your game based on the SVGA library, the X-libraries, or on a
text-screen? If it's text-based, then you don't have any other choice
but the one you've described (cin). However, you can set the terminal
controls differently (look under "man stty"), or better yet, use
ncurses (man curses) to do it for you. If it's X-based, then you'll
have to interpret the XEvents (read /usr/include/X11/Xlib.h) and if
it's SVGA-based, then I don't really know how to do it.

so long,
