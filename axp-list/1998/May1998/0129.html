> Has anyone installed Red Hat v5.1 from the FTP yet? I was surprised

I did. I found a german mirror that had the RedHat directory completly
mirrored, and started an update with the new ramdisk.img. Appereantly
most stuff workes fine (though I havn't tried compiling anything yet),
including xbill (C++ code, crashed with 5.0) and my copy of KDE Beta4.

Jensen (the neverending story ..., of course I got my own version back
working) 'gimp' didn't work on my 8-bit StaticGrey display, but I
believe that's not alpha related.
