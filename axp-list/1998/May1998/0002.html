As I mentioned yesterday on the list, I gave Alan a set of alpha-patches
(more recent than the 2.0.32-0.1 ones, much cleaned-up as well) to go with
2.0.34-pre11b, which didn't go into pre12, and prolly won't go into the
final 2.0.34, unfortunately.

to go on top if it. And perhaps a fully integrated source tree, with all the
latest alpha-patches, as well, just to make it easier.

kernels available, so that I can get feedback on whether those patches do
work on all the platforms. Keep watching this list for an announcement of
their availability around the time that 2.0.34 is official.
