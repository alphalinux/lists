On Sat, 9 May 1998, Miguel A. de Avillez wrote:

> Hi all,
>
> I was trying to compile the kernel 2.1.100 for an PC164LX box. My
> question is:
>
> Where do I inform it that the machine architecture is Alpha?
> Did anyone compilled any 2.1xx kernel in alpha boxes?
>

Tell it that you have an 'LX164'; This machine is running 2.1.98.
Don't be too surprised if some 2.1 kernel versions don't work - they are
development after all!
Remember NOT to enable TGA support, otherwise your VGA adapter won't
work (unless you have a UDB or similar machine with TGA!).
Its also a good idea to disable SMP in the Makefile, COMMENT OUT the
SMP = 1 line to do that.

Some kerensl seem to be touchy and not like 'make xconfig'; 2.1.98 was
fine for me. If you get such a problem, I found that doing a make xconfig
and then doing make config and keeping your finger on return helped.

Good luck,

Dave
