TIA,

You can try http://www.starship.com (choose the computer company, not the
communications company at the first screen. Starship has the (CAUTION:
memory at work!) the Toshiba 340 MB SCSI drives for a pretty stiff price
(about $199). The apparantly can't find a supply that would allow them to
sell at a lower price. There are also 528 and 540 MB Toshibas, but not at
Starship (at least when I last talked to them). I bought a nice 266 MHz udb
with a 540 MB drive in it in like new shape for $500 from Ken Hansen
<khansen@njcc.com>, an axp-list contributor (nice guy who delivers what he
describes). You might e-mail him for an alternate source for Toshiba
drives: he seems to be an old hand a alpha boxes.
