This is one of the "flesh-eating drongos" at LSL taking a quick break from
eating WAY to much Red Meat to reply to this posting (cant take too long,

received the CD or download instructions, or otherwise been contacted by us
may wish to send email to info@lsl.com by the middle of next week. Pricing,

with the CD with a pre-built version of Mozilla. You will also find a
statically linked version of Moz freely on http://www.lsl.com on the 28th
May and also by ftp. Details can be found on the Web pages; follow the
flesh-eating Monster (just kidding).

Netscape on Axp is no mean feat, but it does work and is worth the effort,
