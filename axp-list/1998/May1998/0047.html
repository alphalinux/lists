OK, so I'm stupid!

The answer was *not* to move the ax libs to /usr/em86/lib but to add
/opt/applix/axdata/axshlib to my /etc/ld.so.conf file and run ldconfig.

I believe that the libs in /usr/em86 are specially made to be used by the
emulator itself and normal application libs need to be in their usual place so
they will be called through the emulator if need be.

At least I figured it out myself :-)
