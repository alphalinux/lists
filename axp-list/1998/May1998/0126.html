Dennis, thanks for the kind words - I am not sure of any sources for
small SCSI drives - I believe they are used in Mac portables as well as
some
Thinkpads - so you could try the various Laptop upgrade sources.

Also, I just got a nice big 3.2 Gigabyte 2.5" IDE drive (I know, I
know) for $300, others are cheaper (try
HTTP://www.onsale.com/exchange.htm ).
IDE performance sucks, but if your application is not disk intensive it
could
be just the ticket - also, at one time Mauriece at HardData (at
http://www.harddata.com/ or bigfoot@hardata.com IIRC) at one time was
offering
custom 2.5" IDE drive cables for such aplications..

FWIW, the 3.2 Gigabyte HD is a replacement for an 800 Meg HD, that
others
who have performed the same upgrade on their Librettos (SMALL Toshiba
HandHeld)
seem willing to part with for about $80 + S/H - checkout
http://www.cerfnet.com/~adorable/libretto.html - they have a link to a
mailing
list, an devery so often drives are offered for sale (you can also
ask!).

Good luck,
