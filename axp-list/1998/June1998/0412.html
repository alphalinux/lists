"Ben T. Eisemann" <ben@eisemann.com> wrote:
> While attempting an install of Redhat 5.0 on my UDB I can get as far as
> formating the hard disk but it locks up (actually just freezes, i can still
> alt+F1,alt+F2...) while writing inode tables: 7/241. I have tested the
> hard drive in another machine and it works fine.

> SCSI Card: NCR 810
> Hard disk: Seagate Barracuda 2 ST12550N

I had the same symptoms with a UDB and a Seagate ST15230W and Redhat
4.1. I switched to a Fujitsu disk and it worked fine. The Seagate
drive works great in my x86 system.

Some docs from Starship Computer that came with my UDBs said there is a
known incompatibility between the UDB and Seagate drives. I don't think
any more detailed explanation than that was provided. I would have
guessed that the problem was somehow related to the NCR SCSI chip or
device driver, so I'm surprised that the problem (if it's even the same
one) shows up with the aha adapter.
