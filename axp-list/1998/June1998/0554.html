>>> Greg Lindahl said:
>
> My LX164 machines will not boot with a serial console. They wait
> forever for someone to hit <return>, even though I have set a timeout
> in the AlphaBIOS. The timeout works when I have a keyboard attached.

Yes, Greg, I know about the AlphaBIOS "limitation", but he was using SRM
console, which works correctly in this instance.

> I suppose that all this means is that you'll have to plug in a
> keyboard, not that you need a video card. I don't know how it reacts
> when it has no video card.

Nope; using SRM console, simply say "set console serial" and then look for
control to pass to COM1 after doing an "init" or power-cycling. In fact,
SRM will assume/force serial console if you unplug the keyboard (AlphaBIOS
does as well, but will not behave any better WRT auto-booting, I believe).
