In addition to the above, the problem may well be your version of Milo.
Since RH does not supply a MILO specifically for the XL, you have to use a
substitute which the DOCS lead you to believe works. I had this problem
when I installed RH 5.0 on my XL 266's. The problem would go away if I
installed 4.2! The workaround was to use 4.2's milo (specific for the XL)
or better yet, visit Digital's site and download the milo for 5.0 specific
to this machine. Kudos to Jay E. for providing us with the up to date code.
