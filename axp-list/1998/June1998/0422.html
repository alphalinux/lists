Well I've just got everything updated and running 2.1.101 here on an SX164.
No problems so far, using modules for just about everything.
The kernel source just required the __builtin_memset thingy commenting out
and compiled fairly cleanly using ecgs-1.0.2.

Some minor tweaking of header files was needed to get the updated ppp
(2.3.5) and mount (2.7.1) to compile.

The system is running a little faster, and the new kernel has solved the
business with minicom/pppd/uucp not liking port speeds over 38400.

I'm hoping the problem of emacs becoming unresponsive to the system (and load
avg increasing to ~2.0) will also disappear, although it's a bit early to
tell yet.
