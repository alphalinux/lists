>
>I've tried to find a network time protocol program for Linux, but I can't
>find it. Can someone help?

Go to http://tycho.usno.navy.mil and follow the "computer time service"
link. It is an excellant site for obtaining, configuring, and maintaining
ntp. You can download xntpd (not an X app) from a link. I had trouble
compiling xntpd with gcc 2.7.2.3, but a quick upgrade to gcc 2.8.1 compiled
xntpd perfectly.
