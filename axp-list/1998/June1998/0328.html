Chad L. Tipton wrote:
>
> OK, this one was really weird. I happened to look up while I was trying to
> type something and I saw my modem lights light up. Simply removing the
> modem from the COM port solved the problem.
>
> Has anyone else seen this when they were trying to work at the MILO>
> prompt?

This is perfectly normal. MILO and the kernel echo everything to the
first COM port. There is a compilation option in recent kernels to
disable this, I'm not sure if there is a way to disable it in MILO.

I'd guess this was done to make troubleshooting easier in case of video
problems, or maybe for headless boots.
