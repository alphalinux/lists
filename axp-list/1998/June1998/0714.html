To expand on the above, it is not a limitation of the system, it was a
statement of how many then-current hard drives you could squeeze into
the
box. I suspect you could easily put two 3 1/2" SCSI half-height drives
of
almost any size, being cautious of heat (as always).

Also, the CD-ROM space appears to get very little ventilation, so DEC
warns
against installing anything other than a CD-ROM in that 5 1/4" position.

to
your multisync monitor.
