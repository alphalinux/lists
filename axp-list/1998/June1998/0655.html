From: Cyndi Harper <Cyndi.Harper@digital.com>
Subject: Powerful Quotes!

FYI...pretty hot stuff!!

----------
From: President NA Sales & Mktg - Mike Gallup
Sent: Friday, May 08, 1998 2:47 PM
Subject: Use these powerful quotes to help you sell!

To: North America Sales and Marketing

Info Type: Product Information

In a series of five short but poignant speeches delivered at the UNIX
Executive Summit held in New York City, May 4-6, attendees heard from
the CEOs of three of the IT industry's heaviest hitters -- DIGITAL's Bob
Palmer, Compaq's Eckhard Pfeiffer and Oracle's Larry Ellison -- and two
high-powered UNIX-based companies, Netscape and Commerzbank. The net
result: there is no question that DIGITAL UNIX is well positioned to
become the de facto industry standard UNIX for enterprise-level 64-bit
computing in the new millennia. Additionally, there is no question
Compaq will drive Alpha to become an open, industry-standard 64-bit
architecture.

Any lingering doubt about these facts was erased last Tuesday. Look at
these quotes.

When a member of the press asked Eckhard Pfeiffer point blank, "Are you
saying that DIGITAL UNIX is going to be Compaq's 64-bit UNIX strategic
platform?" Pfeiffer replied, simply, "The answer is, YES.

Pfeiffer made it clear how he feels about Alpha when he said, "I believe
we can establish Alpha as an industry standard, building on DIGITAL's
Alpha licensing agreements with Intel and Samsung... and its proposed
agreement with AMD. In fact, I think customers can buy Alpha systems
today with renewed confidence in Alpha's long-term future."

When asked if Compaq would continue to support OpenVMS. Pfeiffer said,
"We will continue to support OpenVMS as long as customers want it."

THESE ARE STRONG STATEMENTS OF COMPAQ'S COMMITMENT TO DIGITAL
TECHNOLOGY. IT IS ABSOLUTELY CRUCIAL THAT WE CONVEY THEM, AND THE OTHER
GREAT STATEMENTS MADE AT THE EVENT, TO OUR CUSTOMERS AND PARTNERS --
IMMEDIATELY!

To resounding applause, Larry Ellison, Oracle Chairman and CEO,
powerfully announced that, using an Oracle application, DIGITAL has
achieved a record breaking TPC-C benchmark result for a large-scale
AlphaServer 8400 5/625 cluster. The test recorded a tpmC figure of
102,541.85 - which is almost double the previous best-performing system,
IBM's RS/6000. This achievement clearly demonstrates the sustained
ability of DIGITAL and Oracle to provide customers the most scalable
computing solutions for their business-critical applications.

Following the main tent presentations, customers and partners were
invited to attend a number of hard-hitting, content-rich breakout
sessions delivered by DIGITAL senior VPs about DIGITAL UNIX, Alpha, data
warehousing, enterprise applications, Internet, Oracle solutions and
DIGITAL Services.

Jesse Lipcon, DIGITAL VP, UNIX and OpenVMS Systems Business Unit,
delivered a masterful presentation on the Future of Alpha. His remarks
clearly drove home the message: Alpha delivers 50% to 100% higher
performance than Merced at 20% the cost. "Jesse Lipcon," commented a
customer, "was really phenomenal. His knowledge and confidence are
captivating and reassuring!"

Customers who attended the event responded favorably to what they heard.

"The type of messages I received at this event helped me represent
DIGITAL inside my organization, which at times is no easy task. The
102K benchmark result with Oracle is an easy message my CEO will
understand... DIGITAL is twice as fast as the competition and costs
less!"

"I had no idea that DIGITAL was such a major player in data warehousing,
but when I heard and saw the real-world results of Acxiom, it made us
rethink including DIGITAL on our next shortlist."

Dan Belanger, State Street Corporation account manager, said, "This
event scored a big home run with my customers. Actually, it was more
like a GRAND SLAM!! They came to New York with a number of concerns,
they heard everything they needed to hear, and left feeling confident in
their decision to go with DIGITAL!"

I must say, I was really proud to be part of the team at this event.
The whole experience was exhilarating and gave a well-earned boost to
everyone's spirits - customers, partners and DIGITAL attendees alike.
