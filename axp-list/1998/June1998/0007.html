i've got the strangest problem with the sound on my 164SX.

my setup:

ISA Sound Blaster 16, *non*-PnP
533 SX, 64mb ram...etc. redhat 5.0
kernel 2.0.32 with Jay's patches

the problem:

the sound will work fine for a while... maybe a few months. then all of
a sudden, the WHOLE system will lock-up during the play of a sound.
cannot telnet to the machine, cannot ping... etc.

then once upon time when i was trying to diagnose this problem, i stuck
the card in my pentium system. it worked! so i stuck it back in the alpha,
and it worked!!

so i started thinking... hmmm... i thought that maybe the BIOS of the
pentium reset something on the sound card that doesn't get reset from the
alpha...

and thus my question... is the hypothesis above true at all?? is there
anyway around this? the lock-up problem is the motivation behind this
message... i don't want to have to tear apart my two systems again!!! :)

any ideas?
