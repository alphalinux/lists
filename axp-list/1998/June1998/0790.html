>>>>> "Paul" == Paul B Brown <pbrown@btechnet.com> writes:

Paul> Hello All, Does anyone have any suggestions about what to use as
Paul> a second ethernet card for a UDB??

Get a DE-500 or other DC2114x based cards. At least two or three
brands are available in the under $50 range.

The cards are as fast and robust as any, and use the same driver as
the builtin 10BaseT. When you install one, it will be eth0 and the
builtin will become eth1.
