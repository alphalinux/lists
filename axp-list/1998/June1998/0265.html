Edwin Koshimoto wrote:
>
> I cruised over and reviewed the list of
> supported hardware. I'm using a 2940UW
> with two internal drives, but I'd like to
> use the external bus as well.
>
> Does RH 5.0 like this?

Not sure about the Alpha but it works fine on an Intel box. Be aware
that with that card you can't use all three ports. There are two
internal ports (one wide and one narrow) and one external wide, which
you can use for narrow devices with the appropriate cable.

You may not, for example, use both the wide and the narrow internal
ports (say for a wide drive and a narrow CDRM) *and* the external port
at the same time.

Any combination of two ports is OK.

The kernel has no way to know if a device is internal or external.
