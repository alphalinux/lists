On Mon, 29 Jun 1998, Steve Frampton wrote:

> VFS: Cannot open root device 08:02
> Kernel panic: VFS: Unable to mount root fs on 08:02
>
> Just wondering what I might have done wrong. :-(

My guesses here are that 08:02 is not your root filesystem, that you
compiled something as a module that you shouldn't have (like your HD
driver, or ext2fs, or something else needed to operate the disk), or that
your setup is basically wrong. If you select default values for
everything, for instance, on a UDB, the sound card conflicts with the SCSI
controller.
