Howdy;

For those of you who mailed me last night regarding my request for help
getting MILO to boot on my 433, thanks. The problem was in the PCI slot
I had my video card in. After trying about 20-30 different combinations
of my SCSI card (2940U) and my video card (Matrox Mystique), I
discovered that MILO would boot if my SCSI card was in slots 1-3 (32 bit
PCI) and my video card was in 4-5 (64 bit PCI).

MILO now succesfully boots!

Now, my ongoing problem:

When I try to make a set of 'boot' and 'root' disks to start an
installation for either 4.2 or 5.0, I have no success.

Questions:

1)Given the recent vintage of my Miata, should I be using 5.0? I do
want something stable, but would also like to play with the 2.1 kernels.

2) Depending on individual answers to #1 above, where do I get recent
boot and root disks for that installation. Also, to confirm what my boot
comannd from the MILO prompt should be:

'boot fd0:vmlinux.gz root=/dev/fd0 load_ramdisk=1 prompt_ramdisk=1'

right?

Thanks for all your help...
