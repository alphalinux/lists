We are trying to install RH Linux on an AlphaStation 200. With the correct milo disk (avanti), and the correct ARC setup, we get as far as the linux loader, the stanzas print out, we get some lines and the PALcode switch, then the screen flashes and changes to colored blocks and we can't read anything.

If we try other /milo/images/ files and go through the same process, the screen remains readable, but the system halts and hangs at the PALcode switch. We have also tried any number of milo images off ftp.gatekeeper.dec.com and other sites, but still get stuck. At that point there is nothing to do but turn the ALpha off, or reset.

After also trying a more generic video card with no luck, we have tried at least 8 different boot images and modified several of them with alternate versions of milo.

For a test of the hardware, NT 4.0 was installed, and the ethernet, video and sound all worked fine.

So, does anyone have ideas or suggestions, OR, a working milo for this system?
