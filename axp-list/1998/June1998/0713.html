Hello

>I just recently bought a DEC 4/166 and i havent recieved it yet but... i
>got a few questions
>1)Will it take a SCSI-2 Narrow Fast hdd?

yes.

>2)I've read docs on digital's page and it said it only holds 6.4 gigs is
>this BS?

thats when 2Gb disks were considered big. the unit can hold (1)3.5"HH,
(1)3.5"FH, (1)CD-Rom and (1)floppy.

>3)How many mem slots are open and does the machine need matching simms like
>a pentium?

the system has 3 memory banks, consisting of 2 slots per bank, so, 6 total.

>4)Does it use a standard 3 rows of 5 pins (SVGA) Monitor?

depends on the video card it ships with.

>5)How many devices (SCSI) does this machine take?

tops 3.

>6)Whats the cable connector interface pin # 50 or 80?

if you mean scsi, its 50.
