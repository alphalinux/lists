I used the 2.0.33 kernel sources (from tsx-11) and the 2.0.32 patches
from gatekeeper. In addition there is one additional small patch needed
to a.out.h. I'm at work and don't have it here but you should be able to
find it by using dejanews to search for em86 and a.out.h.

Jay Estabrook posted here recently that he has some experimental alpha
patches for 2.0.34pre, you might want to play with them as well. You
could also try a recent 2.1.x kernel if you want to experiment - I
understand it should support alpha and em86 without patching.
