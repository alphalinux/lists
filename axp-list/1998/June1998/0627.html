On Thu, May 14, 1998 at 09:51:58AM +0200, Magnus@TIC.Thyssen.com wrote:
> > RedHat 5
> > Alpha XL 266
> > 32MB Ram
> TELES ISDN CARD 16.0
>
> I have built a kernel with Telse Support.
>
> When I insert the Card and boot i get the following Message:
>
> -ISDN subsystem Rev: 1.28/1.23/1.29/1.20/1.6
> -Teles 16.0 found io: d80 mem: d0000 irq: 15 proto EDSS1
> -HSCX version A:4 B:4
>
> Then the system hangs .

there are serveral problems with the Teles support in 2.0.32. First dump
the Teles driver and use the Hisax driver. Second the Teles/Hisax driver
in 2.0.32/33 isn't 64bit clean. Third the Teles 16/0 part isn't aware of
the Alpha write buffers (missing mb()).

I've fixed these things during an isdn4linux hacker session and tested
a Teles 16/3 and Teles 16/0 in my Noname. As my changes weren't against
2.0.33, but the isdn4linux cvs repository, I can't make patches. I'm not
sure, if my changes are part of Alan's 2.0.34pre patches.
