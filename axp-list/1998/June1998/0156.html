This might also depend on how the cable modem service works as well. The
only Cable modem internet service offered here (Australia) only works on
Windows or Macs. NOTHING else.
To transfer data some authenication daemon must be running on your machine,
the protocol is a secret, no source, and asking is pointless. Its windows
or mac only. Any one else, go away. As far as im aware no one has managed
to reverse engineer it either. People are hooking Linux machines up via a
windows box running proxy packages and the authenticator.
Messy to say the least. On the other hand many US offerings are some what
more open!
