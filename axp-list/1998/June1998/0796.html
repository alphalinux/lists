This is in the archives. I think DejaNews keeps track of this list (you
can at least do searches from there). 5.0 *should* be updated to compile
out of the box, 4.2 will need an update (libc? I forget) from the "alpha"
or "testing" directory. I've compiled PCMCIA 3.0 and it works fine.

will need to make a minor change there as well.
