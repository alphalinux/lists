> Get a DE-500 or other DC2114x based cards. At least two or three
> brands are available in the under $50 range.

the problem of course being that the tulip's have to bus master to a
largely alligned address so they can't copy the the frame such that the ip
headers are aligned for the alpha to pick them up. so you have to go from
the card, once through memory, and then to the final resting place. the
eepro and 905 don't have this problem, and the 905b has checksumming
hardware.

the tulip will work fine in an alpha, it just won't be bleeding edge :)
