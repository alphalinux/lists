I have not extensively tested this debugger, but it appears to work
reasonably well. I did manage to induce a crash when fiddling with one of
the preference settings. I have not looked further at this.

There are 4 rpms.
Documentation, plus 3 binaries.
The dynamically linked binary is against glibc2 and LessTif 0.85.
The semistatic binary is dynamically linked against glibc2 and statically
linked against LessTif 0.85.
The static binary is fully statically linked.
