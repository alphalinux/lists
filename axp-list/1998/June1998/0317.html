Kinnunen> Has anyone managed to get 2.1.x-kernel working with SX164?

Yes, I have 2.1.101 running nicely here on an SX164.

Kinnunen> I've compiled the kernel with NCR53c8xx and Tulip support
Kinnunen> with or without sound (regular GUS) with loadable module
Kinnunen> support. No additional patches were applied.

I only hand patched the __builtin_memset lines from string.h, as has
been mentioned here previously, and commented out the SMP line in the
makefile. No other patches applied.

The kernel was compiled with ecgs-1.0.2.

I am also using the NCR53c8xx and Tulip drivers. The NCR53c8xx driver was
compiled into the kernel (for my boot drive) and the Tulip driver compiled
as a module.

Initially I compiled a 'non-modular' kernel, then compiled the new
modutils, then compiled a new kernel using modules for just about
everything. I don't know whether this was absolutely necessary, but the
documentation seemed to suggest that it was. (Perhaps this is the "trick").
