I guess I don't have the appropriate floppy. I
know I don't have a recovery CDROM. I'll have to
track one down (does anyone have a part number?).

SRM console was being loaded. I then noticed that
the keyboard was active, so I powered off and swapped
the ZLXp-E2 card for the Mach64-based card I was
using. After restarting the system I could read
the screen and change to the ARC console. After

card back in and it worked. Switching back to SRM
kept the screen blank, although I could blindly
type and switch back to ARC.

Before I added the two 28F020 devices I could use
my Mach64-based card with the SRM console. So
