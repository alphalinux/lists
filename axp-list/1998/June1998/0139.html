I'm also having trouble loading the ramdisk on a 164SX. I get the prompt
to load it, put the floppy in, and hit enter and then the floppy drives
spins until I turn the machine off. (I've let it spin for hours just to
see if anything would happen.)

I noticed that just before this happened, as the kernel was booting, I
got unexpected interrupt messages as the IDE drives would being found
and as the floppy was being found. On the chance that the problem
was due to a kernel incompatibility, I tried using the 5.1 MILO and
ramdisk with the *5.0* kernel floppy. This allowed me to successfully
load the ramdisk and get into the early stages of install. (I ran into
another problem then and haven't gone back to see what was going on.)
