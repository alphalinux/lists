Hi !

> I have the HP SureStore 5000 (DAT) on a UDB with kernel 2.0.30
> and NCR53c8xx driver. I have had no problems or hangups.

There are (at moment) no problems with HP35470/80 streamers on a
NCR53c8xx (i386/AXP).
I don't think that machine specific bugs exists.
Bugs are related to tape-drives and/or hostadapters (drivers).
Old tape-drives without disconnect/reconnect may cause heavy trouble
under Linux at all. (Old SCSI-QIC streamer, old Archive Phyton DAT,
some old 8mm-Streamer (Mountain/Maynard)).

I had a special setup with some disks, SCSI-QIC and a noname scanner
connected to a NCR53c810.
When I used the generic NCR driver, the scanner works and tape access
caused panic, when using NCR53c8xx the tape worked and the scanner
looked up my system. (was arround 2.0.28 i586)
