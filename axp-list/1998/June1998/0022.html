On Sun, 21 Jun 1998, Christopher Curtis wrote:

> bios32.sio_fixup: weird, device 8086:0484 coming in on slot 7 has no irq line!!
>
> Hmm, wonder what that is? Anywhoo...

It's an ancient "Intel 82378ZB System I/O (SIO)" PCI to ISA bridge which
predates PCI 2.0 specs and as such it does not identify itself as a bridge
class device. Only bridge class PCI devices are allowed to have no IRQ
line -- all others have to be assigned one by PCI BIOS.
