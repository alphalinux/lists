For those who are interested (even if you're not, this will get to the
archives proving my stupidity :-) I fixed the ethernet problem.

It seems that the configuration process has changed and you have to pretend
that you have a PCI ethernet card in your UDB (even though you don't
really). Once you enable the PCI ethernet card options, select the DE4x5
ethernet card, rebuild kernel and it's back on the air.
