I compiled mozilla with LSL Motif and it works pretty fine. Please see

ftp://ftp.eni.co.jp/.2/Linux-Alpha-JP/ftp.statabo.rim.or.jp/Motif/Mozilla

This mozilla is statically linked with LSL Motif and works on
glibc-2.0.x(it may work on Redhat 5.x). The named "x_locale" mozilla
requires libX11 compiled with X_LOCALE option, but it can display wide
characters. I think it's enouth to use non-X_LOCALE version on the
normal usage.

I also made Mosaic-2.6 with Motif and works fine.
