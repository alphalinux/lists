Hi,
Clock skew is probably caused by at one time or another you having the
clock set wrongly (probably a few years ahead) and then bringing it
back. At a guess you untarred the kernel when the clock was wrong in
the future. The error is coming from make because it can't possibly
produce an object file newer than your future source file.

Presuming you now have your clock set correctly, I suggest doing:

find directoryname -exec touch {} \;

where directoryname holds your source. Then do a make clean
and you should be OK.
