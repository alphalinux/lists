Hi Steve !

> For some reason I tried to do a 'tar zcvf /dev/sr0 *' and -- things
> started getting copied -- about 5 Mb of stuff before I noticed the tape
> drive wasn't spinning, so I aborted.
>
> If I do a 'tar ztvf /dev/sr0' I see all the files that got tarred before I
> aborted. I removed the tape from the drive and tried again -- yep, there
> they are again!
>
> I was just wondering what this device is -- a ramdisk, perhaps? -- and if
> I might have done something bad. :-(

On old systems /dev/sr* are the SCSI-CDROM's. But if tar is working
that way, your should do a ls -l /dev/sr0 and check the major number
against /usr/src/linux/Documentation/devices.{txt,tex}

For tape access, /dev/nst0 and /dev/st0 may work, the later one
performs a rewind on close; but I'm shure that you know that.
