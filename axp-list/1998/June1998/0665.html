NO!

the limit for *file* size is 2G. partitions can be very large (we have
several ~8G and many ~4G partitions on our machines here, as well as as
two systems with multiple 4G and 8G partitions combined into 8G and 16G
RAID0 drives).

if you want a single 23G partition, i'd go for it. get your boot/root
drive set up first, then try to partition/format your 23G'er. bust it up
into smaller partitions only if it doesn't work.
