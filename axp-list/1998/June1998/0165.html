Two suggestions:
1. Make sure you have all the correct stuff compiled into your kernel, and
that you've booted with that kernel. ("make boot" doesn't put in where MILO
can find it, unlike what "make zlilo" does on Intel)
2. Try and ftp the ipfwadm packages from 5.0 and install them instead. There
might be a bug in 5.1.
