It's not so much that "compaq rules out-right", but what is happening
is that Compaq, DEC, and Tandem are hammering out a unified business
plan that combines the work of the three entities. The entity that
carries out this plan will be called "Compaq". The executive committee
(the "strings" that pull the company along) is made up of executives
from all three companies (although, admittedly, this committee is
heavily dominated by executives from the company the original Compaq).

As I said, though; the best way to get Linux support from this new
company is to let them know, as customers and potential customers,
that you want it.
