That's supposed to become a FAQ.
OK. Here is the trick. The whole thing has nothing to do with X or the
compiler. Some kernel code is broken, namely the SysV-IPC code. This causes
in general XSHM to fail with _any_ program.
Solution is to get either 2.1.10? kernel or to apply the alpha-2.0.34-?.diff
from gatekeeper (don't know the exact URL anymore).
After that everything should work fine.
CU
nils

PS: I wonder why this patch is not in the so called 'stable' 2.0.34 kernel?
