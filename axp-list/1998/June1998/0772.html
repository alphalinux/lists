On Fri, 22 May 1998, Chad Campbell wrote:

> Hmmm... mine has been doing the startx problem you describe lately. I'm
> playing with all the experimental kernels though, so we could have
> completely different causes. I have a Millenium II.

Get the latest XFree3.3.2 SVGA driver binary. And then edit
/etc/X11/XF86Config to explicitly state the amount of memory on your
card, thus preventing autoprobing.

The above two measures should hopefully stop the hangs. It is thought to
be a video card hardware bug rather than a Linux or XFree bug.
