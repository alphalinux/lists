1) I *think* the celeron is SMP capable, but I don't know...

2) No cache is not a problem, if your RAM is fast enough, but the PPro
   has a 256K cache vs. 0 in Celeron...

>Seems 2 celleron system would be cheaper than a PPro... and upgradeable
>to PIIx450 (at least)

The CPUs would be cheaper, the perfomance would be a bit more, and the
motherboard would be a lot more expensive (twice the price?) If you really
think you will upgrade the machine during it's likely service life, the
investment in a PII SMP machine is a good idea, if you want a darn fast
smp box without spending a lot of money, the PPro is a very good choice,
IMHO...
