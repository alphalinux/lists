FYI - I have recently built-up an SMP PPro system for very little money,
the
Intel Providence motherboards are good values (IMHO) at under $100 with
built-in
sound, 10/100 Mbit ethernet, and SCSI, add an ATX case ($80 for a nice
one),
two PPro 180s ($100/each) - you can overclock them to 200 Mhz without
too
much trouble, some memory (EDO ECC SIMMs are a bit pricey compared to
generic
RAM prices these days, but not really too far out of line) and a video
card
and you are on your way - $750 would build a nice system...

SS10s are nice, but the cost of fast processors will eat your budget
alive!

Hope this helps...
