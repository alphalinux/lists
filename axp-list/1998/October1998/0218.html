> > > egcs build scripts default to building libI77 with -mieee?

> > Would make sense to have the libraries built both ways.
> > That way people who don't want to fix the bugs in their

Ask yourself how many customers would pay you to
produce a NaN for them....

create NaN's in the range of values you expect

I get paid to get a good result, not nonsense.

Research and academic code may consider it valid
but I've never had a customer who would accept such
output.
