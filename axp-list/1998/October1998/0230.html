Bill Anderson wrote:
>
> Does anyone know where I can find a copy of the latest Alpha/Linux hardware
> compatibility lists? SCSI-HDD, DVD perhaps, CD, NICs, memory, etc.? For
> 164sx.

SCSI-HDD - Any (I prefer IBM);
DVD - Dunno (don't have one);
CD - If SCSI, about any will do (I prefer Plextor);
NIC's - Anything with a DEC Tulip chip on it or 3Com's;
Mem - Any quality brand ECC (I prefer Kingston and Samsung);
