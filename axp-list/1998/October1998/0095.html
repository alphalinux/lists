Hi!

I got a 2.1.128 working on my AS 200 4/166 today. It seems a lot faster
than my old 2.0.33, and that is good. I have two probs, though:

* The sound card gives very low output. If I set my dsp output to 100
(max) I can hear the output very quietly, but it gets a little bit
distorted.

Nov 15 15:49:54 octavia kernel: <MSS audio codec (AD1848)> at 0x534 irq 9
dma 3,3

* The floppy doesn't like me. At boot, I get:

Nov 15 15:49:54 octavia kernel: Floppy drive(s): fd0 is 2.88M
Nov 15 15:49:54 octavia kernel: DOR0=4
Nov 15 15:49:54 octavia kernel: floppy interrupt on bizarre fdc 1
Nov 15 15:49:54 octavia kernel: handler=0000000000000000
Nov 15 15:49:54 octavia kernel: FDC 0 is a National Semiconductor PC87306

And then it f***s out when I try to mount a floppy. (I had this problem on
earlier 2.1-kernels that I tried before).
But as this kernel seems fast and good in all other ways, I'd really like
to stick to it, so if anyone had some kind of hint or something, I would
be really grateful.
