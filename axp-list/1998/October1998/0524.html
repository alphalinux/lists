kernel doing it. So since I had not upgraded my milo in ages.. I
grabbed Nikita Schmidt's new Milo images (since his web/ftp site were
unavailable, I pulled it out of the Loic Prylli's newest rescue disk
for the debian boot disks), saved my old copy of Milo and booted up
the new copy of milo (the changes look nice) but the problem persists.

This output happens in milo when I do something thats wrong.. like
'ls sda' instead of 'ls sda1'. it also happens a lot when I'm
scrolling around in most or when some vt100/ncurses type screen
movement occurs..

Other than a hardware fault (pci riser board maybe?), I'm stumped..
any suggestions to what I could try to resolve this would be
appreciated...
