On Thu, 26 Nov 1998, B. James Phillippe wrote:

> ** The X problem I am having: I have a TGA ZLXp-E2 driving a brand new
> Imagic 778L 17" monitor. I have almost all the video mode the monitor
> support working in XFree86-3.3.3. However, my 1280x1024 mode is really
> unstable and wavers a lot. The other modes, from 1152x900, work stably,
> but they all have a "ghosting" effect, where I can see faint shadows
> extending to the right of every image. Does anyone else have this problem?
> I'm trying to determine if it's a timing problem, or (hopefully not) a
> video card or monitor problem.

This is a hardware problem rather than an X problem. I have an ZLXp-E2 as
well running on an LX164, under Digital Unix, though. The problem is that
only the R and B signals are terminated with 50 Ohms on the graphics card.
The G signal has high impedance! Just solder a 50 Ohm resistor (47 will do
as well) between the G analog output of the card and GND and the shadows
will disappear.
