Hi,

Could someone confirm that traceroute works Ok on RedHat 5.2
(preferably on avanti, but any other platform would do)? I am trying
to figure out some weird networking problems that started to show up
after I have upgraded from 5.1.

I have 2 machines running 5.2, an Alpha and a Pentium. Their
configuration is identical--except that they have different IPs and
the Pentrium's Ethernet is a PCMCIA. They are both on the same subnet
(a.b.c.122 and a.b.c.85).

- from the Pentium, traceroute reaches the Alpha as expected, in one
  hop

- from the Alpha, traceroute doesn't reach the Pentium at all; it
  shows 1 * * *
        2 * * *
        3 * * *
  and so on

There's another strange problem with the Alpha when both eth0 and ppp0
are up--see my previous posting. I'm just trying to narrow it
down... Any other suggestions are welcome. Thanks!
