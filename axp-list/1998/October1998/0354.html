The Alpha-optimized source of David Mosberger is at
ftp://ftp.azstarnet.com/pub/linux/axp/misc/axpsrc-mpeg_play-2.3axp-2.tar.gz

Is anybody aware of other optimized alpha tools, like mpg123 or mpeg?

The difference between normal 2.3 mpeg_play and this version is huge,
its up to 3 times as fast.
