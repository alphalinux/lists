my recollection of SIGFPE on alpha, is that is u dont use the -mieee switch,
u will never be able to recover from an FPE error as:
    1) u cant tell what instruction did u in,
    2) cant fix the error.
    3) shouldn't return to the point where the error occured, in case u dont
do a long-jmp

I suppose u can catch the error, but in essence the fp calculations up to
that point may/would be useless because something along the line got messed
up. ignoring the error, will just trash any potentially usefull results.
These are pragmatic rationalizations.
