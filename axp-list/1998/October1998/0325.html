> Has anybody successfully compiled PGP 5.0i (international version) on

>
> I've tried to hack the source of pgpMD5.c with my limited skills of C, but hadn't had any
> success. (by the way, I used the source RPM).

The problem is not in pgpMD5.c. The problem is in the way PGP declares
a 64-bit type.

You want to edit pgpUsuals.h such that word64 is defined as a long, not
a long long or any other weirdness.

IIRC, heres what I did ..

comment out lines 51-57 in pgpUsuals.h, and on lines 71 and 72 change
"long long" to "long"

That should be it, I think ..

you may not have to change lines 71 and 72.. its been awhile since I've
compiled it on my Alpha.

If you still have trouble, let me know and I'll work up an actual patch.
