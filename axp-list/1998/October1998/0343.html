Just to let you know that I a similar setup (but I'm running on an
Alcor-XL366), i.e.
        -kernel 2.1.128
        -egcs-1.1b
        -Netscape DU 4.5
        -DU 4.0d libraries

And it works, sort of...
There is a "bug" or misfeature..
        * Netscape (DU binary) apparently doesn't know how to query my ISP's
DNS which I put in /etc/resolv.conf. It only works with IP addresses.
This is when I configure it to point direct (i.e. no proxy)
        * I got around this problem by using Squid 2.1 (release). Installed and
configured it and pointed Netscape at squid (localhost:3128) at
_IT_WORKS_ properly now.. Not exactly elegant, but I can live with that.
A pleasant side effect is you get your own personal proxy ;-) (but of
course, Squid _WILL_ eat up your resources...)

Good luck.
