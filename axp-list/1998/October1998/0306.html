> PIIs have 512K L2 cache and 32K L1 cache so they will perform even better than
> an overclocked celeron.

        Well before you get all hopped up in your glory, goto
www.tomshardwareguide.com or www.anandtech.com and see how an o/c'd
450 Celeron performs comparable to PII450.

        Both sites indicate that over several Winbech (I know, puke..)
benchmarks that a 450 celeron is *VERY* close. Now while I agree that
benchmarks can themselves be very miusleading and inaccurate, my 'seat of
the pants' benchmarks (QuakeII Frame rates, kernel compiles for identical
setups, software compiles on different setups) indicate to me that my home
Celeron 450 is *very* close to my PII450 Dell Workstation at the office.

        Real world price/peformance on my o/c'd celeron is (IMO)
unbeatable.

        Chris
