Scott Lewis wrote:
>
> Well, I too am impatient. And I would really love to see what my Alpha
> can do. What language you _should_ use depends on what you are trying to
> do. Math Ops in assembly will blow the doors off of C, but I wouldn't
> want to even think about an application in assembly.

I've done a lot of assembly programming, not on Alphas, I've
worked on 250,000+ line systems in assembly. So I agree 100%
you don't want to do it. But to gain 90% of the speed advantage
all you need to do is code the inner most loops in assembly.
If you can write a convolution or FFT routine that may be all
you need to do. Normally you spend 90% of your time in only
5% of your code. Just re-write that 5%.
