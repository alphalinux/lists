Hello

>Some interesting notes:
>The very popular Diamond Fireport40 UltraSCSI card is now a discontinued
>product.
>Intraserver still makes their very popular 3140U series with the same
>Symbios Logic 53C875 chip on it. This is a very nice card, and ( of course)
>Intraserver support Linux on Alpha.
>
>Another note:
>The Digital 2114x (tulip) series of ethernet chips are becoming scarce, so
>if purchasing a Nettgear FA310TX or similar, MAKE SURE which chip you are
>getting! Netgear, for example, has switched to a LiteOn ethernet chip. It
>works, but is not as high-performance as the real tulip chip.

FYI: After speaking to IntraServer the other day, I was informed that Compaq
will not be making the tulip ethernet chips anymore. It appears Compaq will
go with the Intel ethernet chipset.
