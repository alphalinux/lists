I'm not aware of a paper, but the following rules may serve as a
starting point:
 - Don't store pointers in ints (it should be safe to use unsigned
   longs for this purpose, but why not use pointer variables).
 - Don't make assumptions on the size of data types, always use
   the sizeof () operator.
 - Avoid assembly language.
