I used RH5.1 on an Intel machine and did masquerading. But, after that
machine died, I decided to get an Alpha. I spent several days fiddling with
ipfwadm trying to get it to work, saying to myself, "I *know* this should
work, but it isn't...." :-)

I decided to search the web to see if anyone else had problems like this.
The only article I found was dated in August of this year (sorry, I forgot
the author's name and where it was located). The author had said that
it appeared as if the ip_fw structure was not correct and was dependant
upon a 32-bit architecture. I just changed the 2 32-bit declarations
so they would be correct on a 64-bit architecture. I recompiled the
kernel and ipfwadm, and that did the trick.
