> On Thu, 19 Nov 1998, Eric Cifreo wrote:
>
> > I had this same problem. Switch the speed in your connect script to
> > 38400, even if your modem is a 56K. You can do a 'setserial
> > /dev/modem spd_hi' after you get it working, to boost your bandwidth.
>
> Will this raise the speed, even though the script says 38k4?
>

You should in fact use 'setserial /dev/modem spd_vhi' which means the serial
port will work at 115200 (even though the script would say 38400). You can put
this command in rc.serial so that it gets executed at boot time automatically.
Modems compress the data, so the serial port speed should be higher than that
of the modem, to make full use odf the bandwith.
