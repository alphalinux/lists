Most email programs keep a 'sent items' folder. It does seem that internet
marketers are getting more belligerent. If you read the print at the
bottom of a recent spam, if you don't exactly follow their procedure to the
letter, you aren't off their list. And they don't take kindly to any mail
bomb, either.

The spammers probably have to run a tight computing ship, as a spammer is
likely to tick off a proficient cracker once in a while, never mind the
masses of general 'linux hackers' - the kind that know their way around
protocols enough to screw people over. Enough so that they can find the
REAL originating computer and down it - regardless of OS. Maybe this is
why spammers tend to AOL subscribers...

But this doesn't get to the real heart of the problem - people can be real
jerks, and others powerless to do anything about it.
