Some people say that egcs-1.0.3 is the best (for Linux/AXP). I don't know
if I'd agree, personally. egcs-1.0.3 has a bug in the Haifa scheduler,

in 1.1+, also. Anyhow, I'm using the egcs-1.1.1 pre-release on my
Linux/AXP. I got the RPM's from rawhide.redhat.com (if you plan on doing
it, you'll also need the newest libstdc++ RPM). YMMV.
