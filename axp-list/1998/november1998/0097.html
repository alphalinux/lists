>>> Gerhard Poul writes:

gp> hmmm... Is there anyone who got a Netscape for i386 running in em86 on the
gp> alpha???

Yes I did.

-get the libc (not glibc) version of communicator 4.5 (x.86, of
 course)
-install em86
-disable async DNS and the about splash. I use the following script to
 start it up:

 #!/bin/sh

 export MOZILLA_HOME=/usr/local/netscape
 export MOZILLA_NO_ASYNC_DNS=True
 exec ${MOZILLA_HOME}/netscape -no-about-splash $* &

This worked for me on RH5.1 with one of Jay's kernels from gatekeeper
(alpha-sources-2.0.35-0.1.tar.gz) on an Avanti (AS200 4/233). However,
sometimes it hangs for a long time, not even redrawing the
window. (This may be because my hardware is slow.) It was an
interesting experience, but personally I don't use it. Acrobat3 on the
other hand is tolerable. YMMV.
