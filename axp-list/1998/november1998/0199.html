I don't know why you'd be leery of upgrade installs. My intel box has been
incrementally upgraded from RedHat 2.<something> all the way up to 5.1,
usually waiting for a few weeks or months after the release comes out to make
sure all the important bugs are fixed. I haven't upgraded my Alpha yet
because it's an important server and I can't afford to have it out of action
for any number of days or weeks.
