Hello,

        I just discovered the "blackbox" window-manager, an elegant,
light-weight, small-footprint X11R6 window-manager written in C++. It
compiles and runs very nice on Linux/AXP. Anyhow, for those interested,
I built an RPM of it and put it on ftp://ftp.terran.org/pub/AXP/...
The binary RPM requires libstdc++-2.9 (available from rawhide.redhat.com).
There is also a SRPM.
