The Buslogic are also quite functional and performant. In Alpha the Symbios
based cards are probably most popular because of native BIOS support in
many of the ARC and AlphaBIOS variants ( though not all, as we discovered).
Adaptec can be less than desirable due to some nagging problems with
drivers and changing Adaptec chipsets, coupled with the fact that the
performance is not all that overwhelming.
I think the cheapest has been the (now discontinued) Diamond Fireport40,
but as Jeff discovered may not work in some models of Alpha, in particular
the Cabriolet aka EB64+ machines.
