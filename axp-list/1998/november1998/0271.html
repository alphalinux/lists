Hi everyone,

I'm trying to setup two network adaptors in one machine, which is a
Digital Workstation 500a. It has a onboard DC21142/3 ethernet adaptor. I
added in another PCI NetGear FA310TX w/ a DC21140 ethernet controller.

My problem is this, when I boot the system, the onboard DC21142
controller dies. Both controllers are detected without a problem.
However, when the system begins to run inetd, the link light from the
DC21142 starts to flicker very fast, and then dies (no light).

In ifconfig, it reports that both eth0 and eth1 are up and going... my
connection through eth0 (the Netgear DC21140) works fine. But eth1
doesn't work(onboard DC21142). I'm not sure what is happening.

Has anyone had a similar experience? And how did you resolve it?

By the way, when I take out the Netgear card, the onboard DC21143
ethernet works fine... except it them becomes eth0 and not eth1. I
thought that was odd... I was expecting that the add-on Netgear card
would become eth1, but instead it took eth0... I've verified this by
noting the MAC addresses of the two controllers.

Also, I'm using the tulip drivers 0.58H and have them compiled into the
kernel (not modules).

Please send me any suggestions. I would really appreciate them. Thanks
in advance!

Masuda
