I did it (although it was an upgrade, not a fresh install).

I used x.cwru.edu -- it's not the fastest mirror for me, but it was one
of the first to mirror the 5.2 distribution. Just give it the name
(or IP address, sometimes the FTP install has problems with names:
129.22.251.177), give it /pub/redhat/redhat-5.2/alpha as the
directory, and have at it!
