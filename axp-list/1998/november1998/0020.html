The 1.33 version compiled fine on my 5.1 installation. What I used was the
source rpm (qt-1.33-i386.src.rpm (or some such) ), and ran rpm --rebuild on it,
then installed it. It worked fine.
        I personally rarely pick up a binary rpm. I almost always pick up the
source rpms and re-compile them. had too many trouble with the binaries after
the glibc change...

        just my 2 cents.
