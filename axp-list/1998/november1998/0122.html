On Sun, 18 Jul 1982 John Katzmaier wrote:
>On Fri, 13 Nov 1998, Jochen Kuepper wrote:
>>On Fri, 13 Nov 1998 Dragos-Anton Manolescu wrote:
>>
>>>m> Has anyone gotten QT 1.41 (or any version that works with KDE for that
>>>m> matter) to work on an Alpha? I've got an avanti, and it just won't
>>>m> compile:
>>>
>>>I managed to compile qt-1.33 on RedHat 5.1 and qt-1.41 on 5.2--for the
>>>latter I used egcs 1.1b; it gives an internal compiler error at one of
>>>the examples, but you don't need those for KDE.
>>
>>With egcs-1.1.1-prerelease it does all work ! Also there are no more ICEs with
>>KDE any more.
>
>QT 1.41 also compiles with egcs 1.0.3a and binutils 2.9.1.0.15

Correct. Actually you need binutils >= 2.9.1.0.13.
But KDE does not work with egcs-1.1, though Qt compiles fine. egcs-1.1 will get
internal compiler errors later on when including Qt headers.
egcs-1.1.1pre does solve that problem.

The new binutils are needed for large shared libs on AXP anyway :-)
