> traceroute-source hasn't changed a bit in the last

 It's traceroute that's broken. The structs it's using get aligned
differently from what it expects on 64-bit platforms. This results in
bogus UDP checksums. You may get a fixed SRPM package from
'ftp://ftp.amg.gda.pl/pub/OS/Linux/people/macro/SRPMS.glibc21'. Note,
this package is IPv6 aware and needs glibc 2.1 to compile. Just take the
appropriate patch from inside and rebuild traceroute, if you do not have
glibc 2.1. There is also glibc 2.1 binary RPM package available nearby
for these who like to experiment.
