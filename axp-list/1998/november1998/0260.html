Why do you want this? Is it to test out SMP code in the kernel
or to do real work?

If you want cheap and slow get a dual socket 7 M/B and a pair
of 200Mhz pentiums.

If you want performence look for a CPU chip with lots of
built-in cache. The Pent. pro 180 gives good bang for the
buck.

Look also at SPARC. The SPARC 10 can be had at a good
price and wil do two or four way SMP.
