Anyhow, I'm trying to get my AS200 4/166 up an runnin. Bare drive with no
OS. SRM only. So cool, far out, I do the rawrite thang, got my avanti-s.img
disk all ready, and I got the updated 5.0 ramdrive.img (Thank You! to Jay
for pointing out the hiccup in the 5.1 ramdrive.img) on another floppy.

canonical name for the floppy, right? so hell, you can predict the rest...

bare HD for a file system and panics

no dice, the box thinks I'm insane for suggesting it has a floppy device.
and root=/dva0 does not work, of course, so I guess I'm at a juncture where
I need to punt. A thorough scouring of Dejanews and list archive only
proves that I am either alone in the world on this one, or the worst
possible typist of boolean search engine query strings.
