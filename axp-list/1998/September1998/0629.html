> The problem is not the images, it is the hardware. Try another SCSI
> adapter, or be sure that your adapter is working correctly. From my
> expernece with that type of problem, the hardware was not found duing
> autodetection, and the software that you need is not loaded off the boot
> images.
>
> I had the same problem with a network card, the kernel did not find it and
> insmod was not loaded off the images. The second I changed the network
> card to a 3com 509, the machine was happy.
>
> I would try a Adaptech AHA 2940, they are rather low cost and they work
> 'ok' in my 21164sx.
