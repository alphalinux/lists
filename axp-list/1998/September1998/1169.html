The error happens most of them time when things get changed in the
firmware of the system. Usually the only thing that is required is to
reboot the system. Most of the time the error will not appear unless
you do make changes within the firmware. In other words is a known
problem and I don't believe that (so far at least) there is a cure other
then rebooting the system.
