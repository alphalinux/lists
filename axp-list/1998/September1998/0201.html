Quoting Steve Frampton (frampton@mail.flarc.edu.on.ca):
> (But then nothing happens, other than a 'switching to runlevel 6' entry
> appear in my /var/log/messages and some daemons restarting).
>
> Has anybody else experienced this problem? How can I get my system to
> restart (other than typing a 'reboot' command!!)?

That happened to me once. Do you still have any root login sessions? Is it
still accepting logins? If so, try doing a sync or three, then start killing
stuff. I'd start with 'kill -1 1' and go on from there. I don't think you'll
ever get it into a stage where it will shutdown gracefully, but if you sync at
least you won't lose too much.
