Stephane Casset <casset@erm6.u-strasbg.fr> writes:
> I am considering updating a RH5.0 to RH5.1 but as I have a Jensen system
> I don't what to go through all the stuff with booting with the diskimage
> and all... It is such a pain...

Once you installed 'aboot' on the Jensen, which you probably did, it's
no more pain to update than on any other system:

Boot into an interactiv aboot ("boot dka0 -fl i" at the SRM prompt).
Put the disk containing the 'ramdisk.img'(*) into the floppy, and type
'2/vmlinux.gz root=/dev/fd0 load_ramdisk=1'

Then select 'Update' instead of 'Installation'

Mayby you have to use a different argument to specify the kernel, but
I guess you're familiar with that since you using newer kernels.

HTH, Martin

(*) I heard that 'ramdisk.img' from RH-5.1 has problems recognising
minlabel partitions. If that is true, try the RH-5.0 image instead.
