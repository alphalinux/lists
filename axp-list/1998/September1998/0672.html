Mike,
In many Linux installations it is common to only have two
partitions on your HD, one for swap, one for root (read "everything
else") - the problem I ususally have when I try and create seperate
filesystems for / (root) and /usr is just what you ran into, I do
not allocate enough space to / (root).

The Red Hat install process only tracks/reports *total* disk
space required, and not so much in root, so much in /usr, etc.

My suggestion would be to install as above, with two partitions.

Good luck,
