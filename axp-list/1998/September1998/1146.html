Adaptec controllers have quite a few "interesting" issues - starting
from a termination, which is often wrong, and going through a few more
subtle gotchas. Saying that on ftp://ftp.dialnet.net/pub/linux/aic7xxx/
you will find new drivers for Adaptec. I believe that there are
there also some documents which discuss problems and possible solutions.
