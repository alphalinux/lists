YOu simply need to go in the arc console and change the time...its a
couple of menus deep...
Then make sure you use -A on the /sbin/clock commands
to make arc compatible commands, and also you can simply edit the redhat
configs once its up to make it arc compatible (/etc/sysconfig/clock), make
it ARC=true

after you make changes do a
Do a /sbin/clock -w -A
then a /sbin/clock -A
to make sure its storing the time correctly
