I'm using 2.1.106, everythings working fine including video 4 linux radio
stuff with some minor fixes. Kernel ver 2.1.120 radio stuff compiles but
doesn't work for the packardbel-aztech. Replace the 3c59x driver with a
new one if you need 3c905B working and add "options 3c59x
options=0x04" in /etc/conf.modules for 100m/b . The card doesn't
auto-negotiate right.
