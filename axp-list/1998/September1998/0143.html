Hello!

> i have the same weird problem with my SX164... when playing sounds my
> Soundblaster 16 (vibra chipset) will just stop midstream and my system
> will be completely locked.
Which kernel?

> but i have found that if i take the card out, plug it into my pentium
> system, it works fine. then i put the card back into my SX and it works!
> and then maybe three or four weeks later, while playing a sound it will
> lock up again!! i can't seem to see any pattern to what i'm doing when the
> lock-ups occur... it's very strange.
I would say that's just luck, not more.

> i don't know much about how ISA devices actually work, but it almost seems
> as though the SX system isn't clearing some kind of buffer or something on
> my sound card... and so that when i put it in my pentium, it is
> cleared...???
No no ... I do not believe this. When pulling the big red switch the SB16 is
completely resetted.
I have the conclusive thought that the hardware interrupts on the SX164
using kernels later than 2.0.33 are not handled properly anymore. Maybe I
will find the bug... wish me luck, I will need it ;)
So in case you are using 2.1.* try stepping back to 2.0.33 with the 2.0.32
alpha patches applied. This works at least for me with the floppy and
interrupt problem. Still causing trouble is using threads, i.e. memory
corruptions.
