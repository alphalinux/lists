>>> RHS Linux User said:
>
> I'm currently trying to blown Milo in a flash rom ,
> on an AlphaPC 164 LX

Please don't do this! MILO does not know all the hardware details, and really
depends on ARC or AlphaBIOS to have done all the lowest level initialization,
which will *NOT* take place if you use MILO from FLASH.

In addition, the code for FMU is not being maintained to keep up with MILO
changes, so chances are it will not work correctly, and you may damage your
machine.
