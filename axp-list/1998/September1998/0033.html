Yeah, but ttyS0 is supposed to be the "good" one, right? And it's certainly
the one that's supposed to be running getty. After all, cua? is going away in
future kernels.

In the old days, you ran modems on cua? (so that in-coming connections could
be recognized and handled along with outgoing connections) and terminals on
ttyS?.

I'm totally frustrated by this whole matter. A system that has been mostly
stable for months suddenly has things stop running properly (one disk drive
and this terminal) just because I shut down overnight, and possibly because I
changed ONE MEASELY LITTLE CONFIG VARIABLE in the kernel.
