Hello all!
Maybe this could be of interest for some of you and maybe someone from the
list is willing to help with informations...
What's it about?
I am starting an archive for special Linux Alpha informations. Alphas are
mostly like "normal" PCs as you should know by now, but only mostly. Some
things do not work as one expects it, other things are just different and
may cause trouble. Many questions on the list are asked many times and there
is no real archive of the solutions.
This archive is intented to be a place to collect all kind of useful
information and present it in a way that even novice users may be able to
track down problems and find possible solutions.
But there are many problems that are architecture specific. I do not have
access to all types of Alphas although I already collected three types myself
(Jensen, Noname and SX164). So I need the help of other Alpha users to
complete the missing parts for the other architectures.
I just started with this and would be very happy of you could have a look at
the pages I already made and would tell me your opinion. Please visit the
pages at
http://www.home.unix-ag.org/nils/Alpha/index.html

Maybe this can help exisiting and future Alpha users...
Thanks for your attention!
