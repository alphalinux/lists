Ioan MURESAN wrote:

> From where can I get an MP3player for RH5.1 that run to my DIGITAL
> AlphaServer 400 ?

The stock mpg123 works with a little hacking: you have to edit the
Makefile.
* find where it refers to the Linux build target, and remove
all Intel references (i.e. the Intel assembly files)
* find where it refers to the Digital UNIX build target, and
copy the gcc arguments from there and add them to the Intel
gcc line (i.e. the Alpha assembly files)

Basically what you're doing is telling the Makefile to use the Linux
build target with the Digital UNIX assembly source files instead of
the Intel files.

If you need more explicit directions, email me privately at
morrildl@nycap.rr.com and I'll look into it this evening at home.

Once this works, it's great: according to top, rc5des (at nice 20)
was eating 95% of my CPU time while mpg123 was eating < 5%. *grin*
