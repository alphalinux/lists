Richard Griswold wrote:
>
> I'm using a ZLXp2-E2 in my AS200 4/233. My card only has 24 planes and
> 8MB of memory. It sounds like you may have a ZLXp2-E3, which has a
> Z-buffer and 16MB of memory.
>
> You can get the 24-bit TGA X-server from
>
> http://www.cs.brown.edu/~tor/xfree86/
>
> The latest server is XF86_TGA-980823.gz. Once you get the server, gunzip
> it (if your browser hasn't already), and replace /usr/X11R6/bin/XF86_TGA
> with it. I'm using XF86_TGA-980706.gz, and it works fine, if a bit
> slowly, on my system.
>
> Richard Griswold
