Except:

1) People say that there are no kernel errors unterminated by a
newline (other than ones which are quickly followed by one that does
have a newline), and

2) There is a patch for klogd which fixes the buffer overflow that

klogd patch does not fix the overall problem.

For #2 I am not sure I am remembering things right, but I'd swear that
there was a new klogd rpm issued for rh 5.1 specifically for that
particular buffer overflow problem.
