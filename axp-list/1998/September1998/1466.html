> I am soon to be the proud owner of a new AS 200 4/233 with the 233MHZ in
> it. I also have a couple AXPCI33 mother boards. If I put 1 MB cache and
> the 233MHZ cpu in the AXPCI33 will it be faster, better, more stable, etc.,

NOOOOOOOOOOOOOOOOO!

They're *NOT* the same CPU!

AS200 4/233 uses EV45, AXPPCI33 uses LCA4/LCA45 (depending on speed).

EV45 performs *much* better than LCA45, even at slower clock rate.

Put all your energies into the AS200.
