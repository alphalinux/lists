On Mon, 26 Oct 1998, Alessio Sangalli wrote:

> Can anybody give me some advice about alpha hw???
> I want to build a small-rendering station, using alpha cpu & mobo, and
> cheap hdd - video adapter - ethernet card.

cheap video card: S3 Cards
HDD: IDE stuff
ethernet: NE2000

> I have to use ECC sdrams, right??

No. That depends on your Mainboard, there're Mainboards for all types of
RAM from ECC to SDRAM.

> Which motherboard & cpu do you think now have the best price/performance
> ratio???

PC164 Based Mainboards for the 21164 CPU Architecture,
Multia for the 21064 CPU Architecture.

> it's possible - as in pc world - to overclock succesfully alpha cpus???

I have heard such things, but ofcause i never would do this with an alpha.
The Overclocking game may be fun with a 80$ x86 CPU. Overclocking a
1000$ Alpha chip is pure nonsense, especially if you consider that the CPU
core is'nt the bottleneck in an alpha system.

May i advice you to read the Linux Alpha Howto at
http://sunsite.unc.edu/LDP/HOWTO/Alpha-HOWTO.html
competly. You will find a lot of your questions answerded here.
