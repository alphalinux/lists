With regards to your message at 10:15 AM 10-26-98 -0500, Randy Carpenter.
Where you stated:
>I just got a DEC PWS 433a. It has no external cache, and I was wondering
>if anyone has any comments on how much speed it could gain by adding 2 or
>4 Megs of cache? And along those lines do you know where to get the
>cache, and how much it would it cost?
>
General performance gains on Integer are around 30% with 2MB, and 40% with
4MB. On FP it is about 45% and 70%.
Cost of the highly proprietary cache board are about $1200 and $2600
respectiviely for 2 and 4MB. Without the cache it is a bit slower than an
SX at the same clock rate.
