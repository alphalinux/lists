After several tries with RedHat sources, I found that I obtained
a more stable kernel from pristine sources+patches.
Also, if you choose this way this list gives you a better support ;-)

It should also be noted that RedHat 5.1 is slightly inconsistent,
in that it distributes egcs (1.0.2) as a compiler, but Alpha
kernels (or at least the Ruffian one) have been compiled with
gcc, and therefore you cannot recreate the distributed kernel
from within the distribution itself.
