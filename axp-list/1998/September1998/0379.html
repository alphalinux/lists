On Thu, Sep 17, 1998 at 04:22:08PM +0300, Matti Aarnio wrote:
> I have never understood basics for this, why won't it work ?
> What in the compiler code can't be done at 32-bit host which
> works at 64-bit host ?

It's not a matter of impossibility, it is just that quite a
lot of the compiler wimps out when it can't fit the value in
a HOST_WIDE_INT.

There is actually a way around this -- to arrange for HOST_WIDE_INT
to be a long long. It's not for the faint of heart, but have a
gander at the sparc config files for how to do this.
