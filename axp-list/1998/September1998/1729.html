The easiest way is to purchase a firmware CD from digital. Call
1-800-DIGITAL. Tell them you need part number QZ-003AA-E8ALPHA. That
way, you'll have a nice CD-Rom copy of your firmware and you can go from SRM
to ARC as many times as you desire. All you have to do is boot off of the
CD-ROM. It couldn't be easier. The CD Costs 30 bucks.

Hope this helps.
