> The kernel: 2.0.34 (with serial ports and ppp support included)
> The modem: US Robotics 56K Fax external.
> The port: ttyS0 UART:16550A
> The daemon: v2.3.3
> The problem: The exact same configuration works on my Intel/linux
> machine. (But it's a slow P75 with a small hard drive, and now the
> wife wants it for a Winbox.) Syslog reports the connect script up
> to connection and then "serial line looped back."

Hi Eric,

one reason for that it is working on your slower Pentium is, that the
other side is too slow. This is a known timing problem.
Try to put one or more \d in the last string you send
in your chat script (the one that is probably empty now)
to delay the startup of your local pppd.

If this is not working, it might be useful to have some more
debugging output (insert the debug option to /etc/ppp/options and
enable logging of facility daemon.debug in /etc/syslog.conf)

Cheers, Chris
