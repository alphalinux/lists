Subscription Information
------------------------
To subscribe to a list send mail with a subject of 'subscribe' (without the
tick marks) to <LISTNAME-request@redhat.com> (or @gnome.org as appropriate)
where LISTNAME is replaced with the name of the list you wish to join.

To un-subscribe to a list send mail with a subject of 'unsubscribe' (without
the tick marks) to <LISTNAME-request@redhat.com> (or @gnome.org as appropriate)
where LISTNAME is replaced with the name of the list you wish to join.

-- 
To unsubscribe: send e-mail to axp-list-request@redhat.com with
'unsubscribe' as the subject.  Do not send it to axp-list@redhat.com
