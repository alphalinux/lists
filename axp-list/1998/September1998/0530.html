Hi Nils,

you're probably unaware, that somebody (forgot the name, but I believe
it was annouced on this list) allready started a similar project: A
user maintained Linux/Alpha FAQ, available at

http://www.nyu.edu/~jjv200/axp-cache/1.html

Now, by looking at this, we see that it isn't really attractive yet:
There aren't many entries, and it isn't linked to at a prominent place
(<http://www.azstarnet.com/~axplinux/hot.html>, that is).

Also, the "official" Linux/Alpha FAQ hasn't been updated in a while,
although I mailed some updates to Geerten Kuiper, who is the current
keeper of the FAQ. I haven't heard (read, that is) anything from
Geerten for several BTW, the last thing I discussed with him was
splitting the FAQ in several sub-FAQs, one for each architecture.

Like you I do believe that we do need a better an more up-to-date
rescource collection. So as at seems that you're willing to invest
some time, it would probalby be nice if you could take some care to
coordinate the existing efforts :-) Or maybe somebody else likes to do
this?
