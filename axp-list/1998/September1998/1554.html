I've had this problem before, or at least one similar to it. It has to do
with the directory structure or something. The files you have on your FTP
server... Are they straight off of the CD or did you download them from
RedHat. If they are the CD files, you cannot install them just by sharing
the CD on your FTP server. It won't work. I don't really know why. I was
never able to get it to work. Even copying them to my HD was not
sufficient. Try FTP to the redhat site and directory and it should go. If
you have an NT server/workstation machine on your network, pull the NT
services for Unix package from microsoft and create a NFS share for your CD,
and install via NFS. That works great, IMHO.

Hope this helps.

Steve
