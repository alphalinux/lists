> in. Now the XWindows server DOES come partially up, but it never quite gets
> there. Just a bare desktop, no bars, windows, icons or anything. The Big X
> cursor in the middle of this vast vanilla wasteland taunts me by responding

Try starting X with "startx" instead of "X". There are several other
processes that normally occur for a typical X session, particularly the use
of a window manager. RH-5.1 should include a rich selection of window
managers. You can see which ones are installed by running something like:

rpm -qa --queryformat "%{NAME} - %{GROUP}\n"|grep "Window Managers"

BTW, after you get your window manager up, can you send me a message
(privately, please) describing the color depth, resolution and quality of
your display (clarity, stability, etc), and also the type of monitor you
are using?
