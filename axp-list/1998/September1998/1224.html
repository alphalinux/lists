On Wed, 27 Jul 48489, Peter Petrakis wrote:

> Has anyone got Gnome .30 to run on Alpha? It's giving me a real hard time I
> can't compile gnome-libs. Is it just me?

I had to hack /usr/lib/glib/include/glibconfig.h to build gnome-libs.
Just add #define ATEXIT 1 and you should be on your way. This is a bug
somewhere.

Gnome .3 works on Alpha, but it seems somewhat unstable.
You can get most of the programs to crash with some ill-treatment.
I don't know if it's more stable on Intel platform, but I'd guess so.
Of course you shouldn't expect too much from a development release!
