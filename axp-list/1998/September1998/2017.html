Alessio Sangalli wrote:

> At 17.01 26/10/98 +0100, Armin Ollig wrote:
>
> > HDD: IDE stuff
> I've heard on newsgroup that IDE is VERY slow. It's true? I know scsi is a
> very good choice, it's fast, it has multitasking capabilities... but, for
> my work, I need very little hdd usage...

It's pretty slow in general, but for a LX164 motherboard (not the cheaper
SX164) and a recent kernel (2.1.115 or later), it can be quite fast. I get
better than three times the speedup with less than 1/4 the CPU load -> about
1/15 the CPU time for big disk transfers vs. earlier kernels.
