>>> Rolf Bamert said:
>
> We are trying, since several days, to make work an Alpha with the RedHut 5.1
> Alpha distribution.
>
> Here are the details on our machine:
>
> APC 164UX/BX (Ruffian) from Samsung (http://www.intl.samsungsemi.com)
> ARCS Bios 1.3.061 (from Deskstation Technology) this is the latest one
> Videocard Matrox Millenium on the 64-Bit PCI-Bus
>
> Redhat 5.1 Alpha
>
> What we have made:
>
> We have copied the Linload.exe from /milo/arc to a new formated disk
> and the milo-image from the /milo subdir and changed the name to "milo".
> We have tried the following milo-images: PC164, LX164, Ruffian

Sorry, RH 5.1 did not have the correct MILO setup for the UX; it will *NOT*
work with linload.exe.

Grab the following file from gatekeeper.dec.com:

/pub/DEC/Linux-Alpha/Kernels/Test-Milos-2.0.30+/ruffian.img

and "dd" it as usual onto a floppy, and then boot it normally.

Good luck.
