> you pick the screen layout. You can utilize this feature with many
> combinations of these cards:
>
> * Matrox Millennium
> * Matrox Millennium II
> * Matrox Mystique
> * Matrox Mystique 220
>
> NOTE: Only one Mystique or Mystique 220 (not both) may be

> ===ROBUST PERFORMANCE===
>

> quick and easy.
