>>>>> "GK" == Graham Knopp <graham@zimm.ucsf.edu> writes:

GK> I am having trouble setting my time zone, which is EDT and
GK> should be PDT. date --set=blah doesn't have any effect on the
GK> time zone, just the time.

Make certain /etc/localtime points to the appropriate time zone
information file. I reside in Indiana, hence localtime is a symlink
to ../usr/share/zoneinfo/US/East-Indiana. If you're on the eastern
seaboard of the United States, you'd make a symlink to .../US/Eastern.
