Jay.Estabrook@digital.com. Where you stated:
>
>> 5. I would like to mount the local HDs read-only (the have "AdvFS" on
>> them). Anybody has done this ? Is it possible ?
>
>I don't believe there's *any* version of AdvFS under LINUX; anyone know

Stephen Tweedie is working at Red Hat now, and rumour has it he is working
on a Journaling filesystem. As AdvFs is basically licensed Veitas code, it
is expensive, but we hope he comes up with a "free" solution. As disks and
filespaces get bigger and more complex we could all use it.
