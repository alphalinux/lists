> >I just got a DEC PWS 433a. It has no external cache, and I was wondering
> >if anyone has any comments on how much speed it could gain by adding 2 or
> >4 Megs of cache? And along those lines do you know where to get the
> >cache, and how much it would it cost?
> >
> General performance gains on Integer are around 30% with 2MB, and 40% with
> 4MB. On FP it is about 45% and 70%.
> Cost of the highly proprietary cache board are about $1200 and $2600
> respectiviely for 2 and 4MB. Without the cache it is a bit slower than an
> SX at the same clock rate.

Thanks for your comments. What I am mainly interested in is general
performance. I will be using this as a workstation. I will be running a
lot of xterms, and netscape, word processing and general apps like that.
I would like to get em86 running to run things like Applixware and
such. Compiling the kernel and various packages will be about the limit
of my usage on this machine. I have read that adding L3 cache actually
slows down memory access for programs which are larger than the size of
the cache, so is it really worth it to get the cache?

thanks again for your comments,
-Randy
