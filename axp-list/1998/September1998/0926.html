As I learned from my "noname" system board, you do not want to run
with the case open... Maurice sold me the unit and explained when
my system would halt for "no apparent reason" when "the case was
open to provide maximum airflow" - as Maurice explained, the case
is like a tube, and the fan needs to take the air from somewhere
and exhaust it somewhere else, if the case is open, nothing is sucked
out of the case, it all stays pretty stagnant, and the CPU will
overheat and halt.

Is your case open?
