Two issues:
1) Best PCI video card:
Hands down: Matrox Millennium. Has all the support you need,
with more colors than you'll use. This is probably the single best
supported/performing card going. They cost just a hair more, but as they
say: You get what you pay for.

2) Starting xdm. simple:
vi /etc/inittab

Look for the following entry:

id:3:initdefault:

Change it to:
id:5:initdefault:
(change the 3 to a 5). This will auto start xdm at boot time.
Then be sure to take it out of your rc.local.

Enjoy!
