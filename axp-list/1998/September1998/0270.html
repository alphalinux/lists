>>> teoks@csm.st.com.sg said:
>
> I would like to know if 2.0.35, 2.0.36pre series or the Linux 2.1.12x
> series can be installed on the fllwg Digital machines:
>
> Multia
> AlphaServer 1000
> AlphaServer 1200
> AlphaServer 2100
> AlphaServer 4100

Multia, aka UDB, is in the NONAME family. and supported by all those
kernels; AS1000 (MIKASA), AS1000A (NORITAKE), and AS2100 (SABLE), are
also supported, in both 4/xxx and 5/xxx models.

No Alphas are supported for SMP under the 2.0.xx kernels.

AS1200 (TINCUP) and AS4000/4100 (RAWHIDE) are *only* supported under the
2.1.xx kernels, and will be supported for SMP by the 2.1.122 and later
kernels, as will AS2100.

Also, AS1000, AS1000A, AS1200, AS2100, AS4000, AS4100 are *only* supported
when booting from SRM console; ie, there are no official MILOs for any of
them, nor are there ever likely to be.

> Any particular linux distributions, eg Redhat would install cleanly without
> additional patches?

Red Hat 5.1 has kernels for (nearly) all the above, but there are some
installation problems (related to SRM booting) that one must be aware of
and work around. Get in touch directly if you need more info...

> Stability in a production environment?

That's for other's to say; I don't use any of them in a production
environment, at the moment...
