> whether anyone has a fix.
>
> It may be an Xserver problem (I'm using XFree-SVGA with a Millenium II), or
> may be a glitch in xdvi itself. I have tried upgrading to the latest xdvi

> crash/lockup.
> My machine is an SX146 currently running 2.1.123.

and XL300 whith S3VirgeDX) and I have used xdvi a lot. I have always
used the normal xdvi in the teTeX distribution that came whith RedHat
3.5 - 5.1, without any problem whith the magnifying glass.
