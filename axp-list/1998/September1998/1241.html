I had a similar problem with mine, except it was going the opposite
direction, it kept jumping up to 2048 or same such... What I did:

Shut it down, and pull the plug (safety first!).
Open the case, and locate the battery.
Remove the battery, and let it all sit for about 60 seconds
Reinstall battery, close case, plug it back in, and restart.

You'll need to re-set your NVRAM stuff, it'll all be defaulted.

This corrected the problem I had with mine.

Good Luck!
