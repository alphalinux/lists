>
> I've now tried AlphaBios 5.66 as supplied by Juan Perron of Aspen
> systems, no luck. MILO still detects SCSI Devices, gives a message about
> FAST/WIDE mode, and hangs. Any other ideas? I'll call aspen on monday and

This is most likely a MILO problem, or rather, a problem with the NCR SCSI
driver in MILO. What version of the SCSI driver is listed during the MILO
startup messages?

The latest LX164 MILO is available on gatekeeper.dec.com:

/pub/DEC/Linux-Alpha/Kernels/milo-lx-980810

Try it, and see if it helps.
