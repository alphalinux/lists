ixsoft Softwareentwicklung- und vertrieb wrote:
>
> Since I have the same problems with my PC164 LX Boards, I wonder if there
> is a standard time setting procedure for Alpha PCs unter Red Hat Linux ?

Yes. And is even documented. Check what -A flag to 'clock' means.

> So far nothing worked (and clock and timetool even do different things -
> arghh !)

???? Make sure that your /etc/sysconfig/clock looks somewhat like that

UTC=true
ARC=true

where values 'true' and 'false' reflect reality. Set system clock
properly and write it back with 'clock -Auw' (once again - flags should
correspond to YOUR settings and this also assume that your timezone
is sane). So far I never seen a problem on these numerous LX boards
which passed through my hands.

I am not even trying to guess what timetool attempts to do. Quite
possibly the right thing but I will leave that for you to check.
