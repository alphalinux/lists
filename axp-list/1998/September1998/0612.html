I loaded slink last week on my SX164 box. Overall it went well. There
are a few problems to shake out before release, mostly they are lacking a
complete set of boot kernels and milo images, and dpkg has a bug exposed
by glibc and recent kernels.

It's a very complete a usable distribution IMO. They are now including
egcs-1.1. I've built and installed KDE without problems. I haven't yet
tried any RH 5.1 binaries but I don't expect much trouble since the
current stuff is based also on glibc 2.0.7.
