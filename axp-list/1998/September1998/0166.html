It's pretty easy...Just boot using the standard installation methods,
choose an installation method that involves initzalizing the network
(i.e. NFS or FTP install) and then move to VT 2 or 3 where you can
manually mount your NFS root as "/mnt". From there just move according to
the menus and avoid the steps that involve actually formattting or doing
other nasty hardware-related disk thingies.

Worked for me,
