Well I got metro x for the Alpha today and it works great. Configuration
was a snap. It supports my Fire GL 1000 pro very well and I don't get
any more errors when I shutdown X. It configures your monitor , mouse ,
and keyboard. In short a neebee could figure it out easily. There are
plans to support the MS intellmouse and the little wheel but that
doesn't help me since I have a mouseman +. It's worth it especially if
you have the matrox cards which support multiple monitors. I'm very
satisfied with it and I recommend it to anyone who has a Permedia II
based card.
