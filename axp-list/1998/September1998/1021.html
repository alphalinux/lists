Well, on my UDB I have ARC console which you can create multiple boot
selections. During your installation you should have created a DOS partition
about 2MB in size. The command to place your milo disk into this partition
is : dd if=/dev/fd0 of=/dev/sda1 bs=1440k .

This assumes your 2MB partition is /dev/sda1 change as is appropriate for
your configuration. Make the changes to ARC or AlphaBios to reflect the
location of MILO.

Hope this helps.

Bruce
