Hi all --
I've built a couple of the onsale AS200's that came with the #9 video
card, and they went pretty much by the book. I realized today that
the third one was sold minus said card. No problem, I stuck in a
Cirrus Logic PCI card that was lying around. SRM is happy with it.
I grabbed my avanti-s.img and ramdisk.img floppies and went for it.
About the time the kernel gets to Probing PCI Hardware, the display
gets moved elsewhere. I made sure that console was set to graphics,
and added the iconfig entries to match a working machine. This may
be part of the problem as I have no useful documentation on the
Cirrus card. I didn't think this would be a problem, as, AFAIK, the
kernel couldn't care less (as opposed to X, which cares deeply).
Anyone been through this exercise already and can lend a hand?
TIA,
