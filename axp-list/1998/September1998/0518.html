It looks like there isn't an 'aboot' with the vmlinux-rawhide.gz
file , IE: it is probably the kernel only .
The above may even be that it hasn't compiled with SRM support
built in ?? But the message above should not be caused
by lack of SRM support in the kernel .

The below is assuming that you have the 'aboot' facilities
compiled . Then what you might try doing is:

cp vmlinux-rawhide.gz vmlinux.gz
fdformat /dev/fd0
mke2fs /dev/fd0
swriteboot /dev/fd0 bootlx vmlinux.gz

I highly recommend the URL: below concerning aboot .

http://www.redhat.com/support/docs/rhl/alpha/RHL-5.1-Alpha-Installation-Addendum/manual/doc025.html
