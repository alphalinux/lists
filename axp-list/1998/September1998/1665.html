Hi all ...
I have just uploaded some patches to the Alpha-Linxu archive.
The first is against xosview-1.6 and enables display of more than 24
interupts. Most Alphas have more than that (e.g. my SX has 40). It will be
included in the next xosview release.
The second is against xcopilot-0.6.6 (the USR Pilot emulator). Without the
patch xcopilot will not run at all. The patch fixes some things and Copilot
is usable now but still has two bugs: X shared memory does not work and
Copilot's clock ticks too fast. But it is usable ;)
Both can be found at
http://www.unix-ag.org/Linux-Alpha/Packages/
