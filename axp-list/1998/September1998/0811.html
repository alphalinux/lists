I'd say the general rule with the UDB is, the older the card the better...

What I mean is, the ARC and SRM console code is most likely three years old
(even the newest), and so, WRT driver capabilities, sadly out of date.

The newer cards should be usable once you get MILO going, however, since that
*is* kept (mostly :-) up to date.

This holds true not only for SCSI cards, but also graphics; the BIOS
emulators in ARC and SRM are old as well; at the time of the last console
release, they didn't have to deal with some of the sophisticated BIOS code
found on new cards, like Millenniums and such.
