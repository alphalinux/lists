On Tue, 29 Sep 1998, James T. Robson wrote:

> Hello all, I hope someone can help me with this one.
>
> I want to install a second network adapter in the spare PCI slot in a Digital UDB (Multia).
Reading the Ethernet Howto section 3.2 would be good...but here it goes...

If its a module use /etc/conf.modules to make it aware, if its not
modules, or its not redhat pass this to the kernel

ether=eth0,ether=eth1

that should do it.
>
> When I do this, both adapters seems to be recognized by the bios as they are included in the hardware setups in the bios, but when it boots, Linux (Red Hat 5.0), recognizes only the plug-in PCI board and not the on-board adapter. It sees it as eth0 and assigns it an IRQ 10, but I see no mention of eth1 in the boot operation.
>
> I have tried a SMC with a Digital 21041-AA DC1017BA 21-40756-01 chip on it.
> I have also tried a RTL 8029 with similar results.
>
> The plug-in board works just fine but it seems to deactivate the on-board adapter.
>
> Thank you kindly for any attention that any of you can give to this problem.
>
