okay everybody, i've solved my screwed-up ARC problem...

i ended up getting myself back to the SRM console. with which i booted a
RH 5.1 avanti-s.img diskette... once i was back at the prompt of bash, i
set the CMOS clock with the following commands:

date 100421171998
clock -w

this set the system time to 04 Oct 1998 21:17, which i then wrote to CMOS
with the clock command. i then rebooted and checked the date in the SRM...
all was good! the time stuck...

so then updated the firmware to 'arc' again... and this time the ARC
allowed me to enter a time and date without complaining... and viola! i am
able to boot again from the ARC console.

i must have just screwed-up the time in the CMOS so badly that ARC just
couldn't comprehend it...??? i'm not really sure.

i *did* try the "leave the battery out overnight" thing... but didn't
work.

matt
