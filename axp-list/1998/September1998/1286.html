i use to have ipmasq going on my SX164 with rh5.0... it worked flawlessly.
then i bought an AS200 and put a fresh copy of rh5.1 on it... to my
dismay, the same ipmasq commands that worked on my SX164 didn't work
here... i wasn't aware of the ipmasq differences that appartantly some
other people on this list are.

so i ended up erasing the rh5.1 ipmasq rpm and installing the rh5.0 rpm...
then everything worked great. it looks like the two rpms are the same
version of ipmasq, just different builds.

for reading i would suggest looking at the mini-howto out there:

http://sunsite.unc.edu/mdw/HOWTO/mini/IP-Masquerade.html

there's not that much involved with doing what you want... just make sure
you have the correct things compiled into your kernel... then point all of
the machines behind the ipmasq to gateway to the machine doing the
masquerading. then execute commands such as:

ipfwadm -F -p deny
ipfwadm -F -a m -S 192.168.1.0/24 -D 0.0.0.0/0

adjusting the 192.168.1.0 address as necessary.

matt
