> Do you have an appropriate symlink in the /etc/rc.d/rc*.d
directories? On
> mine it's called S15nfsfs, but it might be called something else.
But when
> booting up the system doesn't look in /etc/rc.d/init.d, it looks in
the
> appropriate rc?.d directory (if booting the standard runlevel 3, it
looks in
> /etc/rc.d/rc3.d/) for files begging with a capital S, and executes
them in
> alphanumeric order with the argument "start". These files are all
symlinks to
> the scripts in /etc/rc.d/init.d/.
