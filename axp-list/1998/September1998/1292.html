Why say "maybe"? The strace posted earlier makes it clear that syslogd
is printing that error because it is trying to read from a bogus fd
number. Somehow it got overwritten. That somehow could revolve around
long messages.

> I'm moving slow on my changes to klogd, does anyone have any patch or

Exactly how did you build 2.0.35? No one else has reported that level
of problem. If you look through the archive of this list you will find
instructions on how to take vanilla 2.0.35 and apply the 2.0.34 axp
patch to it. I'm running 67 machines with 2.0.35 and have never gotten
a mysterious crash.
