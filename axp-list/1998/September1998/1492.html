Hi guys,
I just purchased an external scsi zip today and to my disapointment
I've found it has a 25 pin scsi connection that doesn't jive with the
back of my 2940UW. So I went looking on the net and found
a 25-50 pin scsi gender changer for $22 bucks. Then I thought since I
have a free isa slot that i'll probably never use I should look for a
scsi card just for the zip and compare the price to the gender changer.
I found the adaptec AVA-1505 for $55 at my local CompUSA which ain't
bad. So for $20 more bucks I can get a scsi card dedicated to my zip and
be able to hook up a scanner and a web cam later but I have some
concerns. Being behind the PCI bridge what kind of performance
degradation should I expect? Will I have problems with the kernal's
driver and is this card even supported well? One more, Anybody know of a
web cam that has a scsi interface? Thanks alot.
