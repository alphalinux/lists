Using your NT or Win95, start a dos session and on your RedHat CD is a
directory called dosutils. In it is the utility to create your avanti disk
and your ramdisk (rawrite.exe). Your Milo disk can be created by formatting
a disk under Win95 machine and copying milo and linload.exe from your cd
images directory or by getting it from DEC's site.
