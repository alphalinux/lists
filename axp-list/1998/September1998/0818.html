Hey ho,

I got "moz-static124-0429.tgz" from lsl.
After unpacking the tgz I wrote following bash script, that makes my netscape
work.

Netscape:
- ---
#!/bin/sh

export NETSCAPE=./netscape
export MOZILLA_HOME=/usr/local/netscape
export LIBRARY_PATH=$MOZILLA_HOME/bin:$MOZILLA_HOME/lib:$LIBRARY_PATH
export LD_LIBRARY_PATH=$MOZILLA_HOME/bin:$MOZILLA_HOME/lib:$LD_LIBRARY_PATH
export PATH=$MOZILLA_HOME/bin:$PATH

cd $MOZILLA_HOME/bin

../$NETSCAPE ${1+"$@"}

- ---

Running this scrpit starts netscape pretty nicely.
But netscape itself doesn't work probablly. You can't type in any URL or edit
any input-fields on html-pages.
The bookmarks doesnt' work, too.

But it's better than nothing.

Hope that will help!
