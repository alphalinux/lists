On 28-Sep-98 Barrett G. Lyon wrote:
>
> Hello all,
>
> I would like to take an aduit of system configurations using the 21164sx.
> I have a very unstable machine, and I feel a few other people do also. I
> would like to see what people do differently to see if there is any way to
> find out what makes someone's system stable/not-stable.
>
> I will post my findings to this group, and a website.
>
> This is for the 21164sx only.
> [cut here]----------------------------------------------------------
> Kernel Version....................:2.0.33 w/alpha patches from gatekeeper
> OS Distribution...................:RH 5.0 w/all errata
> Best Uptime.......................:96 days (took down for system upgrade)
> Stability Rating Scale of 1-10....:10
> Network Adapter(s)................:Cheetah (Tulip chipset)
> SCSI Adapter(s)...................:53c810 and 53c875
> Video Card........................:S3 trio
> MILO Version......................:Built against kernel 2.0.30 w/SX clock fix
> HDD TYPES/BRAND...................:Seagate ST19171W (9.1Gb UW) and external
Jaz drive
> Memory type(s)....................:192Mb (2x32 and 2x64)
> Extra Cooling.....................:CPU fan and case fan
> CACHE.............................:stock SX
> CPU Speed.........................:533
> Compiler..........................:EGCS 1.1b (now)
> Adv Load..........................:?If you mean "average", it is low
> Local or Remote Logging...........:local
> file-max setting..................:default
> inode-max setting.................:default
> Non-standard patches/upgrades.....:SwIM Motif 2.0 for axp, KDE 1.0
> Other Notes.......................:IDE CDROM, Seagate SCSI tape drive, both
internal. HP Scanjet-4C, ISA SB clone card *does* work fine. DU 3.2g libs
installed for running DU Netscape (yes I have a license).

This system has never locked up on me except when I do something stupid. It goes
down only for hardware upgrades. I am very pleased with it and have no
complaints. System was purchased from DCG Computers (Thanks Steve).
