With proftpd, it's a 1-line change to the config file
("DefaultRoot ~", I believe). With wu-ftpd, it's doable but I don't know
much about it. I vaguely recall it being more painful, but again can't
say for sure.

You can grab proftpd (s)rpms from ftp://rpm@prophet.dodds.net/.
The 1.2.0pre1 version is patched to work with pam. The 1.0.3 version is
not, but should work fine on a glibc2 system (needs md5-fallthrough
crypt).
