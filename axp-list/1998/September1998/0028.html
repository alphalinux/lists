My .got exceeds the maximum allowed size, because my usual compilation
technique for my big C++ library is to cat all the files together and

binutils (".got exceeds 64K blah blah...").

my code is broken, or egcs is broken.

well enough that future versions will stay unbroken.

Life is just different with big C++ programs.
