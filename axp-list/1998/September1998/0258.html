Answer: the second case. It is not a matter of leaving 32MB free.
Even with 512MB of physical RAM, my PC164UX's (all three of them) are
unstable when Linux uses more than 224MB. Up to 224MB they are
stable as rocks.
This perhaps explains why some people on this list did not
have problems, while other did.

My management is now asking me to give up and swap these
machines with LX's, or Intel boxes. This is the last week of
experimentation, then we say goodbye to the UX. Sigh. It was fast.
