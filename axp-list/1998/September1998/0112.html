Richard Henderson wrote:
>
> On Fri, Sep 11, 1998 at 02:44:49PM -0400, Bret Orsburn wrote:
> > This means that the .got segment of a shared library (.so) must not
> > exceed 64KB?
>
> No. It means that none of the .o files that were fed to ld to create
> the .so must have (generate) a .got segment that exceeds 64k.
>
> You will get link-time errors if this happens. It will not be silent.

I get no link-time errors. Just a segment fault at run time.

If I break the shared library into two pieces, it works fine.

(I would do just that if it was our library, but it's a 3rd party lib and I need
something a little stronger than one anecdote before I ask them to repackage
their product.)
