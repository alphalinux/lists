On Thu, 8 Oct 1998, Scott Alexander wrote:

> I'm getting ready to purchase some new systems and am wondering how
> soon we'll start to see 21264 systems. What are pricing and configurations
> likely to be?
>

Should be any day now. Pricing? You don't want to know. The chip itself
will be well into 4 figures at first. But this chip is unlike most any
other as far as raw power goes. What are the systems to be used for? You
may consider waiting until the 21264 is out for a month or two at which
time the 21164 prices should go down even more. A 21164 at 600 or 633MHz
is "still" one hell of a machine.
