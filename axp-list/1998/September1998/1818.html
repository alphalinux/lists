1) mixviews works and is available on Debian/Alpha (You´ll need the ivtools (InterViews) library for that)

1) Snd2.0 compiles and works with lesstif-0.83 (see ftp://ftp-ccrma.stanford.edu/pub/Lisp)

2) xwave should work too, and is probably the easiest to compile and install

3) mix is a protools like multichannel soundfile mixer, where you can apply envelopes and effects to 8
channels of wavfiles. (This is probably what you want to use, together with one of the editors). (lesstif-0.83)
I have a patched version for Alpha around, if you want to try it, contact me.

4) Broadcast used to work too, but it has been long since I tried it.
