The reason for the error is that it's trying to load
/lib/libc.so.6 which more than likely is your alpha binary. You're
correct about needing a copy of ld-linux.so.2 that searches in
/usr/x86/lib instead of /lib. I actually have such a thing on my machine,
but I seem to have lost the patches to build it. This is a problem as the
binary I have always segfaults. :-(

If you get it working, lemme know. 'Til then, I have some ideas
(hacks) I'm going to try out; I'll let you know if any of 'em work.
