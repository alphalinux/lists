On Thu, 15 Oct 1998, Bryan Call wrote:

> I am looking at buying a few 3c905B Cyclone cards and would like to know
> if anyone has had good results with these cards. Currently I am using
> some 3c905 and DE500. They have been both working very well.

I got one of these when I got USWest's xDSL service. I believe it's a
3c905b-tx. I got it working last Thursday, and it's been working ever
since. I don't see anything goofy about it.

I'm running RH 5.0 for Alpha on a 133MHz UDB. I had to download a
different (later) version of 3c59x.c from:
http://cesdis.gsfc.nasa.gov/linux/drivers/vortex.html
to get the kernel to recognize the 3c905b-tx.

I statically compiled the 3c59x.c code into the kernel, as I am too lame
to figure out how to do it as a loadable module.
