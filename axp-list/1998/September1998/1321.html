Ok, has _anyone_ successfully gotten another PCI video card working
in Xfree86 on thier UDB? Ive been trying to get a matrox millenium
working, without luck. If i put the card in and boot the system,
the box freezes up at the bios emulation init. It leaves the
screen readable, but text is overlapping and blinking. If i set
a switch on the matrox card to "disable vga" the box boots up normally,
with all video going thru the tga card. Now, if i run Xconfigurator,
it actually probes the Matrox card, but when i run startx, i get
the error: ***None of the configured devices were detected.***
Yes, I am using the XF86_SVGA server :)

In any case, if you have gotten any card working in X on your UDB,
I would love to know exactly:
1) what card/chipset are you using?
2) what is the firmware version on your UDB?
3) what is your kernel config set for
CONFIG_TGA_CONSOLE and CONFIG_VGA_CONSOLE
4) what is the sequence that text is output on each video card?
i.e., console messages start on TGA card, then move to VGA card
at milo? VGA card becomes primary at what point?

Thanks in advance,
