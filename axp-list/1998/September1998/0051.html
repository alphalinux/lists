Hi!

> Sound cards on the SX have always been a problem. However, 4Front
I do not think so. The soundcard is _not_ the problem. I would even go as
far as saying that every ISA device sending many interrupts, like sondcards
normally do, will cause trouble.
Some investigations turned out that the interrupt code used in 2.1.* kernels
is broken for the SX164 (like the floppy is too, but that's another story).
You can easily test this by starting ldescent for Alpha; on my machine it
freezes the machine completely either at once or after a few seconds. XQuake
show a similar problem only that it continues when the mouse is moved. At
the moments when the machine has a momentary hang no hardware interrupts
come through, i.e. no ping, no keyboard, nothing, just the mouse, until the
complete freeze ;)
After that I took the 2.0.33 kernel and applied the alpha-2.0.32 diff and
see... it works! Also floppy works ;)
So there have been some changes from 2.0.33 to 2.1.* which were deadly for
SX164, just what?!

> Technologies has done the drivers. They are at a moderate price and can be

> FYI: They even did enhanced drivers for the AWE 64 Gold which are NOT
> available under NT for Alpha!
Nice to know but does not help in that situation, sorry.

If someone knows what has changed or if someone knows someone who knows...
