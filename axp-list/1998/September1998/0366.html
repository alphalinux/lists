i personally cannot attest to PCI problems with my 164SX.

PCI cards i have installed:

-NCR810 scsi-2 (with 3 drives attached)
-Matrox Millennium
-Accton 10/100Mb (tulip chipset)

haven't had any of the problems you are describing... however i *have* had
problems with the use of my ISA devices... standard SB16 won't work
consistantly, usually locks the machine up when playing sounds. i also
tried to use my internal USR 28.8 modem to no avail... i ended up buying
an external thinking i had fried the internal somehow... :(

maybe your ISA 3c509 is screwing things up??
