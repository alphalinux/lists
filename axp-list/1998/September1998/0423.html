On Thu, 17 Sep 1998 Kirk Vogelsang wrote:
>I've just compiled Qt-1.40. It compiled with no errors or
>warnings, but anything that gets linked to libqt segfaults.
>
>I've tried configure'ing w/ both linux-g++-shared and
>linux-g++-static.

Well, shared does not work for Qt-1.40. RTH found an linker bug causing that
and is working on it, I guess (hope).

>gcc -v:
