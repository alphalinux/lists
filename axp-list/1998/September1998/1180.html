I was getting it about once every two or three days on my one and only Alpha.
Then I stopped running syslog for a while (and worried like hell about what
was happening to my system), and then I started syslog to a separate box, and
got a 39 day uptime (until a lightning storm forced me to shut down). But
after the storm, after 11 days I got the run-away even with remote logging.
Last weekend I upgraded my system to RedHat 5.1 (thanks to the Cable Modem an
FTP upgrade takes less time than a CD-ROM upgrade used to). I've been running
for 6 days without a runaway, and (knock wood) I'm hoping that's it.
