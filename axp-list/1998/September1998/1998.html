Hy,

Jus a positive Alpha story to get you through the day....

I ran across an nice Milennium for few $ and wanted to try and get a
long-time wish -- a multi headed setup - fore filled.

After reading a positive story about Metro-X for Alpha I decided to
take the plunge. And it works great!! I have an XL300 with two
(original model I) milleniums (one with 2 and one with 4 Mb) hooked to
a 15" color monitor and a 21" gray scale (EIZO 6500) monitor.

Apart from a problem with configX (the adjust image button did not
work) that was resolved by downloading a path nothing seriously is
broken (the help function dumps core at the moment).

Performance seems slightly better than the XF86 X server but that was
quick enough for my anyway. It's the multi headed feature that makes
Metro-X worth the $39,- for me. Of course I only have it running for a
couple of days so their might be some problems ahead...

Cheers -- Jaap
