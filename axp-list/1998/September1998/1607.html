>>> Aideen McConville said:
>
> I'm an Linux/Alpha newbie and haven't been able to get an X Server
> running. Any suggestions of what might be amiss would be welcome!
>
> AlphaStation 255/233
> with S3 Trio64 (SVGA) Plug-N-Play, 2.0 Mb
> (Number Nine GXE64 with S3 Trio64)
> Red Hat 5.1, Kernel 2.0.34 (booted from SRM)
>
> startx crashes the whole system.

Try adding the following to the Devices Section of your XF86Config file:

Option "no_pci_disconnect"
Option "sw_cursor"
VideoRam 2048

Good luck.
