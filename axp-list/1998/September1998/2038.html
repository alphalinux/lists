In one Alpha and one Intel Multia I have standard notebook drives.
They are 1.0 gig, from Integral Peripherals, purchased from Onsale.
These drives have no on-board cache, which limits performance, but at
least on the 166Mhz VX40 Alpha, that's not much of a problem.

In one Intel Multia I have a 3.5" Maxtor, wasting the UDMA capabilities of
the drive. I'm not sure the power supply in an Alpha version would
support this drive. The Intel unit runs a LOT cooler.
