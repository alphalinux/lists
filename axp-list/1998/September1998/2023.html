Daniel Stringfield wrote:
>
> I am trying to install Redhat 5.1 off of CD-ROM. I have a AlphaServer
> 4100, which under directions I'm using the 5.0 root disk to handle the BSD
> disk labels.
>
> I get about ~230Mb into the installation of RPM's, and then it tells me
> "No space left on device". But I'm installing to a partition which
> has ~1.9Gb space.
>

Most likely you're not on a single partition and
one of them was too small. Even if one of your
partitions is huge, it won't matter if you made
/ or /usr too small. Most space is consumed by /usr.

Better re-check what your partitions look like.
