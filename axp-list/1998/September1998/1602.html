Barrett G. Lyon wrote:
>
> Regardless of its exact name, when I compiled the DE4x5 support into my
> kernel the machine would hang the second eth0 was configured.

Some versions of de4x5, in my experience, were doing that with some
kernels. Actually I have seen scrolling very fast, too fast too read,
"Aiee...." messages.

In my tests changes to de4x5 in the latests 2.0.36pre kernels cured
that problem. You will need Jay's patches for 2.0.35 as well.
OTOH tulip driver included there (0.89H) also seems to work fine
for cards which were causing previous headaches. Take your pick.

YMMV
