I own an alphapc164lx with a Western Digital Caviar 34300 IDE drive.

appears as "jka0/jka100", and i have no more dka0 to boot from.
IDE cdrom stays jka100/jka0 normally.

If i remember correctly, "jka0" refers to a magneto-optic drive, in
openvms systems. Quite a cheap way to change your ide drive into a mod.. :)
but that's not really what i expected..

any ideas ?
