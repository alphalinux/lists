>> It does not come to the milo prompt.

>The most popular reasons for the above are:
> - You are using wrong milo for your particular board. Unfortunately
> choices are multiple and often baffling as the same boards are known
> under different monikers - depending on who named them.
> - You are using video card which happens NOT to be properly
> initialized by BIOS a emulator used in milo (the only cure is a
> different video card).
> - You are using UX, aka. Ruffian, and your video is not in 64-bit
> PCI slot; the current milo for Ruffian can initialize only video
> in a slot in front of a PCI bridge and you happen to have only
> one like that. This is supposed to change soon.
>
>The above list does not pretend to be exhaustive.
> Michal
