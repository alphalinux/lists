Uhm, the internal and external SCSI connectors are physically on the
same (Fast SCSI-2) bus. An internal device will not run faster than an
external one -- the machine doesn't know the difference.

I have 80MB in mine, and it needs more. These things are memory hogs.
Speed seems on par with a Pentium 133.
