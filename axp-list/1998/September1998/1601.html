>Lots of people can get the kernel to compile with tulip.c, and there
>is no "de500" driver -- you must mean the de4x5 driver.

DE425, DE434, DE435, DE450, DE500 support
This is support for the DIGITAL series of PCI/EISA Ethernet
cards. These include the DE425, DE434, DE435, DE450 and DE500
models.

Regardless of its exact name, when I compiled the DE4x5 support into my
kernel the machine would hang the second eth0 was configured.

>used to use, while v0.89H or whatever is happy with 2.0.34 or
>up. tulip v0.83 won't work with 2.0.35, because an interface changed.
