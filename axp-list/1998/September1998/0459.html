I have a similar problem with the serial port but it 's with a router. Though
it only happens somtimes, usally when I reboot. Just don't turn on the modem
until milo is finished and it's jumped to the kernel. Also next time you
rebuild your kernel disable "out kernal xxx to com1" or stick you modem on
another serial port. Try using hard links instead of sym links with the kernal
prob.
