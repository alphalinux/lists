Hello Jeff

>In order to get out of the debug monitor. You need serial connection to
>a terminal. Terminal has to be set to 8/n/1 (just like your modem for
>BBS's usually was set). You connect your serial terminal to com port 1.
>After you do that you should see a display on your screen. You can type
>bootopt NT to get into the arc console.
>
>Unfortunately if you don't have the terminal you are in trouble, because
>there is no other way to get out of this option. The reason being is
>because all of the output is going now to the serial com port, and none
>of it is going to the screen.
>
>Hope this helps
>Marek Dohojda
>Carrera Computers
