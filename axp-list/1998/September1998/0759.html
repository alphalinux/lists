> Just an anecdotal confirmation; here, on at least two AS200s, dhcpcd does
> work with 2.0.35 but NOT with 2.1.xxx.

I do not remember now details, as I do not have much to do now with
dhcpcd, but in 2.1.xxx there are changes how this is supported.
In /usr/src/linux/Documentation/ you will find quite a list of
modifications and required updates before things will work with 2.1.xxx.

>From what I am told an old hack used by dhcpc has a potential of causing
broadcast storm on networks (and actually does that from time to time);
it is also banned by some standards. Apparently current versions
of dhcpcd work with 2.1.xxx just fine, but I doubt that you will find such
updated daemon in any rpm packages from Red Hat. You will have to
fire up this old compiler yourself.
