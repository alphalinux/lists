Okay... well. Another update (darn, I'm busy today. =P):

I increased my SHM_MAX to 0x420000 as seen in an old post, and that *seems*
to have helped matters... but I still have problems with playing audio,
especially from a pipe (like cdparanoia | tplay). The odd, random app
segfaults seem to have stopped... but...

When the sounds stuff crashes sometimes, it freezes the box. Not a total
freeze, but a kind of freeze that I've never seen anywhere else. It stops
all processes from running, but the kernel is still awake. I can switch
virtual consoles, I can connect to TCP ports, I can ping the box, that all
works fine... all the apps are dead, though. I can't type anything, after
a TCP port opens up to a service, the service never negotiates, etc.

Again, I'm using OSS/Linux [commercial] on an AWE64 Gold ISA, kernel 2.0.35
on a PC164LX 21164a 533MHz with 256MB RAM.
