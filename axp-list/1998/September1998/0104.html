Hi.

I've had good luck with the Netgear FA310TX, built with the 21140 chip,
around $35. They also sell a "networking kit" with two adapters, cables,
and a 100-bTX 4-port hub for about $110.

If you like development kernels, use the 0.89 version of the Tulip
driver, from Donald Becker's site. He does great work.

Robb.
