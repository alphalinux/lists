On 14-Sep-98 Jonathan Vafai wrote:
> I am trying to load DU netscape (standalone) under RH5.1
>
> I have all the libs (that is, it does not complain that any are missing.)
> but it will not start up because of the error:
>
> % netscape &
> 2871:/usr/local/bin/netscape: /sbin/loader: Fatal Error:
> set_program_attributes failed to set heap start
>
>
> Any idea as to how I can fix this?

Sounds like maybe you are trying to run with the DU 4.x libs without a new
enough development kernel, or 3.x libs with a 2.0.x kernel that is too old.

What kernel, what version of netscape and what version of DU libs are you
running?

Using DU libs 4.x requires at least (I think) 2.1.8x and still yields problems.

Best bet is to use 3.xx DU libs, and Netscape 3.0x, this works fine for me with
kernel 2.0.33.
