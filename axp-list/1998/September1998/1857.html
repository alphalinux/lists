On Mon, Oct 19, 1998 at 12:12:59PM -0400, Jason Strand wrote:
> The kernel was recently compiled, but it was done by a friend who uses
> Slackware for Intel. He may have messed the kernel up, but when I checked
> it there was support for Logitech Mousemen, and all the other mice had no
> support. One thing I'm worried about is the fact that my time keeps
> resetting to August 8th, 1942 whenever I reset the computer. I'm wondering
> if that affects the kernel.
>
I believe the Logitech Mouseman option in the kernel configuration is
for Logitech mouseman that is connected to a proprietary Logitech card in an
ISA slot. You need to enable PS/2 mouse support. I have a Logitech 3 button
mouse and that's what I use.
