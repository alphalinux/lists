I had exactly the same kind of problem, but with the net card (21140).
I had to replace the card because the old one was broken. With the new
one, the first 3 days were a hell. Or the machine didn't boot at all,
with the same kind of PYXIS error, or the card begun to complain
before/when booting the kerrnel (long list of numbers). After 3 days,
don't ask me why, things settled down. Now, I can reboot 100 times the
machine and no more booting errors.

Nothing, SW or HW has been changed...

It seems that the machine has to become "friend" with a new card !!!

BTW, I have an LX with a Matrox and an EIDE HD (works fine with 2.1.125)
