On Fri, Sep 11, 1998 at 02:44:49PM -0400, Bret Orsburn wrote:
> This means that the .got segment of a shared library (.so) must not
> exceed 64KB?

No. It means that none of the .o files that were fed to ld to create
the .so must have (generate) a .got segment that exceeds 64k.

You will get link-time errors if this happens. It will not be silent.
