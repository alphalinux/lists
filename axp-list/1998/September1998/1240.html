Hi there!

I finished up a lapack-rpm that makes use of K. Goto's (currently
available in libgemm-980927) assembler dgemm/sgemm, as well as M.
Dayde's and Iain Duff's RISC-optimized Level 3 BLAS. The package is
based on the lapack-2.0-10.src.rpm.

Some problems I had compiling dlarfx.f and slarfx.f with optimization
have gone away - by upgrading to binutils-2.9.1.0.13-2 and egcs-1.1b.
Kotau to the egcs/g77 people.

More comments in the spec file at our ftp server:

ftp://ftp.neuro.uni-oldenburg.de/pub/alpha/

You can also get the patches there. Someday soon I'll put up an
alpha.rpm and a complete, if some the people involved agree.

Feedback welcome! Happy hacking,

Jo

P.S.: One oops: I just noticed that Source0 and Source1 aren't there
anymore. Just grab the lapack-2.0-10.src.rpm from the
contrib/manhattan/SRPMS area...
