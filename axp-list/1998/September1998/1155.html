Gcc as far as C++ is extremely broken on RedHat 5.0/Alpha. You will need to use
egcs to compile KDE. I used egcs 1.0.3a and it worked just fine. 1.1b is now
out and I have read of some problems with it but I am not sure if they relate
to the Alpha or not. I've used 1.1b to compile a few programs but have had no
reason to re-compile KDE.
