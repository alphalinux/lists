With regards to your message at 12:28 PM 09-23-98 +0200, Julian Reichwald.
Where you stated:
>HI!
>
>I have an APC164-UX-Board and the following problem:
>
>I downloaded the correct ldmilo.exe and all the other stuff
>needed from gatekeeper's ftp site (also tried the original stuff from
>deskstation). I copied everything to a DOS-Disk and started
>ldmilo.exe from BIOS level.
>
>
>ldmilo came up, loaded osfpal.nh and milo.ful.
>
>But when milo.ful was loaded, the system stuck.
>No milo-prompt, the cursor jumped to the top of the screen and nothing
>happened.
>
PUT THE VIDEO CARD IN THE FIRST pci SLOT. This is the one nearest the RAM
sockets.
This is a known bug in the current software/Linux kernel for UX.

Also, why the 3COM ethernet? The onboard ethernet interface is at least as
fast as the 3C905.
