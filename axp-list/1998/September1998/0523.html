> PS : I did check that the NFS server worked and allowed mounts on the
> /RedHat dir. Also I could ping the Alpha from the NFS server.

A NFS mount attempt may "hang" for many reasons, assuming that ping
works; even if you sit in front of a nfs-root machine and all future
nfs-mount attempts fail ;-}

1) loopback not up.
solution:
>ifconfig lo 127.0.0.1 netmask 255.0.0.0 broadcast 127.255.255.255 up
>route add -host 127.0.0.1 dev lo
2) portmap not running
solution
>portmap

I do nfs-mounts very carefully; on a system that has
a disk mounted on / you can simple do a
mount nfsserver:/dir /
then call bash or something like that and enjoy funny
getwd-errors; no cool way to recover. (2.1.122)
