I have just gotten this working, 2.5" IDE drive in a VX40A.

1) Do the firmware update. This was the key! Before, no recognition,
connecting the drive even hung SRM. After the update, life works as
expected.

2) If you have the 50-pin internal SCSI cable, it will double as a mini-IDE
cable! (It covers the unit-select pins on the drive, but that's ok) I
had to file a milimeter or so off of one end because it's a tight fit on
the motherboard next to the floppy connector.

IDE performance on the UDB has a really bad reputation, but it doesn't
seem bad compared to my external SCSI-1 drive that I was using. I haven't
looked at the CPU load yet.
