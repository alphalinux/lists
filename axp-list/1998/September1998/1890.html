Brock Williams,

On top of what Jay has to offer, you must have a previous version of SRM
(e.g. T5.0-1) in order to load the SRM from DEC's site. This little problem
allows Digital to make sure that you have an SRM license.

Other than that, try Jay's MILO. We use it here and it works fine with the
Intraserver 3140 an 53c875 chip based scsi controller.
