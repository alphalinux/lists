> I wish to use my alphastation 200 as a firewall and have successfully
> installed redhat 5.1. I recall reading in this newsgroup that the
> ipfwadm program shiped with 5.1 should not be used and that instead
> one should compile the source from the 5.0 SRPM.

I believe recompiling the 5.0 sources will produce non-functioning ipfwadm
also.

> I do not have the 5.0 distribution and have been unable to locate the
> 5.0 SRPMs online (I have tried a number of the mirrors). Can someone
