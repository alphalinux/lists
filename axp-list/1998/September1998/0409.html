Hi Guys,
Remember about a month I asked some questions about ISDN and setting
up my linux box with it. Well I finally did it. My ISP is www.wn.net .
$40 a month unlimited CVS 112k. I bought a Bay Networks used RT328
router for $235 and a pci D Link net card with the dec 21140 chipset. I
had the network setup in Linux in seconds while it took me a half-hour
in NT, Don't ask. I'm can finally get online with my Linux box and I
love it. I really want to thank everyone for there help and insights. I
wrote a page on ISDN for that sums up the technology and how to go about
getting it. That 3 weeks of research didn't go down the drain. it's at
http://math.stcc.mass.edu/petrakis/isdn.html. Thanks again.
