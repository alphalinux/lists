I just thought I'd drop a note on the list. I've had problems trying
to get the NCR driver working with my lx (I've updated the milo already).

I went thorugh the kernel (pre2.1.124) today and discovered "make config" isn't
including the CONFIG_NCR53C8XX=y in the .config file. It includes
the CONFIG_NCR53C7xx=y when you select the ncr controller in the scsi
section. I added the CONFIG_NCR53C8XX=y and ran "make config" again and
the option appeared.

I'm not to experienced with the way the config & dependencies work in
the kernel source, but this seems to work for me...
