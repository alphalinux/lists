Oops, sorry, this was a typo!
mtvp accepts a filename directly on the commandline, no redirections needed,
i.e. in your case
mtvp starwars.mpg
But I think this is not your problem. Still it would be nice if you could
test this. You should also check your libraries doing a 'ldd' on mtvp. Maybe
you have older libs installed than mtvp expects.
I have such effects only when playing video _and_ doing hard work on the
machine at the same time, e.g. compiling the kernel with -j2 or more.
