We have one here in the department. Don't waste your money. We have
nothing but problems with them. I believe it is an HP SureStore
somethingorother. Haven't tried it on Linux yet, but under Windoze, it
constantly suffers from buffer underruns. That can be an expensive
mistake after a while. It also tends to balk on the TOC write, plus some
sense key errors. I can't recommend SCSI enough for CD burning. IDE
tends to be error-prone, but not nearly as bad as the parallel port
models.
