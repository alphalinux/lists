>Want a quick newbie rundown?
>
>Get your 3 separate diskette images (by FTP or off the CD) milo.dd,
avanti.img, and ramdisk.img.
>(I'm assuming your machine didn't come with arc)
>
>Put milo.dd in the floppy drive and type "boot dva0"
>This will get you to the Milo prompt.
>
>Replace Milo with avanti.img and type "boot fd0:vmlinux.gz root=/dev/fd0
load_ramdisk=1 prompt_ramdisk=1"
>After the kernel boots, it will ask for a root disk.
>
>Insert your ramdisk.img floppy, and the Redhat install will begin (after
you press enter).
>
>Caveats:
>Make sure you have at least 32M of RAM. I had to buy more to install.
>Partition your drives smaller than 2 G apiece.
>Still figuring out external modems. Get back to you on that one.
>
>Have fun.
