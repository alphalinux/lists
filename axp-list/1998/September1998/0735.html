Errr,
>From your /proc/pci you seems to have a DEC TGA2 based card; i.e. a
Powerstorm!
The Powerstorm 3D30 (2MB) and 4D20 (16MB) are both based on the TGA2
card, so you _MAY_ have the 4D20 and that I'm afraid is unfortunately
not supported by XFree86... (unfortunate as they are darn good cards,
they scream in Digital Unix).

The X-server which you're using is for the TGA1 (i.e. the 21030) based
cards i.e. the ZLXp-E[123] which Tim Rowley most excellently hacked it
to support the ZLXp-E[23] (24bit), previous it only supported the
ZLXp-E1 (8bit).

Your choices are:
1. Write your own X-server (but I'm afraid you'll need a NDA to get the
specs) for the TGA2
2. Change your card (the Millenium I and II are very well supported in
the Alpha as are the cheaper S3 Virge-based cards).

HTH,
Wai-Sun
