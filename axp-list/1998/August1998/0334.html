OK, here we go:
These cards are ALL using Symbios Logic 53c8xx chips.
The fireport40 uses 53c875J
The Intraserver uses 53c875
The Netstorm uses 53c885

For those confused about NCR 53c8xx versus Symbios Logic. Symbios Logic
used to be the SCSI division of NCR until it got sold to Hyundai.
