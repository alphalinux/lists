Folks!

Linux/UNIX ferver aside, there was never any real doubt that Compaq would
push NT over UNIX - there is almost certainly more *money* in it for them.
So let them push NT. Maybe even encourge them to push NT. Because
when/(if/whatever) NT is proven worse than UNIX the Compaq *CUSTOMERS*
will move to UNIX/Linux.

With great respect to all on this list, just how much *real* impact will we
have on what Compaq decide to do? We stand *way* more chance of sucess by
staying calm, working hard, making Linux *THE BEST* and then just calmly
offering it to all those disenchanted NT folk.

Ammunition is not required. Offering NT as a UNIX solution is as dangerous
as giving a 3 year-old a loaded gun..... Surely NT *is* the ammunition!

Just 10c worth.....
