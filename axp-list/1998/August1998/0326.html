LX and SX don't have build in debug monitor like previous version of DEC
boards. EV5 for instance you could put it into that mode and then you
could do anything and everything headless. SRM in the new LX will let
you boot IDE device or SCSI however I still don't think that you could
use that future to completely make it headless. However what you can do
is use a serial monitor plug it into the serial port and then from then
you could start installing, I am not sure how far it will let you. Let
me know if you succeed.
