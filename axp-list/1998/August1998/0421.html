> I get the "delaying eth1 message" but never any indcation of the FA310tx
> being found. I have swapped the card with another one (working in an

I do not recally seeing any note from you telling us HOW you are trying
to force a detection of this card. UDB has already one ethernet port
on a board, so this will be the second one. This is an excerpt from
BootPrompt-HOWTO (on your disk or CD):

The most common use of this parameter is to force probing for a second
ethercard, as the default is to only probe for one. This can be
accomplished with a simple:

______________________________________________________________________
ether=0,0,eth1

Are you doing something like that?

Another option is to add a driver for the second card as a module and
put a line similar to:

alias eth1 <name of your driver module here>

in your /etc/modules.conf file. I did not try with UDB and your
particular card buth in other configurations (Alpha) worked seamlessly.
