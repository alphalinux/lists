Hi Graham !

> Hello. I have just purchased an alpha that will be used as our
> departmental server. We have some ancient WYSE terminals that I can't
> seem to wean people off of. My question is, what sort of hardware will be
> required to support these dumb terminals? Someone mentioned a serial
> card, but could anyone be more specific than this? What sort of serial
> cards are supported with RH5.1/Alpha, if any?

First: Buy nothing.
Check if the WYSE terminals work when using COM1 (ttyS0) or COM2 (ttyS1).
Look into the serial-howto for the cable. If WYSE has an DB25 and your
ALPHA has an DB9 connector, use 2-2 3-3 7-7 for XON/XOFF protocol.

If they work (maybe because they are VT220 compatible :-}) then I recommend
using Cyclades cards or an external Terminal-Server.

Contact support@cyclades.com for future informations. Their cards run
on Linux/Alpha.

Nearly every terminal-server should work, too (equinox, digi, dec).
