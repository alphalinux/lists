Jelsma, Jean-Pierre wrote:
>
> Running mouseconfig means after probing
> I am asked to give in what kind of mouse I have. After selecting =
> 'microsoft'
> (gawwwd) protocol on cua1 X still refuses to respond to my mouse.

Regardless of what type of mouse you have the fact that it does not
respond on /dev/cua1 is not surprising at all. 'cua?' are "dial-out"
ports (with a half-assed locking protocol, which are obsolete and which
totally disappear in the future as there is no good way to make them work
reliably beyond most trivial situations). Serial mouse most likely will
be on /dev/ttyS0, or maybe /dev/ttyS1.

> Standard serial
> mouse, nothing fancy like Logitech, 3 button mouse.

Which also means that 'microsoft' protocol is most likely a wrong
one as well. Typing 'gpm -t help' when "gpm" is NOT running will
present you with other choices.

All of the above has NOTHING to do with Alpha. My advice would
be to consult /usr/doc/HOWTO/mini/3-Button-Mouse on your disk
before posting. If you choose not install documentation this reading
material will be still on your CD.
