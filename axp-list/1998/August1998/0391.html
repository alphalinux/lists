On Fri, 07 Aug 1998 12:48:11 -0300, Kit Cosper wrote:

>Umm, Adaptec joined Linux International several months ago and has
>made some rather aggressive steps to embrace the Open Source (TM)
>community. I have heard through the grapevine that the keeper of the
>Linux Adaptec drivers is nearly in need of a larger home to store all
>the product and documentation that Adaptec has bestowed upon him. I
>also know that there are some distribution producers who have seen
>this largese as well.

While we are on this topic, I have to say that Adaptec has been *very*
cooperative with us. They had me out there a few weeks ago to talk
about what they needed to do to be "Linux friendly". The people I've
been working with are 100% in favor of open source and open specs. They
have sent Doug Ledford (AIC7xxx driver author) all their specs and
requested that he ensure that all code derived from those specs be open
source and funneled into the mainstream Linux kernel.

They have also over-flowed my test lab with every card they have ever
made. I never thought I would be flooded by Adaptec. :) They are
also working with us to open up the source code on some tools they use
internally for driver testing. I've been very pleased with the effort
I've seen from Adaptec.
