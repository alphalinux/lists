Well, it depends on what the little boot interpreter in the Alpha BIOS
can do insofar as the SCSI BIOS on the card goes. I know that the DEC
DE500 works fine. Your local computer dealer can order it from their
favorite distributor for about the same price as a 3c590 or similar
name-brand card. It's a quality card, and the one that Mad Dog
etc. use, so you know the driver works :-).

I'm about to check out a current Netgear "Tulip" card to see how it
works. If it works great, but I'm not betting yet...
