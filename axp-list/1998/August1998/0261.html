Got it to compile just fine, but it dies on a floating exception. Here's a
log of what I did to build it:

In the beginning, there was the 7/28/1998 tarball.

setenv MOZILLA_CLIENT 1
setenv MOZ_MEDIUM 1
setenv NO_SECURITY 1
setenv NO_MDUPDATE 1
setenv BUILD_OPT 1
make >& make.log
[edit dbm/include/hash.h to add typedefs for uint16, uint32, int32]
make >& make2.log
[edit cmd/xfe/xfe.h line 679, change the first two 'jint's to 'long
int's]
make >& make3.log
[edit cmd/xfe/src/RDFImage.cpp line 36, same as xfe.h; and
line 211 change both 'jint's to 'long int's]
make > & make4.log
cd dist/Linux2.1.111_alpha_OPT.OBJ/bin/
mozilla-export &
Floating exception!
setenv LD_LIBRARY_PATH /lib:/usr/lib:/usr/local/lib:/usr/X11R6/lib:.

moz-export &
Floating exception!

On 8/5/1998 at about 4PM:

cvs -z3 update -dP
make >& make5.log
[Errors due to missing classes nsHashKey and nsHashTable]

At this point I gave up. Maybe I'll hack it some more tomorrow. I'd be glad
to provide you with diffs for any of the files in question.
