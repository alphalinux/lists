Well I simply copied the linboot.exe and avanti from the /milo directory
to a dos floppy, this ended up working from ARC console. Only I had no
ARC console, just SRM. So I got the AS200 6.8 flash update from digitals
site, used the msdos mkboot file to put the exe on the floppy and did a

boot dva0

and the flash update booted and I flashed in a new srm 6.8, and a arc
console 4.5x. I now have ARC console and milo works great from there. I
have concerns whether this was a good idea as my machine looks like a NT
machine boot manager startup now (:P), but I might even put NT on it some
day, and I will never do digital unix on it I am sure.

my isa settings are ok as it suggested in the update info. As soon as I
