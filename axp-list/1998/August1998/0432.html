dca2 wrote:
>
> I am reading this to mean I cannot access NTFS as I do DOS FAT16 from
> Linux on Intel?

Yes, if you have the driver installed in your kernel. You just mount-n-go.
However, the NTFS driver is Read-ONLY. It reads compressed and uncompressed
files ok, but write support is extremely experimental. If you want to try
writing, do it on a partition that you are willing to chance trashing.

As for your other question, I have used a Symbios/NCR810 and a Adaptec 3940UW
in my PC64. The Symbios cards are cheap, simple and work well.
