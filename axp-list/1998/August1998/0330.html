> Hello. I have just purchased an alpha that will be used as our
> departmental server. We have some ancient WYSE terminals that I can't
> seem to wean people off of. My question is, what sort of hardware will be
> required to support these dumb terminals? Someone mentioned a serial
> card, but could anyone be more specific than this? What sort of serial
> cards are supported with RH5.1/Alpha, if any?
> Thank you,
> Graham Knopp

If it's just one or two terminals, I'd say hook them up to the normal
serial ports.

If it's several I'd recommend a used Livingston Portmaster pm2 series
terminal server. (www.ams.com is a good place to start) Probably $400+
starting with 10 ports. Plug this into your network and terminal users
will telnet/rlogin into your linux box over your LAN. These are well
supported by ISPs, Livingston/Lucent, and many linux users.

-Jason
