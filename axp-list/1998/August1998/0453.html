One really simple fix, provided that your compiler is not at fault, is to just grab the ssh source code (NOT the src rpm). I really don't understand why people get so hooked on those things. The URL is ftp://ftp.hut.cs.fi/pub/ssh/ssh-1.2.26.tar.gz , it compiled like a champ on my UDB running patched/updated RH5.0
