> virtual terminals when in X. This results in a complete system hang.
> [Running SVGA server with Matrox Millenium II card and 24 bit

I have this behaviour on an *ix86* box and Millenium. It doesn't depend on
the kernel, as far as I can tell (2.0.xx, 2.1.xx, 2.1.10y, y<=106). It does
