> What do I need to do to get my compiler to work using the Redhat 5.1 CD?

>
> I installed everything that starts with egcs (rpm -ivh egcs*) and I made
> sure:
> /lib
> /usr/lib
> /usr/local/lib
> /usr/X11/lib
>

>
> I also installed all of the rpms that looked important (libstc , libgr ,
> etc...)
>
> and when I try to run configure to compile the latest mutt and apache, it
