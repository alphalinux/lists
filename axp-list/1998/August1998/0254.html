On Wed, 5 Aug 1998, wade wrote:

> I would use SMR, but I cant figure out how to
> 1. tell SRM to boot automatically when I am not there (whether its to
> start milo, or aboot)

Set 'auto_action' console variable to 'boot' and 'boot_osflags' one to a
value that corresponds to an appropriate entry of '/etc/aboot.conf'. It's
explained deeply in "SRM HOWTO". Looking through the manual of your
machine may prove to be worthwhile, too.

> 2. how aboot works, as milo was so easy and it looked like it would take
> more time...I may dig into it later

Study the "SRM HOWTO" -- it's available somewhere at
'http://www.azstarnet.com/~axplinux/' and also in RH 5.1.
