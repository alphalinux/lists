On Sun, 2 Aug 1998, Larry Creech wrote:

> I've noticed very high CPU utilization (~50%)using the precompiled 2.0.34
> from redhat on the AS-200 4/233. Is this a proc problem? If I kill klogd
> and not syslog, things look normal.

Usually this means something very Bad[tm] is happening. This is generating
so much logging info, that the log daemon hogs the cpu.
The best thing to do is to shoot the daemon and start checking the huge
logfile in /var/log to see the _real_ problem that caused it. When it is
fixed, restart the log daemon.
