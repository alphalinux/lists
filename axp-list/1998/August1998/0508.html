> Does anybody know if there are some "really" good fortran

You are probably looking for a better libm, first of all. There were some
discussions on a Cray math library for Alpha CPUs here some time ago. I am
wondering what's going on there ? Will it be in glibc-2.1 ?

If you do have DU machines and a DU license for your Linux box, you could
compile your program on the DU machine and run it on Linux.

For development you might still use egcs-g77, it's warnings are much better
that DUs f77 does.
