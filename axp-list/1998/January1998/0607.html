>
> we just installed RedHat5.0 on an AlphaStation 600 5/266 and have problems
> compiling the kernel. GCC gets an `internal error - fatal signal x' where
> x is either 6 or 11 when it tries to compile one of the SCSI modules

Sigh; yes, it's the options you chose for the BusLogic driver, which you
probably don't want anyway.

Say "YES" to the "Omit FlashPoint support" question and it will allow the
rest of the BusLogic driver to be compiled. Or just say "NO" to the BusLogic
SCSI support question.
