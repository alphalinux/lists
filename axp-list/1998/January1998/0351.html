Stefan Traby writes:
> I don't know it, but your posting makes me think
> that masking is not possible on AXP, so this info-file is simply
> outdated because it depends on FPUs where exceptions may be masked.
> Is this assumption correct ?

No, it is not. Kernel provides ieee_set_fp_control() (see <asm/fph.h>)
which does what it implies **provided** -mieee flag was used
during a compilation.

This facility may have bugs and it is not really documented, unless
a comment in the mentioned header may be taken for a documentation, but
it is there and it works - to an extent. Sources imply that its
prototype should be 'unsigned long ieee_set_fp_control(unsigned long);'
but do not quote me on that. :-)
