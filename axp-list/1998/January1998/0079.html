There is a cost effective AND legal way to obtain a SRM license. Call
Digital at 800-344-4825 and order an EBSDK kit (Evaluation Board Software
Design Kit) part number QR-21B02-03, rev 3.1. This contains the SRM for
every motherboard Digital Semiconductor ever made, cost $75.00. Includes the
LX and SX.

FYI:The SX is due to be officially released this month with Digital UNIX.
