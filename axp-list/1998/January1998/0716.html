Where you stated:
>With Intel beginning to push Merced onto a pedestal, I have a couple of

>current Alphas? Is there any information on new DEC chip technologies or
>ongoing development of the Alpha architecture? Is DEC going the way of

3 comments:
1) Merced ships at the earliest in 1999.
2) Intel prerelease indicates SPECmarks about the same as current
AlphaPC164LX at 600MHz, and up to about 50% faster for first year of
release ( this takes us into 2000)
3) Digital has said that 21264 is shipping in June, 1998. First takes on
this is 2 to 3 times the speed of the aforementioned LX.
