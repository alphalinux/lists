the Digital Unix shared libraries. It doesn't work for everything (the
Sybase 11.02 server doesn't work :( and script interpriters don't get
called correctly with #!/name/of/program), but it will work for netscape
and others. There's a HOWTO, but unfortunatly, I don't have the URL.
