Hi.

> Just browsed around there, and there is a public_c directory, but it
> only has source for layer 3 decoding, not encoding. :/

That's right. Two different ftp-sites were mentioned here,
and the first one (ftp.fhg.de/pub/layer3/) had only the
decoder-source. You'll find the encoder source at

ftp.tnt.uni-hannover.de

in the directory:

/pub/MPEG/audio/mpeg2/software/technical_report/

The encoder compiled alright on RH 5.0, except for some
apparently harmless warnings about gets, but it is SLOW
(164LX/533 MHz encodes about as fast as my old P200/Linux
using l3enc) and its codec is most likely anything but
state-of-the-art.

But it's only demo software (ie. how NOT to do it ;-) and
at least it's something to build on.
