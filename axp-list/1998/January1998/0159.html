Hi Keith !

> I am trying to use XDM to log onto my alpha... how owuld I configure it to
> allow connections... I keep getting denied errors from my Xserver...

hmm. A good starting point is "man xauth" ....

... and a good ending point may be:

'xauth extract - $DISPLAY | rsh bill.command.com xauth merge -'

This should be started from the X-Server system.
Replace bill.command.com with the interface name (hostname is relative on
multihomed hosts) of your X-client.

Hope it helps.
