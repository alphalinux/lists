With regards to your message at 05:37 PM 01-16-98 -0500, Dan Weeks. Where
you stated:
>
>Does anyone know of vendors that sell the 233MHz 21066a upgrade
>processor to the UDB? Does anyone have a spare that they are not
>using?
>
Bear in mind that most (all?) 166MHz UDB's used soldered CPU's.
In most cases they can NOT be upgraded with a 233 chip.
Also, unless you are planning to expand the cache ( which is also alomost
impossible on a UDB as they used a proprietary module) you won't gain as
much as the clockspeed would indicate.
