Lionel Provost writes:
> I have some applications (xinit, xf86config... ) which I
> can not execute (the message "command not found" is dis-
> played ). The path seems ok and when I try a "whereis" com-
> mand the good path is displayed. File Permissions seem ok.
>
> Have you an idea of this pb ?
>

This means it is looking for a loader in /lib (ld.so.1 or
ld-linux.so.2) that is not present. You will need to upgrade or
recompile the binary to link against the one installed on your system.
