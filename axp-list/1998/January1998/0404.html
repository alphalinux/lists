Several months ago I had two UDBs fail w/in a couple of weeks of each
other exactly this way. After weeks of working with this I traced it
down to bad p/s to m/b connectors. The greed leads carry 3.3 volts to
the chip, and the plastic on the connector had discolored from the
resistive connection. Rather than pay DEC ~$130 each for replacement
p/s, I nipped off the bad connectors and butt spliced others on. I
had to settle for cutting up p/s extension cords as I could not find
the connectors and pins as piece parts. They haven't failed once
since then.

-- Michael
