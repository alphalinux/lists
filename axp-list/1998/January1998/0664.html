Here's some hot-off-the-press news: According to

http://www2.computerworld.com/home/print9497.nsf/CWFlash/SL3lapha1799E

DEC is going to introduce a line of Alphas that will run ONLY Windows NT.
They, according to the article, are going to remove the firmware that
would allow it to boot other OSes (including DEC UNIX and OpenVMS ... and
presumably Linux). According to the report, it is doing this "at a time
when it is struggling to grow Alpha product revenue."

I think perhaps the Alpha may be dead afterall ...

So much for the price cuts ...
