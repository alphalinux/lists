Robert Williams wrote:
>
> Following the directions at
> http://www.redhat.com/support/docs/rhl/RHL-5.0-Installation-Guide-HTML/manual/doc107.html
>
> I enter "boot floppy" at the MILO> prompt,
> after inserting my boot floppy,
> and I get the following, with no activity on the floppy drive:
>
> Ramdisk driver initialized : 16 ramdisks of 4096K size
> hda: Maxtor....
> ide0: at ...
> Floppy drive(s): fd0 is 2.88M
> FDC 0 is post-1991 82077
>
> That's all. No floppy light, no boot...
> Any clues?

Well I just installed 5.0 last night. At the MILO> prompt I typed:

boot fd0:vmlinux.gz root=/dev/fd0 load_ramdisk=1 prompt_ramdisk=1

The installation went just fine from there.

I was surprised (and pleased) at the NeXT-style GUI.
