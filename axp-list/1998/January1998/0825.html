On Tue, 27 Jan 1998, W. Kyle Gilbertson wrote:

> Anyone know how to mirror/stripe drives in Linux?

Apologies beforehand, but all that info is online. Look in the
Documentation/ directory of your kernel sources. The driver is 'md'.

It supports RAID 0,1,2 and 1+2 (I think). I was going to use it on a
SPARC I have lying around, but I set it up with 50MB of swap on a 200MB
internal disk. After about 20 minutes, the cron log filled the remaining
space and I haven't played with it since. I'm waiting for RH 5 for SPARC
to be released before I go any further.
