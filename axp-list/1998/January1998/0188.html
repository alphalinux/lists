Granted DU is not as open as Linux, but they do have optimized
compilers for the Alpha. When you take a static binary compiled on DU
and run it on Linux/Alpha you get a 10-30% speed improvement on the
exact same hardware. That alone makes sense to me for commercial
applications of Linux/Alpha. And as we all know if Linux makes it in
commercial environments then it is better for all of us.
