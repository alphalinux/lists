Thomas,

Probably not the same problem as you have, but I was compiling RH5 on
my UDB. I had configured the Microsoft Sound System compatible device on the
mother
board for address 530, dma 3 and irq 9. Whwn compiling it go to the scsi low
level
device drivers ie buslogic and froze on flashpoint. I did a make menuconfig
or xconfig
and there is a selection under scsi low level device drivers to forfeit
using flashpoint
support. read this carefully. It actually is set no, so set it to yes. I am
also using the
integrated ncr810? scsi controller. Your results may vary dpendent upon your
scsi
controller. After this the make boot worked fine. It worked for me. Good
luck.

Scott Matthews
