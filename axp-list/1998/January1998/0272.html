You are on an automated mailing list. Somehow you got on it, and hopefully
you can help
yourself get off it. I can only act as a guide, as you will have to send the
mail to remove
yourself.

Please send mail to this account:

axp-list-request@redhat.com

In the "subject" line of your mail that you send, put the word "unsubscribe".
Put nothing
in the body of the mail.

This *should* remove you. Allow a little time for your mail to get through
the system,
turn off the mechanism, and allow the mechanism to flush a few more mail
messages out of
its system.

If this does not remove you, that probably means that someone has subscribed a
mail LIST that
you belong to, rather than just your name. In this case it will take a little
more time
and effort to get removed.

Please keep track of the time that you first received mail from our mailing
list (date and
time). This will allow the list maintainer to go back through their records
and see who
was added when.

Please be assured that no one on this mailing list wants you on it if you do
not want to be
on it. We get no pleasure (in fact, we get quite a bit of pain) when people
get on the list
who do not want to be on it. It was in all probability a non-list member who
subscribed you
or your group. We will do our best to remove you as soon as possible.

I am responding to you simply because I happen to be here at my system as the
first few
of your mail messages came in. I will help to get you removed.

I would suggest that you do not keep sending messages to the mailing list
saying "DON'T WRITE
ME ANY MORE". Past experience has shown that this is counter-productive, and
in the end
causes even more pain.

If you would send me your telephone number, I will be happy to call you and
help you get
removed from the list.

Sincerely,
