Terry L. Ridder wrote:

> Forget the car, buy an Alpha.
> With the car you have all the extra expenses, insurance, gas, oil, repairs,
> parking fees, tollway fee, etc.
> The Alpha you just have the expense of the electricity it uses.
>
> If you are concerned about dates do not be.
> My oldest daughter (college senior) has had several guys dating her just
> so they could get a look at my Alpha. ;-)
>
> So find a girl with a car, who is interested in computers, buy the Alpha,
> and you will be okay.

And to think that back when I was in High School (late middle ages) I
had a young lady ask me for a date. Turned out that she wanted to see
the DEC PDP-8/L that we had at our school.

For those of you too young to know, this machine had 4K of "core"
memory, one accumulator register, a 1.6 usec. memory cycle time, paper
tape for "mass" storage and an ASR-33 teletype for a "terminal". There
was not one single Integrated Circuit in the machine.

I'm still not really sure if the machine or the girl was more fun,
certainly one was more predictable :-)

Guess nothing ever changes.

Please pardon the somewhat off-topic commentary.
