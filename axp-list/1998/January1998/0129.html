In addition to your second mail concerning '-mieee':

The 'l' modifier is not apllicable to the 'f' format specification
in printf, according to the printf man page 'f' is already suitable
for double.
Here is what gcc says with '-Wall':

warning: use of `l' length character with `f' type character
