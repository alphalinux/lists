There are two available...X-Win32 I found an older copy at TU-Cows and the
one that Micro Images gives out (listed on this list previously and at
www.microimages.com).

The Micro Images one is truely free and has almost no features. No root
menus, and you have to kick start it by telnetting into the remote box and
setting your DISPLAY environment var. It is also all in one application
window type.

The X-Win32 has more features and is an "un-rooted" desktop on 95 or NT.
But the older version I have has some bugs and the latest "beta" has a two
hour session time limit.
