Tom Browder wrote:
>
> I just installed RH 5.0 and can't get my SCSI
> DAT to work. Under RH 4.2 the /dev/tape was linked
> to /dev/st0 and I could use tar, e.g.,
>
> tar -cvf /dev/tape <files>
>
> Under RH 4.2 I could NOT use tar directly
> with /dev/st0 (??).
>
> Now tar doesn't work at all with either
> device name, although the DAT is recognized
> by the kernel upon bootup. I must admit
> I didn't try tar with the DAT before I used
> the supposedly nifty BRU--well that didn't
> recognize anything I had installed!!

So what errors - if any do you get? Check your logs
for SCSI errors.
Is the kernel compiled with (or have you loaded)
the SCSI Tape module?
Does mt -f /dev/st0 give you sensible messages.
What does ls -l /dev/st0 or /dev/tape show?

Dave (who has not yet tried his DAT on RH5.0)
