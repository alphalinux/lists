I'm presuming that you're looking at the 164SX from Aspen. I'm running
the 164SX from Enorex (which is presumably fairly similar) with the
Millenium II without problems. The only downside is that the 164SX
is new enough that there is no standard support in any of the kernels
(except the kernel from Red Hat), which limits ones ability to build
experimental kernels. Nonetheless, it's fast, fun, and cheap.
