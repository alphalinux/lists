far as I know. You aren't overloading the Power Supply are you? It seems
to have some kind of overtemp/circit breaker. I had a 2 gig Baracuda
internal on my UDB for about a week and it just one day shut down. I
turned it off and back on. It worked fine for the next 5 min and then did
the same thing. I moved the drive out and it worked fine ever since.
