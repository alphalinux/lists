Hello,

Does anybody know what happened to the 'adjtimex' system call at about
2.1.79? The value of 'tick' used to be equal to 977 on Alpha systems --
now it changes wildly. It prevents `xntpd' from starting up and running.
All other fields of 'struct timex' seem to be correct (as reported by
`adjtimex -print'). The 'adjtimex' system call works as usual on Intel
systems (where 'tick' is 10000 upon startup). Other than this, 2.1.82
seems to be rock-solid.

Cheers.
