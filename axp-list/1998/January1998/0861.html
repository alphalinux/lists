On Thu, 29 Jan 1998, Tommy Cheung wrote:

> Well, pardon me, but I visited ftp.kernel.org, the 2.1.82 is for Alpha,
> but the 2.0.33 only FOR INTEL (Eeeks). Any idea where I can grab 2.0.33
> for multi architecture?

ftp.kernel.org will do fine. Grab that i386 stuff and apply the
alpha-patches for 2.0.32 (sic!) and watch the ARCH=i386 become ARCH=alpha!

Applying the patch can be somewhat tricky, though: I need to have dirs
(symlinks) called /usr/src/new/ and /usr/src/old/ and /usr/src/linux/
all pointing at /usr/src/linux-2.0.33/ for it to apply correctly. This
seems weird, but the kernel's a rock. Even my tulip worked with the stock
driver.

alpha-patches are on ftp.digital.com (a.k.a. gatekeeper.digital.com) --
dir has been posted a zillion times here.
