> Only FTP software from trusted sites (eg, a vendor).

btw, it is NOT sufficient to "trust" an ftp site. an ftp site may be
run by your mother, but that doesn't mean the stuff you're downloading
is completely without risk. the only way to assure validity of data on
an open network (e.g. the internet) is to have the sender start off with
something they know is valid, encrypt it or sign it with some encryption
alogrithm and then either decrypt it or check the signature when it gets
to you.
