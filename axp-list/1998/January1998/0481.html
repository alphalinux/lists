They seem to have been broken in the alpha patch. If you look at
"alpha-patches-2.0.32-0.1", it changes {linux,asm}/resource.h around in
a manner that seems incompatible with glibc-{,devel-}2.0.5c-13. My
2.0.33+patch based machine behaves the same way.

Dirk> moment I am a little bit lost... My configuration is standard redhat 5.0

What I have done with other compiles of smaller packages (xntp, ssh...)
is to change the /usr/src/linux symlink to point back to the RedHat
installed kernel headers (you did keep those around "just in case",
right?) and do the compile. This may cause some problems with egcs but
its at least worth trying. Hopefully, a corrected patch will come out
soon.
