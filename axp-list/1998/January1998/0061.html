>>> Eric Goebelbecker said:
> I've just finished setting up a PWS 433a with RedHat 5.0.
>
> A. This workstation has an Adaptec 2940 Wide SCSI adaptor. Disk
> access seems slow.

Sigh; this is probably because its (essentially) working in polled mode, rather
than using interrupts. Let me explain.

There are two versions of MIATA motherboards: they differ in the PCI "slot"
where the onboard PCI-to-PCI bridge lives.

In attempting to add fixes that would allow use of combo boards (with
PCI-PCI bridges of their own) either in front or behind the builtin bridge,
I forgot about the two motherboard versions and broke it for one of them,
unfortunately yours. These are the patches that RH used to build the kernel.

Solution: grab the MIATA kernel from RH 4.2, it should work fine on boxes
that don't have one of those combo boards installed.

Sorry, gang, I'll have new patch sets available in the next few days...
