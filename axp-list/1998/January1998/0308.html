If you try to use a helper app with netscape 3.0.1 for DU the helper app
seems to never run. I did some tracking with strace and found that
netscape is doing an indirect system call through system call 0. This
call is not handled from what I see. What I think is happening is that
some form of exec call is not being handled properly.

any thoughts or suggestions?
