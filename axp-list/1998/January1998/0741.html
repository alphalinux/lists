Sure, here ya go:

http://www.partner.digital.com/www-swdev/pages/Home/TECH/documents/Digital_U
NIX/V4.0/AA-PS31D-TET1_html/TOC.html

It's the Digital Unix 4.0 Assembly Language Programmer's Guide. See
section 3.8 Special-Purpose Instructions. Admittedly, it's not too
detailed but it's good for a quick reference.

The Digital Semiconductor Library has the 3rd Edition of the Alpha
Architecture Manual online in PDF and Postscript formats. It's at
http://ftp.digital.com/pub/Digital/info/semiconductor/literature/dsc-library
.html#alpha.
