> bios32.sio_fixup: weird, device 8086:0484 coming in on slot 7 has
> no irq line!!
> ^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^

This can be ignored, it's just that the PCI-ISA bridge doesn't identify
itself *as* a bridge, so the code expects that it will want an interrupt
to be allocated for it, but then it doesn't indicate *whic* interrupt it
wants (because it doesn't want one :-).

> Sound initialization started
> Sound initialization complete
> In the alpha I have Soundblaster awe32, matrox millenium, Hauppauge
> WinTV/pci.
> The soundcard works fine in WinNT.

Is the SB AWE32 a Plug-n-Play? If so, you need to modularize that driver
and get ahold of the isapnptools package to get it working. Search the
axp-list archives at redhat.com for "isapnptools" to get the details.
