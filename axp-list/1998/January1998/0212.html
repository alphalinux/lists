There's a MILO for the XL-233 and XL-266 (aka XL) located on gatekeeper:

/pub/DEC/Linux-Alpha/Kernels/Test-Milos-2.0.30+/xl

There's a kernel there, too:

/pub/DEC/Linux-Alpha/Kernels/xl-2030-fixed.gz

Of course, you should also be able to use the XL MILO and kernel "xl.img"
files from RH 4.2, instead.

The AVANTI kernels and MILOs will work on an XL *only* if the XL really has
an AVANTI motherboard. There *is* a "true" XL motherboard which WILL NOT run
the AVANTI kernel or MILO; they will fail to have a usable floppy, which is
pretty important during installation.
