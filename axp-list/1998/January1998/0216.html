On Fri, 9 Jan 1998, Matt Wagner wrote:

> has anyone successfully compiled apache on a glibc2 system? i thought i
> remembered someone posting here a week or two ago that something needs to
> be removed from an apache src file first... but i can't seem to find this
> posting in the mail archives.
>
> by the way, is it just me, or does the search engine for the redhat mail
> archives BLOW GOATS! :) i tried on time to search for a keyword that was
> in the subject and message of an old posting of mine, and it couldn't find
> it!!
>
> seems like a good source of info like that *should* have a better search
> system set-up.

You could comment HAVE_SYS_RESOURCE_H out of the LINUX section of conf.h,
or you could grab a modified resourcebits.h, such as the one at
ftp://ftp.linuxdot.com/pub/patches/glibc/glibc-2.0.5c-resource.h. I chose
option two, which allowed me to compile Apache, and a number of other
programs without error.

Good luck!
