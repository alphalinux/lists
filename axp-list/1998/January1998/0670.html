The DEC naming scheme makes about as much sense to me as the naming scheme
for the PPC chips - none at all. I'm not sure that I ever recall you

>
> Uh, when have we *not* been struggling? ;-}

True. But the question, I think, remains valid. :)

> > So much for the price cuts ...
