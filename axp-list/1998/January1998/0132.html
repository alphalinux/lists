If you find a good deal on a udb you won't be dissapointed either... About
a year ago I found myself a UDB (Technicaly a Multia but WinNT 3.51 was not
for me). It is only a 166 but it did come with 24meg ram and 340meg scsi
hd... All for $225. While I would love a $2000+ alpha for the speed I
think I got a lot more than I payed for. This blows away any compareable
intel box at $225 :)
I have since added a Seagate Baracuda (in an external box as the power
supply didn't like the draw of the baracuda) now it really flies.

BTW: I am looking for the floppy mounting parts for a udb... I have a
floppy in mine but it doesn't have all the mounting hardware... Also, does
anyone have the Scsi riser card with the pci slot on it. Mine used to have
an internal scsi 3.5in and now it doesn't and I would like to put a pci
card in it.
