On Tue, 20 Jan 1998 Jay.Estabrook@digital.com wrote:

> Do you think they'll be so cheap that everybody will want to buy them and
> stop buying any of our other boxes? Do you believe they'll be in the $2-4K
> range? Do you believe they will offer hardware/performance/whatever that
> none of our other boxes do or will?

That depends. The non-DEC 533Mhz 21164PC machines are getting close to
that "What the heck, I really want an AXP Linux machine at home" price
range, and they can be had for >$2k. I'm just looking for about $300-600
less in that category, and I'll certainly be bringing one home. If DEC
had an offering in the same price range that ran Linux and NT, I think
there's a chance that it would pick up fairly well. If DEC could
convince MS to make NT 5.0 64-bit clean, I think that they could create a
market. My personal criteria is that Linux *has* to be able to run there
though (like most folks on this list), though if it doesn't when the
machines come out of the gate, I'm fairly sure that it will eventually.
