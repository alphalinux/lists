Yes, there is a rumor that older versions of pgsql do indeed
compile and "run" on Linux/Alpha. But that has never been confirmed or
very well tested. The current version, 6.2.2 (I think) does compile, and
sort of runs (fails part way through initdb), but it not really useable
at the moment. As for previous versions, they might run and they might
not, so use them at your own risk. Hopefully there will be a current and
working version of pgsql for Linux/Alpha soon.
