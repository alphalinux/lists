I contacted xanim's author about cinepak codec, which is available for
i386. In short, cinepak codec is binary only released and author will make
Linux/Alpha binary in case someone provides him with cross-compiler for
Linux/Alpha which will run od Sparc SunOS 4.1.3 machine.

I hope there is someone with enough skill, will and bandwidth who can
contribute such a cross-compiler to him so we all can get fully
operational software video player (free for non-commercial use), with same
functionality as is one in Linux/x86.

His address is podlipec@baynetworks.com.

Also, I contacted MpegTV team (www.mpegtv.com). They also have nothing
against porting to Linux/Alpha, except they do not have platform. I do not
have enough bandwidth to provide access to my alphas for them, but if
someone thinks he can, we can contact them again.

Thanks, dd
