With regards to your message at 01:38 PM 01-19-98 +0200, Hirsim{ki Mika.
Where you stated:
>Hi.
>
>Does anyone know if any mpeg level 3 (audio layer)
>encoders have been ported to Linux/Alpha? Frauenhofer's
>l3enc seems to be available only as an intel-binary.
>
In an earlier posting I mentioned a player for MP3's.
Of course you also asked for an encoder.
Currently I think that L3ENC is your likely best bet.
Some links:
PCs running Linux: l3v272.linux.tar.gz encoder, decoder and documentation
at:
ftp://ftp.iis.fhg.de/pub/layer3/l3v272.linux.tar.gz

For testing purpose there is an MP3 encoded bitstream at:
ftp://ftp.fhg.de/pub/layer3/mp3-bitstreams.tar.gz

Many other sites have MP3's, of course, but I hesitate to post them as they
seem to all contain copyrighted music material, perhaps posted illegally.
For some more general info, also look at:
http://www.iis.fhg.de/departs/amm/layer3/index.html

Good luck in your quest!
