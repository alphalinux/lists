I don't believe that an 'off-the-shelf' gcc.2.7.2.3 will correctly
be configured for RH 5.0 (that old a version of gcc needs patches
to be able to recognize the target).

RH 5.0 comes with a working version of gcc 2.7.2.3 installed - why
do you need to rebuild it? If you want to upgrade your compiler,
I recommend either gcc 2.8.1 or the latest egcs (1.0.2 will be available
shortly).
