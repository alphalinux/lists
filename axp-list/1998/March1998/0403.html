> MFlops - CPU MHz -- OS --- Compilers ----- BLAS

> 657. --- 625 --- Dec Unix -- Dec -------- Kazushige Goto's fast dgemm

> 575. --- 625 --- Dec Unix -- Dec -------- Dec DXML dgemm

> 23. --- 433 --- Dec Unix -- Dec -------- Public Domain dgemm (netlib)
> 24. --- 433 --- Linux --- egcs-980221 -- Public Domain dgemm (netlib)

Are you shure about the last three values ? The netlib dgemm
performed with 27 Mflops when compiled with g77 on a 500 Mhz -Linux
box we use here. This compares fine with your 24 Mflops having in
mind the frequency difference. But when compiled under DU I get 115
Mflops for the same routine. Matrix sizes were different however (small
matrices 32x32 up to 128x128).

Where can one obtain the assembler version of K. Goto ?
