#which grep

#ls -l /bin/grep
-rwxr-xr-x 1 root root 100280 Oct 20 08:29 /bin/grep

And this grep is from a standard 5.0 . I suggest you to type:

file /usr/bin/grep

It may answer why it's not possible to execute this file (It may be a binary
for other platform). Also look in /bin .
