Hi there,

We have an Alpha Box from Hard Data here in Canada. It works like a
charm, running a modified RedHat 4.2.

We still have two problems tho. One we managed to work around, and that
is the failure of ypbind. (I just got an email that says there may be a
fix, which I haven't tested yet).

The other one is 'user tracking'. When a user logs out, the system still
reports him as logged in. for example, a 'top' shows 11 users logged in.
A 'who' shows:

gdi ttyp1 Feb 27 09:54 (devel1)
gdi ttyp1 Feb 27 10:00 (devel1)
gdi ttyp1 Feb 27 10:02 (k2)
gbr ttyp1 Feb 27 10:33 (everest)
gbr ttyp3 Feb 27 10:35 (everest)
gbr ttyp1 Feb 27 12:33 (everest)
gbr ttyp3 Feb 27 12:46 (everest)
gdi ttyp3 Feb 27 15:55 (k2)
gbr ttyp1 Mar 2 08:40 (everest)
gbr ttyp2 Mar 2 08:40 (everest)
gbr ttyp1 Mar 2 09:31 (everest)

while a 'w' shows

USER TTY FROM LOGIN@ IDLE JCPU PCPU WHAT
gbr ttyp2 everest 8:40am 51:56 23.87s 23.73s top
gbr ttyp1 everest 9:31am 0.00s 0.11s 0.03s w

The 'w' output is correct.

Any solutions?

Gerald
