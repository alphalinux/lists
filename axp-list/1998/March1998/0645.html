Try:

ftp://ftp.digital.com/pub/Digital/Alpha/firmware/index.html

in your favorite browser. On the left side of the page, there is a list
of Alpha systems. At the very bottom of the list, under the heading
"Other", in the last position, you will find "Multia/Universal Desktop
Box" Click on this link. If you read the following pages and follow the
links, you will find the latest firmware. The page says that the
firmware has not been updated for a while. The "readme.txt" file gives
very explicit instructions for downloading the firmware update and
installing it.

Good Luck
