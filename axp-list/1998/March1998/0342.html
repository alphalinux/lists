I posted a message in a discussion thread a while ago about a
source for cheap DEC XL300 300mhz Alpha workstations. I had such an
overwhelming response that I missed some people who responded. I am
therefore mentioning again that I have a source that I am willing to pass
along for those parties that are interested.

The source is a DEC VAR that is liquidating XL300 workstations for
DEC. DEC themselves are liquidating these models because they are
'obsolete' in their minds (given their availability of 433, 500 and 600mhz
workstations). The XL300 makes a superb Linux/NT workstation
with integer performance in the realm of the PPRO 200 and floating
point performance about double that of a PPro 200.

The nicest thing about these units is the *FULL* 3 year DEC
warranty available from your local DEC warranty center.

I bought two of these units and have them running the DES64
cracking contest right now and they smoke. I would highly recommend these
units to anyone looking for awesome 'bang for their buck performance'.

I do not intend for this message to be construed as spam, and I am
sorry if anyone takes it that way. Just trying to inform fellow AXP users
of an awesome deal.

Send me email if you are interested....
