Ronny Ranerup wrote:
>
> Since some of you probably have tried this, and some resellers
> are listening... what would it cost (and would it be possible)
> to buy a DEC UNIX licence for a 164LX. I know you can get DUNIX
> if you buy a new system (e.g from DCG), but I would like
> to buy just the licence. My local PC-shop has tried to buy
> from the nearest DEC reseller, but of course(?) they don't want
> to sell anything except hilariously priced complete systems
> which in my book means that they don't want to sell at all...

We just upgraded a Microway Screamer/500 to DEC UNIX. This required a new
drive (SCSI required), a new graphics card (Required), a SCSI CDROM and a
DEC UNIX license. We did not have to purchase the DEC UNIX media (>$1000)
Our cost was ~ $2400.
