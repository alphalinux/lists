Also make sure that /var/spool/lpd/<printername> directory exists under lp
uid. Otherwise printjobs won't spool up...you probably wanna watch the logs
on another console too.

>#
>crayon|dj550c|color|HP DekJet 550C:\
> :lp=/dev/lp1:sd=/var/spool/lpd/crayon:sh:mx#0:\
> :if=/usr/local/bin/dj550c-filter
>#
>#
