>>> Robert Williams said:
>
> What family is the 1000 in?

Uh, 1000 is MIKASA, 1000A is NORITAKE. Both can be either EV4-based
(models 4/xxx) or EV5-based (models 5/xxx).

I believe that RH 5.0 or 4.2 have a mikasa-s.img available for booting from
SRM console, prolly EV4 only. Use of MILO is deprecated on MIKASA, and
unavailable on NORITAKE. Kernels for NORITAKE, booting from SRM, are:

noritake-2030-srm-ev4-07nov.img # models 4/xxx
noritake-2030-srm-ev5-07nov.img # models 5/xxx
