Sorry if I'm a bit late with my reply:

At 06:28 PM 14/3/98 -0800, you wrote:
>I get the following error message when trying to build 2.1.88 for alpha.
>
>
>gcc -D__KERNEL__ -I/usr/src/linux-2.1.88/include -Wall -Wstrict-prototypes
-O2 -fomit-frame-pointer -pipe -mno-fp-regs -ffixed-8 -c -o sr.o sr.c
>sr.c:481: warning: `/*' within comment
>sr.c: In function `do_sr_request':
>sr.c:536: parse error before `void'
>sr.c:542: `tries' undeclared (first use this function)
>
(etc.)

it's just a typo that somehow made it into the 2.1.88 sources: somewhere
around line 475 you'll find a line of comment, starting with /*, ending
with just *. Add the / at the end and you'll be fine.
