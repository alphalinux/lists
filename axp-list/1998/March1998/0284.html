David Tucker wrote:
>
> Anyone know why on 2.1.64, 2.1.76, and 2.1.89 when I try to "route add
> default" it tells me:
>
> ioctl(SIOCADDRT) device route: Operation not supported by device
> and after PPP connects, it tells me
> pppd[430]: ioctl(SIOCADDRT) device route: Network is down

This is known for 2.1 kernels on all plattforms. There are some patches
around for this. Mainly
they just comment the ioctl() command that adds the route in the pppd
sources.
