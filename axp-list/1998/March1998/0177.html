On Sat, 7 Mar 1998, Brian E.W. Wood wrote:

> pancho@galaxy.lca.uevora.pt wrote:
> >
> > hello all
> >
> > My multia (233 MHz cpu) seems a little slow. How can I accelerate it?
> >
> > also the scrolling in the xterm is slow, is there any way to change this?
>
> If you haven't already you can push the speed on the CPU to 266Mhz.
> Looking at the front of the SIMM modules with the CPU to your right
> there are three jumpers W7, W8 and W9. For 233Mhz. they are in-out-in.
> For 266 make them out-in-in.
>
> Make sure you have adequate air around the case, and run it vertically
> if you can.
>
> I ran a Multia for over a year at 266 but I can't take responsibility if
> you burn up your CPU.
>
> The machine will still seem slow even if you do this.
