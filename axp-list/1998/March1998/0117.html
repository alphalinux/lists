According to Robert Herzog:
> I am having a hell of a time trying to get a 100 mbit card on this
> machine.
> It works nicely with an old faithful WD8013.
> I tried without success :
> - a DE500 (but it has a 21143 chip, rather tahn the 21142 usually
> metioned, and the BIOS does not find it suitable either)
> - a 3C905 for which I tried to buid a kernel with some recent patch
> found on gatekeeper. Thsi kernel hangs when adressing the card.
>
> I did not find a SMC 100 mbit card around here. Can someone point me to
> an european source ?
>

I'm using the SMC 10/100, but this is the older one based on the DEC 21140
chip, not the newer one that uses the SMC device.

>From /proc/pci:

Bus 0, device 5, function 0:
Ethernet controller: DEC DC21140 (rev 18).
Medium devsel. Fast back-to-back capable. IRQ 18. Master Capable. Latency=32.
I/O at 0x9000.
Non-prefetchable 32 bit memory at 0xc000000.

I tried the 3C905 and got the same results you did.

The SMC 10/100 works fine and I get 4 - 5 megabytes/second FTP transfers
(from a Linux/Intel system using the 3C905).
