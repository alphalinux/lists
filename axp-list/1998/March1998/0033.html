First, thanks to all the replies I have received so far.
I will in particular follow up on the suggestions regarding
the Adaptec 2940AU controller.

I second David's comment that transtec had by
far the cheapest price (in Austria as well) .

In the meantime, I made the following unscientific test: I
had all these sig11 errors when building egcs-1.0.1. Well,
today I started from scratch; however, I left the box of the machine
open. (The airflow in this miditower box is a desaster...)
Result: Uninterrupted compilation without errors (actually,
a number of enquire: arithmetic trap errors during the
compilation, but they look OK to me). Thus, I know at
least that my main enemy is heat; whether it gets too hot so
that failure results necessarily, or whether one component is
of lower quality so that the increased heat under
load with a closed box is too much, I can't decide. At least
I think that I have sufficient information to contact the
company.

Any additional helpful hints are of course most welcome,

Stefan
