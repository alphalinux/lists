> I managed to compile the pnp extensions by Tom Lees
> (http://www-jcr.lmh.ox.ac.uk/~pnp) into a 2.1.88 kernel for alpha. That
> might be an alternative ?

Possibly, but I do not know. I found in the meantime that version
1.13 is available and this once compiles after minor tweaks and runs
on Alpha. I uploaded to ftp.redhat.com source rpm with modifications
to compile cleanly and rpm with alpha binaries. Guess that they
should show up in contrib at some moment.

After all this work it turned out that the card in question does not
want to admit that is has anything to do with PnP. On the other hand it
can be set, in software, to some values of port and IRQ and, when
booting with ether=11,0x300 to kernel, it works. Otherwise scanning
for IRQ fails. Oh, well....
