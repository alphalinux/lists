So, you haven't applied any of the updates, I take it?

They're available at: ftp.redhat.com/pub/redhat/updates/5.0/alpha/

Download 'em and rpm -Uhv them. They don't fix all of the problems with
5.0, but they do fix a fair number of them.

> Otherwise management is wondering why they justified getting rid of NT and

> would love to see the same thing on Alpha.

If 5.0 still isn't performing up to snuff after you've installed the
updates, then you have two choices: go back to 4.2 (which more than one
person has done, and which is probably best for a business environment)
or wait for the inevitable 5.1.
