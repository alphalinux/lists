>My multia (233 MHz cpu) seems a little slow. How can I accelerate it?
>also the scrolling in the xterm is slow, is there any way to change this?

Remove all the W7, W8 and W9 jumpers.
Then your Multia will run on 300MHz.
I have many Multia machines on 266MHz or 300MHz, and everything seemd fine.

If you set W9 installed, then your Multia will run on 266MHz.
