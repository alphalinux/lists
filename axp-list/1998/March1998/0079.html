On Mar 03 David Alan Gilbert (dg@cogency.co.uk) wrote:

> The case is a pretty standard ATX mini tower case with three front
> visible 5.25" bays and 2 front visible 3.5" bays;

I have quite the same case (but from Vobis). It has a secondary
Fan in the front on the bottom, blowing right at the motherboard
and the cpu.

I used to have 3x9GB in there and they got pretty hot (too hot to
touch for a while). Now I put them in an old Towercase which I
modified. I made a tunnel around the drives to the power supply,
so the air has no other way than to flow around the drives.
Now they are really cool and as I used some kind of foam (similar
to the one used in speakers) it's now really quiet :)

It wasn't much work and cost me nothing (material was lying around
and these 2 hours were well spent).

Barracudas and all of them made this noise at startup since I got
them. I guess it's normal, but it's worrying...
