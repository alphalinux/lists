Well, I'm certainly not "the owners" of this board, but I do have one on
loan, and a little experience installing RH 5.0 on it:

1. you *MUST* use MTools to make the DOS FS on the MILO/ldmilo.exe/osfpal.nh
floppy (ie "mformat" after formatting with "fdformat"). I had no luck with
a DOS FS made with "mkdosfs" under LINUX.

2. you must use the latest binaries for the MILO floppy contents, and the
kernel floppy, as found on their website:

http://www.deskstation.com/linux/

3. you do NOT want to use their RAMdisk floppy image, as that's for RH 4.2.
I was successful using the stock/updated RH 5.0 RAMdisk floppy image.

The following may be helpful as well:

1. I had to use an ISA VGA instead of a PCI version; their MILO's BIOS emulator
just did not seem to find the BIOS ROM on the PCI card, and thus the screen
remained black. I was only able to tell by hooking up a serial line to COM1
and observing the MILO debug messages.

2. none of the latest alpha-patches or MILO sources that I've made available
will successfully build a RUFFIAN kernel or MILO. I'm working the issue,
but until then, you'll have to use the MILO and kernel sources available
on the Deskstation website.

Good luck.
