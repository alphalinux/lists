After replacing the Diamond Fireport 40 DUAL with a standard
Fireport 40, which uses the Symbios 875J chip set, things
now work. Note the single channel card states it supports
both PCI 2.0 and 2.1. I believe the problem is that Alphabios
or whatever runs the PC164LX PCI bus must only be PCI 2.0
capable. So, to all you folks out there, beware. Check what
level of PCI bus your adapters support!

Does Digital have firmware/?? that IS PCI 2.1 capable??
