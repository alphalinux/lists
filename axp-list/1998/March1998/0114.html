With regards to your message at 09:26 AM 03-05-98 -0700, Brian Manley.
Where you stated:
>Hey all...
>
>Recently got a free (lucky me!) Multia box that has the onboard NCRC810
SCSI controller. Can anyone shed some light on what kind of SCSI this is?
Ultra? Wide? Fast? Ultra Wide? Ultra Fast? Ultra Ultra Fast? ;-)
>
It's a SCSI-2 FAST, narrow controller..
But don't despair, in tests on x86 and Alpha, on same narrow SCSI Ultra
hard drives it benchmarks about as fast as an Adaptec 2940U.

It, like most SCSI on Alpha, is happiest if you use an active terminator on
the end of the external SCSI bus.
