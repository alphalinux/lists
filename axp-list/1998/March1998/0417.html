I had exectly the same problem. The solotion came from Christopher Curtis
<ccurtis@ee.fit.edu>. He was nice enough to send me some new include
files:

/usr/include/asm/resource.h, 1369 bytes
/usr/include/linux/resource.h, 2707 bytes
/usr/include/resourcebits.h, 6144 bytes

It all seems to be working now, although I haven't tried to compile
anything else. One of these days I will, and I will post the result the
list.
