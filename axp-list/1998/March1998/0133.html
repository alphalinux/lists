On Thu, 5 Mar 1998, Paul M. Hirsch wrote:

> I beleive someone suggested a NetGear FA310TX card earlier. It is a
> lovely card for Intel boxes, but only receives packets into long-aligned
> buffers, which doesn't mix so well with the word-oriented Alpha.

this is a good point, thanks for reminding me :). It basically means that
the alpha can't have the tulip busmaster the packet right into the skb..
it has to do a memory copy.. which is why I try to use eepros or 905s in
my alpha. It won't really be an issue unless you're pushing lots and lots
of data..
