Hello

Your machine is refered to as an AVANTI. You'll find a wealth of information
at
http://www.xs4all.nl/~warp/axplinux/axplinux-index.html
That's how I got linux alpha going on "my" alphastation200 4/100 (although it
took my some trying..)

Perhaps a good try would be to boot up to MILO, it might tell you more
about your hardware. An easy way to do that is to use the avanti.img
image and "rawrite" it to a disk and boot the alpha from there (you'll have
to switch back to the SRM console though)
I don't know much about alphas but I can't imagine they would change the
config much for the same computer so you probably have:

ethernet : DEChip Tulip
SCSI: 53c810 is the driver needed but I don't know anything about its specific
settings
Sound card: Microsoft sound something compatible

Philippe Girolami
