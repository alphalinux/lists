>>> "Alexander V. Mirgorodsky" said:
> Has anyone successeeded in installing linux on AS 800 (Corelle, I
> beleive) ?

Yes, LINUX/Alpha will run on the CORELLE, it's part of the NORITAKE family.

> What method should I use (SRM, AlphaBios, etc.) ?

Please, use SRM, as there's no MILO for any of that family.

> What images are available ?

I believe that the CORELLE only comes as a 5/xxx model, so you can find the
appropriate image file on gatekeeper.dec.com:

/pub/DEC/Linux-Alpha/Kernels/noritake-2030-srm-ev5-07nov.img

"dd" that onto a floppy and boot with:

boot dva0 -file vmlinux.gz -flags "load_ramdisk=1 root=/dev/fd0"

Good luck.
