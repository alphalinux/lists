On Sun, 15 Feb 1998, Keith Baker wrote:

> BTW: I have had probelms Gzipping my kernel... I made one and it was just
> sitting in the /usr/src/linux/ directory so I attempted to gzip it and that
> blew up on boot. I tried the kernel raw and it did boot (though it is
> huge). Its not a "problem" but it would be nice to know.

The thing is, never gzip it yourself. You have to do that with some nice
parameters to get the kernel work. make boot does this automagically, and
your vmlinux.gz is to be found in /usr/src/linux/arch/alpha/boot/

Try that, and you'll be rewarded.
