Hi Dan !

> Is there a way to switch kernels without reboot the system, as the way our
> routers work here, as long as the machine is "up" everthing is fine, but to
> reboot my system to switch kernels can take two hours to get straight. Ideally
> I would like to be able to not have to exit the running kernel to load the new
> one, a hot-swapping kernel kinda vibe, but exiting to MILO will suffice if the
> former is not possible. I appreciate any advice anyone can give on this.

Hot swapping the kernel will _never_ work.
You can't even hot swap modules; you can do rmmod xxx ; insmod xxx
as most developers do but it's not hot swapping (doing it while a
module is used will not work).
