Correct. Actually there are 2 main typess in this.
The first, more common on PC's is the DTK style, or old IBM AT
The second, like DEC used is called the Acer style.
Hint lies here: If you can get an internal cable set from an old Acer
motherboard it will work fine. These can even be ordered from many cable
and accessory suppliers.
Or look up in your yellow pages someone who sell Acer or Acer Open
components and see if they will sell you a cable set.. Should cost about $5
or less..
