Eric Van Tassell 1 wrote:
>
> I'm trying to get rh5.0 up on a alphastation 200(4/100). Other postings have
> hinted at video adapter hassles so, I'm running an ati mach64. I have roms
> for both srm and arc consoles but seemed to have even less joy with aboot.
>

Plug a null modem into com1, and the other end into a live machine. Run
the interface at 9600 baud, N-8-1.

You'll be able to find out where you're dying. There's a bunch being
echoed out to the serial port before milo figures out how to handle the
video. Most likely culprit is the video bios detection, but you need to
know for sure.

Stick with the ARC console. Previous posts from DEC guru's say it's all
they're supporting anymore (contrary to the RedHat documentation).
