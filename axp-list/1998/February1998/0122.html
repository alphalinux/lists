>This is quite normal actually :)
>You have a MILO image that has serial console support. When soft rebooting,
>MILO sends output to the serial console. You likely have a modem connected
and
>on, and it will be confused by all the messages MILO is telling it. So it
says
>"ERROR". MILO happily responds to that, and you end up with a line of input
that
>reaches the max, and transmission will stop (MILO will then only allow you
do
>use backspace to delete input scharacters.
>Turn of your modem during reboot, or recompile milo without serial console
>support.
