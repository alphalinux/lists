armin@data.tops.net wrote:
>
> i know this might be somewhat of topic, but my biggest problem with my
> alpha (PC164/500mhz) is the extreme noise that it makes.
> There are 3 fans installed. One on the front side to blow fresh air into
> the box - this one is quiet, anotherone sits on the cpu - also quiet, the
> third one is my problem: inside the power supply - it looks like a heavy
> duty turbo-prop and make extreme noise. I wonder if i can just replace it
> with a good and quiet x86-power supply fan ??

With an at least similar system (PC164 / 500MHz / three fans overall)
I did the following:
I took the fan on the front side and built it into the power supply.
I bought a Papst variofan as a replacement for the front fan.

This works at least for me since several months.

For I short while I had two Papst variofans installed (power supply
and front fan). But in this configuration the air coming out of the
box was rather hot ...
