> Well,
>
> signal 11 is almost due to hardware problems. On the intel I know it is a
> sign that the cooling fan is not working properly on the CPU in about 80%
> of the cases.

... and on the alpha this almost always is due to bugs in gcc, and
particulary in the optimizer. I had the same problem with egcs, and (as
usual) lowering the optimization (changing -O2 to -O or removing it
altogether) was enough to make the file compile. With gcc 2.7 I have also
received this if -funroll-loops or -funroll-all-loops are activated when
compiling some particular c-files.
