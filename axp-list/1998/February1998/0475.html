gatgul@voicenet.com said:
> everytime i try to run control-panel on redhat 5.0/alpha i get error
> badaccess ( attempt to access private resource denied)

This is from GTK. Basically, the kernel support for SHM is broken, or doesn't
agree with glibc, or both. Get a more recent kernel from the usual places
and try that. 2.0.32, and most 2.1 kernels work.

You may have to update your MILO if updated kernels don't boot, but I
think the RH5.0 MILO is OK.
