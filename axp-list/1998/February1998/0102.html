Sorry to continue this long dead thread, but I'm having trouble getting
the disk performance results I would expect from my LX164 (aforementioned)
with intraserver. Right now I have it split down to one disk per channel,
and ehre are my results:

Using the command: [root@mail1 /root]# /sbin/hdparm -t -T /dev/sda

I get:

/dev/sda: (seagate barracuda UW 4G)
Timing buffer-cache reads: 32 MB in 0.58 seconds =55.01 MB/sec
Timing buffered disk reads: 16 MB in 3.27 seconds = 4.89 MB/sec
/dev/sdb: (IBM UW 4G)
Timing buffer-cache reads: 32 MB in 0.55 seconds =57.83 MB/sec
Timing buffered disk reads: 16 MB in 2.07 seconds = 7.74 MB/sec
/dev/sdc: (IBM UW 4G)
Timing buffer-cache reads: 32 MB in 0.51 seconds =62.33 MB/sec
Timing buffered disk reads: 16 MB in 2.12 seconds = 7.56 MB/sec
/dev/md0: (aggregate RAID0 of sdb and sdc)
Timing buffer-cache reads: 32 MB in 0.49 seconds =65.57 MB/sec
Timing buffered disk reads: 16 MB in 1.27 seconds =12.63 MB/sec

These numbers seem a little low to me.. any ideas why?
