> egcs-1.0.1 (a fix posted on this list by Richard applied) seemed to me
> work just find. I did put in a new version of g++ libraries in, though.
> They are avaialable as a source rpm in contrib/hurricane/SRPMS on

> I do not know what you did but likely this was not something valid.
> Just to check things I did recompile all four egcs compilers and
> protoize and unprotoize as well and all worked fine
> (updated RH 5.0 with 2.0.33+alpha patches kerneli, egcs-1.0.1).

Well ackwardly enough much of these problems were due to the used kernel.

I at first used the method propposed on this list. Making a new kernel
directory with a new kernel (2.0.33) and the alpha patches. The kernel was
fine but among the hints was the advise to relink the /usr/src/linux
directory to the original (redhat shipped) kernel directory.

Well I finally decided to switch it to the running kernel directory and
things seem to be running much better now.

I will do some testing (compiling source RPM's) and I guess much more
packages will build fine.
