> and +12 volt power at the disk, both fine. I disconnected the narrow
> devices, no change. I reconnected the narrow devices and disconnected
> the hard disk, everything worked fine (except of course I couldn't boot
> NT).

Connect all narrow devices; connect the uw-disk but don't plug the power
connector to the disk (but to all narrow devices).

Make a device scan (ARC).
If it doesn't work there are two possibilities:

a) SCSI-cable broken (5 %)
b) Wrong termination settings. (95 %)
Since your disk get TERMPWR from the SCSI-cable you don't need
to connect it to real power to check this.

If it does work; INQUIRY are not much data, make the reverse check.
Plug all drives to SCSI-bus and but only the disk to power.
Retry device scan (ARC).

I bet a good austrian beer that your termination settings a wrong.
