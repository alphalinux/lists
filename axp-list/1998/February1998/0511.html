After just over 3 hours of netsearching and pulling my hair out, I've
solved this little problem.

It has to do with the fact that you can boot from any named boot image
at all, but you _MUST_ have a file called "vmlinux.gz" to copy over
from your boot disks during installation. Nowhere in the install guide
does it give anything remotely approaching an indication that this is the
case, and it was only by virtue of the fact that I was desperate enough
to try anything that I figured this out :)

Anyhow, I now have a booted and running Linux/Alpha so I'm happy ;-)

Thanks to the people who emailed me with suggestions.
