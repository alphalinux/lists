On 16 Feb, Gerald Brandt wrote:

> We seem to be having a problem with f2c (RedHat 4.2).
>
> At the end of almost every sub-routine, we get a floating point exception
> (during the f2c run).
>
> This works VERY well under Intel Linux (RedHat 4.2).
>
> We've tried g77, and the egcs g77, and both work well... except that we
> need double precision, and they don't support that, while f2c does.

The alpha is very sensitive to floating point exceptions, there's a bug
in the kernel. A few people are working on it, I'm one of them, but
I've not got a patch as of yet.

You can usually get around these problems by not over/underflowing.
Catch those exceptions before they happen.
