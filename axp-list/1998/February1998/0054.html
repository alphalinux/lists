I thought I'd let the list know that I have had success
with a Miata (PW433a) and an unpatched kernel 2.1.86.

Cheers to Jay and all the hard work.

Some gotchas:

1. Do not enable TGA console
2. Enable Unix domain sockets (not enabled by default)
3. 3c905 driver causes kernel oops - don't use.
4. tulip does not autosense 10baseT-no-MII connection - I must
force it to use 10baseT.
