Fishing -

Locally - Try playing with locate
WWW - Try http://www-personal.engin.umich.edu/~jgotts/linux.html
for Linux, this includes a link to online HOWTOS.

If you installed these onto your machine, try looking in /usr/doc/HOWTO/

The default with pppd is to assume that you will be assigned a dynamic IP
unless told otherwise, so that is fine. But you will only be assigned one
address, not a group of them, so you will have to look into ipfwadm and
masquerading support within the kernel (I believe that this is in the default
RH5.0 kernel) to allow all the other machines on your network to seem to
the rest of the world as though they are using your one and only valid IP
address.
