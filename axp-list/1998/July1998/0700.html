ld.so is part of libc5 (or compatible old glibc). libc6 (=glibc)
uses ld-linux.so.2 (which is a symlink to ld-2.0.x.so). You might check
for glibc-1.99.x, though I can't understand not just recompiling the
program... that old glibc was rather... unstable (at least, it was/is on
ppc).
