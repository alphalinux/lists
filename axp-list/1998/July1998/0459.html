Larry Zimmerman wrote:
> The server works for me at 1024x768 8bit using the 980706 version.
> When I try X -bpp 24, I see 3 images spread horizontally across the screen.
> (Like there is a separate image for each color, with space between them?)

Hmmm... do the images extend the whole height of the screen, or
are they shortened? About how much space is there between the
images?

> I've never used anything other than 8-bit color in X, so I'll try
> reading the manual pages to see what I need to change in the config file.

It sounds like your XF86Config already has a Display section with
a depth of 32. Besides having such a section, nothing else
should have to change.

> > sync_on_green is evil. :) At 16bpp it continously forces my monitor
> How do you pass this option to the server?

Add the line

Option "sync_on_green"

to the Device section and prepare to watch your monitor flail,
according to reports I've read. Oddly enough my monitor syncs
fine with this enabled, but doesn't notice that it's a SOG
signal.
