I have a vanilla installed UDB with redhat 5.0 on it and am having some
trouble with, what I think is, the SCSI bus.

I get an error to the effect of "NCR SCSI controller timeout" which gives
an error serial number. After a few of these it says dropping bus speed
from 10Mb/s to 5Mb/s then finally locks up...

The time it takes to do this appears to be completely random. Sometimes it
won't boot and at other times it will run for 15 minutes or more!

Anybody any ideas?
