Hey ho!
Me again :)
As we say in Germany: What takes long time finally becomes good!
And here we are with a great and mostly useless driver, a driver for the
eight diagnostic LEDs on the AXPpci33 board.
The driver can be found on my homepage or directly downloaded via
http://www.si.unix-ag.org/~nils/LED_module.tar.gz

It comes with two demo programs.
The first is 'procled' which displays a bar-graph of the system load up to
load 1.0. Over load 1.0 the last bit will flash to show that it's more.
The second is the mostly useless but nice knight-rider type running light,
i.e. just one LED 'moving' from left to right and vice versa.

Any comments about the driver, enhancements or additional programs are
welcome!
My special thanks go to Thomas Bogendoerfer and Jay Estabrook. Thomas found
the correct adresses of the LEDs by disassembling the SROM code (urgh!) and
Jay took some of his time to answer my stupid questions ;)
