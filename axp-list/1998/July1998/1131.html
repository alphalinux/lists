on my 5.0 cd it's /milo/images/avanti.img. i apologize for not being
able to give you specifics in 5.1. look for files named avanti.img on
the cd.

if you can't find it on the cd search the net for milo or specifically
'milo.dd' files. these can be written to floppy with rawrite.

a good source for alpha linux stuff is ftp://ftp.digital.com/pub/. i
found a milo image for my 400 4/233 here a while ago.

also, i don't know if your machine has it but i found it easier to
install from nt/arc than the srm console.
