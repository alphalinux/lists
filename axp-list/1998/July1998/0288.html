Hello,

Glad to find somebody with the same machine.

My as200 4/166 doesn't have FDD , has a SCSI CDD.
For SRM , this is what I did.
shutdown -h now to come to SRM from DEC OSF/1.
>>> boot dka500 -fi kernels/avanti-s.gz -fl "root=/dev/sr0
load_ramdisk=1"

// dka500.5.0.6.0 is my external SCSI CDD

It boots , uncompresses and sends BOOTP and RARP , fails and looks for
the floppy disks which I don't have :-( , so my installation fails.

But you have FDD , so it must work.
Once installation is completed, I guess MILO won't be needed as you will
be able to set one of the SCSI /IDE partitions as bootable and set the
env-vars auto_action, boot_dev , boot_file etc.
( Correct me if I am wrong )

If you are using MILO , then I suppose it must be milo/avanti because
another as200 of mine with NT ARC accepts it and loads MILO from the 2MB
FAT partition .

-Mohanan
