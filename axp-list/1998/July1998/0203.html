They will build out of the box. But to work around a binutils 2.7.0.2
assembler bug, you must upgrade them all simultaneously. And since I'll
warrant you didn't do a complete three stage bootstrap of all of those
packages, a simultaneous upgrade was not what you were doing.

Months and months ago I posted, several times, complete instructions
for performing this bootstrap. You may check this archives if you like,
but I would up just telling folks to upgrade their distribution. It is
easier.
