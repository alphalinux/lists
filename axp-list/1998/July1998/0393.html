Hello all..

I recently tried the 24-bit TGA server on my UDB and did not get
working results (as Larry Z. reported, however, save/restore works).

I tried 16 and 32 bit modes at 800x600. I know my monitor can support
at these resolutions as I use this monitor on a PC in these modes and
higher.

At >8 bpp at 800x600 my monitor simply goes blank, and declares that
no signal is being sent. Curiously, at 640x480x16bpp the screen
goes blank, but the monitor claims to be recieving a signal.

sync_on_green is evil. :) At 16bpp it continously forces my monitor
to change modes continously, and displays no picture. (? this is
apparently what happens.. I hear the relays flip inside the monitor, it
apparently loops doing this).
