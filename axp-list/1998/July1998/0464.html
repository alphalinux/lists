On Mon, 6 Jul 1998, Larry Zimmerman wrote:

> > sync_on_green is evil. :) At 16bpp it continously forces my monitor
> How do you pass this option to the server?

You'll need to edit /etc/X11/XF86Config by hand. Find the graphics device
section, and put Option "sync_on_green" in there. A good spot is after
the Videoram keyword in the TGA section, this Videoram keyword should be
commented out because X will autodetect, but it is a good landmark.
