Hello,

I'm still quite new to Alpha, and have seen some log messages that
I have never seen in all my years of Linux/Intel experience. Can someone
tell me the significance of these?

I see these occasionally, from various apps. I don't know how to reproduce
it:

Jun 26 00:55:07 earth kernel: minicom: memory violation at pc=12001f0f0
rp=12001f0a4 (bad address = 00000000)

And I see these on every bootup of 2.0.35 w/alpha patches:

Jun 26 00:51:09 earth kernel: Alpha PCI BIOS32 revision 0.03
Jun 26 00:51:09 earth kernel: Probing PCI hardware.
Jun 26 00:51:09 earth kernel: bios32 WARNING: slot 13, function 0 requests
0x8000000 bytes of contig address space---don't use sparse memory
accesses on this device!!
Jun 26 00:51:09 earth kernel: bios32.sio_fixup: weird, device 8086:0484
coming in on slot 7 has no irq line!!

The former message appears to be in reference to my video adapter, a DEC
DC21030 TGA ZLXp-E2. The second message, regarding device 8086:0484 is in
reference to my onboard Intel 82378 I/O controller. The system is AS 200
Avanti booting from SRM console version 6.8.

thanks,
