RH has (in the past) decided not to use modules; in fact, I'm not even sure
whether one set of modules would actually work across all the different
kernels that have to be built (anyone know/guess?).

So, the kernels are monolithic, and include what are the most common
drivers: tulip/de4x5, ne2000, 3com 503/509/905/59x, IIRC. For the best
chance of support, choose one of the TULIP-based cards (DEC or SMC or ???).
