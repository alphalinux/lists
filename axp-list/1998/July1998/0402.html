For what its worth, PGP 5.0i compiles on the Alpha just fine, all that
needs to be done is a small section of code needs to be commented out

I haven't messed with PGP 2.* in awhile.. is there any particular reason
why you want to use 2.* and not 5.0?

bug, and the other to fix the expiry date (ie, remove it).. and, like
I say, for the Alpha, set it up to recognize word64.
