I believe your XL 233 is one of the "true" XLs, and CANNOT use a real AVANTI
kernel because of certain hardware limitations. There are a couple of "true"
XL kernels available, IIRC, on gatekeeper.dec.com:

/pub/DEC/Linux-Alpha/Kernels/xl-2030-fixed.gz

is the oldest, but first available XL kernel that fixed a problem encountered
only when a true XL was equipped with a Matrox Millennium.

/pub/DEC/Linux-Alpha/Kernels/Test-pre34-11b/XL.gz

is the latest and (hopefully, IMHO) greatest, and should work at least as
well as the older one.

Oh, and this:

ln -s Avanti /etc/alpha_systype

is really the correct one; run the right kernel, I believe, and it'll all
"just work"... ;-}

Good luck.
