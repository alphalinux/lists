it so I post it again ;)
I just got such a card, i.e. a Haupauge WinTV (mono), and tried all night to
get it running. Now after the about tenth reboot of my Noname I have lost my
motivation for tonight :(

problems) but still the driver does crash my machine upon first try to
display a frame. I tried this using RH5.1 and a S3 Virge graphics card. All
