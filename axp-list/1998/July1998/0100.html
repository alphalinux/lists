I experienced the system locking up on startx 3 times now in the last 6
months. It always happends after the machine has been up for more then 2
weeks. We have a PC164LX with a Millenium II running the stock 2.0.30
RedHat 5.0 kernel. I have not been able to associate the lock up with
anything specific, but the machine has to be hard-reset (causing filesystem
inconsistancies).
I also notice that after being up for over 10 days or so 'who' will
respond with a segmentation fault. This also results in the halting of all
logging in /var/log. A simple reboot (not a hard reset) fixes the problem.
Again I have no suggestions on how to fix the problem. It could be a slow
kernel memory leak.
