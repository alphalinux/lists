> when i exicute i recive the error

> ./java in /usr/local/jdk115/bin/alpha/green_threads/java

Sounds like 'java' is a shell script and the shell it's trying to execute
isn't there.

I don't think it's anything else, but do a 'file java' and see what it
says and if it's not a shell script, do a 'ldd java' and make sure all
your libraries are found. Other than that, make sure it's chmod +x'd,
plus all the regular stuff.
