With regards to your message at 09:15 PM 08-03-98 +0200, Torsten Zirzlaff.
Where you stated:
>Hi.
>
> Just a short question. I got a new firmwar file for my old AXPpci33
>and don't know how to proceed. The file looks like a mere DOS binary
>(decaxppci33_v1.6.exe). Do I had to dd the file on a disk and "boot"
>from it? A short answer would be nice.
copy it to a disk, renamed as fwupdate.exe
Put the disk in the floppy drive.
Then you can go to ARC menu, "supplementary menu", "Install New Firmware"
and there ya go..
