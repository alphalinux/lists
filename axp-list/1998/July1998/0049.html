>>> brinkmann@nmsu.edu said:
> I've had limited success installing modules on my system, an EB164 running
> 2.0.30.
>
> 2) Installing the sound module locks up the keyboard and mouse. I
> have a Soundblaster 16 card. The system still runs, since I
> can telnet in. I suspected an I/O address or IRQ conflict but
> find:
>
> Device IRQ Addresses
>
> Keyboard 1 ?
> PS/2 Mouse 12 ?
> Sound 7 audio 0x0220-33
> midi 0x0330
> game 0x0200-7
> synth 0x0388-B
> cdrom 0x0170-7
> 0x0376-7
>
> I get the same problem if I statically compile sound support
> into the kernel.

Don't use IRQ 7, that is normally the parallel port. Use 5, 9, 10, or 11.

Also, sound support as a module has been notoriously variable in stability.
