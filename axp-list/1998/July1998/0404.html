Is the printer EitherTalk or AppleTalk ( appletalk uses a serial connector
which is round ). If its AppleTalk, and I suspect it is, you can't get there
from here. The problem is that Linux does not do AppleTalk it only does
EtherTalk and your Macs can do one or the other but not both. You can get a
device that does EtherTalk to Appletalk. Look at www.warehouse.com for one of
these devices.
