One benefit to the tulip is that the driver actually works on the alpha. The
3Com just gave alignment errors. The 3c905B is a royal piece of garbage...

alpha took 5 minutes and worked the first time.

I have 3Com cards in all but one of my machines, but after the 905B I'll
probably stick with DEC chipsets.
