Jay Estabrook, of Digital/Compaq, wrote the 980606 MILO, and the link from
my page links to one of the digital.com ftp sites.

As for this MILO, I'm surprised to hear of the problems, since I know of
MANY people that have used it and it worked fine. As an alternate, you
could follow the instructions on my website to go through the serial
console, which I did before this MILO was out, and it worked fine. Once you
get the machine installed, and MILO on a boot partition, you shouldn't need
to be able to read the screen as MILO does its stuff anyway, if you are
having problems.

My website is http://www.toast.net/~strycher/AS200.html
