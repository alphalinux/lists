i took a look and was surprised to find that the ftp site directory WAS
empty for 5.1. (?) anyway, you did what i would have suggested - use the
version of milo from 5.0. your red hat and milo versions don't have to
agree, and as long as milo gets you up and running without problems i
dont' see why you would need the newest version.

to get back to srm from arc, i pick 'supplementary menu', then 'setup
system', then 'switch to open vms or digital unix console'. srm is the
'open vms' option. i don't remember what version of arc i have so yours
may be a bit different, poke around.

again, to get back to arc from srm use 'set os_type=NT'. i haven't gone
into the digital unix because i don't want to get stuck there and not
know how to get back to arc or srm :) i hate the way arc looks and the
nt references too :-P
