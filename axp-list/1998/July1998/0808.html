Hi,

This takes up more space but I config the latest egcs compilers with
--prefix=/usr/local/egcs (/usr/local is NFS shared from our server) and
leave the system areas [1] and compilers alone. If you happen to need
the bleeding edge binutils for the egcs compiler, /usr/local/egcs is a
good place for it also.
