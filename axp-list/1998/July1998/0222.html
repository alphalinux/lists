Christoph Nadig <Christoph.Nadig@stest.ch> writes:
> recently I bought a SoundBlaster 16 Value PnP card and got Linux
> to recognise it (Actually as a SoundBlaster 16, not Value PnP).
>
> The Mixer device is working fine, however, 16 bit audiostreams do not
> work at all (DRQ/IRQ timout after some seconds) while 8 bit
> audiostreams basically seem to work, but the maschine crashes
> after a random time.
[..]
> Any ideas, or someone succeded to make any SB 16 card to work properly?

If it's a card with the Vibra chip, it simply lacks the 16 bit DMA
channel. You have to configure the sound driver as an 8 bit card,
disabling the 16 bit DMA option. There's help available in the kernel
setup.
