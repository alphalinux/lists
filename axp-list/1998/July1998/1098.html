I'm not sure if Earthlink charges by the minute. I think you get a certain
amount of time and then start paying for additional time but check with them to
be sure.

The real problem is that your telco charges for ISDN are likely to be by the
minute. Here in NJ the base rate is 1 cent a minute but there are packages that
give you a certain number of minute for a flat rate - ranging up to about
$230/month for unlimited use. This is a real pain. Some areas have flat-rate
ISDN at more reasonable rates though.
