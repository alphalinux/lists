Jaz drives work fine for me under Intel Linux. Don't think there
should be a problem under Alpha Linux. They are just SCSI devices
with the minor quirk that they spin down when not in use. They
have to be started back up so if the SCSI DD knows about that,
things should work OK. I guess your CD writer is not external so
you have to go to the NT box? I have a CD writer on my PII with
Linux and use cdrecord which works well for data CD's. Haven't
tried audio CD's with it though.
