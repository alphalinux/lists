Hi !

> There is an Adaptec 1742 SCSI controller with
> 2 Harddisks already.

> When i try to disable the floppy port with the
> EISA configuration disk (i.e. choosing an AHA 1740)
> i get an EISA configuration error after reboot and
> the system jumps to the WIN NT console.
>
> Anyone knows if there is a jumper on the AHA1742
> to disable the floppy ? I stopped trying out after
> a jumper went up in smoke.

There IS a jumper for disabling the floppy port.

>From the AHA-1742A Installation Guide:
"... disable the floppy disk controller on the AHA-1742A
by removing the rightmost jumper from jumper block J6 in
the lower left corner of the board."

Hope this helps..
