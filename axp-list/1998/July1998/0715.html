>>> Jiann-Ming Su said:
> For some reason, my BusLogic card has been assigned IRQ 24 on my SX164
> computer. Any clues as to why this would happen?

Well, the PCI interrupts are NOT routed through the PIC (normal 0-15 ISA IRQs)
on the SX (and most other Alphas as well), and so needed to be numbered in some
way that didn't conflict.

IRQ 24 is just the 9th PCI interrupt, but we couldn't call it IRQ 9... ;-}

Does your machine/BusLogic *work* using IRQ 24? Try cat /proc/interrupts...
