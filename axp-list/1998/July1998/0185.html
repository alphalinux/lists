Unless the bug has something to do with RedHat specifically (ie, init
scripts, etc) do not simply report it to redhat - report it to the person
in charge of the program. You can Cc: it to bugs@redhat.com (is that the
proper address?) but they shouldn't be your primary contact. Remember
that RedHat is not Linux; they may make a patch but that doesn't mean
it'll get back to the author. Getting the author should be priority 1.
