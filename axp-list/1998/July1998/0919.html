Hello all,
I've been trying for the last two weeks now on and off to get RH 5.1
to detect my modem. I have two isa pnp cards on my computer. One is a
diamond 33.6 pnp and the other is a SB 64 AWE value pnp. I have the
isapnptools installed and did pnpdump and configured my kernal with the
right values for the sound card. My sound card works fine if I comment
any reference to the modem. If I try to set the modem I won't get sound
and I still can't get the modem detected. I had problems with these
cards under NT too because when they're first installed they won't
except anything else except irq 5 and the modem refuses to be anywere
else but com 3. I solved the problem there by changing the dma values on
the SB from 1 5 to 3 7 then NT let me switch to irq 7. Thing is the
isapnp.conf file doesn't give me that option. I'm running RH 5.1 on a
Durango II 533 LX and my kernel is 2.0.34. Any help is greatly
appreciated, Thanks.
