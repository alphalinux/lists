On Sat, May 23, 1998 at 03:23:06PM -0700, Mike "Ford" Ditto wrote:
> I am trying to build binutils 2.9.1 under RedHat Linux 4.1 for alpha,
> using the stock gcc 2.7.2.1.

Won't work unless you rebuild all your libraries. There was a serious
bug in the assembler up through binutils 2.7.0.9, hidden by another bug
in the linker.

Red Hat decided to stay with 2.7.0.2 through RH 4.2 for this very reason.
RH 5.0, along with moving to glibc 2.0.5, upgraded binutils to 2.8.0.1,
which has the problem fixed.
