I have used Arc for a while... it is fairly simple... I am taking a wild
guess that you do not have a fat partition to load milo from... You need
a 2 meg fat partition to boot milo from. You must copy milo and
linload.exe to this partition. Tell Arc to launch linload.exe as the
bootloader and milo as the boot file (I think... its been a while)...
from there you can read ext2 partitions (aka where your kernel resides)
