> Can some one explain to me what this isp is talking about and if it's a good

What they say makes sense. You can do voice over ISDN or
data over ISDN. Looks like Bell Atlantic charges for
the data mode, but not voice mode. You loose 12.5% or
so in speed but save $2/hour on your ISDN line charges.
You configure the RT328 to use the mode you want for
your connection.

Here in Dallas, Tx area, you get flat rate ISDN so I pay
about $65 a month for the ISDN lines. No alternatives to ISDN
in my suburb of Dallas yet. I read ADSL was coming by the
end of this year but not much noise lately. If ADSL is
no more than ISDN when it gets here, I'll probably switch.

FYI... I consider Ascends to be over priced compared to
Netgear and 3com's offerings. Also, Ascend places restrictions
on their low end gear about how many systems can use it
at once, from what I remember.
