On my 166 MHz UDB (24 MB RAM), running RH 4.2, I am having problems with
playing CD's. I have already tried the original player from RH 4.2 multimedia
package and the xmcd from the 4.2 powertools as well as player from KDE beta 4.1
and also recompiled from sources the more recent version of cd player from
RH 5.0 multimedia package. With all these players, it starts to play, but after
stopping and switching the tracks several times, the program crashes and the
machine has to be switched off since it does not react to anything (in
/var/log/messages a memory violation is always reported). I find it hard
to believe that all four players I tried might be broken in the same way,
perhaps there's something wrong with my configuration or something else.
When mounting the CD drive (Pioneer SCSI DR-U12X) with data CD's, however,
there's no problem and everything works fine. Does anybody have an idea what
may the problem be ?
