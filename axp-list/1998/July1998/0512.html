Hi. I'm trying to put a 2nd ethernet board in an alphastation 200,
with redhat 5.1. "ifconfig eth1" always gives me "unknown interface".

They're both pci boards. One is a DEC DC21040, and the other
is a AMD 79C970.

I thought the boards were probed at boot time, but only the
DEC board shows up. cat'ing /proc/pci shows both of them.

I modified drivers/net/Space.c to put pcnet32_probe at the top,
followed by de4x5_probe. This results in the AMD board being
found, but then the DEC board isn't, and "ifconfig eth1" still
returns "unknown interface".

What am I missing?

Also, the AMD code is generating LOTS of "unaligned trap" messages.
