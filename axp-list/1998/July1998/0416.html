> I'm running stock RedHat-5.1 on an Avanti Alphabox and I notice
> that at the text console, the colors red and blue are swapped (everywhere).
> Is this something anyone else has run into? The video card is a ZLXp-E2.

I hate to ask, but is your video cable hooked up correctly? If your
monitor has separate inputs for r/g/b, you might have the "r" and "b"
swapped (I've made that mistake myself; arrange r/g/b to match the
order on the back of the monitor with the plug-ends facing me, then
turn them around to plug 'em in and they're backwards).
