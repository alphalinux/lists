On 02-Aug-98 Michael Williams wrote:
> Do you want to go with an expensive ISDN router like an Ascend or do you
> want to go the cheaper way and just buy an ISDN terminal adapter like a 3COM
> or Diamond and do the routing function within Linux for free??

The Netgear router is prices very competively with the serial-type terminal
adapters. Granted that Linux can do the routing I'd rather have the higher
performance of not having to have a serial link involved.

You can also get an ISDN card to go into your machine, not sure if any of them
work on Alphas though.
