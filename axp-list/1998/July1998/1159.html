Folks,

I'm close to the solution: When I disconnect the GREEN signal, the display
looks sharp without any shadows! Of course the colors are wrong then.

With the monitor disconnected, I've measured the resistance in the
BNC connectors. RED and BLUE have 75 Ohm as they should have. But
GREEN has a high impedance !!

I've tried to solder a 75 Ohm resistor between GREEN and GREEN(RETURN)
(pins 2 and 7) on the VGA connector of the board but this does not
help!!

So my conclusion is, that the original Digital cable BC13L-10 which
is said to work, does something special. Maybe they have a spare
pin assigned with a special function and the cable is shortening
some pins in the connector or something.

Any insight in this anyone?

Thanks // Tom
