Quoting dca2 (jmd@infinet.com):
> Karrazzi, Zakk wrote:
> > How do you I configure my modem for SLIP/PPP.
> There are several files that actually need to be editied.
> (/etc/hosts.allow, /etc/hosts.deny...couple of others.)

/etc/hosts.allow and /etc/hosts.deny have ABSOLUTELY nothing to do with
setting up ppp. They allow you to control access to your system from outside,
but they are not necessary for setting up ppp.

Files you might need to configure, though:
/etc/host.conf (the default settings should be right)
/etc/resolv.conf (add your ISP's name servers)
But I think netcfg will take care of all those things for you.
