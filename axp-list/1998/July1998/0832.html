Jeremy,

Are you trying to set up ypserv and ypbind on the
alpha? I could not tell for sure. You say your
Slackware system binds to "the server" --
but is the "server" and alpha?

If both, have you tried to run ypserv -debug and
review the data? That usually gives good clues.
There is also a ypbind -debug. Most useful,
lots better than working NIS on SunOS 4.1.x.

I just got ypbind on an LX164 running yesterday
with the same error (it must bind to an IPC which
the alpha will replace hopefully in the near
future)

While I was pulling my hair out on the same error
you got, I was interrupted by a phone call. When
I returned to the machine, it had bound. I haven't
looked yet at why it take minutes to bind
(it pings across the LAN near instantaneously).
Run ypbind -debug and see if if eventually bind.

Hope this gives you a few ideas.

Mike Meyer
