On Sun, 5 Jul 1998, Michael wrote:

> I just picked up a UDB cheep as sorta a little alpha toy... (already have
> a xlt300 running 5.0 ). When I power him up the power light comes on there
> is a pause then the keyboard lights flash and the fan starts up and the
> ethernet light starts flashing. I never get any other responses from it
> however the cpu's heatsink does get warm. Any ideas what could be wrong ??

I had this happen to my UDB a while back. It turned out the power
connectors on the motherboard had corroded due to the heat (it was about
2-3, if not more years old by this point). I took apart the power
connector coming from the power supply, cleaned those contacts, and the
ones on the board. When I put it back together, it all worked fine. Though
I taped a fan (24V, running at ~12-14V off separate supply) to the
top-front of the case when it is standing on end, and have that running
24/7. The fans kicks out nice warm air, but the machine has been rock
solid since (5 months).
I would suggest checking out the power contacts as a possible
cause of your problems. Good Luck!
