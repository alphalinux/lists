>>> Hermelito Go said:
>
> I have a PWS500a and I would like to know on to configure the
> kernel so it would support the sound device.

MIATA has an ES1888 builtin, which is (at least) SBPro compatible, I believe.

Set the parameters:

base 0x220
IRQ 5
DRQ 1

Good luck.
