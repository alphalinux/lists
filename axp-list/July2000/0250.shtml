I´ve tried the jumpstart-CD on my avanti AS200 4/233.

Booted via SRM "...boot dka400" starts the aboot-console.
"aboot> 1/kernels/generic.gz root=/dev/scd0 " loads the kernel-image and
I got Tux on the upper left corner. Now normaly I saw the boot-messages
on the screen below Tux. This time there is nothing. In the next 10-20 sec.
the led of cd-rom and harddisk is flashing but nothing happens. After few
minutes the led´s are flashing sometimes. If I now press ctrl+alt+del the
system goes down and reboot to SRM.
