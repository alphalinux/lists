On Tue, 18 Jul 2000 roberto@neron.smart.cl wrote:

> The fan that came out was a NIDEC TA3500 10cm big, which i replaced
> (discarding the plastic bracket) with a gigantic Sanyo Denki 12cm fan that
> barely fits nut cools the interior with grace .... after 10 houts of
> work , one can touch the processor .

Anothing option is to examine the current rating on the existing fan,
usually .12 - .16 amp, and try to find a fan of the same size but with a
higher rating.

> The only problem is the noise ... i'll drill some extra holes in the front
> plate to try to smoothen the airflow.

Apparently using rubber gromments where you attach the fan isolate the
fan's vibrations from the rest of the system and is supposed to reduce
noise somewhat. An alternative I've also heard of is a few small dabs of
silicone. There are a huge number of cooling hints and tips at
www.overclockers.com.
