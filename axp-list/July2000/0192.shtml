I have seen the very same problem on both an Alpastation
275 and 500. There seems to be no workaround. I went as
far as to try FreeBSD/AXP, with no results .

I have had very good experience with 3com 3c905B, Intel
EtherExpress Pro 100 B and Digital's own 21041 chipset
based cards. The 21041 based cards should be easy to
get on swap-meets and provide good performance.

Any specialist on Digital PC's will also sell you 21040 based
cards (10BT, 10B2 and AUI) for a song.

I think it's a very bad idea to put an ISA nic on a 21264, i
don't know how long it takes for the CPU to flush the pipelines
to serve ISA interrupts, but i would stick to the rule
of putting only "intelligent" bus mastering capable devices
on such deeply pipelined machines.
