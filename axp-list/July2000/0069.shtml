This is way after the fact, but I didn't see it come up
on this thread:

ext2 doesn't have a 2GB limitation even on 2.2 x86; the
2GB limitation comes from the VFS layer, and thus affects
all filesystems. Similarly, Alpha should enable >2GB on
all (reasonable) filesystems.
