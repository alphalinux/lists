I'm trying to get software raid running on some disks
and it keeps bailing with:

[root@wsb46 /root]# mkraid /dev/md0
handling MD device /dev/md0
analyzing super-block
disk 0: /dev/hde1, 60029928kB, raid superblock at 60029824kB
disk 1: /dev/hdg1, 60029928kB, raid superblock at 60029824kB
disk 2: /dev/hdi1, 60029928kB, raid superblock at 60029824kB
disk 3: /dev/hdk1, 60029928kB, raid superblock at 60029824kB
mkraid: aborted, see the syslog and /proc/mdstat for potential clues.

/proc/mdstat:

[root@wsb46 /root]# cat /proc/mdstat
Personalities : [1 linear] [2 raid0]
read_ahead not set
md0 : inactive
md1 : inactive
md2 : inactive
md3 : inactive

Nothing in /var/log/messages or dmesg output.

/etc/raidtab:

raiddev /dev/md0
        raid-level -1
        nr-raid-disks 4
        persistent-superblock 1

        device /dev/hde1
        raid-disk 0
        device /dev/hdg1
        raid-disk 1
        device /dev/hdi1
        raid-disk 2
        device /dev/hdk1
        raid-disk 3

The raidtab entry is taken from "man raidtab" and
slightly modified.

What did I do wrong?
