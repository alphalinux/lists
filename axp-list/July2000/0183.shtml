The problem with the Multia stems from the onboard TGA video. It seems SRM
looks for the TGA and has problems with installed video cards. Note, this
is a Multia/UDB issue. I have SRM running on my other boxes fine with
exotic cards. It's only the Multia and it's SRM that are giving me problems.
