Hi!
 I tried 2.2.x SMP kernels (2.2.14 and 2.2.16) on AlphaServer 2100 4/275
(Sable) 2xCPU 275MHz, 192MB RAM, NCR53C810, DEC Tulip.
 I found 2 strange messages:
1) The BogoMips. 1st CPU get 543.16, but the 2nd 8.39 only.
2) T2 machine check: OS-specific PAL bugcheck (retryable)...

Does anyone know, whether they are harmless or there is something wrong?
Note that I must enable 'PCI Quirks: Y', otherwise the kernel does not
found SCSI controller and panics (unable to mount root, of course).

Despite these messages the kernel seemes to work well...
