On Tue, Jul 25, 2000 at 11:47:19AM -0700, Jamesvi@asu.edu wrote:
> Borland just open sourced interbase. Would there be any advantage to spending
> mmy time trying to get this to compile and run on my PWS (miata) rather than
> MySQL? My particular paramteres are basically: secure, scalable, easy to
> write web-enabled access. Thanks for any input.
>

It has been a while since I used either of those databases, but my
impression of Interbase of VERY favorable. It is fast, small, and
robust. It has a concept of transactions which I don't think MySQL
has. I think it also has foreign keys and triggers as well as a
sqlplus (Oracle's scripting language) like language for creating
procedures and functions that are stored in the database. I would
say it is worth at least looking at.
