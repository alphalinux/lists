On Thu, Jul 13, 2000 at 08:08:41AM -0400, Rich Payne wrote:
>
> I don't have experience with those cards...perhaps somebody else on the
> list can comment on whether SRM sees those cards or not. I know that some
> it will use w/o any trouble, whereas others it will ignore. My guess would
> be that it's looking at the PCI vendor ID and if it doesn't match SRM
> preset list it just ignores the card.....but that's a guess.

I think this is an accurate assessment.

I tried one of these cards in an XP1000 with V5.7 SRM, and it didn't
recognize the card. It was fine after booting into LINUX, however,
but that doesn't help with BOOTP.

Best bet is either the DEC tulip cards (DE435/450/500) or Intel EEPro100
(82557/82558/82559?), the latter with more recent consoles.
