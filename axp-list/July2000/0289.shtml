> Apparently Compaq has received 64 bit "sledgehammer" chips from AMD and is
> planning on building servers with them. I wonder where this leaves Compaq
> in regards to the Alpha?

Sledgehammer, just like existing IA-32 chips, is significantly slower than
the Alpha on floating point. I doubt that Sledgehammer changes a thing.
Microsoft-written OSes certainly won't support Sledgehammer's 64 bit mode
anytime soon; no doubt Linux will the day it is released to consumers.
