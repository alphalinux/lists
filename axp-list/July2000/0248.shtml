Dear all,

I recently changed my old pas16 for a (slightly less old) Tropez+ sound
board. It seems to be a big mistake! Whatever i do.. problems, even
completely hanging the machine.
I have used the OSS support (as a module and in the kernel) as wel as alsa
but the results are the same. I can play 8bit files but 16 bit cd quality
files lock up the machine. mpg123 complains with things like:
can't rewind stream by 82 bits!
I tried both the pnp support that comes with the driver and isapnp but the
results are the same.

One thing i noticed is that the CS4232 chip on the Tropez+ only uses 8bit
dma (0--3) while i could use dma 5 (16 bit) on the PAS16... could this be
the problem? Any suggestions to get this to work? A pci card is no option
as all (3) the pci slots are in use.

Thanks --- Jaap
