I'm trying to get my Multia to "see" a video card (which
I put in the available PCI slot). Yes, I switched the
monitor over to this card. :-) I've tried the Matrox Mystique,
S3 968, and an S3 Virge/GX. All of these cards fit, and work fine
on other Alpha's I have running Linux.

In addition, the S3 968 is recognized by the ARC firmware on
the Multia...it's only the SRM firmware that won't recognize
it (ie. the monitor just sits in standbye mode and I never get
any text on the screen).

Has anyone gotten the Multia to recognize any non-TGA card under
SRM?

Note: Please, only answer if you've tried this on the Multia/UDB
and are using SRM. I don't care about the Noname, nor about
ARC/AlphaBIOS success stories.
