ppetrakis@alphalinux.org said:
> Does v3 has support for files larger than 4GB?

Yes, it does. Though I have not read files larger than 4GB (yet). But I did
read files larger than 2GB. I have a 2.4.0-test kernel. Works on x86 too...

You might want to get in touch with Trond Myklebust in case you suspect NFS
problems. He was very helpful in stomping out some NFS bugs I was having a
while back on large files over NFS...

How do you get the file on the alpha BTW ? If not via NFS, you might want to
check that the program on the Sun used to get it (i.e., ftpd) was compiled
with large files enabled...
