I won't reiterate my whining :-) I have the answer.

You really _do_ need an SRM-supported video card (as in not just an S3,
but an S3 with a supported bios onboard). What I saw on the serial
console was in fact ARC UI stuff. Once it was displayed on the native
console, it was trivial to revert back to SRM. Anyway, thanks John and
John, and many thanks Jim!

This might deserve a small comment in a FAQ somewhere, as the usual
"at the Apu-> prompt, type update srm" doesn't apply to a box that
has 'half-flash' and ARC loaded.
