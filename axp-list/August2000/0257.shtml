Having talked with Hedrick, this is a know problem with Maxtor. You
really need to go to the 2.2.16 Kernel with the patches. I had a
machine running 8 40GB drives, all Maxtor and I spoke with him and he
said that the latest 2.2.16 patch will fix 99% of the problems with
those drives. The other 1% had to do with software raids.

As far as partitioning, you will need to get a cylinder and head count
from the drive and use expert mode in fdisk to change what is reported.
This will allow you to get all space on the drive to show up.
