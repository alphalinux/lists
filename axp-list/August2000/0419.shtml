Here is the correct settings to add to /etc/glx.conf. I was going
from memory and just looked up what I actually had in my file:

mach64_dma=3
mach64_dmaadr=224
mach64_dmasize=32

You will need to set the last two depending on how much DMA memory
you reserved in /etc/aboot.conf.

I am going to probe further to see if DMA is actually being used,
and not just PIO. I *think* I saw in the log file though that DMA
was being taken advantage of, though by the speed I'm getting I'm
doubting it.
