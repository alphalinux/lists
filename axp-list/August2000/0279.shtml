Alvin Starr wrote:
>
> So are you saying that I can but both types of partitions on a single
> disk?(my impression is no).

Partitioning is determined by a table, and there is only one per disk,
so you can have on one disk only one kind but you may have multiple
disks with different types of partitioning.

The one on a boot device has to be of the kind which is acceptable
to your firmware - obviously enough. Other than that - feel free.
