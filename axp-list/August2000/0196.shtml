On Wed, Aug 16, 2000 at 01:18:37PM -0500, Brad Lucier wrote:
> I have a DS20 clone (264DP, Tsunami chip set, dual 500 MHz processors)
> running RedHat that I am quite happy with. I may have some money later
> this year for upgrades. I plan to upgrade the memory from 2 GB to 4 GB
> (no problem there), but I would also like to upgrade the CPUs.
>
> I'm asking for speculation here. Do people in this group think it will be
> possible to get 1 GHz+ upgrades for this machine in a January time frame?
> Or do people know that Compaq is, say, going with the UP2000 form factor
> with CPUs and I'll be out of luck?

Officially, there are no CPU upgrades for DS20/264DP.

CPU upgrades (to 667MHz, IIRC) are currently available for DS20E from
COMPAQ.

There may be plans by COMPAQ to go higher than 667MHz (perhaps to
833MHz) for DS20E, but there has been no official announcement, one
way or the other, AFAIK.

Would the DS20E daughtercards work in DS20/264DP? Probably.

Could you get COMPAQ support for that configuration? NO.

Sorry.
