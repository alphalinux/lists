On Sat, 5 Aug 2000, hari wrote:

> Can Anyone tell me how and configure and operate an HP 7500 Plus CD
> ReWriter in Red Hat Linux 6.2

        You must recompile your kernel with IDE-SCSI emulation
enabled. Then add to your boot options "hdx=ide-scsi" where hdx is
your cd writer. I use cdrecord and cdrdao to burn my data and audio
CDs.
