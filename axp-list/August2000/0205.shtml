My quest for a decent 24 bit frame buffer that works under Linux and Digital
Unix has turned out the following results :

The original Powerstorm 4D10 was a basic 3Dlabs Permedia2 based card, this
was replaced by Compaq with the Elsa Gloria Synergy, which is basically the
same thing.

However, users outside the US and Germany might find them rather difficult
to find.

I´ve verified the following cards to be a useful replacemnet.

Diamond FireGL 1000 PRO (PCI, 8MB)
Creative Labs Graphics Blaster Exxtreme Pro (PCI, 8MB)
(I paid aprox US$ 40 for each... seems to be a fair price)

Both go undetected by srm on my AS 500/333 but are correctly recognized
as comet0 by Digital Unix 4.0E and work ok in 16 and 24 bit color.

If you need documentation on how to enable the higher color dephts on Digital Unix,
let me know ....

They also have been proven by me to work under Linux and Xfree86 3.3.6 with no
config troubles.
