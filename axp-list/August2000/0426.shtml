As far as the API 1100 . . . Is anyone selling them? I have several SX boxes I
want to replace.

I've had some success with commenting out the prep and build directories of
some .spec files. You just have to remember to add things like cd
/usr/src/redhat/BUILD/app_path in the %install section. This works as long as
things set in %prep are not needed in the %install. Which means the the kde
source rpms can't work that way (they require several file settings).

I personally think the idea of adding -bb -ba -bs to the short-circuit of rpm is
great. Anybody else want to give it a shot? ;) For the people that have
worked on rpm it doesn't seem like that hard of a feature to add. (spoken
like a person who hasn't even looked at the source). Of course an
rpm -bb --continue_failed would be best.
