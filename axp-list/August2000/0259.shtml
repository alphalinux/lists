You can set the jumpers in the Multia to a lower resolution so the
TGA can sync with your monitor. Pull the board out and look to
the lower right quadrant (under where the PCI card would be). The
connectors on the board are to your right. You'll see 4 unmarked
jumpers. Set the two left most jumpers and you'll boot in
640x480 @ 60Hz. The lowest possible for the TGA. :-)

That should get you by...as for other options, I've tried these to
good effect in the Multia:

#9 Motion 771 (S3 chipset)
S3 Virge/GX
Matrox Mystique

Note that you'll be blind in SRM though. You have to move the monitor
over to TGA until everything is set up. Then just autoboot blind.
Works for me.
