I have one of the Onsale.com AS200 4/233. I went to install a memory
upgrade and then the thing wouldn't boot. I kept getting the 1-3-3 beep
code and finally discovered that one of the teeth in the 72 pin slots had
come out, stuck in the solder on the back of one of the old chips. So I'm
looking for a motherboard to replace this one. The part number is
54-23254-03. It's just a box I'm experimenting with so it's not critical
that I fix it, but if the price is right.
