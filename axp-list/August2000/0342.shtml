Matt Wilson: [mailto:msw@redhat.com] wrote:

>Everyone enjoy! Let me know if you find apps that really hose up
>under Tru64 compatibility. We know that Netscape + Java == hang in 30
>seconds due to some threading conflicts, and that's going to be quite
>hard to fix.

Applications which have been binary translated (via mx or FreePort Express)
partially work under Linux/Alpha when the relevant libraries are also
installed, but not all. Signal handling seems to be problematic, and
there are some colormap problems. Its probably just a 'small' matter
of someone fixing up the sigcontext crud in the runtimes ;-)

Thinking along those lines, anyone interested in putting together a
Slowaris (SPARC V9) fast interpreter and runtime library? I wouldn't
mind taking Sun on again with one of those.
