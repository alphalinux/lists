This card seems to be bad news for many (all?) LX164's, though it works
fine on many other Alpha's. Back when this was first discovered (about
1.5 years ago) it was reported that the es1371 played fast and loose with
the PCI spec, and that this was what caused the lockups.

Interestingly enough, the same card has problems with a few i386 MB's
as well (such as the ABIT BP6), but works fine in the overwhelming
majority of i386 systems.

My solution was to get a relatively cheap ISA card (a Turtle Beach Malibu).
Even the archaic ISA bus has plenty of bandwidth for 2x44.1KS/s audio.
It's not like there is much else you'd want to put in those ISA slots,
and it lets you use a PCI slot for something else...

                -Ed
