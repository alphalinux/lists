hardware:
-164SX with 128MB
-Eicon DIVA PCI ISDN card

software:
-Distribution: Redhat 5.2
-Compiler: EGCS 1.1.2
-Kernel: 2.2.5-ac4 with richard's patch applied
-ISDN and ISDN4k-utils: version 990413 from ftp://ftp.suse.com
-a few scripts taken from intel suse and modified to my needs
