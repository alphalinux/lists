Do you have a domain set up? This sounds like the behavior when you have
an MX record for your domain that does not point to your computer.
Outside email never reaches this computer because it is not directed
there. Inside mail will reach the recipients because sendmail thinks it
is the domain it is sending to. Does that fit?
