Jeff,
 I was just looking last weekend on ATI's at the details on this card. They say the TV out will work in Linux or OS/2 or any operating system. You only get their fancy control panel if you use Windows. Otherwise the card looks for a load on the TV out line when the system is started and displays there if it finds one. There was only one resolution available on those OSes other than windows.
