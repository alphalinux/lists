>
> I am running 2.2.6 on an UX2. No known kernel problems until now. BUT
> tulip.c is a problem for me and many other UX users.

Build-in tulip on UX is indeed a PITA. It is quirky. On some boards
it does work, on other it refuses to cooperate. Also there is a bunch
of UX boards out there which appear to have an incorrect crystal
for ethernet (wrong type).

The current version of a tulip driver is 0.91 and it seems to be
a bit better. If your built-in ethernet really does not want to
work the best solution I know at this moment is to add an extra
ethernet card (even tulip and they can be found rather cheap) and
use that instead. These problems are not likely to change with
a kernel version. The driver is exactly the same one in 2.0
and 2.2 kernels and you can always drop in another version of it
without affecting anything else.

On eb64+ I do have have a tulip card, but with 21140 chip.
Currently one can see it as:

tulip.c:v0.91 4/14/99 becker@cesdis.gsfc.nasa.gov
eth0: Digital DS21140 Tulip rev 34 at 0x8000, 00:60:67:32:6B:E9, IRQ 18.

and it works very well. OTOH this does not say very much as this
particular tulip card never had any hiccups with any driver from
0.89h up.
