You probably also have a modem on COM1. MILO echoes to COM1, and
accepts input either from the keyboard or COM1. When the modem sees
'a' it determines the baud rate, and begins echoing. MILO echoes, the
modem echoes....

Move the modem to COM2 and this problem should go away.
