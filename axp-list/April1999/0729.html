Hello;

> I'm just a bit frazzled at the moment and looking for solace. Should I
> try to get this working with 2.2, or is it a known problem that 2.2 and
> alpha just don't mix right now?

I do not have a UDB, but...

I run RH5.2 with kernel 2.2.5 ("stock" sources - no more special alpha
patches anymore) with no problems on a bunch of 164LX boxes and an
AS200 4/233; all monolithic kernels, though, and I don't use IP
aliasing or masquerading.

In going to 2.2 from 2.0 you must upgrade various packages - there are
instructions on redhat's errata page and at linuxhq.com, for example,
on what needs updating. It is worth checking that you did not miss
any of these...

Many have noted recently that 2.2.6 has some problem with modules -
you can check the archives for details on this. As a solution, you
may want to back down to 2.2.5 (or try 2.2.6 w/out modules); I can't
recall off of the top of my head if there was a patch to fix the
modules problem in 2.2.6; would be in the archives, though.

But in my experience, 2.2.x on alpha is pretty great. :-)
