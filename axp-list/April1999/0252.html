This becomes a FAQ so once more, for the archives:

if your xntpd stopped working complaining about some
value being too large, you need to get the sources and
recompile. Everything is dandy after that. I personally
got xntp3-5.93-export.tar.gz and xntp3-5.93[a,b,c,d,e].diff
and happily run a 5.93e here now. Try www.ntp.org for
sources, or an archive of your choice. No guarantees for
other versions. (None for this one either ;)
