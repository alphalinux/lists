24 Meg is not enough for the latest Red Hat images... 32 Meg should
be fine, if you can boost the memory up you should be fine...

If I can assume for a minute, your Multia/UDB probably has 2 12 Meg
"special" SIMMs, that means you can simply add 2 4 Meg SIMMs
and finish your install. I bet that if you post a request here you will
find someone that has a couple of 4 Meg SIMMs they'd sell cheap,
since most AS200s and other low-end Alphas came with these
"valuable" upgrades...

Good luck, HTH,
