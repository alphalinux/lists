Sounds to me like to plugged the cable in wrong -- and sent the power
pins to data lines.. you probably killed the drive with that.

I've never seen a definative statement on how the hell this is supposed
to work -- how is an IDE drive supposed to be plugged into the UDB MB?

I fried a 340M drive before getting a 2.2g working -- which has now been
removed due to heat problems ... I've gone back to SCSI with an external
box and tacked on a CDROM drive as well.

I should have made a sketch of the cabling and drive before I yanked it
all apart, but I didn't think of it at the time.

IIRC, the case that screwed the 340M drive was with the drive sitting
upright in the bay and the cable going straight through to the MB.

Anyone have a pin out for the Multia's IDE connector?
