Actually, if these are the same ones as I have purchased in the past (as far as
I can tell they are) then they work perfectly fine with a standard ATX power
supply. There is a soft power pin on the motherboard that you plug into the
power button, and it works great.

The only downside is the ports on the back are in a non-standard configuration,
and hence don't work with the standard back plates you get with an ATX case.

Brock
