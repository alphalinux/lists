On Thu, 1 Apr 1999, Chris Price wrote:

> I may have had 'no worries' as I used two cards that are specifically
> supported by the AlphaBios. I wonder what a NCR/SymBios 53c875 card
> would work like in my system config..?? (spaking of which, what cards
> ARE specifically supported by the AlphaBios?).

I have a Tekram 53C875 based card in my XL300, along with the built-in
53C810, and I haven't had any problems. One interesting thing is that
AlphaBIOS sees both controllers, but no partitions on the second bus
(Tekram) drive.

Redhat 5.2 and Rawhide installed with no hitches, with my root partition
on sda2, and MILO on sda1.
