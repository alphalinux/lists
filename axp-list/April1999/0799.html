The last time I looked at isapnp documentation it clearly stated
that isapnp has to initialize resources __before__ you attempt to
load device drivers. So, either you have to load them as modules
or you have to load your kernel via initrd doing isapnp from a ram
disk before reloading your "true" kernel with monolithic drivers.

I load PnP sound as a bunch of modules on my Intel machine and this
works just fine.
