There was a fix for this in 2.2.5-ac3. Unfortunately there was
something else broken in ac3 and ac4 on Alpha (n_tty.c), that in turn
broke most remote access programs (in.telnetd, sshd, etc).

I haven't had a chance to test the newest ac patches to see if this
problem was resolved, but.. if it's not.. removing the modifications
to n_tty.c in the patch, seems to have worked for me as temporary fix
on my 164SX.
