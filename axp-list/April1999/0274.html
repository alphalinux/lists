You're out of luck! L-(
The rpm for 8.9.3 is compiled under Rawhide (the experimental distro)
using glibc2.1. I don't think _ANYONE_ is using glibc2.1 under
production systems! (If you do, I congratulate you for being a total
nutter!! ;-)

Anyways, just grab:
ftp://rpmfind.net/linux/rawhide/1.0/SRPMS/SRPMS/sendmail-8.9.3-7.src.rpm

Then, do a: # rpm --rebuild sendmail-8.9.3-7.src.rpm
and that's it, you got a new rpm!
