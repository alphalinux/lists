I have seen mechanisms that have an additional circuit on the fan that can
be connected to a pin pair on a serial port. A friend of mine wrote a
small spec for a "serial port sensor" mechanism that would incorporate
UPS/cooling fan/etc failure detection.

The APS UPS's use this method. 3 pins are used to indicated hi/lo power
and failure.

This could be implemented in a very similar fashion to a serial mouse driver.
