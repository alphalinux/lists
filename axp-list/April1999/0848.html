> Best Power had always a good attitude and was helpful to Linux users

Best Power has excellent tech support also. I had a problem with
one of their batteries after a few years' use, and told them
the manual said it should only need replacing after 3-5. They
just mailed me a new one. I packed up the old unit in the box
and mailed it back. No hassle, just an e-mail.

They also ship the source code for their Unix drivers, as well
as pre-compiled binaries for popular platforms. The drivers
work very well. Nice hardware (my Patriot Pro keeps up my power-
sucking setup where cheaper models (APC) who claimed the same
Volt/Amp ratings just fell right over).
