Ron Farrer wrote:
>
> onboard NCR SCSI with Kernel 2.0.35.

And which NCR is that?

> Are there better NCR SCSI drivers in
> newer kernels? Using a DEC TZ87 on a AS200 showed similar problems...

There is bunch of "new and improved" drivers on
ftp://ftp.tux.org/pub/roudier/. They will work both with older and
newer kernels. Gerard put up some "bundles" with complete patches
for some 2.0 and 2.2 kernels but there are not really some particular
version bound. This is just a convenience. I used various these
drivers with different 2.0 and 2.2 kernels and they simply work.
What you have in a "standard 2.0.35" is quite obsolete by now.

Depending on your board you may want to investigate sym53c8xx driver
instead of ncr53c8xx one.
