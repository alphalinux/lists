Stewart,

I'm not sure what your problem was but I also had trouble with AlphaBIOS and
milo on my Pws433a, also a Miata.

My first step was to upgrade the AlphaBIOS which can be found at
http://www.compaq.com/support/files/alphant/firmware/

Then, after several failed attempts to load milo from the cd miata image, I
downloaded a test version from
ftp://ftp.digital.com/pub/DEC/Linux-Alpha/Kernels/Test-Milos-981008/miata
which worked fine. (i can't remember whether I replaced the image on the
floppy with this or rawrite'd it out - either way it worked.

Good luck,

Mark
