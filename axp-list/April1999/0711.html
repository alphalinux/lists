On Mon, 26 Apr 1999 20:41:38 -0400, "Jeff Simpson" <djali@fiji.crosswinds.net> said:
> Any suggestions for installing XFree86 with a Compaq
> QVision? (DECpc AXP 150 EISA video). I can't even get it to work
> with Generic VGA/16 colors. Thanx!

As stated in the FAQ, the QVision is still not supported in the
Jensen. If you want to change that, look at the patches for the S3
server which made it work on the Jensen (they are available at my web
page) and adopt them to the VGA/16 server.

But since the QVision isn't supported by XFree other than with the
VGA/16 server, that's probably pointless since 640x480/16 is probably
as good as a text console ...
