I recently wrote that I could not get my AS200 to boot with the Matrox
Mystique 220 card. I now can say that was wrong. Apparently, my
problems was my version of firmware when I first got my machine. Now
that I am running the latest firmware I am running it fine. I ditched
the #9 GXE64 Pro and have had no lockups in XFree86 since I installed
the Matrox card.
