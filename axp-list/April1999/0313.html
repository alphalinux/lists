OK, it is time for the "Usual Adaptec Statement":

Turn off "Auto-termination"
Use REAL active terminators on ends of the cables.
Do NOT use the terminators on the drive ( jumper enabled)
Finally, consider a better SCSI card.
A note from a customer, which I got this last week:

"..we had serious SCSI troubles with the
machines.....we discovered that the problems were related
with intensive usage of two disks with an Adaptec controller, and that
they disappeared entirely by using Intraserver controllers. So we put
Intraservers on our four servers and they run very nicely since then."

Please note: We are NOT stating this as an advert for Intraserver.
We DO use their cards, but they don't own us and we don't have shares in
them.
Their products DO work, and their support is good.
