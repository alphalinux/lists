FYI: I bid and won also; about 60 bidders with about 200 units up for "Dutch Auction"; I think these units require a special power supply for the ATX Chassis, available at http://www.omnistep.com/~advantag/pwrsup.htm (no affiliation!) they sell for $95 - 300w modified. The auction seller only offers 7 day DOA return policy; I emailed an got a reply which didn't answer question about special required Power Supply or extending DOA return. So if you did bid - or do when the other 140 or so show up again, you might want to consider this too. Actually, I think the stock power supply can work, but only by plugging in & unplugging AC cord.
