Given the number of people complaining about sound
not working correctly, I kind of doubt it's a HW
problem. More likely device drivers are having problems
of some sort, or, configuring sound is very touchy.
I don't use sound so I can't offer any advice on what
to look for.

Last two times I had flakey stuff going on with the kinds
of symptoms being described, it was the graphics card driver (X)
mucking with my network card and a kernel bug in handling paging,
not the HW.

Although, to fix the graphics problem, I did switch to a new
card instead of chasing why that particular card had problems.
