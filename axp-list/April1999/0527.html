> I am having a hard time compiling a new kernel for my Multia.(RH5.2 W/
> 2.0.35)... As per my other 2 Intel machines, make Xconfig, make dep and make
> clean work as they should. But when I make zdisk or make zImage it returns a
> single line with "*stop" at the end (I am not close enough to the machine to
> give a verbose listing)

I wouldn't use xconfig on Alpha, it's know to produce strange results, use
just a make config instead.

>
> Am I missing a flag for the make zdisk that is not required on the Intel
> machines?

Try make boot instead, this will leave you with a compressed kernel image
in /usr/src/linux-2.0.35/arch/alpha/boot

> The readme in the /usr/src/linux-2.0.35 and all other docs I have read seem to
