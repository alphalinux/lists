> OK, it is time for the "Usual Adaptec Statement":
>
> Turn off "Auto-termination"
> Use REAL active terminators on ends of the cables.
> Do NOT use the terminators on the drive ( jumper enabled)

Also, keep in mind there are folks that have no serious
troubles with Adaptec. I own 3 cards and all work
without a hitch. The only caveat I have about them is
that the aic7xxx driver is a work in progress. You have
to find a version that works with your card. Some do,
some don't. If you want something that is usually trouble
free and requires little hunting for a working driver version,
then get a Symbios(aka NCR) based card like the 810/815/875
etc. Although, I did read somewhere along the line recently
even that driver had hiccups.
