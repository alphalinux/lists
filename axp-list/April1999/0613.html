I had similar memory problems with my LX board with the Pyxsis? chipset.
Being that I work for Compaq's Alpha division, I went and asked about the
issue. The problem is loading on the memory wires. The chipset doesn't
drive the JEDEC standard and that is why when you put 2 in its ok, but with
4 its shaky. I would say the less chips the better, but it will be fun
trying to get 1GB of RAM in that board. Ask the manufacturer what memory
sticks are verified to work and go buy those.

-Brian
