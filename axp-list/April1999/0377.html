>
> How can I get ALL trap messages into a log instead of to the console?

>
> *.* /var/log/all_messages
>

Depends on your intent here. Are you just trying to
keep them off the system console, or, are you collecting
them so you can debug the problem? If it's for debug,
you can easily retrieve the messages using dmesg and get
them that way. If it's just to remove them, I'd suggest
fixing the print in the kernel and build a new one.
