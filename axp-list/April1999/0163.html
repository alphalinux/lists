Andrew,

Did you install the firmware from the PC164 in which the card is
installed?

This may not be too relevent, but I saw this with my PCI-64 and a KZPSA
which had previously been in an AS 200/233:

Every time I moved the card between the PCI-64 and the AS 200/233, the
disks on the card were not visible to the BIOS (either ARC or SRM).
Diagnostics showed everything was correct; versions, POST, etc. When
booting off of a different adapter, the OS's could see the disks, but
they were 'offline'.

To solve this, I re-flashed the BIOS, and everything was happy again. I
don't pretend to know whether this is an EPROM issue or static or
anything else;
it's just a waorkaround that might be worth trying.

...Mike Pearl
