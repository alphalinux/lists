depends on kernel IDE-SCSI emulation, I compiled that as a module. Things
seem fine, the CD drives get recognized as SCSI devices. However, when I
tried to use the actual SCSI device (/dev/scd0 for example) the machine
reboots immediately, jumps back to MILO and stays locked there.
