Just a quick follow up regarding the problems I had using a Promise
Ultra 100 TX2 controller.

I managed to borrow the Promise Ultra 66 controller and it just works
(with one minor problem I'll go into below). I've managed to swap the
100 TX2 for this one so both my friend and I are happy (he's going to
use it under Windows 2000).

The problem I have is when I shut the box down in that it hangs just
after the section where the disks are unmounted. I've checked and I can
manually dismount the IDE disk without problem. Any suggestions on how
to debug this would be appreciated - nothing appears in
/var/log/messages. Something may be scheduled to be written in the log
but I have to halt the system and they may not get written.
