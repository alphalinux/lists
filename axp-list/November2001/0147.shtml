>
> The problem I have is when I shut the box down in that it hangs just
> after the section where the disks are unmounted. I've checked and I can
> manually dismount the IDE disk without problem. Any suggestions on how
> to debug this would be appreciated

Turn on magic-sysrq support (assuming that available in a kernel
you are using) by 'echo 1 > /proc/sys/kernel/sysrq'. To make that
survive reboots edit /etc/sysctl.conf if you have such file.
Read documentation (in kernel-doc and/or in kernel-source packages).
You are most likely interested in a task list.

> - nothing appears in
> /var/log/messages.

It will show up afterwards, even if not on a console, but you likely
want to force sync before shutting everything down.
