If he is indeed talking about the KZPSA adapter (the wide differential one
with an Intel i960 chip on it - full length), no, it's not the MYLEX RAID
controller. It's a strange DEC contraption (IIRC), and when I mentioned
opening the specs for someone to write an Open Source driver to a person
that lived through getting the adapter working on Tru64, his eyes didn't
just roll back - I think they made a couple of loops around.

Apparently it has some rather nasty gotchas that made debug a real bear,
and didn't exactly make anyone want to work on it once it was working. I
can check again inside about making the specs available (I believe it's an
EOL adapter, so that _shouldn't_ be too far-fetched), but first someone
who actually _wants_ to write a driver for this adapter and has the skills
to would have to step forward. Anyone up for this kind of pain? :)
