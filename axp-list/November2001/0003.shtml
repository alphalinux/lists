On Thu, Nov 01, 2001 at 09:42:59AM +0100, Peter Bech wrote:
>
> 1. Cannot upgrade to RH7.1
> 2. Downloadable RPM's for RH 6.2 does not work on RH 6.2
>
> What do you do? Crap Linux and go back to Wintel?

1. Download all the updates for RedHat 6.2 Alpha
2. Upgrade rpm, glibc and the kernel in that order
     (remember to rebuild your database before and after upgrading rpm
      and read the HOWTO before upgrading the kernel)
3. Apply all other updates, including netscape.

[seyman@munshine seyman]$ rpm -q redhat-release
redhat-release-6.2-1
[seyman@munshine seyman]$ rpm -qa | grep netscape
netscape-common-4.77-0.6.2
netscape-communicator-4.77-0.6.2

I'ld also grab the .src.rpm for Mozilla 0.9.5 from the Mozilla
ftp site and rebuild it if I were you.
