You can span the whole disk with c, you just have to remember its
purpose is for dumps of a whole system disk. You will need to use
"swriteboot -f3" to tell it to ignore the fact that the boot partition
and the c partition overlap, per "man swriteboot". Also if you look at
one of the console outputs at the end of the install you will see a
message about swriteboot failing due to overlap.
