> > "This is the result of a buggy SRM, and unfortunately, it isn't fixed in the
> > final digital SRM release for the UDB.

> > advanced properly. ....

> Well, that *used* to be true; current 2.4.x kernels have the appropriate

True, but installation CDs and floppies have a kernel based on 2.4.3 and
I doubt if that one is "current" in the sense above. Those kernels have
other problems as well and should be immediately replaced by newer ones
from updates to 7.1, or by something custom, but this is not a great
help in first moments unless one is able, and willing, to hack boot
floppies.

I have no idea if the fix you mention is incorporated in current 7.1 RH
update kernels for alpha ("...9241495 Nov 1 12:58 kernel-2.4.9-12.alpha.rpm"
on a mirror I am checking this and there is also "kernel-BOOT-2.4.9-12",
quite a bit smaller, there) but I am not aware of updated images for
boot floppies.
