I just upgraded some nodes from linux-2.2.19 to
2.4.12. My NFS performance has going down significantly
due to this. The performance hit is only on XP1000's
with the DE500 ethernet card. I do not have the problem
with UP2000's with the DE600 (eepro100) ethernet cards.

I am using the de4x5 driver and not the tulip driver.
I have verified that the card is in 100Mb/s, full duplex.

I tried running netperf with UDP from an XP1000 to
a 'good' node. Netperf is reporting that I am seeing
30 Mb/s. From the UP2000 netperf is reporting > 90 Mb/s.
Netperf show good performance using TCP (> 90 Mb/s).

My NFS servers are UDP, so this is a big problem.
I am trying the 2.4.14 kernel, but I have not seen anything
that would indicate this is the fix.

Any suggestions?

Craig
