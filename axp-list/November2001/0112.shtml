The fix appears to have gone into official 2.4.10. That doesn't
necessarily mean that RH has or has not ported that back into
2.4.9-whatever - you'd have to scan their sources. The pertinent
file is arch/alpha/kernel/traps.c, look for "opDEC_testing".

And you're right, of course, re: the updated boot images for floppies,
I don't know if RH does that as a matter of course. It's not too hard
to take a new kernel and put it into/onto a floppy, but it does require
a machine already running Linux, in most cases... ;-}
