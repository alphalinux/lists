I think I can reproduce your problem: When I have LABEL=/ in (stock RH7.1)
fstab, and boot from milo, df tells me /dev/sda2 is mounted as root, despite
/dev/sdc3 (the correct one from the kernel param "root=/dev/sdc3") is
actually mounted (!!). Just take a look at the partition sizes. Needless to
say, this behaviour is quite dangerous..
Om my machine, the fix was easy: I replaced all the LABEL=.. stuff in fstab
by the proper /dev/sd.. designators.
