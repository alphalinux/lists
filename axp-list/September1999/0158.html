With regards to your message at 07:58 PM 9/11/99 -0700, Designer Seven.
Where you stated:
>Hi, hoping for some help here....
>
>I just tried using an ELSA Gloria XL on my Alpha with
>the XFree 3DLabs X server... and it ended up telling
>me that no GLINT/Permedia based card found. I had this
>same problem with an STB GLyder GOLD.
>
>Can someone confirm that ELSA Gloria XL doesn't work
>or does work on Alpha? The XFree86 sites claims that
>it is supported.
>
>If I can't get these two video cards to work, I'm
>going to sell them... so if anyone is interested:
>
>STB GLyder GOLD 8MB+8MB PCI : $100
>ELSA Gloria XL 16MB+24MB PCI: $250
They may work with XFree, but it is unlikely that the AlphaBIOS emulation
for video is compatible with them.
One card that does work is the Elsa Gloria "Synergy" . Compaq calls this a
4D10T and it is supported in both Alphabios and SRM (current versions).
