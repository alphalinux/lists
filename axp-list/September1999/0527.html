WASHINGTON (AP) - In the weeks before the start of the Microsoft Corp.
(Nasdaq:MSFT - news) antitrust trial, the company's lawyer elicited a

September over claims that the company held monopoly power because its

``Apple, Unix,'' Fisher answered. ``There are a couple of other minor ones.
