Well, to each his own. I have Adaptecs that work just fine.
As has been discussed previously, Adaptecs can have troubles
with auto-termination and in general you should use active
terminators (no matter what chip set you use). I've also found
some versions of the Adaptec driver work for my cards and some
don't so you have to do a bit of looking to find one thats
stable for your cards. The symptom I see when I don't use a
compatible driver is the timeouts you describe.
