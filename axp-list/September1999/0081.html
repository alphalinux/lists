On Thu, 9 Sep 1999, MERANTE, FRANK wrote:

> Hi there, just looking for a little help :)
>
> I've been trying to install RH6 on my alpha the last few days. I have a
> few problems.
>
> 1. I can't boot linux from the arc console without putting the kernel
> diskette in. I made a boot selection for MILO but it won't load the
> kernel. So, I make due by typing the following; boot fd0:vmlinux.gz
> root=/dev/sda3 load_ramdisk=1 Can someone tell me how to configure arc
> to boot linux from the drive?

Suppose your linload.exe and milo reside in the DOS partition sda1. You
arc setting should be like:
SYSTEMPARTITION=scsi(0)disk(0)rdisk(0)partition(0)
OSLOADER=scsi(0)disk(0)rdisk(0)partition(1)\linload.exe
OSLOADPARTITION=scsi(0)disk(0)rdisk(0)partition(1)
OSLOADOPTIONS=boot sda3:vmlinux.gz root=/dev/sda3
