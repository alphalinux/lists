No, it means whoever wrote libproc is an idiot. In one place they scale
HZ by the number of processors on the system (presumably so that top's
percentages come to 100 not N*100), and in another place they sanity check
the HZ value against one of the "known" values.

You can see the same effect on Intel and Sparc hardware.
