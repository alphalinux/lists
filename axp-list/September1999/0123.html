    You have to create a small FAT partition and save milo in it in order
to boot from hard disk. For information about it, you can visit my
webpage. After that, you should change your arc setting.

    Suppose your linload.exe and milo reside in the DOS partition sda1,
and your root partition is sda3. Your arc setting should be like:

SYSTEMPARTITION=scsi(0)disk(0)rdisk(0)partition(0)
OSLOADER=scsi(0)disk(0)rdisk(0)partition(1)\linload.exe
OSLOADPARTITION=scsi(0)disk(0)rdisk(0)partition(1)
OSLOADOPTIONS=boot sda3:vmlinux.gz root=/dev/sda3
