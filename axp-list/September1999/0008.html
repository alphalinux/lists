On Tue, Sep 07, 1999 at 03:59:44PM -0400, James Peret wrote:
> Has anyone had any experience getting Iomega Jaz drives (either 1Gb or
> 2Gb) working under alphalinux (5.1, 5.2 or 6.0)?

While I don't have my jaz on my alpha, it should work fine.
 
> If not, what other removeable media is available?

Zip, LE-120 (or whatever those big disks that can also read floppies
are called), and various types of M/O disks work fine AFAIK. You need
to have the right modules compiled (ppa/imm in the case of zip,
ide/atapi floppy in the case of LE-120.

None of this should be arch-dependant.
