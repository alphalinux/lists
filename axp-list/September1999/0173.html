With regards to your message at 03:59 PM 9/7/99 -0400, James Peret. Where
you stated:
>Has anyone had any experience getting Iomega Jaz drives (either 1Gb or
>2Gb) working under alphalinux (5.1, 5.2 or 6.0)?
>
>If not, what other removeable media is available?

JAZ works fine.
2 things I had to do:
look in source for a ROM string with something like JAZ 1.0 and change it
to JAZ 2.0 for 2GB drives.
Figure out how to unlock new cartridges which are software write-protected.
grep through for the "magic string" which is something like :
"A Place to Keep Your Stuff".

Finally, if using an external, and it is the last device on the chain if
you turn it off it will not provide termination power and SCSI termination
will fail. Either leave it powered all the time, or chain something else to
the bus which provides term power and termination all the time
