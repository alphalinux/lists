anyway, while I'm wasting bandwidth:

bladeenc imo really sucks. i used to use it, and i was happy with it. i
thought the distortions (warbles almost) it produced in mp3's were just an
unavoidable fact of mp3 life. Then i tried the Lame iso patches.... so so so
much better. The quality is nearly as good as CD.

Lame also actually compiles on Alpha.. latest bladeenc didn't for me. Which
means you can try out Compaq libcpml.a and ccc to gain some speed.

So bladeenc users, search for lame on freshmeat, and give it a try.
