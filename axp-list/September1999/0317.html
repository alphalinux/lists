Qlogic QLA1020. Methinks under Digital UNIX (Tru 64) 5.0, QLA1040, 1080
and the newer Qlogic U3 controllers are supported. QLA1020 is a SCSI II
Fast/Wide controller with excellent performance and a fully programmable
(tweakable) EEPROM.
