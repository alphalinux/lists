Has anyone attempted to upgrade the cpu on an alphastation 200? What would
be problematic? Chipset incompatibilities with alpha platform? (i.e.
21064/21164 or ev4/5 inc)

The cpu is in a ZIF socket and the oscillator for it is right next to it.
It would be a simple matter to drop in another chip and osc package.

FYI the osc is 2x speed, as my current example is indicated as "466MHz" for
an alpha cpu of 233MHz
