make sure that they aren't both set to master or slave....
sounds like this is the most likely cause of this
usually a good way to do this is put the hdd as master, and put the cdrom
drive as slave
-I am also a believer in NEVER using CS (cable select) if your drives have
that option. messes up too much for me

you may also want to try putting each on it's own controller. if you do this
it makes no difference which one is where
milo may fail to boot due to the added hard drive becuase of it's
configuration in alphaBIOS. it may be one drive letter off now
