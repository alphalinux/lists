tramposch wrote:
>
> can any one tell me what video card other than a matrox millenium II
> with work in a Cabriolet.

Most anything reasonable. From what I tried myself various S3,
different cards from ATI Mach64 series (older and "Xpert 98/Rage"
class), Permedia cards like ELSA Gloria, other cards with SVGA driver.
Not something which require 32-bit BIOS emulation like TNT and, of
course, not AGP cards.

> Also, does any one know if a 3com 3c905B-tx
> fastetherlink 100 Mbps ethernet card will work in this machine.

If you have Linux driver then it should work. Unless you are booting
over a network then you do not have problem if firmware does recognize
it. I do not think that I ever played with this card.
