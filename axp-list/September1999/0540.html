It is Jensen specific, see Martin Osterman's very useful Jensen site,
specifically, the section on /sbin/clock:
http://www.comnets.rwth-aachen.de/~ost/alpha/jensen.html#clock

I installed his RPM on my Jensen with RH6.0 and it solved the clock/date
problem.
