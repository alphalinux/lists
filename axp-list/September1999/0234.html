OK. You need to tell us a little more about your card etc before we
can really be of any help. Specifically,

1)did your card work once and now doesn't?

2)What type is your card?

3)Is it pci or isa?

4)Are you using the kernel that comes with your distribution or have
you compiled your own?

5)Can you give us the output of "dmesg | grep eth" and/or
"grep eth /var/log/boot.log"

With this sort of info, I'm sure someone will be able to help you.
