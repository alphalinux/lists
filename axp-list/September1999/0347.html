wrong.) I had exactly the same error message from aboot when I switched
from my old 4gb drive to the new 9gb drive. The exact aboot-kernel-disk,
that worked on the old one produced the unzip:failure only on the 9gb, it
worked like a charm on the 4gb. So either get another harddisk or cop out
and get a working kernel on a readabnle partition (best under the 2gb
limit at least for old aboot)
