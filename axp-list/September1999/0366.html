These lines should be added to /etc/conf.modules.

alias ppp-compress-21 ppp_deflate
alias ppp-compress-24 bsd_comp
alias ppp-compress-26 equ

Sean

P.S. Try "modprobe -c" sometime. It prints out a pretty good
approximation of /etc/conf.modules. You'll probably want to cut all
the "path" lines tho.
