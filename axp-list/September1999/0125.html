I used to have one of these. They will run D/Unix ( now TRU64 ), OpenVMS,
WindowsNT, and Linux. They are also known as a "Jensen" box.

150 MHz 21064
7 EISA slots.
0 ( none/ nada ) PCI
SRM and ARC both in flash

The Jensen was the first of the NT capable Alphas, and is the only NT
capable Alpha that does NOT have any PCI slots.
