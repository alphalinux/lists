My alpha has an on-board symbios sym53c875 scsi controller. I have
four harddrives in there, 3 of which I have in a raid5 partition. It
works fantastically well. Recently I did a test of contiuous parallel
builds of 2.2, 2.3 and mozilla. For a week load avg was constantly
between 25 and 110, and all the disks were whirring, but I had no
problems at all.
