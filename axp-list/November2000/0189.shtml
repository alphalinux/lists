I know I can't distribute RPMS of qmail but I can sure make SRPMS :-).
The basis for the creation of the RPMS came from the
Qmail-VMailMgr-Courier-imap-HOWTO, but
with all the packages updated to their latest versions.

In,

ftp://ftp.alphalinux.org/pub/contrib/internet/qmail-complete/

You'll find everything you need to have a build a complete qmail setup with
vhosts and all
the extras. Simply folow the directions ,
ftp://ftp.alphalinux.org/pub/contrib/internet/qmail-complete/DIRECTIONS.
There is also support for SSL pop3 and imap or whatever you want
really since stunnel can encrypt anything you tell it to. See
www.stunnel.org for details.

All of the rpms are ccc & cxx compiled.

enjoy!
