With regards to your message at 02:53 PM 11/6/00, Vanco, Donald. Where you
stated:
>Cooling dual 833s in 1U should be interesting as well...
>
>Don
In our experience you need at least 45cfm of air moved per CPU with the
UP2000 slot modules.
As one can fit at most a 40mm fan standing up, and they generally can move
up to 9cfm, so doing the math this takes at least 6 fans. Add to that
cooling for boards, RAM, chipset, and hard disk. So about 9 fans.
  Or, you can go to a blower for the CPUs.
This would help by bringing in higher pressure air to flow through the CPU
heatsinks.
In either case, it is certainly not an easy task, and I commend them for
the efforts!
