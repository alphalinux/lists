> Also, the CS20 has a single PCI slot available so if that's

There's 3 differnet things being announced here. First is the UP2000+
motherboard, it's basically and improved UP2000. The second is the 833MHz,
4MB DDR slotBs (which you can only use in a UP2000+ I might add, please
don't put them into a UP2000). There are no 667/750 DDR SlotBs.

The third is the CS20, which is a new motherboard, similar to the
UP2000(+). The CPUs are soldered to the board and it uses an ALi
southbridge instead of the Cypress on the 2K, plus it has onboard Utlra-3
SCSI (LSI) instead of adaptec, 2-64 bit PCI slots......and comes with
either dual 667 4MB (DDR) CPUs, or single or dual 833 4MB (DDR) slotBs.

Does that help?
