On Wed, Nov 22, 2000 at 10:37:49AM -0500, Robert Williams wrote:
> I tried pre11 yesterday on my UP1000,
....
> I checked /dev/hda with
> fdisk and saw that there were no partitions
> declaired.

Most likely you forgot to configure

CONFIG_MSDOS_PARTITION=y

and/or

CONFIG_OSF_PARTITION=y

"dot-config" files for 2.2 and 2.4 are not exactly the same even
if similar.

OTOH just yesterday Ivan Kokshaysky posted on linux-kernel, and also on
this list, a series of patches for 2.4.0-test11 which affect how PCI
(bridges, in particular) behave. These may affect also what happens
on your machine even if some of these were Pyxis, or even Ruffian,
specific.

In any case, with a patched 2.4.0-test11 I was able for the first
time to boot Ruffian from a SCSI disk and with a kernel from 2.4
series. I posted a message about this both to linux-kernel and
to axp-list; it looks like that the later copy is still stuck somewhere.
