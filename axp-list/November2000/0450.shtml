Kenneth Jacker (khj@cs.appstate.edu) wrote:

> I use both Linux/Intel and Linux/PPC. The problem with the later
> (though I've used it for 2-3 years) is the lack of up-to-date
> applications, libraries, and security patches.
>
> If I convert my DU/Tru64 system to Linux, will I be in the same boat

referring to commercial software? Maybe not... I can't comment on

Plus Alpha was the second arch Linux ran on...
