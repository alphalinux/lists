a quick search of the axp-list archives turns up problems with 6.1 and scsi
drivers on the pws installs, so maybe the ans is yes. 

----
in older firmware the ide is handled as though its scsi (emulation) and is
assigned as the first scsi ctrlr (a), the on board scsi was second (b), the
add in card third (c) so dkc0 is the first disk on the third scsi controller
(addin card)

I believe newer firmware counts them differently, also if you switch from
"set os_type unix" to "set os_type vms" the device names will also change.

But that has nothing to do with your problem, just fyi
