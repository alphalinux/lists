Hi Andrei --
I'm using a couple of SCA disks in AS200's with 80-50 pin adapters.
This is with the internal 53C810 scsi host. In my SX, I have a
Symbios 8751 card and a pair of 68 pin WD's working very well. I've
not really paid much attention to performance on the AS200's, but
the SX performance is more than acceptable. This card also has a
50-pin interface that I use for tape and scanner.
