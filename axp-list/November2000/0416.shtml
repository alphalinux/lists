On Tue, Nov 21, 2000 at 11:04:39AM -0500, SteveCraft wrote:
>
> I have my Alpha, a Multia VX40, dedicated for mail only. It, along with
> my Pentium Multias, are all crammed into a small spot (I'm in a small
> apartment), and I don't have any external SCSI stuff to mess with. The
> SCSI tape drive is internal and physically in my NT Server. I also own
> BackupExec for NT.
>
> This is getting crazy....

In such case, instead of all this Rube Goldberg-type proposed stuff, I
would just install Samba on Multia (this server is not that big, after
all, and configuring it is not a brain surgery), export to NT whatever
is there to backup, and let NT to deal with it as a "network share".

You can administer your Samba setup on Multia using any web browser
for which you will allow an acces to 'swat' on Multia.
