I also had a problem w/ milo at one point. I needed to put the kernel in the
same FAT partition as milo and linload to get it to work (this was when I
was booting off IDE... as soon as I went to SCSI almost all my problems went
away). This was on a PC164SX though. I got tired of the whole AlphaBIOS/
milo thing and went straight to SRM on all my systems.
