Hi Greg,
I have a Voodoo 3 2000 in my LX and XFree86 3.3.6 didn't work for me,
too. It didn't show a blank screen, but the pixels seemed to be mixed
all around the screen.

> At this point I decide to go with XFree 4.0 (bad idea)
> every time I've tried XFree 4 it spits me into SRM
> with some error, this time i think it was a double
> panic.
>
> At any rate I seem to remember that in the mailing
> list some of you have LX's with working XFree4 perhaps
> even using voodoo 3's. My question to you then is.
> Where do you get Xfree 4 bin's that work? Will I have
> to patch the source and compile it myself ?

I'm one of those, who got it to work. I compiled XFree 4.0 myself which
went pretty well (just a make World and a make install, AFAIR).
I added the VideoRam-parameter in the device-section, which stops XFree
from trying to detect the amount of video-memory on startup. You might
try this option, because a error in this detection may result in a
panic.
