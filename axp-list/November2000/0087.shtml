> After I got 2.4.0-test10 to compile (thanks for the stupid user fix - goes

> binary support for elf, a.out, misc binaries with the Intel ELF binary

Hmmm...I have nearly the same selections on an SX (generic kernel) and it
worked fine. The only thing that I didn't bother selecting is the Intel

that really help speed things up under small-moderate load. Supposedly,
it's still falling on its face when heavily loaded, but I haven't hit that
point yet.
