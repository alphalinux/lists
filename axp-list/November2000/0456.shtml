I tried pre11 yesterday on my UP1000,
alphabios, with
CONFIG_ALPHA_LEGACY_START_ADDRESS=y.
The kernel loaded and started up fsck,
but then reported that several partitions
of my ide disk (containing /boot and / )
were corrupt. I checked /dev/hda with
fdisk and saw that there were no partitions
declaired. Upon rebooting to a patched 2.2.16,
everything worked well, and the partitions
were fine.
