On Wed, 8 Nov 2000, Nelson Brito wrote:

> I'm trying to compile a program (a commercial pack) and it needs
> the dnet_stub lib! Unfortunately i don't know where to find it, at first
> i thought it was an X lib but it doesn't seems so! Can someone tell me
> where to look for it?

dnet_stub is only needed (and available) on Tru64/DigitalUNIX. You may
want to check the autoconf scripts or Imakefile to see if the authors were
making some generalised alpha*-* configuration decision. They often
assume that DigitalUNIX/Tru64 is the only OS that runs on Alpha.

What programme is it? I may already have a patch...
