On Wed, Nov 22, 2000 at 03:07:57PM +0100, Padiyath Kumar wrote:
>

> I am trying to Upgrade the Redhat5.2 to Redhat6.2 on
> Alphastation > 500/333 machine. My cd device is dka400. I wrote
> the following command:
>
> boot dka400 -fi /kernels/generic.gz -fl root=/dev/scd0
>
> The system reads the CD and writes the information on the
> terminal. Then I was expecting a window for the selection of
> update. But I am NOT seeing anything on the terminal - a black
> screen. Is there a way to see what is going on?

Do the upgrade in text mode:

     boot dka400 -fi /kernels/generic.gz -fl "root=/dev/scd0 text"
