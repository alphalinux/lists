Oddly enough our Veritas rep was just here (literally), and it looks like
they are slowly testing the Linux waters (but he's not commit to their
intentions). I've asked about a server, but have to wait for an engineer to
call me. To the best of my knowledge there is currently an agent (client)
for Linux, just no server.

That being said, if you're looking for an Enterprise class backup/restore
I'd suggest looking at BakBone NetVault - http://www.bakbone.com. It's
pretty slick, and from what I've seen works exceptionally well in
heterogeneous environments. No idea if "Linux" support includes Alpha, it
looks x86 only. Although, there is support for Tru64......
