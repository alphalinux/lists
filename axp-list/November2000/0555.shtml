Alexei Klimenko wrote:
>
> Hi!
>
> Help! Help! Help!
>
> I started administering a few Alpha machines
> couple of weeks ago and I ran into unsolvable
> for me problem.
>
> The problem is only on AlphaPC DP264 2CPU.
> (AS500 works perfectly).
>
> Originally OS was RH 6.2, but in etempts to
> eliminate the problem I installed kernel 2.2.17
>
> At first I've just noticed that the network
> is pretty slow. Then I tried 'ifconfig' and
> discovered, that about 10% of outgoing packages
> have errors. I spent last week trying to figure
> out what's wrong.
>
> The problem is not connected with the network card
> or the network itself. Originally the ethernet card
> was DE500-BA. I tried RealTek8139 without any
> changes in the depicted above.
>
> Looking forward to getting any ideas
> about where am I wrong.
>

Most likely, you have a mismatch between your
switch/hub and your card. Usually that gets
negotiated correctly but sometimes not. If your
hub is half duplex, the card must also be half
duplex. Same for full duplex. Check for a module
parameter to force the selection on the card
and try it both ways to see which one works.
