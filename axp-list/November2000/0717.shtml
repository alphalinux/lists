That's perfectly normal -- the bus speed may be 20 or 40 MB/sec, but the rate at
which a HD can transfer data to the host is dependant on bit-density, rotational
speed, etc. 16MB/sec is good for a 9GB SCSI drive. For more modern IDE drives,
you'll be able to realize 30-40 MB/sec sustained transfer rates.
