Well, Jim Paradis started the first 32-bit port of Linux to Alpha
sometime in 1994 and soon after that maddog was instrumental in getting
an Alpha machine to Linus. The first public appearance of Linux on
Alpha was in 1995 in Atlanta. Taking that into account to expect a
mention of Linux on an Alpha picture from 1992 would be a bit too much,
I think. :-)

I know that this feels like ages on "Internet time" but this was
not that long time ago.
