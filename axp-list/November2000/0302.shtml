> 1. What are the pinouts so I can connect a serial cable up to get
> into the box (or can I use a standard serial cable with null
> modem?)

Standard serial cable and null modem. 9600-8N1
 
> 2. Can I not use my voodoo3k card with SRM? (worked with arc
> consle)

I know the voodoos work on the later machines...but I've never had a PC164
to play with. It does strike me as strange that the x86 emulator in ARC
has no problem, but the SRM one fails, besides the Voodoos have worked for
quite a while.

> 3. if I have to do this blind, can I go back to ARC console? I
> have made a arc console update disk

Oh, there should be some fail safe recovery procedure. There's usually a
jumper you cap, then put AlphaBIOS onto a DOS floppy in the floppy drive
and it should load it from there (this is how the LX does it, I'm assuming
PC164 is similar).
