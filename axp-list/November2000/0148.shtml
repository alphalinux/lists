On Mon, Nov 06, 2000 at 11:39:15PM -0600, terrylr@blauedonau.com wrote:
>
> on my pc164 i have only used milo. in attempting
> to get rlinux-3.0-pre9 working i am wondering if
> there is any difference between the pal code in
> milo and srm.
>
> i thought i was close to getting rtlinux to work
> but now when it needs to execute a rdps statment
> (really a call_pal inline) the machine hangs solid.
>
> would anyone have any ideas?

It's almost certain that the PALcode supplied by MILO is missing
certain functionality, among which ight be the "rdps".

Since RTLinux was originally ported to DS10 with SRM console, it
may very well have dependencies on that level of support in PALcode.

Best bet is to go to SRM console and see. Be aware that there can be
problems associated with this, as noted by Bob Williams in another
posting (lack of support in SRM for devices that were fine under ARC).

Good luck.
