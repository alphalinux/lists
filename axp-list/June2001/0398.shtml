On Sun, 1 Jul 2001, Peter Watkinson wrote:
> Seeing is this is probably the best place to ask can anyone tell me for how
> long into the future willl Redhat be supporting Alpha Linux.

Red Hat is a business. They could drop it tomorrow if it suits them.
However Alpha/Linux is here to stay in one form or another. There is
nothing proprietary about Red Hat's distribution. Debian is probably a
better bet in the long term. Meanwhile, 7.1 is/will be available.

Hardware support is a more pressing concern. Time will tell just how many
UP1500's will be available, and for how long... I don't recall hearing any
statements from Samsung/API.
