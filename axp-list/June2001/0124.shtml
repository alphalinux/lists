> I try to install XFree86 4.1.0 from Rawhide (on my RH 7.0) and it
> seems that a library is missing from XFree86-libs : libXIE.so.6 which
> is required by Mozilla (at last 0.9).

dependency go away (It worked for 0.9.1, at least; I downloaded
mozilla-0.9.1-1.src.rpm and ran rpm --rebuild mozilla*src.rpm).
