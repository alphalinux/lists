On Sun, Jun 17, 2001 at 06:00:38PM -0600, Michal Jaegermann wrote:
>
> I know only what I have seen on a small sample of cards with
> which I tried to bring a server up. :-)

Well, I've seen one G400 without 2nd head; works fine in 2D, can't get
the 3D stuff going on UP1100.

> With the card I was experimenting I had various "nasties" in a picture
> on the second screen in any resolution above 1024x768, IIRC. No
> problems on the first screen or when resolution was low enough. No idea
> what was the trouble here. Could be that my hardware has
> "imperfections" or something is funny in software but the later would be
> a bit strange, I think.

Does the server try to run int10 on the BIOS for the second head?
What I mean is, does the BIOS emulation run on both the primary and
secondary heads, or does it look like it's trying them one at a time?

Reason I ask: I've played around a bit with putting two cards into
some of the Alphas (with DS10 and XP1000, one can put 2 3DLabs VX1s
in, and the console won't barf. Note that this is only with VX1 cards,
they are looked for specifically). What usually happens under XFree86
is that the int10 BIOS emulator runs on the first card but not the
second, and because the second doesn't get "officially" initialized,
video is significantly screwed up.
