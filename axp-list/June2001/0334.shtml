With regards to your message at 12:09 PM 6/26/01, Peter Watkinson. Where
you stated:
> >
>
>This could be a really dumb suggestion -
>
>but would it be possible to have an Open source processor?
>
>we already have an open source OS
>
>Anything has to be better than being screwed by Intel
>
It is a great suggestion, and has been bandied about before.
Couple of problems:
Processor development nowadays seems to take a team of highly expert, and
experienced engineers, armed with advanced tools, fabrication facilities,
and a heck of a lot of funding.
I have heard that P4 had an engineering cost of around 1.3 billion $ to date.

Where do we get the funding to pay for that?

Next up, do we want an inexpensive processor for the masses, or a high end
CPU like an Alpha for the high performance technical computing segment?

Finally, who do we get to fab it?
