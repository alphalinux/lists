Red Hat dropped support of booting from ARC/AlphaBios with 7.0 and later.
SRM/aboot is the prefered method now. I feel your pain; I have an XL366 too.
But, life goes on. That doesn't mean it won't necessarily work however. It
just means they haven't gone out of their way to make sure it works.

I'm running RH 6.2 on my XLT with a Permedia 2 video card. I didn't really
have any problems with that. Before that I tried Mandrake and an ancient
version of Suse (6.1 I think) and couldn't get either of them to work. RH
6.2 installed fine. Oh - I have AlphaBios 5.70 too. I believe that is the
final version available.

I'm going to give Debian a try next.
