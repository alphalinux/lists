I have a powerstorm card also that sits in my drawer next to my desk.
Some Powerstorm series will work. It depends on the chipset on the card.
Mine (4d30t) uses an Evans and Sutherland proprietary. This is bad. Some
used, IIRC, an S3 chipset, in which case they have been made to work.
There is more info in the archive about which powerstorm cards have the S3
and will work.

I can't help on the ATI. Sorry.
