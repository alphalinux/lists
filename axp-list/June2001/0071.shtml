Dwight,
 I just had similar problem with our DS10L's. I am not sure which
 driver you are using. If you have the DE500-XX based NIC cards you
 better usr the de4x5 drivers.
 The next thing, you need to worry about is the what kind of switch
 you are connected to. I have had no problem with the DE500's connected
 to a Nortel Switch (I could use both the tulip & de4x5) with auto,Fast
 FastFD SRM setting. But we recently changed our switch to CISCO and I
 got the same behaviour as you are seeing (similar), the only I could
 get it to work was force the CISCO switch to 100Mb full duplex and
 set the SRM setting to FastFD and use the de4x5 driver.

 Cheers
 Mohan
