Hello,

Our little cluster (5 DS-10's) is just chugging along, even though I haven't
really had enough time to do much more than load it up with jobs. Still,
nodes have a tendency to just sort of "drift off", they become completely
non-responsive, well almost, I can ping them but nothing more than that.

There seem to be a series of events that leads to a hang-up. If the nodes
sit idle for awhile (by awhile I mean overnight) they seem to work perfectly
fine if my first contact with them is via an "rlogin" command. If my first
contact is an rsh or rcp, then things stop working and the node becomes
unreachable. So, there is apparently something strange going on when these
commands are the first issued. I should note that rsh and rcp work just
fine after the node has been "awakened" with an rlogin.

Anyone have any ideas?

Dwight
