Speedracer,

Microsoft dropped Alpha support on Win 2k "Whistler" (XP). There will be no
production version.
IA-64 architecture is based on Alpha architecture (i.e. SAL/PAL, etc.),
except that processor speeds can exceed 1GHz. Additionally, newer industry
standards are being incorporated (i.e. Intel's EFI, ACPI) into 64-bit
processing.

I am booting 16 processor IA-64 Itanium w/ Beta 2 (Build 2462) "Whistler"
Datacenter.
