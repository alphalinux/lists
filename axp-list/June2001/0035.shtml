Yes, there was a partnership between Digital and Kryotech right around the
time of the Compaq merger. It was a 767Mhz EV56 chilled with the Kryotech
"refrigerator" slapped onto one side of the normal Miata GL case. I think
they sold for about $24K at the time.

I had heard at one time they were playing with chilled EV6's but that was a
while ago and nothing ever came of it. I also heard that Kryotech was
chilling Athlon based machines. That was also a while ago and I've never
seen one.
