ended up with a dead XServer. I checked the config
files (which moved during the 4.02 install to
/etc/X11) and all seemed well. When the Xserver
started however, the Xserver crashed and reported
"unresolved" for a number of graphic functions
pointing to my accelerator's driver (mga_drv.o). I
