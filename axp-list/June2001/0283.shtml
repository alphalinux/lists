alvin wrote:
> Well I guess it it time to reconsider purchasing those UP1500s :(

Not really.

>>From my POV the UP1500 is a good buy for 64-bit hardware. IA-64 isn't ready
yet, the compilers and os'es are still immature. And from what I understand,
IA-64 costs considerably more. I sincerely hope Samsung keeps manufacturing for
a while yet.

One could argue that the Alpha has outlived its usefulness... 64-bitness is now
(almost) mainstream and processor speed is no longer the major computing
bottleneck. SPARC, MIPS and Alpha have all become indistinguishable to some
degree.

There may be a good market for low-power chips ahead, even in server computing,
due to space/power considerations. But the Alpha isn't a good candidate there.
(I wonder what leaving mine on around the clock has done to my electricity
bill...)
