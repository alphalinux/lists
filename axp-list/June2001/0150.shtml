michal@ellpspace.math.ualberta.ca said:
> This HALlib module from Matrox is needed to get a picture on the
> second screen of a G400 card. Without it everything seems to be just
> fine if you look at log files but there is this small detail that you
> cannot see anything on the second screen (the first one is fine).

There's no code in XFree86 to deal with the second head of the G400. If it
claims to be trying to do so, that's a bug.

The second head of the G400 is reasonably well-documented, and example code
exists in the Linux matroxfb driver - the author of which has given
permission for his code to be reused under the XFree86 licence. All that's
missing is a volunteer with sufficient motivation and time to port it.

> With the card I was experimenting I had various "nasties" in a
> picture on the second screen in any resolution above 1024x768, IIRC.
> No problems on the first screen or when resolution was low enough. No
> idea what was the trouble here. Could be that my hardware has
> "imperfections" or something is funny in software but the later would
> be a bit strange, I think.

I've heard reports of strangeness with some modelines. It's working fine
here, albeit on i386, with 1600x1200 on the second head. It doesn't use
int10.
