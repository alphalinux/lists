On Tue, Jul 03, 2001 at 01:27:10PM +0200, Peter Mittermayer wrote:
>
> Is there any support for the 3dlabs Oxygene VX1 graphics card on Alpha
> Linux?? This card uses the GLINT R3 chip and I can"t find any
> information on that in the XFree literature.

Yes, there is support in XFree86, starting with 4.0.3 (possibly
earlier).

However, on Alpha, there are some problems with 4.0.3 that are fixed by
patches available on gatekeeper.dec.com:

        /pub/DEC/Linux-Alpha/X11/diffs-403-01apr2150.gz

Note that these patches have been applied by Red Hat for their 7.1,
and SuSE for their 7.1. Debian has these patches also, but I don't
know if they are part of their distribution yet.

Note also that these patches are already part of 4.1.0.

Good luck.
