Interesting that at ftp.kde.org there are binaries for alpha and sparc under
Redhat 7.0. None under Redhat 6.2 and the SRPMS for 6.2 aren't competely up yet
either. The rh7 binaries require . . .

[root@chaos rh7]# rpm -ivh htdig-3.2.0-0.b2.alpha.rpm
error: failed dependencies:
