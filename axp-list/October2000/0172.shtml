What's the exchange rate these days AU--->USD? I am only asking because I
have nearly the same system for sale here in the US for 500.00$ + shipping
I say nearly because I have 256 MEG Ram, and have switched from 3DLABS GLINT
based graphics to S3 and loaded REDHAT 6.2. Sounds like this use to be a NT
workstation. You may run into trouble trying to read CDR's with that SCSI
CD drive. I had that problem and switch to and IDE CDROM drive which REDHAT
supports just fine. You can get NT 4.0 to support the IDE CDROM until you
try to load SP4 or higher. Then you start to have serious problems such as
it will not boot. IDE hard drives are not supported under WINNT.

If you have any other questions please email me off list. I can answer some
of them. My experience with alpha is mostly in the NT world. I am new to
Linux/Alpha.
