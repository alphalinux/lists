        In my employment we have relied heavilly on the taso option to port
code from 32bit VMS to tru64. It works great, However my understanding was
that ccc on alpha linux does not support -taso. Even if it does what you
really need support for is the -xtaso and -xtaso_short to force 32 bit
pointers and to allow a 32bit pointer to make it easy otherwise you have
to deal with alignments and structure sizing issues.
