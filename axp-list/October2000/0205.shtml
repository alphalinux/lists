On Thu, Oct 19, 2000 at 02:51:18PM +0200, Jonas Gustavsson wrote:
>
> On my little venture on getting games ported to Alpha Linux, I´ve
> got a XP1000 for testing the apps, does anyone know what gfx cards
> work with DRI or HW accelerated OGL on this machine?

XP1000 is PCI only, and since there's no PCI-GART driver (yet), the best
bet is to use a Voodoo3 card (which doesn't require GART support).

I've had both a Voodoo Banshee and a Voodoo3 3000 in an XP1000 working
fine (though 2D only, I'm sorry to say).

The DRI/GLX support is getting there on Alpha; Daryll Strauss claims it
is close, though you may have to pull from both the DRI and XFree86 CVS
tress and do your own integration... ;-}

Good luck, let us know how you make out...
