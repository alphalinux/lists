Red Hat Linux 7 will have it out of the box. The packages from 7
won't install out of the box on a 6.x system, though. You'll need to
upgrade to RPM 3.0.5.

When building, we use -mcpu=ev5 - this allows us to schedule for newer
processors while remaining compatible with EV4.
