Robert M. Riches Jr. wrote:
>
> (Sorry if this isn't an alpha-specific problem.)
>
> My SCSI scanner keeps shifting device names, which really annoys
> the device finder in xscanimage.

Will xscanimage be happy with something like "/dev/scanner"?
If yes, then make /dev/scanner a link which you adjust automatically
in your startup scripts.

Also make your scanner the highest SCSI id in use and/or use labels
to mount your disk partitions instead of "raw" device names.
