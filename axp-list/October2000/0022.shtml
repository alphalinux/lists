Seems more likely a SW bug than a HW bug. I'd suggest you
have your customer send you his TMP files and you try to do
something with them on your system. Why are you compiling
your code for a particular "size"? Why not use an argument
or better yet, your code looks at it's input files and decides
how much memory it needs to process them? Anyway, these are
most likely code bugs.
