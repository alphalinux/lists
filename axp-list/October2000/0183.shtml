>
> To force the de4x5 driver (args='eth0:autosenes=100Mb') doesn't help.
> I don't know about the tulip (I don't know how to force the tulip).

Like that - in /etc/modules.conf put a line

options tulip options=14

and reload your module.

The "magic number" is an index into 'medianame' array from tulip.c
(0-based). '14' above means "MII 100baseTx-FD" where MII is a
transmitter type. Pick up the one you really need.

Also try 'modinfo -p tulip' (and 'man modinfo') even if it will lie
a bit about value boundaries. Adding something like 'debug=3' to
options when you are trying to resolve problems may have some uses.

On www.scyld.com you will find some diagnostic tools for your tulip
card.
