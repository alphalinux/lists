I recently put an i82557 Intel old-generation ethernet card into my
formerly-trusty UX box. To my surprise the machine locked solid very
shortly after completing the boot. This was on stock kernels 2.2.16,
2.2.17 with network drivers monolithic or as modules.

A 3COM card in the same place works fine.

The Intel card in a UP2000 works fine.

Does anyone else have similar experiences? I'm doing updates for the
(now vintage) FAQ chapter so anything else of interest will be
gratefully received.
