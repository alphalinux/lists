Hmmm... I rummaged around, finally figured out what my password was,
and found a reference to a patch back in January. The poster quoted
the patch and asked if it had been integrated yet. The patch just added
one line that poked a register and a comment about preventing the
drive from "going to sleep."

badly that I had to use a jumpstart CD to recover, and eventually

reports that it has found no floppy controllers during boot.

Is it possible that you're thinking of a different patch than the

recent one? I guess I'll try upgrading to the 2.2.16 kernel.
