Some Ruffians are temperamental with 100mbit ethernet. I have one
such machine. Occasionally on power-up my Ruffian was unable to get
a connection established. I would see the 100mbit light on my hub
for a second or two, then a collision and the light goes out. Repeat
ad inifinitum.

I found that recent 2.2 series kernels seem to work. I use the tulip
driver and 2.2.17. I also have a suspicion that changing the port on
the hub it was connected to improved things. If you try a different
hub and find that it makes things better I would be interested to
know.

Regards,
Stig Telfer
