Hello,

I'm fairly sure that your graphics card will at least get to
standard 80x24 character mode, without starting "X". If so,
you might try running /usr/X11R6/bin/SuperProbe. This will
query your card and give you information about the chipset,
DAC converter and memory on the video card.

Hope this helps.

Ed Lukacs
