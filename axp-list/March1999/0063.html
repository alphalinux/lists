>>> "Notari, Ed" said:
> I have a new install of RHv5.2 on an AS200 (Avanti with 32MB RAM & standard
> #9 card) and whenever I run startx after about 2-3 minutes of working in the
> GUI my system locks up. The only thing that seems to work is the mouse but
> nothing under it is clickable. At this point I cannot even ping the box.
> Then my only option is to do a hard boot!

Add the following to your /etc/X11/XF86Config file's Device Section:

        Option "no_pci_disconnect"
        Option "sw_cursor"

The #9 you have, based on Trio64, is somewhat problematic without those...
