> - $(OBJECTS) -o $(BINNAME) -lm $(AUDIO_LIB)
> + $(OBJECTS) -o $(BINNAME) /usr/local/lib/libcpml.a $(AUDIO_LIB)
                                             ^^^^^
Shudder!!! Don't hardwire location of libraries that way in Makefiles.
This is **stupid**! You will find yourself why in not so distant
future.

First of all your compiler, nearly certainly, searches /usr/local/lib
so -lcpml would be just great. The second, if /usr/local/lib is
not searched by a compiler (not bloody likely) you can always ask
for that using -L option.
