Bianca Passarelli wrote:
>
> I have two DDS 3 tape drives installed in two of my servers and I can't
> figure out the right arguments to give dump or mt

mt? What parameters? Do you mean that a version included in RH 5.2
i.e. 0.5, is broken? Last year, August or so, there was version 0.5b
released with fixes for boo-boos in 0.5. It compiles without any
accidents on Alpha as well. Replacing sources in src.rpm and rebuilding
binaries is trivial.

> to be able to use
> up the full 24gig capacity of the backup tapes.

The simplest trick is to dump on stdout with a redirection to a tape.
dump does not check tape length when writing on stdout. A bad side
effect is that you cannot do multivolume dumps that way. Do you really
need that with 24 gig of storage? Then its time to dig out some
pre-historical data on tapes and fire up your calculator.
