At 08:37 AM 3/14/99 -0800, you wrote:
>I am planning on purchasing an Alpha here in the next month or so to run
>RedHat 5.2 on. I am wondering where you all would recommend looking for a
>decent used machine and what you would recommend purchasing. Also, I am
>going to have about $500.00 to play with for this, is this reasonable or am
>I dreaming?

Not at all, except you might not get yourself anything "high-end". UDB's
sell for almost nothing, and after you buy ram, hdd, and other toys $500 is
about what you will hit. Then again a UDB is not a DS20 or anything. :)

(don't get me wrong, I have about 10 UDB's and I love 'em.)

>One other thing, I already have RedHat 5.2 on my Intel, but I am totally
>unfamiliar with Alpha, where would I find fairly detailed instructions on
>getting it running on a Alpha, I assume I can use the same RedHat 5.2 CD I
>have for the distro right?

You must buy the Alpha version of RedHat (or download it). I'm sure they
have enough space on the CD's to put both, but that is bad marketing.. :)

Take a look at www.alphalinux.org, that might help you out a bit.

Good luck,
