I did a little hardware shuffling on my 164SX before booting 2.2.3 this
evening. I removed every card (isa & pci) but the es1371 and the video card,
and then tried the es1371 in slot[0-3] (0 & 1 being 64-bit). The card in
slot0 is assigned irq 27, slot1 irq 26, etc. The results were always the
same: repeated loading of the es1371 module to get it working at all and
screeching during heavy disk activity. It doesn't seem to be a hardware
configuration issue, given that it works flawlessly every time I boot NT, and
OSS/costly and ALSA haven't ever worked for me.
