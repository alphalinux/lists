Matt,

A number of programs are designed to grab memory and hang onto it based on
events that occur. Some grab a pretty big chunk at the initialization
phase while others try to play nice and only grab chucks as requirements
dictate.

Do your have X running? It's a resource hog.
How about a commercial web server like Netscape?
How about DNS?
How about Squid or other NAT/Firewalling/Security type package.

Review your list of deamons and see where the memory is being used.

ps -auxwww | more

Let us know what you find . . . .
