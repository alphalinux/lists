> I am interesting in finding out about the state-of-the-art hardware
> and Compaq UK doesn't seem to know much. They have only 500MHz
> machines on sale currently. I recall seeing someone mention some
> 667MHz alpha's on this list, or was I just dreaming?

Alphas come with 2 different generations of CPUs at many different
speeds. The latest and greatest is the XP1000 box, with a 21264 cpu at
500 mhz. I'm not sure if you can buy faster 21264-based boxes yet. The
previous generation is the 21164 chip. Your PC164LX has this chip, and
indeed you can buy them at 533, 600, or 667 mhz. Compaq should be able
to show you benchmarks for various machines; the 21264 is much faster
than the 21164 at the same clock speed at most tasks.
