There's one thing I haven't seen mentioned in this thread. I don't
know if this is still the case with Adaptec, but a few years ago, they
were putting out a new revision of the firmware on a _very_ frequent
basis, (read: fixing bugs and inserting new ones) and they didn't
always update the firmware version numbers. It wouldn't surprise me in
the least that this may, in some way, be the cause of many people's
problems - it's not trivial to write drivers for `N' (where N is very
large) different versions of the `same' card/firmware.

Just my $0.02
