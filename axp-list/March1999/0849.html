> Depends. Check that the CPU isn't soldered on: I gather this applies
> to the majority of the 166MHz models. If you can get hold of a 233MHz
> one they overclock to 266MHz quite straightforwardly -- that's what
> I've done with mine. Look for the UDB hardware documentation (linked
> as a PDF from <http://www.alphalinux.org.>, I believe). You'll
> probably also want to check that it's got the small (physically small,
> that is) hard disk: otherwise you lose the PCI slot. Another thing to
> look for is that it's got the external SCSI connector.
