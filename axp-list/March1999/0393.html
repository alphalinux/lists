Yeah, this is they way I understand it (sorry) but some people DO claim to
have it working. Even on the very similar systems (same make) people get
different results. It is likely to be a timing issue, and the problem is
related to dma/interrupts - when the card requests more data the system
dies.

I assume your card causes a hard crash with little/no sound output?
All I can suggest at the moment is to try different PCI slots -
particularly the 64bit/(first bus) slots to see if the results are
different.

If anyone wants to help debug it, I can give you precise locations in the
code of where it crashes.
