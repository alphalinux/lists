you stated:
>
>On Thu, 11 Mar 1999, Greg Lindahl wrote:
>
>> I would love to see 264DP systems with 4-8 gigs of ram. They have 16
>> dimm slots.
>
>Looking at the "AlphaPC 264DP Technical Reference Maunual", it says memory
>configurations of 128MB - 4GB are supported. Why only 4GB, is it just that
>larger DIMMs where not available at the time??

Currently the 256MB SODIMM is the largest shipping.

As the DP264 has 8 slots per CPU for RAM, this means a maximum of 16x.25GB
per CPU.
Which yields 2GB, and with 2CPUs 4GB.
