No. Fortran and C use the same underlying math library. He said
that the same value did not give a SIGFPE from a C program. Thus,
the guilty party has to be the f2c/g77 glue arond the libm exp(), and
not the libm exp().

Of course, he can easily experiment and prove this, by using one of
the alternate libraries you mention.
