Eric Brown wrote:
>
> Is there a version (ANY version) of dhcpcd that both works
> on an alpha and with kernel 2.2?

As Changes file says you need a new version of dhcpcd (at least 1.3.17
or maybe later) to work with 2.2 kernels. None of 0.xx will work on
any platform. I did not actually try if the new ones work anywhere. :-)

> Does anybody know why versions of dhcpcd higher than 0.65
> (which shipped with RedHat 5.0) don't work on Alphas?

No, but some people reported that 0.70 works for them on Alphas (with
2.0.y kernels). This was not my experience so this seems to depend also
on what the other end is doing.

> Also, are Alphas little endian (like x86) or big endian?

Little endian; but you may see that for yourself with a help of
a very short C program. :-)

In general - various programs require updates for 2.2 kernels.
Check Changes file in Documentation subdirectory of kernel sources.
Be wary of modutils-2.1.121. They are **not** really backwards compatible
with 2.0 kernels. Current modutils-2.2.2-pre are in much better shape.
You will find them on http://www.pi.se/blox/modutils/
