Try a different kernel level. I'm using 2.1.129 on
my PC164 with 2940UW without a problem. I've noticed
that the aic7xxx driver's stability comes and goes
depending on what kernel level is used. 2.0.32 was stable
and now 2.1.129 has been stable for me at least.
2.0.3[3-x] were not stable on it though. Also, if you use
2.1.129, it spits out what it thinks the setup of your
adapter is so you can confirm it knows what sort of
cables/termination are attached to each of the connectors.
Also, use active termination on everything...
