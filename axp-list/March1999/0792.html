The three I underlined are "asking too much" for an Alpha - period. At least
at the moment. SO5 and WP8 aren't available for Alpha/Linux. Rumor has it
that em86 is being tweaked to handle WP8, so you could theoretically run WP8
very slowly at some point in the future. There's no Netscape either - you're
stuck with lynx, kfm, or Mozilla (barring em86 or the DU libraries).

For office-type apps on Linux for Alphas, there's Applix.
