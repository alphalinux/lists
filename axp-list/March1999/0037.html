On Mon, 1 Mar 1999, James Hamilton wrote:

> Hi, I've read that you can flash MILO to your multia so that it pops up
> every time you boot (w/o a floppy). The docs I read suggested this may be
> bad because it limited future hardware upgrades. My multia has probably
> seen it's last hardware upgrade (short adding some ram). Do any of you
> have experience with this. If so is there a better (for novices) howto on
> this than the MILO Howto?

I have had mixed luck with flashing milo onto our Nonames. Most of them
seem to work but we have a few that will not run with the most recient
Milos. I am not sure if it is a power or heat related problem. I have
heard that there are several hardware mods that DEC has made to the Noname
boards but the boards I have seem to be lacking in hardware mods.

For the most part I perfer Milo over ARC or SRM. All milo needs now is the
ability to do network booting and then it would be just perfect. A little
bit more work and it could boot NT then it would be possible to use it to
completley replace ARC in situations that require The evil dredid NT ;)
