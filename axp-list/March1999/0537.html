Well, 256MB DIMMs are $510 at the chipmerchant. Which works out to ~$2 per
MB. Now, the issue, do you want to install 8 Dimms per machine in 256
machines? Thats typically where the little extra, say enough to get up to $3/
dimm. Actual vendor pricing is typically higher (compaq, dell, hp, ibm,
intergraph, etc).

As for the $100K. When you start to build a machine with a LOT of components,
you start to run into a lot of hard failures. Its just the FIT rate catching
up with you. Whether you plan it or not, you can easily end up having someone
who who ends up just replacing parts.
