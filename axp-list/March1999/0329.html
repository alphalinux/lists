2.2.2 does not compile out of the box on Alpha. Go and get an Alan Cox
patch to 2.2.2 (ac4 or later), apply it and all will be well.

ftp://ftp.alphalinux.org/pub/AXP/patches/patch-2.2.2-ac4.bz2
