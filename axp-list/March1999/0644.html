> If your box has an S3 Trio64 based card in it, add the following to the
> Device Section of /etc/X11/XF86Config:
>
> Option "no_pci_disconnect"
> Option "sw_cursor"
>
> --Jay++

    Is this problem specific to certain Alpha boxes ? Is the Trio64
    different from the "Trio64 V+" in this respect ? I have the latter
    in my UX2 (Ruffian) box and it works fine without these options.
    My Device section has just this:

    Section "Device"
        Identifier "S3-Trio64"
        VendorName "Unknown"
        BoardName "Unknown"
        option "power_saver"
    EndSection

    A minor problem I have is that it will occasionally snap into
    power saver mode and blank the screen and almost immediately snap
    back to display screen contents.
