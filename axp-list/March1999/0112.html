Kerry Boguszewski-Odom wrote:
>
> Hi,
> My problem is that I cannot get my ppp connection going despite
> being able to do it in minicom. I've tried putting the "\~" " " at the
> end of the chat script and I've tried switching from ttyS2 to ttyS3 both
> on IRQ 5. I tried ttyS1 with irq 3 but was unable to get minicom to work
> under this--I suspect because com2 already has this port and I do not
> know how to disable a port in my alpha. The modem is a 3com 56K fax
> modem and it works fine with minicom. pppd seems to die after it makes
> the serial connection and issues the log message ppp0 <--> ttyS3. I am
> running redhat 5.2 alpha.
>
> I am considering trying to reload the entire system from scratch as my
> next step in trying to get this to work. What puzzles me is why it works
> under minicom but not pppd. If anybody has a 3com 56K internal modem
> working in an Alpha AS200 4/233 and has seen this problem, I would be
> much indebted to know how you got past it. Below is a portion of the
> logfile. Thanks in advance. Kerry

Is it possible that your minicom script force the rate down to 38300.
While your chat script tries to connect @56k?
Add the line
/bin/setserial /dev/ttyS3 spd_vhi
to your startup script and try again.
