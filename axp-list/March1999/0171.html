> Any reason one wouldn't want to just replace libm with libffm or libcpml?

libffm isn't as complete as libm, and it has larger errors.

If libcpml is complete, then you could use it as a replacement,
because it's always more accurate. But it's worth testing first,
and LD_PRELOAD is easy to arrange...
