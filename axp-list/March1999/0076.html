>Tom,

>The general syntax of the MILO nomenclature is that of the ARC firmware
>specification, i.e., roughly, bus(#)adapter(#)device(#). In your
>example, the device is (E)ISA floppy zero. The "multi" means the bus is
>integral to the motherboard. The ARC specification can be found on the
>Microsoft Web site somewhere, I know, but I wasn't able to locate it
>this morning.
