Well, IMHO if you put in 90MB RAM or more, it is much more like a P120.
But you have to remember these 166MHz machines are like 6 years
old... If you want to compare it to a x86, you must compare it to one of
the same vintage (P66 or so) to make it fair. Old Alphas (pre 21264
vintage anyway) are typical RISC processors and CISC processors at the
same MHz are usually faster.

I would recommend a AS200 (get a 4/233) as these systems are a little
nicer then a UDB (IMHO), have more internal space and have more expansion
slots. Have a look at http://computers.ebay.com (click on hardware then do
a search for "alpha"), there are usually several of these for sale there.

Good luck!
