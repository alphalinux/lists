> a 166mzh cpu in it, can that be replaced with a 233 nor something like

The answer is "probably not". Most of them (in fact every one I've
ever seen) have a soldered-down CPU. There are sine variants with
socketed CPUs, but they're harder to find.

Myself, I'd love to buy a 233 MHz Multia to replace the 166 in my
stereo system. The 166 doesn't *quite* seem to have enough punch for
x11amp with all its nifty features (though it runs mpg123 at full
quality).
