On 14-Mar-99 Paul B. Brown wrote:
> Bill,
>
> Multia 166 MHz = Pentium 90
> External CD-ROM can be purchased to attach to the built-in SCSI II
> adaptor.
> An external modem can be attached to the built-in serial port.
> You can add a good video card if you choose.
> You can also add an external backup device using the SCSI II adpator.

Some models of the Multia have the external SCSI port, others do not. I have
also seen reports of success using a PCMCIA modem in one of the slots instead
of an external modem (though I don't see much advantage in this unless you were
using both serial ports for something else).

The Multia was a good learning tool for me. What I mainly learned was that I
wanted a more modern Alpha box:-)
