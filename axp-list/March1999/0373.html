> Is it legal to distributed binaries compiled against a static library
whose
> license prevents redistribution?

In the general case, probably not. For example the libraries distributed
with Digital Unix (now True64 Unix for the politically sensitive) can not be
redistributed. A binary file statically linked (non_shared) to these
libraries may not be legally used anywhere except on a legally licensed
Digital Unix system.

Just to head off the fire storm... This is not a Digital or Compaq decision
point. The libraries are encumbered through their OSF roots.
