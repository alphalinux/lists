I tried many times to get RH loaded with the 16MB of ram that came with
the machine. I never did get it to work with 16MB. I ended up adding
64MB and all went smooth. I believe Eric Cifreo posted something
regarding this several months ago and the conclusion was you can't do it
with the stock disks from redhat. The easy fix, add memory. I believe
we determined that 32MB was the minimum to get a smooth install.
