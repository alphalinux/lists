On Sat, 6 Mar 1999, Munagala V. S. Ramanath wrote:
> PPP runs fine on my UX2 with a Philips EasyConnect 56K modem on
> ttyS0; there is a minor problem in that modem-to-modem inbound
> speeds (for file downloads) seem to never go higher than 3KB/s
> though I sometimes see outbound speeds (for file uploads) between
> 4KB and 5KB/s.

I think you may have that reversed. With 56k you WILL see D/L speeds that
get up to the 4-6K region, but uploading will NEVER be much above 3.3k
(assuming binary/low compressability files) Thats the way 56k (k56 flex,
X2 and V.90) work. Double check and let me know, because if you ARE
getting better upload than D/L I'd like to test that modem (here on a
bench with a scope)
