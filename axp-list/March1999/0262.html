Elsa Gloria Synergy is a Permedia2 based card ( normally equipped with 8MB)
and is supported under XFree86 on Alpha using the 3DLABS server. It IS
necessary to use the 3.3.3.1 server, as the one in 3.3.3 is broken.
This is the same chip as the new 4D10 card uses, and is usable with
Tru64(formerly Digital UNIX) 4.0E as well.
