I loaded mc-4.1.3[35]-2.[alpha|intel].rpm from RH5.2 on my
Intel and Alpha and it works as expected on either system. So,
either RH5.1 has a bad mc*rpm or you need to look at the
commands at the bottom of the mc screen. They refer to the
function key to press to make the requested function occur.

"4Edit" means hit F4.

Or, type "man mc" and read what it says.

Also, make sure your file permissions give you read authority.
