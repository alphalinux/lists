On Wed, 03 Mar 1999, norvierk@muohio.edu wrote:
>> floating point has been optimized in the last snapshot of egcs (1999-02-28)
>> with my test about 23% "quicker"...
>
>I just compared results from nbench compiled with egcs-1.1.1 and the 99-02-28
>snapshot and the results were pretty inconclusive. I ran them on the same
>kernel, compiled against the same libffm and libm. Mind if I ask what your
>test is?

It's the nbench-byte-2.1 test too, with lffm and lfm. I didn' t use it with
egcs-1.1 but with an earlier release last time... I noticed this improvment
(with 1999-02-28) on a 21164SX 533 Mhz and a 21164A 633 Mhz...
Here are the CFLAGS used.

CFLAGS =-s -static -O3 -fomit-frame-pointer -Wall -fforce-addr \
          -fforce-mem -funroll-all-loops -fschedule-insns2 -fexpensive-optimizations \
          -mfp-regs -lffm -lfm -lm -L.
