I argee completely, most people fall into the Intel marketing "pit" and
go "gee I can buy a 500MHz PIII for 2K or a 500MHz Alpha 21264 for 7K" and
they go off to buy a PIII because it's "as fast" but cheaper.

AAAAHHHHHH!!! There I feel better now :)

A 21264 is roughly 2x faster then a 21164 at the same clock speed. i.e. a
21264 @ 500MHz is roughly about the same as a 21164 @ 1000MHz (if such a
beast existed :)

Some fun numbers:

Alpha 21264 500MHz 30.3 47.7

PowerPC 604e 332MHz 14.4 12.6
PII 450MHz 17.2 12.9
Xeon 400MHz 16.5 13.8

www.spec.org has lots more.
