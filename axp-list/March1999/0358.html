'bzip2' and 'bunzip2' (a symlink to bzip2).

Here's a silly little example that
extracts a kernel and applies patches:

-- cd /usr/src
(get a listing of the archive)
-- bzip2 -cd <somepath>/linux-2.2.0.tar.bz2 | tar -tf -
(extract everything)
-- bzip2 -cd <somepath>/linux-2.2.0.tar.bz2 | tar -xvf -
-- bzip2 -cd <somepath>/patch-2.2.1.bz2 | patch -p0
-- bzip2 -cd <somepath>/patch-2.2.2.bz2 | patch -p0
-- bzip2 -cd <somepath>/patch-2.2.2-ac5.bz2 | patch -p0
