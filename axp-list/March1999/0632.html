Hi!

I don't have any experience with soundcards on Alpha, but I have
SB16-compatible card running under Linux on the Intel box. Few questions:

1. You mention that You used isapnptools with no success. Why? Did
   pnpdump say anything at all? Did you read FAQ for isapnptools at
   http://www.roestock.demon.co.uk/isapnptools/isapnpfaq.html?
2. Why are you trying to load module sound.o? Why are You using
   insmod instead of modprobe? On Intel box to load the sound support
   into the kernel I write something like

   /sbin/modprobe -a sb io=0x220 irq=5 dma=1

   This loads at least soundcore, sound, uart401 and sb modules.
   When I don't specify the parameters, I'm getting similar error
   messages, about device being busy and stuff.
