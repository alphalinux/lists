There are new releases of the boot/install floppies that Loic Prylli just
finished this week. FYI, I've been working on the Alpha port of Debian
for well over a year now and, believe me, install is *MUCH* easier than it
was before (we had to untar the base tarball onto the hard drive back
then, then move the drive over to the Alpha). I just ran an install onto
my Noname about two weeks ago and everything went really smoothly.

<BLATANT PLUG>If you have a spare disk, give it a try. Alot of work has

packages that RH has are fixed in Debian</BLATANT PLUG>.

2.0.7 on my Noname, though) and things are working VERY nicely so far...
