If you have SRM, you can use the following command to boot from CD

P00>>>boot dka0 -fi /kernels/generic.gz -fl "root=/dev/scd0"

( suppose dka0 represents SCSI CD-ROM in your box. You can also try
xlt.gz or xlt-s.gz)
