Permedia cards are directly supported in RH6.0.

My card:

  Bus 0, device 13, function 0:
    Display controller: Texas Instruments TVP4020 Permedia 2 (rev 1).
      Medium devsel. Fast back-to-back capable. IRQ 32. Master Capable. Latency=255. Min
Gnt=192.Max Lat=192.
      Non-prefetchable 32 bit memory at 0x9020000 [0x9020000].
      Non-prefetchable 32 bit memory at 0x9800000 [0x9800000].
      Non-prefetchable 32 bit memory at 0xa000000 [0xa000000].

It was recognized and configured without a hitch.

Unless you have one of the new UPx000 boards, I don't think
other Alphas have AGP. Also, I believe there are new Permedia
cards that aren't supported.
