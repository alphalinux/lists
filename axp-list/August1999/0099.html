I'm no user of IDE hard disks, but I would think that if you are only
using 1/2 the theoretical bandwidth (16 MBps on standard EIDE?) as it
is, quadrupling that theoretical bandwidth isn't going to help that
much. You might get a speed increase from the switch in chipset if the
Promise chip is better supported, though.
