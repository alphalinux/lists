The first time I tried to build it, I didn't notice the hgl
directory... it's going much better now. bmake in the hgl directory
just worked, and:

CVSHOME=/software/ishmail ./bmake
(after going into apps/hgl/linux and making the symlinks:
        ln -s . usr
        ln -s . include
        ln -s . lib
        ln -s .. hgl
I obviously don't understand their build environment. I tried to get
around this other ways first, but eventually settled on this.)

works for a while. There are a few warnings about void* being cast to
an enum in ComplexMsgFindWinC.C.

Because I don't have Motif, and LessTif installed some things slightly
strangely (like there isn't a libXm.a file) I did a search and replace
on bmake to replace $MLIBDIR/libXm.a with -lXm but that caused a
problem because they use the LIBS variable as a dependency, so I added
the empty rule:
        -lXm:
to the Makefile.

Then it builds. I ran the ishmail.shmx file and it brings up a
window. I wasn't sure quite what it wanted, so I hit help, and it
segfaulted. It might be the casting above, or not. Enough on this
for now. Over to someone else...
