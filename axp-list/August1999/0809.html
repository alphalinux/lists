I have a PC164 w/ 256RAM and an Adaptec AIC-7881U SCSI card. My disks
and CDROM are on the SCSI, except for a single IDE drive.

I compiled 2.2.11 with egcs 1.1.2 and the kernel wouldn't even boot.
It got stuck printing (slowly) SCSI Reset Timed out or somesuch and
never got past initializing the SCSI bus.

I compiled 2.2.12 and although it booted and ran for a while, it
eventually hung with the disk light stuck on. I don't have a lot of
time to fuss with things, so I just went back to my working 2.2.9
kernel.

It seems like the problem is with the SCSI, perhaps the Adaptec
board in particular. My 2.2.9 kernel seems to be working just fine.
