I am running my old UDB box as a firewall now and it doesn't have a
monitor, keyboard or mouse attached. I just did the install with them,
then shutdown, remove everything bar the power and two ethernet cables and
brought it back up. It runs fine and has been up around three weeks since
I last rebooted it to apply updates and upgrade the kernel. This is under
RH6.0 which seems very happy and stable on the alphas (UDB, 4x164LX and
1x164SX currently) which is at odds with my experience with a Celeron 400
which seems to fall over every few days.
