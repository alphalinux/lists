That's not quite right. It's www.alsa-project.org - www.alsa.org was
already taken when we changed from alsa.jcu.cz. Btw, if you're willing to
change the PCI DMA window on your Alpha, the Hoontech NX card (uses the
trident NX chipset) is a really nice card that works perfectly well under
Linux/Alpha with a 2.2 kernel. It has things like an S/PDIF output and
support for hardware mixing of programs, so I can play games and have mp3s
running in the background, while using speak-freely to chat with people.
:-)
        Not a plug, just mentioning some of the possibilities for
Linux/Alpha with alsa. I'm pretty happy on that front.
