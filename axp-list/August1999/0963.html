The only thing that I can mention is that some of the Powerstorm series (I
don't know if all) have a cirrus chip on them for handling standard vga
stuff - not to be confused with the Evans&Southorland that actually handles
the hard stuff and makes the card so good. However, there are no drivers for
any Powerstorm cards under Linux. This means either it works for you with the
cirrus or not at all.
For reference I have a powerstorm 4d30T. I have run RH 5.2 on it without an
problems (other than you can only load the black and white X server.)
