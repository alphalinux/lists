On Tue, 3 Aug 1999, Darin Walters wrote:

> Hello all
>
> I am curious if anyone has information on load averages. Is redhat
> tapped out at 10. Do they recommend keeping it under a specific load
> average for performance?
>
> I am running a box with just sendmail and the average tends to hoover
> around 15. I am thinking I need a CPU upgrade to get my load down
> some. Does anyone have input?

The ideal is < 1 on a single CPU system. The load is the number of
processes that are waiting to get some CPU cycles

I would suggest replacing sendmail with a more efficient MTA. Sendmail is
not the most efficeient MTA for high load situations. I use zmailer but
you could use Qmail or Postfix.
