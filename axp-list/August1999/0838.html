On Tue, 31 Aug 1999, nm wrote:

> Well I have ran Redhat on my AXPPCI33 based system (NOT a multia/UDB), and
> I thought it would be a nice change to run digital unix on it for a while
> since my school has a special program that allows me to run it (with 1 user).
>
> I own two graphics cards. A matrox millenium (1) and an S3 virge card. Both
> are PCI 4 meg cards. With the matrox card I can get CrappyVGA
> (640x480 16 colors). Is there anyway I can get a better resolution under X
> with digital unix (4.0D) and either of my two video cards?

No way. You need an old S3 Trio64 card (not all of them will do, though)
or an Elsa GLoria Synergy card. These are the cheapest options for up to
1280x1024 under Digital Unix. Currently the Elsa card seems to be the
only affordable way to get state-of-the-art graphics capabilities.

Unfortunately there's no XFree port to Digital Unix. You could earn
lots of credits by porting XFree to DU. Any volunteers out there ?
