Greetings,

After a blistering-fast 10-hour compile on my war-hero 233Mhz 21064, I have
a mozilla binary, and scores of libraries. It runs. It even (correctly)
renders some medium-complexity web pages. It bombs on others. But all in
all, it is extremely ssllooww, and the widgets are quite homely. Homely in
that it's both ugly, but at the same time deserving of sympathy. You feel
sorry for it, and you run it anyway. "You're beautiful on the inside".

What are your experiences?
