The DS10s I've installed RH6.0 with no problems had QLOGIC scsi.
I've encountered the NCR/SYM5C8XX scsi problems on a DS20 but
have never successfully gotten the kernel to boot - scsi hangs
during disk initialization.

Concerning the statements below: From the various combos Jay and
I have tried, the below statements are true on DS20s (don't know if
Jay has tried DS10s). However, enabling SMP "makes" the problem
go away (compiling on RH6.0). If you're up to it, give it a try...
