Richard Henderson wrote:
> This has been successfully tested on pyxis (sx164), cia (pc164),
> and tsunami (ds20). As of last night it nearly, but didn't quite,
> work on mcpcia (as4100). More work to do there, but this patch

There's a minor typo in the pyxis support:

--- include/asm-alpha/core_pyxis.h.OLD Sat Aug 31 01:40:17 2019
+++ include/asm-alpha/core_pyxis.h Sat Aug 31 01:40:37 2019
@@ -443,7 +443,7 @@
 #define __raw_readb(addr) __readb((addr))
 #define __raw_readw(addr) __readw((addr))
 #define __raw_writeb(b, addr) __writeb((b),(addr))
-#define __raw_writeb(b, addr) __writew((b),(addr))
+#define __raw_writew(b, addr) __writew((b),(addr))
 #define __raw_readl(a) __readl((unsigned long)(a))
 #define __raw_readq(a) __readq((unsigned long)(a))
 #define __raw_writel(v,a) __writel((v),(unsigned long)(a))

With that fixed it compiles clean and seems to work on my SX164.
