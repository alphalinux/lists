rich wrote:
>
> Hi,
>
> Just thought I would ask the group... Where have you had best pricing
> and which platform do you find best "bang for the buck" when looking
> for a good desktop alpha? I guess I am looking for a good vendor -- best
> pricing, and a good reputation. Any recommendations would be greatly
> appreciated. I am looking to purchase between 6-12 systems.
>

That would depend on your price range, and how much
you want state of the art performance. I have a PC164,
PC164LX, and XP1000. I'd go for the XP1000 over the
first two any day. It's a bit pricey though and if you
want good bang for the buck, a DS10 might be what you're
looking for. Again, pricing is an issue. An XP1000 goes
for $7000-8000. A DS10 is $3000-4000. If you're not
interested in paying for state of the art, then consider
a PC164SX, or a PC164LX. They should be quite a bit cheaper
than the first two since they are a generation back from
the DS10/XP1000. If you're thinking low buck, then those
cheap Multias might be worthwhile but they sound pretty
labor intensive to get going. Don't own one but just from
the comments on the list, they seem problematic.

Just my $.02.
