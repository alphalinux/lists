1) what is mean? why do not swriteboot ? >> ./swriteboot -f3
/dev/sda /boot/bootlx Existing disk label is corrupt <--- what?
Couldn'tget a valid disk label, exiting

Did you use BSD disklabeling to create the partitions? If you did,
you must have destroyed it carelessly. This message means aboot can not
find BSD disklabel in your hard disk. As a consequence, aboot can not
find the vmlinux.gz on your hard disk. So the system will hang.

For detailed information about BSD labeling, please refer to SRM howto, or
come to my website for a quick guide.
