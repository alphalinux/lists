>
> On Tue, 31 Aug 1999, nm wrote:
>
> > Elsa Gloria huh? I will see if I can find one.
>
> It might not be that easy, though. Apparently there's a DEC OEM'd version
> of the card which is more expensive than the original ELSA version.
> However I have contratictory reports about these two versions: Some people
> have claimed that the original ELSA version did not work with DU, others
> (namely a DEC/CPQ employee in comp.sys.dec or was it comp.os.unix.osf1?)
> said that the DEC OEM'd version is not modified in any way.

IIRC "standard" ELSA Gloria works at least with DU 4.0e but this may
be not the case with earlier revisions. I do not know for sure one way or
another.

Regular S3 video cards (something which will be hard to find nowadays)
did work with various variants of 4.0 but often had, ahem, "interesting"
visual effects on a text console. :-)
