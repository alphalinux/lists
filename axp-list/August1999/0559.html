> I get close, as the log below shows. I compiled the 2.2.11 kernel (with
> serial+serial console support), the updates redhat-6.0 ramdisk. Anyone
> have some hints out there?

You aren't close.

You need to unpack the ramdisk and change /dev/tty[1-4] to point to
the serial ports instead of the virtual terminals. But even then no
one has reported success with the redhat install program, which wants
to open all 4 of them, and you only actually have 2. It *might* work,
but... personally I installed one with a video card, and then cloned
from there.

> Freeing unused kernel memory: 120k freed
> Warning: unable to open an initial console.

The last line here is init saying "Hey! /dev/console isn't a valid
device!" You can grep in the kernel to see where.
