On Thu, 26 Aug 1999, Peter Lizon (home) wrote:

> Ok,
>
> let the beginner questions begin: The RedHat site says there is no SoundCard
> and scanner support on AlphaLinux. Is this true? To what extent?I mostly
> have plain SB-16s in my Alphas an I also have a few HP-ScanJet 4c scanners
> that were the only scanners that ran on AlphaNT since day one . .

Soundcards are very much supported, especially the SB16 cards, those work
very well. Some of the newer PCI cards don't seem to work that well (at
least that's what I remember reading here).

As for scanners you'll want to look for the SANE project. If my
history is right, when David Mosberger-Tang started the SANE project, most
if not all the work was done on Alphas, so it should work there!
