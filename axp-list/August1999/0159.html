Thanks to all on the list for your help.

There were a lot of great tips and suggestions. However, the resolution turned out that I had to do the install of RH using FDISK (but NOT BSD labeling (yet!)), and then label w/ BSD style after the initial install. I had been trying to do the install w/BSD labeling from the beginning. After that, things went very smoothly and I now have a bootable RH 5.2 Dec Alpha system. Now that I understand what my problem was, it is time to go to 6.0, set-up IP masquerading.........

Thanks again to all!
