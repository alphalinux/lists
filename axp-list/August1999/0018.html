> A default 'ev6' configuration indeed required new assembler and linker
> (which I recompiled from the latest binutuls I found on egcs.cygnus.com;

Indeed, the problem is in the as - it complains about:

> > /tmp/cc2jzCeM.s:3711: Error: unknown opcode `sqrttsu'
> > /tmp/cc2jzCeM.s:3781: Error: unknown opcode `sqrttsu'

What really surprised me was that egcs 19990712 (three weeks old)
built just fine. Considering that it was proclaimed to be in a "deep
freeze" one would at least expect no major changes...

In my case such issues do matter - I'm dealing with production ultra
high-availability machines (no downtime allowed), so moving to a new
"unofficial" (since RedHat won't release it) binutils would require a
huge amount of testing. OTOH, staying with the "official" release of
binutils leaves me with two "stable" releases of egcs (1.0.3 and
1.1.2) each of which has well-known bugs that could bite us every
minute...
