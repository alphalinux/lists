> What windowmanager is generaly considered to be the most stable/supported one out there(MetroLink, Xi or Xfree86)? Preferably hardware accelerated stuff.

Ah, you're asking about X Servers. Window managers are things like dtwm,
windowmaker, enlightenment, blackbox, kwm etc......

As for X servers, Xfree86 is fairly stable, I've not had to much traouble
with it (except for the X Font Server that is). MetroLink currently has
their OpenGL hardware acceleration in beta and I think Xig is going to do
OpenGL HA as well. though I can't remember seeing anything official from
them.
 
> The same goes for Audio(OSS or something else)?

I've had better luck witg ALSA (www.alsa-project.org) then with the OSS
stuff, but of course YMMV.
