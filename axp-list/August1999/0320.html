On Fri, 13 Aug 1999 pancho@galaxy.lca.uevora.pt wrote:

> Hi,
>
> I am looking for a SCSI controller for my Multia. Would anyone please give
> me any indications where to find these? Is there any chance I can add
> another SCSI controller, besides the original, to the Multia machine?

PCI SCSI controllers should work if you have PCI riser for the Multia,
otherwise your option is PCMCIA. In either case, just make sure the
card is based on a well-supported (ie, not the necessarily the newest
wiz-bang SCSI chipset) chipset.
