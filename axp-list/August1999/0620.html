Pn the hang problem:
check that your kernel is not a link!
Redhat likes to bundle these things with the kernel linked like:

        /boot/vmlinux -> vmlinux-2.2.5.16.<whatever>

If your booting from SRM that won't work. aboot won't follow links.
Blow the link away, and mv the vmlinux-2.2.<whatever> to vmlinux (or
vmlinux.gz depending).
and you should be ok.

Ric
