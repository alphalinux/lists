On Mon, 23 Aug 1999, Phillip Ezolt wrote:
> I just tried 2.3.15-pre2 on my AS500, and it hangs after aboot says "now
> starting kernel".

Same here (on a Ruffian). I run 2.3.6 without problems, after that
there always is something broken.
For 2.2.8 and later you also need:

--- linux/arch/alpha/kernel/entry.S Thu Jun 24 12:12:39 1999
+++ linux-2.3/arch/alpha/kernel/entry.S Sun May 30 20:22:06 1999
@@ -525,8 +525,7 @@
        .prologue 0
        bsr $1,do_switch_stack
        call_pal PAL_swpctx
- lda $16,-2($31)
- call_pal PAL_tbi
+ unop
        bsr $1,undo_switch_stack
        mov $17,$0
        ret $31,($26),1
