>>> Padiyath Kumar said:
>
> On the card I could read the following:
>
> GXE64
> 86C764X
> Number Nine and
> S3-trio64

A problematic card in the AS2x0 boxes.

Change the Device section to the following:

Section "Device"
    Identifier "Trio32/Trio64"
    VendorName "Unknown"
    BoardName "Unknown"
    VideoRam 2048
    Option "no_pci_disconnect"
    Option "sw_cursor"
EndSection
