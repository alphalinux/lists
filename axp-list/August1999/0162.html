On Mon, 9 Aug 1999, David Reitz wrote:

> hello everyone,
>
> i have a multia with redhat 6.0 on it, and i would like to know if it is
> possible to run it w/o a monitor or keyboard? if so, how do i go about
> doing this?

Yeah .. of course its possible. Just install, set up your network, and
unplug the monitor and keyboard.

If you don't have a network, then you'll to set up a serial line or
something to another computer to access the multia.
