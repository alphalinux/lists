Get the latest ramdisk from their errata site. And do not use the first
(cylinder 1) and the LAST cylinder, when creating the label, the room at the
begining is for aboot, skipping the last cylinder is to prevent wrapping,
wiping out your label when you write.

Do not use the defaults, delete them.
general example if you had 211 cylinder on the disk

a cyl 2-100 (ext2)
b 101-200 (swap)
c 201-210 (msdos or ext2)
