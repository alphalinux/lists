Aakin Nipun Patel wrote:
>
> : Unless you have one of the new UPx000 boards, I don't think
> : other Alphas have AGP. Also, I believe there are new Permedia
> : cards that aren't supported.
>
> I believe that this is one of the older cards, so it ought to be
> supported. How do I check to see whether or not it is an UPx000 board?

If the connector is white, it's a PCI slot. I believe AGP
use a brown plastic connector and there is only one.
The UPx000 boards are in beta test so having one would be unusual.
Besides, you said it was a PC164 board. They do not have AGP
on any version.
