Yes, but beyond all this, the RedHat installer doesn't work correctly on serial
console. Even once you hack together the right pieces (fix the /dev stuff),
the last thing you'll see is something akin to "Starting installer". I brought
this up about a month ago and rth said that davem had fixed a seemingly
identical problem on sparc. I'm still waiting to hear how they fixed it so we
can move that on into alpha - try asking him/them yourself; if you ping enough
it just might make it through any mail filters.

the message. Just keep pinging...
