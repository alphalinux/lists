Alpha Servers often have a case switch, i.e. a switch that detects if the
case is opened. If the case is open it shows exactly the behaviour you
explained.
So have a look at your case, search for the switch and examine if it broken
or still in place. Be sure to close the case properly so the switch makes
contact.
