> I have a PC164 and according to the manual, all that it
> accepts is True parity memory.

The PC164 requires 4 or 8 matched 72-pin simms.
All of the memory should be 36-bit( (8+1)*4 ) FPM (Fast Page Mode).
EDO won't work.
