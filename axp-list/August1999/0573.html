On Wed, 25 Aug 1999, Imran Siddiq wrote:

Try:

swriteboot /dev/sda /boot/bootlx -f3

the -f3 is only necessary if you've made the 'c' partition the entire
disk. Remember, you want the disk to be bootable, not the
partition.

This may also help:
http://www.alphalinux.org/faq/srm.html

If you get this error again (with the above command) then chances are you
didn't partition with BSD disklabels.
