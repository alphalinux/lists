The 3300 is the NT only :^) version of the AS800, It runs Linux just fine.
Just switch it to the SRM console (its in the firmware). and use a Generic
kernel. I've had good luck with 2.2.11
To switch type F2 during powerup and get to the setup menu, select CMOS,
then Advanced (F6), Console selection is 3/4 way down the screen. Select
"SRM UNIX" follow instruction to save settings (F10).
then Power cycle.

If you ever need to switch back to AlphaBIOS from SRM type "set os_type nt"
at the srm console prompt ">>>" and power cycle.
