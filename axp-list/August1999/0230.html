With just the cheetah and cdrom, it is stable. With 2 additional
internal disks, it seemed a bit unreliable so I put the other
disks in an external case and routed a UW jumper cable out a
back slot. It does get hot, particularly on top where the processor
is. I've found those disk cooler fan plates to be quite
helpful in reducing disk heat. ($10-$20ea)
