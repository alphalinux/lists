"Alex.Lee" wrote:
>
> Dear all:
> Sorry to bother you if you're not interested in this, or maybe someone
> asked it before!!
> After I installed official Redhat 6.0, finally I can use X-windows with my
> Diamond FireGL 1000PRO!!
> But I got a problem:
> When I try to mount floppy use "mount -t vfat /dev/fd0 /mnt/floppy", it
> says: vfat support by kernel!! Neither than my NT's hard drive.!!
> What's going on? I think the kernel I use is bundle with RH6.0 (kernel
> 2.2.5, I think.)
>

Your message isn't clear but I think you're trying to say
you can't mount a floppy. Perhaps your kernel has vfat as
a module in which case you need to do an insmod to allow
mounting DOS partitions. If your NT partition is NTFS, I
don't think there is complete support for it in the Linux
kernel. If it's a DOS partition, then it could be suffering
from the same problem as the floppy.
