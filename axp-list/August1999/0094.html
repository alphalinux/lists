try this:

start the install again, when you get to the part where you use fdisk, switch
the the "console" with a '#' prompt. Alt-F4 I think. Then do a

dd if=/dev/zero of=/dev/sda count=8 (substitute /dev/sda with your disk if
                                      needed).

this will wipe out partition info. Then go back to the install console
and do fdisk. Make sure you start partition a at cylinder 2!!! Then
make partitions b,g, and h or b,d,e,f depending on your taste.
