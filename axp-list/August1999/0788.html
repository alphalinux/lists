> What are the possible side effects of changing this window?
You have to recompile your kernel and all loadable kernel modules.
Nothing really past that. The window is meant to be configurable, so this
is just configuring it to be different. THe trident chipset is broken and
can only do 30 bit addressing (not dword, either) and so needs its DMA
buffers to be within the first gig of RAM. Normally the pci dma window
starts at 1 Gig, I just moved the window down to 512 MBs starting at 512
MB.
        Not all alpha chipsets may require this, though, not all alphas
map their dma the same way, I don't think.
