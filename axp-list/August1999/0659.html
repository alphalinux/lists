Ok, for the sake of posterity, and to make the search for the next person
who's in the same pickle I was a little easier, here's what was wrong and
what I did to fix it:

First, if you're using a windows box, which I was, you MUST use mkboot to
copy fwupdate.exe onto the floppy.

Second, if your SRM console is in secure mode, like mine was, you gotta
clear it.. Log in and type 'clear password'

And, finally, you should then be able to type 'boot dva0', and much
rejoicing shall occur.

this was on an alphastation 200 4/233. I hope this makes the next person's
life a little easier. :)
