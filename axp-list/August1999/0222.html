Eric Jones wrote:

> I picked up a card with the smb 53c810a chipset a few weeks back, and it
> has worked flawlessly with my PC164 machine. I snagged the patch for
> 2.2.11, built a new kernel, and now the ncr detection craps out with a
> "enable caching or write-through" error. The README in linux/drivers/scsi
> for the ncr driver has a pcifix option that supposedly does something to
> this, but it only works for intel. Has anyone seen this error before?
>

I just ran headlong into this type of problem with my rawhide which has a
builtin ncr53c810 (note the lack of an "a" at the end). Lucky for you, you
can use the sym53c8xx driver - try that and see what happens. The 810a is
supposed to be supported by the sym driver.

 - Pete (who's still trying to figure out why the qlogic driver is now
completely toast as well...)
