hi all!

just wanted to let you know that some problems I had with rh6 on
Linux/AXP, such as

* ghostscript having problems with displaying some kind of images/bitmaps
* xfig being unable to display splines
* xfs crashing (eg. when opening xterms)
* ...

where solved (for me at least) by recompiling the corresponding src.rpm's
with gcc 2.95 (recompiling them with rh6's egcs-1.1.2 wouldn't help)

btw: gcc 2.95 builds successfully on alphapca56-unknown-linux-gnu with the
binutils shipped with rh6
