On Tue, Aug 24, 1999 at 10:13:49AM -0400, Champigny, Michael wrote:
>
> Has anyone seen this behavior? I got a TGA-2 (ZLXP-E2) working in
> 24 bit mode on an AlphaStation 200, but I'm getting a "wavy" display
> effect in X. :-( It really is annoying, plus I get an intermittent screen
> black out.

Mostly, when I've seen 'wavy' displays were either because the
monitor was on its "last legs" or it was being pushed too hard.

I'd try the following:
1. a different monitor
2. adding "(+ or -) hsync" (or vsync) to your modelines
3. reducing your refresh.
4. running at a lower resolution than the monitor is rated for.

If (#1 or #4) work, but (#2 and #3) don't, there's a good chance
that your monitor is on the way out.
