On Sun, 29 Aug 1999, Michael Rich wrote:

> i entered the wrong ip address (192.168.1.75) during setup, and was
> wondering if someone could explain how to change it to 192.168.0.75.

I presume your Running RedHat? If so you want to run the RedHat
control-panel application (It is probably on a menu somewhere - failing
that type control-panel at a shell as root).

Click on the network icon, you get a box giving you the option of
names/hosts/interface/routing and select 'interfaces'. Click on your
ethernet interace and click on 'edit'.
