This is a part of kernel sources so you have to have at least kernel
headers installed, with 'asm-alpha' linked to 'asm', and search through
these. Actually it is only needed to get a few "magical constants"
so you shoudl be able to get away with these instead:

#define SSIN_UACPROC 6
#define UAC_SIGBUS 4
#define UAC_NOPRINT 1
#define SSI_NVPAIRS 1
