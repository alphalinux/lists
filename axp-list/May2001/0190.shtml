According to Jay Estabrook:
"These primarily fix problems found on pre-EV6 CPU machines, though some
patches fix generic problems."

I believe these patches will be included in the next XFree86 4.x
release. I had the same problems with XFree86 4.0.3 on a Miata (PWS
600a) and an ES40, although these happened to me while running
Slackware. Nevertheless, applying the patch listed above fixed the
problems. No more X lockups. I give it two thumbs up. *grin*

Unfortunately, I rebuilt XFree86 as a Slackware package rather than a RPM,
so I can't just give you a copy of the patched server and send you on your
merry way. perhaps someone else has created/will create an updated RPM.

good luck!
