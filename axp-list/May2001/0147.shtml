On Thu, May 24, 2001 at 09:46:28AM -0400, Kurt Ludwig wrote:
>
> I've seen differences between what drivers were included in a particular SRM
> relase. for example, I've seen releases of SRM where adaptecs work on LX's
> but not on Miatas...

Those folks with LX and SX machines lucked out; when the Adaptec
drivers were ported to SRM for the EV6 machines, the engineer who
builds LX and SX SRM consoles happily built those with the new driver
as well... :-)

Unfortunately, MIATA SRM console does not support Adaptecs, since it
stopped being updated before the driver was ported.

I believe all the recent EV6 machines' SRM consoles support some
Adaptecs, but I'm not sure if that includes the U2W and 160 ones.
