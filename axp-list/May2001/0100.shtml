Daniel

You should order the drives that you need for the system new, already in the
drive carriers. The carriers are very expensive as a FRU (Field replaceable
Unit) orderable item. New drives w/ carriers are less expensive.

18.2GB Ultra2 SCSI 7.2K rpm 3R-A0527-AA
18.2GB Ultra3 SCSI 10K rpm 3R-A0585-AA
36.4GB Ultra3 SCSI 10K rpm 3R-A0919-AA

These are Compaq part numbers. Use one of these.
Also, by adding purchased drives from outside of Compaq, Compaq support
frowns on supporting these drives. Even though (by law) they have too. These
are the same drives used in the Storage Building Block system for the large
External Drive Arrays.
