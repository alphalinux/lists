I just got two DS20E's and I would like to add a couple of big drives to
them for large calculations. Unlike my XP-1000's, which have simple drive
brackets (like a PC), however, the DS20E comes with a six-slot drive cage.
Each of the 1" drives are inserted into the cage with a "drive carrier",
which locks them into place and connects them to the Ultra2 adapter.

My question: where can I buy drive carriers? Our local computer dealers know
not one whit of such things, and the documents resulting from a search of
the Compaq site make me worry that there will be limited interchangeability
(i.e., that I'll have to buy Compaq disks for this to work).
