I was just thinking the same thing a couple of days ago as we had
90+ degree weather here in Portland, Oregon. :-)

I too have a LX. It has four fans + the cpu fan. Sometimes I think
the two IBM drives I have in it make more noise than the fans. Still,
I'd really like to cut out the noise. Interestingly enough before
this balmy heat wave, /. had a article on a watercooled case.
To me, it looks very interesting and tempting. I would appriciate
any feedback. The company selling them is here: http://www.koolance.com
My only thoughts on this case are the whether on not the cooler
attachment would fit over a Alpha 21164A CPU. In a worst case scenario,
I think I may have to leave the heatsink on it and put the cooler on the
heatsink. I'm not sure how efficient that would be. Then there's the
other matter of the CPU fan ok indicator to the motherboard... I
imagine
that the fan will still need to be connected so the board did not shut
itself down.

Can anyone else please share any other thoughts/ideas/experiences?

Alan
