You need a compatible controller - ADAPTEC controllers DO NOT work through
the SRM

You will need a Qlogic controller - we sell them - or an NCR 53C875/895 that
is supported on SRM

Be careful - the Koutech 53C875 controller does not work - Checksum errors
etc
