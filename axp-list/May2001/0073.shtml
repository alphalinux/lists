> One thing that I would like to put together would be a list of kernel
> choices for common alphas. For instance, which modules to compile for
> sound on a multia, or which modules support the built in ethernet on
> (insert your favorite alpha here). This is pretty important with things
> like the PC164, the LX and SX, which all have slight little quirks in
> kernel compilation that would get the most out of them.
>
> I've been told that you should enable PCI quirks for my PC 164. That was

> we can add a page to ALO?

> I'll start off
>
> Things to remember when compiling your kernel on a Multia (UDB)
> With a Multia (that cheap cheap old old alpha)
>
> Use the "Microsoft Sound" module
> Do not compile in any MIDI support
> IRQ = 9
> DMA = 3
> I/O = 530
>
> The scsi adapter is an NCR 53c810
> The ethernet usually DEC 21040 10BaseT
> TGA video
