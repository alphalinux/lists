I'm having difficulty finding some info on recovering
from the forbidable "bad magic number" superblock
corruption issue associated with a) dorking out and

procedures with regards to installing new software c)
hardware problem forcing a hard reboot from the PS/2
power switch on the power supply.

alternate supperblocks for a UFS partition (which
