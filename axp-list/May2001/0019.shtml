With regards to your message at 10:39 AM 5/8/01, Uncle George. Where you
stated:
>does any one know where exactly I can get a copy of the 53c825
>(programming) manual? searching through NCR does not get any results.
>Nor At Compaq. :-/
>Calling the NCR/800 number does not do any good either, and so far the
>literature center has not replied.
NCR SCSI got bought out by Hyundai, who sold it to Symbios Logic, who got
bought out by LSI..

So, the LSI site is the best bet, I think!
If the card in question was produced by an OEM company using hteir chips,
the support contact list is at:
http://www.lsilogic.com/support/support+drivers/otheroem.html

If you want developer information and code for the 53C8xx family, then you
need to agree to the terms and conditions on a webpage at:
http://www.lsilogic.com/support/support+drivers/license-develop.html

That is the entry point for tools and code on these SCSI chips..
