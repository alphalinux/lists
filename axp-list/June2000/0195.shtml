ok here's the deal on the 3300 (again)
to get out of alphaBIOS:
go into setup (F2)
go into CMOS/ BIOS settings (one of the 2... i forget which)
hit (f6) to get into advanced settings
tab down to the OS selection (should say windows NT)....
change it so that it reads either VMS (SRM) or Digital UNIX (SRM)
hit F10 to save
hit F10 again to save
at this point, power the machine off and back on again.

when it boots it should come up just to an SRM prompt, if not email me back
and I'll get you exact directions.

the 3300 series runs linux just fine assuming you have at least a 2.2.14
kernel and if it's that kernel, it needs to be compiled generic otherwise
it drops the kb/ mouse when you boot it. I'm not sure on newer kernels 'cuz
my DS3300 has been in a box since I got home from college =)
