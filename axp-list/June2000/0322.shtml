On Mon, 26 Jun 2000, Vock Reinhard wrote:
> but I run a DEC AS250 (4/266), RedHat 6.2, 2.2.15, and I want to activate
> the soundcard. Some fellow told me to use the ad1848 driver with io=0x533
> but that inevitably locks my machine.

that fellow assumed that you have a microsoft sound system card. there's
a good chance that's true, but you should best check inside your machine
to be sure. i finally got my mss card working with:

modprobe ad1848 inq=7 dma=3 io=0x530

good luck!

 -ross
