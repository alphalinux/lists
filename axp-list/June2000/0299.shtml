Well I figured it out... I'll post a little note on the 'proper' build order
for helix gnome later.
For those of you who want sawfish here ya go.

download and install the following packages from the helix-gnome directory.
You will have to upgarde over the existing rpms.

librep-0.11.3-0_helix_1.alpha.rpm
sawfish-0.27.2-0_helix_1.alpha.rpm
librep-devel-0.11.3-0_helix_1.alpha.rpm
sawfish-gnome-0.27.2-0_helix_1.alpha.rpm
rep-gtk-0.10-0_helix_1.alpha.rpm
sawfish-themer-0.27.2-0_helix_1.alpha.rpm
rep-gtk-gnome-0.10-0_helix_1.alpha.rpm
sawfish-themes-0.2-0_helix_1.alpha.rpm
rep-gtk-libglade-0.10-0_helix_1.alpha.rpm

Enjoy!
