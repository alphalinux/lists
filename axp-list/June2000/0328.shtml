The DAC960's (thats the chipset) are supported by the linux kernel BUT only
those with firmware rev v3 and higher. Most of the older DEC <cough> Compaq
controllers have only a v2 bios and AFAIR are currently unsupported. As for
booting from SRM, does it show up as a device/bus in SRM on the DP? You should
consult the axp-list archives for more info , this has been discused
thoroughly before.
