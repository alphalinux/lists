when you get to that nice screeen where it starts looking at your memory
and what you have on your SCSI controllers, hit F2
then go into CMOS setup
then hit F6 for advanced settings.
tab down to the OS selection and set it to "UNIX (SRM)"
F10 to save then F10 again
power cycle the machine and your all set

I have one of these (cept 400MHz) and it runs linux just fine. I had to
change it from AlphaBIOS to SRM so I could get to aboot though =)
