Similar but different:
On a 164UX4/533, my 21143 refuses to work with the tulip drivers. I get no messages printed or logged anywhere, and the hub's 100/10 like lights are on. On switching to the de4x5 driver (the tulip documentation recommends you switch im almost all cases to this driver) I am unable to use 100Mb, and from AUTO it reverts to 10Mb.
When recounting this tale in the past some people said "some of the chips are flaky, when they work they're lovely - when they don't they're 'orrid. Just put up with it." or words to that effect.

My advice is try de4x5 instead. I am not an expert, I am simply in the Victim's Support Group...
