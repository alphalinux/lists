>>> Eric Jones said:
> I have an STB PCI video card at home, and I am considering using it with my
> UDB.
> How exactly do you use secondary cards with these critters? Do I just build
> in VGA support into the kernel and stick my card in, or do I have to disable
> the built-in TGA card? If I have to disable it, *how* do I do so?

There's no way to disable the TGA; however, the ARC/SRM consoles will choose
to use the VGA over the TGA, should they find a VGA present.

So, simply plug it in, and move the monitor cable to the VGA output, and it
should all "just work".
