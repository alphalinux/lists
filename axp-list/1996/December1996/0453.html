Terry Carroll wrote:

> When shopping for a monitor, are there any UDB- or TGA- specific gotchas
> I should keep in mind, or should any old VGA monitor work okay?

I use my UDB with a iiyiama (sp?) monitor, which happens to be a great
monitor IMHO, and it works fine - I would suggest looking in the UDB
manual
for the various resolutions & scan frequencies the UDB supports and then
getting the best monitor you can afford that supports the highest
resolution
and scan freq. combination the UDB wants.

Don't skimp on the monitor (if you run it at 640x480 you are really
missing
out!) it will be "in your face everyday."
