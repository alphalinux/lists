| I'm installing RH 4.0 on a UDB (sorry) with 32M, on a 2.1G SCSI drive. Can
| anyone pass on any good suggestions/prejudices on partition arrangements
| and sizes? I figure the 2.1G is overkill for now, but I can use some
| partitions in unused space as backups for others.

Here's mine. You'd be wise to add a partition for /tmp as well.
My root partition is way oversized, due entirely to the fact
that I added the 2.1g drive after I had already installed on the
UDB's internal drive.

The Alpha, by the way, is extremely memory-hungry. I installed
64MB (for a total of 88), and it now works much, much more quickly.
