> Does anyone know if exists fortran 77 or 90 for axp running linux?

f2c (fort77 driver is preferable over f77 one) runs and basically
works. So far all hiccups I have seen can be worked around.

g77 project for axp is still in works (it can be run).

Microway claims to sell 77 compiler for Linux axp; I did not see
this one in action, but the company has a good opinion in general
(www.microway.com). Not sure if Absoft plans to move their Linux
compiler to axp (www.absoft.com).

I am not aware of any Fortran 90 compilers.
