- the gdb as shipped on rh4.0 has a patch missing that makes it
fail to understand how to load shared library info; I'm not
sure whether an updatd gdb RPM has been made available, Erik?

it impossible to backtrace or do any other meaningful debugging
when inside a shared library (that comes with rh4.0); I think/hope
RH plans to ship libraries in the future with enough debugging info
that makes at least backtracing possible
