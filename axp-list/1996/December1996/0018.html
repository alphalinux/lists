channels LINE1 LINE2 and LINE3 (LINE2 and 3 being the one making
the feedback (at least on my system)). Then you should atleast be
able to use MSS for playing sound, but recording seems flaky (asuming
that the static discharge did not destroy the MSS on my system 8-)).
