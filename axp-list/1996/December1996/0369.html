Daryll Strauss wrote:
>
> I did manage to get my Alpha box running, but just barely. I've got a
> easily reproducible kernel oops when I get multiple processes talking to
> the disk at once.
>
This sounds very like a similar problem i had with my AS/400 and the
NCR controller.

I´m quite sure the reason was the too high scsi-transfer rate.
(10 Mbyte/sec). Shortening the scsi-cable made things much better,
but the machine still crashed under heavy usage of diiferent hard-
disks at once. At last, i reduced the transfer rate to 5 Mbyte, and
the problems were gone.
