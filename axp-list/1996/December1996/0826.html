I have found that changing the UDB time in arc console, and then changing
/etc/rc.d/rc.sysinit from
time -A -a -u
to
time -A -a
works. There is an actual reason for this, having to do with arc and time
or offsets since the paleolithic era, but I am sure someone else will know
about that.
