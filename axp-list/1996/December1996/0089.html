On Tue, 3 Dec 1996, Terry Carroll wrote:

> have a 2G 3.5", so my query is whether there are any problems associated
> with putting a 3.5" in there -- special mounting brackets needed, power
> restrictions (physical or wattage), heat sensitivity, etc. -- that kind
> of thing.

In the UDB 40-F3 (the one I have), there is only a 3.5" hard disk of
340MB. There is space for it, but you loose your PCI expansion slot...

BTW, being the UDB as compact as it is, the internal SCSI cable should be
plied carefully between the internal SCSI port ant the output conector. In
my case this cable seems to be a little bit damaged, do you know if
Digital sells it as a spare (or there is a second source for it?).
