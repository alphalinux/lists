On 9 Dec 1996, Martin Ostermann wrote:

> 10.4 Can Linux/Alpha Run Without Graphics Card?
>
> The firmware can be controlled over a serial line terminal at 9600
> Baud and it is possible to boot Linux/Alpha in that manner, but Linux
> itself does not currently support headless boots. There are kernel
> patches floating on the net that add this functionality, however.
>
> Unfortunately, I have no idea who got those patches...

I have an old patch for linux to move the console to one serial port.
I havn't tried it on any new kernels, but I know it worked around 1.3.50
or something like that. One thing I didn't like about it though: It
totally erases the VC's so that I couldn't bring X up on my monosync
monitor while having the console on serial. But you probably won't have a
problem with that.

It's 864 lines long, so I won't send it to this mailing list since most
people aren't interrested in it I imagine. I have put it up on ftp though:

ftp://wopr.campus.luth.se/pub/linux/new_ser_con.diff
