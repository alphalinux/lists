Despite some comments otherwise Scott, the UDB ( and other AXP boards) use
parity 36 bit memory. A better page for you to look at might be:
http://www.dataram.com/bytes/echeck.htm#ecc
This goes into explaining the differences between 36 bit parity and 40 bit (
or sometimes 39 bit) ECC RAM.
