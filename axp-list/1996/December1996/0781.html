You didn't mention what type of scanner you will be using. If it is one
of the HP types xvscan is a very good combination of a driver and an
application program. It basically puts scanner support on top of the
xv program. I originally got it to compile on the alpha platform with
very minor changes and I understand that it will now automatically
detect that it is running on Linux Alpha and compile with no problems
at all.

It is "slightly" commercial ($50) and this incluides a registration for
xv (normally $25).

For more info check out www.tummy.com
