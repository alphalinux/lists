Hmm, I got this exact same error recently after trying to upgrade my UDB's
memory with what turned out to be BAD SIMMs. The ARC console registered the
new memory fine, but I saw the above error where you did and when I tried to
format my disks.

Took out the offending SIMMs and the process went smoothly...
