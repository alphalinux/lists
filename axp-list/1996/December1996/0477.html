Dear list,

The EB164 allows the ARC and SRM firmware to be installed concurently. I
have tried the same on the PC164 but they seem to overwrite each other. The
manual for the PC164 states that the board has 1MB of flash rom but that
it is divided into two banks, and that writing a 1 or a 0 to the
FLASH_ADR19 register at IO location 800h selects the upper or lower bank.

Neither the SRM nor the ARC seem to be aware of this, is there a smooth way
of using this feature the allow both the SRM and ARC to installed at the
same time?

Many thanks,
