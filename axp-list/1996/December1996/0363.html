Anyone know the number of heads, cylinders and sectors for the Quantum
Fireball 1280S?

I'm trying to install RedHat 4.0 and fdisk requires these to be manually
entered. While initializing, Linux displays "2503872 sectors for SCSI
device sda", but fdisk will not take a number of sectors above 63. (Heads
are limited to 256, and cylinders are limited to 65535.) Quantum's
website was no help, nor was the tech support of APS, where I got the
drive.

If anyone can help, I'd greatly appreciate it.

I have this info, which doesn't help me, but it may help you:

>Disk Drive Configuration
>
>Disks 2
>Heads/Recording Surfaces
> 4
>Tracks per Surface
> 4,142
>Sectors per Track
> 95 to 177
>Bytes per Sector
> 512
>Track Density (tpi)
> 4,270
>Flux Density (fci)
> 115,774
>Recording Density (bpi)
> 108,964
>Encoding Method
> 16/17 PRML
