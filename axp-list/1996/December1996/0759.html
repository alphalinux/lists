As a note: UPS "Standard" service from U.S. to Canada is quoted at $26.85 (Cdn).
This is about $20.00 U.S. Customs clearing fees and taxes may be additional,
as will be G.S.T. Depending on how it is cleared through Canadaina Customs,
a duty of up to 3.9% may be charged ( although technically this can be
cleared "duty-free". Total costs could conceivable run as high as $ 176 if
worst case for everything occurs.
Point to consider: If you are importing from U.S., it is probably best to do
your own customs clearing to save money. Either having goods shipped by
mail, or into a "bonded customs warehouse" gives you this option. Airline
Cargo will permit this, and is usually quite inexpensive. You will need to
figure out the customs tariff number for a computer, which can be trying.
Fortunately, having done it many times, I can tell you the number: 8471.49.10.00
This can all be a bit trying for the uninitiated, but having done it many
times, it is not insurmountable. Often people pay much higher rates for the
service of having it done for them. On a $10,000 shipment this is quite
reasonable. But on a $1000 or less one, the percentages are often pretty steep.
