Why don't you su to root in an xterm, then issue

/sbin/shutdown -h -t 10 &

then quit the window manager. In 10 seconds, shutdown will commence.

I believe the TGA server doesn't support virtual consoles while the X-server is
running. This is *NOT* the case if a PCI VGA card is used on a Multia. I have
a Diamond Stealth 64 Video VRAM installed with full virtual console support
with a running X-server.
