On Sun, 1 Dec 1996, Miguel Avillez wrote:

>
> hi people
>
> I have several questions to do:
>
> 1. in /proc exists a file named kcore
> is this a kernel core file? does its space has naything to do with the
> dimension of / ?
Right, this is not a real file and needs no space on the harddisk. All
files under /proc are virtual.

>
> 2. I have new disk and want to mv filesystems to that disk. Say I want to
> move / from the old disk to the new one. How do I do it?
>
I asume you have the new disk on /mnt, then do:

cd /
tar -cf - . |(cd /mnt;tar -xvf -)

Thats all.
