> > > 2. I have new disk and want to mv filesystems to that disk. Say I want to

> > >
> > I asume you have the new disk on /mnt, then do:
> >
> > cd /
> > tar -cf - . |(cd /mnt;tar -xvf -)
> >
>
> Does this conserves simbolic links?

It does, but it will loose some information. You rather need '-xpf'
on extract. Also this will try to copy /proc and a mount point.
Bad move! 'tar -C / -clf - . | tar -C /mnt -xpf -' will work nicer,
but 'cp -a' will be simpler and works just fine (exclude /proc and
a mout point from things to copy and do 'mkdir proc' on a top of
your new file system before mounting it).
