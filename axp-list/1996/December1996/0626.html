Yes. The constant that gcc is giving the assembler is not DBL_MIN
but in fact a denormal just smaller than that. And the default
FP exception flags signal underflows.

There is a patch: ftp.redhat.com:/pub/alphabits/rth-gcc-2.7.2-9611*.
It should be applied to the RPM from RH40.
