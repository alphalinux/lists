> I am trying to get ipfwadm running on my AS255, but I'm missing ld.so.1
> and libc.so.6. I checked on the CD but didn't see them there either.

If you don't have those libraries, you are running an ECOFF
system like RH 3.0.3 or before.

There's a bit more to things than just installing those two
files. Your best bet is to upgrade your entire system to an
ELF-based distribution -- something that is beneficial for
other reasons as well.

On the other hand you could just recompile ipfwadm yourself.
