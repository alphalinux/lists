bruce a. campbell wrote:
>
> chmod a+s /usr/X11R6/bin/XF_xx
> chown root /usr/X11R6/bin/XF_xx
>
> baruch@aod.com

Umm, I think that really wants to be:

chown root /usr/X11R6/bin/XF_xx
chmod u+s /usr/X11R6/bin/XF_xx

On most systems, a chown will automatically clear the suid flag to
prevent security problems. Additionally, you probably don't want it to
be suid world, do you?
