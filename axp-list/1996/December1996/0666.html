> Does any ISDN card work inside a DEC Alpha running Linux ?
>
> I would love to use the Teles PCMCIA inside my Multia/UDB, but I don't
> even know whether ISA cards would work.

The current isdn4linux driver does only support ISA cards (ICN and Teles) -
the optional HiSax driver (an improved version, read the isdn4linux
mailinglist about this one) supports some more Siemens-chipset based cards
than the stock teles driver.

I don't know it the driver works on an alpha, but I am quite sure that
PCMCIA cards are not yet supported.
