I have made a patch against insmod-bfd-0.2 which enables you to insmod
modules into linux-2.1.7 and newer. This patch ONLY works with newer
kernels since I didn't bother to check kernelversion and use the old
method if it's lower than 2.1.7.

Anyway, it's very small and simple and available at:

ftp://wopr.campus.luth.se/pub/axp/insmod-bfd-0.2-2.1.7+.diff
