My 233mhz UDB is configured with the 2 original 12 meg 70ns ecc simms in
the first two slots and 2 32 meg 60ns true parity simms in the other two
slots. When I first installed the additional 64 megs, I encountered some
problems getting both the ARC console and linux to recognize the
additional memory but after swapping the simms in and out a few times,
suddenly the 88 megs magically appeared. Sorry I can't offer any
additional help, I just removed and reinserted the simms a couple of
times. But it does appear as though the udb can handle a combination of
60ns & 70ns simms.
