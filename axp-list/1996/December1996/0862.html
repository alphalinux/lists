At 09:35 hrs. 12-25-96 -0500, you wrote:
>With the holidays at an end, I have enough money left over to buy a new video
>card for my Multia... does anyone have some suggestions on what would be a
>good option? I'm mainly interested in speed, and stability of the X Server.
>I was very pleased w/ the performance of the built-in TGA video, but can't live
>with 8-bit color. If anyone has any suggestions, successes, I'd like to hear
>about them. If a generic S3 card will do the trick, great, if not, I'd like
>something that will.
>
In my experience many of the S3 based products work very well. I have had
consistenly good results with the Diamond Stealth64 DRAM Video(2100 and
2200) and VRAM Video(3000, 3200, 3240, 3400) based cards. Beware of the
model "2001 Graphics", however, as it uses an ArkLogic chip which is ( I
believe) unsupported. Also, recently there is word of the Matrox Millenium
now working. If so, this is an excellent card, and _extremely_ fast.
