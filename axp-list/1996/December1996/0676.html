I've got a few more details on the problem I had with streams in
libg++. It turns out that the SRPM has the correct patch for libio.h to
get _IO_getc defined correctly. I built and installed the SRPM, but the
corrected libio.h doesn't get installed. RPM tells me that glibc-devel
owns /usr/include/libio.h. So, I guess that libg++ isn't building and
installing libio and that glibc-devel doesn't have the same
patch. Since, it is just some #defines in the header, I'll just copy
that around, but it should be fixed in the distribution, otherwise C++
is fairly unusable.
