> I was wondering how i would go about doing a low-level format
> of my scsi disk on an Alpha? I looked around in the SRM console and didn't
> find anything that I could reconise to do it.

AFAIK, you can't - you would have to call an external program to do that,
eg. by running it from the console or by booting a mini-Linux from floppy.

I always wanted to write such a tool, however, without a disk to test (needs
to have reasonably new firmware *and* bad blocks to test), there was no
chance and need.

Are you sure that you need to format your disk? All SCSI disks I know come
pre-formatted from the factory.
