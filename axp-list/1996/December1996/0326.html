> > > | I have entries in /etc/hosts, but they are not recognized.
> > > Put 'order local,bind' in your /etc/resolv.conf.
> > This doesnt work.
> Perhaps it should be: order hosts,bind

I had the same problem and the problem was fixed by doing something
a little different:

Put this in /etc/hosts.conf:

order hosts, bind

This is what I have in /etc/resolv.conf (hch.com is my bogus domain name):

search hch.com
nameserver 206.84.208.4
nameserver 208.84.208.3

Your local nameservers will of course differ.

> But you really need /etc/nsswitch.conf as mentioned earlier.

I agree along with everyone else on this.
Also make sure you have a 127.0.0.1 localhost loopback
in your /etc/hosts file.
