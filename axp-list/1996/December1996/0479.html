All,
I've updated the MILO sources and these will either now or soon be on gatekeeper.
I've made MILO able to guess what format an image is (one of zipped-elf, zipped-coff, elf
or coff). So, now you don't need to use the .gz suffix. The sources are in V2.0/src/
milo-2.0.27(-ELF).tar.gz and V2.1/src/milo-2.1.13.tar.gz, the 2.1.13 sources are ELF only.
Sometime before Christmas I will build all new MILO 2.0.27 (with Digital BIOS) images...
