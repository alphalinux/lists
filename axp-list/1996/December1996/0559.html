You asked about DIGITAL UNIX syscalls:

For mcr:
SYS_142(0x11ffff358, 0x11fffb210, 0x44, 0, 0x14001a6e8) = -1 EPERM
SYS_251(0x11fffb2d8, 0x11fffb2d0, 0, 0x140035fe0, 0x140035fc0) = 0
With maple
SYS_112(0, 0x11ffff500, 0, 0x11ffff4d8, 0x140026268) = -1 EPERM

The syscalls you observed are:

112 sigstack(instack, outstack) Sets and gets signal stack context
142 gethostid() Gets the unique identifier of the current host
251 usleep_thread not documented; it is called from sleep() and usleep()
in libc.

The above is based on reading the manual pages and listing contents of /lib/libc.a
