Noticing a lack of contributed RPMS for AXP on Redhat's site, I just uploaded a
couple of packages in ftp://ftp.redhat.com/pub/Incoming that may be of interest.

xfmail-1.0-1.axp.rpm - an X-windows (XForms-based) email agent that is
pretty nice. Check out the README.
xfmail-1.0-1.src.rpm - the source rpm for above

xfmail-1.0-1.i386.rpm - an i386 version of above

xforms-axp-elf-0.81-1.src.rpm - the XForms-0.81 library for AXP-ELF

All were build on RedHat 4.0.
