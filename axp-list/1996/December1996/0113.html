> The box, however, came with almost no technical information, which was

>
> 1. my 32Meg is in the form of two SIMMs. Is there any requirement on
> using, for example, the left or right pair of SIMM slots?
>
> 2. D I have to worry about any jumper settings, whether for the memory or
> anything else? If so, what?

I also own a UDB box that came with no documentation. After checking
around I found some sources of information about the memory system wtc,
and as far as I know the only restriction is that you must use parity
SIMMS (true 72 bit) as the memory system uses the extra bits for ECC. I
have tried normal SIMMS (non-parity) just for the hell of it and they were
not even detected.

My 2 SIMMS are in the rightmost sockets as viewed from the front.
