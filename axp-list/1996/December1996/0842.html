On Mon, 23 Dec 1996, Michael Champigny wrote:
> I've just set up XDM and XBanner for my UDB and it's working OK as far
> as logging in. However, I have no idea how to get back to a console in
> order to do an orderly shutdown. Whenever I exit my window manager, I'm
> thrown back into the login widget. Also, how do I jump into the "text"
> mode console and back into X again?

When you get back to the XDM login screen, you can type
Control-R, which will shut down X. You could also login
under XDM, open an xterm or whatever, su, and run
/sbin/shutdown from there.
