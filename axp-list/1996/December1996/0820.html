On Mon, 23 Dec 1996, Steven N. Hirsch wrote:

> I am always willing to be on the cutting edge, and am downloading the
> development glibc for Alpha as we speak.. Can you give me any hints as
> to why this is "not an easy upgrade"? What areas can problems be
> expected in?

Enough changes have been made to break associated programs like PAM (which
is used for all of your authentication). I've managed to upgrade a couple
of running systems by hand though. Try this sequence:

1) upgrade PAM and the primary glibc simultaneously
2) upgrade util-linux

Now, switch VC's and log in again. If you can't, try reading /var/log/messages
for clues. If you can, pat yourself on the back.

Now, everything that uses crypt() is broken as there is a new libcrypt(). I
don't know what these are anymore but this is a partial list of things that
want to link against it:

XFree86
apache
elm
mc
sh-utils
tcsh
util-linux
wu-ftpd

Good luck ;-)
