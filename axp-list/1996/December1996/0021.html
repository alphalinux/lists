> While we're on the subject, I'm only getting a constant high-pitched
> tone from my UDB sound system when I insmod the sound.o module.
> Here is my .config. I would appreciate it if someone could
> tell me if I've done anything wrong. Otherwise, I'll have to call
> the Digital Repair people.

[snip]

> CONFIG_ADLIB=y

I've got sound to work on the UDB (even audio digitizing). -- I'm running
a stock Redhat 4.0 Kernel (2.0.18) -- Sound support is compiled in the
kernel. -- I haven't turned on the "CONFIG_ADLIB" option in my
"soundconf".

At first audio digitizing did not work for me. -- I used a microphone
connected to the microphone jack of the UDB. -- The microphone was one of
those types that you'd normally use with your stereo set. (Dynamic
microphone, 500 Ohm impedance)

When I tried to digitize anything with that (known good) microphone, it
did not work at all (as if no microphone was connected to the socket).
Fiddling around with the various sliders in "aumix" did not help.

Then I replaced the microphone with an "el cheapo" one for about 7$ that
you can pin to your tie (I used that once for fooling around w. ears and
FVWM) -- and suddenly everything worked like a charm.

There are two things left that I haven't quite sorted out concerning sound
support:

+) All the audio mixers I've tried seem to behave rather strange (i.e:
master volume slider does not work, strange things happen when I fool
around with the "LineX" sliders in aumix...)

+) I've never got "/dev/sequencer" to work no matter which config-options
I've tried concerning midi and such.

Just my $0.02. -- I hope it helps a bit.. ;-)
