I installed a Stealth 64 Video VRAM last night on my UDB, and found
that I needed to have a kernel with TGA support disabled to get
screen output in VGA. Once I compiled the new kernel and booted off
of it, everything worked.

David

> I threw a Diamond Stealth 3D 3000 into my UDB last nite (tight fit!) and had
