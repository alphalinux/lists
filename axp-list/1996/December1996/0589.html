UNQUOTE

The current situation is (as of g77-0.5.19) that several problems
on 64 bit architectures aren't solved. Most notable of these is
COMPLEX (and DOUBLE COMPLEX) support, but other code could give
problems as well. The solution of this problems is the highest
priority item on the list of things to repair in g77. Stay tuned !
