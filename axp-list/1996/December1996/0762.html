> I am thinking of playing with the Matrox Millenium under Linux Redhat but I
> don't want to loose the ability to run Digital Unix. Will that card work
> with Digital UNIX 4.0?

I've been pursuing the same question regarding the Number Nine Motion
771, which uses the S3 Vision 968 graphics processor. After
extensively searching the official and unofficial DEC sites over the
last month, I find that the only drivers in DEC UNIX 4.0 are for the
ATI Mach 64, S3 Trio 64 and S3 Vision 864 third party graphics
processors. There was some talk on the alpha-osf-managers mailing list
(http://www.ornl.gov/its/archives/mailing-lists/alpha-osf-managers/1996/11/msg00473.html)
regarding what processors were used in the 3D10 PowerStorm card. The
consensus was that it uses the ATI Mach 64 processor.

Bottom line: There are no drivers for the Matrox Millenium card in the
DEC UNIX 4.0 distribution.

> Also will the current version of svgalib at least do basic graphics on
> the card?

Without a driver, you get no graphics at all. Without kernel
support, libraries are useless.

Stick with Linux!

Jon
