I'm not sure if you have a 166Mhz. UDB with the 340Mb. drive or the
233 version with the 540Mb.

The 233 units come with a Toshiba MK2526FB 540Mb. drive but their
performance is nothing to write home about.

IMHO the best bet is to go with an external SCSI drive. That way
you can get a high-speed high-capacity unit and not pay the premium
price for the small form factor drives.
