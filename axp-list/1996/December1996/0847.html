Don't know how much MIPS machines cost, but I really think the nice
burst in the Linux with Alpha is from the large number of low cost
machines that DEC was dumping on the market.

For a comparable quality intel system with the same options these were
very nice deals.

Therefore, I guess the question is where can I get a MIPS machine for
less than 1000 bucks that comes complete expect for monitor? I think
you would see similiar interest in MIPS.

I guess the question is will DEC pick on this new found market or will
it just drop it and go for the high end only??? I guess they really
can't make any money going for this market because the margins are so
slim. Anyway the Intel based clones would probably drop the price so
low it would drive them out the low end market anyway :-(. The
Intel/Cyrix/AMD, etc. marketing clout is so strong it would be hard
for any company to make a serious dent in the low end market. Kind of
like M$ I guess.
