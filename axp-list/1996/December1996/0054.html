In your kernel config, be sure that
CONFIG_SCSI_GENERIC_NCR5380 is not set; you don't need it to use the
NCR53C8xx drivers. I accidentally left it enabled, and encountered
the same symptoms: 'scsi bus busy' and then a hang.
