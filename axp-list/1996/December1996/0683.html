> I would love to use the Teles PCMCIA inside my Multia/UDB, but I don't
> even know whether ISA cards would work.

The Teles PCMCIA card is supposed to be compatible with their ISA card (S0?),
but AFAIK no one has made it work with Linux even on Intel boxes.

And AFAIK no one has tested the non-PCMCIA Teles linux drivers on Alpha.
It might or might not have the typical problems with sizes of longs and
pointers.

I would buy a Teles PCMCIA card to work with on my UDB, except that here in
the USA they cost over $500. I thought Teles cards were supposed to be cheap?
They certainly don't contain anything expensive. I'm tempted to start
manufacturing an equivalent card myself.
