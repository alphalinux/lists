Pim Zandbergen spake unto me, saying:
>
>
> "make menuconfig" on alpha hides certain configuration options
> that "make config" does not (eg. CONFIG_SCSI_NCR53C8XX).
>
> Is this intentional or a bug ?

As far as I can tell (from all of the helpful folks who assisted _me_
in getting 2.1.13 compiled...), "make menuconfig" simply lags behind
the bleeding edge of development. No one has gotten around to
updating it since some of those options were added, I guess. So it's
really neither intentional, nor precisely a bug.

Moral of the story? Use plain 'ol "make config", at least for the
time being.
