> That's a big problem. It look slike Digital Unix isn't using the rock
> ridge extensions.

OH OH OH!!!! I actually know the answer to that one. If you want rock
ridge exentsions under Digital Unix, you have to ask for them:

mount -t cdfs -o ro,rrip /dev/rz4c /mnt

should do the trick. The rrip part is what turns on rock ridge.
