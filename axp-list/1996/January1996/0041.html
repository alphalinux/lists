SysVinit 2.58 is not available on ftp.redhat.com in the updates directory.
I've tested it with 1.3.52, and it works fine. If you use is with older
kernels, you may get some kernel messages dumped to your screen that
start with "<sc". If this happens, upgrade your kernel (David MT recommends
1.3.52, and I'd listen to him).
