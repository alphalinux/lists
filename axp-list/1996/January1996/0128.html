I'd be interested in the results of these tests - please post findings to the list.
Personally I think that the Alpha machine (a standard noname in our case) does not *feel* at all nippy - despite the whopping BogoMIPs figure.
Now thats basically because the libraries and compiler are not optimised yet, and this, the lack of shared libraries, combined with alignment constraints (things align at 8 bytes (64 bit) rather than 32 bit), means that you use even more memory than you might expect.

Also, for our application, the inability to reboot is an absolute killer. A system that goes down and stays down is not good!
