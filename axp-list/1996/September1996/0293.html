OK folks, I've been checking around and have found that ITS (URL:
http://www.itsinfonet.com) (1-847-639-0775) has the best prices I can find. I
found them on the list of known suppliers on the FAQ at Red Hat. They do NOT
have any of these in stock, but are willing to order and can deliver in 10-14
days.

Although they sell the Noname (233?) boards, their best deals (IMHO) seem to
be:

VX40 (166Mhz) with 24MB, 340MB disk, etc: $550
This is the liquidated unit, 3 Mo warranty

VX42 (233Mhz) same config $750
Production unit, 3 year Digital Warranty

If you call them, _please_ ask to speak with Kevin Kaus. He was extremely
helpful in tracking down the availability and pricing on these units.

For the record, I have _not_ yet purchased one of these, nor am I being
compensated for posting this. It just seemed like a good deal to pass on the
results of my research.
