I don't recall seeing anybody answering this. The short answer: the
old math library is very incomplete. GNU libc comes with fdlibm and
it contains both of the functions you want. If you can wait, look
forward to one of the forthcoming ELF/glibc based distributions. If
you can't wait, you might want to grep the axp-list mailing list
archive at www.redhat.com for info on how to upgrade to glibc.
