Ben> Matter of fact, I just found out posting fails - in.nnrpd bombs
Ben> out from bad address 0. Everything else works like a charm ...

This is a side effect of inet_adresses declared in the source as
"unsigned long". In the "inn4-unoff4"-sources I found a document
UNOFF-NOTES/DEC-Alpha
which describes the neccessary changes (mostly "unsigned long" ->
"unsigned int").

After applying these patches and taking a suitable "config.data"
(mostly generated from the RedHat unoff3-rpm) there was just the need
of adding some includes in "include/clibrary.h", namely:

sys/time.h
sys/types.h
unistd.h

I have put all these patches together in a diff-file under (you'll
find the inn4-unoff4-sources also):

ftp://franck.pc.uni-koeln.de/pub

Hope this helps
Markus

Bye the way: Erik seems to be willing of creating a new "rpm"-packages
with these patches included.
