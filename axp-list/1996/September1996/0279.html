I just got off the phone with one of the marketing managers of the Multia...er,
ah, the UDB. She informs me that the last of the 233 systems will be shipped
from Digital this week. However, our Multivendor Customer Services group
(MCS) bought "a large number" of both types of systems for spare parts, and
she anticipates them supporting them "for some time".

There were a LOT of 166 UDBs sold, particularly at the end. When will they be
out of the channel of sales? I don't know. On the other hand, I anticipate
a large market of used UDBs floating around for some time, just as there is
a large market for 386s and other machines. Probably a note posted to a news
group would find you one or two in short order.
