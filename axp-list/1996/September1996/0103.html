Now that I got a working gdb, I was able to debug the important
stuff. xpilot (any version 3.[2345].x I tried, and the RedHat ELF-rpm)
crashes when compiled with gcc-2.7.2 with segmentation fault.

The oops location is in paint.c:1027, a very simple loop within a very
large function (teribble coding). Seems like the condition of the
if-loop doesn't get tested when compiled with -O2. Compiling paint.c
with just -O yields a working xpilot binary.
