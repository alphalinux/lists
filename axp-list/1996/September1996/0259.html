On Wed, 11 Sep 1996, Nils Faerber wrote:
>
> Just in case someone is interested in it I just wanted to report that I just
> managed to compile and run bochs-9608?? on my Jensen! And yes: My Jensen
> booted DOS like a real ... XT :(
> Yes it's slow but could be usable in the future. I think this could be a
> solution for upcoming Linux platforms to support DOS and even Windows
> software! The emulator itself is free for non-commercial use. It will even
> emulate a 386 in the future! Minix is reported to boot too!

Note that one fun thing that might be worth testing is to emulate Linux/x86
binaries. That's a LOT easier than doing generic protected more 386
emulation, and it should be pretty easy to "emulate" the system calls (doing
them natively). That would allow running stuff like ApplixWare on top of
Linux/alpha (the same way that FX!32 runs 386 NT binaries on top of alpha
NT).
