Sean (et al),
the EB164 and PC164 are different boards and different designs.
PC164 uses an EV56 CPU (faster than the EV5 and with byte/word memory access
and some other go faster bits). The interrupt handling is different between
the two and the PCI/Memory support chipset is a later version on the PC164.
EB164 was relatively expensive (all Alphas are I guess) but I'm told that
the PC164 is cheaper and the volumes for the PC164 are going to be much much
higher than for the EB164.
