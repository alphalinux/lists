<rebuke type=gentle>

Next time, read up on how a particular mailing list (or mailing lists
in general) operate before you go subscribing to it (or them). As a
rule, every mailing list (e.g. "axp-list") has a corresponding
- -request addrress (e.g. "axp-list-request"). Embedding an unsubscribe
command in the body or subject (with the list name) is usually
sufficient. Or, better yet, a subject of "HELP" can get you even
farther.

Also, after you subscribe, save the little "how to use this list"
message it sends you. Broadcasting your ignorance servers only to
lessen you in the sight of others, especially when a solution is as
obvious as axp-list makes it.

</rebuke>
