There is a tiny patch available as:

ftp://ftp.azstarnet.com/pub/linux/axp/sys/axp-diffs-2.0.16+1.gz

it fixes a problem that was introduced by the dual-console support.
The bug has the effect of breaking Shift-PgUp/Shift-PgDn on VGA
consoles (I didn't notice since the TGA console doesn't support
scrolling around). The patch also changes defconfig to enable both
TGA and VGA to protect the innocent. For performance and minimal
kernel-size, you'll want to turn off the one that you don't need.

I made this an incremental patch to 2.0.16+axp-diffs since it looks
like 2.0.17 will be shortlived anyway.
