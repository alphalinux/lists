Yes, but when it get congested, it gets *really* congested. Last
night at about 7:00PM, the ftp transfer rate was down to about 5KByte/sec.

and I am trying to see when the really bad times of day are and when the
good ones are. In the middle of the night the rates got about as high as
100Kbyte/sec going to some other machine not in my domain.
