> What do i need to be able to upgrade 3.0.3/AXP from gcc 2.7.0 to gcc
> 2.7.2 ?
>
> I installed libc which includes ld.so.1 but i guessed that the kernel
> supplied with 3.0.3 doesn't include ELF support. But when i recompile
> the kernel it complains in binfmt_elf.c about dump_fps being missing ???

You need a newer kernel. Grab 2.0.16 and apply the patches found at
ftp://ftp.azstarnet.com/pub/linux/axp/sys/axp-diffs-2.0.16.gz.

As for the rest of your system, poke through the axp-list archives
on www.redhat.com; there have been a couple of sets of step-by-step
instructions posted.
