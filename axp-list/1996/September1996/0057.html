Can you check out the "pc" and "rp" addresses of the panics with gdb?

Just do a "gdb /usr/src/linux/vmlinux" (make sure it's the same image
that you had problems with, unstripped), and do a

disassemble 0xfffffc000033ce40 /* pc */

or similar (or you can just look up the function in the system.map file).
