As far as i understand it, this is nothing to worry about as far as your
fdisk problems go. Some programs (such as fdisk) have not been
rewrritten for the alpha (64 bit) architecture, causing unaligned
acesses. These are warnings and everything should be working ok.

As for the boot problems, i remember reading somewhere that the RedHat
kernel assumes that sda2 is the root partition, i.e. sda1 is your swap,
sda2 your root. This might have something to do with it...
