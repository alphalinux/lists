> Does anyone know what jumper J12 is for?

Yes, IDE is available, just as on the NONAME.

HOWEVER, the connector is one of those funky miniature ones, not the standard
size. Also, I'm not sure whether power is supplied via that connector as well;
if not, you can pull power of the riser card (there are 4 pins at one end for
this purpose, though they do also get used for a 3.5" internal SCSI if
present).
