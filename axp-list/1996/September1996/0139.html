On Thu, 5 Sep 1996, Eric Jones wrote:

> I recently saw a post on this list regarding upgrading to elf via rpm
> files. Could someone re-send the message, or tell me where it might be
> archived?

Though this may not dissuade you, I have to try....

The packages in Red Hat's devel tree (the only ELF/Alpha RPM's available
from ftp.redhat.com) are *not* supported. They change daily and contain
known problems. The next release of Red Hat Linux for Alpha based machines
will be all ELF and include a smooth upgrade path for users of previous
releases. We suggest that our customers wait until the release is available
if at all possible.
