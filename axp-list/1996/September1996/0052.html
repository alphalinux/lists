Hallo,

The eb164 is the old style board with supports the 21164 processor up to
speeds 333MHz true and you can also get stable running 366 MHZ with is a
upclocked version of the 333 MHZ.

The PC164 is a ATX-based Board with fits in the Standard ATX-Cases.

At www.quant-x.com or on the european mirror site at
www.quant-x.at

At /boards/Products.html you will find the series-3/ATX and there you
have at the bottom of this side also a picture and the board layout. You
can find also this information for the cabriolet and the EB5 at
/boards/mb-comp.html.

I hope this helps you.
