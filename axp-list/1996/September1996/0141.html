My recommendation would be for DEC to license and develop an Alpha-based
variant of Applixware integrated commercial application that ports with
Linux. DEC did not miscalculate the market potential for its UDBs, what
it errored was not bundle the product with an commercial application
that's compliant with its unique platform. The golden rule of the game
has always been: "a killer product needs a killer application!" Had DEC
done its homework right, both Intel and Microsoft would have no way
avoiding a heavy punch. It probably is not too late......
