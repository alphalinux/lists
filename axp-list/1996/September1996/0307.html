> > Things like "elm", "tin", and "irc" have this REALLY annoying
> >I/O buffering problem. It seems that it takes several characters of

I do not know about "irc", as I am not using it. Supplied on APX CD
"elm" binaries, from Red Hat, work for me like they should. As for
"tin" I recompiled recently tin-1.3beta-950824; "released" tin-1.2
has very annoying bugs which show up when an article reference more
groups. This required some interventions in Makefile, config.h and
here and there in other places. The crucial thing was to tell it
to use termios.h and that that I do have tc{set,get}... functions.
Now it works.

'config.h' from tin makes too many false assumptions what 'linux' means.
Exactly the same problems crop out when you try to recompile tin on
an Intel box with the current Linux distributions.

If somebody wants patches I may e-mail what I used. AFAIK
tin-1.3beta-950824 is the latest available.
