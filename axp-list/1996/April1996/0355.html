Torsten Zirzlaff spake unto me, saying:
>
> Hi.
>
> I fear I blowed it up. I just working under X and emacs suddenly
> everything freezes up and the only thing I could do was hard reset or
> switch off. Well after that nothing happened. No Milo SRM-console just
> starting the fan and rattling the harddisk. Not even a twitch on the
> screen.
>
> My question is as you already guessed what crashed down? Memory,
> cache? Any help would be helpfull. And of following Murphy's law it
> happened Friday evening.

Well, I have no way of knowing for certain, but it _might_ be the clock
crystal. My motherboard (Cabriolet) exhibited a very similar failure
about a day after I installed it. As it turned out, the clock crystal
had died. I'm not sure how you can test it, but I don't believe the
crystal is a terribly expensive item. If your machine is still under
warranty, you can ask your dealer to look at it. Otherwise, the exact
part number/spec for the crystal should be listed in the motherboard
manual, and you can just pop a new one in and see if it makes a difference.

Hope this helps
