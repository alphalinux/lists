Guys...I seem to have found someone with an AlphaStation 200
for sale. It's got DEC Unix (ugh, influences the price some) and
it's a 100mhz model. What's the deal with those boards? Can the
clock be easily upped? I'm not ruling out the possibility of actually
buying a CPU for it if need be, but I'd also be interested in
trying to overclock it (if it really *is* only able to do 100mhz
it isn't worth much anyway :-).

So, a) can the board accept a faster CPU and be clocked up, b)
does anyone have any actual experiences with those boxes and overclocking,

it, so....
