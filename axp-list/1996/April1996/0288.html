It must be `mount -t dos /dev/sdb1 /image' (Or similar, check if
/image exists. It's _not_ /mnt.

> I figured out how to break the program so I could enter commands directly

Well, the problem is, that most people here asume, that anybody
experienced Linux before ;-). You can switch "virtual consoles" by
pressing Alt-Fx. On Alt-F2 you'll find a shell during the RedHat
Install, and Alt-F1 will bring you back to the installation
program.

method, just entering nothing to the questions asked. Make sure the
