> HOWTO's (which seem to conflict). Do I really need to hook up a 1.44 floppy

No, the stock 2.88 works just fine...

> The error I am getting is during the read of the root disk during the
> copyboot.exe procedure. It never gets through the read. It looks like it
> is trying to read a 2.88 floppy when it's really a 1.44. Hooking up
> another floppy would be a pain :(. anyway... which of the HOWTO's should

You might have a flaky floppy drive or a bad floppy disk. copybot.exe is
designed to copy exactly 1440Kb. Try formatting a new floppy and putting
the boot disk image on there and see what happens.

BTW - it's the BOOT disk that you're using copyboot for, RIGHT (not the
"root" disk as you said above)?
