The BLADE binutils don't understand weak externals. As a work-around,
simply define two functions memcpy() and memset() that call __memcpy()
and __memset() respectively (passing on the arguments and return
value). Assuming there are no other show-stoppers, you should then be
able to build a kernel.

Look in arch/alpha/lib/{memcpy.c,memset.c} and search for .weakext to
find where the memcpy/memset symbols are supposed to get defined (and
this will work properly once you have a newer binutils package
installed).
