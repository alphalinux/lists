I tried to upgrade the firmware of my Jensen to v2.2 and tried v1.9
also. In both cases, aboot no longer works, while a SRM bootable
kernel (the one from Jim Paradis' Jensen install kit) does. Now, the
symptoms are:

INIT-S-CPU
.
. [some more like that]
.
AUDIT_LOAD_DONE

aboot: Linux/Alpha bootloader version 0.3 [ it is 0.31 in real ]

?O2 KSP INVAL

>>> [the prompt again]
