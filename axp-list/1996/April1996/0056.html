Yes, yes, yes! :-)

At least, that's true for the Noname: ECC enabled, I have seen a
*very* occasional ECC machine check (correctable, fortunately :-) and
the PALcode does the scrubbing.

There is also a utility called "sysinfo" that will tell you most of
the interesting stuff about how the system is setup. Unfortunately,
on the Noname some of the "interesting" registers are write-only, but
still..

Look for

ftp://ftp.azstarnet.com/pub/linux/axp/misc/axpsrc-sysinfo-0.0.tar.gz
