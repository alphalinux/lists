I just finished moving our Proxy & Second Web Server from an Intel/Linux
Box
to a Multia/Linux with Kernel 1.3.85.

First: The SCSI device HAS automatic termination detection. I have hooked
up a Nakachima 7 CD-ROM Changer and it just worked fine. Just make
sure that your CD-ROM is terminated ! Furthermore I am running a external
2 GB Disk in a daisy chain, just terminate the last device in the chain.

After booting, make sure you get the 1.3.85 Kernel & Milo SOURCES from
gatekeeper
and RedHat and build them.
