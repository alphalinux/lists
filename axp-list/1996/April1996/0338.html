>>>>> On Wed, 24 Apr 1996 17:19:31 -0600, jb@galileo.apo.nmsu.edu (Jon Brinkmann) said:

Jon> I've been told that Fast-Wide SCSI drives are not supported by
Jon> Linux/Alpha. Is this correct? If so, does anyone know when
Jon> the drivers will be available?

The "Fast" part is no problem. The "wide" would require a controller
other than the ncr53c810. It may be that a wide controller in the
ncr53c8xx family might be reasonably easy to get to work, but AFAIK,
nobody tried so far.

One caveat about the current 53c810 driver: the DISCONNECT option is
known not to work reliably, so it should be turned off (unless you
have a tape drive, in which case you might have no choice but to turn
it on). Ugly, isn't it?

--david
