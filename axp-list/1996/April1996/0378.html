There is, in fact, a scheduling pass already in gcc. It isn't as
good as it could be -- partially because it isn't so good with
superscalar architechtures and partially because the its 21064 model
is not exactly accurate. Whether this later is due to failings in
gcc or something else, I'm not certain.

It would, however, be an interesting project to replace the model
currently in gcc with the one used in this scheduler.
