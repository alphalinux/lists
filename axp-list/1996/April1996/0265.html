Chris Dale <chrisd@resntl.bhp.com.au> writes:
> I am attempting to get XF86 working on my multia (UDB) but am at a loss
> as to what sort of video card is present in this machine. Most of the
> doc's reference TGA - this means little to me (from a non-pc background).

TGA is DEC proprietary. It's not compatible with anything else (except
certain older DEC graphics cards).

What you need is Jay Estabrook's experimental TGA X server, available at
http://gatekeeper.dec.com/pub/DEC/Linux-Alpha/X11/axpbin-Xtga-BETA-0.2.gz.

Make sure you read the README first:
http://gatekeeper.dec.com/pub/DEC/Linux-Alpha/X11/axpbin-Xtga-BETA-0.0.README

It works quite well other than the known problems listed in the README.
(Thanks, Jay!)

Cheers,
