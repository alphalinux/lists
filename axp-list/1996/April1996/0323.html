There is work, though nothing yet results in actual libraries
yet. We've been promised and are still waiting for the official
ELF standard from DEC. However, this is taking so long even
DEC people are becomming peeved.

In the mean time, I've re-worked the Alpha back end to gas to
make it a (relatively) simple matter to modify for ELF support.
If you'd like to have a look at the snapshots and perhaps test
it out and send me bug reports, they can be found in

ftp://atheist.tamu.edu/pub/richard/linux/axp/

It correctly assembles the kernel (insofar as it actually runs;
I havn't actually inspected it instruction by instruction) and
today's snapshot makes it through Milo's PALcode (though I've
not yet booted off of it).

If you like, I'll add you to the list that receives notifications
of new editions and any other noteworthy ELF developments.
