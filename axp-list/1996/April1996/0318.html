> Hello,
>
> I'm interested to turn my useless Windows NT Alpha XL266 into a useful Linux
> workstation. The Alpha XL came with a Matrox Millenium graphics card which,
> according to the FAQ list at http://www.azstarnet.com/~axplinux/FAQ-4.html,
> is unsupported by the Linux X11 server.
>
> So my question is, what graphics cards for the Alpha XL ARE supported by the
> Linux X11 server?
>
> Many thanks,
> Boris Blankleider
> Flinders University
> South Australia

Almost any PCI bus S3-based card, ie Diamond Stealth 64, #9 GXE64, ELSA, etc.
ATI's Mach64 is also suppported, though the server is not as stable as the S3.
And DEC's TGA (ZLXp-E1) is also supported.

You might want to ask both X-Inside and Metro-Link whether/when they will
have an Alpha version of their commercial server for the Matrox. Rumors have
it that they may be working on an Imagine-128 server as well.
