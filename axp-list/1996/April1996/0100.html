Dave Rusling wrote:

> these two extra definitions in hwrpb.h:

> #define EV56_CPU 7 /* EV5.6 (21164) */
> #define EV6_CPU 8 /* EV6 (21164) */

The comments are irritating!
EV56 is the 21164A, a enhanced version of the 21164, implemented in
a 0.35u process and using a mixed 2V / 3.3V supply scheme.
It's clocked at about 433MHz. Other changes compared to the 21164
are new byte-wide memory access instructions and larger D-cache (?).
It has been announced and there was a short article in Electronic Design,
February 19, 1996.
EV6 must be the 21264 but it has not been announced yet.
