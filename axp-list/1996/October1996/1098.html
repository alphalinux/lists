> What are the modes that TGA can be set in? What are its possible clock
> rates? After playing with it a bit I could not get it to set anything. I'm
> still new enough to the UDB (~six weeks) that I'm not even sure where to
> look for these details. Thanks for any further information you can give.

SVGATextMode and svgalib will *NOT* work with the TGA in the UDB or any other
Alphas. The TGA is *NOT* a VGA.

The "modes" the TGA will work as, are fixed in the kernel sources (to create
a VGA-like 640x480 @ 60Hz, text mode 25 lines by 80 columns), and in the old
Xserver (the supported resolution/refresh rates are in the XF86Config sample
that comes as part of the README).

There'll be a new TGA server available as part of XFree86 Release 3.2 very
soon now, which has much more flexible clock setup code. Have a look into
that when it's out, as the sources will be included.
