On 20 Oct 1996, Matthew X Economou wrote:

> DM> into. A round of quake would likely prove more productive.
>
> Ok. So how do I get Quake to work on Alpha (*grin*)? I found the
> alpha-ELF-quake binary on azstarnet, and I got the shareware archive
> (9 meg zip file), so how do I de-ice quake and get sound, video to
> work? (A URL or other such pointer would be quite nice.)

Should be no problem.

I compiled sound as a module, then i got a working insmod from azstarnet,
also the Linus-xquake from Linus' server. I bought also the MS-DOS CD,
installed it on the DOS machine, rebooted the machine into the right OS
and copied the id1 directory to the alpha. Thats it.

And yes, my soundblaster didn't work with IRQ7 so I used IRQ5. Don't
forget to insmod the sound module.
