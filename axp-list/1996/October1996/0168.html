On Fri, 4 Oct 1996, Brian Walters wrote:

> I apologize for being a little off topic but I've seen a few people
> mention ISDN modems hooked to thier UDBs and I wanted to get
> recomendations on which one to buy and personal whys.
>
> Motorola bitsurfer pro
> US Robotics
> Netopia
> ????
>
> my ISP said he's having great luck with the Motorolas. Any opions????
> TIA

At work our official recommendation to our customers is the Motorola
Bitsurfr Pro, and we've had great luck with them (they call into our
Portmaster which has the 5-BRI card installed). However, my personal link
to my home network is done via 3Com Impact ISDN modems, and I've also had
very good luck with those (when I bought them the Bitsurfr Pro wasn't out
yet, and I wanted Multilink PPP). I have to admit though, the Bitsurfr
looks a lot cooler. *grin*

At least one Netopia I've seen (pictures only, never in person) looked
suspiciously like a 3Com Impact painted blue and relabled, so I suspect
it's as reliable as the Impact.

Beyond that there's offerings from US Robotics and from Zyxel; I haven't
used either although i've heard good things about the Zyxels.
