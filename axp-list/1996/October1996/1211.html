"Stephen Hoang" writes:
>After applying all the Colgate errata for AXP, I couldn't log in anymore. What
>do I need to do to fix it ?

I don't know for sure what your problem is. It may be a truncated
/etc/pam.conf

If so, boot in single-user mode (use the "single" boot-time argument)
and then the following script should fix the problem:

for i in $(rpm -q --whatrequires pamconfig) ; do
rpm --qf '%{POSTIN}' $i | sh -x
done
