Hi!

David Mosberger wrote:
>
> Anybody knows whether the TGA "card" implemented on the UDB can do
> sync-on-green? It seems the TGA/DAC can do it, but it's not so
> clear whether the board has the necessary wiring.
>

Actually, I'm not sure wheter the UDB supports sync on green, but it
is possible to get a "sync on green" signal from what comes out
of a standard VGA-connector.

I'm currently trying to get an old IBM 5081 RT monitor (that needs sync
on
green) to work under XFree86 with Linux/Intel. -- I just purchased the
cable (VGA - BNC w. sync on green) for about 30$.

Anyway, I could also provide you with various circuit arrangements to
generate a "sync on green" signal from HSYNC VSYNC and GREEN signals...

Hope that helps a bit...
