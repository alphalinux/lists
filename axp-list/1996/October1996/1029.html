You *may* be running into another manifestation of the Quake problem; ie
mismatched kernel and Xserver. The mismatch occurs in the area of the MIT-SHM
extension of the server, but only comes into play when an application wishes
to use certain facilities (Quake wants to setup a SHM area between it and the
server; if the server's SHM data structures don't match those of the kernel's
SHM, a problem occurs).

Working combinations, as per my previous mail on this subject, are:

1. ECOFF Xservers and linux kernels WITHOUT the axp-diffs

2. ELF Xservers and linux kernels WITH the axp-diffs

(The mismatch is actually between GLIBC used by the ELF Xservers, and the
stock linux kernels).
