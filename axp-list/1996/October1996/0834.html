Hello all,

Basically seems like the Redhat-4.0 noname.img is broken.

I did a:
dd if=noname.img of=/dev/fd0H1440 bs=8192

(the disk was fdformatted with no errors on the UDB).

Rebooted, and in milo I said: (from the redhat manual).
MILO> boot fd0:vmlinux.gz root=/dev/fd0 load_ramdisk=1 prompt_ramdisk=1

I get:
24 lines of booting or so.
VFS: DIsk change detected on device 2/0
#############################################################################
########################################done.
bad filehdr magic number 0x457f should be 0x183
MILO: Failed to load the kernel

I'd suspect an stupid error on my part but apparently 4 or so other people
made this EXACT mistake with the same error that emailed this list.

Any ideas?
