I bet that you have a wrong rooting table on a loopback (net address
of 127.0.0.1 instead of 127.0.0.0). This is a bug in Red Hat
distribution and is not limited to Alpha. Older kernels could live
with it, but newer ones are unhappy and rightly so.

In ifup-lo you should have a line which reads
route add -net 127.0.0.0
and not
'route add -net 127.0.0.1' or 'route add -net ${IPADDR}' or something
like that.
