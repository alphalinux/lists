Travis, et. al.

Just to clarify for everyone:

o Digital is no longer making the UDB or the Multia, either in
Alpha or Intel form. Our FS group has reserved a number of them
for spare parts, so service will be available into the foreseeable
future.

o Digital has sold off all UDBs and Alpha Multias to distributors

o If you want to get one, you will either have to go to some
reseller or distributor that might have them left (i.e. scan
the web), or find someone who wishes (for some reason) to sell
theirs.

To show you that irony plays real well here, I wanted to buy a 233 Mhz UDB,
but as the final ones went out the door, my order was lost. They made real
nice systems to take to trade shows, since I could (easily) carry them on
an airplane.

I am glad that most, if not all of the people that bought them were happy with
them, and I am sad that there are people who wanted them, but could not get
them (like me).
