>>>>> On Sun, 20 Oct 1996 19:00:58 +0500, jestabro@amt.tay1.dec.com said:

Jay> !!!!!!!!!! YOU CAN NOT BUILD MILO UNDER AN "ELF" SYSTEM !!!!!!!!!!!!!!

Jay> Well, maybe you *can*, but it's bound to be a *lot* of trouble.

Yes, you can, but yes, it's trouble. It's not really _that_ bad and I
sent patches to both Dave Rusling and this list that allow to do this
(I haven't checked the MILO sources recently, so the patches may have
made it into the official sources already).

In any case, BIOS emulation is a no go unless the free emulator works
for you (which it does for me since I have the graphics cards that the
free emulator was written for... ;-).

What's worse: the Cabriolet version of MILO works perfectly fine, but
the Noname version has some problem. I'm not sure it's really ELF
related. Unfortunately, the current MILO setup makes it all but
impossible for me to track down the problem. I suspect it's just
something stoopid, but first you've gotta find it.

Anyhow, the conclusion remains the same: DON'T try rebuilding MILO
yourself unless you know what you're getting yourself into. A round
of quake would likely prove more productive.
