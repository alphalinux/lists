I apologize in advance to the subscribers of this list for this message.
However, I'm starting to get VERY perturbed at the TOTAL unresponsiveness
of the sales office at Redhat.

To: sales@redhat.com

Over the last 2 1/2 weeks I've sent you NO LESS than 6 messages
SPECIFICALLY requesting information on you upgrade policy for Alpha
owners who recently purchased 3.0.3 and are now offered 4.x. NOT ONE of
my messages has elicited ANY response from you.

You're busy - great! Are you so busy that you are going to ignore one of
you paying customers? Do you know and appreciate the power of the "rule
of 10"?

One of your other offers (ApplixWare), that I have been considering,
expires the end of October. Since you haven't responded to ANY of my
previous mail, if I did decide to purchase ApplixWare and got no response
from you until November/December, am I going to be precluded from
purchasing under the terms of the offer?

Come on sales@redhat - you've got a paying customer here who is getting
VERY upset? How are you going to respond?
