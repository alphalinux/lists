I successfully compiled the MSS (Microsoft Sound System) driver on a
486 with the MSS hardware installed. There is OPL support on that
board, so I'd guess that the MSS compatible hardware in the UDBs does
support Yamaha OPL3. I DID have much problems compiling the driver as
it did not include the proper sequencer sources in the makefile. Once
I hacked the Makefile and configuration header, it compiled and booted
properly. Note that I have not tried to play anything with the
device. The kernel reports that it found the OPL3 port.

Sound initialization started
<MSS audio codec (AD1848)> at 0x534 irq 11 dma 3,3
<Yamaha OPL3 FM> at 0x388
Sound initialization complete
