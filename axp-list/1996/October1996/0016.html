Now that's the understatement of the year. How many other
software/hardware houses do you know of that provide such even-handed
support for a red-headed step-child of an OS that might possibly compete

Still, I think we'd all like to see DEC allow limited public use of some
of it's OSF libs, at least for a while. I've used both DEC UNIX and
Linux, and while on the whole I prefer Linux, DEC UNIX does have all the
bells and whistles that Linux-AXP still lacks. But like a lot of people,
our DEC boxen are rather limited UDB's, where DEC UNIX isn't really an
option.
