case (elf support). I checked all the previous posts (in the 4000 message
queue) and didn't see anyone say the same thing, just similiar error reports.
I probably missed something.

In any case I've very pleased.

I got tga working at 1024x768, makes it alot more useful (didn't have room
for X before).

I didn't expect such a bonus on the disk space end, with
redhat-3.0.3 It was tight to get a working system.

With redhat-4.0 I installed everything except:
tetex, games, emacs.

Very useful machine even without any additional disk, things are compiling
much easier.
