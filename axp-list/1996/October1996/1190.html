The DE422 EISA Ethernet card is supported on the Jensen; I'm surprised its
not part of the standard Jensen kernel as built by Red Hat. Erik?

The COMPAQ QVision card is *not* currently supported by XFree86 as more than
a generic SVGA, I think, but worse, it's not been ported to the Alpha. If you
can find the old #9 GXE ISA card that also used to ship in the Jensen, that
one *does* have an Xserver that will run. Unfortunately, you can't just stick
any old ISA VGA in there, as the console firmware may not work with it very
well, if at all.

Good luck.
