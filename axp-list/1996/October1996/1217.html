As we all probably know by now, the DEC UDB (aka Multia) has a drive that
is too
small. Most of us have been adding on and external SCSI expansion unit of
some sort
with a much larger hard drive capacity. While this works is appears to
defeat the
original purpose of a small footprint machine.
The drive that I have in my 166Mhz machine is a Toshiba is (I believe) a
MK-1824FBV
drive (352MB capacity unformatted)
Has anyone tried any of the following drives with their UDB's??
Toshiba MK-1924FBV 540MB
Toshiba MK-1926FBV 815MB
These drives look like they might be candidates for replacements of the
2.5 inch
slimline drives in the UDB.
