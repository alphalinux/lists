> I was wondering if anyone has seen the following compile error:
> /usr/include/stdio.h:152: previous declaration of 'dprintf'

Sure. It means your program is providing a declaration of dprintf
that conflicts with the one in <stdio.h>.

They almost certainly differ on the const-ness of the second argument.

The correct solution in my view is to remove the declaration from
your program. If programs think they help things by providing
declarations that are missing from some system, I'll guarantee
it'll be wrong for another system.
