I accumulated some SpecInt/SpecFP information from the net. Of
course, you know that these numbers must be taken for what they are.
The one thing that appears to be significant is the operating system.
Comparable analyses must be done on the same operating system. Most
people recommend using UN*X and not WindowsNT as NT tends to deliver
sub-optimal performance. This conclusion comes from reading several
score of web pages.
