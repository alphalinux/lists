Erik Troan <ewt@sunsite.unc.edu> writes:
> If we lowered our price on the Alpha to $50, we
> wouldn't make enough money to justify the product (remember that we
> sell most Alpha's to distributors at about 50% of retail).

I believe this is a false economy. If it were priced at $50 I would buy it.
If I bought it through a distributor you would get $25. But at $100 it's
worth my time to FTP it and cut my own CD, so you get $0.

A few suggestions:

1) Offer it for $50, and be happy to get $25 from the distributor instead
of $0.

2) Offer it for $50 direct, and don't sell it through distributors.

3) Don't distribute it on your FTP site. This one probably won't work, and
will piss off lots of customers.

Cheers,
Eric
