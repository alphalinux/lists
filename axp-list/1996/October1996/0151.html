On Fri, 4 Oct 1996, Daniel Simmons wrote:

> The problem is, now I'd like to install a newer MILO that does
> understand ELF, but I don't know any easy way to do that. Must I make
> up a floppy with linload.exe and milo on it, and then hard boot and
> play with things in the ARC console, or is there some way to have MILO
> upgrade itself or something?

You probably have a dos partition has /dev/sda1 which holds the milo
image. If you mount thatand replace the milo image with a newer
one, you'll be all set.
