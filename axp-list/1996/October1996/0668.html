On Thu, 17 Oct 1996, Hunter Andreas Ware wrote:

>
> I just added a jaz drive onto my system and I'm getting all kinds
> of medium errors. I think I heard something about this being a problem
> not actually related to the jaz drive and was wondering what you-all thought?

The device reports:

- sense key medium error
- unrecovered read error
- the command that seems to get error is a WRITE6

You got a read error while attempting to write data to the device.
For the moment I donnot have any explanation about this weirdness.
However, I think that the problem is related to the
device and/or the medium of target 3 (probably the Jaz).

I never try Jaz drive, but I heard that these devices spin down and
reject tagged commands while spinning up.
So, do not enable tagged command queuing for your Jaz device.
