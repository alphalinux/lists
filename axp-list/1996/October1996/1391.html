I think, as someone else mentioned, your problem is a blown keyboard
controller. Don't feel bad... I did that myself on a UDB 8-) 8-(

Someone else mentioned replacing the keyboard controller. On the
UDB/Multia, the keyboard controller is soldered in; replacing it
is not trivial.

> to attach a lopp in serial port #1, as soon as i find the corrrect
> attaschment. I need help to look for this becauset he serial port has
> fifthteen pin.(i have never seen anythin' like it before).

That is the wrong connector. The 15-pin "D" connector is for thickwire
Ethernet. There should be two 25-pin "D" connectors on the back; the
female one is for the printer port, and the male one is for the serial
port.

As for warranty/service/whatnot, unfortunately that's between you and
the seller... and it appears as though you're going to have a difficult
time getting service through the seller. Your only other alternative
will be to get the system serviced by Digital; this is possible, but
of course you'll have to pay for it.
