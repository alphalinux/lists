OK, to upgrade to a new MILO on the UDB (or any other WNT ARC based boot)
follow the instructions on how to boot from ARC (they're in the MILO HOWTO)
and invent a second boot option (follow the blue screens). I usually
have a test.arc next to my milo.arc so that I can test new MILOs (yes, I do
test them!). So, no you do not need a floppy to try out a new MILO.
