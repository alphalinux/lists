Nothing. Geometry is irrelevant for logical block addressable
devices (like SCSI and IDE) and even more irrelevant for variable
geometry drives (like the Maxtor). As long as the overall capacity
is observed, just about any convenient geoemtry will do.

Anyway, fdisk won't do more than 63 spt- it's s'posed to be compatible
to PC bios'.
