> anyone know how good the emulation is? We have just received a

I have only tried running statically linked executables, but my experience
is that the emulation is quite good. The only problem I have found is that
the FlexLM license manager tries to do an IOCTL that Linux does not support.
I assume that it is trying to determine the hardware eithernet address.
