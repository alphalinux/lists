>
> Also, has anyone got mgetty+sendfax to work under Linux AXP? Specifically,
> the voice tools. If so which version and were there any patches required?

I did not try voice tools as I did not have a sound support on this
particular alpha. :-) Otherwise a compilation required minimal patches
and after short correspondence with me the author of mgetty included
them in the current "experimental" release (0.99 - whatever is the
date). So you should be able to compile now mgetty on Linux AXP by
typing 'make' (roughly) if you will get something dated at least
September of this year.

mgetty on AXP works just fine - at least data modem functions and,
most likely, fax functions as well. As for voice stuff, I am curious
as well. :-)
