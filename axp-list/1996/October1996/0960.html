I think that the other replies sum this up quite well. It may appear
to work, but I have had nothing but troubles with RH 3.0.3 and 4.0.
Perhaps there is something fishy in the way that I installed RH, but I
have never had problems with the other popular distribution--it
doesn't support Alpha. It is likely that the problems will be fixed,
eventually, but I have been recommending to my clients to stay away
from RH until after 4.0 shakes down.

Note that 3.0.3 did install and does run. However, several packages
did not work (I can't recall which ones at the moment) until I
configured and compiled them myself. We've had trouble with the rc
scripts, with UUCP, with sendmail, with samba, with the compiler, and
with NFS. It is unfortunate because RH has many promising features.
