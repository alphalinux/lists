On Thu, 31 Oct 1996, Joshua M. Thompson wrote:

> Do the Adaptec 2940 series cards work OK in the UDBs? I have a need for
> two SCSI busses on our news server and I'd like to throw an Adaptec card
> in there. Initially I'm gonna use the 2940U that I have lying around, but
> eventually I want to add the 2940UW (wide) because all the drives in our
> RAID 5 array are wide units.

Don't get 2940 cards for Linux, period. Adaptec does not cooperate with
Linux developers and change the composition of their boards w/o telling
anyone. If you want a good SCSI card, I strongly suggest you get a
Buslogic or Qlogic card -- both companies support Linux developers.
