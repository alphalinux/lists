JZ> I hate to do this, but I can't compile any kernel except for
JZ> the kernel-source.RPM included on the RH 4.0 CD.
...
JZ> The problem has to do with a missing 'timespecbits.h' in the
JZ> include/linux dir along with a few other odd problems.

You MUST apply axp-diffs because the system uses glibc and not
linux-libc. <linux/timespecbits.h> is required by glibc and you won't
be able to compile anything (apparently not even the kernel) without
it.

On my RedHat 4.0 system (with all updates applied), a (clean) Linux
2.0.23 kernel compiled cleanly with axp-diffs-2.0.21.
