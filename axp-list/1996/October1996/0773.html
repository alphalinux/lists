>>> geerten kuiper said:
>
> 4. Recently there was some discussion on this list about the newer NT
> Firmware not being able to load MILO/Linux. Are you sure I can run both Win
> NT 4.0 _and_ Linux on these boards ?

The PC164 (and thus probably the Durango) comes with half_FLASH, ie just
enough FLASH ROM to hold SRM or ARC but not both. Note that I said "ARC";
because these boards are produced by Digital Semiconductor and not the
workstation group, they are not required to have AlphaBIOS, and so they
don't... :-)

And yes, they happily dual boot LINUX and NT from ARC console.
