I have used a pretty long internal cable to connect my extermal scsi
devices. It is probably 3 feet long with maybe 12 inches between
connectors. I use an old xt power supply to power the drive and have no
terminator. I called the guys at www.onsale.com, where I got the alpha
and they said that scsi-II doesn't need to have a terminator. Well I
don't need one and it doesn't seem to matter if the cable is too long
provided it doesn't go over I think the 6 foot limit... that might not be
the right length, but something like that. I haven't had any problems,
like errors or slow speeds with it either, and the external tape and hard
drive don't seem to mind sitting on the table either. Not too pretty, but
it works...
