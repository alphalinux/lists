>
> On a side note, how does one check out a core dump? I know I need gdb, but
> what other steps are necessary?

Likely the simplest way will be

gdb <executable> <core_made_by_it>

Even if <executable> does not have debugging symbols you may get
a valuable information. Come to think of it, if you have an
orphaned core, then use any compiled program for <executable>.
gdb will tell you who really owns the core. :-)
