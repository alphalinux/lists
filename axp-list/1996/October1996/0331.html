As told to me recently by someone else:

boot your computer into ARC, in the setup menu select the reboot
into OSF option, and cycle the power. Once you're into the SRM console
, with the ">>>" prompt, type "set ewa0_mode Twisted-Pair" or something
like that (it will tell you if you do it wrong). Once you get that set,
quit out of there (with "quit") and try rebooting your system.

for some reason the network auto-detection quit working between the
redhat-3.0 and redhat-4.0 releases...
