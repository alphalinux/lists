> I'm afraid that my earlier mail on the 21264 contained an error: the chip is

> particular the estimated SPECint95/SPECfp95 is indeed 30+/50+.

It's only four way issue, but they now have six execution units (four integer,
two floating point). This is probably largely responsible for the great
integer performance. Of course, they wouldn't be able to dispatch four integer
instructions very often in typical code if they hadn't put in the reorder unit
and register renaming.
