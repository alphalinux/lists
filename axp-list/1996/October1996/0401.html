Hello!

For those of you who have a Digital Unix License, here is what you need to
run Netscape for Digital Unix:

in /usr/shlib:
libX11.so libXmu.so libdnet_stub.so
libXext.so libXt.so libm.so
libXm.so libc.so libnullplugin.so

in /etc: svc.conf

and in /sbin: loader

Thats from a 3.2c Version of Digital Unix. Netscape works fine with it,
other Digital Unix Binaries should do also.
