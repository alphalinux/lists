> I've never made use of the network-transparent nature of X-windows.
> Now I want to login to a computer that runs X11R6 on a SaprcStation. How do I

On the local machine do:

xhost name_of_sparcstation

Then on the sparcstation do:

setenv DISPLAY name_of_local_machine:0
xclock
