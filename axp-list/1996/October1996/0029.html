> Is Alpha assembly friendly for human programmers? I know it's RISC, but
> at least orthogonal RISC, I hope?

Oh yes. None of the nasty bits like branch delay slots and
required data delay slots exist on the Alpha. There are only
a couple of rules for instruction scheduling, so it is easy
(once you get the hang or it) to obtain optimal instruction
ordering by hand.
