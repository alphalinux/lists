> > Does anyone know of a low cost PCI based framegrapper that will work on

> I, too, have been loking for such a beast. The trouble is that the

There is a driver for Matrox' Meteor framegrabber for Linux/x86.

ftp://ftp.rwii.com/pub/linux/system/Meteor

(This driver is derived form the FreeBSD driver.)

I don't know how to make it work on Alpha, but the programming info
for this card is available.
