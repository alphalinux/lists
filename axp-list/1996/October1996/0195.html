> Hello Allan

> >Can anyone recommend a SCSI controller that will work in all PCI slots in a
> >PC164/EB164. Have tried:
> >
> > 1. cheap clone NCR810 - only runs in one slot
> > 2. Qlogic FAS974, rom version 4.03 - not even the SRM can find it

> >Prefer NCR810.
>
> I think you have either bad NCR card or bad slots. There should be no
> reason why you can't run the NCR in any slot. I've seen this before and it
> was a bad motherboard.
>
> One quick check is try the video card in the slots to make sure they work.
> If the video works than I suspect the NCR card.

No, slots are fine, we have several EB/PC164s and they all have the same
behavior with this one cheap Tiawanese NCR810 clone, the only NCR we could
get off the shelf. We have 5 of the cards and they all behave the same way.

I *think* the reason is that the card is not PCI compliant and the designers
may have somehow hardwired the IRQ register in the cards PCI configuation
space. I'm not sure what MILO would do with that one. Could be electrical
though as the PCI specs are very particular about line lengths and this card
has some long! lines.

What I need is the model number of an NCR card that does work. With the
model number I can probably order some from the states.
