At 10:33 AM 10-06-96 GMT, you wrote:
>Hi,
>
>I am trying to install my new Micropolis 3243AV HD and I have problems:
>I partitioned it with WinNT 4.0 (128Mb Swap, 2943Mb Linux and 1024Mb NT),
>reboot in to Linux (Red Hat 3.0.3), use cfdisk /dev/sdc and changed the types
>for the swap and linux partitions, do a "mkswap /dev/sdc1", then "mkw2fs -c
>/dev/sdc2" and the process hangs.
>
>What is the geometry of the disk ? Cfdisk says 64 heads, 32 sectors and 4095
>Cylinders, is it correct?
Look at:
http://www.micropolis.com/3243AV.html
and:
http://www.micropolis.com/3243AVConfig.html
and:
http://www.micropolis.com/Disktab.html
and:
http://www.micropolis.com/Format_dat.html
Specifically you will find some Sun and HP info, such as a Sun format.dat
which tells you:
3243 Drive Geometry

disk_type = " MICROP 3243 - 19 " \
:ctlr = SCSI : fmt_time = 9 : rpm = 7200 \
:ncyl = 3154 : acyl = 3 : pcyl = 3956 \
--------^^^^------------------------------
:nhead = 19 : nsect = 139 : bpt = 65536
---------^^ --------^^^
Which is logical cylinders, heads and sectors per track.
