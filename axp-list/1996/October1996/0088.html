I had simular problems when I upgraded my Noname from 256k of cache to 1 Mb of
chache.
The Noname has a jumper on the motherboard to set cache speed to either 15 or
20 nsec.
20 nsec works. Check the speed of those SIMMs you bought. Does your machine
have a cache speed
select jumper ? Replace the SIMMs with faster ones.

If you're into FX32 check out Digital Fusion for Alpha/NT 4.0. It was very
popular at SIGGRAPH.
