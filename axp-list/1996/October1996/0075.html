>I just received my UDB.

>There is one serial port which actually is two port. Please let me know
>where I can found a cheap Y cable for it. The vendor told me Dec sell it
>for $157 which make me hard to believe.
This is the pinout:

UDB Combined Serial Port Pin Signals:

2 Port A TXD
3 Port A RXD
4 Port A RTS
5 Port A CTS
6 Port A DSR
7 Port A GND
8 Port A DCD
20 Port A DTR
22 Port A RI

7 Port B GND
11 Port B DTR
12 Port B DCD
13 Port B CTS
14 Port B TXD
16 Port B RXD
19 Port B RTS
23 Port B DSR
25 Port B RI

Radio Shack should be able to supply the connectors and cable for you.
Probably $15 in parts, maximum...
