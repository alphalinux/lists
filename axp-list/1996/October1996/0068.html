I just received my UDB. The first thing I need to do is how to increase
its HD capacity. I looked at internal hard drive and it is a 2.5" one.
There is not much space left for replacing it with a 3.5 inch hard
drive. Anyone tried this before?

I talked to the vendor and they told me that the best is to use an
external hard drive because the internal power supply is only 75 watt.
Besides that the Alpha processor is pretty hot at running and put a 3.5
inch HD will do no good to the the CPU. Sound reasonable. But I would
still like to see if anyone tried the replacement yet.

There is an IDE connector in the motherboard and I don't know if I can
make any use of it.

There is one serial port which actually is two port. Please let me know
where I can found a cheap Y cable for it. The vendor told me Dec sell it
for $157 which make me hard to believe.

There is some talking about replace CPU on the motherboard. I don't
believe any vendor will have this service because it just too difficult.
