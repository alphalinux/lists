At 06:03 hrs. 10-16-96 -0400, you wrote:
>I recently inherited a AS250 that has a external SCSI HD case plugged
>into it. This case holds 3 SCSI drives and a tape drive at the moment.
>
>Apparently, DU sees the HD's as a single drive and mounts them on a
>single filesystem. My question is whether or not I will have to
>anything special when I convert this system over to Linux?

as an ADVFS volume. This will be irrelevant as you will repartition and mkfs
these disks.
