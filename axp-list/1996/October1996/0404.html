He has a point, you know. It's one thing to download Red Hat distribution,
on get it on Infomagic CD for $25, and quite another to pay $100 for it. In
my personal experience I once tried calling them for a support question, and
also sent email. I gave all the info on registration ( this was a purchased
RedHat 3.03 CD) and it took 8 days before there was any reply. This reply, I
might add, was less than useful. I ended up getting my support form others
on the list, notably Jay. We seem to get our support from others on this

more calls than they expected.
