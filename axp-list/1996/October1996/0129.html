> until i switched to the SRM Console.

I assume you tried the instructions supplied in Jim Paradis's original
instructions, available from ftp.digital.com. While they do work (I
actually did it that way month back), there are now updated
instructions (und tools) provided, with support for the aboot
loader. Maybe you want to try those, they are available from

ftp://ftp.azstarnet.com/pub/linux/axp/jensen/install/

For other questions specific to the Jensen, consult the Jensen Section
of the Linux/Axp HOWTO

http://www.comnets.rwth-aachen.de/~ost/alpha/FAQ-9.html

In case that won't help either, please describe in more detail what
you were actually doing.

Good luck, Martin
