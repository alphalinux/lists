Hello,

I suggest you unplug every computer on your 100Mb network, one by one,
until you find the one(s) which cause the extra pingback packets. Your
traceroute output is weird : the (ttl=64!) seems to mean that something is
emitting bogus ICMP packets somehow, or that a gateway on the network
incorrectly decreases the TTL of the packets (metric problem ?).
