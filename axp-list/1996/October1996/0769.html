To: All the people who were asking what the Durango board is I mentioned
in Thursdays email.
"Durango" is ASPENs name for the EV56 PC164-board originally designed by
Digital. Some of you might know ASPEN as a leading manufacturer of
alpha-based boards and Award-winning workstations.
ASPEN makes those boards even more reliable, tests them well to sort the
crap out. The great expertise of the ASPEN guys is well known in the
market.
Our company ARCHTEC, based in Freising/Munich -Germany, is the best
european source for ASPENs stuff and more...

Some guys of the axp-list were asking why we use a 300 watts power supply
instead of a 400 watts type. Well, it not only depends on the power it
offers, it depends on quality of the device itself.
The 400 watts ATX-type offered is CRAP! That is why!
