but ... your computer is trying a gethostbyADDR ... it means that when you try
to get a hostname giving and IP. That's a reverse dns call and your dns server
should reply localhost to a gethostbyaddr with 127.0.0.1

The existence of a hosts file is not required for this to work properly, just
a well configured dns server.
