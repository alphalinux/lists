> I was trying to extract a set of tar archives off of floppies tonight w/o
> any luck. It was one file called filepro.gz which was approximately
[...]
> My UDB on the other justs starts to read the first disk and then quits.

do following:
dd if=/dev/fd0 of=x1
.
.
dd if=/dev/fd0 of=x5
cat x1 x2 x3 x4 x5 >x
