All,
I've just updated the machine that gatekeeper grabs Milo stuff
from. The update itself will happen overnight US EST. The latest
sources are milo-1.3.73.tgz and I've updated most of the images in
/test-images (not eb66 or eb66+). The changes are:

(1) New linload.exe (/arc/linload.exe). This is v1.3. It's now
small again and this, together with new Milo images solves the
problem where Milo gets confused on memory size in large systems when
loading from WNT ARC.

(2) Preliminary MSDOS support. It works ok on SCSI and pretty well
on floppy. Use "blah -t msdos blah".

(3) Preliminary (test) image for the Platform 2000 (in /test-images/
Platform2000/milo).

Dave
