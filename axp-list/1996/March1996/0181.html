All,
I've updated the sources on gatekeeper with milo-1.3.75.tgz.
This includes the now fully working MSDOS support and builds against
kernels 1.3.74 and onwards. To build against earlier kernels,
change the ifdef around __get_free_pages() in devices.c. The only
images that I've updated are in /test-images/alphapc64 (which is
strangely enough my main development machine). I'll update more over
time and let you all know but right now I'm concentrating on fixing
the reboot stuff so that it works in all circumstances.
