On Mon, 18 Mar 1996 eli@imsa.edu wrote:

> 1.What is necessary to install on a machine with a kenetics motherboard (seperate distribution or boot disk???)

You need a separate kernel and Milo (the Kinetics patches have been merged
into the standard source) which the Kinetics folks can point you to. Red
Hat works fine with those. Yes, the SCSI is supported.
