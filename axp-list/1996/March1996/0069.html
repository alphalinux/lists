Hello Alpha users

I'm trying to put together a hardware compatible list of components together
that run on the Digital Alpha Cabriolet and EB164 motherboards with Red Hat
Linux.

I currently have a list of approved NT devices and such that are solid.
However, Linux is somewhat different and I need some help. So if you can
send me some e-mail on what you have running in your Cabriolet or EB164 I
would greatly appreciate it.

Items needed,
SCSI-2 PCI based (anything besides NCR53c810 based)
Wide SCSI controllers PCI (anything exist?)
Tape drive support, 1/4", DAT, etc...
Sound cards
Video Cards (have a list already, but its small)
Modem cards
ISDN
Token Ring
Any type of network card FDDI, etc...

I personally had problems with any disks larger than 2GB. Has
anyone been able to address a drive larger than 2Gb on Linux?

At the end of this month I will release the list receive to this news group
to help others in their quest to build an Alpha system with supported hardware.

If anyone wants the list I have on supported NT hardware drop me a note and
I'll send it along.

Thank you,
