>How much does it cost? I browsed the web server of digital, can not find any
>price information about the new 500 alphastation

Digital as a company normally does not display set prices on the web (unless
they are what we call "Maynard List Prices", which are usually very high)
for a variety of reasons. We *may* post figures of "around" or "starting at",
but hardly ever fixed prices.

The reason for this is that we sell a lot of equipment through VARs,
distributors and resellers who tend to set their own prices due to volumes
sold, special configurations, etc. We do not want to "pre-set" the prices
of these channels, and particularly in the price-sensitive market of Linux.

In the Linux space Digital encourages customers to buy from our VARs,
distributors and resellers because we feel that you will get the best prices
on Linux machines from them due to their ability to mix and match third party
peripherals for the highest possible performance at the lowest possible cost
to you.

I encourage you to use the dealer list located on the FAQ at

http://www.azstarnet.com/~axplinux

or the dealers pointed to by the Red Hat Alpha FAQ:

http://speedy.redhat.com/alpha/

or other places. Call around for the best deal, or send e-mail. Most of these
vendors listed have web sites, and they MOST DEFINITELY list prices. :-)
