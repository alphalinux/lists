>hI, does the soundsystem in UDB support FM synth?
>I tried to open /dev/sequencer but it said no such device.

No; the UDB is only a degenerate Microsoft Sound System compatible.

The CODEC is there, at 0x534, but there's no IRQ or DMA channel registers
to allow those things to be programmed. Thus its always IRQ #9, DMA #3.

Works quite well, though, and requires no configuration oddities, thanks
to Hannu and his sound drivers... :-)
