Hello Eli

>I have found, after looking at various benchmarks, that intel based systems
generally have higher performance than comparably priced alpha systems.
But, I have found that 64bit RISC architecture is actually just as fast in
multitasking environments. What I am really interested in knowing is how
the 21066 166mhz and 233mhz systems compare to intel systems performing
general UNIX tasks.

Best rule is the SPEC marks

P133 21066-166Mhz 21066-233Mhz
SPECint 147 70 98
SPECfp 109 91 128

So, as you can see if your doing something that requires a lot of floating
point calculations than the 233Mhz is faster than a P133. You don't see any
real speed on Alpha until you move up to a 21064a or 21164 processors.
