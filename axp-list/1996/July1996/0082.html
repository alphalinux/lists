Hello Jay,

many thanks for your work. "Your" binary XEmacs19.14 on Alpha-Linux
works excellent.

by the way: do you use "TM"? This is for MIME support of Gnus, VM etc.
in Emacsen.

As I am german and because I often get Mails with "german umlauts",
this package is quite useful. TM and the "Mime Viewer" works perfectly
with Gnus, but in VM-Mode it is not possible to scroll an EMail that
does not fit onto one page. Normaly one has to press "space" to scroll
an email but in VM-mode "space" skips to the next message.

In Gnus-mode scrolling of longer postings works perfectly. Thats why I
think its a problem with VM on alpha platforms. By the way everything
is fine in Intel boxes with the same configuration.
