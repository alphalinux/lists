> Here is a description of the SIMMs:

> The working simms have this (4Mb)
> 7* MCM54400AN70 1* MCM36100ASG70 4* MCM51100AJ70
> TQQKJ9345 APAPMA9344 UQQCM9345

> The not working ones (16Mb)
> 8* TI KOREA - 60 1* BP44C
> ZA17400 G 8001C-6
> GA 66Z230 G 9607

> As I wrote, I put the 4*16Mb SIMMS in my pentium and they work, so I don't
> think they are bad.

While these numbers do not tell me much, the fact that they work in
the P5 may just mean that the 32 data bits are OK. The parity bits
may still be bad since, AFAIK, none of the P5 chipsets support parity.
