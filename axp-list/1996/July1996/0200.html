Benjamin C. Goodwin wrote:
> The elfball doesn't have crypt in it - so I need to build up my own
> glibc.. I'm getting a bit confused as to where to get what I need to
> build it.

Crypt is a problem. Roland is trying to figure out what to do about
that now. He'd had a good plan with an implicitly loaded libcrypt.so,
but it turns out that it is illegal to export software that even makes
it easy to drop in an encryption module of foreign origin.

*sigh* There are some amazingly stupid people making decisions
about things of which they have no clue.

Anyway, as for the rest, you need

> -rw-r--r-- 1 4841 24 29904 Jul 18 05:07 libc-960717-alpha.diff.gz

as well as glibc snapshot 960717. The last I'll make available as
the three files libc-960714.tar.gz, libc-960715-960716.diff.gz, and
libc-960716-960717.diff.gz in my linux/axp/private directory on atheist.

It isn't too terribly hard to add the bits from glibc-1.09-crypt.tar.gz,
if you are impatient. I should think that they'll decide what they
want to do administratively with crypt this week though.
