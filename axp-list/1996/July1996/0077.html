Akio
most video cards look pretty good, but the S3 based ones
(eg Number 9 PCI) are very stable. Jay will know which X servers work
best on EV5/EV56/Alcor based systems. Zxlp should work fine but I've
heard of a few problems with the Mach64s (I'm running the #9 on
my PC164).

As for videos, the Qlogic ISP fast&wide is now very good as
is the old NCR 810. I'd go for the Qlogic if I were you.

As for WNT - I had loads of trouble with the Mach64, this
may just have been me but again, the #9 works just fine. For ethernet
I would use the 21041/21040 based cards.

Have fun, Dave
