I've built a binary distribution of the latest XEmacs (19.14) for
Linux/Alpha, and made it available at gatekeeper.dec.com as:

pub/DEC/Linux-Alpha/X11/xemacs-19.14-alpha-unknown-linux2.0.0.tgz

This was built on an EB164 running Red Hat Linux 3.0.3 and kernel 2.0.0.

Note that this archive has *only* the executable binaries; look for the
"common" archive for XEmacs 19.14 at www.xemacs.org, its home site.

The patches necessary were *very* small and *very* trivial; I'll try to make
them available as well.

Let me know of any problems...
