> tar czvf (name of file).tar (path to directory)/*
>
> This will get you everything in the selected directory and compress
> it...:)

not really true, this won't include any "dot files" (.*) in "path to directory"
(and command line might be *way* too long for some shells/Unices when there
are lots of files in "path to directory" !)

also you shouldn't use the extension ".tar" for compressed tar files.
so I'd suggest

tar cvf (name of file).tar (path to directory)/.
or
tar czvf (name of file).tar.gz (path to directory)/.
