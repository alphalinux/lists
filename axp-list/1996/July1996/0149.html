Read your posting to Red Hat mailer and I thought I would suggest the following:

BusLogic BT946C ( SCSI-2 FAST) or 956C ( FAST and Wide ) or 958C (
Fast/Wide/Ultra )
These are in the 2.04 kernels already, run well, and are bloddy quick!
The 956 and 958 have both 50 pin and 68 pin Wide internal connectors, aso
you can connect 2 strings directly to it with normal cabling. FAST/WIDE on
the 68 and the rest, inc. your tapes, CD's and older drives on the 50
connector. This makes internal cabling a lot more straitforward. One
"gotcha" is that you can onlu use 2 of the 3 available connectors at once,
so if you use both internal, you can't use the external connector. Still,
this is usually not a big deal.
We are selling the 956 and 958 boards, as are various others around. Our
selling price, with no Windoze kit, is $ 255 ( U.S. dollars )
One other thing, Buslogic and Mylex recently merged, so they should be
healthy for a while.

Hope this helps...
