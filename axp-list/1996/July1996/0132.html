Greetings! I'm a long time user of Linux on Intel boxes who couldn't pass
up the deal of a brand new 166 MHz Universal Desktop Box for $689. I've
visited the web page and read the FAQ, so I know this box is supported,
but I have a couple other questions before my new box arrives:

1. How stable is Alpha Linux? Could I replace my P166 box (it routes
my 128K ISDN line to my Ethernet...this is the most important thing) with
the Alpha (and thus free up the Pentium for Windows 95 use)? Could I
replace our P100 news server at the office with an Alpha?

2. (Most importantly) Are my Intel ext2 partitions compatible with Alpha
Linux? I can borrow a tape drive from the office and do a backup/restore
if I need to, but I'd rather just plug in my existing SCSI drives into the
Alpha, upgrade to Alpha binaries (with RedHat Linux, of course :) ), and
go to town.

Thanks in advance. I'm looking forward to getting my Alpha box late next
week (if only it could be sooner *grin*)
