Akio

> Most video cards look pretty good, but the S3 based ones
> (eg Number 9 PCI) are very stable. Jay will know which X servers work
> best on EV5/EV56/Alcor based systems. Zxlp should work fine but I've
> heard of a few problems with the Mach64s (I'm running the #9 on
> my PC164).

1. most S3 cards *do* seem to work fine, although I've not heard of the
new VIRGE chipset being tested on Alpha.

2. DEC's TGA (ZLXp-E1) is fully supported for console and Xserver; the
newest DEC graphics cards, the PowerStorm series, are *not*, however.

3. The MATROX Millenium will prolly work for console, but there's no XFree86
server available or planned; perhaps a commercial X house like X-Inside
or Metro-Link have plans, but I haven't heard anything at this time.

4. Mach64 is *VERY* unstable with the EV5/EV56 machines when running X,
although it seems to work fine for console text mode, and running X
on the EV4/EV45-based platforms.
