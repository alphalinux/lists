I'm still seeing problems with the 53c7,8xx SCSI driver and enabling
disconnects. I get hangs and panics of various types (including the
scheduling while in an interrupt). With disconnect disabled, things
are very stable.

I've switched back to the ncr53c8xx driver. It appears to be much more
stable with disconnects enabled.
