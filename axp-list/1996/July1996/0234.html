> Does anyone know how/if the de4x5 driver can be told to put the
> card into 10Mbps mode? I saw the bit about the autoconfig problem
> with the de500, but didn't see, e.g., a kernel command-line switch
> to manually configure it.

change following line in drivers/net/CONFIG from

DE4X5_OPTS = -DDE4X5_AUTOSENSE=AUTO

to

DE4X5_OPTS = -DDE4X5_AUTOSENSE=_10Mb
