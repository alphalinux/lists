Hello Ethan!

> Is the SCSI version of the Iomega 100Mb zip drive supported for Linux
> on the Alpha?

Sure. It functions in the same way as on all intel-driven Linux systems:
you use them like hard disks or removable disks.

Note that the only visible partition is sd?4 on preformatted ZIP-disks
(they are always pre-formatted with MSDOS-FAT and MSDOS-filesystem at the
moment). I did not manage to put other filesystems on it!
