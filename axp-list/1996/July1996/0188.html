As recently discussed, the UDB by default boots its SRM console in a high
resolution mode that won't work on plain old VGA monitors. There are jumpers
on the motherboard to change this.

No matter what mode you start in, when you fire up ARC it goes into a mode
that old monitors can handle. So, if you don't want to muck about with your
motherboard, you can boot the thing with no monitor attached, wait a few
minutes, type in "arc" and hit return, wait a few more minutes, and attach a
plain old VGA monitor. (I'm installing the second of two systems this way;
this system will have an SVGA monitor on it when I get it home.)
