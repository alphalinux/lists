To use a DAT drive, you must have the tape driver in the kernel. In addition,
you must enable SCSI disconnects in the SCSI adapter driver. I would suggest
using the 53c8xx driver, *not* the 53c7,8xx driver, which has known problems
with disconnect enabled.
