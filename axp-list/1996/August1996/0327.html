After much trial-and-error with less than helpful messages, I finally
tracked down the problem. In the PCMCIA sources there is an include to io.h
which in turn has includes to one of several files depending on whether
CONFIG_ALPHA_xxx is defined. As it turns out, unless you put in -D__alpha__
and -DCONFIG_ALPHA_LCA in the compiler debug flags during the PCMAIA config
make step, it wont work on the UDB because the default for io.h is to
include jensen.h (which has an undefined function known as _bus_inb - the
one the machine was telling me was missing). Also, some of the other
makefiles probably should be looked at since they don't carry through the
debug flags put in during the config make (These are the cardmgr and probe
items. They both included jensen.h anyway since the debug flags weren't
carried into the makefile in the cardmgr directory).

Anyhow, things work now. Wish I didn't have to figure it out myself, though...
