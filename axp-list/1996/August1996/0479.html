Robert,
yes you can exit the script, or rather work around it. Rather
usefully, Red Hat gives you two terminal windows. Use ALT-F1 and ALT-F2
to flick between them. The non-install one (F2) has just a su (root) hash
(#) prompt. You actually only need two disks:

(1) DOS - vmlinux.gz , linload.exe, milo.arc
(2) Ramdisk
