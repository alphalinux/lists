At 12:36 PM 8/8/96 -0400, you wrote:
>Every time I try to compile a 2.0.11 kernel on my UDB with MSS sound support
> it fails. When I compile without MSS sound support it compiles fine.
>Does anyone have any ideas.

Yes; you probably took the default for FM synthesizer, YM3812 or some such.
Such does not exist as part of an MSS, at least on the UDB, so you get some
undefines when you link.

If such is not the case (or even if it is), PLEASE INCLUDE MORE INFO the next
time you ask for help. Just an "it fails" is not enough, most times.
