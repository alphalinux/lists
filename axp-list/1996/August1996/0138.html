On Mon, 5 Aug 1996, Brian Walters wrote:

> I'm trying to ftp into my redhat alpha and it just won't let me unless I
> use anonymous. I would like to know how to "loosen" things up since I'm
> not on the net.

Create an /etc/shells that has the line "/bin/bash" in it, and a line for
any other shells you use.

This was a bug in Red Hat 3.0.3/Alpha.
