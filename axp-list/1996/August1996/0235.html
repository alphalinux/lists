Max Hadersbeck wrote:
>
> Hi
> I want to overwrite my NVRAM of my UDB with WinNT settings to boot
> directly linux. But its not possible
> I can write the changes in arc mode, but after power down it forgets my
> new settings.
> My aim is to bring the UDB to start LINUX immedeatly. Without any input
> from the console. Are there other possibilities ?
> Thanks MAX
>

I got the solution from Stephen J. Gaudet, <sjg@dcginc.com> :
1). go in under manage boot selection and delete the WinNT.

2). back out on and save the new setting. The save option is the option
on
the lower most portion of the screen.

I did this and it works now.

Thanks to Stephen
