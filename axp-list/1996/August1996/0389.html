To use arcinst, start up the NT boot options screen. Choose "Run a
program". For a SCSI CD-ROM drive, enter "cd:\alpha\arcinst.exe" with
the NT 3.51 CD in the drive. Be aware that this will repartition your
drive, so it will wipe out anything you have on the drive.
