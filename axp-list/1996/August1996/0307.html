At 02:07 hrs. 08-11-96 -0700, you wrote:
>Hi,
>
>Does anyone know what the default refresh a UDB spits out? I have a
>15" CTX multi-synch, and I want to make sure that my UDB is driving
>it within its normal operating range. The image looks good... but
>I don't want to fry my monitor in a few weeks.
>
It's not written in stane, as the defaults are configurable by jumpers in
the UDB. Most that I have seen are configured for 1024x768 at 70Hz. Pretty
safe rate as long as you have 1024 supported by your monitor. This is the
vESA standard refresh rate.
