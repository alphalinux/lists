Wrong ! The UDB serial port pinout is ACTUALLY as follows ( you forgot the
ground pin, which is NOT a good thing..)
More good info on UDB can also be found at:
http://www.annex.co.uk/systems/udb.html

UDB Combined Serial Port Pin Signals:

2 Port A TXD
3 Port A RXD
4 Port A RTS
5 Port A CTS
6 Port A DSR
7 Port A GND
8 Port A DCD
20 Port A DTR
22 Port A RI

7 Port B GND
11 Port B DTR
12 Port B DCD
13 Port B CTS
14 Port B TXD
16 Port B RXD
19 Port B RTS
23 Port B DSR
25 Port B RI
