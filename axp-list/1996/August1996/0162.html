A working bonnie binary is at
ftp://ftp.azstarnet.com/pub/linux/axp/misc/axpbin-bonnie.gz. It's a
static ELF binary, so be sure you have a reasonably recent kernel
running (e.g., 2.0.11).

On the 275MHz Cabriolet, I get the following with ncr53c7,8xx. It's
not very meaningful to compare these numbers with other systems, but
I don't have time right now to try out and measure the other ncr driver.

--david
