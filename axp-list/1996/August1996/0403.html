For all the UDB owners out there, old and new, check out:

ftp://ftp.digital.com/pub/DEC/Alpha/firmware/interim/udb

This directory contains materials for (in the words of the author):

UDB console firmware install/update/recovery

Contents are 2 floppy images and a README; one image is for updating
the firmware to the latest released version, V3.8-3; the other image
is used by the FailSafe Loader to recover from catastrophic loss of
firmware ROM contents.

Check them out, see what you think, let me know of any problems or
suggestions for improvement.
