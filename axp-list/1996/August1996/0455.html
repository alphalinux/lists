For anyone who cares....
I've noticed that a few people have been asking why they should upgrade
the firmware on their UDB. Here's my experience.
Before the upgrade, I'd boot the Alpha Multia 166 (even when it was just
a quick one for reboot) and my cheap 2x Mashusita Cdrom would freak out
and I'd hang up while scanning the SCSI bus.
After the upgrade, I haven't had this problem. That's it! If you think it
might help you, try it.
I work for a software company in Houston, Tx so I understand that just
because the chief programmer runs in saying how great the new version is,
sometimes it's better to let the ginny pigs try i first. This ginny pig
is very happy with the results.
