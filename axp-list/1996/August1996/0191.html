> Who had successfully installed Linux to Alpha Server 1000 4/233,
> please give me tips. What is the way of installing milo from Digital Unix
> SRM console? What boot and root images should I use?

At the time I installed it there were no ready made images. You can find them
now on gatekeeper.dec.com:/pub/DEC/Linux-Alpha/mikasa*.
I put them also to ftp.aec.at:/x/axp/
On ftp.aec.at:/x/axp/ you can also find an floppy-image of milo to boot via
srm (milo-2.0.11-mikasa-srm.dd.gz)
in srm
boot dva0
should bring up milo.

2.0.11-8xx.gz is the kernel i currently run on my A1000 4/266 with the
ncr53c8xx driver. It should work for you.

> I've read in FAQ such a command :
>
> cat mboot bootm > /dev/fd0

after building milo you get them with make bootm and make mboot.
But you should be fine with one of the ready made diskimages.

If you need anything special in the kernel just mail me. I think I went
through most of the problems one can have booting a Mikasa system.
