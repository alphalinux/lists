Consider one of the DEC cards, they are VERY likely to work. Besides they
are fast and not bad cards in general. Price isn't bad. Digital DE500 (Combo
co-ax and 10/100T is about $160.00.
Alternatively, their KZPSM-AA is Digital 21040AA ether with all
three ports, PLUS a Qlogic ISP1020A SCSI running a 68 pin, WIDE SCSI bus
(20MB/sec bandwidth). All on ONE PCI card!
Not REALLY cheap tho' as it's about $500, still, no more than the
cost of the separate cards together. One small catch, NO external SCSI port.
You have to make a special cable with one, if you REALLY need it.

The SMC ULtras and 3COM 509's work fine too... Mainly thanks to the work of :
Paul Gortmaker <gpg109@rsphy1.anu.edu.au> who has done a bang-up job
on the drivers, and sometimes can be absolutely wizard at making
this stuff work.
