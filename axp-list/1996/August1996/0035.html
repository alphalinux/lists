The regular kernel sources do not contain Alpha ELF support (yet).
You'll need the patch at:

ftp.azstarnet.com:/pub/linux/axp/sys/axp-diffs-*.gz

The latest patch is for 2.0.7 (I think), but it probably applies
cleanly on top of 2.0.10 as well. I'll update the patch soon
(hopefully no later than this weekend). Linus plans on adding Alpha
ELF support to the 2.0 kernel, so this problem will be gone soon.
