>I know nothing about the Durango board, but several of the DEC platforms
>that use the EV5 or EV56 are quite stable at this time. Among them are
>the EB164 and PC164, both Digital Semiconductor boards, and the ALCOR
>or AlphaStation 600, made by the workstation group. Others could probably
>be stable as well, if we could get our hands on them, such as the XLT or
>XL-366, Noritake or AlphaServer 1000A, Brett or AlphaStation 500, etc.
>But, at the moment, only the first three mentioned above are know to be stable.
>
>Note that a number of Alpha clone makers, such as Carerra and Microway, have
>boards based on the EB164 (and soon PC164), and these are also known to be
>stable.
