On Mon, 5 Aug 1996, Martin Cooke wrote:
>
> Our noname gets in a funky state sometimes where we
> can't telnet or rlogin into it. At the console
> I can login as a user but when I su it just hangs.
>
> We are have red-hat 2.1 and the kernal is 2.0.8
>
> If someone has seen this before and knows the fix,
> please let me know.

Similar behaviour was seen on x86 machines with broken syslogd's (syslogd
goes bad, doesn't read any syslog messages, and thus anything that wants
to log messages blocks).

Try to update your syslogd/klogd, and hopefully the problems will be gone
(maybe you can just use the 3.0.3 rpms, they seem to work at least for me)
