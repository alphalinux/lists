Here is the UDB dual serial port pinout. You can find some more usefull
information on UDB on this site:
http://www.brouhaha.com/~eric/computers/udb.html

UDB Communications Port Pinouts
===============================

Port Pin Signal
-----------------------

COM1
2 TXD
3 RXD
4 RTS
5 CTS
6 DSR
7 GND
8 DCD
20 DTR
22 RI

COM2
11 DTR
12 DCD
13 CTS
14 TXD
16 RXD
19 RTS
23 DSR
25 RI
