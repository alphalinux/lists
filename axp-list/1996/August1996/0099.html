My linked kernel (vmlinux) is around 2.5Mbytes, the stripped (using quickstrip)
kernel is around 1.4Mbytes and the gzip'd stripped kernel is about 0.5Mbytes.
So, your kernel is more than a little strange at 8Mbytes. Leaving that
aside, once you build a milo bootable kernel like this:

$ make vmlinux
$ quickstrip vmlinux vmlinux.stripped
$ gzip vmlinux.stripped

This gives you vmlinux.stripped.gz which you can boot. As for:
