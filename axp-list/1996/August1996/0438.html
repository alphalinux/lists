> the subject sais it all. The Saskatoon Linux Group (SLG) has organized a

> So is it possible? Blade/Craftworks/Redhat-Linux/APX do not seem to mention
> the AlphaStation series >400 anywhere. Would someone agree to give us a

I believe the AS500 (internal name Bret?) is a "clone" or the AS600 (internal
name ALCOR), so, if you use the kernel for the ALCOR, it *ought* to work.

You'll need to boot from SRM console, as there's no MILO for ALCOR/BRET at
this time. Hope that's possible, as I've heard that machines are now being
built with "half-FLASH"; ie, FLASH ROM big enough to hold ARC or SRM but
not both. Ask for an AS500 set-up to run Digital UNIX and you'll get SRM.

But, of course, your mileage may vary, as I know of none who have actually
tried this...

Good luck, let us know of your experiences.
