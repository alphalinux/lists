you'll need to compile the PCMCIA sources with the <undocumented> switches I
described, namely the -DCONFIG_ALPHA_LCA and -D__alpha__ in order for things
to work. I did go back and try it and just doing that works. I didn't go
back and reinstall my old kernel (1.3.90) to see if it would have worked
with it, though. YMMV.
