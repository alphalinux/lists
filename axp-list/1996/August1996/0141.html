On Tue, 6 Aug 1996, Linus Torvalds wrote:

> Any redhat people want to fix this? It's silly not to have /bin/sh in
> /etc/shells, even if /bin/sh is only a symlink to /bin/bash..

It was a bug -- RPM was a tad broken w/ executing postinstall scripts for
a release or two, and that release happened to make it onto the Alpha. Problems
like that are blindingly obvious on Intel's (ldconfig never gets run), but
more subtle in this case.

It's already been fixed in-house, but all those CD's are a bear to fix :-(
