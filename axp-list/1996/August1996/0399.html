As I recall, there was a concerted effort to rid the alpha port (and 2.0.x
especially) of these pesky little devils. I got one at
fffffc0000382cd0: fffffc0000534c19 29 2
If I look in the System.map in my kernel tree, the first mem address says
it's in ipx_rcv. Interestingly, I get the message at regular intervals
(except the message ceases when the PC hooked up to my little lan is turned
off). The PC uses ipx protocol for some things. I know that these messages
are supposed to be ignorable but this one is a real pain.....
