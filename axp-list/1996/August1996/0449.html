At 12:31 AM 08-16-96 -0500, Christopher S. Rider" <syzygy@mcs.net> wrote:
> I am attaching a new Micropolis 4221 drive to the SCSI chain and need
>to know if the 4221's default of supplying terminator power to pin
>26 of the SCSI bus needs to be removed.
>
You normally do not need to worry about this. All you need is at least 1
device providing term power. More is not a bad thing. SCSI devices are diode
protected on this line, so that a "back-flow" of current is not possible. In
some cases multiple devices providing term power is even desirable, as it
means that sufficient current is available on the bus to provide the
termination power diffential needed to power the passive terminators on most
devices.
