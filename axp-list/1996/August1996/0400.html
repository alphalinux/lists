Thunderhawk (et al),

I had a little look at ./net/ipx/af_ipx.c to see if anything was obvious, for
example a short being randomly pulled from an unaligned byte pointer. Looking at
ipx.h the typedef struct ipx_address worries me, the first field is defined as
"unsigned long net" which gives 4 bytes on Intel and 8 bytes on Alpha. It is used
in the ipx packet definition a little later on. So, does this look wierd to anyone else
and has anyone got ipx working between Intel and Alpha?
