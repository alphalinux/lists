options, and see where it starts working correctly again...

like the IP_FORWARD stuff, but many of them may be *completely* untested, or
at least untested in such large combinations.

Some may indeed be more appropriate to start with, given the symptoms you
describe, but I'm such a networking newbie I wouldn't know what to suggest;
maybe some people out there have successfully use some of the options, or
knwo a bit about the code of some of the options, to suggest where to start.

configuration which has always worked.

occur; I'll bet not...
