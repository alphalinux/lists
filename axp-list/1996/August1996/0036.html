The ELF support in the normal kernelsource tree does *NOT* work on
alphas and can't be compiled. Either stay with only ecoff support,
or apply david mt's axp-diffs... If you want to do the latter, you
should be prepared to work with code that might not be fully working,
and join the axplinux-libc mailinglist (echo "subscribe axplinux-libc" |
mail majordomo@azstarnet.com)...
