After compiling 2.0.11 kernel on mikasa with EISA DE425
I came accross strange ethernet behavior :

1) every time I reboot (3 finger salute or reset - it doesn't affect anything)
ifconfig and arp display different hardware ethernet address (mostly wrong).

2) I ping any host without problem and arp table on the host I pinged
is filled with this wrong address. Also I can do DNS lookups, NFS mounts
etc. via UDP service, but not TCP. Sometimes I have true address (or change
it by hand) and can use TCP.

3) ping times vary from 0.0 to even 90 ms on thin ethernet segment which
consists of 2 computers i.e. not loaded at all.
