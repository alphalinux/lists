Hello *,

While tracking a problem with multisession CDs in my Toshiba
3701B SCSI CD-ROM, I have encountered the following question:

On the one hand, Alphas don't have segmentation (says
~linux/asm-alpha/segment.h), but on the other hand, the
verify_area() in ~linux/mm/memory.c checks for it, and thus
cannot find a vma to verify (thus EFAULT).

While debugging this latter function, it appears that it is
not so much used as I thought. Where do I misunderstand the
use of verify_area, or is the use so little that it hasn't
been noted yet?

When someone knowledgeable would be so kind as to explain the
vma-stuff to me, I would be most obliged.

Met vriendelijke groeten, Wim van Dorst

messages would be nice too :-).
