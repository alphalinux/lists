I am currently running Craftworks linux, and have had the system up, on
the net for about 30 days with no problems. I have fixed the FTP
problem, and it seems stable. Not a lot was included in the distribution
that I have. I had to compile my own send mail and pine/pico. They seem
to have a competent service dept. BUT they charge for it. Also there're
are no manuals last I checked.

I am very happy with the performance. They webserver (apache) simply
SCREAMS. So far so good. I hear under NT that it is pertty quick also.
Well see, I'm getting another machine, and that will be a dual boot machine.
