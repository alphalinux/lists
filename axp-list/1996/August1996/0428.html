According to sp0t the w0nder slug:
> On Thu, 15 Aug 1996, Andreas Johansson wrote:
>
> > Has anyone got the EtherLink 3 (3c509) network driver working on Linux/Alpha?
> > I have tried a little, but my driver hangs when I ifconfig.
> > --
> >
>
> You were able to get that far? When icompile in 3c509 support nothing
> happens. At bootup it detects the SCSI controller/drives, then nothing
> when it's supposed to detect the ethernet card, it just moves on as if i
> hadn't compiled in ehternet support. Could you tell me how you were able
> to get it that far?

There was a problem with the SLOW_DOWN_IO macro. It's way to quick.
This means that the EEPROM is not read correctly.

I replaced it with an empty loop to 2 000 000. This is naturally nothing
but a quick-and-dirty hack, but it works on my noname 233 MHz.
