Applix has been around for about 10-12 years and runs on several
platforms including Sun, SGI, HP. It is much more expensive on those
platforms i.e. just Applix Word is $795 or so and there are several
similarly priced modules included in the Redhat version. As I understand
things the Redhat version is the result of Redhat negotiation with
Applix rather than Applix aggressively marketing their wares to Linux
users. Good work Redhat. There probably needs to be a reasonable
Linux/Alpha population to warrant the same effort (read investment) in
that direction. Though I think a lot of the work would be the common
given that it is a Linux port both times. I hope Redhat does it, I'd buy
it at the Linux prices, Applix has some good stuff.
