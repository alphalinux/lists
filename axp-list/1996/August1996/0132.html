On Mon, 5 Aug 1996, Brian Walters wrote:
>
> I'm trying to ftp into my redhat alpha and it just won't let me unless I
> use anonymous. I would like to know how to "loosen" things up since I'm
> not on the net.

Do you have "/bin/sh" as your login shell?

RedHat doesn't seem to have /bin/sh listed in /etc/shells, resulting in
ftpd not accepting logins from real users that have /bin/sh as the shell.

Any redhat people want to fix this? It's silly not to have /bin/sh in
/etc/shells, even if /bin/sh is only a symlink to /bin/bash..

Linus
