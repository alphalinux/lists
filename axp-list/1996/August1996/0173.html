EB164, Kernel 2.0.10. ncr53c8xx driver, config options appended.

Raw I/O doesn't seem any faster to me (I susspect both drivers push the
limits of the device), but it can sucessfully drive my DAT, for which the
7,8xx driver fails -- crashes with disconnects enabled and timeouts with
them disabled.

The tagged queueing is a bonus here too, as it seems to aid streaming.
(No numbers on that though.)
