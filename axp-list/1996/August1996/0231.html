After reading another message from teh list about slow serial response, I
decided to play around in Minicom to see what was going on. What I
discovered was that either the kernel or the C library on the Alpha
doesn't seem to support the calls used on the Intel version to set speeds
greater than 38,400 bps. My solution was to simply set spd_vhi on the
serial port (thus making 38,400 actually 115,200) and then telling the
PPP daemon to set the serial port to 38.4k. Works like a charm now.

Of course, now I'm getting occasional receive errors (one or two errors
for every 200 packets or so) but I think I can live with that for now.
