For all those who have been trying out ELF bits, I have a new
set of gcc patches. This time, it's against a stock 2.7.2
instead of a prerelease. I'd like for as many people as are
able to try it out and find out if it goes boom.

I'd gone with a prerelease in the first place because of bugs
in the optimizer, but I'd also hoped that an official 2.8
release would be sometime soon. Such is not to be.

Anyway, the goods are at

ftp://atheist.tamu.edu/pub/richard/linux/axp/rth-gcc-2.7.2-960804.diff.gz

Thanks for any and all feedback you can provide.
