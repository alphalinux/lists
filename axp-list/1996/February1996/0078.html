Hi,

Anybody else having problems with Maelstrom under recent kernels?

It used to run fine, under 1.3.43 and, I think 1.3.48, but with 1.3.57 on,
to the latest I've tried, 1.3.61, it's got problems. It consistently hangs
just before starting to play, sometimes leaving the screen completely dark,
other times not, just not doing anything. It's not crashing the system, just
itself.

I wonder if it needs porting of some kind to the latest kernels or sound
drivers or something? I realize the binaries have been around for quite a
while, maybe it would be a Good Thing (tm) to recompile the latest version
(recently announced, I believe... :-) with the latest GCC/LIBC and all?

Have Linus' patches ever been made public?

Reason I ask, it makes a very nice demo on the UDB, with its builtin sound,
and shows off the TGA Xserver nicely... :-)

Thanks for any help.
