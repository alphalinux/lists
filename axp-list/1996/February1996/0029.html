> I was just talking to someone who told me that digital unix (OSF/1)
> binaries would run under Linux/AXP.
>
> WIll some/all programs do this? Netscape would be the most usefull.

Some programs, ie the statically linked ones, should run, but may not for
various reasons (different root FS directory structure, etc). The dynamically
linked ones, the ones using DU shared libraries, may also run, but you'd
better have a license for using those libraries, as well as the loader which
knows how to link them together at runtime...

Netscape (currently) is dynamically linked, but there's an NCSA Mosaic
binary for Alpha Linux at ftp.redhat.com, that works quite well...
