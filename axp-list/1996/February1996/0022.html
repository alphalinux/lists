A couple of things:

First, start your XEmacs port with the patches David M-T had for Emacs
19.2[89] or thereabouts, on his site (ftp.azstarnet.com). That is, unless
Emacs 19.30 incorporated those changes for Alpha-Linux, *and* the latest
XEmacs is based on 19.30. I did have to make a few additional patches, but
unfortunately they are lost... :-(

If you get desperate, I'll make a binary available of my port, but its at
least 6 months old and was never compiled -O2, so it is *big* and somewhat
slow...

Good luck!
