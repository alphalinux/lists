I think I remember a rumor that Adaptec PCI cards were also supported on the
Alpha. AHA-2940 perhaps ? There is a Linux/BSD Adaptec mail list to try.
Something like: majordomo@FreeBSD.org .

I've heard on the Linux/BSD NCR SCSI list (majordomo@cs.Colorado.EDU ?) that
the NCR chips "don't like to share PCI cycles with other cards". I have heard
also that early ET4000w32p PCI cards (rev 0) "steal PCI cycles" therefore try an
ISA VGA card if you experience SCSI problems that can't be explained by
termination or other logical reasons... Otherwise I see no problems with
the ncr53c810s on the NoNames.
