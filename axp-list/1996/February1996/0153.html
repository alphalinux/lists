System: Noname
Dist: RedHat 2.1/Release
Kernel: 1.3.65-1.3.66
Problem: unresolved symbols in several modules..

I've tried to build both .65 & .66 with:

CONFIG_MODULES=y
CONFIG_MODVERSIONS=y

and

CONFIG_MODULES=y
# CONFIG_MODVERSIONS is not set

Both versions, with and without Davids patch (which adds -dc to the modflags),
in fact, his patch doesn't seem to make any differance at all.. :-)

Working modules: minix.o, ip_alias.o, lp.o, dummy.o, sr_mod.o, st.o
Modules that are missing memcpy: floppy.o, nfs.o
fat.o misses: strtok, strchr

Oh, and I also noticed that haveing DISCONNECT enabled was 'suicide'.. Maybe
haveing an ifndef __alpha__ around that option should be a good thing?

just fine! :-)
