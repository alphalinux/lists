Thank you for contacting me through the House of
Representatives' Constituent Electronic Mail System (CEMS). I
appreciate hearing from you and am pleased to be a part of this
effort to offer citizens a quick and efficient way to communicate
with their representatives in Congress.

I value your opinions and will keep your concerns in mind.
If you are a resident of North Carolina's 11th Congressional
district (and you included your name and address in your E-Mail),
I will respond to you by the U.S. Mail. If you are not a
resident of the 11th District, however, I encourage you to
contact your own Representative. The Internet addresses for the
Representatives currently using the CEMS service as well as
information on the service itself can be found at the Internet
address CONGRESS@HR.HOUSE.GOV.

Again, thank you for contacting me to express your concerns.
If I can be of any service to you or your family in the future,
please do not hesitate to contact me.

Sincerely,

CHARLES H. TAYLOR
MEMBER OF CONGRESS
