Zachary Kessin wrote:
>
> I was just talking to someone who told me that digital unix (OSF/1)
> binaries would run under Linux/AXP.
>
> WIll some/all programs do this? Netscape would be the most usefull.

Yes and no. Statically linked Digital Unix binaries will run under
Linux. Dynamically linked Digital Unix binaries will run under
Linux if you have the Digital Unix shared libraries.

Mosaic is availabe for Linux/Alpha if you are just looking for a
broser.
