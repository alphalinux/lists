Just uploaded a new Alpha patch and kernels for Noname and Cabrio:

ftp://ftp.azstarnet.com/pub/linux/axp/sys/vmlinux-1.3.67-{cabrio,noname}.gz

and

ftp://ftp.azstarnet.com/pub/linux/axp/sys/axp-diff-1.3.67.gz

The patch is relative to a clean 1.3.67 (i.e., sans Linus's super-67
patch) and includes Dave Rusling's bios32 patch. It also adds syscall
entry points for sendmsg/recvmsg and the new nanosleep call.

Try it out---the NFS read caching works like a charm! :-)
