The bootpd in what I belive to be the current , NetKit-A-0.08, assertion
fails on startup.

Changing the unsigned long definitions in the bootp struct in bootp.h
has made a bootpd that actually works. It still isnt perfect, I got
bootpd[2046]: ioctl SIOCSARP: Invalid argument error, but #undef SIOCSARP
caused it to use the arp command instead, makes it work..

It now seems happy to serve my xterminal a bootp reply. Unfortunatly my
Xterminal and in.tftpd disagree on some thing...
