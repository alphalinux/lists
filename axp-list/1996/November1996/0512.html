On Fri, 8 Nov 1996, Rolf Karlstad wrote:

> Also, of course, / and /usr must be on the same partition. (4.0 bug)

There is an *easy* workaround for this. Install your system w/ separate
partitions and 'mv /usr/lib/libnss* /lib; ldconfig'. Shutdown your system and
reboot it. It will come up dirty the first time through, but work fine
after that.
