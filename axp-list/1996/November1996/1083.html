It's a stock PC164, just with better pricing than ever before... :-)

The PC164 has a CMD646 EIDE controller on the motherboard, directly on the
PCI bus. I believe it *is* busmastering, but there's no LINUX driver
specifically for it, at the moment. It is *NOT* a CMD640, so don't bother
configuring that driver in for the sake of this chip. Simply use the default
IDE and it should all work, although it will not be optimal use of this
chip, until a real driver is written for it.
