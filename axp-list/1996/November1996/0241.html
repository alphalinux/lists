According to D. Steven Daniel:

> > Is the modeline in the sample XF86Config file valid for "1280x1024" 74Hz
> > and 78.85Khz? I know the timing numbers are meaningless but when I try
> > this mode the server responds with no valid modes, the one above it
> > "1280x1024" 61 Hz , 56.5 Khz works any ideas? I need the higher sync
> > rate. thanks

The TGA card isn't hardwired to work at those frequencies in 1280x1024
mode. There are only 15 set refresh rate/resolution/pixel clock modes
that it understands. :-( There is a clock to match 1280x1024 at 75 Mhz
and I believe the clock is 135.00. You could try that. I am working on
a list of UDB/TGA resolutions and will post the results soon. It will
then be necessary to just pick the modes that your *monitor* supports.
