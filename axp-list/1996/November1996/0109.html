Due to negligence at 10:37 AM 11/3/96 -0500, Fude Yao let this roam freely
into my inbox:
|> My upgrade from 3.0.3 to 4.0 is not quite success. I decided
|>that I might need to do an install form scratch. I plan to order CDs for
|>the installation. Who can recommend a good CD-ROM for UDB? Can we make
|>use of the parallel port and PCMCIA ports? I also need to buy a hard
|>drive of 2-3 gig, any suggestions?

Check out APS' externel HDs and CDs (http://www.apstech.com/). They seem to
have fairly decent prices :) Ask for a SCSI 2/Wide -> SCSI 2/Narrow cable,
if you're going to use them with the standard SCSI port on the UDB.
