Hi,

I have just uploaded a new Jensen kernel (2.0.22) on azstarnet, which
includes support for the DE422 ethernet card, plus the 'usual' stuff:
- NE2000, WD80x3, SMC Ultra
- SCSI: AH1742, disk/tape/cdrom, no generic
- parallel port
- serial ports, PS/2 mouse

It would be nice if someone owning a DE422 card could confirm that this
works for them.
