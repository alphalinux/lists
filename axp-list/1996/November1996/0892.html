The "test-images" milo from gatekeeper is good.
This must be on a dos formatted disk together with
linload.exe.
Read the RedHat manual about milo and RedHat installation.
You need two other disks:
2. an image disk with pc164.img,
3. and an image disk with ramdisk.img.
