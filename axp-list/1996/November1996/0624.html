Patches to make xli-1.16 and xplot work on the Alpha are in directory:

ftp://ftp.azstarnet.com/pub/linux/axp/misc/

in files:

axpsrc-xli-1.16.patch.gz
axpsrc-xplot.patch.gz

xli is an image-loader (similar to xv or xloadimage) and xplot is a
nice 3d plotting tool. The sources to both programs can be found on
sunsite.
