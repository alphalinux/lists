AVANTI kernels and MILO will run on AS400 and AS200/205/250/255,
but *NOT* on the XL, unless it's motherboard is really an AS400 MB.
There were some like that early in the product cycle, but the later
XL MB was *not* compatible is certain subtle but important ways.

XL kernels and MILO will run on XL-233/266 as well as AVANTI.
