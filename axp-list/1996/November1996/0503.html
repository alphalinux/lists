Aren't you confusing the refresh rate (vertical sync rate), which is
usually in the 30-100 Hz range, and the horizontal sync rate, which is
roughly equal to refresh rate divided by the number of lines on your
display (typically in the 30-60 kHz (KILO Hertz) range)?

For details on dot clock/hsync/vsync see Eric Raymond's writeup, often found in
/usr/X11R6/lib/X11/doc/VideoModes.doc
