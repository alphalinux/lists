>>> Eduardo Augusto Trettel said:
>
> I have a XL266 with a Matrox Millenium (MGA2064W). I know there is now
> a beta driver for Matrox into the new XFree86 3.2 release (XF86_SVGA server).
> But the SVGA server isn't available for Linux/Alpha... Anyone has more info
> about the Matrox support for AXP? (Yeah, I have already looked at most of
> the related Web pages ;-)

Well, good news and bad... ;-/

The good news is, I have a patch set against the 3.2 release of XFree86 that
seem to mostly work on the Alphas (8, 16, 32-bit depths).

The bad news: the MGA server requires that some *kernel* patches be in place.
I am *extremely* reluctant to put out some kernel patches that a) Linus isn't
crazy about, and thus b) don't have much chance of making their way into the
official sources. So, I am currently working on negotiating the form of the
support the server needs in order for them to be acceptable.

Be patient; this *will* happen...
