Randale Sechrest <rsechres@homer.libby.org> wrote:
> I've got my Multia setup with sda1 1440K, sda2 as root, and sda3 as
> swap. How do I get linloader and milo onto the sda1 partition so I can
> setup autobooting? sda1 is set up as a dos type partition.

Unless I misunderstand the question:

mount -t msdos /dev/sda1 /mnt
cp milo linload.exe /mnt
umount /mnt

or something to that effect.
