I know that Japanese are considered as a lazy people for new
inovations and such, and I agree it at some aspect, but I think most

What kind of "Support" do you have in your mind? Can you say something
particular? Do you have your machine already be installed some
distribution? If not, I recomment a binary package at:

ftp://ftp.eni.co.jp/pub/Linux-Alpha/Linux-Alpha-JP/Goto-Archive/binary/
(There are no "installer", so you must un-tar them on some unices)

environment by yourself from sources. You can find patches and sources
for many application at:
ftp://ftp.eni.co.jp/pub/Linux-Alpha/Linux-Alpha-JP/Goto-Archive/sources/

for later case, I don't know if current RedHat can host some
application well or not - you know, there are no working multibyte
locates for glibc yet. Newer releases of XFree (all of elf version?)
no longer is build with X_LOCALE option and may causes problem. You
