Well, I can say that I found mkdosfs to work quite well. There
did seem to be a case of problems if the partition to be formatted
was over 32Mb, but that is unlikely to be a problem :-) I think
that it also produced a number of misaligned traps, but that is
not a problem.
This partition can then be mounted (with -t msdos) and you can
thus change your Milo very easily. The bigger it is the more Milo's
you can play with ;-)
