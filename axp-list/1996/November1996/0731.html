i've been noticing with 2.1.7 on my udb that pipes seem to break an
awful lot now. something like the following invariably results in a
broken pipe:

zcat large.tar.gz | (cd directory; tar zxBf -)

on second thought, this actually might be a general problem as i think
something similar might be happening with my 486 (running 2.1.9). in
addition, doing something like the following also fails miserably
(with short reads):
tar czf file.tar.gz directory
<suspend and background the tar>
error message --
tar: only wrote <small #> of <large #> bytes to file.tar.gz

has anyone else seen anything like this behaviour or have any
speculations on what might be causing it?
