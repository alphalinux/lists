Add ViaCrypt PGP to the list of Linux/Alpha unsupported software;
ViaCrypt supplies only binaries. Linux is only marginally supported now
by ViaCrypt (they have an outside firm do the port for them). I have no
idea how much Linux business ViaCrypt does for Linux, but I suspect that
Linux/Alpha will have to show some consistent demand for a while before
they will do it. This is just a gut feel based on discussing things with
them 6 months ago; I've not talked with them since PGP bought them out.
