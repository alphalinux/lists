> I have a UDB 266Mhz w/ 56MB RAM with RedHat 4.0. The X server is
> XF86_TGA.
>
> I keep gettin these errors:
>
[...]
> Nov 16 13:54:20 lysithea kernel: X(1027): unaligned trap at 0000000120159174: 0000000120730d84 29 4
>
> and X logs me out of my current session and back to xdm.

This makes absolutely no sense to me, but I think I tracked down what
was causing this sort of message for me... While I was upgrading my
166MHz UDB from RH 3.0.3 to 4.0, I upgraded to the dev-2.4-1 rpm.
After that, I started getting errors like that and sound stopped
working for things like xquake and xgal. I could still "cat
english.au > /dev/audio" and hear Linus's voice though. (Is it just
me, or is that how everyone tests sound? :) Anyway, I thought maybe
/dev/dsp or one of the other audio devices was messed up, so I did a
"MAKEDEV audio". Sound started working right after that, plus I
stopped getting "unaligned trap" messages from X (or at least not the
volume I'd been getting).

Keep in mind that all this was with the ELF TGA X server that came
with RH 4.0. I've since upgraded to XFree 3.2.

Does anyone have a logical explanation for this?
