this is just wrong configuration of your monitor characteristics,
check the monitor section!

> (**) S3: Monitor ID: "My Monitor"

> (--) S3: Mode "800x600" needs hsync freq of 48.08 kHz. Deleted.
> (--) S3: Mode "1024x768" needs hsync freq of 48.36 kHz. Deleted.

obviously 48kHz isn't in the specified frequency range
