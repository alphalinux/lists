Here's how we go about testing. Because we qualify processors for higher
speeds we do the following testing.

1) Test all processors on test beds for 72 hours under Windows NT HCTs.

2) When a system is sold we pull from our processor bin and retest system
and processor for additional 72 hours and then ship.

So when all is said and done its been tested by us, for 144 hours under NT
HCTs. I might also add we have our entire product line is at Microsoft
qualified for Windows NT 4.0 which is no easy feat. Microsoft tries to
break the system to the best of their ability so they in fact don't look bad.

Check the list you'll see we are on it!
