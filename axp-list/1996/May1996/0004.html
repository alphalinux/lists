I'm looking for enough documentation to port a couple of compilers to the
Alpha. When I ported them to the sparc, the documentation I used was called
the "Sparc architecture manual". I need to know the (user mode) instruction
set, bit patterns, any restrictions, recommended ways of using the
instruction set (eg I gather there is a standard macro for division by
numbers smaller than 1000) and anything else anyone might think relevant (eg
any documents describing the differences in scheduling required for efficient
execution). If anyone could tell me the titles and part numbers for the
documentation I need, I would be very grateful. Knowing which portion of
Digital supplies/sells these documents would be useful as well. I've tried
various portions of Digital here in England, but without much luck so far. I
suspect that what I'm after is not of much interest to all bar a few people,
so I'll excuse Digital on this one :-)

The box due to arrive is a 21164 box (yummy yummy!), but I'd like to write a
compiler for the architecture, rather than a specific implementation.
Obviously some scheduling differences will exist, but I'm not aware of
anything other than this which would need attention.

Any other useful pointers would be much appreciated.

TIA

Borris

PS - the compilers are Algol68 and Fortran90 compilers, if anyone is
interested. They probably won't be available for free as they have
substantial numbers of man years in them already.
