> Does anyone know if any flavor of Linux/Alpha supports the
> Qlogic Fast!SCSI IQ PCI and PCI-D fast/wide cards? They use
> Qlogic's RISC chip rather than the NCR controller chips.

Well, there was a pointer to a beta driver for the ISP-1020 a week or so
ago, so, I grabbed a copy (dated from September of 1995) off sunsite, and
proceeded to port it to Linux-Alpha.

I had some initial success, ie I was able to install RedHat 2.1/AXP to a
wide Barracuda I hooked up, but it doesn't seem to be ready for prime time
just yet. I've encountered some kind of timing problem, such that more than
half the time, the LINUX kernel (or MILO) will not complete the scan of
devices on the ISP-1020 SCSI bus.

If you've got the time and want to help, I sure could use it, as I'm not
a SCSI heavy in any sense but the physical... :-)
