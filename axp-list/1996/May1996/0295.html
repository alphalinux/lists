I have been trying for the past few hours to get sound working
on a UDB (Multia) machine. I am using pre2.0.4 since pre2.0.5 seems to
break registering rpc. I am trying to use the Microsoft Sound system
driver with the settings: ioport=0x530, IRQ=9, DMA=3 (all other settings
I have tried seem to fail in worse ways than these). It seems that
I get garbled sound out of /dev/audio and /dev/dsp. /dev/mixer,
/dev/sequencer, and /dev/midi* give me a no such device message. The
devices do have the right major/minor numbers.

The Linux/Alpha FAQ mentioned that people have gotten this to
work but gave no details at all. I have not been able to find anything
else about this.

Has anyone been sucessful with this?
