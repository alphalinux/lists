FYI:I have shipped over 100, 233 running at 266MHz without one problem. One
final note on overclocking.

Digital does not stamp a Alpha processor with a speed UNTIL they are sold.
What this means is that when they get an order say for 1000, 300Mhz 21164-P2
chips they just test them via bin lots, than they get stamped. Now the same
bin may have 333mHz chips and higher speed value chips no one knows until
they get tested and sold.

Therefore, you in most cases have a chip capable of running a good 10% above
the intended clock speed listed on the top of the chip.
