> But stops when it comes to the text
> Swapping to PALcode at 0x0 ...
> What am I doing wrong ?

Obtain the latest MILO from ftp.digital.com:/pub/DEC/Linux-Alpha/Miniloader.
You didn't specify what platform you're running on, but chances are you've
got a MILO that doesn't work with your video adapter (e.g. you have a TGA
and milo.bios.arc will only initialize certain VGA's). The latest MILO is
much more intelligent about detecting and using the video hardware. If
you've got a video hardware/software mismatch then you'll see nothing after
the "swapping to PALcode" message because after that point it's trying to
use MILO's video drivers rather than the ARC video drivers...
