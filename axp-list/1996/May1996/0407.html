Howdy!

Just finished building a couple of srpms, and the matching .axp.rpm's for
alphas. x86 dudes should be able to build the .i386.rpm's straight out of
the box from the matching srpms. All theses programs works excellent for me,
but YMMV though... :-)

ircii-2.8.2-6
Fixed the annoying termio(s) problem on linux/alpha. Does not affect
linux/x86. Also fixed the docs dir (was missing in the old offical rpm)

ircii-help-2.8.2-6
matching helpfiles for the above. Added a link for the manpage.

strace-3.0.14-1
Newest and greatest strace. Builds,and works(!) straight out of the box
even for alphas!

emacs-19.31-1
Newest and greatest emacs. Includes Erlend Midttun's patch for unknown-lignux
-> gnu-linux, and also the old emacs-19.30-configure.patch for getting some
stuff in the right place where FSSTND wants it. Removed the other patches
from the .30-rpm because they are not needed any more. Cleaned up the
specfile a bit (2 of the .el files in .30 is not in the .31 dist any more).

emacs-nox-19.31-1
wo support for the Athena X11 widgetset.

emacs-el-19.31-1
The source for the elisp files.

You can find the above files in both redhats incoming (actually UL at the
moment but should be finished in a while), and also on my machine,
nova.campus.luth.se/pub/linux/SRPMS & /pub/linux/axp/RPMS.

Enjoy! All comments are welcome.

/Mikael
