> 1) Does anyone use Linux as platform for commercial products?

Yes - ton's of folks. Alan Cox maintains a very complete list of commercial
products available for Linux, though you'd have to poke around a bit to
find it.

> 2) Is it legal to use Linux as platform for commercial products, or
> will I run into problems regarding copyright and license policies for
> Linux (f.x. GPL)?

No, but read the licenses for the libraries before you start. I wrote an
article for the Nov/Dec X Journal which covers the Linux copyright issues
for commercial developers if you can find it...
