"Almoast eXactly Prism", though DEC officially denies that,
however that's what the architects did talk about...

stuff integrated in it, but it was cancelled about 2 months before
first silicon was due to be produced, and they went to MIPS R2000
bandwagon to make DECstation machines -- not bad ones those, just

(When compared against Trinitrons..) (And the mouse was ... well,
"odd"...)
