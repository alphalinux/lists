For all of you anxiously awaiting ELF and shared libraries, good
news -- a milestone was reached this evening:

An ELF GCC sucessfully bootstrapped both itself, the
accompanying binutils, and gdb as statically linked
executables.

While shared libraries, not to mention a public release, are still
a little ways off, this is an amazingly encouraging development.
