OK, I just uploaded the latest set of glibc patches. The files are:

ftp://ftp.azstarnet.com/pub/linux/axp/glibc:
-rw-r--r-- 1 davidm other 10442 May 24 10:39 axp-extras-960524.tar.gz
-rw-r--r-- 1 davidm other 25770 May 24 10:48 libc-960524-alpha.diff.gz

There are no new kernel-header patches, so I didn't update that file.
The patches clean up a lot of things and you'll notice have gotten a
lot smaller since many of the changes are now back in the regular
glibc source tree.

Of ELF interest is that the assembly files now use the END() macro
instead of .end. If I missed any files, let me know.

Enjoy,
--david
