It's pretty important to have a MILO and linload.exe that are in sync, as
linload.exe gets the memory size from the ARC console and passes it on to
MILO.

Try getting the latest (and greatest... :-) linload.exe and MILO from off
gatekeeper.dec.com:pub/DEC/Linux-Alpha/Miniloader.
