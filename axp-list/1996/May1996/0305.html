> I have tried to use xmixer and aumix (the ones that come with
>RedHat-3.0.3) and they give me messages like "can't read the volume
>setting" and they do do something but I can't tell if it is correct. The
>overall volume knob doesn't work.

Well, as I understand it, some of the sound controllers don't support all
the sound "devices"; ie, you can modify the DSP output level for MSS, but
it doesn't have volume or bass/treble or the like. Whereas an SB16, will
have almost all the "devices" that are supported by the sound subsystem.
It's totally dependent on the hardware of the sound controller.

One of the things I've noticed about the pseudo-MSS in the UDB, is that the
setting of some of the ancillary controls *does* seem to affect the overall
sound, so try this: using "aumix", try reducing the "line3" setting (I think
it's only line3 that has this affect). I find that above a certain level on
the "line3" device greatly increases the overall distortion. But reducing it
too low will limit the overall volume, so its an adjustment problem.

See if that helps and let me know...
