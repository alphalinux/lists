> Is there somewhere a _simple_ firmware-upgrade-HOWTO floating
around?

I haven't found any, but you just need an image with the files:

`arcdos.exe jensfw.bin jnupdate.exe restenv.exe saveenv.exe'

Newer Versions also contain a separate 'language support
disk'. You may safely ignore any other files in the dec2000
directories. Installing it is a no-brainer: Switch to the ARC-Console,
select `install new firmware', and follow the instructions. You may
use 'restenv.exe/saveenv.exe' to save and restore your environment
variables, but I never thought this to be worthwhile.
