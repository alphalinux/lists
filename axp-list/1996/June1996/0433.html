Hmmm... Looking in the Multia (UDB) book I find two sets of jumper settings
for 1024x768. One lists a vertical refresh rate of 70.07Hz. and the other

I don't know how wide the "capture window" of your monitor is but I
would expect that to would at least grab the higher rate.

Give it a go (IMHO) and if you need to get a new monitor would that be so
bad? :-)
