> I just got off the phone from Number 9. Their sales rep says that

I have been using the #9 FX Motion 771 for some time with the BLADE 0.3
distribution and XFree86 3.1.2, and haven't had any problems so far.
You DO need that or later version of XFree, however. I used the
XF86Config settings recommended by XFree86 (cf.
www.XFree86.Org/3.1.2/S3-1.html). I'm running an Aspen Alpine 21064
(i.e. noname).
