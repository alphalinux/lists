>> They say that they couldn't get their Alpha, running Linux, to work
>> with the Number 9 9FX Motion 771, which uses the S3 Vision968 chip.

>weird BIOS) or only to run X11 using XFree86 ?

Sorry, I guess I wasn't specific. They said that the 771 was producing
garbled text in text mode and was unstable and unusable on the Alpha.
I'm not sure whether the problems occured with Linux, Windows-NT, or
even the ARC, MILO or SRM consoles. Since it was deemed completely
unusable, I assume that it was all of the above, e.g., some hardware or
BIOS interaction with the EB164 board.

I just got off the phone from Number 9. Their sales rep says that
the 771 is NOT compatible with the Alpha. No explanation why.
