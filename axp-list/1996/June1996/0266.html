I thought I would inform you how the Aspen Systems workstations correlate
with the standard kernels. There are some misprints in the documentatino.

Aspen Systems DEC closest match
------------- -----------------
Alpine EB64+
Telluride PC64
Timberline EB164
Summit EB164

Since the DEC systems are not exact matches to our hardware, some slots have
problems for bus mastering on the PC64 and EB164 kernels.
