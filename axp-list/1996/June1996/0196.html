There is now procps-1.01-3 for Red Hat (both Intel and Alpha platforms)
available as updates to the 3.0.3 distributions.

I think I've gotten all of the various patches that have been floating around.
I've removed the older releases of procps-1.01 from Incoming and placed this
one in there to try and eliminate duplication.

Everyone who has created procps patches may want to look at this version
and make sure it works for them. Please let me know if you find any problems.
