On Fri, 14 Jun 1996 bobahut@wichita.fn.net wrote:

> I have an EB64+ Aspen Timberline 275Mz that I have loaded with the Redhat
> 2.1/AXP Linux. I have an NE2000 set @ irq 10 and address 0x300h. I am
> using the Milo loader that came on the CD-ROM. My problem is that it
> doesn't recognize the ethernet card, and I don't see anywhere where I can
> force it too. Any suggestions? Do I need another card?

You probably just need to rebuild your kernel with ne2000 support enabled. The
2.1/Alpha Linux kernels weren't all built that way - only the noname kernel
supported the ne2000.

All of the kernels in Red Hat 3.0.3/Alpha support ne2000's though (I think).
