> 1. About what timeframe will Linux/Alpha convert to ELF?
> 2. After that time will ECOFF still be an option?
> 3. If so, how long will ECOFF be supported?

Hmmm... when you say "Linux/Alpha", do you mean Red Hat, or do you mean
Linux/Alpha in general?

My guess is that ECOFF will be usable in some form or other forever and
ever on Linux/Alpha. The support is in the kernel and toolset now, and
it's not going to go away. Elf support will be *added* to the toolset,
but the old ECOFF stuff should still work.

Of course, it's likely that *distributions* will have all-ELF utilities
and libraries because ELF is going to support shared libraries long before
ECOFF will. You'll still be able to use the tools on such a system to
generate ECOFF binaries of your own, though.
