On Tue, 25 Jun 1996, Steve Frampton wrote:

> Okay, that's fine, as I can FTP the distribution to the (Intel) Linux box.
> Once I boot into MILO I can just mount NFS and go? Ummm...is it a big
> deal to get NFS set up (on the server)?

It's a piece of cake <mostly>. This should do it:

1) ftp everything in RedHat/RPMS and RedHat/base from the
the distribution to /mnt/alpha/RedHat/RPMS and
/mnt/alpha/RedHat/base (for example)
2) add "/mnt/alpha *.my.domain.com" to /etc/exports on
your Linux box. Stop and restart NFS on the Linux box
(w/ Red Hat this means /etc/rc.d/init.d/nfs stop,
/etc/rc.d/init.d/nfs start)

Make boot/root/milo disks, and give it a shot.
