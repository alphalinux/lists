We have INN (1.4unoff4) running under Linux-Alpha. It's stable, and has
caused us no grief - once we got it built. If you'd like, I'd be happy to
send you a copy of our config.data file as well as a list of other
modifications that were made.

E-mail me if you'd like these.

One thing to make sure of is that you are running the most recent version
of syslogd. With our original version of syslogd (sorry, I don't remember
the version number) our system would hang when we ran inndstart.
