Actually, I hate to break it to you this way folks but the Easy Web
Server has been discontinued as well. The product manager for the
Multia product line where I work announced it this week and debited
our cost to match the UDB as we are overstocked.
The Multia product line has become the responsibility of the PCBU,
(Personal Computer Business Unit) at DEC and apparently they don't
want any competition with their Prioris Web Server product. I
suspect that they don't like the commodity price point either, much
cheaper than a Venturis PC or the Intel version of the Multia box.

The good news in all of this is that digital usually sells product
for at least 30 to 60 days after End of Life announcement and most
distributors and resellers will be having a fire sale on remaining
stock.
