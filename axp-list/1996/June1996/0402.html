Sigh, I hope you don't get too much mail about this.

The "To unsubscribe:" item is, unfortunately, designed for UNIX users
exclusively. Compuserve isn't UNIX. The message should probably say
"To unsubscribe: send to user axp-list-request@redhat.com an empty

send "unsubscribe" messages to the list, asking them to read, and
maybe you'll get some such email from others, but, in my opinion,
you did just fine. Sorry the sign-off wasn't general enough!
