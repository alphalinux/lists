>Isn't it also possible to set up the 4 partitions from
>Linux, before it installs? Does it have to be from NT?

I think one often gets the box with NT pre-installed,
and then adds LINUX. In this case, simply modifying the
partition table, preserving the ones with NT and boot-stuff
in them, one could re-allocate the remaining space for LINUX
and swap without having to re-install NT and such.

Seems a smart way to go, unless the partition table is really
not to ones liking...
