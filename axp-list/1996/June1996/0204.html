OK, I looked at it and based on your other input, fixed a few more
things. There is now an additional patch at:

ftp://ftp.azstarnet.com/pub/linux/axp/glibc/procps-1.01axp-2.patch.gz

It does the following:

- always use -lbfd -liberty as EXTRALIBS in Makefile (I assume
it's OK to assume that anybody compiling procps on Linux has
these installed)

- enable bfdupdate on Alpha and SPARC (ELF gets automatically
enabled if __ELF__ is defined)

- fixed typos in elfupdate.c

- fix xcpustate to work with 16/32bpp displays (thanks to
Harald Koenig for this fix)

- a fix for command-line handling (this comes mostly from
Red Hat's SRPM patch---I don't know who the author is)

Note that the above patch is in addition to procps-1.01axp.patch.gz in
the same directory.
