There is no backup plan unless you count an emergency boot block
floppy (the jumpers are under the disk and mean the same as tehy do
on noname). However, there are little differences between noname and
multia around serial port interrupt handling that get glossed over
by the WNT ARC firmware. I'd stick with ARC if I were you.
