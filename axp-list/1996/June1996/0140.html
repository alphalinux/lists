Welcome to the Linux/Alpha libc and ELF developer's mailing list!


This list is primarily intended as a discussion forum for people that
are actively working on library (libc, libm, etc) and ELF development
for Linux/Alpha.  Linux/Alpha is the port of Linux to platforms based
on Digital's Alpha architecture (yeah, the fast guys...).  The "Alpha"
is not meant to imply anything on the quality of the software.


Anybody is welcome to subscribe and look at what's going on, but the
intention is to keep noise to a minimum (you won't be tarred &
feathered for accidentally asking a "newbie" question, but there are
other lists that better serve this kind of needs.


Happy hacking!
