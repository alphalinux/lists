There are some patches being tested by a small group of g77
developers that have made a big dent in getting g77 to work on
Alphas. I've been too busy the past couple of weeks to get
further, plus, I didn't have any actual failing cases to
investigate until about a week ago. But, I now have two in
my in-box, which I hope to get to this weekend, if not earlier.

The timing on g77/Alpha is that, although I'm still wrapping
up work on a temp job, I'm using weekends and other spare time
to continue work on this. 0.6 won't be started until sometime
after I finish the temp work, which puts it no earlier than
July, and I doubt earlier than August. (We're probably going
to finish our basement in July, which will yield a more workable
office for me, plus we're taking a vacation in late July.)

Dunno how long 0.6 will take. Having an Alpha to do the work on,
if I'm able to keep this loaner for a while longer, will make a
big difference. (Thanks again to Craftworks, Carrera, and DCG
for making this machine available to me! ;-)
