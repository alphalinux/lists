I had a conversation today with technical support at SAG Electronics.
They say that they couldn't get their Alpha, running Linux, to work
with the Number 9 9FX Motion 771, which uses the S3 Vision968 chip.
This was very surprising, since Jay Estabrook mentioned a month or so
ago that the "S3-968 is well supported by the XFree86 3.1.2 server".

Has anyone had any experience with this card?

On a similar note, does anyone know what, if any, 3rd party cards are
supported by DEC UNIX? (I apologize in advance if this is an
inappropriate or ill mannered question for this list!)
