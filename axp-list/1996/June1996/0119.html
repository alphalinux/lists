> 1. About what timeframe will Linux/Alpha convert to ELF?
>
> 2. After that time will ECOFF still be an option?
>
> 3. If so, how long will ECOFF be supported?

ECOFF will be supported as an executable format in the kernel
for ever. It will remain in gcc and the binutils for ever.
And if it doesn't become a pain, glibc should continue to be
supported as well.

Of course, unless someone ever decides to duplicate DEC's
ECOFF shared libraries, you'll won't have those on Linux
without ELF.
