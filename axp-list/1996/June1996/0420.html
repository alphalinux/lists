I think this can happen on the receiving end of the mail or sending end.. I
have this happen to me on several different mail servers (Not just Linux,
but SunOS too).

Sometimes the sending sendmail doesn't think the entire message was received
and resends even though the local mail has already dispatched it.

More common for me is the local mail queue will (for some reason I haven't
discovered yet) fail to delete the message after dispatching it to the local
user. Since it is still in the queue, it will keep sending it till it is
delete (either by itself or by me).

I even once had the same message over 800 times in my mail box and it was
still growing. I had to delete it manually from the spool to stop it.
