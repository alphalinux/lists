I thought I saw a reply where somebody answered you stating
your disk/partition names need changing - meaning you probably
configured Linux without the Tru64 disks in the "shelf".

Anyway, try changing your linux root to /dev/sdc2 (from /dev/sda2).
The linux disk is the 3rd scsi disk encountered.
