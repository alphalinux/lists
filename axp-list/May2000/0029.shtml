Per the manual (and some experience) it indicates that only one device, a
CDROM is supported on the IDE channel. I actually tested this out. It seems
to hold true. I couldn't (this was a while ago), as I recall, access any
devices when I had more than one drive on the ide channel. There is support
to boot from it in the SRM though. Just type 'show dev' and it will give you
a list of the device names. I believe it is dka.
