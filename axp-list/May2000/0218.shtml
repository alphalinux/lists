> Greg, you mentioned this once. Can you add more info?

Measure the current at the main. It's the lazy way of getting the right
answer. Your vendor ought to be able to answer your question about how hot
it should be. The usual measurement is at the exhaust external to the case,
since it won't cool properly when the case is off. I don't have a UP2000 in
house so I can't tell you what it's supposed to be.
