Robert Williams [SMTP:bob@bob.usuhs.mil] wrote:

> My UP1000 runs at about 43 degrees C.
> When the room is warm, it goes up to 45.

Measured where? If that's the environmental temperature,
you are over spec and need get the room cooler, but I'd
be surprised if that were the case, since that's a VERY
warm room

If you are measuring someplace else, that's a different story.
An ambient temperature of 35C is obviously going to translate
into higher temperatures than that at the exhaust, at the heat
sink, and at the junction (each higher than the last).
