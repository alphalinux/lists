> I am trying to install linux using a serial desplay , but somehow it
> says no serial driver found ? does anybody know how to solve this ?

Are you booting from SRM or ABIOS?
Under SRM, you should be using the boot flags 'console=ttySx,9600'.
Think you can do the same from Milo under ARC/ABIOS.
It is possible that the kernel you are booting from has the serial
driver yanked - particularly if its redhat.
