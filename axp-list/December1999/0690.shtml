On all of our Alpha's I've had trouble with autonegotiation with the ethernet.
Considering forcing the DS10 to 10Mbit. We had a switch that was supposed to
autonegotiate, and our XP1000 and the DS10 were supposed to, they didn't. We had
really slow, below 10Mbit transfer times. When we forced them all to the same
thing(we're using 100Mbit, it worked great.

Just a thought.
