On Fri, 10 Dec 1999, Jim Barriault QMG wrote:

> The manual at
> http://ftp.digital.com/pub/Digital/info/semiconductor/literature/pc164trm.pdf
> contains the necessary settings.
>
> Or you can just pull all the jumpers off since I think that it the
> correct config for 500Mhz.
>
> > Regarding the crystal, do you know what I might look for to see whether I
> > need to get a replacement?
>
> Each of the boards I received came with a tube of crystals. Look for on
> that is marked 50.000Mhz, they are keyed so make sure the new one is in
> correctly. Also look at page 150 of the above manual.

Perfect--that's exactly what I need! That document's even got two
