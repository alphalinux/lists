I recently got PCMCIA going on RH6.0 on multia w/out a hitch. Download the PCMCIA
source (search for it at google.com). Read build and installation instructions
and give it a whirl. I remember the compilation hanging at the end on
something that turned out not to be necessary (some .dat file or something).

>From what I read it's best to download the PCMCIA source and build yourself
rather than rely on Redhat's prepackaged PCMCIA stuff (but I don't know if
that's still true from RH6.1).
