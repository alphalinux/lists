If you have RH 5.2 on your box and you are (after seeing
http://www.redhat.com/support/errata/rh52-errata-general.html#syslogd )
considering upgrading syslogd/klogd to sysklogd-1.3.31-1.5: please
consider bug http://bugzilla.redhat.com/bugzilla/show_bug.cgi?id=7214
titled "logs stop after upgrade to sysklogd-1.3.31-1.5" .
As far as I can tell this bug works exactly as claimed ;-)

The previous errata sysklogd-1.3.31-0.5 does not suffer of this
problem, and can still be found on ill-maintained mirrors; but
hopefully a sysklogd-1.3.31-2.5 is not far away.

I will spare the obvious comments.
