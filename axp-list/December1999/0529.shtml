Tiels, Frank wrote:
>
> Does anyone know if there exists a ssh for redhat alpha?
> I don't seem to be able to find it anywhere?

ftp://ftp.zedz.net/

and you will find there at least SRPMS for 1.2.27. Just do
'rpm --rebuild ....' and you are set.

Pay attention to recent CERT advisories about buffer overflows
in RSAREF2 library (but I guess that you are outside of US and you
would not using that anyway). Version 1.2.28 of ssh is on its way.

Red Hat cannot include ready ssh binaries in their distributions for
obvious legal reasons.
