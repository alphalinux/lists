Speed (MHz) is not everything, Having a stable system that is fast is much
more important to me. Originally the speed of the Alphas attracted me, but
now it is much more the stability factor. This goes as much for servers
that I install as my own workstations.

If you want to be a sheep, go ahead, add to the un-informed masses.
Personally, I have no desire to have "inhell inside" stuck to anything I
own. I will go out of my way to avoid the brain dead architecture that
still has backward compatibility to the 4004.
