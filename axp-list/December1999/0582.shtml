        I already mentioned this to Rich. (he's in the lab right
        now) This is for everyone else.

        Rawrite is just a dos app. That'll get handled by the
        built-in 286 emulator in Alpha NT. FX!32 is for Win32
        apps primarily. Although it has some 386 emulation in
        it, it doesn't allow you to run dos 386 apps. (of which
        there aren't a hell of alot. I can think of Phar Lap off
        the top of my head??)

        So, rawrite away.
