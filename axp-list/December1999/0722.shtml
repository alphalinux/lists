Test Subject:

UP2000 with 512MB
667 4MB single processor
4GB Digital Drive
Elsa Gloria 8MB Video Card
Intraserver 6100
Kingston 10/100
Toshiba 40X IDE
Sony 1.44MB Floppy

Installation: All went perfect. Password worked fine for root.
Graphical Installation went fine.

Problems: Couldn't get / to fall on sda1. Installation wanted it on
sda2.

Notes of Interest: Damn thing even left a few cylinders at the front to
install aboot for SRM!!! This was greatly unexpected.

Hopes this helps some of you in your decision to go to 6.1 for the AXP.
