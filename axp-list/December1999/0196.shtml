    SPECfp), but when translated to realworld applications(atleast ours) the
    difference isn앖 that significant(since we can앖 get a hold of Intels internal

    applications) since Athlon is cheaper and faster :-)

    benefit from the new Mobos/Proc압).

6: Limited memory bandwith on Intel hardware(atleast compared to Alphas)

    the machines(had to go back to the FCC for testing, affected the XP1000, DS-20,
