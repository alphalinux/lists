Most of the machines I heard about are inverted from mine. I think it is
shimply a different electrical set up in the DIP switches. I didn't send
the updated version to the list, I think it is linked from alphalinux.org
somewhere under hardware / PWS / miata, but the 'update' simply has a note
that some machines have inverted switches.

If you've tried the inverted for the 500 and it doesn't work, maybe you have
a CPU that isn't very overclockable.

I still run my 500 as 500, I decided I wanted more reliability / longer
service life or wait until I find a cheap 600+ CPU.
