(2.2.14pre report)

> Alpha is buggy for reporting exceptions wrt. user space accesses
> in the kernel logs, in fact it's a possible DoS attack on Alpha/Linux
> machines.

Dave is correct. Under digi^H^H^H tru64, you can toggle reporting
of exceptions thrown by userland processes. I disable exception reporting
with my "production" 2.2 mods; unless you are debugging quirky
code, their presence is more annoying than helpful.
Linux is not alone, either. Its been my experience with tru64 that the
level of 'noise' warrants the option to turn off the reporting entirely.
