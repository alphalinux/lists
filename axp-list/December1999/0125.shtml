The alphaserver 2000 4/XXX belongs to the sable family, and is supported,
unless yours (like my 2100) was updated to 2000 A spec, via new
motherboard (codename Lynx) which is not supported by any free OS.

If the motheroboard has 7 EISA slots and 2 pci slots (8 and 3 respectively
for 2100), it is a sable (4/XXX) or sbale-gamma (5/XXX) . If it has
7 PCI slots and 2 EISA slots (again 8 and 3 for 2100) it is a Lynx and
you are out of luck.

If installing RH 6.0 on sable, use the generic image , if using another
distribution, try the sable image
