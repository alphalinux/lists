I received the following message from Compaq yesterday. They have made the SRM firmware for the PC64 available without requiring the purchase of the Firmware Developer's kit.

In checking the referenced directory, it _appears_ to be a new build, although I haven't had a chance to test it..

There is also new firmware for the AphaPC164, the AlphaPC164LX and the AlphaPC164sx.

My _guess_ is that Compaq has just released SRM console firmware for systems that previously shipped with only the ARC/AlphaBIOS NT firmware.
