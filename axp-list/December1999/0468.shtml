I just thought I'd pass this along as I often see
people hunting for a good SCSI controller that will
work with Linux/Alpha.

check out: http://www.compgeeks.com

under the "Drives" section they have a Symbios 8751SP
UW SCSI controller. The chip is 53c875 and they are
new. ($48)

just FYI, this controller works on PCI based
UltraSPARCs too...
