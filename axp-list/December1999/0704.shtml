On i386, PCMCIA is included in the kernel-pcmcia_cs rpm. On Alpha, there
is no kernel-pcmcia_cs rpm probably because the Multia is listed on the
HCL as "Compatible/Unsupported". This means if you want PCMCIA on the
Multia, you must
"Use the Source, Luke... Let go your compiler... Trust Me..."
