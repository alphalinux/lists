Ever on the quest for a better browser for Alpha/Linux, I noticed that there
was a new version of Amaya (2.3) available yesterday. I tweaked the SRPM a few
characters and built it on my RH 5.x EV4 Alphastation. Source and binary RPMs
are available at ftp://orangesherbert.ces.clemson.edu/pub/amaya

The good news about this version is that the horizontal scrollbar appears to
work now.

I've also updated the Lesstif version and added a binary of the "links" web
browser (similar to lynx, but displays tables/colors) to the site.

Any word on Mozilla M12?
