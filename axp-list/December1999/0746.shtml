With regards to your message at 10:14 AM 12/23/99 -0500, Rich Payne. Where
you stated:
>On Thu, 23 Dec 1999, Jeong Hwan Park wrote:
> > Is there anyone to succeed to install Tru64 on UP2000 system?
> >
> > It has adaptec SCSI U2W controller but it is useless for Tru64.
> >
> > Which SCSI controller and network interface card are recommanded for Tru64
> > on UP2000?
>
>Several of the Intraserver cards are supported under Tru64 (6100L and
>6200L I know are good), just make sure you buy a Tru64 version of the
>card! As for ethernet, I know the DE500 series (tulip cards) work, but I
>think they are also being phased out, not sure what the replacement is.
>
>--rdp
Or, the Intraserver ITI4140UE-U has both Ultra Wide SCSI and 10/100T
ethernet compatible with Tru64.
The ITI-6110U2-V also is Ultra2(LVD) Wide SCSI and 10/100T, UNIX compatible.
Note the last digit it in the part number in each case (U or V) which
designates the (UNIX and VMS compatible versions).
