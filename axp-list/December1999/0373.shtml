Hmm. That is a strange-looking beastie indeed, D7.
For those who have not yet looked, it is listed as the
following (only the relevant parts shown):
- Digital Alpha Pw 500a
- "Manufactured May 99"
- 500Mhz 21164
- max 2GB memory in 6 slots
- 8 slots - 2 64-bit PCI, 2 32-bit PCI, 2 shared, 2 EISA
- Integrated Qlogic controller
- 1 Parallel, 2 USB, 2 serial connectors

I am almost inclined to say it is a Digital Ultimate Workstation
thats been mislabeled, or perhaps a 500a upgraded to an XP1000
(if thats possible?).
