Rich Payne wrote:
>
> In order to reflash to SRM you'll need the .rom image (I think), last I
> knew the images on the Compaq ftp site would only upgrade an existing SRM,
> however with the demise of NT and AlphaBIOS the Q may have become a little
> more generous about that.

If you jumper the board for fail safe booting (see your manual) and use
a floppy with the update util and srm image, you can load and flash any
image. It's been a while since I've done it but I believe the disk has
to be dos formated. Just remeber that after flashing you have to power
down and remove the jumper.
