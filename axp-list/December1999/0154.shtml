Hi,

I installed Raw Hide 1991031 on my XL300, and lately started building
SRPMS from the latest release of Rawhide. I get dependency errors that are
obviously wrong. To wit:

[root@katia alpha]# rpm -Uvh gtop-1.0.5-1.alpha.rpm --test
error: failed dependencies:
        libgdk-1.2.so.0 is needed by gtop-1.0.5-1
        libglib-1.2.so.0 is needed by gtop-1.0.5-1
        libgmodule-1.2.so.0 is needed by gtop-1.0.5-1
        libgtk-1.2.so.0 is needed by gtop-1.0.5-1
        libgtop.so.1 is needed by gtop-1.0.5-1
        libgtop_common.so.1 is needed by gtop-1.0.5-1
        libgtop_sysdeps.so.1 is needed by gtop-1.0.5-1

But, these files exist:
[root@katia alpha]# rpm -qf /usr/lib/libgdk-1.2.so.0
gtk+-1.2.6-1
[root@katia alpha]# rpm -ql gtk+ | grep /usr/lib/libgdk-1.2.so.0
/usr/lib/libgdk-1.2.so.0
/usr/lib/libgdk-1.2.so.0.5.1

Here's some system info:
rpm-3.0.4-0.1
Linux katia.killsoft.com 2.2.13 #3 Sat Nov 13 11:45:01 CST 1999 alpha unknown
Raw Hide 19991031

I rebuilt the rpm database -- no change. Has anyone else seen this?
