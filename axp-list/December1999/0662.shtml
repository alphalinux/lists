>>> "Michael Rich" said:
> How do I cut the graphical installer off? It detected my 3dlabs video card,
> but has some parameter very wrong because its wavey as heck and I can barely
> read anything.

Add the word "text" to the boot command arguments, ie:

MILO> boot sr0:kernels/generic.gz root=/dev/scd0 text

or

>>> boot dka400 -file kernels/generic.gz -flags "root=/dev/scd0 text"

Good luck.
