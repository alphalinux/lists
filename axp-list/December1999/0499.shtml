I know this isn't exactly Linux related, but I recently ordered (and
received) the $99 Tru64 Non-commercial kit. A couple of weeks later, I
received another box from Compaq with a bunch of OpenVMS stuff in it. It
seems to be the software product library for OpenVMS and part of a
"Software Loan Program" with short time licenses for OpenVMS and related
products. Did anyone else get this ? It doesn't seem like the OpenVMS
operating system is on one of the CDs, but there seems to be time limited
licenses for it. Kind of makes me curious about playing with OpenVMS and
reliving my grad student days when I used VAX.....
