On Tue, Dec 21, 1999 at 01:26:06AM +0800, Jen-Shiang Yu wrote:
>
> My machine is a PC164LX running SRM v5.4. While I tried to set
> some SRM parameters, such as "set auto_action boot" or "set ewa0_mode fast",
> the console just halted itself and I had to press the reset switch to
> boot it up again. Yet the parameter remained unchanged. In fact none of the
> parameters could be changed. The reaction was all the same: halted. Is there
> any way to get the setting work? Or had I done something wrong?
>

I solved it by flashing back to AlphaBIOS v5.6 with the original disk, then
flash again to SRM v5.6-2 by the lx164srm.rom from ftp.digital.com. Now
everything works fine, but this is really a stupid solution.
