> On Thu, 9 Dec 1999, Jim Barriault QMG wrote:

> > I purchased them 9/17/99, and recieved a replacement 10/5/99. So yes I

I suspect that is an answer to the question I've just been asking
myself... how hard and fast is the 30-day deadline? I've had mine about
38 days; didn't get a power supply until a week ago, grrrr.

For the record, it would appear mine was jumpered to a 10x CPU multiplier
(IRQ 2, which seems to be a default looking at the manual, and should be
11x but evidentally isn't). The clock crystal was a 36.6 MHz one, so I'm
currently running at 366MHz. There was a full pack of crystals, though,
so I was going to probably go up to 10x 50MHz = 500MHz when I got
everything working.

I'm going to be more careful the next time I buy something on eBay.
