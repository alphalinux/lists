This could be one of those 'truly interesting' (ya can't get there
from here) problems. I just looked in my DU (very old -- 4.0-no letter)
box. In /usr/shlib, there's two more libs that care about yp:
libaccmgr.so and libsecurity.so. You could add them, but I don't
think that would help. I seriously doubt that they would know to call
the linux ypbind to get info back from the yp server. In fact, the
libc.so and libc_r.so also seem to have some knowledge of yp, but the
same problem is likely to exist there.

DISCLAIMER:
There are many here sharper on this stuff than I. If I'm full of
crap (on this ;-), please speak up!
