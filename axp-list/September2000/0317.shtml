> I am new at alpha linux, but I had my paart of intel based linux, so I am
> making a good progress, but I have problems finding good applications. Most
> packages are intel based, and I have never compiled sources myself.
>
> Any hints on compiling sources, and where to find decent audio- and video
> editing soft on alpha linux? Do these applications take fully advantage from
> the 64-bit architecture?

Most good source packages are not too difficult to use. For
most of the good ones, you just download and untar the sources,
cd to the top-level package directory, then do the following
three simple steps:

    ./configure
    make
    make -install (done as root in many/most cases)

I am also looking for andio (and video) editing software.
