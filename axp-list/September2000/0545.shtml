Nevermind, I got it. I doubt anyone else on this list
is interested in the answer, but personally I hate it
when I'm searching the net for a problem and I find
references to it with no solution given--so I'm
responding to myself in order to leave a "paper
trail".

I used tkined to view the snmp data coming from the
switch. Snags I ran into include:

1) Hooking a terminal to the switch to configure it
(use the port on the left to get the menu, the one on
the right is the OBM)

2) The docs, MIBs and firmware (I didn't upgrade the
firmware from 4.1.1) are now stored at www.dnpg.com,
not www.networks.digital.com

3) In order to manage the repeaters, you have to
configure the DECrepeaters (900TM) themselves to have
IP addresses (don't just configure the slots on the
switch, you have to actually do a "redirect" from the
switch menu to get to the repeater menu and config the
IP address.

4) Use the rfc1516 which is in tkined's menu as
"snmpDot3RptrMgt" to see the by-port error info.

All I used tkined for was to view the information in
there. I didn't modify anything (as of this writing,
anyway) such as segments.
