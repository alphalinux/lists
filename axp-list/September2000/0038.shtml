        Funny thing, i was just thinking the other day how nice it would be to
play 3d games on the Alpha box i am developing on now, especially since it is
about 3 times as fast as my Pentium Pro 180 (according to bogomips), and also
has a very nice Permedia 2 card in it (unlike my PPro which has an older ATI
Rage Pro with *no* 3d support whatsoever under Linux). The only thing i'm
missing is a sound card, and those are cheap . . .
        Also, i've always thought that my other Alpha, a Multia, resembled a
console game system: it's small, has builtin sound and video, and isn't
extraordinarily powerful, but makes a decent dedicated mp3 player. If you
could pare down some games or port older ones they would probably run very
nicely on Multias.
