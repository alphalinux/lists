Thomas

There are two ways that you might do this. Either swap the power supply
for a hard power power supply or if there is a green and black wire
attached to the power supply, connect it with the black wire connector
(sounds dumb, but you'll see what I mean when you look at the power
supply). If you use the black and green wire combination, it will
render your power button useless and you will have to use a power
strip. Hope this helps.
