On Mon, 11 Sep 2000, jmd wrote:

> sorry to post this to the general mailing list as it's hardware specific but
> i'm not subscribed to axp hardware. with my PWS 500a on ebay someone asked
> if this was an EV5. i dug around the alphalinux site some and found a
> reference or two but i want to be sure. on the cpu there is: 21164-P7-500
> (there is no *A*) so i am assuming this cpu is EV5 and not EV56. am i
> correct?
>
        I think no ... i have both an EV5 (Alphastation 500/333) and
an EV56 (AS 2100A 5/375) and both CPU's read 21164 ... i think from
memory that all PWS are 21164A's ... BTW why sell it ? it's quite a nice
machine (that is, unless you're upgrading to an XP1000) ... i would bid
for it but i'm rather far away to collect it (payment would be a hassle
to ...) ... Much more important to your buyer should be if your machine
has the 2Mbyte cache option. By my own measurement, the "A" on 21164's
is not worth more than 2% in speed ... hardly an issue , eh ?
