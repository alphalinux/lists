Depends on your definition of "fine".
If you are running one device on the card, and it is a 7200rpm or slower
device, this will keep up.
However it will NOT as anything other than a dumb, 8 bit SCSI, no SCSI
scripting, and most importantly only SCSI FAST, not UltraSCSI.
20MB/sec max on wide devices, rather than the 40/sec the chip is capable of.
