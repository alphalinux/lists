Is there anyone who has knowledge of how FPU underflows of denormal numbers
are to be processed in Alpha/linux. The concept of a ieee rolling underflow
seems to stop at the smallest FPU normalized number. Tests expect that the
smallest should go to e-324. on True64 the test will go till e-324 if u use
the ieee switch. On alpha/linux I cannot get it to go beyond e-308, no
matter what FloatingPointControlRegister bits i set.
