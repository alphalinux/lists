>I have 10 PWS 500a, 2 of which have what appear to be dead ethernet
>controllers. These devices present themselves to the AlphaBIOS as Digital
>21143-PB/C & DS21143 Tulip (rev 48). The card is about is 3.5 inches
>square and is connected to the system board by a cable. I swapped these
>little boards out to isolate the ones that do not work, and oddly enough,
>it appears as though the MAC address for the system is contained on the
>system board as that address does not change when swapping the card. I
>have never had any experience with these little guys. Can anyone tell me
>where I might find a couple of replacements for these and what they are
>called and maybe about how much they cost? Any help would be appreciated.

These cards are typically refered to as MAUs (Media Access Units). The 10/100
Mbit TP MAU seems only be available through DECpaq service, as only a 10-BaseT/2
is listed as option in the soc at
http://www.compaq.com/products/quickspecs/SOC/QBQ23APF.PDF.
