#1, No, SRM will not see your Adaptec card. If SRM usage is high on your
list of priorities, look for a Symbios based card. Intraserver makes one
that, although expensive, will work with TRU64. Other companies also make
cards based on the 53C810 ( narrow ), 53C875 ( wide SCSI ), or 53C895 (
wide Ultra 2 SCSI ).

#2, I am pleased with my Diamond FireGL1000 Pro 8 MB video board. That card
is based on the Permedia2 chip, which is showing it's age a little, but
still a decent performer. The ELSA GLoria, the DEC Powerstorm 4D10T, and
others are also based on that chip.
