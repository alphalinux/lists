Why would you use a 2.5" drive? When I bought my multia It came with an
internal 3.5" drive bay. there were evidently two different SCSI
controllers in the Multia. both went into the PCI slot on the mobo, one
provided a PCI slot at a right angle and an external SCSI connector, and
the other provided only a single 50 pin SCSI cable, this one came with the
bay that fit into the space where a PCI card would sit on the *other* card
and would accomodate a half-height 3.5" drive. I've got the internal SCSI
adapter and bay, if you want it, pay the shipping, then you can put a
50-pin half-height SCSI drive in there.(I've actually got the whole
multia, however the Power supply appears to be broken, but if you want the
system(No RAM or hard drive) let me know.
on a side note, does anyone know if there are drivers for the onboard
sound card?
