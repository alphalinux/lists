  Yes they used it primarily as a render box for their compositing software &
as workstations for the Windows NT LightWave group ...Back then Alphas were teh
fastest thing around!! Heck thats why i bought one back then :)... Currently,
all Alphas have been retired to the render farms either as linux boxes for the
compositing farm or on the windows NT Alpha renderfarm primarily for LightWave

I suspect now that houdini and Maya, renderman and hint hint..perhasp LightWave
are all comming out for linux intel and or Mac OS X that many studios just
might switch over to linux since it is so darned open and makes lots of the
scripting that is used in the infra structure of digital studios much
easier....
