There are certain cards which are detected by SRM and others which
are not detected by SRM. Yet, most of those are detected by
AlphaBIOS.

My guess is that BIOS emulator X2.01 in SRM v5.6-3 is the source
of all this trouble. What is the BIOS emulator version in SRM v5.8?

If it is still X2.01, then there is no hope.

Dear Compaq, how about making the BIOS emulator source public?
