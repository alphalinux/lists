Of course you ignore the quite clear fact that ONE: I can get software for the
Athlon. and TWO, that for the price of an Alpha I can buy several very complete
Ahlon systems.

As a 3D animator cost effectiveness is an important factor. It is far cheaper
and cost effective to purchase several Athlon boxes for a render farm than to
buy a single Alpha. You will also find that for the cost, I can build a much
faster setup than the Alpha for far less money.

Regardless of the superior design of the Alpha, the cost and software trade offs
just don't add up for all markets.
