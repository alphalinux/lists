Joerg Bruehe wrote:
>
>
> A whole series of machines delivered to "my" company all showed the
> same symptom: The moment you added a "narrow" SCSI device to the
> external port (which is "wide", so you used a "wide-to-narrow"
> adapter cable), the whole adapter stopped working - it would
> not even list the devices connected, let alone use them and boot.
>
> >
> > anyone have any ideas on what to do next? change cables? update scsi bios?
> > viking funeral?
>
> Make sure you leave the wide external port and do not touch it.
>

When you add a narrow device to the wide connector, make sure
there are no wides after it and use a 68-50 pin adapter that
terminates the high 8 bits of the wide bus. Most cheap cables
don't terminate them so the bus stops working. Adaptec make
an adapter specifically to do this. Other cards have the same
problem.

>
> My personal conclusion is not to use that brand.
>

No. Terminate your bus correctly.
