> Hey there ladies and gentlemen,

> I have a small problem and I hope you can help me out. I have the RH
> 2.2.16-3 Kernel that I am trying to compile. I need GDT support in the
> kernel (Yeah, 3 73GB drives in a raid 5 and 1 as a boot drive!) The
> problem I am having is that, of course, RH doesn't supply their .config

Yes, they do. Install the kernel-source RPM, then go into /usr/src/linux,
in there is a directory called configs (I think), in there are the three
.config files RH used to build that kernel (UP,SMP and BOOT). Just copy
the file you want to /usr/src/linux/.config Run a make oldconfig and then
add/change what you need.

--rdp
