Ivy Wang wrote:
> what does "cat uses obsolete /proc/pci interface" mean? Thanks.

Just what it says. However, cat doesn't actually use /proc/pci, somebody
probably typed "cat /proc/pci" and the kernel doesn't know better.

Presumably, /proc/pci will go away sometime in the future and that warning is
meant to help identify processes that depend on it, so they can be fixed now.
Feel free to ignore it.
