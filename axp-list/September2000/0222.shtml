> On a side note, I'd recommend investigating blender (www.blender.nl) for
> lower end of film/video and 3D game production. It's fast, fully
> implemented in opengl, and its on alpha.

I had thought of Blender on the way home from work today. I've been trying
to get my son to use it. Blender not only runs on Alpha... it runs on Alpha
Linux!! It's maturing nicely according to a friend who uses it both on Alpha
Linux and his SGI Indigo2.

One other idea I think worth looking into is having Athlon boxes for
workstation and Alphas to do the rendering. Isn't this what Digital Domain
did for the Titanic movie (write up in Linux Journal a couple of years ago).
I realize this is not a solution for rendering individual frames on a given
box...but that is where is where SGI shines I think.
