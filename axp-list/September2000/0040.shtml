I'd love to see some of the big games ported and optimized for alpha.
Tribes, Quake 3, Deus Ex, Soldier of Fortune, etc. all come to mind. I
think that with the superior floating point on the alphas, it may help
reduce their price if gaming becomes popular for 'em. Of course, you'd have
to support Nvidia and 3dfx's video cards extensively, and probably get
alpha optimized drivers for 'em.

But yeah, I think an alpha doing gaming would be impressive as hell.
