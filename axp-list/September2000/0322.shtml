> It's planning time here, and I am trying to find a rough price
> estimate, and
> reliable supplier, for a machine where the main requirement is a
> large amount
> of RAM, namely 8GB.

This is unfortunately hard to do for cheap in an Alpha. Ideally you'd be
able to get a small machine which happens to have 8+ RAM slots, and buy
expensive 1 GB DIMMS for the slots, and you're done. Unfortunately for you
(and me, I wanted to build a 1 TByte cluster for $2 million), low-end Alphas
have a chipset combination which restricts them to 512 MB dimms. So you have
to use an ES40 or higher, which is pretty damn expensive. So, people who
want lots of RAM and not much CPU are kind of stuck.

If you can get away with only 4 GB ram, you could use the DP264, which is
nearly obsolete.
