As I have mentioned I just converted 2 pc164's over to redhat 6.2 and they
seem to work great. They only problem I have is that seti seems to be about
1 hour slower than it was on alpha/NT. I haven't recompiled redhat leaving
out anything as I am new to Linux. On NT work units were running 7.5-8.5
hours on Linux they are 8.5-9.5 hours. I have tried both the true64 and
linux-axp versions of seti, the true64 is faster but still not as fast as
when they were running NT.
