If I remember correctly, Dr. Blinn is correct in his summation of the
situation for post 5.1 Tru64. Support is/was being removed for select
systems such as all of the DEC3000 series with TurboChannel, the DEC4000
series aka cobras, the DEC2000 series aka jensens, and lastly the
DEC7000/10000 series aka ruby's. The removal of support is not
necessarily intended to phase out cpu's but rather phase out what have
become "oddball" system buses such as TurboChannel and whatever it was
the 4000's had. However I do not know about the plans of companies such
as Oracle, but such a move would make sense.
