Matthew Jacob wrote:

> Do you know if TurboLaser (8X00) still be supported?
>

Yes, TurboLaser is still supported, just not the XMI bus on it. That means that
any devices you have hanging off of the XMI will not show up (you'll just have to
replace them with PCI counterparts).

If this is a problem for anyone, the issue should definitely be raised ASAP
through the appropriate Compaq/partner rep. I don't know if anything will change,
but it will give you the most time to plan for any necessary work. Oh, and don't
forget - this is only with the _next_ version of the OS, not V5.1 which we just
shipped. You won't see this anywhere until sometime next year. (Plenty of time
for capital upgrade requests! ;)

 - Pete
