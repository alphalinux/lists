Hi Faustino!
Yes, there are a lot of Jensen users here.
Linux working on Jensen successfully. RedHat 6.2, 7.0 are fine.
Please refer http://www.alphalinux.org/docs/jensen.shtml
you find all information about it.
One thing: If you have WinNT on your Jensen - do not use ECU disk provided
by alphalinux or others - you just destroy your NT resurce.
Use only disk for NT usage provided.
