> And we had an Enterprise 3000 and it wasn't better. The only thing
> is, there seem to be no Alpha <= $25000 with more than
> 2 GB RAM and at least 750 MHz AFAIK.

Right. The problem there is memory. API's UP2000 lets you
mix-and-match the CPU speed, but is limited to 2 GB. The DS20 doesn't
go to 833 MHz yet (I forget the memory limit; it has 8 DIMM slots),
and the ES40 has both lots of memory slots and the fastest cpus, but
costs mucho $$.

If you're willing to live with 2 GB of memory, you're all set. With
luck, the next generation of lower-end Alphas from both Compaq and API
will support > 2GB.
