We have here a Ultra60 330 MHz and a UP2000 2x750/8MB and
the SUN is *dead* slow. As a rough estimate an Intel P3/800 is twice
as fast as the SUN and the Alpha is 2-3 times faster then the P3 per
processor.

So its about 1 x 750 MHz 21267 >= 4 * 300 MHz UltraSparc 2

And we had an Enterprise 3000 and it wasn't better. The only thing
is, there seem to be no Alpha <= $25000 with more than
2 GB RAM and at least 750 MHz AFAIK.
