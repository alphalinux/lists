Hello,

I have a Digital Personal Workstation au433 recorded as Miata configuration
for Linux/axp under Redhat 7.0 (kernel 2.4.2).

I have a very annoying trouble with XFree86 3.3.6 and 4.0.x.
My video card is an old Matrox|MGA 1064 SG [Mystique] (svga under 3.3.6 and
mga driver under 4.0.x) but I had the same problem with an S3 Virge DX.

My problem is that my Xsession, after a while varying between 1hour and
several days, becomes frozen. In this case, my keyboard is not responding but
sometimes my mouse keep to work. Then, the only solution that I have is to
kill X through my network with another computer.
When I watch the log (message or X log), I don't find anything at the
date/hour/minute of my crash. Notice that this crash occurs every time with X
activity under KDE: e.g. the closing of a window, the unfolding of a menu, ...

Does somebody has the same problem ? Which solution can I adopt ?
Tell me if you want some precisions of my case.
