On Wed, 2 May 2001, Michal Jaegermann wrote:

> Saying that - if cxx really follows g++ conventions, as it is supposed
> to, then maybe you can find something in a documentation for GCC
> compilers set? You may also have a 'demangle' utility.

I don't think cxx follows g++ conventions (hence the compaq demangler
support that isn't in upstream binutils -- only exists in HJ Lu's
releases and Compaq's). If you're using a binutils from HJ Lu, you can
always run c++filt on those symbols to find out what they were mangled
from:
        c++filt -s compaq __7setComparer__7UVectorFPFPvT1_c
