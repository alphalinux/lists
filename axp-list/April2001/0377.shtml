With regards to your message at 10:12 PM 4/15/01, Jean-Claude Magras. Where
you stated:
>I am not sure ISDN is that great, MY laptops's 57.6 modem+W98seems a
>good deal faster that my BITSURFER PRO and Morningstar PPP on
>a sparcstation 5/85 Mhz .
A single B channel does 64K versus about 40K for an average "56K modem"
connection, BUT:
The ISDN connection may support compression which will give it a lot better
throughput in some cases. Additionally most ISDN connections give you two
of the B channels, which your modem should be able to bond together for
better throughput yet.
Bottom line is to check with your service provider to see if they support
compression and channel bonding. If not, it is probably not worth the extra
costs.
