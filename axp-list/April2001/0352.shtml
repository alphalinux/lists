> Now should it be possible for me to use a x86 BIOSed PCI video card ?

With 5.7 you should be able to use PCI cards with chipsets using ATI
Rage128 (xpert 97/98),
Rage Pro, Permedia 2, Any Matrox card (G200 is a good one), Permedia/2,
and probably the voodoo 2000 and 3000. Cards to void are the TNT2 and
Radeon.
