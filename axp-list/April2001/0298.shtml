If AlphaBIOS/MILO is working for you, use it. Just because RedHat no longer
"supports" it doesn't mean that it no longer works. However if you are
wanting to switch to SRM/aboot, you will have to get rid of the Adaptec. I
am using TekRam 390U2W SCSI cards in a couple of LX boards here with
excellent results. The TekRAM 390 U2W is Symbios 53C895 based and has an
isolation chip so you can attach both LVD and Single Ended SCSI devices with
out degrading the performance of the entire system to Single Ended.

HTH...
