On Thu, Apr 12, 2001 at 11:17:10AM -0400, Peter Petrakis wrote:
>
> To upgrade your firmware. First download.
>
> ftp://ftp.digital.com/pub/DEC/Alpha/firmware/v5.9/alpha1000a_ev5/fwupdate.exe
>
> and copy it to a fat formated floppy. pop is into the alpha and at the
> SRM prompt type.
>
> >>> fwupdate
>
> what a couple moments. Soon you'll be presented with a menu. type
> 'update'
> and press enter. Comfirm the version updated and wait. When it's done
> type 'exit' and the console will reset and load the new firmware.

PLEASE: read the release notes, as there are some platforms that require
the upgrade in two stages, ie 4.7 -> 5.3, then 5.3 -> 5.9 or whatever.

And 4.7 is *really* old... ;-}
