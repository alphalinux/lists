You can find precompiled alpha binaries for glibc-2.2 in "rawhide"
from Red Hat. I am making rpms for glibc-2.1 too but they are still
somewhat in an "experimental" stage. To recompile on your own is
not that big deal either but you need somewhere around 700 MB of a free
disk space (without hacking spec files to cut down on a number of
packages produced).

The above, of course, can use only that information about graphics
card which is publicly available.
