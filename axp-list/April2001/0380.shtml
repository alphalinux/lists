* Kenneth Dunn <luser@xtra.co.nz> [010416 14:52]:

> Has anyone any experiences with PCI->USB cards on Alphas ?
> I'm looking at buying one.

I've been using an OHCI card for quite a while, but nowadays, UHCI stuff should
work, too. I've been trying to get the onboard USB interfaces of the DS10/DS20
machines running, but due to wrong initialization of the USB devices on the
SRM side (??), it's not recognized under Linux at all or it shows you wrong
interrupt mapping. So you're probably better off with plugging a cheap usb pci
adaptor into your Alpha.
