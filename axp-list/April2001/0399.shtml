The QLA1020 is a Wide, not Ultra Wide controller with maximum sustainable
transfer rates of ASYNCH 10MB/SEC.

And note - this is MAXIMUM - in a Lab in perfect conditions with a
specifically designed cable on a short burst in perfect weather !

I would guess REAL values are going to be in the 2-5MB range
