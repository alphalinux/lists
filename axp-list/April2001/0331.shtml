> I've downloaded it, but "fwupdate" didn't do anything. Do I have
> to tell it to use the floppy drive ?

AHA! You may not have "fwupdate" on your system even though your SRM
is above 4.4.

from the old SRM manual, page 31.
http://ftp.digital.com/pub/Digital/info/semiconductor/literature/srmcons.pdf

>>>fwupdate
Note: Alpha SRM Console versions prior to Version 4.4 1 do not recognize
the fwupdate command.
For versions prior to Version 4.4 1,
enter the following commands at the Alpha SRM Console prompt:

>>>cat fat:fwupdate.exe/dva0 > pmem:900000
>>>stop -drivers
>>>jtopal 900000

Try executing the above commands in sequence and cross your fingers :-)
