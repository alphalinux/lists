> I'm curious what exactly makes EV6 expensive, besides that all
> configurations available have 2MB cache or more. If somebody were to
> offer a low-cost 1MB version, it would fill a need but also
> diminish much
> of the reason for buying an alpha in the first place:
> performance. (Don't
> flame me because I don't think OpenVMS is a reason!)

        The cache is external on an Alpha. I think the
        smallest cache size you can use with an EV6 is
        1.75MB due to the way it was/is designed. (memory
        to cache mapping? Interleaving? Can't remember)

        Cache must be run at a multiple of clock speed.
        Usually 1/3rd to 1/2 or 1/4, depending on the
        clock rate. External cache is expensive. DDR
        cache helps with performance because it gives
        you a theoretically higher clock rate.

        Putting all that cache down on a motherboard means
        lots of connections to pin out. That means
        signalling/electrical "stuff". That means more
        board layers, etc... It's nothing
        that can't be done and hasn't been done, but
        it's not as simple as plugging in a Pentium with
        onchip cache. It takes time to develop a complicated
        quality product.

        Also, volume is a key factor in price. The term
        "cheaper by the dozen" is a factor.

>
> Meanwhile, you can still get a UP1100 mainboard w/ CPU for
> $2k, but how
> many people on this thread have bought one? Talk is cheap.

        An excellent point. Anytime someone comes up with anything
        that's closER to affordable, the bitching about "it doesn't
        have the <foobar> performance" comes up. ie: for the UP1000,
        you only heard about the percieved lack of memory bandwidth
        over a XP1000 or UP2000. Nobody seemed to get the fact that
        the UP1000 and UP1100 cost about 1/3rd of an XP1000 and
        actually had/has fairly decent performance under certain
        apps. Nope, all you'd hear was "too expensive". Then that
        person would probably buy a Mac or a Sun. :)
