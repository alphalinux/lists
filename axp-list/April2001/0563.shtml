I don't believe that your question of "will it run microsoft apps". Well that
is a complex question. If your talking about office products, I've had wine
run office 97/98. I would suggest to go to sun.com and download staroffice
5.2. That is a very similar product to Microsofts Office Suite. It's made to
run on a NT/95/98/Solaris/Linux/etc platforms. Also its very easy to convert
between office and staroffice word/excel/etc documents.

I would suggest picking a "version" ie Red Hat, Free BSD, etc. and getting use
to the feel. You won't see anymore blue screen of deaths. To be honest with
ya, its a different operation system and thus the feel will different, but once
you get use to it, you won't go back to windows. (well maybe to play games).
Besides, its free and there isn't anything to loose. Good luck..
