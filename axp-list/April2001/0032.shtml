> With regards to your message at 10:20 PM 4/10/01, bill mccall. Where you
> stated:
> >its an lx164.
> OK, there is a jumper on this board to connect a momentary switch for the
> halt function.
>
> J22 is the halt button connector, and is located immediately to the front,
> right of the right-hand corner of the 64bit PCI slot closest to the CPU.
> It is immediately behind J21 which is the reset connector ( about .8"
> apart). There are no other connectors immediately adjacent to it.
> I can send you the LX Technical Reference manual , or just the jumper
> layout page if you wish. They are PDF files.
