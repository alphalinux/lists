I couldn't tell ya how much a 6 CPU alpha system costs. The E3500 fully
configured is well over US$ 150k though (w/ 8CPUs and 8GB RAM). That's of
course not cheating out the power supplies to a non-Sun vendor. (which you
couldn't do w/ Compaq because either they don't exist or would void the
plastic shrink-wrap warnings)

As far as the sun blade 1000 goes, if it's really that slow, is it worth
spending the money to have one other than just the geek factor of having
one? If it was just for geek factor, you could go on eBay and buy a multia,
LX/SX/UX/ etc. If you want to really get work done, well then you need to
spend some money.

any of the higher end systems are not going to be super cheap no matter
which chip / chipset you decide to go with.
