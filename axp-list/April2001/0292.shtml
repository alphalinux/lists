do your disks need to spin up?

At the SRM prompt try doing the following.

>>> init -driver pk

wait a few seconds ...

>>> show dev

this procedure will reset the scsi bus and force a rescan of the ids.
hopefully spinning the attached disk, if that indeed is the cause.
Usefull for folks hot plugging SCA disks on their Alphas :-)

Peter
