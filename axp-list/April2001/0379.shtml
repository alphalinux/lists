As a former ISDN user I can recommend that you skip over looking
for an ISDN adapter and pickup something like a Netgear RT328 ISDN
router.They go for less than $100 on ebay. Can support two B channels,
CSV (saves alot of money), have two phone jacks, and support NAT plus
other networking niceities :-). They usually have 3 year warrenrities
too.
