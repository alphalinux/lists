I am getting 25 MB/s write, 20 Mb/s read. I have heard
that I should be getting 30 MB/s both ways though. This is
with Lanai 7 boards, linux-2.2.19, gm-1.4, and a FC RAID that
is doing 55MB/s writes, and 35 Mb/s reads. These read and write
numbers for NFS are for proto=tcp or udp, {r,w}size=8192 or 32768.

I am going to setup a machine with a few drives and raid them together.
If I my NFS performance results change for the better, I will let you know.
I think my performance is roughly half of the RAID device because NFS and the
qlogic driver are fighting over the CPU. I have not confirmed this though.

The question is why is there such a disparity between reads and
writes? I am writing files that are much larger (4x to 16x) larger
than RAM so caching should not be an issue.
