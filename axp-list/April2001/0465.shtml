Looks like you don't have the symbolic links to the kernel headers:

I _think_ they should be:

ln -sf /usr/src/linux/include/linux /usr/include/linux
ln -sf /usr/src/linux/include/asm /usr/include/asm

but PLEASE verify before you set them...
