While the performance question is at hand - A friend of mine works at
Motorola running systems, including clusters, etc. for them. They have a
number of architectures - Sun, Alpha, IBM. For their needs the Alpha's
outperform the Suns by at least 2 to 1 cost/performance. However, it is
painful to try to get an engineer who 'grew up' on Suns at the University
to use an Alpha.

Just another plug for the Alphas.
