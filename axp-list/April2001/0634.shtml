Oh yes, there is a quite decent one. Try gtv or plaympeg, bot are bundled
with smpeg, the MPEG library from Loki.
I don't have an URL handy right now, but just visit Loki's site.
Don't forget to compile with -mieee otherwise you'll probably get
FPEs. ccc with optimizations improves performance by quite a bit.
