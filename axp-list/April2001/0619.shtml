On Thu, May 03, 2001 at 02:16:58PM +0200, Reto Baettig wrote:

> NFS is _very_ slow and I get a lot of RPC timeouts. I checked the
> network (switched) and did not find any lost packets. Did anyone have
> similar problems with NFS on Alphalinux? I saw that there are some
> patches around (Trond's NFS patches) which fix SMP problems in the
> SunRPC code but that did not help :-(

The sunrpc patch you're looking at solves a hang, not slow NFS.

In addition to checking the duplex on your network, you might want to
update your ethernet driver. We switched to Becker's eepro100 driver
on our ES40, for example, because the driver in RedHat 6.2 wasn't up
to snuff.
