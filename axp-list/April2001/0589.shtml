Hi Luiz,

JumpStart! 1.4.1 works fine on the Jensen we have in the lab here, so I'm
going to assume that it's something about your setup that is causing the
failure.

> I burned a CDR using the command:
> cdrecord dev=6,0 speed=8 fs=3m ./jumpstart-1.4.1.iso

Most of the JumpStart problems I see (especially with
Jensens) are caused by a bad CD - it seems that the CD-ROM drives in
Jensen machines are prety picky. Could you try burning a new CD (at a
lower speed, like 4)? the "attempt to read past eof" could indicate that
part of the CD is damaged..

> Notice that I am running Alpha System ROM 1.7 and SRM 32f.

Hmm. I think my machine has ROM 2.2, SRM 370. You might want to upgrade,
if you can find the firmware. It should be available here:
http://ftp.digital.com/pub/DEC/Alpha/firmware/readmes/dec2000.html

good luck!
