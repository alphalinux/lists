Robert Williams wrote:
>
> Is there a known problem with the tulip ethernet driver
> for axp/PC164? (running RedHat 6.1)

There is now identified a long standing problem with a tulip driver (and
other, like eepro100, for example) on all platforms. A circular list of
skbufs may get empty why it should not be and this results in an attempt
by your kernel to dereference the address 0 which translates to crash.
For some reasons you are seem more likely to see that condition with
current 2.2 kernels but you need a pretty heavy net traffic for that.

Tulip fixes were included in 2.2.14; eepro100 will show up in 2.2.15.
