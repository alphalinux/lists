COMPAQ (DEC) sold both Millennium I and Millennium II cards in boxes;
the I has a DIP switch to disable VGA mode, the II does NOT. I do not
know what non-OEM versions of those cards might have had.

Most notable difference between the cards was the big MGA chip: 2064 on I,
2164 on II, IIRC. Some I's were labeled "Storm" instead, apparently, from
the one I have, which does not say 2064... :-\

Note also that the ELSA Gloria Synergy OEM version that COMPAQ is using
these days DOES have a VGA disable switch, while at least one retail
version I've seen does not, so be careful when buying something sight
unseen.
