My 6.2 on a PC164 freezes just where the startup
sound starts to play. No freeze occurs if remove
my SB16 prior to booting.
Here is my sound configuration:

# more conf.modules
alias parport_lowlevel parport_pc
alias sound sb
pre-install sound /sbin/insmod sound dmabuf=1
alias midi opl3
options opl3 io=0x388
options sb io=0x220 irq=5 dma=0 dma16=5 mpu_io=0x330
alias scsi_hostadapter BusLogic
alias eth0 3c509
