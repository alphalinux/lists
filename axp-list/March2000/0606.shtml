On Thu, 30 Mar 2000, Donald Maner wrote:

> I am attempting to re-install Tru64 on a machine that currently has RedHat
> 6.1 on it. I cannot re-disklabel the machine from the Tru64 installation
> CD. It disklabel in Tru64 errors with "Partition 'a' is in-use; Cannot
> modify its size or location." This is not true, as I have booted off the
> CD. How can I wipe this drive clean?

I suggest booting up Linux to a shell prompt and doing a:

# dd if=/dev/zero of=/dev/sda ibs=128k count=10
