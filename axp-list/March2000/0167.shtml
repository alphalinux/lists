My DEC PW 433a came with an awesome *looking* videocard. I wasn't able to
configure X to run with it, so I put in a Matrox Millenium II and it works
great. Still, I'm curious about this card. I tried to do some web research
to figure out exactly what it is, with no success. Here's what it looks
like:

It's a dual PCI card -- it takes up two side-by-side PCI slots.
It has two 15-pin monitor outs and one DIN TV-out
It redefines the term "full-length"
It has TWO 168-pin DIMM sockets
It has three black pointy heat sinks
It has a Cirrus Logic GD5440 chip on it (and more I can't read)
It has "Intergraph Computer Systems" written all over it
  [Intergraph has a inpenetrable website]
It has a 60-pin (?) IDE-like socket

Any clues to what make/model it might be would be appreciated. Thanks!!
