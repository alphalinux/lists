> 1. What video cards will work with both the enthusiast
> non-commercial True64 Unix and Linux (on a PC164)?

I can't speak about Tru64, but I had trouble with two different video cards
on my DEC PW433. They ran fine as VGA cards, but wouln't work with XFree86.

In the end I used a Matrox Millenium II -- I understand it's one of the most
universally supported video cards in the free *nix world. The Matrox G200
and G400 are it's descendants.
