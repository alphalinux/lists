Fab cost isn't everything. R&D, advertising, promotion, etc... -- these monies
all have to be recovered somehow. There's no real way to judge just how much the
chip really ought to cost without knowning how much money was actually spent on
the entire project. And $500 is a lot, if it's just for fab, so there's it's no
wonder the chip is so expensive.
