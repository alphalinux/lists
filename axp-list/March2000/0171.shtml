Redhat 6.1 is a pain in the ass to install on an AS200 with SRM!
I booted the generic.img disk via

boot dva0 -file vmlinux.gz -flags "root=/dev/fd0" and the kernel
loaded up just fine and asked for the root disk, then I inserted
ramdisk.img and pressed enter and it immediately dropped to SRM
(back to the blue screen) with no kind of error message what so
ever. These disks are brand new, just formatted. Pretty weird.
