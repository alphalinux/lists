The key here is "complain". No one likes listening to
somone whine about a problem. Keep in mind the people
that work on open source do so because they like to and
probably have a use for the code they create. Sitting
around whining because someone doesn't do what you want
is non-productive. If you don't program, you need to
consider how to get someone interested in what you want.

Getting 3D acceleration going, while useful, is not,
apparently, what most folks who work on Alphas care
about. I know I don't have any use for it and I have
quite a few Alphas.

I do want a fast standard X though. I've been satisfied
with XFree so far.

YMMV
