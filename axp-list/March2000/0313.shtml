Gentlemen,
Regarding this thread about fab costs. The figures for the cost of
manufacture came from me in a private conversation between Dan and Myself.
The actual figures told to me by a trusted friend that is in the "computer
business" but works outside of Compaq/API. The costs for the chips was a
quote from a silcon fab that given X transitors, RISC design, and Y
complexity that this is how much it would cost. Around $500. The board not
included. That is fact. Whether that is the "actual" fab cost of an Alpha?
I can only speculate. I am very sorry for the disruption this has caused
in the Alphalinux community and in the future I will make sure that
private information does not end up on this list or any other list ever
again.
