On Thu, 9 Mar 2000, Andrei A. Dergatchev wrote:

> I remember that when I was shopping for an Alpha, LX models were
> considerably more expensive then the SX. Anyone can comment on
> it ? Is it only due to 2 MB cache or is 21164A (LX) is much better then
> the 21164PC (SX) in some issues ?

The LX boards can have either 2 or 4MB or cache, SX only has one. The SX
takes the pca56 processor that also has less internal cache (I think it's
half but I could wrong on that) than the ev56. The LX boards can take up
to 1Gig of memory, SX only takes 512MB. There may also be memory bandwith
issues here as well, haven't really looked at that.

The pca56 and ev56 processors are not pin compatible so you can't put an
ev56 into an SX or a pca56 into an LX/UX.
