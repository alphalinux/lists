> > (FYI, you'll probably always want to use /dev/nst0 instead of /dev/st0)
>
> I know that /dev/nst0 won't rewind the tape everytime I write to it,
> but how do I know that it's correctly point to st0?

use ls and check the major and minor device numbers. The major should be the
same.
Eg. on my i686 box:

$ ls -l /dev/st0 /dev/nst0
crw-rw---- 1 root disk 9, 128 May 6 1998 /dev/nst0
crw-rw---- 1 root disk 9, 0 May 6 1998 /dev/st0
