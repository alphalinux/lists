ObOnTopic: I'm somewhat in the same boat as Dan - I always had a thing for
DEC hardware (and I still can't bring myself to call them Compaq). I also just
am a fan of the "make it run everywhere" concept. Right now I don't even have
a functioning linux/i386 box (although I really should put one toghether soon).
I'm doing all my work on linux/alpha, linux/arm, linux/ppc, and linux/sparc32.
I also have some linux/m68k and linux/mips hardware lying around but I haven't
played with them much lately. I think one of the neat things about free
software is that you have some leverege to avoid being locked into a hardware
world dominated by intel or anyone else.
