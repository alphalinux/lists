Michal Jaegermann <michal@ellpspace.math.ualberta.ca> writes:

> If you are looking for 'sendmail' replacement for whatever reasons
> I would rather recommend a peek at 'postfix'. There are multiple

Exim (http://www.exim.org/) is pretty nice too, though it probably
won't scale to the same volumes of mail as postfix, qmail, or zmailer.

I like Exim's RBL support more than postfix's because you can reject
based on some RBL lists and just filter (i.e. add an X-RBL-Warning
header) on others. I can reject mail from hosts in the RBL and filter
from hosts in ORBS.

Of course postfix may have gained this feature recently, so I could be
wrong there :)
