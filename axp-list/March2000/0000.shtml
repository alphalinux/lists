1) you've got a boot disk with milo in and have to make two disks with
rawrite general.img and ramdisk.img.
2) on MILO prompt type: boot fd0:vmlinux.gz root=/dev/fd0 load_ramdisk=1
3) insert kernel disk... then ramdisk...
4) at this point the installation starts....
