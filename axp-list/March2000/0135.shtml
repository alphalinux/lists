The 21164PC is the chip used on the 164SX motherboards. The pc164, 164LX
and 164UX boards all use the 21164(a) processor. Not pin compatible.
However if someone was to work on a DVD/MPEG2 player that used MVI, it would
sure help out those of us with 164SX boards ( and also people with EV6
processors).
