> I run two Elsa Gloria Synergy and lots of Matrox Millenium I.
> Both are very stable with X. Some S3's need special parms to
> behave. Search the archives for the correct options.

Yes, the ELSA and the Millenniums (I and II) work very well in most boxes.

And yes, S3 Trio64 is notorious, especially in AS200/AS400 boxes. Try adding
the following to the Device section of your XF86Config file:

        Option "no_pci_disconnect"

and it should behave better.

Good luck.
