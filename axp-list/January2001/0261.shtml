On Thu, Jan 18, 2001 at 09:02:06AM -0800, Robert M. Riches Jr. wrote:
> Are there any gotchas/problems/quirks to watch out for when
> installing RH7 on a one-processor UP2000? The machine currently
> has RH6.1 and uses SRM.

Not that I know of; but get immediately all updates to packages you
installed.

If you have a reasonably decent network connection start with
updates to up2date and rhnsd and you may try to automate the whole
process with these.

It is pretty likely that you want various, but not necessarily all,
'compat' packages. Most prominently 'compat-libstdc++' and
'compat-glibc' - probably in that order.
