That's due to DDR cache, which has nothing to do with Samsung or the fab.
Samsung did fab Alphas in 0.25 micron, which was significantly better than
the Hudson fab's 0.35 micron process. But that resulted in the higher clock
rate chips that you see in both the DS20 and the DS20E.
