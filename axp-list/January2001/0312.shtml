On Mon, Jan 22, 2001 at 11:57:47AM +0100, Peter Rasmussen wrote:
> The problem was that mkdosfs wasn't installed

That would, indeed, explain why it would not work. But note that
'mkdosfs' and 'dosfsck' are in a root PATH so if you did 'su' instead
of 'su -' from a non-root account then you have to call them by a full
name.

> and I couldn't find one for the
> alpha on RedHat.

$ rpm -Uvh dosfstools*alpha.rpm
$ rpm -ql dosfstools
/sbin/dosfsck
/sbin/fsck.msdos
/sbin/mkdosfs
/sbin/mkfs.msdos
/usr/man/man8/dosfsck.8.gz
/usr/man/man8/mkdosfs.8.gz
/usr/man/man8/mkfs.msdos.8.gz
$ rpm -q --queryformat '%{ARCH}\n' dosfstools
alpha

Hm... OK.
