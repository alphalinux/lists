On Fri, 9 Feb 2001, Ron Farrer wrote:
> Plus I've always found that Alphas multitask better (not sure
> why that one is).

i have no wish to get involved in either a x86-vs.-alpha flamewar or a
win-vs.-linux flamewar, but i thought i would offer an explanation for
your observation. alpha's timer interrupt occurs at 1024Hz whereas x86's
occurs at 100Hz, so alpha gets much better scheduling granularity. this
is most likely the reason that your alpha subjectively feels smoother.

of course, the fact that you like it better might possibly be influencing
your opinion a bit as well ;)

trying to be a fair alpha-advocate,

 -ross
