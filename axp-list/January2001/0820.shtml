Just about any SCSI disk will do from IBM/Quantum/Western Digital. Seagate
drives are characteristically whinny with the high pitched whirring sound
and loud startup being dead giveaways to a Seagate driven disk sub.

The newer 9.1 GB IBM low profile Ultra and Ultra 2 drives are super quiet
and run fairly cool, even at 10K. The 7200 RPM UltraStar drives can be had
for cheap on any of the surplus and auction sites.
