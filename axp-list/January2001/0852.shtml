On Fri, Mar 02, 2001 at 12:55:17PM -0500, Kurt Ludwig wrote:
> since it's a slightly older rev DEC tulip card, you may want to try the
> tulip, or old_tulip driver instead (I forget exactly which one it is in RH7)

Both. It is up to you to pick one which fits.

> there was also a problem w/ the on board NICs on the ruffians, but
> considering you had it working in 5.2, I think your board is exempt from it.

Unless different ruffian boards have different models of tulip built-in
then, AFAIK, this NIC works (on 2.2 kernels, forget about de4x5 for 2.4)
with all three drivers, i.e. de4x5, old_tulip and tulip. OTOH it seems
to be very picky about what is on the other end of a wire and it may
fail to negotiate (which implies that it will not work) with some hubs
while this is peachy with other models. These things happen _before_
your driver even comes into a play.
