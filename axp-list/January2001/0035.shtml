On Thu, 4 Jan 2001, Mohan wrote:

> Greetings,
> Is there a utility that will clean the "/var/log/lastlog" file?
> It seems to get huge over time? Does such a beast exist?

Files like that need to be rotated. "Cleaning" is usually not a good idea
(assuming you mean either truncating, or deleting and then recreating it).

An easy fix might be to checkout /etc/logrotate.conf (assuming Red Hat)
and create an entry similar to the one for wtmp.
