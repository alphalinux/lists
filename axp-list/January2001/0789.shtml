For what it's worth:
After we migrated from BNC to 10/100 twisted pair,
I had problems getting any 100Mb/s card to work reliably
in four different pc164 machines (not sx or lx).
I tried 3com 905, EtherExpress Pro/100,
RealTek, SMC etherpower II, and several flavors of Tulip.
(Some of you may recall my whining on this topic.)
Suspecting a problem with the PCI subsystem and speed,
I eventually connected these machines through
10 Mb/s hubs and switches and now they work fine
on any of these cards.

Bob Williams
