Hi Wes,

Your suggestion does make more sense. Originally, I had
tried to make the parameter a flag that you pass to aboot. That
did not work because aboot tried to boot off of the "mounted"
filesystem before parsing the flags. So I ended up using the
boot device environment variable to accomplish what I needed.
Now that I think about it, I think the filename is passed as a
seperate environment variable like the boot device. I'll take
a look at making your change and reposting the patch.

Thanks,
Alan
