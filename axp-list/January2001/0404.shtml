On Sun, Jan 28, 2001 at 11:30:21AM -0500, Andrew A. Gill wrote:
> On Sun, 28 Jan 2001, Michal Jaegermann antagonized the horn and now...
> >
> > Er... The last time I looked RH 7 CD included both 4.0.1 and 3.3.6
> > X servers. Not to mention such things like a compiler.
>
> Yeah, but what TO compile?

A server for your card from X 3.3.6 sources. But somebody likely
already did that for you and left results on your CD. Just install,
make sure that /etc/X11/X points to it, and that your XF86Config is
right. You do not have to worry about the remaining X stuff. It
will still work. Version 4 loads server specific stuff as modules,
so there are no separate servers for different cards, but you do
not have to be concerned about that for the moment. Even config
files may be separated into XF86Config and XF86Config-4 (the later
is optional but if it exists then v4 will use that one and not
XF86Config).
