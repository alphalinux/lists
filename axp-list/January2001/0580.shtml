Mike Foley wrote:
> If you have the source, recompile. If you don't have
> the source, find something that does and recompile.
> The more native apps on Alpha the better.

100% agreed. That said, it'd be nice to have something like Winelib on
Alpha/Linux, so certain Win32 programs could be ported with less effort.

Forget binary emulation. You can still have a degree of source compatibility
with the right libraries. And if Winelib were usable on Alpha, that might even
help convince some of the AlphaNT vendors to come support Linux.
