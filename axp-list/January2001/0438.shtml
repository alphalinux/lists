With regards to your message at 06:42 AM 1/31/01, Helge Kreutzmann. Where
you stated:
>Hello !
>We have a ruffian which we can boot fine without keyboard but if we
>remove the graphic card it beeps three times, the hard disks spin up
>and nothing else happens. I read the thread of March last year but
>there was no conclusion then.
>We will get a serial cable and see how far it goes especially since
>there are several entries in the ArcsBios relating to video (which we
>not fully understand) but has someone actually tried booting the UX
>without a graphics board ?
It is dependant on this because of ARCSBIOS (the Deskstation NT-oriented
BIOS that comes on it).
PC164LX had that limitation too, but later (SRM) BIOS allowed headless
operations.
In this sense it is similar to an Intel PC board which requires a video
card for console.
