I've installed RH6.2 on mine. (haven't been daring enough to try 7.0 yet).

to change it to SRM, turn the power on and hold the HALT button in until it
comes up in SRM. when it comes up, at the 'P00>>' prompt type in:
'set os_type Unix'

the next time that you reboot it'll come up in SRM from now on.

There's only a few issues to watch for when you install on it.

->The onboard SCSI card is a 53c810 and shows up as the first controller in
SRM. The only thing that's on it is the CDRom.

I'm not sure if the Advansys card works in AlphaLinux or is recognised by
SRM. if it isn't recognised by SRM you can't boot off of it. My 5300 has an
intraserver card w/ dual 53c875's and a tulip on it that is fully supported.

check and see if you have the 'stock' dual 875 based card in there too.

good luck,
Kurt
