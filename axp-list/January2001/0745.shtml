On Wed, Feb 21, 2001 at 10:37:48AM -0500, Speedracer wrote:
>
> 3. Any pointers on running 2 video cards in a PC164?

I suspect that you'll have to find a second card that has a VGA disable
DIP switch on it, like the OEM'ed Millennium I's (not II's) we sold, and
the ELSA Synergy Permedia2's. Otherwise, both cards will respond to the
VGA addresses as the SRM will not disable one or the other.

Oh, wait, maybe you could use a TGA or TGA2 for the second head, though
then you may have to deal with which head is chosen by the SRM and which
by the kernel as "primary"... ;-}
