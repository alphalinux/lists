>Hello Luiz,
>
>the ELSA winner 1000 ISA/EISA comes to mind, which is a S3 based
>card. This thing actually works in jensens. Bur alas it does not work
>with the supplied S3-server binary from X 3.3.6 (at least not with
>the packages that come with RedHat 6.2 or SuSE 6.4). The work around is to
>use the binary from the 3.3.5-packages with the rest of 3.3.6.
> And you have to chose the S3 server as this is the only one to
>support the jensens addressing scheme in X3. There is some rumor over at
>the debian-alpha list that jensen support can be enabled in X4 in the
>SVGA-server. If that really works your Qvision might be usable after all.
>
>Good luck
> Sebastioan Moeller
