Maurice Hilarius wrote:
> If I look at the various documents (mainly Powerpoint slides) of DEC,
> Compaq, and Samsung/API conferences and releases of 4 years ago until last
> year, I see a common thread in that we should already be running EV7, newer
> Alpha chipsets, and well over 1GHz.

Probably. But what's the hangup on clock speed? If Intel/AMD have everyone
thinking their slower Alpha/PPC/SPARC/whatever is inferior, they've won a major
marketing battle.

1GHz isn't a real "barrier" in any engineering sense. The memory wall is very
real in comparison, and has a far more profound impact on system performance
nowadays than clock speed. But nobody is interested in that :(

Gimmee faster cache, and lots of it...
