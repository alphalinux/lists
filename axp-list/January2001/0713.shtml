Nice machine. The DS20s seem to work well with linux. The PowerStorm
4D10T isn't supported. You can replace it with a cheap S3 if you aren't
doing graphics intensive work. The DEC3000 has turbochannel. This isn't
well supported. There is some mention of contacts in the list archives
for those that are working on it.
