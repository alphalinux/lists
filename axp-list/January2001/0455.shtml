Do these cards talk to the same network cards/switches/whatever on
the "other end"? This seems to important for attempts of an
autonegotiation as there is two to tango.

Also keep in mind that there is, AFAIK, seven quite different chipsets,
with different bugs in hardware/firmware in what is known as eepro100,
for example. I would not even try to count different variations of
"tulip". So your NICs may be not the same even if outwardly they
appear to be.
