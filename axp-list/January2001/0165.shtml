I think you'll need an engineer.
Those DLTs (from what I remember) have a "male" leader inside which then hooks
into the tape inside the DLT cartridge. The leader sometimes comes off - which
leaves the cartridge stuck and the device inoperable. Hopefully it may not be
that severe - but get someone in if you can!
