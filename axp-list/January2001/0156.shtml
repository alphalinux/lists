TGA2 is DEC proprietary, fabbed by IBM; models are 3D30 and 4D20. They
have a silverish IBM chip (the TGA2) on 'em.

E&S are among the higher end cards, whose models are Powerstorm 300/350,
IIRC.

Some Intergraphc cards were also OEM'ed, and were models 4D30/40/50/60,
again IIRC.

I think some of this info is on our webpages...
