I had similar problems when I was running AlphaBIOS on my PC164SX w/ a
SymBIOS 53c895 and a 9GB IBM drive. It would work fine when only 1 drive was
on the controller. But, when I put more disks on it to build a software RAID
array, I had similar problems to what you are experiencing. I ended up
changing to SRM and all my problems w/ multiple drives went away.

It may not be the best answer to the question, but at least for me it did
work.

good luck!
Kurt Ludwig
