Nope! In some aspects development of 2.2.x kernels is ahead of those
from 2.4 series. Linus is pretty conservative in what he accepts.
Virtual memory system was redesigned quite substantially between 2.2
and 2.4 kernels and things do not translate directly in this area.
Various things will be carried over, eventually, from 2.2 to 2.4 -
especially in drivers - but not now.

OTOH a similar VM behaviour you observe with 2.2 and 2.4 kernels,
despite different virtual memory systems, may indicate that something
deeper is going on here. In that light numbers with VM patches, or from
the latest 2.2.19pre, could be even more telling. It is really hard
to guess what will happen without measurements.
