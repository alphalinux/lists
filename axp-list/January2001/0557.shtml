Depend on your config...

> > The QL ISP1020 is an Ultra Wide SCSI adapter, which means you can get a
>> maximum transfer rate of 40 MB/s. The disks Compaq shipped with my XP-1000
>> are all Ultra3 SCSI, which have a max. rate of 160 MB/s. This fabulous

>The disks are SCSI/160, but that does not mean they can transfer 160 MB/s.

Using an ITI LVD cards, on 1 port, 2 disk, md, I saw something like 70MB/sec
on 18G 15,000RPM disks.
I also tried 2 port 4 disk(2disk/port) over 120MB/sec.... not too difficult.
Pretty easy with RAID0

On 2.2.17 I used raid-2.2.17-A0 patch back then.

I hope this info helps a bit...
