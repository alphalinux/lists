Perhaps it would work to upgrade to 3.0.5-9.6x first, then
upgrade to 4.*. Just yesterday, I downloaded the 3.0.5-9.6x
packages from Redhat and upgraded my 6.1 system. I'm told the
3.0.5-9.6x packages can handle rpm v4 packages, so maybe a
two-step approach would work. (Many thanks to Michal Jaegermann
for excellent advice.)

(No, I have not attempted the second part, from 3.0.5-9.6x to
4.*, so I cannot speak with certainty about that step.)

Good luck.
