When you look in the drive, can you see the tape leader? There's
a little prong it should be hooked onto. If not, the tape leader
has slipped off and wound inside the drive. In that case you can
either send it in for service, or take it apart and reattach the
tape leader yourself. I do the latter as it's faster by weeks.

You can modify the directions at http://www.sprague-europe.com/frame/tape.htm
They're for putting in a whole new tape leader. Reattaching an existing tape
leader is similar. If you have new tape leaders on hand I'd recommend
putting in a new one - once they start slipping, it happens again
and again. IME.
