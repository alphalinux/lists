Maybe I missed it but why do you actually care what the Mhz of
the processor is? Also, keep in mind the lmbench routine is often
wrong so you would be better off looking at how the kernel determines
the Mhz. I haven't seen that go wrong on any of my systems as yet.
Including PII/PIII/P4/G4/Alphas. You might be able to lift that piece
of code and use it which would give you exactly what you're looking
for. Note that is not the same thing as Bogomips. I suspect it will
be assembly language though. Check the "arch/*/kernel/time.c" routines
to see how the kernel does it.
