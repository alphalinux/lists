On Thu, Jan 18, 2001 at 02:39:33PM -0500, Rich Payne wrote:
> >
> > Is is possible to install RedHat Linux 6.2 on an alpha system, with
> > console over serial -- or just with a graphical console?
>
> yes, just add console=ttyS0 to your boot flags.

Actually I do not think that you need to do that - at least with some
versions of an SRM console. In an absence of keyboard/graphics card
a console i/o gets redirected to a serial line automagically.

In some of my tests it was even _better_ not to do this 'console=...'
stuff. If I did then a console was "silent" until I was getting
'login: ' prompt all of sudden. Without that I had a full complement
of boot messages on my screen and that can be easily captured with
various terminal emulator programs in a case of troubles.
