This sounds like the MILO problem.....the Ruffian MILO in 6.2 can't handle
kernels over a certain size. Get the MILO and ldmilo.exe from:
ftp://ftp.alphalinux.org/pub/Linux-Alpha/Kernels/
 ldmilo-ruffian-981020
 milo-ruffian-981020

Also, it should be:
boot hda:/kernels/generic.gz root=/dev/hda

root= not root:

that should fix it.

--rdp
