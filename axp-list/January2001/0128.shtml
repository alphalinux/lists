> Hello,
> does anybody know how to compile Scilab 2.5 with compaq fortran?
> I can't get a g77 for alpha linux (running Red Hat 6.2) and f2c
> gives too much problem.

Use fort - the fortran compiler from compaq. If you have some kind
of makefile which calls for g77 or f77 the best is to do a simlink
f77 -> fort.

You can get the it at http://www5.compaq.com/products/software/
