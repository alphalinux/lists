> There is very little, if any, luck in using these. I have tried 4d20s,
> 3D30s, and 300s. They do not work.

XFree86 4.0.2 release has the start of TGA2 support; that is, for the
Powerstorm 3D30 (8-plane) and 4D20 (24-plane) cards. Limited to 1024x768
at 72Hz resolution/refresh. Has some accelerations that make it quite
usable. XFree86 CVS now has support for additional resolutions.

Still not (and prolly never will be) supported are the 4D40/50/60 and
300/350.
