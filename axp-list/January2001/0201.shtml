> That interface *works*. I'm using it right now. It just doesn't work under
> 2.4 with the de4x5 that's included in the source.

'de4x5' driver was always broken to some extent despite of working in
some selected situations better than 'tulip'. If you want technical
reasons then "it doesn't use rx_copybreak and overflows standard socket
buffers with its always MTU sized skbuffs" (a quote from Andi Kleen).
This, in particular, means that it disintegrates under load. It looks
like that the situation with 2.4 just got more pronounced. Try 'tulip'
driver instead.
