The Diamond FireGL1000 Pro should work nicely with SRM ( it is compatible
with the DEC Powerstorm 4D10T and the ELSA GLoria Synergy ).
If all else fails on your SCSI, you could setup your system to boot from
floppy, and then put MILO on your floppy. ( or small IDE hard drive if you
have IDE built into your Motherboard )
