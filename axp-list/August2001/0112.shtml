Everyone says the PROMISE UDMA 66/100 controllers work well. You won't be
able to boot from it though. You will have to boot from either floppy, CD,
or a SCSI Hard drive attached to the SCSI port on the Motherboard. ( The
hard drive could be old/small/slow, you're really just needing to load a
kernel that has the Promise driver compiled in )
