I have an Aspen Telluride (EB64, I think) that won't boot to even the
firmware on the first pass (keyboard flashes once, display signal
indicator changes to orange [no signal]). I also went through, reseating
boards, etc. Even replaced the RAM and sent it back to Aspen to
be checked out - worked fine for them.

Finally...one time I just hit the "reset" button after the lock-up - it
worked.
Now, every time I start it up, it locks up, I hit reset, it comes up fine.

Odd, but it works for me, maybe it'll work for you.
