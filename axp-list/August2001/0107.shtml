The AL means it has a built-in 10/100Mb Enet. Saves having to put
a card in. I've used up thru 1.5Ghz P4's without problems. Don't
know anyone using 1.7Ghz. Cost/performance of the 1.7Ghz were not
as good as the 1.3-1.4Ghz chips last I checked so I don't buy
them for what I do. One thing I can think of. There are two models
of D850's, D850GB and D850GBC. The ones with Enet just add AL to
the name. The ones with 'C' in them are the newest boards. Maybe
you need those new boards instead of the older board?
