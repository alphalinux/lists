On Friday 03 August 2001 16:11, you wrote:
> (--) SVGA: Mode "1280x1024" needs hsync freq of 107.16 kHz. Deleted.
> (--) SVGA: Mode "1600x1200" needs hsync freq of 105.77 kHz. Deleted.
> (--) SVGA: Mode "1800X1440" needs hsync freq of 96.15 kHz. Deleted.
> (--) SVGA: Mode "1800X1440" needs hsync freq of 104.52 kHz. Deleted.
> Fatal server error:
> No valid modes found.
You defined 4 video-modes in your XF86Config, but all four of them are out of
spec for your screen! This is BAD. So you can change line 175 to

    HorizSync 30.0-110.0

As I think that your 21" should be able to handle this frequency, this
shouldn't be any trouble.
 Have fun.
