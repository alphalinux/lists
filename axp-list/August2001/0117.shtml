I know this is an alpha list, but a little P4 bashing might not hurt.

We have just received some P4/1700 (Siemens) and I started
to benchmark them against our 21264s.

stream was ok, but when I used flops I though
"I know the Alphas where faster, but so much ???"

After serveral days we found that the APM and Fansettings in the
BIOS made very huge differences in the performance.
It seemed the box started to sleep if nobody was typing, this
slowed the Fan down and than somthing braked the cpu to 1/10 th
of its speed.

Now after fixin the settings it is working ok, but the benchmarks
still show a variance of 5% . We have no idea why.

I read the P4 has a thermal sensor in the cpu that can slow it down
if it gets to hot. Unfortunately there seems to be no was to find out
when this is taking place. So it's easy, to make complete correct
benchmark runs were the P4 is solwer then a P3/500

Thorsten
