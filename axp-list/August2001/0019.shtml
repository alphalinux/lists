Iso-H wrote:

> Has anyone any idea what kind of schematic-diagram is
> for 164sx's USB-connection?
> For example what are resistors R128/133? Are they pull up or down
> resistors? Or is +5V supplied thru them to the connectors?

Far as I remember the USB solderpoints on the sx164 are not actually
wired into the cypress IO controller. I gave up and bought the el cheapo
USB PCI card from CompUSA for about $30 (ripped off, I could have got
the same for £10 in the UK) and I can drive a USB wacom graphite
graphics tablet through X with it.

On board Cypress chip
00:05.3 USB Controller: Contaq Microsystems 82c693

PCI addon USB
00:08.0 USB Controller: OPTi Inc. 82C861 (rev 10)

wacom 7104 0 (unused)
mousedev 7088 1
hid 27008 0 (unused)
input 5744 0 [wacom mousedev hid]
usb-ohci 28736 0 (unused)
usbcore 77312 1 [wacom hid usb-ohci]
