I'm not sure about Linux, but I know that Tru64/OpenVMS/SRM and
ARC/AlphaBios/WindowsNT stored the time in different formats in the TOY. It
was a very old internal DEC turf war from what I've gathered and we seem to
be stuck with it.

Does Linux expect it in the Arc/NT format (historical from the MILO days)?
Possible I guess but I'm not familiar with that part of the Linux kernel
code.
