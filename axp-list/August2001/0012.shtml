>We have the fan of the power supply and the fan on the CPU. Currently the
>case is open to (hopefully) reduce heat congestion inside the box.
>
>Do you think it is sensible to add some additional fans ?
Absolutely!
The fan in the power supply is definitely not enough to cool this type of
machine.
I strongly recommend adding another fan, preferably at the back pulling air
out of the case but one in the front pushing air can be OK too, as long as
the airflow path goes over the components.

Also, what is the ambient temperature in the room?
