> The following message is a courtesy copy of an article

> I have an AS255/300 with DU/Tru64Unix (4.0D) on one hard drive and
> AlphaLinux (RH7.1) on the other.
>
> As I transition from DU to Linux, I find myself booting back and forth
> between the two OSs. Unfortunately, each time I reboot, the date/time

gotten to the upstream kernel yet. Look for the changes in 2.4.9 or
2.4.10, though. I can probably whip up a patch against your favourite
kernel if you'd like. Turns out also that SRM's date command may or may
not actually show the date using the VMS epoch(s) by default.
