Hi Luke,
I have a XL266 with ARC 4.59. I got the binary from ~stefan and dumped it on
a dos formatted floppy together with linload.exe (from any release). This
boots fine into MILO. Then I use the 'boot' command but without the '-t
iso9660'. Later I copied milo to the fat partition on one of my harddisks
and now I boot from there. Much faster. I use the OPTIONS string in ARC to
give the boot string to MILO, so effectively I have an autobooting system
all the way into RH 7.1.

I do not have the MILO binary available here. I suggest that you download it
again yourself.

Good luck this time
Egbert Jan
