This sounds like the problme that DEC had with StorageWorks vs the MTI
Mustang... For heat reasons DEC "spun down" the disk drives in the storage
array. MTI put in "big enough" fans.

The result was a throughput difference of something like 3 to 1 (MTI over
StoragWorks) simply because of the "insignificant" latency introduced by
spinning down the drives when they weren't being accesseds -- to cut down
on overheating problems.
