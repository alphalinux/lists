Actually, after con(in)sulting Mike over a few emails 8), one issue does pop up
in my mind. What resolution/dpi are you attempting to run in? I mention this
because I'm a bit of a 'must cram more xterms into desktop, must have more
development windows open' type person so I tended to run at 1280x1024 and 24dpi
as my monitor becomes a moving target at high resolutions and I tend to get
banding of background gradiants in 16 bit mode 8/

At my normal working resolutions, DRI is not possible, however, Mike pointed out
that with only 16Mb video RAM available DRI rendering is only available in
16bpi. I'm running some of the ultra nasty demo's
(/usr/X11R6/lib/xscreensaver/pular -texture -fog -light -antialias -do_depth)
which without DRI kicks the alpha in the proverbial CPU crotch and attains a
meer 0.4 frames a second while with DRI enabled, bounces back at about 30-40
frames per second depending on other screen/machine activity on the sx164 (PCA56
533)
