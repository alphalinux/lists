Hello Mike,

I think You have done to much. I suppose Your Alphas are OK, but the
Dallas-Clockchips are to old. My Digital AXPpci33 Alpha starts
sometimes 1 hour after switching on if it was not in use acouple of
days.
Try following: switch it on, wait 3 hours
               switch off, wait for a minute and switch on again
               shorten the on-times in 15 minutes steps until you
               reach 15 minutes on-time
If it not starts by this 'play' switch it on for ten or 15 hours and
try again.

I hope it will help You
Reinhard
