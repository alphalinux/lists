huw> I'm attempting to port some software (a computer simulator)
huw> which runs under Intel Linux to my Alpha.

Before switching to AlphaLinux, I used DU/Tru64 for many years on my
AS255/300. I, too, had trouble getting a machine simulator
(spim/xspim) to run correctly. As others have suggested, the problem
was cause by the original authors assuming that ints/ptrs were 32 not
64 bits.

In particular, after many hours of debugging with 'gdb', I found the
problem: they had copied a negative, 32 bit value into a 64 bit
integer without proper (two's compl) sign extension.

The length dependencies are subtle and hard to find. But with
perseverance, you'll eventually succeed!

Good luck,
