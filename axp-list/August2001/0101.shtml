Hi Folks,

I was wondering how to get onto a Pentium Linux discussion group?
I have a problem that may be arcane or mundane, I don't know, but I
need to find a solution as quickly as possible. I have just gotten
a number of P4/1.7 GHz systems running RH 7.1, 2.4.2-2. These are
using the 850GB boards originally designed for workstations, but
installed into rackmount enclosures with 400 W power supplies and
surrounded by fans.

The problem is that on 3 of them, when i run the benchFFT program from
fftw.org, they freeze up after some period of running. On a 4th one,
the program has actually run through completion without hanging the
machine, but the performance seems to be inconsistent.

I was suspecting that they may be running into Linux incompatibility
problems, or the Intel "speed-step" technology that's slowing or
shutting down the processor when FP operations make the CPU too hot.
However, their exhaust temperatures are consistently a few degrees
lower than the UP2000+ systes I have in the same rack.

So, does anyone know of a source of information pertaining to P4/linux
and their problems?
