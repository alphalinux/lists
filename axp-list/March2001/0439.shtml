I saw one reported problem and I reproduced it. Basically you cannot
build 2.4.3 on Alpha. It seems like there was some changes in syntax
for pte_alloc and pmd_alloc (I think) and the prototypes done match
in different places.

I have not had luck with 2.4.2 on Alpha (SMP ES40). Writing big files over
NFS will cause the system to hang. Error messages on the console indicate
more of an SMP problem than NFS per se, but I don't have them written down
here to post them.

I am having a lot better success with 2.2.19 with the ES40s. I have
some UP2000s, but I plan to stick with 2.2.XX for them.

What qlogicfc drivers have you tried? I think that the qlogic driver
in the kernel is from UNH. Qlogic has their own driver that seems to
have been improving for Alpha. It (v4.24) even compiles cleanly for
alpha now and from my (limited) tests doesn't corrupt data. However
I have not tried this under 2.4, but it seems that they have been focusing
their development there and not on 2.2. There is also a driver at
www.feral.com for a FC driver for linux. I don't know how it plays
with 2.4 though.
