Not really haveing the time to follow the kernel list I follow some of the
more prominent members comments in their on-line diaries. Quick scan
once a week and I'm done. One interesting quote is from one of the RedHat
developers who's diary entry looks like this

"Went into the office to fit the drives. Watched the alpha much through
the cerberus tests without the kernel breaking which seems to be a good
thing. Pity every 2.4.x kernel out there at present will corrupt your
memory/disk."

-Philip Copeland-

So could this explain why my 2.4 series kernels always lock up on my
alpha? If I didn't have a zillion things due yesterday, or the day before
I'd be digging through the kernel mailing list. Thought others on the
list would get some use out of this.
