On Thu, 29 Mar 2001, Eugene Chu wrote:

> Thanks to Rich Payne and John Jason for pointing me to the mingo RAID
> patches. I have retrieved the file raid-2.2.17-A0, but being Linux
> ignorant, I'm not sure how to utilize this file. Can someone show me?

is your 2.2.17 kernel saved in or linked to /usr/src/linux?

In that case, go into /usr/src/linux, do a 'make clean', then cd .., then
do a 'patch -p0 </path/to/raid-2.2.17-A0' -- if I recall correctly.
