Padiyath Kumar wrote:
>
> Hi,
> What is the maximum transfer rate(MB/Sec) with ftp over giga bit
> ethernet with Alpha and intel machines?

I have two Giganix cards that can do a memory cached file 40MB/sec
into /dev/null on the other side. Using disk, you'll be constrained
by the disk speed, at least most folks will. This was between an Athlon
and a PIII. Haven't had a chance to try that card in an Alpha. They go
for $150 for the PC/Linux card and the Linux driver source comes with.
You pay around $230 for the card that works with a PPC Mac. Then again,
new PPC Macs come with Gigabit anyway so it's probably not a high volume
card. Probably something to do with Open Firmware also I suspect.
