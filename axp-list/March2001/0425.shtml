Alvin,

Adaptec RAID stuff was never tested or certified under Alpha, I would advise
sticking with Mylex where commonality between what Digital/Compaq OEM
(particularly through the StorageWorks group) and the commercially available
product is extremely similar if not identical. Note that I have only begun
playing with Ultra2 based RAID controllers on Alpha, though eXtremeRAID 1100
(EOL) and eXtremeRAID 2000 should be functional assuming the efforts to port
the drivers remains on target (these are still beta projects). You will
probably still have to boot from a more conventional SCSI controller (such
as the Qlogic 1040B/C or the LSI Logic 875J/895) or netboot to the kernel
from a BOOTP server to bypass lack of support for the RAID controller in SRM
via a supported network card (21x4x Tulip based network controller).

Good luck.
