> I am trying to change from SRM to AlphaBios on my PC164SX MB. At the
> >>> prompt I enter arc and insert the AlphaBios floppy in the diskette drive. The AlphaBios starts to initialize but complains of invalid NVRAM contents and then hangs. The battery seems ok on my VOM and I changed it anyway. How can fix this? I need to back for a while and reinstall my original NT system.

Don, switching from SRM to AlphaBIOS on the SX and LX is
tricky. Somewhere in the later flash updates something
changed that required extra work to go from SRM to
AlphaBIOS. I seem to recall the trick was something
like doing two emergency flash operations in a row,
but I'm not exactly sure. It may have been one emergency
followed by an update from the menu.

The PC164SX board I sent you has SRM loaded, BTW.
