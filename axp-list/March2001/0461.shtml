Here is a followup to my own post. I was complaining about the
DE600 (eepro100) driver under Alpha. It turns out that
the latest driver from scyld.com does indeed work. However
you need to download the netdrivers rpm package. This includes
a makefile that contains the necessary compiler options for
Alpha. If you just follow the instructions at the bottom of
the driver (as directed from the webpage) the driver will not
work correctly.

I am having good success now with 2.2.19 and v1.13 of the
eepro driver on my ES40s. I still have a ways to go but
this is a good start.

Craig
