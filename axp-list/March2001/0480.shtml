You will probably have to switch to the SRM console, or install RH 6.2
RH 7.0 doesn't seem to install very well with MILO. Some have reported
success, but RH even says that they no longer support MILO. You can grab
the latest SRM for you machine at

http://ftp.digital.com/pub/DEC/Alpha/firmware/

Goto the bottom of the page, there is a link for PC164/164sx/164lx boards.
The SRM FAQ can be found at

http://www.alphalinux.org/faq/srm.html

I hope this helps
