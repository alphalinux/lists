SCSI hard drives react differently to the SCSI "FORMAT" command (0x04).
Some may be able to low level format the drive if you can send a SCSI
command 0x04. However you are really only borrowing time until the ultimate
failure of your drive.
My recommendation... Find another drive (quickly).
