stated:
>Wrong, IDE CD's works just fine on srm. Hard drives are also supported in

Let's correct that slightly.
SOME (perhaps most) IDE CDROMs work fine under SRM.
Best test is to try to install TRU64.
We find that in CDROMs you tend to get what you pay for. Most of the better
Japanese brands work fine (Sony, Toshiba, Panasonic, TEAC) and most of the
Taiwanese and Korean models don't cut it (Samsung, Liteon, AFreey, AOpen,
for example).
