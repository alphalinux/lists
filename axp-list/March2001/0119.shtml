> When you start getting the e9.e8.e7.e6. countdown get ready. As soon as
> it flashes to the next part of the booting process start hitting ctrl-c as
> quickly as possible. This should break in. It works for me.
> James

> On Mon, 12 Mar 2001 rdean@watson.chemical.missouri.edu
> wrote:
>
> > How do I interrupt the SRM boot process. I have a alpha station that boots
> > straight to the true 64 OS and I want the SRM prompt.
> >

Most Alphas have a "halt button" - when depressed, the SRM will load, but
the system will not boot... then you can go in and reset the "boot action"
to halt.
