I got 960 Mbs between 2 ES40s (Compaq's GigE cards) using netperf's TCP_STREAM
test. For an ftp, your bottleneck will likely be the disks on each end. Unless
you are ftping files that are cached in RAM on the server and can fit in RAM
on the client or you have a fast RAID on either end.
