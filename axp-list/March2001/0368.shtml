Hi Alexey,

I tested the S3 928 Diamond Stealth Pro ISA and here are the results:
1. using ARC firmware (NT boot), the video card BIOS is displayed, I get the
regular firmware menu, ECU reconfiguration is fine using
config for "Standard VGA video" (PHI...cfg). WinNT4 runs just fine.
2. now, switching to OSF1 things are bad: after power-cycling the machine
the image is lost, that is, I can not see neither Diamond BIOS nor SRM
messages.
Then, to return to ARC I typed:

q (if at aboot prompt)
halt (if at srm prompt)
set os_type nt

and power-cycle the machine again to obtain ARC menu.
Do you know why there are such differences between ARC and SRM video
configurations?

thanks
Luiz
