The AlphaBios only machines (XL's) were their for a reason... Microsoft..
Back then, if we allowed a XL, to be dual booted, AlphaBios/arc for NT, SRM
for Unix/VMS. Microsoft would collect royalties for each machine shipped,
regardless of what OS was installed.. (One reason they ended up in court)..
So our way around it was to use SRM in mfg, then load AlphaBios/arc/NT prior
to shipping, since the flash on these machines could only hold one copy of
firmware, these could be sold as NT only. SRM was never externally made
available for them.

After the Justice took Usoft to court the first time, we could just
designate a orderable part# for NT systems (.ie. PWS's) these had SRM in
them and could be dual booted.
