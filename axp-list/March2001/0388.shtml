Hello Luiz,

On www.ebay.de there are at least two of the elsa cards for sale.
X requires that you use the S3-server (alphalinux, I have no clue about
OSF/DU/T64U). This holds true with X-3. (I have not tried X-4 on the
jensen.) (It is quite possible, that X-3.3.6 does not play easy with your
videocard. My workaround for this problem on rh6.2 and suse6.4 was to
install X-3.3.6 and manually replace the S3-server binary with the
appropriate binary from 3.3.5.)

Ahoi
