Andrei,

Alpha/NT list has a bunch of us, including vendors for SCSI controllers,
storage devices and backup software (BEI's Paul Bunn in particular). The
Alpha/NT list is fairly devoid of useful talk these days owing to our
currently dead/dying OS/platform combination thanks to Compaq and MS. A few
questions about SCSI that don't get to OS specific (as these do not) will
get you quite a few very authorative answers. The Use/News groups for SCSI
are pretty low quality. Tru64's list serve is pretty execllent as well,
remember to maintain good discipline with your replies (change your subject
lines, cut off unnecessary and old parts of the thread) and you'll get a
good audience.
