Eric and all,

The easiest method I have found is to invoke AlphaBIOS from floppy (type
ARC) and the console will load, presenting you with the AlphaBIOS splash
screen. You can then choose to update the firmware console from within the
AlphaBIOS running in memory. You can even use the same disk that you invoked
AlphaBIOS from (the .ROM file), interestingly enough. On SX and LX, the
latest version of AlphaBIOS is 5.7. When the flash operation is complete,
the reboot will come up with AlphaBIOS resident in EEPROM, allowing you to
configure your ARC boot pathes and your system partition.

Good luck,

--KE
