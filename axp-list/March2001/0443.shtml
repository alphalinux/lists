there is a proc interface to the SRM callbacks present in Compaq's
Jumpstart CD available at their sourceforge setup.
http://www.linuxalpha.compaq.com/sourceforge/ . The specification for
the SRM
callback implementation can be found in the Alpha Architecture Handbook.
