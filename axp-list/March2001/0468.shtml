Yes, I've used both the SMC 9332DST and the SMC 9332BDT ( as well as the SMC
8432 ). These all use either the tulip or de4x5 driver. I've never
benchmarked them, but I can tell you that they work. When SMC came out with
the 9432, they used their own custom ASIC. Haven't tried one of these with
linux though. Both of the 9332 boards have a 21140, but different revision
letters. The 8432 uses the 21041 and is 10 Mb only.

FYI... the A-Open AON-315 ( also ALN-315) is 21143 based.
