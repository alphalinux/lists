Kurt,

The cheat is for half flash, if you had AlphaBIOS resident, you would type
"os_type nt" and the system could be rebooted under Alphabios. The
directions I described allow you to bring up AlphaBIOS when you have a
machine flashed with SRM only. You can even use AlphaBIOS's simpler upgrade
facility to update the SRM console to the latest. When you reboot, SRM is
back again and you'll find not a trace of AlphaBIOS. I would suspect (but
have not tried) that if you changed any boot settings in AlphaBIOS they
would be flushed at reboot as the NVRAM contents is formatted differently by
SRM.
