> How can i get Xfree86 version 3.3.3. working on a Digital Alpha 1000A 5/300?

Are you trying to get the onboard VGA to work? It won't - the driver for
Cirrus has never been ported to Alpha.

Either use the Mono server (640x480 only), or disable the onboard via the
jumper and stick another card in there, like Matrox Millennium.

Good luck.
