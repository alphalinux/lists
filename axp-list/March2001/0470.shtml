> How to apply this patch to the kernel?
>
> "patch -p0 < alpha-numa-2" from my /usr/src/linux wont work.

Headers on "diff" lines in this patch say:

diff -urN 2.4.3/arch/alpha/.....

As your top level directory likely has a different than "2.4.3" name
then '-p0' will not work. Change to a directory directly above "arch/"
and do

   patch -p1 < [path_to_wherever_needed]/alpha-numa-2

and this will work. 'man patch' will have more details but usually
using '-p1' from a proper location is a safe, or safer, thing to do.
In general look what is in patch you are trying to apply and how
many levels you have to drop.
