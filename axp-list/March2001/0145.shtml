Fascinating... I'm a long time Digital/Compaq customer (20+ years).
        I've currently got 36 Alphas from ES10s to ES40s and a lot of
        antiques. Most purchased directly from Digital and the new boxes
        from Compaq Alpha VARs since Compaq stopped selling directly.

Never once have I gotten or seen SRM documentation!
        Oh, there are a page or two describing a couple of SRM commands
        burried in the CPU specific Operators manual -- but never anything
        that describes, let alone documents all of the commands visible
        with "help."

My classic beef with the SRM is its significant added cost for no apparent
benefit. Back when you could buy an OEM board with an ARC rom and the
identical board with an SRM rom, you paid $500 more for the SRM board!
A Digital/Compaq VP responsible for the SRM product once explained that
premium as the only way they can justify spending engineering dollars on
the continued existance of SRM. [Implying that SRM is not necessary for
the existance of Alpha, or VMS and Tru64!.]
