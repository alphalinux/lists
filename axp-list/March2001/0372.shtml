Hello Luiz,

I think the ELSA Winner 1000 ISA/EISA is also 928 based. This card works
just dandy in my machine. One thing you might try is to get rid of the
slot definition in the ECU. At least I did not assign a cfg file to the
slot that contains the card. It works under nt and linux, I have no clue
about OSF/1.

good luck
        Sebastian Moeller
