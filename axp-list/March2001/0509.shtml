I realize there is a great deal of discussion that goes on within the
group, specific to pricing of x86, Alpha and others. I've certainly been
wrong before, but I really dislike seeing input such as this.

There are many of us that could broadcast our latest and greatest sales
promotions, but I don't believe that was the intent or this user group. I
should think it far more appropriate to offer this information directly to
those who have pricing questions.

Word gets out quickly when one company or another has a value proposition
to offer.
