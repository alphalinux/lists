With regards to your message at 01:30 AM 3/26/01, W Bauske. Where you stated:
>You're ignoring media cost. Ecrix loses to DLT and various other media.
>Then again, if you don't use many tapes, they should work.
True, and true, although I should point out that DAT and Exabyte tapes have
a lot shorter life than VXA1 cartridges. Specs on these show nearly 3 times
the duty and unload cycles, so this helps a bit.

>Also, keep in mind if you want to read someone else's tape or give them
>your tape, other drives are more likely to be compatible. May or may not be
>an issue to you.
Hell there are problems with that in DAT drives.
Many Seagate, for example, will not read an HP DAT, and vice versa.

>As to what file systems are supported, that's up to your backup SW.
Yup.
