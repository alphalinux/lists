On 164LX/SX machines you can "cheat":

Type "AlphaBIOS" or "ARC" and the system will look for the ROM image on a
floppy. If you have AlphaBIOS 5.7 on floppy (as any good Alpha
administrator who supports NT would) you can boot AlphaBIOS without having
it in ROM. Its slow as molasses but it does work and is a little known
cheat on half-flash Alphas. Most of the PWS line, except for the "A" models
had 2MB flash EEPROM and could hold SRM and AlphaBIOS concurrently, all you
had to do was to switch console to run the OS of your choice.
