I also had problems with an ST15150N on a Symbios 53C875 (on an SX board).
If the drive is attached, MILO would hang probing the SCSI bus. I simply
removed the drive from the (correctly terminated) SCSI bus and everything
worked fine with my other drive. Same drive and HBA work great under NT. (
Alpha )

Sorry I don't have an answer for the problem. I just quit using the drive.
