It seems to me that the problem with video cards is that the Intel emulator
in SRM that is responsible for initializing the video BIOS only handles '286
instructions, and some of the newer cards are using '386 and higher
op-codes. Thus if the BIOS emulator were updated, we would be able to use a
new GeForce 2 MX video card. The pick and choose may still be slim on SCSI
controllers, but I suppose you could always put your kernel ( with
appropriate SCSI drivers ) on an IDE, then mount your root partition on the
SCSI/RAID/whatever...
