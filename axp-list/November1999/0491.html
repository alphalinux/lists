        By any chance, do you have a modem attached to the first serial port
on your computer? If so, turn it off while booting or rebooting. It's a
common mistake (which results in milo prompts being "automatically"
answered). I often forget this, since the I rarely reboot my alpha.

        Also, fmu has not been worked on for a long while. The latest
comments I've heard have basically said "don't use it." Still, I've never
tried it, so I do not speak from experience.
