Now that I've got my slides finished for the API Sales Symposium I can get
caught back up on this thread.

I'm not sure how our food will compare to the vaunted DECUS Singapore food,
but we will be talking with our resellers about both current and future
products at the Sales Symposium next week. As far as the 1.795 GHz EV74
which Compaq says they will ship July 17, 2001, all I can say is that if you
think about the supply chain, product qualification cycles, and past
performance, it's pretty clear that API will be shipping any given speed bin
before Compaq. Although we supply the chips to them the delay isn't because
we get access to the parts earlier, but rather because they are typically
trying to do much more complex systems with the higher speed parts, so they
have longer qualification cycles.
