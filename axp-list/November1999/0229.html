Naturally I will start with the obvious disclaimer that it depends on the
configuration, but, for example:
A UP1000 system with a 600MHz 21264 (Slot B) CPU, 2MB cache, 128MB RAM,
medium tower, 400W power, 9.1GB 7200rpm UDMA66 IBM hard disk, 48X
CDROM, 1.44 floppy, 8MB video card, keyboard, mouse, Linux pre-installed,
no monitor, is $3,499.
It is expected that we shall commence shipping these in next month. While
the boards now exist, the real final production boards are supposed to be
available in December.
