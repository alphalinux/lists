Buy an AlphaServer 1200 or Ultimate Workstation (same board, same
firmware), these are dual EV56 machines with the same chipset. The LX is
based on the 21174 Pyxis chipset which does not support dual processors.
Lodgepole has them in stock.

Good luck in hunting.
