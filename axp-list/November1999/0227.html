> I have an interest in G4 too but the coding style is cumbersome
> at this point since the compilers don't generate Altivec
> instructions automatically.

Generating Altivec is just like vector compilers in the good old days. Not
surprisingly, at least one vendor already has retargeted their vectorizing
compiler. It's not as automatic as it should be yet, due to alignment
issues, but that is a Small Matter of Programming (in the compiler) to fix.

http://www.psrv.com/altivec.html

I suspect we're moving into an era where single precision might become 2-4
times faster than double precision.
