<snip>
>
> So I might be in the market for a new SCSI host adapter. Does anybody
> know if the Intraserver cards work on PC164 w/ ARC console? A combo
> SCSI/ethernet card would be worthwhile for me.

Stephen,

  The Intraserver cards work very well in that machine (same as mine).
I previously had an Adaptec 2940UW in it, and I had constant lockups.
Replaced with a ITI-3140U and the lockups vanished - rock solid for
over 2 years now. I also have the ITI-4140UE (UW SCSI + 10/100
ethernet) in my dual PII, and I haven't had any problems with that
system either. I recommend both cards highly.
