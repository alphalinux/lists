On Wed, 10 Nov 1999, Mark Abene wrote:

>All you should have to do is tie together wires 3 and 4, the black and green.
>Cut away the insulation with a blade on both wires (DON'T cut the wires)
>and wrap them together with electric tape. This is necessary because the
>PC164 doesn't support the ATX "soft" power switch. You'll have to use the
>master power switch on the power supply itself to power on and off.

Or, you can replace the ATX momentary power switch with one from an AT
case, ie an on-off switch. Run the green wire to one side of the switch,
and ground the other.
