On Thu, 18 Nov 1999, Danilo Godec wrote:

> Has anyone succedeed in compiling openssl on Alpha? I fetched a .src.rpm
> and tried rebuilding it, but it uses some -m486 options.

Uhh, I hate replying to myself, but I should've checked better before
posting.

Changing a line in .spec file did the trick:

./Configure --prefix=/usr --openssldir=%{openssldir} linux-elf

changed to

./Configure --prefix=/usr --openssldir=%{openssldir} alpha-gcc
