Some of you might have heard of Satori Paint. It is one of the best paint programs
around, and one of the few that were available for Alpha NT.

Mark Lister from SatoriPaint posted this to the Satori Mailing List. I have talked to
Mark a couple of times regarding Linux and I am taking this message from him as a
possible good sign.

Please take the time and send them feedback. This program is well worth
having.............

Dear Linux/MacOS/BeOS supporters,

We have had many earlier requests to develop Satori versions for Linux, MacOSX and/or
BeOS. The most popular request is currenty for MacOSX version.

If you would like Satori to be available for any one of these operating systems then we
would welcome your feedback and encouragement. You could complete an independent survey
as suggested by Michael Mannall (below), email sales@satoripaint.com or vote directly
by emailing the appropriate address below. It will be interesting to see if the
popularitry of a Linux version exceeds MacOSX.

Linux@satoripaint.com
MacOS@satoripaint.com
BeOS@satoripaint.com

>Now would be a good time for them to get in when there is little competition
>from others, giving them a head start on what will possibly (hopefully) be
>the next main OS.

You might be surprised to hear that we are ready to start porting Satori to MacOSX,
Linux or BeOS but are not prepared to make a commitment alone to a new version.
However, we would be willing to complete this work as a develoment priority provided
that we are able to source a software publisher and/or investor that is willing to fund
the porting and help support the marketing and distribution activities.

If there is indeed an early market opportunity for a new Satori version then this
invitation should generate many applicants, especially given the likely high potential
return on investment.

We are a small independent developer and have already privately invested $1.6M and
eight years of R & D in Satori 2D gaphics technology. The investment required to
sponsor new versions is relatively insignificant and we welcome any interest in this
new business opportunity.

If you would like to find out more about investing in a Satori version for Linux, BeOS
or MacOSX and/or would like to nominate a suitable investor/publisher then please email
me directly.

Regards,

Mark Lister
Sales & Marketing Director / New business Development
MarkLister@satoripaint.com
