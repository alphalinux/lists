Now, you don't mention which version this is but I'm assuming 6.0? There's
a problem with the installer image in 6.0 that causes it to sig 11 when
selecting individual packages.
 
> why is redhat so annoying? I mean I select networking utilities and kernel

Calm down, the sig 11 thing has been around since almost day one. Most of
the problems with 6.0 are listed in the errata.
http://www.redhat.com/errata

From there you can get the updated ramdisk that sure cure the sig11
problem.

Yes, RedHat can be annoying, but we all make mistakes and don't think they
put the sig 11 bug in there just to annoy us!
