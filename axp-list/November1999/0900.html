On Thu, 18 Nov 1999, Nikita Schmidt wrote:

> On Mon, Nov 15, 1999 at 11:42:56PM -0500, Kevin McDonald wrote:
> > I've had my AS200 for a few months now, and I've noticed that the fan is
> > quite loud (the machine is easily 3x louder than a standard modern PC). Is
> > this usual or might I have a problem with my fan? Is there anything I can
> > do to make it quieter (it's really kind of annoying). Thanks for the help.
>
> Check the air flow is not obstructed with dust or something. The less
> efficient the cooling is, the faster and louder the fan spins.
>
> You may also want to try the wtint kernel patch, which cools the CPU down when
> it's idle (like on the x86). It may or may not be in the 2.3 series (I haven't
> check it for a while), but you can find it anyway for both 2.2 and 2.3 at
> ftp://genie.ucd.ie/pub/alpha/patches-*.
>
> This patch will only work if your PALcode supports the wtint PALcall. SRM does
> support it (if you don't go via MILO); MILO generally does not, although recent
> versions of the Avanti MILO support wtint on AS255. It might just work on the
> AS200 as well, though...
>
