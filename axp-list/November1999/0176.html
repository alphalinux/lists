unviable on Alpha. I asked some questions about this package - development
tools in particular. They told me a C compiler was included. I would also
need Fortran90. For DVF on Tru64 I was told I would still have to pay $1600
(minus 20% edu discount). This still puts the 'cost of ownership' much
higher than Linux.
