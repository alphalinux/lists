EV7 and EV8 will MOST PROBABLY have FP-SIMD instructions. At least Compaq
Alpha people from the States approved me stating that they WILL have them
in my DECUS presentation a month ago in Singapore. It would sould good if
the initial EV7 could offer say 5 GFLOPs peak FP single precision
throughput, as EV7 has 12.8 Gbytes/s main memory bandwidth per CPU (8
parallel Direct Rambus channels) plus enormous on-die full-speed L2 cache,
so you could actually sustain most of those peak gigaflops...

Of course, we have to hope for the best when finalized EV7 21364 is
announced. Maybe others can give the feedback to the list and Compaq itself
how useful would they find having SIMD-FP on Alpha (maybe even retrofitted
onto EV68 0.18 um 21264...)
