The word processor is fine. However, it doesn't seem to support "true-type"
fonts, so large characters are pixel-ish.
The spreadsheet functions well, but I noticed that when I resize the window,
it redraws the entire spreadsheet area over and over again, maybe 50 or 100
times, which can be very slow.
The presentation package strikes me as fairly difficult to use. And bullets
never seem to convert properly.
Overall, I guess I was hoping for it to "do everything Office97 does," so i
was disappointed.
It doesn't support transparent images and doesn't use the same key strokes
(you know, like for "copy" and "paste"), among other small things.
Just don't expect that much and you'll probably be impressed.
