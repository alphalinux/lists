A litttle misunderstanding ( and some typical Intel spin) is at work here.
"coppermine", despite it's name is NOT a copper processor!!!

It is still an aluminum technology CPU. What is different with it from a
previous P3 is the integrated 256K L1 cache, running at full clock, and
with better optimizations.
