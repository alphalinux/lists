I have ApplixWare. It does everything I need it to do (only the Word,
Spreadsheet, and Draw programs - I can't speak for the database stuff).
Many of the shortcuts are different from M$ Office and some of the dialog
box stuff takes getting used to, but I have no complaints. As a matter of
fact, the text file import abilities on the Spreadsheet have been a
godsend for me.
