For NT, first install the PnP service; it's on the
NT cdrom in the drvlib\pnpisa\alpha\ directory.

After that you can install the AWE64. The drivers
for PnP SoundBlasters are also on de NT cdrom; just
look in the drvlib\audio\sbpnp\alpha directory.

Good luck,
