Hit the "F2" key during power-up selftest, that should get you into setup.
You can then change the boot path.

That is if you are in AlphaBios, Are you sure your not in SRM and have the
console set to serial?

if the F2 does not work connect a emulator or terminal to the serial port on
the box and hit a few carriage returns, if it echo's back ">>>" your in srm,
serial console. Just type "set console graphics" and it will switch back to
the graphics head after an init. In srm a "set console serial" will switch
the console to the serial port.

From the banner it looks like that may have happened.
