After hanging out on the FreeBSD Alpha list, Debian Alpha, and chatting via email, I have one really big honkin' question..

What made people on this list choose Red Hat as their Linux? This is not a question of challenge, I like Red Hat, but I want to know what is that drove YOU to install it over Debian, Turbo, Stampede, or SuSE.. Is it the way Red Hat supports Linux? The way it runs? The way it installs? I'm really anxious to know everyones' opinion. Certainly you must know that some of the die-hard users of other distros think their's is "true to the meaning of Linux" and not-so-commercial..
