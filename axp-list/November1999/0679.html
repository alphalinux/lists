The kernel will not build with ccc. Nor will it ever build
with ccc given that I don't think ccc will ever accept
gcc "asm" syntax. (Don't take that statement to be cast in stone).
I also doubt that there would be much of a benefit from building
the kernel with ccc.

As has been stated here before, the biggest bang for the buck
with ccc+cpml (vs gcc) is with floating point apps. With integer apps, your
"milage may vary".
