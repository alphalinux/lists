"Dr. David Gilbert" wrote:

> Hi,
> I'm looking for a better graphics card for my Alpha (I currently have
> the Matrox Millenium 2 with 4MB). Looking in one of my local PC dealers
> (www.microdirect.co.uk) I see they are offering a 'Creative Blaster Savage
> 4 32MB Retail PCI' (Based on the S3 Savage chip) and a '3Dfx Voodoo 3 2000
> 16MB PCI'
>

As I recall, the Savage 4 chip is *only* supported on x86 Linux systems...
As for the Voodoo 3 card,. this is from the XFree86 FAQ:

"Support for some of the Voodoo boards has been added to the 3.3.5 release.
The work of Daryll Strauss has been integrated in this release but you might
want to directly check his server at
http://glide.xxedgexx.com/3DfxRPMS_vb_glibc.html. "

I personally haven't had any luck with either card in my Alpha with 3.3.5.
I've also downloaded and compiled the most recent pre-4.0 release in case it
had better support, but I haven't had any luck with that...

My best experience with video card on an Alpha is a Permedia2 card I have
which worked flawlessly with the 3DLabs server. I've also used an S3 Virge
card with the SVGA server...

Good luck... And if you do get one of those cards working, please let me
know. Thanks.

Adam Kirchhoff
