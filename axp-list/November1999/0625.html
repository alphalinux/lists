Shane,

Oops . . . sorry to get cut off. I agree that Linux can hold it's own
in any testing. The real test is the application you use. If you are
talking web servers then you must look at the methods used by the
application. Does it take advantage of Threads? SMP? etc. You will
notice that Apache 1.3.x and Stronghold are not threaded and do not take
full advantage of SMP. However, Zeus does on both counts. Linux is
solid. Better to look toward your application.
