From my experience, using generic SIMMs on Alpha boxes doesn't work
so well. I've purchased lots generic ECC SIMMs for various Alpha's, and
they either don't work or work poorly. Your best bet is to buy
SIMMs that are specific to your box. Most good memory vendors carry
SIMMs for older DEC Alpha systems. And yes, my AS200 SIMMs didn't work
in the Multia. Nor would they work in the Jensen. But all of these
*should* take the same SIMM in principle.

FWIW,

Michael
