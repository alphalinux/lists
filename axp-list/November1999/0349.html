Hi Paul,

I used to have problems with a ZLXp-E2 in a Miata in
the past so I switched to an Elsa Gloria Syn.

As far as I remember, there were no picture
in console mode, so I had to ">>> set console serial".

Do you have a picture on your graphics head?
If yes, double-check wether we talk about the
same graphics option: the ZLXp-E2 is an old
full-lenth PCI card whereas a PowerStorm
3D30 (TGA2) is shorter and is not supported
under Linux.

If you really have a ZLXp-E2 then you might
have to remove VGA out of our kernel to
give way to TGA.

Good luck!

Regards,
