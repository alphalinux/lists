On Tue, 30 Nov 1999, Kristinn E. Arnarsson wrote:

Wait a minute, why are you trying to load MILO from SRM? You can do this
but it seems the long way round. If you've got SRM already then you can
boot Linux with the aboot. I suggest you take a look through
http://www.alphalinux.org/faq/srm.html

The idea is that you use BSD disklabels to partition the disk (use the 'b'
option in fdisk) then place aboot onto the first cylinder of the disk
(directions are in the faq). This is much easier than loading MILO from
SRM.....at least in my mind.
