I can't (yet) speak to the graphics card, but neither the IDE CDROM nor
anything Adaptec 294X will work for Tru64. These are not supported by SRM.
Look to a NCR-chipset SCSI adapter. A (relatively) easy way to check is to
boot your system to its SRM console (if you haven't done this yet, please
check the HOWTOs are read the READMEs. Swithcing consoles is not easy on
all systems, and returning to your presumably currently working configuation
may be a challenge.

...Mike
