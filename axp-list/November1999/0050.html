The replies seem to want to educate me about the many ways that these
warnings may be spurious messages for perfectly good code, or that I
suggested the wrong way to fix these, or that I used the wrong compiler,
or how to fix my clock. Fine. That's not my point. Among other things,
I didn't build the rpms to install them (not after spending months trying
to get a semi-stable 6.0 system), just to find out where problems may
be lurking.

My point is that these messages often indicate real problems, and that
there are one hell of a lot of them. And I see enough "unaligned access"
messages in /var/log/messages and crashes of various tools from RH 6.0 to
indicate that there are real problems lurking among these many warning
messages. People don't have to address that point in their replies,
but I have a feeling that my previous mail to the list didn't make that
point clearly enough.
