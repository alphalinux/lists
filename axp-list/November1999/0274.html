On Sat, 6 Nov 1999, Mark Abene wrote:

> I'm going out on a limb:
> Is anyone on this list using an ISA sound card (like a SB16 PnP) which *isn't*
> locking up their whole machine with frequent use?

I have an XL300 with an ISA SB16Val that works perfectly. I'm running Raw
Hide 103199 and 2.2.13, but it was OK with RH6.0 (well, as good as
anything was with RH6.0 --yuck.)

However, I had so many lockups with my PC164 that I really couldn't
isolate the sound card as a problem. It locked up with or without it. This
was with RH6.0 or Raw Hide. I ended up putting the damn thing in a corner
and forgetting about it for a while. BTW, the PC164 ran fine with DU
4.0F, and I swapped every piece of hardware in the system, so I doubt a
hardware problem. It would lock up under linux when starting X, or when
using the ethernet card, or when playing a sound. Something with IO?
