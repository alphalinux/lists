> Hmmm...I was unable to get the AHA-2940UW to work on my Miata with the
> SRM BIOS. It works under Alpha BIOS, though.

It works under ARC on the PC164.

bapper@piratehaven.org said:
> So far I haven't seen any "flakiness" with a 2.2.10 kernel. This may
> be due to the fact that I haven't really put much of a load on the
> machine.

I had two disks on the internal 68 pin connector. It seemed to take
having both disks going for trouble to occur. And furthermore, the
activity needed to be writes. ... as far as I could tell, 'though it
might just have needed to be "complex" concurrent behavior.

I would get an error from both the drives, the driver would reset the
board, and that's the end. Ctrl-Alt-SysRq started the reboot, but MILO
would lock up when it detected the board.
