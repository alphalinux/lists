This was happening to me here as well. For what it is worth, everything
seemed to settle down here once I upgraded the fontserver (xfs) to the
version from the "Rawhide" distribution. I don't think it is a problem
with gmc at all. The X lockups when opening some files with xpdf or gv
which were *really* annoying have all disappeared.

The only ongoing problem I have noticed is that "upgrading" to RH 6.0
broke em86 and therefore my ability to run Netscape. That's not to say
that there are no other problems, just that I haven't come across them
so far.
