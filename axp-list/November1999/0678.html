I have had to boot with a 2.0.35 kernel a few times in order to pass stuff to
my alpha over the network (becasue of all the network card/driver problemsw/
6.0) I find that it is NOT stable at all. It probably is just me, however, it
will log me out after about 20 -40 seconds, It begins outputting to the
monitor info from the netowrk card, Ibelieve, and eventually (if I wait
long enough) makes it so that I am unable to even enter any text or login at
all. I would say that it is, in my experience, very unstable with the 2.0.
kernels.
