The problem isn't in portmapper...it's in the the initscripts (can't recall
the
filename but here's a guess /etc/rc.d/init/functions?). awk hangs on the
Jensen
and this causes the search for existing pid's to fail when starting up
services.
Red Hat is aware of the problem but doesn't have a Jensen to test on. I
suspect
a glibc or kernel bug since awk works fine on other Alpha's. The Jensen has
some
oddities and has some special support in glibc and the kernel regarding I/O
ports
and memory access.
