That's the latest (last) official release. However, if you grab one of the
unofficial VMS kits for the Multia (aka UDB) at
http://www.openvms.digital.com/freeware/MULTIA/ , you'll get a later,
experimental, SRM with a lot of ordinarily hidden features. The backside is,
you'll have to have access to a VMS system to unpack the kit.

Anyway, I run this SRM on my Multia for a couple of months, no problems so
far. (This Multia triple-boots VMS, Linux and WindowsNT).
