Paul,

From the banner, you are already in SRM, you just aren't getting to the
console prompt or autobooting into the OS. While the DS10 "technically"
supports NT via the AlphaBIOS 5.7 firmware console, your system is
currently not in that mode.

What is the last change you made to this system before it was rebooted? Is
this a new installation?
Reseat your SODIMMS and make sure you are using your PCI slots in the
correct order for your boot devices. Video gets PCI slot 0, etc. Make sure
your CPU card and add on boards are properly seated and that the cooling
fans are all spinning.

Good luck.
