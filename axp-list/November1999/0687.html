I remember having this problem as well: it had something to do
with the netscape (dns helper) program not working totally under
Linux. I fixed it by pointing netscape at a proxy server (in my
case, squid running on the same box). Worked fine after that. In
fact, after month or so I stopped using the proxy and it's been
happy ever since (though i may well have upgraded something
else that made it happy). If you haven't got the resources for
something like squid then junkbuster or the TiS fwtk proxies
are small and work well (although fwtk is a bit old now and seems
to have some problems with java).
