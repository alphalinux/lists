I also used to have an adaptec 2940UW card in my alpha here and had similar
problems... whenever there was a write to 2 disks at the same time it would
reset the entire bus. Sometimes it was so bad that it would just keep
reseting the bus. I went to a DataStorm 32+ (SYM 53c8xx based) and it's
better, but now my problem is just related to the fact that I'm using IBM
drives on the controller which the alpha doesn't like. The only drives I get
timeouts on now are the IBMs

Kurt

164SX 533MHz
kernel 2.2.12 (saw the problems on this kernel and any other kernel I tried
when I had the adaptec board in there)
