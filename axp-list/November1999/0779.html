Sorry, but the stuff in the withviewer archive is everything I have left for
the Alpha. I had to delete most of the tree almost immediately after the
initial build to free space for some Real Work (tm).

Maybe the build at ftp.mozilla.org has this library in a usable form.

> Thanks for your time. I've tried building this on my own, but it
> invariably dies after filling up all 500MB of free space on the build
> partition. On the flip side, I /did/ have a libgtkmozilla at that point...

Try building without the news, mail, and editor components and (especially)
without the tests. You can squeeze down the space you need for the build quite
a bit this way:

./configure --disable-editor --disable-mailnews --disable-tests

This *should* fit on your remaining hard drive space, as I don't think I had
*that* much free while building.

> Incidentally, simplebrowser supposedly has a stop button. But I
> can't check this myself. ;-)

I've also built the beast on my Celeron (where I have plenty of HD space). No
stop button for simplebrowser. :)
