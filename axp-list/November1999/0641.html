>>> Todd Henderson said:
> I found a timing difference between Linux and Tru64. However, it existed even
> when we moved the Tru64 exec. over to the Linux box. On one of our codes,
> there is a 10% hit for using Linux, on the other, there is a 20% hit. These
> are CFD codes.

These differences are probably attributable to either or both of:

1. page coloring for the physical caches
2. granularity hints to minimize TLB thrashing

Tru64 does both, LINUX (currently) does neither.

We are investigating solutions, but have nothing to offer (yet).
