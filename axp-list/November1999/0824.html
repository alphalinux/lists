> >Also, halt() isn't exported to modules even though the call path:
> > __get_free_pages (include/linux/mm.h:381) -->

It doesn't actually CALL halt(), but the macro does include it. Since
halt() is a non-exported function this means modules refuse to load
due to unresolved symbol(s).

I personally think there is no real reason to export halt() to
modules - a simpler solution would be to just do something
like "(int *) 0=0;" in page.h:BUG()
