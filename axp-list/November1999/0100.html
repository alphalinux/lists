> With Coppermine only slightly slower at a given clock rate,
> there is little incentive to buy Athlon. (except those folks who
> despise Intel)

We have drifted off topic, but note that the Athlon does much better on
floating point than the Coppermine at the same clock. Integer speeds and fp
speeds are often not the same; since the average business customer cares
more about integer, you will see a lot of comparisons which are integer
comparisons that don't state that they are integer comparisons.

You get the same situation with Alphas: "Alphas are much faster than Intel
chips" "Um, no, they aren't, look at this integer benchmark..."
