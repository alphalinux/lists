FYI,

I got a DE-422 ethernet card going on my Jensen this weekend.
For those of you who are having trouble, here is the solution.
This card is so old it shipped with the jumper configured for
10base2. To get 10baseT look for a big blue "lego" jumper.
You'll see the settings for "TP" and "Thin Net". Set it for
"TP" and you should be fine. You can either compile in support
or use the depca driver.
