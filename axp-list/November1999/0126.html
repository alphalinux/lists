FYI,

The clock program is obsolete and has been removed in Red Hat 6.1.
Instead hwclock is used, as on x86 platform. I have tested hwclock
on my Alpha boxes, including the venerable Jensen, and it works
perfectly. It takes the same options as clock for backwards
compatibility plus has a few other nice hooks. You can get it
from the RawHide area on the RH site. You'll need updates time
and initscripts to use it properly.
