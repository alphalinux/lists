With regards to your message at 12:32 AM 11/6/99 +0900, Iwao Makino. Where
you stated:

>Luigi,
>
> > I've seen that I could assemble UP1000 or UP2000 based alphas for a
> > reasonable price. However, before attempting it I would like to have some
> > suggestions. In particular, why posts in this list almost never concern
> > them ?? Any particular reason ? I can't believe they work so perfectly
> > and that installation is so easy that none has problems with them...
>Yes, UP1000 is not out to market yet. UP2000 just start to ship, but API
>still? don't have FCC certified enclosures and power supply listed on the
>web page so not many can purchase parts to assemble them.
The fact that API has not published this is fairly irrelevant here, as far
as I can see.
In the past most integrators/OEMs of Alpha have bought cases from a number
of sources.
In our case we manufacture our own, and they are CSA and FCC tested and
approved.
The main thing is that the UP1000 is indeed not out yet, and the 2000 only
started to ship last month. It IS a nice machine, less quirky than the
DS20/DP264, adn a fair bit less money.
Still, the UP2000 is a dual 21264 machine, and as such is not a cheap machine.
