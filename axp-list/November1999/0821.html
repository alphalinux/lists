The 2100 is supported with either ev4 or ev5 cpu's. These are the original 2100s with 8 EISA slots and 3 PCI slots, built in 10Mb ethernet and built in SCSI narrow.

The 2100a is not supported. These were updated to have 8 PCI slots and 3 EISA slots. This is the reason these do not work. Different architecture which no one has worked out and submitted.
