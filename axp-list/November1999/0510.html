Hi,

I don't mean to make an obvious statement, but sometimes you need to use the
SCSI adapters low level format to get the adapter and the drives to play nice
together (at least according to documentation of all the Apdaptec controllers I
have used.) Of course, this is not a trivial task with an in use drive, but with
a new drive it is worth a try.
