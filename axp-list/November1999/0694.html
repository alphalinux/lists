This is an old board, the firmware supports the NCR/Symbios 53C810,
'53C825, QLogic 1020 (but not the 1020A), Adaptec 7770, Adaptec 7880 and
Mylex DAC960P. None of these are "ultra" SCSI III controllers, they are
SCSI II Fast wide (Fast 10, not Fast 20). Remember, these are boot
controllers, you can use whatever you want for a secondary controller since
this will be controlled by NT, not the firmware.

Good luck,

--Kirk
