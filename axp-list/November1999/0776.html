Larry Snyder wrote:

> Hi all --
> This may be slightly OT, as it's probably not Alpha-specific, but it
> _is_ an axp box in question here....
>
> I've been using a PS/2-compatible M$ mouse w/ XF 3.3.5 on an SX
> w/ a 2.2.5 kernel. Someone gave me an IntelliMouse today. It's
> detected as a PS/2 at bootup, and behaves normally as long as I
> leave the X config alone. I tried several protocols there, none of
> which worked. From the docs at the xf86 website, I may want one
> called "xxxPS/2" (haven't seen if the xxx is supposed to be replaced
> by anything). Specifying 'Auto' returns an 'unable to determine'
> error, and 'xxxPS/2' returns 'unsupported by OS'. I looked at my
> kernel .config, and didn't see anything that seemed like it would
> apply.
>
> Has anyone been-there-done-that?

Yes, on Intel. For gpm, use ms3 as the protocol. In XF86Config, use
IntelliMouse as the protocol, Buttons 5, and ZAxisMapping 4 5. It
probably works on alpha too, haven't tried.
