justin bailey writes:
> sorry in advance if this is a faq, but i looked around the alaska archives
> and didn't see anything.
> what do we have that will decode mp3s (not for playback, but for
> conversion to wavs, for example)? neither mpg123 nor cdparanoia nor sox
> seem to support this- do any of the encoders? or is there some other
> tool which performs this function?

mpg123 can dump raw audio to stdout.. You can than use sox to generate
a wav header.
In addition, xaudio (shareware from www.xaudio.com) supports direct
conversion to wav.
