On Tue, 16 Nov 1999, Champigny, Michael wrote:

> You will find other problems, even if you do manage to get awk
> working. I've had hangs on numerous apps (including most GTK based
> apps, kvt, etc.). RH on the Jensen is extremely unstable at best. Oh,
> and did I mention, the stock kernel shipped with RH6.0 and 6.1 doesn't
> work? You'll need to either build your own or get a pre-built one from
> someone else. Basically, it's a losing battle. But good luck anyway!

Well... that's my luck :-)

I'm not discouraged yet. I'm new to Linux on Alpha, but I have a few years
of experience with Linux on Intel. I have to count on this. Despite your
bets, I still hope to get a pretty stable system with my Jensen. I don't
need many applications to run, this machine will be just a server for a
very small company. Basically, I need only a web server, ftp, mail, dns,
ipmasq (is it supported on alpha, btw?), squid and samba. I don't need X
and any other applications. Once it is configured, nobody will play with
it anymore. So, I hope to get to a stable configuration for these
services, then stay with it.

I know the stock kernel from RH is not working. I compiled my own, very
small :-)

Besides, I don't believe in lost battles. Neither in smooth installations.
I still remember those good old days when you had to build your Linux all
by yourself :-) I loved those times! Besides, the support I received on
this list is great, I count on it in the future too. Thank you to all of
you!
