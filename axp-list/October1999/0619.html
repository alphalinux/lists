I talked to one of the tech guys at digital a while ago and he told me that
the SDRAMS were 83Mhz 3.3v 168pin DIMMS ECC. This is for a PWS 433a/au. There
was a user outside the US who sent me the a company he got memory from. It
was a little expensive (and this was before the recent jump). Hope that
helps. If you want to know his source mail me back and I will look it up.
