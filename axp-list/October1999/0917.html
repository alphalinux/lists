At 04:10 PM 11/1/99 -0500, you wrote:
>> I am looking for somthing that will handle multiple GigE cards
>> without bottlenecking to badly, and it would seem a 21264 with a 64bit
>> PCI card would be to only thing able to make a good showing.
>
>The usual bottleneck with GigE is lack of CPU. You aren't going to get more
>than 1 to run at more than 1/2 speed.
>
>The best TCP/IP result ever was 1.1 gigabits, but that was with a 21264 and
>myrinet. Most GigE cards get about half that, and use up all the CPU. And as
>you should know, parallelizing enough of the network stack to get benefit
>from multiple CPUs is hard, and hasn't been done to Linux.
