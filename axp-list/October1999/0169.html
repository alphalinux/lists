> Eh? 10 MB of what??? How did you arrived to that conclusion.

1) I pulled the RAM. I have two 16MB SIMMs.
2) When SRM boots, it shows the devices and reports "32 Meg of system
memory."
3) When I type "show memory" it reports "32 Meg of system memory."

>From these three observations I believe that there really are 32MB RAM
in the computer.

4) When I boot MILO it reports "Memory size is 24MBytes"
5) When I boot Linux /proc/kmem is 24MB in size

>From these two observations I believe that MILO/Linux are under the
impression that there are 24MB RAM.

In my first post I did a "duh" and took the available memory from "top."

I may be blind, but I'm not finding anything in any FAQ that states that
the Multia chews up 8MB "just because." I'm still going through the PDF
manual.

If I had more RAM in this machine, I'd not cry over 8MB, but with 32,
that's 1/4 of the entire RAM, which has me concerned.
