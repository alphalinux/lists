OK, if you are sure that sda1 is your root filesystem then try this:

boot sda1:/boot/vmlinux-2.2.5-16 root=/dev/sda1

this should work for RedHat 6.0, provided sda1 really is your root
filessytem. You can also do an ls sda1: to take a look at the filesystem.
If MILO says it can't read the fs on that device then your root fs is
elsewhere.

--rdp
