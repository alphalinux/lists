Well, this is making me feel like a Linux newbie. :-)

I downloaded the iso image for RH 6.0 from ftp.alphalinux.org, copied
the generic.img and ramdisk.img files to floppies and tried to boot
our test AS2100 with them following the boot instructions for SRM
console in the RH alpha install manual.

The system booted the kernel just fine and asks for the ramdisk
floppy. I put it in and hit enter to continue. The floppy drive
light comes on and about 4 seconds later I get a kernel panic flashed
on the screen and am dumped back to the P000> SRM prompt.

The RH manuals are less than useful at this point and I can't seem to
find anything on the Alphalinux site about this problem. I plan on
burning the iso image on a CD Monday and trying to isntall from CD to
see if that works better.

Is there a website with more details about this install process?
