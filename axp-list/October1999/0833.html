Assuming the system in question is the Linux ready flavor(dj-pb10a-)..yes.
According to Compaq the system mentioned would also have been ordered and
shipped with the scsi controller (kzpba-ca or kzpcm-da), one of their scsi
disks, and a 3x-bn50a-01. If this criteria has been met, then the system is
supported.
