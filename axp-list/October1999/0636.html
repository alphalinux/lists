If you read the man pages for ccc... it cautions about
using -O3 or -O4... as in certain cases, those
optimizations can slow things down. It may be safer to
use -O2 unless you know for sure the optimizations in
-O3 and above will benefit your code.

Or generally, you can just do a -fast.
