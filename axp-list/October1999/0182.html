I needed to try something out on 2.3.20, so I took rth's 2.3.18-CVS patch
and tweaked it for the stock 2.3.20. I'm not sure I got everything right -
I really don't understand the PCI changes in the first place, and its
a little worrisome that one of the patched functions (pci_assign_resource)
disappeared between 2.3.18 and 2.3.20. Anyway, it compiles and boots for
me so maybe someone will find it useful until an official patch is released.
