        Cable modems USUALLY just provide an ethernet connection
        to your system and farm out IP addresses via DHCP or
        other methods.

        You'd need two ethernet ports. One from the cable modem
        to the Multia and from the Multia to the rest of your
        home LAN and route between the two cards.

        I suspect the cable modem guys would look at you like
        a deer caught in the headlights when you tell them it's
        an Alpha NT system. :) I suspect if you set up the card
        for DHCP and just plug it in and renew the IP address, it'll
        just work.
