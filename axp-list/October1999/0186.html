Hi,
It seems that scsi adapter are recognized in the order that can be found
in
    linux/drivers/scsi/hosts.c
           in the
    Scsi_Host_Template_builtin_scsi_hosts
           array.

Just change the order so that it comes after the one you want
first.

Cheers
gaspar
