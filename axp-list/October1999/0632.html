I am using a FireGL 1000 Pro on an Alpha 500a PWS and a FireGL 1000 (not
Pro) on an Alpha Station 255. I am using the 3D Labs X. The 1000 Pro is
on a Digital (Mitsubishi OEM) 21" monitor and I do not have any issues. In
troublshooting, the most important aspect is to have the right monitor
setting in X. If you are having issues from the moment you turn on your
machine you may want double check that you have the latest BIOS. Go to
www.diamondmm.com and install the latest BIOS for that card.
