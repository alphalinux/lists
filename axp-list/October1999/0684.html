> You are really forgetting about I/O. Alpha servers generally have
> much better memory & I/O susbsystems than x86 boxes. When you're
> running a
> webserver, that is often more important than CPU speed.

I disagree. That strongly depends on what you're serving; in most cases, you
aren't doing anything that disk intensive. And the networking is never that
intense for web serving, unless you're either (1) running a meaningless
benchmark, or (2) paying much more for the bandwidth than the computer.

Alpha does have the best PCI performance in the low-end. But I'm probably
one of the few people exploiting it on an Alpha/Linux box.
