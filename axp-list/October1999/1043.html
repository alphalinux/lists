> I've looked through the archive and the docs on www.alphalinux.org, but
> I'm still a little fuzzy on how I would go about upgrading my sx164 to
> the latest SRM (it has ARC now).
>

> ftp://gatekeeper/pub/DEC/Alpha/firmware/v5.5/alphapc164sx/ but I'm not
> really certain where to go from here.
>
> I'm guessing I mcopy fwupdate.exe to a fat-formatted floppy, but do I
> need any of the other files as well? I'd rather not hose my new box
> before I even get the chance to start the Red Hat install. :-)

Ugh... I hate to reply to my own message, but it appears my system is
not an SX164, it's a plain PC164. The machine was sold to me as an SX,
and I didn't think to verify that.

I was able to switch to SRM by getting the files from the correct
directory on Gatekeeper, copying fwupdate.exe and the .rom file to a
fat-formatted floppy, and selecting "Update Firmware" in the ARC
console.

Thanks very much anyway to those who replied,
