> Can anyone tell me what the default sound settings are for the MSS sound
> card in an AS200? (irq, io address, DMA, etc.. ) ?

The codec is an AD1848 (MSS). Default settings were the following,
last time I checked:

iobase=0x530
irq=7
dma=3
