        Burned a copy of the 6.1 SRPMs the other day, and began the
process of rebuilding the distro. Unlike the 6.1 beta, the glibc2 and
binutils seem to have no serious problems building under RH 6.0/axp -
one modification to the spec file was needed for glibc. I've not
finished building the entire distro yet (hey, my box is only 500Mhz..),
but I suspect it will do so successfully.
        This of course begs the question of why RH has not yet released
the 6.1 Alpha build. From some chatter in linux.ports.alpha, it appears
that RH will not release 6.1 for the Alpha architecture; the supported
hardware list appears to verify that:

" Red Hat Linux 6.1 (Cartman)
     * Intel Hardware
     * SPARC Hardware "
      
Was there an official annoucement about this which I missed? SuSE is
looking better and better by the day..
