Greetings,

I've recently started trying to get an old AlphaPC64 board that I own
up and running. I've had the machine for a while but a lack of time
and equipment forced me to shelve the project for a few months.

Anyway, when I was initially playing with it, I remember at some point
running the SRM console on it. Since then I've flashed in MILO, which,
as I recall, over-wrote the spot that SRM occupied in flash.

Now, I'd like to switch back to the SRM console so that I can net-boot
the machine (and because I prefer SRM, in general). But, it's
definitely not on my flash-rom anymore, and I've been un-able to
locate an SRM firmware image for the Cabriolet/AlphaPC64/EB64+ on any
of the various Compaq/DEC sites (including
http://ftp.digital.com/pub/DEC/Alpha/firmware/).

Does anyone have any ideas where I could find the firmware image? Am I
just missing it on the Digital firmware site? Is it filed under a
different name? Does such a beast even exist anymore?

Any assistance you could provide would be greatly appreciated.
