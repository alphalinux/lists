To find out about the processors, "cat /proc/cpuinfo". As for System.map, copy
the one from /usr/src/linux (or wherever the root of the kernel source you
compiled was) to /boot/System-2.x.x.map or whatever kernel version you
compiled. That way, the system will choose the proper System.map file.
