> Hi all. I'm having several problems compiling 2.2.12 on my XLT300, and
> wondered if there were any specific patches that were required for it?
None that I know of. I have had luck with Alan's 2.2.13pre[14-15] on my
systems recently.

In general, you can find Alpha-specific patches at
ftp://ftp.twiddle.net/pub/rth/ or ftp://ftp.alphalinux.org/patches/dfrasnel/
before they are rolled into official kernel releases. The second one
is mine, and has not really been touched since July; sorry, daytime job
cut into my hacking time ;) Rich Henderson's (rth) archive seems to be
down at the moment as well.

I would grab the 2.2.13pre15 patch, apply it to fresh 2.2.12 sources
and go from there. If you see the same problem, let me know and I'll
dig into it further.
