The AS2100 work. IIRC in the 2.1.x series kernels you had separate kernels for EV4 and EV5 cpus. I do not know the status of AS2100 on the newer kernels. The AS2100A last I heard were not linux friendly yet. I have heard of success on the AS4100 but do not know the particulars. Our AS4100 all run Tru64 or OpenVMS. Good luck.
