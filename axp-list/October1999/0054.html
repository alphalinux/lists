I bought a basic SB16 ISA card (wanted to save my PCI slots for better
uses) and it works perfectly. Also on the recommendation of the list I
have been playing with cdparanoia and lame to make mp3s, does a very good
job indeed. Last weekend I also stuck a WinTV PCI card in and got xawtv
running along with alevt which gives me teletext too. The only thing
really lacking is indeed real audio/video support but that is something to
take up with real. Personally I would rather support open standards so
mpeg video and audio is a better choice.
