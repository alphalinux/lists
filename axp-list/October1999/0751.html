After installing Red Hat 6.0 on Alpha you need to visit
http://www.alphalinux.org/docs/redhat6.0-probs.shtml and download some
alpha.rpm files. Your symptoms are classic and I wondered what the heck was
happening too! For example, your PING problems will be fixed when you
download this:

<--snip from URL above -->

netkit-base:

Symptom: Ping generated unaligned traps and erroneously reported receiving
of wrong data
Solution: Download an updated netkit-base-0.10-30.alpha.rpm and upgrade the
package.

<--snip-->

Another good place to search for wierdness is http://support.redhat.com in
the knowledge base. I've had many questions answered there! If you ftp to
ftp.alphalinux.org and go to redhat/6.0/updates you will find the handful of
Alpha RPMs to upgrade your system. It's a great site!
