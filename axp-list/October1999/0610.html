> Sounds to me like you don't have the kernel modules for your es1868 sound
> card

Its actually an ES1688 codec, there is no 1868 afaik.

>From the .config on my 500au:
CONFIG_SOUND=m
CONFIG_SOUND_OSS=m
CONFIG_SOUND_SB=m
CONFIG_SOUND_ADLIB=m
CONFIG_SOUND_MPU401=m

and the commands to load the modules:
insmod soundcore
insmod sound
insmod uart401
insmod sb io=0x220 irq=9 dma=1 dma16=5 mpu_io=0x330 esstype=1688

(note that the irq may differ; config it under srm with isacfg).
