> >Assuming the system in question is the Linux ready flavor

Ummm, no. The KZPBA-CA and KZPBA-CX are fundamentally the same card
(QLogic ISP1040B). Trust me...I have combinations of both of them all
over the place and they all work with the same drivers.

>
> From past experiences I doubt if Compaq themselves are

> in the habit of sticking in anything that vaguely matches
> the product description.
>

Right now if you order a KZPBA-CA, you get a KZPBA-CX. Like I said,
they are the same card. Someone probably changed a firmware rev. or
something, and hence a new part number. Regardless, if the ds10 didn't
install correctly following the given install data (like what Jay just
sent out) there is another problem. Wierd tho' that it didn't work
before...hmm...
