On Mon, 18 Oct 1999, Shaffer, Earl (CAP, GEFA, Contractor) wrote:

> I am trying to de-install RH 5.2 from a shared client PC. Although I thought
> I did, when the PC (a laptop) reboots, I get a load error as though a piece
> of Linux is still there. Maybe LILO? In any case, it is in limbo now (no OS
> at all).
>
> Any ideas?

I suppose this is a i386 machine, so you can use a DOS boot diskette. You
should also have FDISK.EXE on this diskette, so you can boot DOS and then
use FDISK /MBR to remove lilo from master boot record and install DOS
loader instead.
