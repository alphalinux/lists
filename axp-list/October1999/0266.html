On 13-Oct-99 Blake Carlson wrote:
> I tried doing killall X and killall xinit. Those processes are gone and
> it's still not responding??!!!?? None of the remaining processes look
> like the culprit. Am I destined to reboot?!! (I'm really sad .....)

As long as they're really dead (killall without the -9 option sometimes won't
really kill X if it's hung), you might try restarting X from the remote
terminal. Sometimes this wakes the video up again.

At least, it worked the one time I tried it. ;)
