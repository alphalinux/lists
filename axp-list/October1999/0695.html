Phillip Ezolt wrote:
>
> Greg,
> You are really forgetting about I/O. Alpha servers generally have
> much better memory & I/O susbsystems than x86 boxes. When you're running a
> webserver, that is often more important than CPU speed.
>

Then how come the box being pushed uses EIDE?? (I know
there is a SCSI option but Compaq SCSI prices are high
in a price sensitive situation)

I suspect putting in some Athlons or PIII's is the
way to go for web apps. Even with EIDE on those boxes
things fly and SCSI is cheap for them if you want/need
it. And, as Greg mentioned, buying bandwidth into the
Internet does exceed the box cost quickly. My local provider
lists 1.5Mb (T1-SDSL) at $500 a month. That's pretty damn
cheap for T1 speed and it still catches the box cost quickly,
of course depending on how many boxes you buy.

Then again, your people cost far outways both so just buy
what costs you the least people to run.
