Hello there...

I had posted this in the past but got no answer. For those who will answer that
this is a faq , it isn't . I have an SX164 , booting RedHat Linux 6.0 through
AlphaBios 5.66 . All updates are installed and everything seems to work fine...
Except from the time :-( . Alphabios complains about invalid system time and
clears CMOS settings . I suppose this is something concerning the 'clock'
package , or something relevant , since this problem appears under 2.2.7 2.2.12
and 2.3.18ac10 . However , MILO doesn't complain about any of this during
reboots . This is why I want to flash milo in place of Alphabios. I have tried
several times doing this , mostly using the EB164 fmu.gz from gatekeeper and
milo image 2.0.35-c5.5 . Is something wrong with this? SX164 is a variation of
EB164 , as stated during booting. However , MILO won't flash . If anyone knows
something about this please tell me... Setting the system time at every boot is
really annoying.
