JJ,

I have successfully updated Mylex RAID controllers relabelled by DEC/Compaq
when the following conditions were met:

Board rev C or higher (second EEPROM socket)
2 EEPROMs installed.

You may be able to get the second EEPROM module to provide the necessary
space for version 3 firmware, but Mylex is not particularly keen on this
once they learn it is an OEM through DEC/Compaq, not a retail version. I
was lucky enough to have a dead DAC960PD around to make the conversion
myself on a rev D board. The end result was indistiguishable from the
retail version I had purchased for my own use (firmware 3.51.9).

Good luck,

--Kirk
