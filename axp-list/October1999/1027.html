SCA is a different connection type for a SCSI bus
SCA -> Single connector adapter... basically it combines all the IO and
power into one connector... it's useful for RAID systems that need to have
hot swap the drives, or for us lazy people that only like to plug one thing
in for a drive.
pretty much any SCA UW drive will work
watch out for the IBM's though... there's been quite a good run on this list
about them NOT working too well on alpha (first hand experience right here)
