Wes Bauske wrote:
>
> Mark Holloway wrote:
> >
> > Ok, so I downlaoded/installed make and everything else that goes with
> > it...(except I couldn't find makeinfo).
>
> A quick search on Altavista says makeinfo is
> some sort of TeX utility.

Not really. This is a utility which writes info files (those
in your /usr/info/) from 'texinfo' sources. It is a part of
'texinfo' package. You may run these sources also through TeX
(and texi2html, and texi2man, and ... ) but this another story.
