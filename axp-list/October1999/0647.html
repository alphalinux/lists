For what it's worth, I use the Blackbox wm on my systems, occasionally
with the KDE panel if there's a need. While desktops provide the
benefit of a common 'look' across applications, I find that nothing
beats the efficiency of something you've customized.
But of course, that is just my personal opinion. Most every
Linux desktop environment is available for the Alpha, so I encourage
you to give many of them a spin until you find one you like.
