Hi,
It seems to me that Adaptec 2940UW cards depend on the right firmware
version
to work reliably. Maybe correct cabling is an issue too.
My adaptec is very sensitive about proper
termination , but once I installed external active
termination instead of using the harddrive's
I never had an error again, and performance is great !
(better than intraserver's IMHO)
But I must admit I've seen weird behaviour on similar Adaptec's too.
Maybe I'm just lucky .....

My config:
PC164 SX
128 Mb ram
1 Adaptec 2940 UW
Quantum Viking II 4.5 Gb
1 NCR clone (narrow/ultra)
CD-ROM , CD-RW
