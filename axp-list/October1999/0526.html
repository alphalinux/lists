2.2.13pre17 is doing *much* better on my machine that 2.2.12. I've
been having troubles with occasional hanging within a few (1-2) days
but this kernel has lasted 5 days and is showing no signs of letting
up. I don't want to reboot until I find out how long this kernel will
last, but it is looking like that will be a long wait.

I have an adaptec SCSI board, and I noticed that this version has
some adaptec driver updates, so that may be the fix that has me going.
I know that I produce a fair amount of SCSI traffic, and hangs used to
happen when disk traffic was at its worst.
