OK. This is a different problem than you described
previously. Perhaps you are having problems with
xfs (font server). How about editing your /etc/X11/XF86Config
file and changing:

FontPath "unix/:-1"

to

FontPath
"/usr/X11R6/lib/X11/fonts/misc,/usr/X11R6/lib/X11/fonts/75dpi,/usr/X11R6/lib/X11/fonts/100dpi,/usr/X11R6/lib/X11/fonts/misc,/usr/X11R6/lib/X11/fonts/Type1,/usr/X11R6/lib/X11/fonts/Speedo"

(All one line)
