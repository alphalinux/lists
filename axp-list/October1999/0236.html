I also own one of these, and there is (or was) an SRM; these were early OEM
boards, and were a "reference platform" for DU and VMS. I vaguely remember
calling for support, and getting it in the form of an image on an ftp site.
That may have been Digital, it was a while ago. I'm searching thru my
floppy archives for the image, if I find it I'll let folks know. I do not
know what rules govern copying it, since it was included with the PC64
AFAIK.

If anyone comes across it somewhere on the 'net, please send an email; I'd
rather play Quake with a trackball than continue looking thru old floppies.

...Mike
