> Is there anything significantly different in the configurations of your
> two boxes? Same SCSI and Ethernet models/revs? What kind of video?

The main differences:

500 mhz has video in the form of a ELSA Gloria Synergy, the 667 only has serial
console graphics.

500 mhz shows Qlogic ISP1020 for the scsi controller and the 667 shows ISP10X0.

The PAL code revisions and SRM versions are newer on the 667. Everything else
should be the same.
