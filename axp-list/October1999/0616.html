I got some 128mb sdram sticks from Crucial (Micron) a while ago for a
PWS500a. Seems to work fine. Service (shipping) was great. Price wasn't
too bad. (I got a quote from Great Lakes before ordering from
Crucial.... Just checked Crucial's site..price sure has changed! This
might not be a good lead afterall...)

I ordered through the web (www.crucial.com) & just specified the machine
(PWS500a). Product that came is CT16M72S4D10.18T66 128mb 168 pin dimm
16Mx72 sdram ECC 10ns.

Works fine in the 164SX too incidently. (Both are 66mhz sdram memory &
take dimms in pairs I think.)

Evan
