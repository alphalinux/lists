My experiences were that I had either made a typo in the SRM console
settings or that I was trying to boot an "incorrect" kernel (i.e. non-SMP
for my dual CPU DS-20). As stated, I also had little success with boot
images other than 2.2.12.

        The kernel at ftp://ftp.redhat.com/redhat/updates/6.0/images/alpha/
is dates 9-1-99, but I've no idea what release it is (HELLO RED HAT? How
'bout some useful filenames?) The ramdisk dates back to May, and I was
certain that a new ramdisk image was out as well. Perhaps a bit more
searching is in order, or a link from someone else on the list....
