I presume you want software (kernel) raid, rather than getting a raid
controller. I have been running my alpha in raid 5 for a good while
now, with nary a problem. We also use software mirroring on linux
intel at my workplace, with great success.

Do get the alpha raidtools and patches if you patch your kernel, as
the alpha raidtools included with RH6, while better than the standard
kernel raidtools, are not compatible. If you build a custom kernel
from standard kernel sources and don't apply the patches, you will not
be able to mount your raid volumes.

Sean
