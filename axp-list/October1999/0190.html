At least you managed to get it working.

Permedia2 cards should work. Haven't used one other
than an Elsa Gloria Synergy in my XP1000 for Alphas.
I've used them in Intels without troubles.

I built 4 DS10's in about 4 hours after updating the
install images to correctly handle the SCSI and Tulip
chips that came with them. Used S3 Trio64 video though
since they were cheap. ($25 each) That included configuring
all my network stuff so the systems could see my other
15 machines and misc. stuff. Other folks I'm sure have
cloned systems and installed many more faster than
that. Most of the time was spent in the boot cycle where
SRM was bringing up/down the Enet connections. That
takes way too long.

The real issue it seems is getting a kernel that works
with those system configs.
