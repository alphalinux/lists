On 17-Oct-99 justin bailey wrote:
> just recently got my soundcard pseudo-configured on my as200. it seems to
> sort of work, but not completely.
> when i do a cat /dev/dsp1 or /dev/audio1, i get a 'device not configured'
> message.
> this is what /dev/sndstat says:
>
> [root@grubby /root]# cat /dev/sndstat
> Sound Driver:3.5.4-960630 (Sat Oct 2 15:28:55 EDT 1999 root,
> Linux grubby 2.0.35 #1 Wed Sep 8 21:58:56 EDT 1999 alpha unknown)
> Kernel: Linux grubby 2.0.35 #1 Sat Oct 2 15:31:35 EDT 1999 alpha
> Config options: 0

> Card config:
> MS Sound System at 0x530 irq 9 drq 0
> OPL-2/OPL-3 FM at 0x388 drq 0

Hmm. This is similar to what I get on a 2.0.35 kernel with working audio,
except:

MS Sound System at 0x530 irq 9 drq 3

Maybe you have your DMA settings wrong?

I configured the card this way:
#define MSS_BASE 0x530
#define MSS_IRQ 9
#define MSS_DMA 3

Hope this helps...
