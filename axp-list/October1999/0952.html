Hi,

I've got 3 of those beasts (well two 233, and one 200) running, they are
doing great with Linux and prove to be very helpfull and reliable despite
their 'venerable' age.

But, I could not install them from the boot cd of my Redhat 5.2 or with the
generic image.

May be am I a to lazy and did not I inquire long enought (this is only a
flame protection ;=).

Anyway I downloaded Mikasa specific floppy images from ftp.alphalinux.org
and build them, I messed a little with SRM/ARC/Milo configuration but after
that everything worked fine exactly as on a PC.

I'm currently using 2.2.10 kernel, have done the install with a 2.2.5 and
everything has been working fine (till now).

Hope this may help.
