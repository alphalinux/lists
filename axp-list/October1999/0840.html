Did the VAR provide you with a quote for the kzpba-ca....i've never seen a
kzpba-cx. These folks have to ensure they ship qualified products or risk
satisfaction issues. If they are shipping systems that don't meet the SOC
requirement, they're putting their reseller authorization at risk.

A VAR and the reseller have two choices with regard to internal disk...they
can either take advantage of the integrated IDE controller and add the
5400rpm drives, or include the kzpba-ca/kzpcm-da with a Compaq scsi disk.
If this criteria is not satisfied..it's been my experience that the master
reseller can't ship the system..or even place a legitimate order. In
certain cases this can be worked around via waiver, but this is normally
for larger custom orders.
