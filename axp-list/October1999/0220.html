Thanks to whoever it was (? Elliot Lee) who suggested the cause of the
xfs problems was related to an optimisation bug, and that this has been
solved in the rawhide package.

I've upgraded to the rawhide xfs rpm (XFree86-xfs-3.3.5-5.alpha.rpm)
and this does appear to have solved the problem here. xpdf, xterm and
gv no longer seem to kill the font server as they have in the past.

Now, if I can just get netscape and/or em86 working, everything would be
wonderful again.
