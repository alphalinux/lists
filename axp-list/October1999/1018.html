Michael,

  FYI, Compaq is publicly disclosing the Alpha roadmap through 21464,
it's 1.5 GF/s for EV68 (21264) at 750 MHz (1999),
4 GF/s for EV7 (21364) at 2 GHz (2001),
8 GF/s for EV8 (21464) at 2 GHz (2003).

Also the EV7 in 2001 has 12 GB/s of Rambus I/O in the CPU.
That compares to 2.6 GB/s now which is shared with the PCI busses.

I'm not going to post to this newsgroup, but if you want to use this
info, it's fully public.
