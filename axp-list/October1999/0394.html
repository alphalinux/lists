RH 6.1 pricing was reported as having three levels, all of
which include manuals and installation support:

    Basic: $29.95 MSRP incl. 30 day email installation support.

    Full: $79.95 MSRP incl. 30 day phone install support as well
    as email.

    Secure/Server/Omething else: over $100 IIRC, not too clear
    on this offering...

For $30 you get formal support and a printed manual set. Not a
bad way to start with Linux, IMHO... The Red Hat manuals used to
sell for $15 seperately.
