Greetings...
I have a PC164, and I've found that my SB16 ISA sound card will randomly
freeze my machine solid, usually when the system is busy and I try to play
an mp3, for example. I've tried the stock kernel that comes with redhat 6.0,
as well as the 2.2.5-22 update. I've also built 2.2.12, and in addition tried
2.2.13pre1 and 2.2.13pre18. All have the problem.
This is driving me crazy. Sometimes sounds will play ok, then the machine
will freeze some minutes after, but it always happens.
If I pull the sound card from the machine, it will stay up nearly indefinitely.
I've noticed other people on this list having problems with sound hanging their
machine, but I've been unable to find any clear solution.
I've built kernels with generic cpu support, with PC164 specific support,
with SRM PCI settings enabled (I'm using SRM), with PCI quirks enabled, with
PCI bridge optimization enabled. All these options both on and off, yet they
seem to have no effect on the problem.

What can I do? I know there's nothing wrong with my machine or the sound card,
as I also have NetBSD on the same machine and it doesn't suffer from this
problem.

Please help!
Thanks,
