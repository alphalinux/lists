> We are having more problems installing RH6.0 on our DS10's, particularly
> on NFS...
>
> We know that main problem is to get a good kernel. Could you please tell
> us how or where to get it?. At this moment we are using a 2.2.12 boot
> image from Compaq.
        You probably have the right boot image, but are you using the
latest ramdisk for 6.0? The initial distro of the 6.0/axp installation
tools had some "issues" with network installs. Grab the updated ramdisk
from ftp.alphalinux.org or your local RH mirror.
