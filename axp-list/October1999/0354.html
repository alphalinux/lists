With regards to your message at 08:44 PM 10/11/99 -0700,
robbyrnes@ozemail.com.au. Where you stated:

>I own one of these boards as well, and as far as I have been
>able to find out, they use ARC, not SRM.
>
>Since Compaq (nee Digital) don't support these machines any more,
>you probably won't find any firmware for them. If you do ...
>can you let me know?
>
>Cheers
>
>Rob Byrnes
SRM exists for those machines. Unfortunately it has not been updated for
quite a while.
To obtain it (legally) order the SRM SDK, part number QR-21B02-03 (Alpha EB
S/W Dev Kit;Frmwr V2).
This contains SRM firmware for a number of the boards, including the
Cabriolet and PCI64 motherboards.

List price is about $75 US
