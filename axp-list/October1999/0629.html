I just got a new Diamond FireGL 1000 Pro 8MB PCI card to replace my old S3
Virge and it sort of works but I am having a hard time deciding if the
problem is the card or the machine. It works fine in VGA mode but when
the AlphaBIOS is setting itself up I am getting a stripey effect on the
screen, it looks like the blue signal is missing in blocks, or I get extra
red or green, its a mess! Anyway, Linux boots and I can start X but then
I get a picture which has a strange green cast and flashes with blue as
the server is running. If I change colour depths the effect changes too
but its always there. I guess I'll have to try the card in an Intel box
to prove it one way or another. I am using it with my LG LCD 1024x768
panel but that worked perfectly with the same cable on my S3 card.

I think I am doomed never to have a decent graphics card :-(
