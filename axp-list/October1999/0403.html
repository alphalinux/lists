Luke Deller wrote:
>+ extwl $3,0x6,$4 /* get word 3 of ra (most significant) -> $4 */
>+ extwl $2,0x6,$1 /* get word 3 of rb -> $2 */
>+ cmple $2,$3,$6
>+ cmovne $6,$1,$4

Surely you should compare registers 1 and 4, rather than 2 and 3.

>+ extwl $3,0x4,$5 /* get word 2 of ra -> $5 */
>+ extwl $2,0x4,$1 /* get word 2 of rb -> $1 */
>+ cmple $2,$3,$6
>+ sll $4,16,$4
>+ cmovne $6,$1,$5
>+ or $4,$5,$4

Likewise with 1 and 5. Same elsewhere.

>+.globl emul_maxub_8
>+.ent emul_maxub_8
>+emul_maxsb_8:

ub or sb?

>+ lda $1,0x7f7f($31)
>+ ldah $1,0x7f7f($1)
>+ sll $1,32,$5
>+ or $1,$5,$1
>+ not $1,$1

I think you could get the 0x8080808080808080 pattern shorter by something like:

not $31, $1
zap $1, 0x55, $1
srl $1, 1, $5
xor $1, $5, $1
