Izaak Bozof wrote:
>
> Which is faster? A DS-10 with a 21266 / 466 or an AlphaPC164LX with 21164 533?
>
> Compaq has got DS-10 on sale (http://www.digital.com/alphaserver/ad/linux/index1.html) as a
> complete system for $3K. I can throw together an 164lx system similarly configured for about 60%
> of that.
>
> izaak

If your compiler is good, the 21164 should actually be
faster. They both are capable of 2 FLOPS per clock.
However, I own one of each and I can say even with
Compaq's compilers, the DS10 by far outperforms a 21164,
probably because it is capable of out of order execution
which improves the chip's ability to get work done.

I wouldn't bother with a 21164 based system if pure
performance is your goal. On the other hand, at 60%
of the cost, it would be worth considering cost wise.

At $3K, the DS10 is really a bargain.
