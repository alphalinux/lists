Greetings,

The PC164 does not have embedded driver for later LSI chipsets on which
most (but not all) the IntraServer cards are based. Your embedded drivers
(which cannot be updated, and there is no provision for SCSI BIOS emulation
of the x86 SCSI BIOS on the board) support the Qlogic 1020 (retired
product, but still quite good), the LSI/Symbios Logic 53C825, 53C810,
Adaptec 7880, Adaptec 7870 (there may be a few more, but those are the ones
I remember). Once again, this is a BOOT controller, you can use whatever
you want once the firmware has run the boostrap routines up through MILO.
If you are running SRM on this machine, your choices are the same minus the
Adaptec's ASICs. Boards of this class (Pre Fast-20) can be had for about
$25-$40.00 US

Good luck.

--Kirk
