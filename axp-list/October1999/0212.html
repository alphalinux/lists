Eric Bohm wrote:
> Since the 2.2 series has been nightmarish (IMHO) on
> the alpha, I think a web page summarizing kernel problems and
> solutions would be quite helpful.

A web page could be useful but with "nightmarish" I do not know what you
are talking about. If you mean RH 6.0 that this has not very much to
do with a kernel. I run myself quite a number of different 2.2 kernels
and many our customers use kernels from that series and with quite
substantial uptime numbers. As usual various troubles were discovered,
most of them quite platform independent, but in my experience 2.2
kernels are actually quite a bit better than 2.0 series - especially
when it started.

BTW - some release notes and kernel errata you can find on
http://www.linux.org.uk/
