If you check in the archive you'll see some traffic on this topic. I'll give you a brief rundown of what I remember.

1) The Multia (UDB) in particular and some other platforms have had trouble determining which connection is in use. The solution has been to force the connection type. One way that has worked is to go to the SRM console specify the appropriate value for the variable ewa0_mode IIRC. Do a "sho | more" at the SRM console and it will sho you all of the variables. Some have also said if you use modules that specifying the media type there works.

2) Check your network driver there have been discussions concerning the need for specific or newest drivers depending on platform and kernels.

3) There have been problems with some of the Multia's in the past. Some folks have reported that the 10Base2 (Coax, BNC) port does not work but the other two do. Others have reported problems with the 10BaseT (RJ45, twisted pair) but fine on the other two.

Hope this helps.

Calvin.
