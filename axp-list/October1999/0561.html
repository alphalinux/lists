If your talking the kzpac, dac960 raid from Compaq/Digital, no it will not
work under linux. The reason is the onboard firmware is custom, based on
mylex V2 firmware. To get it to work, you must get a retail mylex board
which has V3 firmware. I do not know if anyone has managed to update an old
kzpac to the V3 Mylex, firmware.. Anybody have an answer??
