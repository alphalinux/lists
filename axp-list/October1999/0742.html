I've been working with the Red Hat folks to get clock/hwclock
working on Jensen, and as of this morning, hwclock works very
nicely. :-) I'm sure the updated util-linux will be in the
next Raw Hide release, but don't quote me. So 6.1 should be better
out of the box on Jensen's. If you do a strace on awk you'll see
there is an iotcl() call hanging. I'm looking into this too and
RH are aware of it. They don't have a Jensen to test on though.

As for a generic kernel that runs on Jensen, that is the remaining
piece of the puzzle. Unrelated to this, if anyone can get a DE-422
going on a Jensen, please share the knowledge with everyone. ;-)
