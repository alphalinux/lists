You wrote:
: Does anybody know hot to solve this problem and use MO drive

I had similar experience. You have dual-channel Qlogic SCSI card
and you connect the MO to the second channel. In my case, a PD drive
was connected second channel and all the other HDDs to the first.
Although I forgot the detail, the PD drive came to the first (sda)
or somethig I do not like. I connected all the SCSI device to the
first port of Qlogic card then, the "alphabet asignment" changed to
an order I like.
