Hi David,

If this is one of API's developer loaner units, it definitely has a 1.44 MB
floppy. If it's from your reseller in the UK, you should double check
concerning what they assembled the system with, but I suspect it's a
standard 1.44 MB floppy as well and you are just being lead astray by a
misleading kernel message.

I see the 2.88 MB message here on systems that I know have 1.44 MB floppies
in them. I don't know whether it means that the controller is 2.88 MB
capable or whether it's just a bug.

Tom
