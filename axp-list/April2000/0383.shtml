A couple points wrt tulips (DE500s).

1) There were multiple cards that went by the name DE500, eg DE500-AC,
or something. Some DE500-xx don't work worth a crap with Linux tulip
drivers and some work like a champ at 100Mbit full duplex. You now
have 3 drivers to experiment with (on RH6.1 and greater) - tulip,
old_tulip and de4x5.

2) Settiong SRM console variables, eg ewa0_mode, have NO effect with linux
and the NIC. Neither the kernel or driver "read" the values of the
SRM variables.
