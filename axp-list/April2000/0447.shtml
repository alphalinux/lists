This installation "stops" if you mistyped a password on the second try.
Recovery may indeed be better. You have to go back to the previous
screen, hit "Next" there and repeat that process until you will get your
password right both times.

Things like one or two letter passwords will be also unacceptable and
the only indication that installer is unhappy with your password is that
installation "stops". Try again.

Yes, I would like to see a button "Yes, I really do know what I am doing
and continue with an empty password, please!" but then some dumb
journalists would have a field day that Linux, not Red Hat, has a
"backdoor" (something close actually happened recently with piranha
package).
