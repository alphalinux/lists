"Hoover, Tony" <hoover@sal.ksu.edu> writes:

> what are the "options" numbers for 10 Mb Full duplex and 10 Mb Half ?

It seems that the answer is, as usual, "RTFS" ;(

static const char * const medianame[] = {
        "10baseT", "10base2", "AUI", "100baseTx",
        "10baseT-FD", "100baseTx-FD", "100baseT4", "100baseFx",
        "100baseFx-FD", "MII 10baseT", "MII 10baseT-FD", "MII",
        "10baseT(forced)", "MII 100baseTx", "MII 100baseTx-FD", "MII 100baseT4",
};

Therefore, 4 for full duplex and 0 for half.
