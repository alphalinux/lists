Rob Williams wrote:
>
> Could someone please tell me which of the QLogic
> controllers will be picked up by the latest SRM? I
> went to flash my Alpha64LX with the newest AlphaBios
> and somehow ended up with SRM on it instead. It is not
> finding my drives on my Adaptec 3940 (even though the
> AlphaBIOS did), and somehow no one has been able to
> simply tell me what controller will work. Someone
> mentioned QLogic ISP cards. Does that mean any card
> that has the ISP chip set? Maybe I should just go back
> to AlphaBIOS and use the Adaptec. I'd rather not. I
> don't need all that crap.
>

Tekram DC390F's work on DS10/XP1000/UP2K and are about
$70. At one point someone found a 53c875 card that was
$50. These are Symbios UW cards.

As to QlogicISP, my XP1000 has one built in and it's a
SCSI storage controller: Q Logic ISP1020 (rev 6)
according to /proc/pci.
