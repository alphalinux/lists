Warmer, warmer - at last. You likely do not have a directory
/lib/modules/2.2.14-6.0BOOT/ nor any modules for THAT kernel
on your disk.

Boot with a kernel which an installation put in your /boot, for which
you likely have also modules installed in a subdirectory of
/lib/modules, and your problems will disappear.

If you are booting from a floppy using BOOT kernel to sort out
things like if your /etc/aboot.conf is really correct, or if you
have a proper boot block, or if in general your booting sequence
is set up properly, then do not expect that this is more than that.
