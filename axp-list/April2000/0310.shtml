On Mon, 24 Apr 2000, Robert Williams wrote:

> I would truely like to hear from anyone
> successfully using a Netgear FA310TX
> on a PC164 motherboard. I have three of them
> that crash when I try to use either version of
> the FA310TX (Lite On and Tulip).
> I've tried just about everything I can think of...
> These same cards work fine in a UP1000.

I've got one running in mine (a Lite-On version), and it's working fine.
However at the moment that box is running FreeBSD.

Is it possible that the bus speed jumpers (don't know if that's the
"correct" terminology for them--the pc164 TRM identifies them as J30,
"configuration jumpers") could be incorrectly set? I'm just guessing so
take this with a large grain of salt, but perhaps the jumpers are set in
such a way that the PCI bus is being over/under clocked.
