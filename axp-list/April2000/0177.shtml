A tip I hope others may find useful:

If you have some source RPMs which give you SIGFPEs which -mieee fixes
then you can put in your roots .rpmrc (~root/.rpmrc):

optflags: alpha -O2 -mieee

and it will build it with -mieee.

(Who has Helix GNOME running here on his LX - very nice).
