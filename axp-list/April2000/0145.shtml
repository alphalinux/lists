Yes, you do need 2.88 MB (or ED/Extended Density) Disks to make this
work. ED disks are commonly identified as being 4 MB unformatted, 2.88 MB
formatted. (Just goes to show you how in-efficient fat is.)

my AS200, but don't know where to buy ED diskettes.
