When you 'set os_type nt' then it should bypass the blue screen of the SRM and
attempt to directly load the ARCBIOS. It sounds like it wants to do this even
though you seem to have no screen output. I would check all of the normal
stuff (card seated properly, everything works) and if it still isn't working
call.
When I first changed to the SRM menu (you can changes this in the ARCBIOS
settings if it comes up) it wouldn't load. They finally had to replace a
daughterboard? on my PWS433.

Is your machine brand new/refurbished/from another user? Maybe the ARCBIOS
needs to be re-flashed?
Just some ideas.
good luck.
