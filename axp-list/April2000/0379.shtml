On the PWS(MIATA)s you can have both loaded at the same time. I have both,
although I choose to mostly use the SRM, for arguments listed here previously.
If you have one you should have the other. I believe that it is only on
boards like the SX and LX (a couple of others I can't recall) that only one
or the other can be loaded. You can and should have both on your machine. I
don't have my machine or my manual on me to look up the jumper. Sorry. If you
still needed I can find it tonight.
