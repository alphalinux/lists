> SCSI ID of MO is "6" and its ID of HDD is "0", but sda is MO and sdb is
> HDD.
> "sda" was HDD when MO was not connected.
> I don' know why MO locates before HDD.
>
Because it detects the adaptec before the qlogic.
And since the SCSI-drives are numbered consecutively, all drives
connected to the adaptec will be assigned letters before any drive
connected to the qlogic.

Solution: either connect the HD to the adaptec or the MO to the qlogic.
Or live with it as it is.

Hmm. Makes sense. How would you expect to set partitions on a MO-drive

Remember: The numbering scheme in Linux and AlphaBIOS (which presumably
is what you're using) are to different things, even when they might look
similar.
