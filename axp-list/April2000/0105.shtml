Greg Lindahl wrote:
>
> > I wish there where something slower than DS10. Something EV6 based
> > with AGP with about the same performance as like a 650Mhz 21164A and of
> > course alot cheaper. Something that could knock a Sun Ultra 5 out of the
> > market :-).
>
> Something slower than the DS10 would not be cheaper; the motherboard chipset
> is quite expensive, and a slower CPU doesn't save any money there. As for
> AGP, wishing won't make it so.
>

The UP2000 uses an expensive chip set, UP1000 doesn't.
The price difference between a UP2000 and UP1000 board
is large. A UP2000 board is over 3x what a UP1000 goes
for. Of course it does 2 cpus and has built-in SCSI so
it should be more.

For the UP1000. You can get a motherboard(~$700) and
600mhz cpu(~$2000) for $2700 total last I looked.
That's as cheap as I've seen. Of course, you have to supply
the case, memory, disk, etc. and assemble it. Note the
cpu is the expensive part here. If API had a slower part,
say 500mhz, that would fit the bill, assuming they were
to price them a bit lower than the 600mhz part.

If anyone's selling UP1000 processors for substantially less,
contact me offline. I'd like to hear about it. I thought a
previous discussion indicated UP1000 processors were cheaper
than that but that's not what I've found.
