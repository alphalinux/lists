-The 2100 with either EV4 or EV5 cpus work. They have 8 EISA slots and 3 PCI slots.
-The 2100A with either EV4 or EV5 is not currently supported. This system has 8 PCI slots and 3 EISA slots. There are other differences but this is the most noticable. This hardware architecture has not been worked out and support developed.
