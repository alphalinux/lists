These units sound very similar to the SPARCBooks that Tadpole used to
sell (3gx, 3gs) that were recently dumped by Herman Miller for
prices ranging from $1,500 to $200 at the very end.

If so, the build of the unit is very nice (think early ThinkPad with metal
case) - 10.4" TFT screen 800x600, Lexmark keyboard, etc., but battery
life is < 1 hour (these RISC chips were not made for battery operation).

I like my SPARCBook 3gx, but it is really a novelty item...
