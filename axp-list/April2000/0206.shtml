> What is the average power consumption of an Alpha chip? Say, of a 500 MHz
> or a 1 GHz?

at 500 mhz, the old 0.35 micron 21264 chips dissipated around 59 watts, plus
30 watts for the motherboard chipset (a small Tsunami config). The 1 GHz
chips don't exist yet; they'll presumably be 0.18 micron, and may be not
much more than 59 watts.

A single cpu Alpha *system* is about 200 watts.
