Make sure that you aren't using all three connectors at the same time (ie 2
int, 1 ext) -- this will cause a problem.

"Disconnections" is how SCSI implements concurrent I/O operations. If you
disable SCSI disconnects for a device then the device will "remain on the
bus" throughout the entire I/O cycle until the operation is complete. If it
is an I/O that involves a seek and takes a while, the bus is unavailable to
other devices. With devices such as tape drives it is essential that you
allow disconnects otherwise the whole bus may be rendered "hung" while a
long operation such as rewind (non-immediate) are performed.
