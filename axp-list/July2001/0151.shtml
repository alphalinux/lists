With regards to your message at 09:42 AM 7/17/01, Island Computers. Where
you stated:
>And what exactly is the difference between a DEC Permedia 2 (Elsa Gloria
>Synergy Permedia2 8MB) and a regular Elsa Synergy Permedia 2
>
>
>David Turner
There are a few variants I have seen:
Some of them had the option to disable VGA, which is need to install a
second card for dual headed operation.
Also, I think that some of the Elsa cards had the option to do TV out.

All of the variants I have seen work just dandy in TRU64, and of course in
Linux.

There are also a few variants of Permedia2 based cards which varied in
ability to work with Alpha.
Most had some shortcomings.
One we found that is very good is the STB GlyderMAX. It is functionally
identical to the Elsa Gloria Synergy. Works in TRU64 and Linux, even in OVMS.
