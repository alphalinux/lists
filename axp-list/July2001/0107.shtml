Hi,
All I did was to 'install' the kernel-source from RH7.1 on my RH6.2-system,
and then recompile this kernel.
- 1st of all, you may try with the bootdisk I made. You just have to tell me

where I may upload it...
- One of the things you gotta take care of is to turn on the option
CONFIG_ALPHA_LEGACY_START_ADDRESS
if not you get the kernel in a wrong adress space (that is, for MILO).
- Put in your machine type (is this really XL266 and not LX266?), the
default
one was never stable for me.
- Try also to get the correct .config-file from the RH7.1-distribution. I
had
to add a couple of options to get it working (like loopback block-device,
cdrom-support, etc.) as I didn't have the original .config around.
 And, after all, I have to admit that I don't run a 2.4-kernel anymore. I
switched back to good ol' 2.2.19, as it's the only one that runs stable on
my
system. Somethings not good with the PCI-driver, as I need to turn on
PCI-quirks. And this one seems still broken in 2.4 ( I tried 2.4.6 ). But to

install RH7.1, it was good enough. So try it and let me know if it worked
out,

ineiti
