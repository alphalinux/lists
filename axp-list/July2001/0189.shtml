On Wed, Jul 18, 2001 at 05:20:28PM -0400, Mike A. Harris wrote:
>
> I do not know for sure, but I have had reports the Xpert 2000
> does not work on i386. That was in 4.0.3 though, not 4.1.0

Well, the r128 PCI-GART stuff was #ifdef'ed only for Alpha in 4.1.0,
because there were some problems with it on x86, IIRC.

> The Banshee at least on i386 may or may not work. I presume on
> axp also. It depends on which XFree86 you use and which card it
> is. A fix is in 4.1.0-0.9.0, but I dunno if it works or not yet
> as I've got no feedback.

I've a pair of identical Banshees, and they work fine on EV6 boxes,
even using "int10". But on EV5 machines, allowing "int10" to run
crashes them back to SRM console. Luckily, one can add the "NoInt10"
option, and all is well...
