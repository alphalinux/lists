If XFree86 4.0.3 and all of the packages it depends on are
installed on a Red Hat Linux 7.0 system, it should work fine
minus DRI. XFree86 4.0.2 and beyond *require* a 2.4.x kernel for
DRI to function, and we do not have a 2.4.x kernel officially
released for 7.0. While 4.0.3 or later may be installed and work
on RHL 7.0, it definitely isn't a supported thing.

If you can do without 3D accel though, it should work fine. I
use it on one box. Ditto for XFree86-4.1.0.

My personal recommendation is to upgrade to 7.1 however.

>I never tried. Well, you will definitely need to update Mesa
