It needs to be "TRUE PARITY" ( n * 36 bit ) FPM 60ns SIMMs. You may also be
able to use 33 bit SIMMs, but AFAIK those items are DEC only P/Ns.

NOTE: "Logic Parity" SIMMs will NOT work.
