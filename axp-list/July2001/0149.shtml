OK, Mike, I'll send them along. They include:

1. support for 3DLabs Permedia-4 (VX1 AGP)
2. enhanced ATI PCI-GART support (Rage 128/Radeon)
3. cleanup of unaligned accesses by "int10"
4. greatly reduce X VM requirements on SPARSE machines
5. fix S3 Savage4 support for 64-bit machines

These patches have been sent to XFree86 folks, but its uncertain
which or whether they will get in to 4.1.x, or instead, wait for 4.2.0.

To reiterate WRT 3D hardware acceleration options for Alpha Linux, one
can choose from:

3DFx Voodoo Banshee PCI
3DFx Voodoo3 2000/3000 PCI
ATI Rage 128 PCI (aka Xpert 2000?)
ATI Radeon 32MB SDR PCI

These require 2.4.x kernels and XFree86 4.1.0 for best performance.

Note that ATI Rage Pro is NOT Rage 128, AFAICT. And Rage 128 Pro is
only AGP, again AFAICT.

Oh, and for those with UP1000 or UP1100 with AGP slots, I'm afraid
that your options are still only the above PCI cards for 3D
acceleration, as I've not yet been able to get the AGP-GART
working... :-\
