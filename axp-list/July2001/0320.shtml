Hello Kjetil,

the samba package should contain a program called testparm, which checks
smb.conf for sanity (I guess). Another thing to avoid is using share-names
longer than 12 characters as those can not be accessed by at least nt4
clients under all conditions (tested against 2.0.8 and 2.2.0)
