On Mon, Jul 23, 2001 at 08:53:03AM -0400, Don Russell wrote:
>
> (specifically to David Turner)
>
> What sort of problems have you had with XP1000s, DPWs and RH7?
>
> We have a large set of XP1000s ( with ELSA Gloria cards) running RH6.0
> and are in the process of of upgrading to RH7.0 (I know we're slow,
> but there is always other stuff to work on). We are also converting a
> bunch of DPW 433/500/600 boxes from Tru64 Unix to RH7. All our testing
> has been without significant trouble, other than things I did wrong
> myself.

I'll answer from my own experiences...

RH 7.0, in its changeover to XFree86 4.0.1a (IIRC), broke support on many
of the older machines. Combinations that had been working fine, such as
DPW/PWS with Millenniums or ELSAs, were hanging the machines. The EV6
boxes were usually OK.

RH7.1 has XFree86 4.0.3 plus a whole mess of patches I did to fix those
problems on the older Alpha machines. Support should be much more stable.
