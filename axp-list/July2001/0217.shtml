On Fri, 20 Jul 2001, Lew Randerson wrote:

> Here's what happens:
> 1) The machine is turned on.
> 2) The CPUs go on
> 3) The monitor sees a video signal.
> 4) The monitor screen turns blue
> with a cursor in the upper left
> corner.
> Then THATS IT!!!
> I have found that if I wait two minutes
> and then hit the Enter key on the keyboard
> the P00>>> prompt will appear and the
> machine will accept SRM commands such

Sounds like it's definitely defaulting to the serial port, so somehow,
your SRM environment vars got screwed up (check the battery). In SRM,
type:
        P00>> set console graphics

And you should see those messages again.
