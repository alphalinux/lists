With regards to your message at 08:17 PM 7/26/01, Eric Brown. Where you stated:
>I feel like giving my 164SX some more RAM. Unfortunately, I've forgotten
>what type of RAM it requires. Can someone tell me what type is required?
SDRAM DIMMs, PC100, ECC, unbuffered.
ECC is not mandatory, but is more reliable.
However do not mix ECC with non-ECC.
