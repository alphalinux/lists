>These patches have been sent to XFree86 folks, but its uncertain

Probably the latter.

>To reiterate WRT 3D hardware acceleration options for Alpha Linux, one
>can choose from:
>
>3DFx Voodoo Banshee PCI
>3DFx Voodoo3 2000/3000 PCI
>ATI Rage 128 PCI (aka Xpert 2000?)
>ATI Radeon 32MB SDR PCI

I do not know for sure, but I have had reports the Xpert 2000
does not work on i386. That was in 4.0.3 though, not 4.1.0

>These require 2.4.x kernels and XFree86 4.1.0 for best performance.
>
>Note that ATI Rage Pro is NOT Rage 128, AFAICT. And Rage 128 Pro is
>only AGP, again AFAICT.

Rage & Rage Pro == Mach64 == No hardware 3D in X yet, but work is
being done by public now on top of Gareth Hughes fine start.

Rage 128 and Rage 128 Pro == r128

Basically any ATI card with 128 in it is a Rage 128 based card
using the r128 driver.

Rage Mobility == confusion. Cards prior to M3 are Mach64, the M3
& M4 are r128. There are newer Mobility cards I think, but I do
not know if they are public yet..

The Banshee at least on i386 may or may not work. I presume on
axp also. It depends on which XFree86 you use and which card it
is. A fix is in 4.1.0-0.9.0, but I dunno if it works or not yet
as I've got no feedback.
