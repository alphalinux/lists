Olga Chamorro Tábara wrote:
>
> Hello, I'm trying to do Linux RAID 5 over partitons of only one disk
> drive.Can I do this?

1) I do not know for sure, I never did RAID myself.

2) The gain in security would be very small (protect against hardware
   damage of single block, but not against damage of whole disk drive),
   and the loss in performance would be high (many more disk accesses).
