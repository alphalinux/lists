Strange

Because I think you're talking about the SN-PBXGK-AA and not the SN-PBXGK-BB

The AA is a DEC (Evans and Sutherland card) with the Permedia Chip
The BB is the Elsa Card with the Permedia 2 Chip

The ELSA card only works on SRM 7.0 and later on the PWS FYI

> > And what exactly is the difference between a DEC Permedia 2 (Elsa Gloria
> > Synergy Permedia2 8MB) and a regular Elsa Synergy Permedia 2
>
> Hardware-wise, probably next to nothing, or nothing that matters.
>
> What is obviously different is the BIOS code.
>
> My test:
>
> 1. plug in the VGA,
> 2. power up,
> 3. if (SRM) console appears on the screen, it passes; if not, it fails.
>
> The ELSA failed, the COMET passed..
>
> The COMET "banner" on the screen (during BIOS emulation) proclaims
> "DEC Powerstorm 4D10T", the ELSA gives (or doesn't :-) its normal one,
> so at least the banner strings are different in the BIOS image... ;-}
>
> Please note that these really are different cards; the COMET has a
> Digital logo/part number/etc on the circuit board, and looks quite
> different from the Gloria Synergy 8MB that we OEM'ed from ELSA (or
> from the retail ELSA Permedia 2), quite apart from the gold
> accents... ;-}
>
> If the BIOS emulator in the console can handle it, all the Permedia 2
> cards should behave the same under X, regardless of who made them.
