Whatever you are doing with Samba please note that serious security
issues were discovered and for about a month now rpms for 2.0.10-2
are available in updates.

> Swat tells me that SMB and NMB is "not running".
> When starting/restarting from swat, it still says "not running".

Make sure that you are not attempting to run smdb and nmbd both
from (x)inedtd and startup scripts. Both ways are possible but
you will have to choose one or another or they will conflict.
 
> Could it be som wrong configuration in smb.conf that cause the service to
> fail ?

It could be. But you can hike debugging levels in smb.conf. Checking
log files after that is often very illuminating.
