On Sunday 15 July 2001 21:02, Ionut Georgescu wrote:
> Hi,
>
> I tried to boot a XP1000 at 667MHz with RedHat 7.1 and rawhide boot
> images. Non of them worked. Also tried with debian, suse 6.3, 7.0, 7.1
> .. No success. Does anyone have any idea ? tru64 seems to boot OK.

Hello Ionut,

I have three XP1000s with Redhat 7.1 that work for me. The only problem that
I have had was that the tulip driver will cause the kernel to oops upon boot,
please be certain that you are using the de4x5 driver. My systems also
work with Debian 2.2 and SuSe 7.0 & 7.1

I would like to suggest that you attach and serial cable to your XP1000 and
capture the messages sent to the console. This would really help find the
problem.

Best Regards,
