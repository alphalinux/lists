Helge Kreutzmann <kreutzm@itp.uni-hannover.de> writes:

> On Sat, Jul 21, 2001 at 10:56:48PM +0200, Falk Hueffner wrote:
> > I have just switched my SX164 from AlphaBios to SRM with aboot, and
> > everything works fine, except aboot doesn't give any output on the
> > screen, which makes debugging pretty hard, and also reduces the
> > usefulness of the interactive mode somewhat. Does it perhaps think I
> > have a serial console? (I have nothing attached to any serial port...)
> > Anybody got an idea?
>
> When you boot into SRM, does it display anything (like E4.E5..) on
> the screen before you hint enter ? If so then you are in serial
> console.

It didn't show anything until I pressed Enter, because indeed console
was set to serial (seems to be default on new install). Setting it to
"graphic" resolved the aboot problem.
