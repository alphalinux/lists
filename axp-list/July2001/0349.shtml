ok... ypbind is running...

take a look at your /etc/nsswitch.conf file and make sure there's a lines
that look similar to this.
that should fix the authentication problems you are seeing. If not, let me
know.

passwd: files nis
group: files nis

as far as the automounting goes, try hard mounting /home and see if you can
get to the users home dir. (shut off autofs when you do this). get back to
me on this one and I'll take a look from there.

Kurt
