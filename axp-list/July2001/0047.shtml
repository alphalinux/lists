        RH 7.0 does not allow installing onto a DAC960 card as part of the
        installation. I'm assuming you have an older KZPAC (FW rev 2.x.x),
which
        explains why linux doesn't see the card (driver only 'sees' cards
with 3.x.x
        and higher FW). If your card supports a FW update to 3.x.x - you
have two
        ROM slots, one used the other is empty - then you can contact Mylex
to
        purchase the $70.00USD FW upgrade to 3.xx.
