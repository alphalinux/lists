Sounds horribly like your kernel has installed beyond a point in the disk that
is reachable by aboot. Something akin to why people used to have issues with
lilo and kernels being installed beyond cylinder 1024. aboot does have a limit
but I never got around to working out what the limit was. I think there has
been some additional work on aboot since at compaq and that issue is addressed,
however I've no other details.

I'm assuming here that you don't have a /boot partition at the front of the
disk (cylinder *2*- i dunno,, through to 32 (30Mb?)
