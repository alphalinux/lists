Hi Sam

I also had problems with booting RH 7.1 after installation.
I read somewhere that you should not partition the disk from block 1 on the
disk.
I repartitioned the disk with fdisk and choose starting at block 10 (to
give some space for partition table I think).
After a fresh installation, it booted OK.

Maybe this is the same problem you have.
