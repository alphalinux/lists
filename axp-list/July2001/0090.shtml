> You are right here. I have a copy of my DU /etc/fstab
> which says UFS. I should have mentioned this earlier.

In that case this should not be a problem. There is a support for
CONFIG_UFS_FS in 2.2 and 2.4 kernels. It is normally not turned on (and
you need really read-only). Once this will show up in your
configuration then you may be asked some more detailed questions (but I
may be mistaken). There is Documentation/filesystems/ufs.txt writeup.
I did use that to read some files from old NeXT disks. I do not
remember if I ever tried that on DU. Maybe.

Make yourself a module and try.
