You also have to terminate the DDS2 tape drive (it's a narrow 8 Bit drive)
on a a wide bus QLA1040C Qlogic controller

The best thing to do here is buy a cheap (we sell them) KZPAA compatible
card for the DDS2 ($69)

It works beautifully on NT, VMS, Tru64 and ARed Hat

It uses the 53C810 chipset
