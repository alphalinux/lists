On Mon, 17 Jan 2000, you wrote:
> Hello Peter,
>
> I had similar problems to yours with the sound driver locking up the
> entire machine under newer kernels (2.2.13 in my case) and SRM console.
>
> I thought I fixed it by using the ALSA driver but that eventually hung the
> machine as well.
>
> As a last test I used the isacfg command on SRM to 'reserve' the sound
> related resources and it seems to work, at least for the last two days.
> I'll observe for a couple of more days and than post to the axp-list. I am
> now back on OSS since some players had problems with ALSA.
>
> The isacfg command is described in the "Alpha SRM Console Guide for Alpha
> Microprocessor Motherboards" (EC-QK8DF-TE), I have the pdf and could send
> it if you need it.
>
> Anyway the isacfg parameters I used corresponded to the setting of the
> card:
> >>> isacfg -slot 1 -dev 0 -mk -etyp 1 -handle SB16 -irq0 5 -iobase0 220
> -iobase1 300 -iobase2 388 -dmachan0 1 -dmachan2 5
>
> After that you have to type 'init' on the console again and boot.
>
> HTH
>
> Rolf
