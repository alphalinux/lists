Peter Petrakis wrote:
>
> This is weird. I've just switched over to SRM (v5.6) on my 600LX and
> installed RH 6.1. My video card is a Permedia 2 (Diamond FireGL 1000
> Pro). When X crashes it wont just die like usual and restart like it's
> supposed to when in init 5 it dumps me straight back to the SRM console

Are you running KDE? On my test 6.1 installation KDE is regularly
crashing my video hardware (Mach64, in this case). I can provoke that
on-demand. None of other window managers, with or without Gnome, which
I tried behaves that way.

After KDE locked up I can still login over a network to a stricken
machine but an experience shows that the only sane way to proceed at
this moment is 'shutdown -r' or the situation will deteriorate quite
fast. Visual effects on a console, until a machine starts to reboot,
are quite interesting. :-)

Actually my other version of KDE, which runs on Alpha over basically 5.2
distribution with updates and qt-1.4 libraries, behaves in a similar
style; only it takes a bit more of effort to crash it. No, I do not
have the slightest plans to even try to debug this big blob of C++.
I run a few KDE applications from Gnome/Enlightment combo, but not
the whole environment, and so far nothing bad happened.
