I have the opposite situation with my Alpha. I have gold leads in tin sockets.
Part of the problem is that the two metals react to each other, unfavorably.
the result is the distruction of one, the other, or both metals...

As a precaution, I pull the chips out about every 6 months or so, and clean
them. They've been in there for about a year and a half, and so far show no
signs of pitting, or other related damage.

In my case, returning them was not an option. But if you DO have that option,
by all means do so. Why run the risk of damaging your system. You can easily
replace the SIMMS, but not the slot they're sitting in!
