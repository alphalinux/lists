I think a lot of us are really curious as to the current status of em86.
Occasionally someone asks, but the message goes unanswered.

Would someone from Compaq care to give us an update???

The speed at which em86 can run things like WordPerfect is very impressive.
The fact that em86 can't properly create or write files makes it rather useless,
however. I think I speak for many people when I say that a properly working
em86 would be a *very* valuable asset. Good for Alpha/Linux, good for Compaq,
and great for all of us. It would expand our software base considerably,
which is a Good Thing. Please don't inadvertently force me to make it a new
personal project. I have enough on my plate right now, but I just hate seeing
such potential as em86 go to waste.

So please someone, Compaq, ANYONE, tell us the deal!

Thank you,
-Mark
