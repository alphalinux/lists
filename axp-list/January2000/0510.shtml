With this firmware you will still not be able to use V5.0. That firmware
was last updated in '96. The 4.0 stream is you best bet. I was able to
put 4.0e on it using the internal scsi drive. What is it 300 MB? I had X
and CDE installed the man pages and developement tools, even emacs. All
the extra stuff I left out. The free space after was not much. You must
choose just the right size partition for / and /usr. Just remember that
/ doesn't need much more space after you have installed, unless you plan
on added more software.
