On Tue, 1 Feb 2000, Hyung Min SEO wrote:

> I'd like to compare x86 linux system with various alpha mother boards.

There are many benchmarks; for a well recognised benchmark you could look
at the SPEC benchmark suite - see http://www.spec.org

These benchamrks use a collection of 'real' programs and thus offer pretty
reasonable results - a lot more believable than any artificial benchmarks
you can dream up!

They aren't perfect, but its a good approach.
