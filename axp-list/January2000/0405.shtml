The Yamaha. See:

http://www.yamaha.co.jp/english/product/computer/index.html

and click on technical support and follow the links.

The U/W negotiation lockup bug was fixed in 1.0G, so if the firmware on your
yamaha is earlier than that[1] (eg 1.0F) then that's why it won't work with
certain SCSI controllers. Current rev is 1.0H.
