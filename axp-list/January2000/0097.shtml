A while ago I tried Trident PCI sound card. For this one I really
needed ALSA driver so I could not tell anything about "better".
It worked fine on UX with 256MB of RAM but I could not get a peep
when dropped into UP2000. Nothing was crashing but interrupts
were obviously not processed and after a while I was getting a timeout
on every attempt to play something. I cannot tell what was really
at fault.

In general, from some other experiences, I have more confidence in
ALSA drivers but I did not see enough of that on Alphas to rank there
various sound setups.
