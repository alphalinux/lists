With regards to your message at 09:56 AM 1/24/00 -0600, Hoover, Tony. Where
you stated:
>Can anyone give me a recommendation for a good SCSI CD-RW or maybe a
>DVD-RAM. I have a 164SX with a Symbios 8751 SCSI host adapter, running
>RedHat 6.1. I was looking at maybe the Ricoh 7060 6x write, 4x re-write,
>24x read.

For a CDR/RW I recommend the Yamaha 6416 or the newer 8416

For a DVDROM, the new Pioneer 104S ( 10X/32X).

One thing to beware of is that the DVDROM drives may not be as comfortable
with CDR or RW media as a regular better quality CDROM only drive.
