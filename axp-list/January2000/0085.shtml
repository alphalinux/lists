>>> "Jonas Gustavsson" said:
>
> About graphics support though, will Compaq make the Powerstorm series
> available on linux(as Intergraph stated that the Wildcat drivers will be
> ported to linux)?

Well, there are quite a few different models of Powerstorms, I assume you
mean the ones that are OEMed from Intergraph?

If the sources to their LINUX drivers are made publically available, anyone
could port them to Alpha. If they make just binaries available, then it would
be a case of their porting it to Alpha themselves, or someone signing an NDA
to do it for them.

Would COMPAQ get involved in any case remains to be seen, ie whether there
appears to be enough payback for the resources that would be
required. Should that be deemed desirable, I'm sure we'd add those cards to
the LINUX Supported Options List fairly quickly.
