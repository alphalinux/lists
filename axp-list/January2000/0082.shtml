>>> Toshinao Ishii said:
>
> Recently I checked the latest snapshot of XFree86 (3.9.17) and
> was disappointed. There was no new driver support found. It is
> already not easy to purchase VGA card suppored by Alpha/Linux
> because the supported models are old (and PCI). I must say that
> Linux/Alpha is already and will be poor system as graphic machine
> compared with Linux/i386.

AGP is an issue that is being worked, both by API (UP1000 is now available
and has an AGP slot) and COMPAQ (nothing yet, but 4X/Pro when available, IIRC).

Aside from that, I think the other thing that is reflected in the limited
default build for Alpha is the fact that there are few folks who seem to be
willing and able to take on the kind of development and testing needed to
port the new (or existing) drivers to Alpha.

YOU could make a difference! Don't wait for other folks to do it, get access
to a UP1000 and the cards you wish to see supported, and go at it! The XFree86
folks will be grateful, the axp-list folks will be grateful, you'll be a hero.
