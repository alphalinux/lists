I have a PCI soundcard with the Ensoniq 1370 chip on it, and it works
flawlessly on my SX164 running RH6.1. We just purchased some more of these
cards for a lab here at school, and paid less than $16 (USD) each for them.
A quick check on pricewatch shows Comp-U-Plus has them for $13.
As for network cards, I usually try to find tulip (DECchip 21x4x) based
cards, however that has been becoming more and more difficult lately.
