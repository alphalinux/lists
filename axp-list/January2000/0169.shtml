Here's one...
How about more than just the UP1000 having a slot that "current" video cards
can plug into? Have any of you tried to find a current generation PCI video
card lately? How long until you gave up and decided to find an old Permedia
2 or Matrox?

So I guess my wish is for ( '264 ) Motherboard/CPU combos that are
reasonably priced, with AGP slot, and multi-processors would be nice. TRU64
and OpenVMS support would be nice extras.
