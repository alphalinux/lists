I just pulled down the client for osf-4.0b and it seems to run with
the libs I have in /usr/shlib:

[larrys@zeppo shlib]$ ls
libICE.so libXext.so libXt.so libcxx.so libm.so
libSM.so libXm.so libc.so libdnet_stub.so libots.so
libX11.so libXmu.so libc_r.so libexc.so
[larrys@zeppo shlib]$

I'm not sure if this matches what comes in the netscape rpm, but it's
what I stuck out there to run ns-4.61 and maybe any additional ones
for ccc, certainly nothing more. The linux client seems to take around
12-14 hr on a 533-SX. Once the currently running linux client finishes
its current work unit I'll kill it off and hand the cpu off to just
the DU one. I'll post the results after a run or two has completed.
