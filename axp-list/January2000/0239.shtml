On Thu, 13 Jan 2000, Dr. Andrew Almond wrote:

> Does anyone have any experience of using the following disk under
> linux/alpha? I assume in principle it should work.

I can't see why not.

> Seagate Barracuda
> Ultra Wide / LVD disk, 50 GB., 3.5 inch 7200 rpm.>
> Also does anyone know what the LVD means?

Low-Voltage-Differential - i.e. you can use longer leads and it goes
faster than conventional Ultra Wide SCSI.
(I think LVD discs are somehow compatible with older controllers but I'm
not sure about that!).
