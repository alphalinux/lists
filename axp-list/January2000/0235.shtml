With regards to your message at 10:37 AM 1/13/00 +0100, Dr. Andrew Almond.
Where you stated:
>Does anyone have any experience of using the following disk under
>linux/alpha? I assume in principle it should work.
It does. only 7200 rpm, so not as fast as some, but most GB per $$ around.

>Seagate Barracuda
>Ultra Wide / LVD disk, 50 GB., 3.5 inch 7200 rpm.
>
>Also does anyone know what the LVD means?
>
Low voltage differential. UltraSCSI 2 by it's another name. It is downward
compatible with UltraSCSI and Single ended SCSI.
