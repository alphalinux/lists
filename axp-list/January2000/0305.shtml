On Mon, 17 Jan 2000, Eugen Neuber wrote:

> Hi to all!
>
> Since I learned that there is a 'cheap' license of Tru64 for non comercial
> use (http://www.unix.digital.com/noncommercial-unix/) I do wonder if
> someone had success to run commercial progs on Linux/AXP:
>
> *) Mathematica
> *) MapleV
> *) etc.

I think that people have reported some success with these apps, take a
look through the axp-list archives (www.alphalinux.org/archives or
others).
 
> can someone please point me to a `Linux/AXP-Tru64-Howto'?

Well, there's some info at http://www.alphalinux.org/docs
go down the bottom and there's directions for Netscape and Acrobat, it
should be fairly similar.

FWIW, some people have reported problems with the libraries from 5.0
(Netscape users report DNS doesn't work etc..) that didn't exist with the
4.0x libraries.
