I had similar problems with KDE on RH6.1, and I see two simple solutions:
1) Download snapshots of qt2.1, kdelibs, kdebase, kdesupport and other kde
apps and run them.
    While it's bleeding edge, the alpha KDE environment seems to work fine,
and is quite fast/stable
.
2) Downgrade to the KDE/Qt found in RH6.0.
