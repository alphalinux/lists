>>> "Dr. Andrew Almond" said:
>
> I have been running kernel 2.3.39 on our UP2000 machine for 3 days now
> with few problems (compiled for generic alpha SMP with Jay Estabrook's
> patches). However, there are occaisonal error messages relating to
> running processes, such as;
>
> Jan 15 19:43:07 hera kernel: select.c:43 spinlock stuck in char.alpha.pv
> at fffffc0000365450(1) owner umount at fffffc0000358d90(0) super.c:43
> Jan 15 19:43:07 hera kernel: select.c:43 spinlock grabbed in
> char.alpha.pv at fffffc0000365450(1) 9468 ticks

Actually, these are not error messages, but merely "informative" messages,
letting the observer know that some times locks are (still) held for really
*long* periods of time (9468 ticks == 9+ seconds on Alpha).

It' would be an error if you saw a "stuck" without a "grabbed" though...

> Also, the machine cannot automatically reboot (as it could with 2.2.14).
> It halts with a spinlock stuck message. Are these known errors in
> 2.3.39? Other than this 2.3.39 seems fairly stable.

That part of the Alpha support hasn't been completely ported to 2.3.x from
2.2.x, apparently. Have a go if you've the time, arch/alpha/kerne/process.c
holds most of the related stuff...
