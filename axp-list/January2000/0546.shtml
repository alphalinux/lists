With regards to your message at 12:37 PM 1/26/00 -0500, Robert Williams.
Where you stated:
>What is a good source for Alpha motherboards, 21164 CPUs,
>CPU Fans, and heat sinks. I have a PC164 that appears
>to have an overheating problem. It freezes when the CPU
>is very active. I would like to change the airflow and maybe
>heat sink before I replace the CPU and motherboard.
>
>Bob Williams
We have all that in stock..
Maybe make sure that the case has proper airflow first. A heatsink and fan
be just dandy, but if the air in the case gets too hot, you are stuck..
Do you have access to a digital thermometer with a sensor probe?

If not Radio Shack have a half-decent usable model for about $25.

You want to measure ambient air temperature in the case as well as at the CPU.
