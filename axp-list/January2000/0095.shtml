On 8 Jan 2000 02:14:27 -0500, Ric Tibbetts <tibbetts@oz.net> wrote:
> Jan 7 22:23:05 alpha modprobe: can't locate module ppp0:0
> .
> This continues all the way up to #ppp0:49
> .
> Jan 7 22:23:05 alpha modprobe: can't locate module ppp0:49
>
>Can some kind soul out there shed some light on this for me? As I said, the
>connection works ok, but the errors are clogging up the logs.

You compiled a kernel without aliasing support, and linuxconf is using
a not-so-great method of detecting aliases. Either compile the kernel with
aliasing support, or remove/comment out the linuxconf-specific portions
of ifup-aliases. A later linuxconf may have this fixed, as well.
