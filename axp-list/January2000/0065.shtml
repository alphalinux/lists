There's an entire section in the FAQ at www.alphalinux.org dedicated to
the Ruffians.....it was based on 6.0 I think but there should still be
some good points in there.

As for partitioning, if Disk Druid is giving you a hard time, try fdisk
instead. As for MILOs, I've always used the "Jay's hacked version" form
ftp.digital.com/pub/DEC/Linux-Alpha/Kernels (I think that's right).
There were problem and even up to RedHat 6.0 they were still shiping the
original Deskstation versions, hopefully that's changed now.

--rdp
