Peter Petrakis wrote:
>
> On Tue, 25 Jan 2000, Ross Knepper wrote:
> >
> > after installing the rpm, netscape started up willingly.
> > unfortunately, it is unable to resolve any hostname i give it.
>
> check you /etc/svc.conf and make sure your "hosts" line looks like this.
>
> hosts=local,bind,yp

I would think that if you are not running NIS, or NIS+, or
something like that then 'yp' there will be counterproductive.
It may actually slow you down considerably if the first
two options failed (you mistyped host name, for example).

I dropped all these ",yp" from all lines in my copy of
/etc/svc.conf, as I do not have a NIS setup here,
and "it works for me" (TM).
