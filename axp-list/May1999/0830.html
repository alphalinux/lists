If you say what it is, I could be more specifically helpful.

alias scsi_hostadapter aic7xxx

...autoloads the aic7xxx module to drive an adaptec scsi card,
 for example. "modprobe -c" will give you a lot more examples. You
could also look in the HOWTOs.

If the module is used it will stay resident.
