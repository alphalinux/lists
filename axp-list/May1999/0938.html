Well this is how I setup my SX164 boxes. Note: I have a 4gig ide
Quantum Fireball hard disk. I setup the boot partition as . . .

hda1 5Meg -- this is a dos partition that holds milo and linload.exe
 
After installing use root to . . .
>mkfs.msdos /dev/hda1
>mkdir /mnt/milo_partition
>mount -t msdos /dev/hda1 /mnt/milo_partition

Then put in your milo floppy and copy milo and linload.exe to
/mnt/milo_partition. Now in the AlphaBIOS use the Utilities -> OS
Seclection Setup to create the Linux Boot. Mine is . . .

Boot Name: RedHat Linux 6.0
Boot File: Disk 0, Partition 1 \linload.exe
OS Path: Disk 0, Partition 2 \
OS Options:

It doesn't matter what partition you pick for the OS Path (it will
complain about all of them) but the \ is needed.
