On 23-May-99 mgraffam@idsi.net wrote:
> Does anyone know if there is a video board option for the Multia that puts
> out NTSC video? I suppose I could get an SVGA to NTSC converter.

I went the SVGA to NTSC converter route. Found a cheap converter on the net so
I could use the TV to display gqmpeg's interface (this uses less CPU than
x11amp, and on a multia you need to save all the CPU time you possibly can if
you want to play mp3s).

> Anyone have a Matrox card in their Multia? How is mpeg video playback?

No slot. I'd expect the Multia to be pretty dismal at it, though, based on my
experience with mine for playing back mpeg audio.
