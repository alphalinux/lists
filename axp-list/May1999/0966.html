Since this question seems to come up from time to time on this list I
thought $0.02 worth.

mmm --- A browser using tcl/tk that works really well. The forms are a bit
funny but on the qhole works pretty well.

        http://pauillac.inria.fr/~rouaix/mmm/about.html

amaya --- Fully featured web editor/broswer, looks very powerfull haven't
played too much but looks pretty good.

        http://www.w3.org/Amaya (Has Alpha RPMS)

Both seem to work well on the Alpha and are fast (not quite as fast as
lynx but much, much faster (and somewhat more correct) than xemacs.

Who needs netscape etc, etc.
