> NVRAM is not supported on the Alpha. Everything works fine with boot
> parameter:
>
> sym53c8xx=nvram:n
>
This isn't entirely surpising; what it probably means is that the driver has
picked up bogus values from the nvram on the card and has decided there aren't
any devices to be probed for on the other busses (probably all 0s or all 1s or
something else silly). I'd imagine that if the SCSI SDMS bios worked everything
might work nicely, or at least you might be able to tweak these settings - does
it? (do you get a hit Ctrl-something to enter setup message out of it or not?).
My only experiece with the nvram stuff is on x86 boxes (I've got a Fireport 40
Dual at home). It just might be possible for you to put the card in an intel
box, set it up on that box and then transfer it, but I really don't know if
that should work or not.
