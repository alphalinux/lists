Just for closure, here is a reference. Phil's got it right, priority is
7-->0,15->8. Table 39, page 84 of the SPI-2 SCSI spec, T10 Project 1142D,
rev 20a. Current rev at T10 is 20b.

Visit http://www.symbios.com/t10/ for the specs...
