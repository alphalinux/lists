AFAIK, most of the PCI cards supported in XFree86 work on the Multia.
However only a few are tested, and therefore shipped, in Red Hat Linux.
The best way to determine which cards work, is to check which X servers
are included. In 5.2 it's the S3 server, and the Matrox portions of the
SVGA server.

As always, YMMV.
