> >I've never heard of retail cards with native alpha setup utilities.

> The Intraserver cards are compliant with every PCI based Alpha I have seen,

OK, this is an UX board with ARCS BIOS 1.3.060. The boot sequence from
power off is:

1) VGA BIOS (Matrox Millenium II)
2) ARCS BIOS
3) ldmilo.exe (I think there is no output on the screen ??)
4) VGA BIOS
5) ?? (There pops up a message of about 3 or 4 lines below the VGA
messages. But it vanishes so fast, I can't read it.)
6) MILO
7) Linux

I tried to hold down ^C or ^A at various times during boot sequence but
without success.

In the end: why should I have this NVRAM thing (the setup utility of the
SCSI controller)? Everything works fine now and as far as I see I can
configure everything I need through software (boot parameter, /proc/scsi).
By the way: termination is dip switched on the board :-)
