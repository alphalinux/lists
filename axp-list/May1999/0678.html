>
> I just noticed that libx11.so is lowercase. I cp'd it to libX11.so and
> now i get
> /sbin/loader: Fatal Error: cannot map libXmu.so
>
> could someone /usr/shlib/ls -l for me?

This is the same problem as before. Do a ldd on the app you want to run to get
a list of libs.
Compare them to your /usr/shlib. Case matters so check libXmu.so for existance
then capitalization. I don't know how you copied your libs over in the first
place to get libx11.so out of libX11.so. All you had to do was disable prompts
in the ftp session, then mget * to the corresponding location on the linux
box. I don't think execution permission is necessary but just in case : chmod
0777 * in the shlib dir. I believe your kernal may already support a.out since
it is successfully calling loader and locating the libs if they are there. But
a kernel rebuild never hurts, while you're at it get the latest stable
release...what is it 2.2.9 now or something and the ac patch if available.
