  Okay,

  I put in some PC-100 DIMMs from the start, it will boot up, I can
install windozeNT, etc. from a SCSI cdrom.
  I can upgrade the AlphaBios Firmware from a floppy I made, Version
5.66 I believe. I realise that I do not have to update the SRM
firmware to load AlphaLinux, but still....

  I have been trying various SCSI HBA's in it in different slots, etc.
What combination works depends on what video card is in there and in
which PCI slot! i.e., an ATI Mach64 will work with an Adaptec 2940uw
either card being in slot 1&2. However, a Trident Providea in slot 1
and an Kouwell HBA in slot 2 will not work, no video displayed. But
move the video to slot 4 and the combo works.. Weird, but kool..

  Back to my original ??, can YOU boot to SRM on your PC164sx, and can
YOU load firmware from a DIGITAL firmware CDROM, or floppy during a
failsafe booter condition?
