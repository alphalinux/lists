> Iss there a fairly basic howtoo on peforming an install via ftp?
> I have a couple 486's I'd like to istall linux on, but neither of them
> have a cdrom.

As a matter of fact, it's simple, just create the bootdisks
(the boot images are on the RedHat cdrom), boot the 486's with
the bootdisk(s) and choose FTP install from the installmenu.

It's a pretty straight forward job after that...
