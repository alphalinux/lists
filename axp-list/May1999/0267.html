At 05:40 PM 5/11/99 -0300, ALEXANDRE A. P. SUAIDE wrote:
>Hello,
>
>I have an alpha station 255 (avanti) and I would like to upgrade
>its RAM. The compaq seller here charge me about US$800 each 64 MB
>module. It is too expensive. I would like to know if PC memories
>works fine. If its works, what kind of memory I should buy?

You need 60ns true parity fast-page-mode (FPM) memory 72-pin. I just
bought 2 32MB modules here in the US for about $150. There are those
who say 70ns is the correct speed, but I'm running 112mb of 60ns in my
AS 200 and it's running just fine.
