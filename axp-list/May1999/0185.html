just got the replacement UX board today. It works fine with 32-bit in
the 64-bit slot, and it powers down everytime, and it doesn't hang
detecting the scsi disk from a cold boot.

strangest is that the original board arrived with the samsung box
packed into a spotless larger box with soft cardboard padding - and
it was defective.

the replacement board was just shipped in it's samsung box, and had
obviously had a rough time, the corners were dented and some kind of
oil had been spilled over half of it! - yet this one works..

the weird and wonderful world of express couriers...
