Sean Hunter wrote:
>
> If you say what it is, I could be more specifically helpful.
>
> alias scsi_hostadapter aic7xxx
>
> ...autoloads the aic7xxx module to drive an adaptec scsi card,
> for example. "modprobe -c" will give you a lot more examples. You
> could also look in the HOWTOs.
>
> If the module is used it will stay resident.

It's a driver for a special porpose piece of hardware which doesn't
really fall into any of the obvious classes. Let's say it is called
"xyz", and the loadable module can be found in
/lib/modules/2.2.x-y/misc/plx.o. What I would like to achieve is the
same effect as:

    insmod xyz

issued from a script somewhere early in the boot process, but instead
driven by a line in /etc/conf.modules.
