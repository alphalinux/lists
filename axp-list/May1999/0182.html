OK, I gotta reply to this.....true Alpha will probably never get cheap
enough to compete at the same leve at Intel, but with the advent of 64 bit
chips from Intel, it could happen. Then one needs the software developement
to happen for Alpha, and finally then marketing people have to market it as
a kick ass system for the money.

NOW....that last sentence REALLY struck a nerve in me!!!!! Intel has
nothing to do with "keeping" Alpha around. Intel will no longer make Alpha
chips in a few months. I have hung out in the lab, and let me tell
you...the Samsung EV67's are smokin' the Intel EV6's. So, once the EV67 is
mainstream (in another few months) Samsung WILL be the main supplier and
Intel is out. Then they can no longer give us crappy/slow EV6s (if such a
thing exists :) ). Long Live Alpha!!!!! Merced is doomed! so is McKinley!!
