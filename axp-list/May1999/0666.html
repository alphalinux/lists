Here is the problem I had on my Alpha PC164LX. Same error.....to fix it,
follow these instructions.

You will need to edit /usr/src/linux/asm-alpha/shmparam.h. Modify SHMMAX to
be 1000000 and then re-compile your kernel.

This should clear up that problem. Any more problems, ler me know. Also
remember that filenames that have spaces in them must have quotes around
them. XCDRoast does not do this. I am not even sure the quotes will make it
work. I always rename the files. Hope this helps....
