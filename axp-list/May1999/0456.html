Interesting observation. There are two answers: first, not all Alpha
project names are Japanese; there's Jensen, Avanti, Mustang, XLT, Dodge,
Durango, and others. What you're noticing is the common theme in naming
volume servers, followed by an accidental similarity in a new host chip
set; the volume servers, Mikasa and Noritake, are named after makers of
fine (dining) china, while the Tsunami is named for a natural
phenomenon, i.e., the monster wave. The former are Alpha servers, the
latter is a host chip set for the EV6. If you want a rather
comprehensive list of Alpha project names, look at
http://dutlbcz.lr.tudelft.nl/alphant/, and take the "Trivia" link.

Cheers,
jwl
