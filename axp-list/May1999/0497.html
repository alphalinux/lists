I like neither the price of RedHat CDs or the stability of their
installation system (although I do like it when it works!); however I do
respect them for employing people like Alan to work on the kernel which I

provide support for critical uses of Linux is important to the way the
Linux bandwagon has started to roll very fast over the last few months.
