>>> David Cross said:
>
> AlphaPC 164LX running Redhat 5.1. Kernel currently 2.0.34. Trying to
> move to 2.0.36. Had similar problems when trying to move to 2.0.35.

Pristine 2.0.34/35/36/37 do NOT contain all the needed Alpha patches.

Please use the (complete) 2.0.35 patched sources available on
gatekeeper.dec.com:

        /pub/DEC/Linux-Alpha/Kernels/alpha-sources-2.0.35-0.2.tar.gz

Unfortunately, the patches have not been ported (by me, anyway) to any of
the later releases (36 and 37), so you're stuck with 2.0.35 unless you can
find later ones elsewhere, or wish to port them yourself.

Good luck.
