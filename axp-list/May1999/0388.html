WARNING!!! THIS MESSAGE IS NOT FOR THE FAINT OF HEART ( or mind )!!

I have NOT switched from SRM to AlphaBIOS. However, I have been told that
the key to switching back is to start a firmware update from SRM, and hit
the reset ( or power ) button while it is loading the image. This will
force the firmware "failsafe" mode which will allow you to re-install
AlphaBIOS.

AGAIN, I have NOT done this, I am only relaying info that I have collected.
I take no responsibility for dead systems.
