This is the best package around that I have found! This should do anything
that you need. Two things that you have to remember. Compile Other SCSI
devices in your kernel. I believe it is the fourth option under SCSI
support and there is a file called shmparam.h in your kernel tree. Do a
find on it. Edit it and change the size of SHMAX or somthing close to that.
I replaced the 3 with an F and that message goes away about SHMget. Good
luck
