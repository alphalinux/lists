as an aside, i now have my samsung 21164a 533 running succesfully at 600mhz
for well over a week. constantly running rc5des and occasional kernel
compiles without any problems.

Ambient temperature was already quite high due to the computer beside it
(with a "Scorch-me" TM K6-233 and a bunch of uw 7k/10k scsi disks), so this
says extremely good things about the 21164a's clockability.

i did have over heating problems at 533mhz causing segfaults, but then i
realised i had the fan on the cpu the wrong round! (you'd think correct
orientation would be with the label on the fan upwards, but no....) :)
