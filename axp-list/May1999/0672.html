I just added a quantum 6 gig disk to my multia 233. I am using an
external box, and away I went. All was well until I started compiling kde
4 packages at a time (Hey I had the disk space, why not?) After getting
through about 1/2 the packages, I started getting scsi CONTROLLER errors.

In fact the damn message read something like
"Duh something bad is happening in the NCR 810(or whatever)"
And then essentially a locked-up machine. I knew the 2940's did this
(often and repeatably in my expierence) but in the 3-4 years I've dealt
with multias, the NCR's have been solid. Was there a code change in
2.2.7?? I'm running from that machine NOW with no provlems, just one
disk drive.
