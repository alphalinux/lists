On Sun, 2 May 1999, designer seven wrote:

> Hello,
>
> I am trying to compile neXtaw 0.5.1 using egcs-1.1.2.
> Unfortunately, I get the following error:
>
> gcc -c -I. -I/usr/X11R6/include -Dlinux
> -D__alpha__ -D_POSIX_C_SOURCE=199309L -D_POSIX_SOURCE
> -D_XOPEN_SOURCE=500L -D_BSD_SOURCE -D_SVID_SOURCE
> -DUSEGRAY -DNO_MENU_LINES -DFUNCPROTO=15 -DNARROWPROTO
> -DHAS_WCHAR_H -DHAS_WCTYPE_H -DNO_WIDEC_H -O6
> -mcpu=ev56 -Wa,-m21164a -finline-functions
> -fomit-frame-pointer Scrollbar.c -o
> unshared/Scrollbar.o
> ../../gcc/function.c:2392: Internal compiler error in
> function fixup_memory_subreg
> make: *** [Scrollbar.o] Error 1

That is most probably a bug in the compiler - try downloading the latest
EGCS snapshots (ftp://egcs.cygnus.com/pub/egcs/snapshots).
Lets see if it is still broken in that.

A quick way out is to try turning down the optimisation a little - that
often causes bugs to go and hide.
