On Thu, May 13, 1999 at 08:53:46AM -0700, David Holley wrote:
>
> Back to my original ??, can YOU boot to SRM on your PC164sx, and can
> YOU load firmware from a DIGITAL firmware CDROM, or floppy during a
> failsafe booter condition?
>

On a PC164SX I bought elsewhere, I WAS able to switch it from running
ARC firmware to SRM. But they seem to be mutually exclusive. I had to
flash the SRM console firmware in, and then it seemed like I lost the
ARC firmware. I'm guessing that maybe the SX164 only has enough NVRAM
for one image. I prefer the SRM console, so it wasn't a problem for
me.

I didn't need to load the firmware from a failsafe floppy, a normal
FAT-formatted one worked just fine as I recall. I'd guess that, so
long as the ARC firmware sees and can talk to your CDROM drive, a
CDROM with the firmware on it would work fine as well.
