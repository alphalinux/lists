I've used succesfully the wget (also standar in the distribution) utility
to fetch whole directories, and no problem with the symbolik links. Also,
when the connection fall down, it will try to connect again, and the most
nice feature, it check the files fully/partially downloaded and continue
from that point (I really like this! :) ). Be sure to specify the deepest
level of recursivity, wich is 5 by default, i. e.

wget -c -r -l0 <url>
