Miller, Scott K. wrote:
>
> Has anyone successfully compiled PGP5.0i on RH5.1? pgpMD5.c appears to
> have problems.

That's what I did:

./lib/pgp/include/pgpUsuals.h:
forced typedef of word64 and int64 types to be unconditional

./apps/pgp/filter.c:
changed type of len in keyValidityOK () from int to size_t, which
lead to a compiler warning and --- more seriously to a SIGSEGV
during runtime when issuing a encryption command like
'pgpe -r scr@iis.fhg.de -sat bla.txt'

Regards,
Stefan Schroepfer
