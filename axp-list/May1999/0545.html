That seems like a *really* shallow view of what Red Hat does...

The easiset difference between RH and MS is that you can
download a complete OS and a copy of the manual for free
(save the cost of the internet connection), MS does not.

The difference between the version of RH that is shrinkwrapped
contains commercial software licensed from third parties (who
want to be paid for their efforts) and support. MS does NOT
support their OS installation directly, they rely on the vendor
(OEM) in nearly all cases - go to http://www.microsoft.com and
look for a phone number to call for problems with WinNT
installation...

There is very little compariosn possible between MS and RH,
their busuness models are so radically different!

Also, it is my understanding that there will be a lower cost (yet
still supported) release of RH Linux 6.0 coming - it will have
email support only, no phone support.
