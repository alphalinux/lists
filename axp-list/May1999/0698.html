Ok.. I just experienced the most bizarre crash on my
Alpha... ending my 40 day uptime since 2.2.7

I'm writing this to the list in case someone has some
insight on how I might avoid this again.

The problem happened in the middle of playing an mp3
file from a smbmounted fs using mpg123... the player
crashed... and then mpg123 refused to die... tried a

killall -KILL mpg123

but it refused to die... then I found out I couldn't
umount the smbfs anymore.. "device busy"...

being use to the windows way of "reboot fixes all" ..
I tried a reboot... but on the reboot the floppy
controller was missing and the keyboard and mouse
timeout... even though at MILO it responded to the ESC
key...

The only way I got around it was ssh'ing in and
shutdown -halt now and doing a cold reboot... now I'm
back...

this was very odd.... anyone with similar
experience...???
