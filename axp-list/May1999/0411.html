Hey everybody..

I am putting together another Multia for use as a bridge/gateway on a
friend's network.

It'll get a PCMCIA ethernet card to bridge a 100bT network (Sun and SGI
boxes he uses for CAD and whatnot) and a 10b2 loop of PCs (game and soon
web browsing machines around the house).

It also gets a 56k PCMCIA modem so that it can establish PPP to his
provider and do IP masquerading for everyone.

The serial ports will likely get used to hand PPP to two older Macs that
don't hook into his lan.

The machine might act as a print server for everyone too.. dunno yet.

I'm pretty confident that the Multia can handle the load -- anyone have
a counterexample?

We are planning on 64M of RAM for the Multia.

Anyone know of a decent 3.5" HD for the Multia that gives reasonable
performance without generating lots of heat? Obviously it'll be a 3600
RPM drive (anyone used 7200 RPM successfully?).
