I have a HP8110i ATAPI cd writer and I had no trouble getting it to work
proprely. Just follow the ATAPI instructions in the xcdroast package.
When I compiled my kernel I included SCSI support (NOT AS A MODULE) this
made everything work. You also need to scsi emulation and generic scsi
support built into the kernel. Then add the line to lilo.conf: append =
"hdX = ide-scsi" with the quotes. Substitute b,c,d etc for X, which
ever your represents your ATAPI cd writer. Also the writer needs to be
the master drive on the second controller. Hope this helps.

Craig Oettle
