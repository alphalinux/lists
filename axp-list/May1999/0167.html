I know nothing about terminators on devices themselves; I suppose if you
have a disk drive with an active terminator switch you can use it that
way--check your documentation of course--but my experience has led me to
avoid terminating within any device (probably because I tend to move
SCSI devices around between several systems). I always terminate the
SCSI chain with an external terminator, even if I have to use an extra
piece of SCSI cable to adapt from the device port to the terminator.
