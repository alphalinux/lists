> Regarding X Windows, how do you permint incoming x client connections to
> the
> local X server? I've tried running a few xterms on remote machines but
> they
> say their access is denied - I'm sure it's somewhere in /etc but I haven't
> found it yet!
>
hi mark,

have a look at the xhost command, to allow all local connections:

xhost +local:

to allow an xterm on machine.somewhere.com to connect to your Xserver:

xhost +inet:machine.somewhere.com

and you can put in your .xinitrc, or .xsession if you use xdm.

regards,

Paul.
