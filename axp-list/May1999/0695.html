Not really. The SRM for the LX is free for download because
when you paid for the LX you also paid for an SRM license. The SX was only
equipped with Alphabios because it was intended as an NT machine so they
didn't bother with an SRM license that was going to go unused. This
contributed to the price difference between the two boards. The SRM
firmware is some where on ftp.digital.com .
