I'm happy to inform you all of some new rpms that are available at
ftp.alphalinux.org

ssh-1.2 & 2.0 built against glibc-2.1, courtesy of designer 7
jdk-12 v5, Uncle George
mozilla M5 and libIDL, courtesy of Leslie Donaldson

If you haven't noticed there's an incoming/ directory in the ftp site
this is open to anyone to upload stuff. I don't need a LSM or anything
just drop me a note telling me what you uploaded. When you do build rpms
that you intend to upload here please build them with -mcpu=ev4 flags so
everyone can use them. Optimized packages are welcome as long as they are
accompanied by an ev4 compliant version.
