On Thu, 20 May 1999, Brian Rerecich wrote:

> Im having the same problem....please tell me explicitly how to fix it and
> where. Thanks a lot in advance!! I hate restarting Netscape all the time!!!

Edit /etc/X11/XF86Config

Find the 'FontPath "unix:-1"` statement and comment it out

Add a 'FontPath "<dir>"' line for each subdirectory of /usr/X11R6/lib/X11/fonts
you have.

Restart X.
