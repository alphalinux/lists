Albrecht Jacobs wrote:
>
> The only way _I_ know to configure the NVRAM of the Intraserver is to put
> it in an intel PC, configure it, put it back. Not much fun.

On many Alpha boards (not on every one, but this definitely works on
LX and UX as I was doing that many times) you hit Ctrl-C when asked
in a power-up sequence and a controller configuration screen comes up.
At least two controllers do show up, as I have seen that myself, and
most likely all your three. :-) You just follow menus and prompts.
