just typ in boot floppy at the milo prompt, this should load the kernel from
floppy, and should , after a while, ask for the Ramdisk
When inserting the ramdisk and pressing enter, the prompt should read:
'found ramdisk at...' . After this you should get prompts for where your
install files are located (FTP... CDROM ...) After you have installed the
lot,

Make an entry in the bootmenu, in which you point to where linload.exe is
located (os loader), give the path pointing to milo, and for os options:
boot sda3:vmlinux.gz root=/dev/sda3

(depending on where your linux partition is located, this points to the
first disk partition 3 SCSI, sdb2 points to the second disk the second
partition SCSI, no experience with ide, must be along the same way)
