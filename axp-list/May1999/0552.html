This is a classic problem that I have had in the past. These errors are
reported my AlphaBIOS? or SRM? They indicate that the value stored by the
board for where to go to boot up are wrong. The cause of my problem was a
dead battery in the motherboard. I have also seen this happen freakishly on
an LX board with a good battery. My suggestion is to find out what the
correct paths for the bootup are and use AlphaBIOS/SRM console to modify
these settings.

If you need help with this, let me know...

-Brian
