Regarding the BIOS.. there is an interesting
experience I had... though I don't know how it applies
to you.

I bought a Millenium II with the latest BIOS at the
time.. and it would not work at all. I went back to
the store and exchanged it (however, the card worked
on regular PCs... they tested it at the store).. and
got the exact same card (I checked the chips on the
card.. as there are two versions.. one with a faster
RAMDAC and faster WRAM (50ns vs 60ns)... and they were
identical except that the BIOS was two versions older
than the previous card... and it worked fine...

As for capabilities.. I'm not sure exactly what you
are looking for.. but I believe it can run at
1600x1400x32bpp @ 75Hz. And the Millenium I is not
capable of PCI bus mastering... thought the Millenium
II is capable.

D.
