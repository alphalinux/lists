On Wed, 26 May 1999, Jaap Schellekens wrote:

> Does anybody know if Bruce Perens' malloc debugger
> works on Alpha/Linux? No Redhat packages are available (they
> are for intel, and they saved me a number of times...)
> which make me wonder if it is worth trying it on the alpha.

 Of course, it works. I am using binaries I built using the source
package that is available at
"ftp://ftp.amg.gda.pl/pub/OS/Linux/people/macro/SRPMS.glibc21/ElectricFence-2.2.0-1.src.rpm",
but if you prefer compiling from a tarball you may get the sources from
"ftp://perens.com/pub/ElectricFence/" (there may be a newer version
available there -- I haven't checked it for a while).
