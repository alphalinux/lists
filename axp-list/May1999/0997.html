I don't know what the first one is, but the rest are things associated with
GNOME. Try ftp://ftp.gnome.org/pub/GNOME/gnome-1.0/redhat/alpha/
I got in just now, but you'll probably have to use one of the mirror sites.

libesd is in esound. gmodule is in glib. The rest should be clear from the
RPM names.

BTW, RH6 comes with GNOME, so most/all of the packages are in
redhat-6.0/alpha/RedHat/RPMS/:
esound-0.2.12-4.alpha.rpm esound-devel-0.2.12-4.alpha.rpm
glib-1.2.1-2.alpha.rpm
glib-devel-1.2.1-2.alpha.rpm
gtk+-1.2.1-10.alpha.rpm
gtk+-devel-1.2.1-10.alpha.rpm
gnome-libs-1.0.8-8.alpha.rpm
gnome-libs-devel-1.0.8-8.alpha.rpm
gnome-core-1.0.4-34.alpha.rpm
gnome-core-devel-1.0.4-34.alpha.rpm
audiofile-0.1.6-5.alpha.rpm
audiofile-devel-0.1.6-5.alpha.rpm
etc...
