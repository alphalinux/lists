There's a problem with the crystal on the original UX boards that causes
autosensing to be problematic at best. I've found that verison 0.89H of
the driver is fairly reliable, though I have yet to try the latest
version.

You can also look into replacing the crystal. I think if you search
through axp-list there's a message about which crystal to buy etc....
