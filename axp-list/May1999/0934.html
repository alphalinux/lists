When you did the install you should have created a small (about 2Meg) FAT
(DOS) partition at the begining of the disk. Put linload.exe and milo in there,
then you can set AlphaBIOS to boot from that partition.

If your partition was /dev/sda1, then put your MILO floppy in and do a:
dd if=/dev/fd0 of=/dev/sda1 bs=1440k

and that'll put both files into that partition.

Then you can remove the floppy drive.
