It appears that the version of rpm in the Red Hat 6.0 for Alpha
distribution is broken wrt. the --prefix option. The rpm file this
version of rpm was taken from is:

    rpm-3.0-6.0.alpha.rpm

A quick look at the code from the correponding SRPM suggests that any
time --prefix is specified the following error message will be issued:

    "only one of --prefix and --relocate may be used"

This will happen even when --relocate is not specified.

Does anyone know of a fixed version?
