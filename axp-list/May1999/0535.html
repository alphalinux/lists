On Mon, 17 May 1999, Paul Jakma wrote:

> 1. How do i get the ARCSBIOS firmware to automatically boot a
> selection.
>
> I've created a couple of different variable sets, they work. i've
> selected one as default. but it won't boot up without human
> intervention. annoying.

It should look like:

AUTOLOAD = YES
COUNTDOWN = 30
...

This works for me (PC164UX2 with ARCSBIOS)

> 2. Is there any way to make the UX secure?

Lock it away. Our Alpha lives in a 19" rack (with door lock). The power
switch and reset switch are behind a lockable closure, the keyboard is
also lockable. I suppose that's not what you are looking for?
