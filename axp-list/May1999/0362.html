Here the obvious solution, but maybe you've already tried this.....

In /usr/shlib put libX11.so from your Tru64 UNIX box.
also try copying /etc/svc.conf to that same location on your Tru64 UNIX
box. This works for me when trying to run Tru64 Netscape 4.51.
Good luck....
