That's what I get for staying up so late - my brain was asleep, but my
fingers were still typing... ;-}

You are absolutely correct; the RH 6.0 generic kernel ought to be later than
the 2.2.3 I pointed you to, and may even have had the sym53c8xx driver in the
configuration.

Are you sure you don't have an 895 or 896 instead of the 875? Those would
be more likely to cause problems.

Also, you could still try moving the card to one of the top slots (ie onto
PCI bus 0, if its not already there); the sym53c8xx driver in the current
sources (through 2.2.9, I believe) doesn't work with controllers on the
second primary PCI bus.

Good luck, and let me know how you make out...
