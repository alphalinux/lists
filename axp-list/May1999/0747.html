Hello Jason:

This controller will likely support the upgrade, however the 3rd party
driver will only function on x86 based Linux systems and not the Alpha
platform. In addition the OEM controller you have will not function
with 3.xx on the Alpha platform.

You may identidy which controller you have by entering the revision
number D040*** on the back of the card as the keyword for a search on
our FAQ.

regards,

Tom Ericksen
