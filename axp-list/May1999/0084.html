>>> Kenneth Crandall said:
>
> AFAIK, most of the PCI cards supported in XFree86 work on the Multia.
> However only a few are tested, and therefore shipped, in Red Hat Linux.
> The best way to determine which cards work, is to check which X servers
> are included. In 5.2 it's the S3 server, and the Matrox portions of the
> SVGA server.

IIRC, the problem with Millennium in Multia was not whether it would fit,
it's whether the BIOS emulator in the ARC console could run the Matrox BIOS
code. And the answer, I believe, was no, not even with the last firmware
release available for the Multia... :-\

Best find a 2-meg S3 of some flavor, their BIOS code is usually simpler
and of no problem to the emulator.

Good luck.
