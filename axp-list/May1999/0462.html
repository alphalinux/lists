>>> Richard Henderson said:
>
> The bastards at Digital ne Compaq changed the spec sometime after
> the last moment. The "CIX" extension has been renamed "FIX" and
> no longer includes cttz, ctlz, or ctpop. Evil people. I really
> wanted those.

Yes, it *is* regrettable that EV6 has no "count" extensions, but "bastards"
and "evil"? Strong words for a pragmatic business decision:

Pass 1 of EV6 had *no* FP support at all.

Pass 2 of EV6 had FP support, and a little extra, and it was decided to ship
these for revenue, after a long unplanned delay (9 months?) getting it
stable (actual rev shipped was 2.3, IIRC).

Pass 3 of EV6 was intended to have full CIX support, but was never
productized; it simply did not make timeliness nor economic sense. Instead,
it has served as the core for EV67 (sampling now internally), and is
believed to have a full CIX implementation by those I consulted.

> Kill the __alpha_cix__ bits in linux/include/asm-alpha/bitops.h.

Save them for EV67 and beyond...
