>
> I am trying to mount a Dec Unix formatted disk under Rh5.2 alpha and I
> don't know what type to specify. Does anyone know?

Yes. If this is UFS then you have to compile yourself a kernel
with UFS support (DEC variant is ok but 2.2 kernels have some
improvements in that area).

DU could also format a disk with advfs type. In that case you need
an original OS machine as this format is prioprietary (read - no
information) and no drivers outside DU. Export then via NFS and
mount this on your Linux box.
