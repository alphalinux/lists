So I did managed to get my Alpha disklabeled drive to read under x86 Linux.

The quick summary is that I went into include/linux/genhd.h in my kernel
source directory and removed the

#ifdef __alpha__
#endif

lines from around

#define CONFIG_OSF_PARTITION 1

which means that I'd have the OSF paritition support that's available in
drivers/block/genhd.c enabled no matter what my platform. Then I
recompiled the kernel, and was able to access all of my partitions.

Reading through genhd.c was quite interesting, by the way. Especially if
you've dealt with partition issues before...

Thanks to all those who helped.
