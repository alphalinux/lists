No experience with that particular RPM (I use a pre-GTK Mozilla for my
browsing because it mostly works), but newer Mozilla builds require a
recent (1.2.x) version of GTK/Glib to be around. The nspr RPM might
also still be required (it was at M3 which is the last "recent"
Mozilla I've built).

By the way, M5 is out now. Haven't tried building it due to lack of
HD space, but someone with a bigger HD might wish to give it a shot.
