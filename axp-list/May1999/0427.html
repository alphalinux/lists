I dont think u mean at different clock rates, but at a (slightly) different
time when the timer hits the interval. Ie resolution of 1ns, cpu clock gets
ticked at 1.1 ns, cpu #2 get ticked at 1.3ns, cpu #3 at 1.5ns. This is to
prevent everyone from grabbing the Semmaphores/mutex's at the same time, where
only one will get the lock, and the other ( quite figurally ) spin whout doing
any usefull work. This was the strategy used on the SMP PDP-10's
