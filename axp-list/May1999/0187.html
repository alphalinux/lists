For the XP1000, I think the upgrade will be a substantial cost if one wants
a faster cache (highly recommended) since the core logic is also on the
board. I don't know what the DS10 is..maybe you ment DS20? The DS20 will be
fully upgradable with a simple new daughter card. It should cost less than
a whole new system, otherwise they wouldn't sell the upgrade. The EV67
parts will be at 667MHz and will also come with a 222MHz DDR cache (so its
really 444MHz). DDR cache = 222MHz Address rate/444MHz Data rate. This will
show a VAST imrovement in overall performance.....

-Brian
