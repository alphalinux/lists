At last, we've our Jensen box running with Red Hat Linux 5.2 !! Thanks
to everbody in this list, specially to Tom King.

The more trickiest thing to solve was the lock of the machine after
loading the kernel; it locked after mount the root filesystem in read only
mode, in the initial stage of the boot. I solve this changing the kernel
on the pseudo-ram disk by the one located on the cd-rom under the
kernels/jensen-s.gz and re-doing the installation. As Tom points to me, we
use the aboot method instead the "traditional" one.

With a little edition of the /etc/aboot.conf, the machine boots well from
the hard disk via the SRM console, altougth don't when the reboot command
is issued from a root session. Well, I can live with this ... :)
