There's some rpms of mozilla M4 at ftp.alphalinux.org that were uploaded
by someone. You can find them in mozilla/. Some thoughts.

- Renders pages much better than M3 did. It does freshmeat , and slashdot
well. ALO looks like hell :)

- Still uses alot of cpu when it renders but it's nowhere near the memory
hog it used to be. It's usable.

- Mail client doesn't work, neither do preferences, font sizes, or right
click menu.

If you don't have a browser or use KFM give it a shot. It's worth the
download.
