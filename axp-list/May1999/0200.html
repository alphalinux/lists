On Thu, 6 May 1999, Kai Schmidt wrote:

> I have a little problem with one of my multia's. I got the machine two
> weeks ago, I installed the OS everything was ok, suddenly the machine
> was dead, no ping no telnet. After rebooting I was able to connect via
> telnet but after a few keystrokes it turned dead again. Only this time
> a reboot didn't help.
>
> There is no screen output I supose no video signal, nothing, the fan
> is still running, is this the well know'n heat death?

I've run into two problems with the multias I've used. One is that the
power connector is *very* sensitive. Try reseating it is seeing if that
helps. In my experience, it may be necessary to leave the cover off as
putting it back on will jiggle the wires and cause them to not connect any
more. Sometimes you get lucky, sometimes you don't...

The second problem I've seen is the internal battery dying. You can get a
replacement easy enough, but everything will have been forgotten (disk
partitions, boot selection, etc).

I've not actually seen one outright die.
