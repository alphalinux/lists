Hello !
One question coming up fairly often is "Which browsers are available on
Linux (Alpha) ?". Since I havn't found a good complete listing, I made one
myself, you can find it on

http://www.itp.uni-hannover.de/~kreutzm/en/lin_browser.html

The list includes a column indicating download-locations for binaries for
Linux/Alpha if available, along with source-locations. For these locations
I need your help.

If you are building one browser or another on a fairly regular basis for
Linux/Alpha I would like to include your ftp-location so that interested
users find the info more easily. Currently I only considered the "official"
download-locations (as mentioned on the homepages of the browsers) and the
distribution-specific locations, the latter probably updated rather
infrequently (compared to the developement). Especially I remember someone
building amaya and mozilla (I didn't find a current one off their homepages).

If you know about more browsers, I would be happy to include them.

If the description is incomplete or misleading (most ones were taken from
the appropriate home pages), please tell me.

If you know a "better" download-location or a download location for a
specialiced package (debian, suse, redhat) I would like to know that also.

Last but not least: If you find errors in my spelling and grammer I will
be more than willing to correct them since I am not a native speaker.

Thanks for your effort.

               Helge
