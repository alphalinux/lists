On Tue, 15 Feb 2000, Joerg Bruehe wrote:

} How do I open this box ?

I've been opening and reopening my 200 4/166 a lot this past week! You
*must* have the key - as it lets you simply slide the cover off [no
screws]. The keyhole is located in the back of the box, on the left, above
the serial ports.

I happen to have 2 keys, so if you want, I can ship my spare off to
you. It's =probably= a standard PC key, as a few years back, every x86 PC
that shipped had keys like these it seemed like. Once you get the key,
just turn it to the unlocked position, and then slide the top/sides of the
case towards you [if you are positioned at the rear of the unit].
