I just got a DEC PW433a and followed the instructions here

ftp://ftp.digital.com/pub/Digital/Alpha/firmware/readmes/v5.6/doc/digitalpw_
v71_fw_relnote.pdf

to upgrade my firmware. I created a firwmware upgrade disk (as described)
and booted the disk from SRM. The firmware upgrade utility gave me the
choice of upgrading some or all of my BIOS elements (SRM, AlphaBIOS,
etc...). I chose "All" and it proceeded without a hitch. Now I'm completely
up-to-date.

Good Luck!!
