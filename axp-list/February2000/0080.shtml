> > I have an Alpha XL266, is it little endian or big endian????
>
> Little.

Partially correct. The processor itself is bi-endian (supporting
 either) as are MIPS, but the kernel runs in little mode. A good
decision to make when porting Linux, as - predictably - more software
would be coded first on the little-endian x86 arch.
