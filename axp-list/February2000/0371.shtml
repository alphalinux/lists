Hmm,
If you want to boot directly off the Adaptec , you should have kept your
AlphaBIOS on it.
>From SRM , the Adaptec won't be recognised.
If you do want to keep using the SRM the options are:
-using a floppy to boot milo or aboot from, which will recognise the
Adaptec
and allow you to boot further from that. (Someone correct me if I'm
wrong about aboot)
-Getting a symbios or ncr based scsi controller.

I myself use an Adaptec with AlphaBIOS on an SX board. I use MILO.

Good luck!
