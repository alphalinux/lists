BogoMIPS is just that, BOGUS MIPS. They're just there so people can compare
their machines against each other's for fun. One should not base any
meaningful decisions on BogoMIPS since it really doesn't accurately reflect
the speed of your machine. Abnormal configurations can produce very very low
BogoMIPS or very high ones with a normal processor.
