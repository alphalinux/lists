On Tue, 22 Feb 2000, Liam Bedford wrote:

> It's AlphaBIOS, I'll have another poke at it this evening

Not on a UX! It's ARCSBios, which is't ARC BIOS or AlphaBIOS. The UX has
it's own firmware and doesn't support AlphaBIOS or SRM.

Chances are that the card's BIOS isn't making it through the x86 BIOS
emulator in the firmware.
