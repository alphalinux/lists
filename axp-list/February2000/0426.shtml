Sometimes Murphy's Law amazes even me.
The only fixes I made to 2.3.47pre-8 were to add include statements
for asm/errno.h where they should have been. Built and booted without
a hitch after that.

About five minutes after hitting multiuser, the system froze and
the kernel began spitting out errors from the scsi driver.
Had to do an emergency sync and shut it down cold. When it came
back up, all filesystems were corrupted. Not sure if I can
repair them. If any errors were written out, they're unretrievable
now.
Controller is a Qlogic 1040B in a DPW 500a.

People wonder why I work on and use 2.2.x....
