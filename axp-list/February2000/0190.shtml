> /sbin/hdparm -Tt /dev/<dev>
Considering bonnie allows you to define the block size, one may pick one
that does not fit in the various device caches. Also if you run enough
iterations the cache may affect the outcome less. As writing to cache is a
lot faster than actually writing to the media, it will soon fill, and then
you are getting real throughput from that point on.
