Paul

No you cannot load AS1200 firmware on the 5300.

But you can switch it to a restricted Unix SRM.

setup->cmos-setup->f6(advanced cmos) then 1/2 down the screen select SRM
Unix console.

Then F10 and follow instructions to save the setting.

followed by a power cycle.

No you cannot run Unix or VMS but it will run Linux just fine.

JJ
note: to switch back type "set os_type nt" at the srm console prompt, and
power cycle.
