Ever since upgrading from the 2.2.5 kernel, my AS 3300 is
crashing frequently. If I do any sort of networking the machine
will hang anywhere from immediately to up to 20 or 30 minutes
afterwards. In particular, FTP causes an immediate crash
with the 2.2.13 kernel. 2.2.14 holds up most of the time but
eventually crashes during long downloads. When I say crash
I mean it's frozen and I have to reboot. I can't even ping from
my other boxes.

I had no previous problems with 2.2.5 under RH6.0. It *seems*
like it might be a race condition in the networking code somewhere.
I'll see if I can get a counter when I reboot and check it against
the system.map. Also, my other Alpha's don't share this problem.
It's only the AS3300. It's not a hardware fail...the same components
work in other boxes and RH6.0/kernel 2.2.5 has no problems on this
box.

Anyone else with flakey network hangs with 2.2.13/14?

/Michael
