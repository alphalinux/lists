This is true for the UDB VX40 models. VX41 and VX42 models will detect
the 12 meg simms when used in slot 0 and 1. I have 2 12 MB simms (together
with 2 32 MB simms) in my UDB-VX42 and they work perfectly well and it is
correctly detected as 88 MB.
(see: page 7 of the "Multia MultiClient Desktop Service Information"
which can be found at http://www.brouhaha.com/~eric/computers/udb-man.pdf).
