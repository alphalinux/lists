A big deal was made when IBM announced their "open" powerpc motherboard
design. Should/could/would Compaq et al. open up past designs? For
example, Digital has had design info on the PC164 at
http://ftp.digital.com/pub/Digital/info/semiconductor/alpha/dsc-software-alpha.html
for a long time. An open, current design would speed the creation of an
inexpensive Alpha box, plus win good will points from "the community."
