OK. I'll state the obvious. You've got the kernels configured
wrong for your machine. What machine are you trying this on
(its HW config too) and what does your .config look like?

There are different models of systems and they have specific
chipsets you need to configure for them. I generally use the
generic option rather than the specific system type and then
only select HW I'm using on my systems.
