I did a minimal, custom install of RH6.1 over HTTP from the rufus site. I
then manually FTP'd sndconfig, playmidi, awesfx, and sox and used rpm
to install them.

Next, I ran sndconfig and told it I had a Windows Sound System
(AD1848/CS4248/CS4231) device at IO530/IRQ9/DMA3/DMA3. The Linus waveform
played fine. But when it tried to do MIDI, sndconfig said "
/lib/modules/2.2.13-.09/misc/opl3.0:init_module : device or resource
busy". sndconfig then went back to the beginning, where I had to say I
had a WSS card, etcetcetc.

What did I mess up there? My /etc/conf.modules now looks like this:

alias eth0 de4x5
alias parport_lowlevel parport_pc
alias eth1 tulip
alias scsi_hostadapter ncr53c8xx
alias sound ad1848
pre-install sound /sbin/insmod sound dmabuf=1
alias midi opl3
options opl3 io=0x388
options ad1848 io=0x530 irq=9 dma=3 dma2=3

Help?
