so, i built several new kernels recently (2.2.5 and 2.2.14) without
problems. they create the vmlinux.gz, which i rename into vmlinuz-2.2.x
and drop into /boot. from there, i go edit /etc/milo.conf to look like:

image=/boot/vmlinuz-2.2.5-22
    label=linux
    root=/dev/hda5
image=/boot/vmlinuz-2.2.14
    label=linux-14
    root=/dev/hda5
image=/boot/vmlinuz-2.2.14
    label=linux-14-try2
    root=/dev/hda

where "linux" is the original redhat install and the other two are the
newly built 2.2.14 kernel. i tried several roots b/c i had read that this
might be a problem -- neither work.

recognizes it as a compressed kernel... uncompresses... give the boot
command... "jumping to kernel..." --- then freeze. nada. deadski.

any suggestions at all are greatly appreciated.
