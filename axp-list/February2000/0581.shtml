On Mon, 28 Feb 2000, Mark Atkinson wrote:
> Has anyone had success at installing 6.1 on a jensen?
I have now since a week installed 6.2beta succefully on my jensen, and
the hole issue is to get the kernel that suites your harware and your OS
you want to install. The older jensen's kernels are not able to do
everything as ment to do. ( You have to do some things to pass some errors
). And the new kernels are for the jensen (EV4) not happy, many times I
had processes that are consuming the hole CPU whithout doing somthing.(EV4
kernel trap patch ).
I made a new kernel for my jensen what works with the 6.1 and 6.2beta
ramdisks. I include the EV4 patch in my kernel and typical jensen
problems are gone. The 6.2beta ramdisk has a bug in it for the network
installs , but local on CD it works fine. More info you can get from
http://nic.ced.tudelft.nl/~henry/jensen/redhat/6.1.92/
I think the next RedHat 6.2 wil be better for us, the beta seems very
good to me and work fine now. ( I only need more memory )
