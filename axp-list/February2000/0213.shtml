There's small pick of the back of one here:
http://www.alphalinux.org/docs/debian-udb-install.html

If it a very small box with onboard SCSI, ethernet, sound on the front
etc.... then it's probably a multia. Try the noname MILO and see what
happens, if it's not the right MILO it will hang the machine, but do no
damage.
