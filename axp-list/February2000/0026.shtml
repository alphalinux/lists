On Tue, 1 Feb 2000, W Bauske wrote:

>
> Where can one find up to date info on tools to detect
> intrusions and possible things to look for that indicate
> something is wrong. I just noticed my wtmp files have
> a gap in them and inetd was restarted and I didn't do
> it on my firewall system.
>
> A URL or anything else helpful would be appreciated.
> (where to start looking in general...)
>
> CERT didn't look all that useful and I'd like Linux
> specific info.

Check out www.securityfocus.com and browse through the Bugtraq archives.
Recently I've seen quite a few reports of hosts running bind-8.2.2_pl3
being compromised, probably via bind. One common element in the reports
seemed to be that a directory named "ADMROCKS" was created somewhere under
either /var or /usr.

In general, sites like www.securityfocus.com are good. Another one I like
is www.shmoo.com.
