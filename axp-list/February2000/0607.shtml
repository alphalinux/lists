I agree with the xset/setterm diagnosis. Any machine I've set up does exactly
what your describing by default. It's a "built in screensaver". You can
evidence this by watching the messages when you fire up xscreensaver. It notes
that xset was displaced, and would need to be restarted by hand if needed (not
sure WHY you would do that.. but you could).

So.. I'd say the behavior is normal. My alpha does exactly the same thing.
After "x" minutes of inactivity, it blanks the screen.

Check the man pages on xset & setterm. The behavior is a background thing, NOT
a forground process.
