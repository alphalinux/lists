okay here is how i fixed the bugger.
i changed the following entries to my conf.modules:

        alias sound ad1848
        pre-install sound /sbin/insmod sound dmabuf=1
        options ad1848 io=0x530 irq=9 dma=0 dma2=3

where io, irq, dma, and dma2 are found in your manual for your alphastation

then i downloaded the latest XMMS source.

i unpacked it and did the following:
        export CC=gcc -mieee
        ./configure
        make
        make install

at this point i needed to change the prefs in XMMS because it defaults to wav
writer instead of audio. tell it to use your oss or esd and you should be
fine.
