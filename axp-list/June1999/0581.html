Yup... I'm running them right now.

Our friends at metrolink saw fit to place a call to
the internal (bad) '__syscall' instead of (good) 'syscall'.
The problem here is that __syscall _is_ exported in glibc-2.0 but
it is _not_ exported in glibc-2.1 ...

My ugly fix was to use a hex-editor (like the good ol' days) :)
        replace '[0x00]__syscall[0x00]mmap'
        with '[0x00]syscall[0x00][0x00][0x00]mmap'.
( haven't had a problem since -- knock on wood )

P.S. Don't do this.
