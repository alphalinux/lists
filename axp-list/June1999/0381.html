Well,
        I'm not sure about Alpha, but, at home, on my putzy intel box (100 mhz
pentium), the included RH6.0 mpg123 is MUCH worse than the one I compiled
myself. I see all the symptons you describe.

Grab a copy of mpg123 source, and try complining it yourself.

You might also want to try running it with -b 1024. This will give the player
1 meg of buffer space.
