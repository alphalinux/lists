> On Mon, 21 Jun 1999, Andrew Hobgood wrote:
> > Anyone have any particularly fast MP3 encoders for linux/alpha that you'd
> > suggest? I get about 1.4x realtime encoding with the 8hz-mp3 encoder compiled
> > against libcpml...
> I like bladeenc. It has much better quality than 8hz but it's binary-only
> (I preload libcmpl though).
I doubt better quality of 8hz because from what I read on their web page (as
long as it existed) they only optimized the ISO sources for speed, not for
better sound.
The first encoder that really changed the quality was encode beacuse some
bugs in the bit allocation were fixed. The second step was lame which
introduced many more features and now even a GPL'ed psycho accoustic model.
