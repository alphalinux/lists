Hi there

Check out Rich Payne and gang's www.AlphaLinux.org (God bless 'em!)

pretty much every thing you asked about is there. take your time.. and if
i am not mistaken, most of the Carrera m/b's were either Aspens or Alpines.

the different HOWTO's for the various Alpha distributions will give you the
lowdown on installation.

to update the firmware, you'll need to positively verify which m/b is in
the system. then go to ftp://ftp.digital.com/pub/DEC/Alpha/firmware/ to
find the firmware that corresponds to your machine.

have a lot of fun!

izaak
