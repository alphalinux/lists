Thanks for your help. I was missing the '-c mount /mnt/path' option. The syntax has changed and I didn't see that reflected in the documentation. Furthermore, you can still give the password on the command line as follows (no more -P):
smbmount '//Netbios/Share' password -c 'mount /mnt/path' -U username
