On Tue, 15 Jun 1999, Paul Jakma wrote:

> Why are there no netscape binaries for Alpha?

I assume you mean Netscape Communicator 4.x. There are no binaries
for this because Netscape didn't port the Communicator components to
the Alpha, probably (e.g. Calendar and all that.) (The Digital Unix
version was built with the 32bit translator hack, IIRC.)

If you mean Mozilla binaries (of which there are also no binaries)
that's because I am the one doing the xptcall port for the Alpha
and I had to learn Alpha assembly first. I believe I now have it 75%
done, and should finish it in the next few weeks. This week though,
I'm at JavaOne, so nothing will get done.

> I notice there are unsupported binaries for PPC, so maybe if we lobbied
> netscape we could get them to provide an Alpha binary aswell.

Actually, they probably can't do this, because to do so would mean porting
and cleaning up a codebase they aren't working on anymore. Hang in there;
Mozilla 5.0 is on the way, and before long the Alpha should be back in
that game.
