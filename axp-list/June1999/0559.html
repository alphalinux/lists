Well Nvida has released the source code for their Intel/Linux X server
and their complete specs so if someone wanted to it could be ported to
Alphalinux. No one has yet step up and undertake this project. So right
now that card won't do anything for you but in the future it'll probably
be supported. Maybe by the time XFree-4.0 is out it'll be supported. If
you're looking for a good 3D card now you should take a look at the
Permedia 2/3 and the Matrox G200.
