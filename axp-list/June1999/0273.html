On Mon, 14 Jun 1999, Aakin Nipun Patel wrote:

> Was looking around ebay and saw a listing for a DEC Alpha 21040.
> I haven't heard anything about that particular chip, and was unable to
> find any details about it on the digital page or the alphalinux page.
> Anyone know anything about it?

21040 is the first of a line of ethernet MAC chips developed at Digital
Semiconductor and now owned by Intel. It has nothing to do with the Alpha
processor. The submitter of the ebay item made a (typographical?) error.
