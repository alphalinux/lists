On Wed, Jun 16, 1999 at 11:00:39PM -0700, Richard Henderson wrote:
> On Wed, Jun 16, 1999 at 01:13:12PM -0400, Daniel J. Frasnelli wrote:
> > By the way, several members of the Alpha Linux Org. including
> > myself have noted that there seem to be major stability problems/strange
> > errors under RH 6.0 with tasks that previously worked fine.
>
> I know. I've seen it myself. DaveM gave me the vital clue
> this evening over sushi -- vfork. The following hack disables
> vfork while I figure out what the real problem is.
>

There is a fix posted today on linux-kernel list (reverting
code from 2.2.7 in entry.S). Works fine for me.
BTW, what was the reason of removing 'call_pal PAL_tbi'?

Ivan.

--- linux-2.2.10/arch/alpha/kernel/entry.S.orig Mon May 10 20:55:21 1999
+++ linux-2.2.10/arch/alpha/kernel/entry.S Thu Jun 17 13:02:59 1999
@@ -525,7 +525,8 @@
         .prologue 0
         bsr $1,do_switch_stack
         call_pal PAL_swpctx
- unop
+ lda $16,-2($31)
+ call_pal PAL_tbi
         bsr $1,undo_switch_stack
         mov $17,$0
         ret $31,($26),1
