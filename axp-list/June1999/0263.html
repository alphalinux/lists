> this did not work (using tulip drivers)... in fact, it
> never worked with anyother NIC either. So I decided
> not to use the onboard and use a 3rd PCI NIC, which
> was a 3com905tx.
>
> It turns out, the 3c59x driver for the 3com card had
> some problems... which resulted in a LOT of errors...
> but it was very odd.. the errors only resulted from
> packets between the Alpha/Linux box and an NT box..
> between other Linux and Win95 it was fine.. though
> still not achieving full speed (not even close....
> 20Mbps on a Fast Ethernet hub that was mildly used). I
> sort of gave up trying to fix that.... since for most
> internet use, my internet connection was less than
> 3Mbps anyway...
>
> Well, one day, I decided to see if the onboard would
> work with the new tulip drivers... and it did.. so now
> I use two tulip NICs .. and the problems between the
> Alpha/Linux and the NT went away.. in fact.. now I can
> transfer files of 650MB without having a single
> error.. before I would get thousands of errors and the
> file would take forever... now I get about 80Mbps..
>
> So.. anyway, just my story for you....
