Hi,

To force the NIC to 100MB Full Duplex, shutdown to the SRM (>>>) console and
set the device there.

>>>set ewa0_mode fastfd
>>>init
>>>boot

This will force the NIC to fast full duplex and avoid any of those nasty
negotiation errors with Cisco Catalyst switches.
