Hello Matthew,

Try booting your RH6.0/Alpha CD-ROM with something
like this:

>>> boot dka0 -file kernels/generic.gz -flags "root=/dev/hda0"

where 'dka0' is your CD-ROM Device. Maybe you have to
adapt the 'root=/dev/hda0' part too. Make an educated guess here.

Good luck!
