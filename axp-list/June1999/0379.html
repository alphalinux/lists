
I recently installed RH6.0 on my alpha and i've noticed that the
included mpg123 player isn't performing very good. It's not able to
decode the MP3 files realtime, it keeps dropping frames all the time.
'top' says it uses max 60% CPU time on my multia with some 10% kernel
CPUtime. Still it drops frames and has glitches. It seems like the
multia isn't fast enough (which seems strange to me, because it
worked perfect with RH5.2). Could it be that the new 2.2.X kernels
are slower with sound or something??

Config:
multia 166Mhz.
40 MB RAM.
