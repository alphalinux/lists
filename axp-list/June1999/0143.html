Are you using the tulip driver as a module? If so, /etc/conf.modules
use 'options tulip options=5,5', after the 'alias' statements, this
will force both adaptors to (try to) use full duplex. Even with the
new driver we've had problems with 2114[23] chips. 21140's seem to work
fine.
