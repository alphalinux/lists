I believe alphalinux supports up to 2 gigs of ram, which is a limitation
thrust upon it by the intel side (i believe, there are probably many more
knowledgeable people on here than me who know more on that) The fastest
processor is the alpha 21264 at 500mhz. To put it simply the 21264 eats the
PIII 500 as an appetizer. As per compaq's site SpecINT95/fp95: 26/49 I'm not
sure what the intel numbers are, but they are lower. All I can give is a
rough idea, for the quickest machine you are going to want to use an alpha,
as for the operating system, that could be the limiting factor. Tru64 (what
was Digital Unix) might be a better choice depending on what you want to do,
and how much you want to spend.
