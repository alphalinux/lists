> I tried the simple way of adjusting linux/include/asm-alpha/core_pyxis.h to:

You also have to change the PYXIS_DMA_WIN_SIZE_DEFAULT, since you have
to align the windows -- you can't have a 1G window at address 512M.

bridge. Just a tad more work than you're interested in, I'm sure.
