Hi Geoff,

    Which tulip driver was installed? If lsmod shows that tulip.o was
installed, try bringing down the interface, rmmod-ing the tulip module,
insmod-ing de4x5, and then bring the interface back up. That has done the
trick for me on some revisions of the tulip chips. What rev of tulip do
you have (e.g. 21040, 21143, etc.)?

 - Pete
