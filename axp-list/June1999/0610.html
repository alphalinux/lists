> I followed the resent posting and, i have a question, i tought i saw

> Would anyone please verify or correct me on these:
> 1. In an PC164SX it is posible to use normal 100 Mhz SDRAM
Yes but not necessarily all kinds of 100MHz DIMMs run! Only buy those DIMMs
if you can bring them back to your dealer in case you are unlucky. My first
pair did not run, the second did...

> 2. Must the powerswitch of an ATX case be modified?
Nope. A normal ATX case with it's normal cabling and switches is just fine
for the SX.
