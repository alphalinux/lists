After taking one of my little multias to RH 6.0 and having numerous
problems. (most of which have come up again and again on the list) I've
backdated to 5.2 and just upgraded the kernel to 2.2.7 (for now) It really
looks like 6.0 is not ready for prime time with the alpha, there are just
too many bugs.

I'm just gonna guess here, or does it ALWAYS seem to be that the best RH
distributions end in 2?? 4.2, 5.2 ?? Do I have to wait until 6.2 for a
good, stable, everything works more and not less distribution??

Owning an alpha for several years, I've been used to the little bugs here
and there, but 6.0 was a big dissapointment. Glad I didn't buy it.
