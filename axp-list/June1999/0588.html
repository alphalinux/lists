> Now u got "symbol version" binding !? is that what I am hearing?

Yes. That's what will save us from a libc.so.7.

> Why should the linker do the 'right thing', and not the dynamic linker.

Are you ready for an exposition on symbol versioning?

Anyway, you *should* have been linking to libc before, as well as
every other library you use -- pthreads being a likely candidate.
Whether or not things just happened to have been working is immaterial.
Not doing so creates an incorrect dependancy graph between the shared
libraries, and they might not get initialized in the right order.
