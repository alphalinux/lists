>>> Tom Mulder said:
>
> Can anyone help me with this one, i bought an pc164sx (yes from e-bay)
> and now if i am in the AlphaBios, i enter the configuration as it said
> in the redhat manual (the AlphaBios section) it will boot linload and
> tries to boot milo, booting milo look fine until it want's to goto the
> milo bootprompt then the screen just freezes up.
>
> What can it be?

Sometimes the consoles, after entering an OS Selection, stop the clock for
some reason, and, if you continue then to try to boot, will simplyu not get
any clock ticks and (appear to) freeze.

Simply powercycle and try again, it should work then...
