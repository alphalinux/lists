For those interested, there's a new version of RasMol 2.6.4, at
ftp.dcs.ed.ac.uk/pub/rasmol. According to the author, "RasMol Version 2.6.4
is a minor update of the RasMol molecuar graphics package. This update
includes mainly minor bug fixes and portability enhancements." It compiles
and works under RH 5.1 out of the box, and the picture quality on a 16-bit
video seems better than in the old version.
