Yeah. It is an ethernet controller, from the DEC "Tulip" family, and is only
capable of 10Mbit ethernet (you need a 2114x for 100Mbit). Not a processor I'm
afraid - as far as I can tell processors are all 21x6x numbers, ethernet
controllers are 21x4x, PCI bridges are 21x5x and motherboard core logic
chipsets are 21x7x.
