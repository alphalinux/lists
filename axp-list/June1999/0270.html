At Usenix, Compaq Custom Solutions was showing a 1U DS10 -- that's a
rackmount case that's 3/4" tall. This is an excellent cluster building
block. It takes one horizontal full-height PCI card, and it has the
usual 2 tulip ethernet connections on the motherboard. It can take at
least one disk and they say that it can only hold "1 gb ram instead of
the usual 2 gb", whatever that means.

Pricing hasn't been decided. Hopefully it will be similar to the
DS10's price. For more information, you can contact
customsystems@compaq.com.
