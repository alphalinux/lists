Is anyone else using the Boehm garbage collector
(http://reality.sgi.com/boehm_mti/gc.html) on Alpha/Linux? It worked well
with earlier distributions... after I installed RH 6.0 I started getting
"undefined reference to `__data_start'" errors.

The GC needs to know the boundaries of the data segment to function
correctly. It assumes the data segment is bounded by __data_start and
_end symbols. I tried [_etext,_end] and found that the text and data
segments are not contiguous on Alpha/Linux.

I have to find a workaround, preferably easier than parsing
/proc/self/maps on startup. Any ideas?
