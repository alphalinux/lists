Jeff,

>
> kernel panic: no init found, use init=..
>
  looks like the kernel didn't mount the root filesystem.

  In MILO you can say "ls sdax:" to list the directory of a partition.
  You may just need different root partition.

  Also, your kernel may not have the correct SCSI controller
  configured. Did the kernel list SCSI disk info, etc. as it booted?
