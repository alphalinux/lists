Well, that's the point. I'll bet you just about anything, that if you
could find the DEC engineers who designed this box, that they envisioned
it in a stand if not in a climate-controlled computer room, where there's
cold air aplenty. That's almost certainly why there are so many UDB
deaths from heat.

But aside from that, if you wanted to cool it, I would blow air into the
right side (the side the floppy's on). I wouldn't think you would need a
tremendous volume, the idea is just to get some air moving in the case.
