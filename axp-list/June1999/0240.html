I apologize in advance since I am quite positive that some
of these questions have arisen already; unfortunately, I haven't
followed this list attentively enough lately, and also I don't know
where/whether(?) there is a good searchable archive of this list.

Just installed RH6.0 on a lx164. Overall things worked out OK, I
do have a workable and internetworked system again (I saw too much
crap happen with upgrades from 5.1/5.2 -> 6.0 on i386, so I didn't
even attempt this on an alpha...).

A number of irritating points remain:

I can't remember anymore, but wasn't there some workaround to get
the system to install files with a date other than 2015... (I did
a find / -name '*' -exec touch {} \; immediately after the
install, but there must be something more intelligent; further, this
doesn't fix dates on symlinks. (I have two more of these alphas
to install...)

Anyone noticed that emacs suddenly gives unaligned traps -- I hadn't
seen any of those in either RH5.1 or 5.2? [I have upgraded the
base-netkit and traceroute with R.H. new src.rpms, so that is fixed
and can't be the reason.]

Remembering some traffic on the list, I shut down the X-fontserver
and run X-Windows without xfs. Did anyone explore the xfs issues
in more details, i.e., is there already a solution. (I wouldn't
mind using xfs in our network...)

Finally, one of the alphas that needs to be upgraded to RH6.0 is
an UX. Any 'campfire stories' to trade with respect
to RH6.0 and the UX? I had a hell of a time getting it stable
under 5.1...

Many thanks in advance,

Stefan
