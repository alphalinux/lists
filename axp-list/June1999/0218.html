Anybody know the peak power consumption for a samsung UX board?
There's list of current requirements for 3.3,+5,-5,+12 and -12V,
which suggests peak power might be around 208W. (eek!)

Reason i ask is that i'm thinking of building a system with a 21164
533MHz, UX and 3 uw drives. Each drive is spec'ed at requiring 30W
max during operation. Is this possible on a 300W peak rated power
supply?

most important factor: does the 208W peak figure for the motherboard
already include the power needs of things like the
processor/video/etc? Because if it does then it should just be
possible. Or do i still need to factor in the requirements
