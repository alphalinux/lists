> Is it possible to have
> several patches in the srpm, and let the 'runtime system' decide upon
> which arch, which patch to use?

You can use the %ifarch stuff in your spec file to do this. However,
it would be preferable to make the patch "generic" by surrounding
your changes with #ifdef's where appropriate. That way you can send
your patches on to the author for inclusion in the stock sources.
