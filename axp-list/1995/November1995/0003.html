You'll have problems using X unless you do the following

mkfontdir /usr/X11R6/lib/X11/fonts/75dpi
mkfontdir /usr/X11R6/lib/X11/fonts/100dpi
mkfontdir /usr/X11R6/lib/X11/fonts/misc

The fonts are now compressed, but the fonts.dir and fonts.alias files didn't
reflect this correctly.
