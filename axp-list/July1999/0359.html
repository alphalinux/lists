Besides that, I think many Alpha owners know from experience not to take
superficial benchmarks at face value. They've heard the hype about "the
fastest CPU in the world" yet know that the results of a quick or naive
port are often underwhelming, for various reasons:

 - quality of optimizing compilers (gcc vs. Compaq's C compiler)
 - different optimization rules for Alpha
 - a relative lack of developers optimizing code for Alpha

Some of the code I've ported demonstrated a 2-3 speedup after I played
with it a bit, trying different compiler flags and rearranging
instructions a bit.
