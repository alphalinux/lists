Well I seem to have found the local problem. My machine was a 533
overclocked to 600 Mhz. I brought that back down to 533 and all my
crash problems have gone away. Kernel compiles go smoothly, I was
able to load my machine up over 6 for an hour without difficulty, all
manner of X usage with no X crashes, etc.

Still running 2.2.7. The bulk of my troubles seem to have been
related to overclocking. Either coincidental with the RH6.0 upgrade,
or somehow the newer kernels trip something the older ones did not.

Thanks for the assistance.
