As of today, I fired up three different clients. It'll be interesting
to see how they shake out:
1. Sparc5/170 sol2.5.1 seti 1.2
2. 486/133 linux-2.2.5 seti 1.3 static
3. AS200-4/233 linux-2.2.5 seti 1.1 for ev5 (ev45 machine)

None of these boxes are anything to write home about, compared to a
533, etc., but they serve my purposes fine and have a _lot_ of idle
time so why not? I'm curious how the figures will fall out after the
first work unit gets finished on all three. So far, it looks like the
axp is killing the sun, and I don't expect anything special at all
from the intel. I have a co-worker that's getting 10 hrs per w/u.
I _will_ find out what he's running :-)
