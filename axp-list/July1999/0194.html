Uncle George wrote:
>
> I thought he was!
> If he was passing the value, then he would have a problem, as the float is passed in the second floating
> point argument register ( rather than the second integer argument register ).
>

I wasn't clear. It's the 'v' in the put_f8() args that I meant.
The actual call to memcpy() is OK. I've seen this occur on other
platforms with high optimization levels, not particularly on Alphas.
That's why I prefer to modify my code since it isn't an Alpha
specific problem. For some reason, pass by value is more dangerous
for floats/doubles than ints/longs when you optimize. Pass by reference
hasn't failed for me yet.
