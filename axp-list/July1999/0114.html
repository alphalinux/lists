Brian, et al..

I have 2 of these units and they definitely do take the IDE-cpu lag off the
sytem completely.

Additionally, these units have 512k cache on the controller. My IBM 6.4
gig TravelStar is very fast with this setup. All the advantage of IDE PIO
4 and multi sector access and no IDE cpu lag.

Additionally, if you want to take the floppy out (if you have one) the
IDE-SCSI adapter allows you to have two IDE drives (master/slave) running
off a single controller.
