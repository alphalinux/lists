Just a quick note on Cooked CPU's

We have to Alpha PC 164's 500Mhz at work running Softimage 3D (Running NT
sorry).
There nice machines, each one renders about 4 times faster then the SGI
Indigo II 250mhz
and I got two Alpha's for half the price of one SGI !

We had a CPU fail about a year ago, and the Vendor replaced it but didn't
quite tighten
the heatsink nuts. As the Motherboard is vertical, over time the nuts worked
loose, and again
another fried CPU. The fan kept on spinning so no alarms were raised by the
system.

Getting the vendor to take the blame was of course impossible
(does US$400 sound OK for a 500mhz 21164?)

A really 'cool' trick is to put small spring washers under the nuts on the
Heat sink, cheap insurance.

well while we are all talking about heat...
