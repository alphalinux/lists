> Alpha 21264-500 4 MB cache fort -O3 8:42

This is the "standard" "safe" optimization level you want:

fort -O4 -arch ev6 -tune ev6

I've found that the ev6 options get about 10% more performance on the
codes I tried. They make a huge difference for stream.
