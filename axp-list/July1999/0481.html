> Vendor: QUANTUM Model: XP39100WD Rev: LYK8

Just a comment. I have 3 XP39100W's and assuming they
are similar to yours (what's the D, differential?), they really aren't
fast enough to worry about whether they're in UW vs W mode.
(At least in what I use them for)

Go to their QM318000 drives or the newer QM39100 and
then it becomes important to look at getting UW working.
(got some of those too) These drives are LVD capable too
though I don't use them that way.

Anyone out there using differential SCSI controllers?
I'm looking to add a tape drive that only runs differential.
By that I mean old differential SCSI, not LVD. Also interested
in LVD controllers too.
