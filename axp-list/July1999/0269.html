>>> Paul Jakma said:
>
> can you change the video mode from within SRM?
>
> I have a DEC PWS433au which boots straight into DEC-UX 4.0D with a
> resolution of 1280x1024.. unfortunately there's only a 15" monitor on it at
> the moment, which can't handle it.
>
> so can i change or set a default video mode from SRM and will DEC-UX pick it
> up and use it?

You must have some kind of TGA/TGA2 in there? If so, you need to set the DIP
switches (or little rotary switch) on the TGA card itself to a more
reasonable resolution for that monitor. See the slist of selections at the
end.

Good luck.
