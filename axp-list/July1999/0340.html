>>> "stig.telfer" said:
>
> Also bear in mind that user-time calculations with some recent SMP
> kernels are way too high. I'm verifying some new boards using SETI@home
> as background load. A dual-EV6 system turns in about 8 results a day -
> but the website reports my average turnaround time is way over 30
> hours. Perhaps there are enough people out there using recent SMP
> kernels (like me) to skew the statistics. Does anyone know of a fix for
> this that would get SETI@home back on the right track?

Try replacing the obvious line in include/asm-alpha/ptrace.h with:

#define user_mode(regs) (((regs)->ps & 8) != 0)

This comes pretty close to correcting the problem.
