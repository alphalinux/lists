You can however change your resolution in du.

you just add "-screen 1024" or "-screen 1280" to the last line in
/usr/var/X11/xdm/Xservers (when you use xdm... the old interface)
/usr/var/dt/Xservers (when using cde)

that like will look like:

:0 Local local@console /usr/bin/X11/X :0 -screen 1280

you need of course restart the X...

good luck
