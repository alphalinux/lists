Well,.. I can at least confirm that the 3com 905tx,
all revs A, B, and C work with the 3c59x driver on
Alpha. However, not very smoothly... I get a few
thousand errors after an hour or so of usage,.. and
transfer rates are slow!

the tulip seems to work very well for me *now*, though
in the beginning, I had problems using the tulip
driver for the onboard ethernet with any other PCI
NIC... that problem is now gone.

It seems relatively difficult these days to find NICs
with the DC21x4x ethernet controllers... but they use
to be really cheap and work very well. You might have
to goto a used equipment store to find them
nowadays...

D.
