PC-100 DIMMs work well in mine. The manual says ECC, but on the SX ( and
I've heard the UX as well ) Non-ECC works. the LX board NEEDS ECC PC-100
DIMMs. Remember, you need 2 DIMMs to make 1 bank of memory, and the largest
it supports is 128Meg DIMMs ( x 2 makes 256 Meg / bank, 2 banks makes 512
Meg MAX RAM for the system.)
