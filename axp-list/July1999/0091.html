I am using good & old kermit (the newer one compiles and works fine on
Alpha) to talk to my UDB. I'm not sure if you can run arc and milo
from the serial console. Why not just use SRM? Taking the battery
out should(?) force the UDB to drop back to SRM. aboot seems to be
quite involved (and not an option if you don't have a local disk.) It
maybe simpler to use bootp if you have another UNIX (like) machine on
the LAN. I can give you the setup details if you want.
