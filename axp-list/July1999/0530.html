Yes, I have used this method before, ie ground pin 14 (PS_ON) - or you
can also short it to pin 15 which is a ground pin.

Seems to work ok. I use the switch on the back of the power supply as
the on/off switch for the machine. On the RAM side, standard 36-bit,
72pin parity RAM seems to work for me. You can either use 4 of these
(128bit bus mode) or 8 (to have 256 bit bus mode). Have not tried the
IDE controller. But I don't think it's a problem...

Hope this helps & have fun.
