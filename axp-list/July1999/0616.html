I don't think it's 164SX specific. I've seen this on my SX, an LX, a DP264
and the new UP2000. It's a problem with the later kernels.

2.2.7 is the last one that seems reliable (unless you want to get into the
patching game, I think that 2.2.11-pre1 + rth patches is also OK, but I
haven't had a change to really abuse that yet).
