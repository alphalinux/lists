I run production servers running mostly standard internet services.
I had bad experiences with 2.2.7, which was apparently stable but
failed under production load. See my post right at the end of
http://www.lib.uaa.alaska.edu/axp-list/archive/1999-06/, where
posts received on december 1969 are located. Yes, it has been
a long story :-)

I gave up with 2.2.x on alpha, at least for this millennium.
Now I have one server on 2.0.37 and other three on 2.0.36 and
they are solid as rocks. Can't run X on the one with 2.0.37,
but this improves security :-)
