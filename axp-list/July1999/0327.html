On Fri, 16 Jul 1999, Tom Browder wrote:

> I still get periodic FPEs and have had several folks suggest that it may be hardware
> related. I have read here that compiling egcs is a great memory stress-tester, and have
> had no problems doing so.

FPEs are rarely a sign of memory trouble - if your getting segmentation
violations then that could well be memory trouble (but not always...) -
try recompiling your application with -mieee

Alpha machines are a bit more touchy about floating point than their Intel
counterparts; in particular doing nasty things like dividing by 0 or
underflowing on an Intel just give you a result marked as invalid, on
Alpha they tend to through an exception - have a crawl around the FAQs.
