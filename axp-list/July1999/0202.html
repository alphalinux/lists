Leslie F. Donaldson wrote:
>
> 1. Gotta have acrobat around for those encrypted files.

Not taking anything from other points and reasons this one particular
item is invalid.

It is true that "out of the box" ghostscript and xpdf will not handle
"encrypted" files - thanks to infamous US regulations. Still gs will
tell you from where in Australia to get a small Postscript file for
a replacement of one which came with gs. You do not have even recompile.

Similarly xpdf documentation spells clearly what to get, from where,
and what to do to get decryption enabled. You have to recompile this
time unless servers like 'replay' have binaries ready.

Frankly it was always a mystery to me what is a real purpose of this
"encryption" for pdf files (not mentioning more general issues :-).
