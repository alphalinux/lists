We got a DS20 with preinstalled WinNT from Compaq.

The AlphaBIOS identifies the two SCSI Adapters as NCRC875. The CD-Rom
is on the first adapter, 3 HDs on the second.

I made myself a bootable RedHat 6.0 CD-Rom.
Booted from the SRM (as explained in
http://www.digital.com/alphaserver/linux/install_guide.html).

The Kernel starts.
Correctly finds the Adapters/CDRom/HDs.
Try to access the CD-ROM and times out while doing so.

I tried the generic.gz and the dp264-s.gz kernels from CD-Rom and the
generic-up-223.img and generic-smp-223.img images from floppy disk.

Is there a solution to this problem?

- other kernel ?
- boot options ?
- reconfiguring the machine ?
