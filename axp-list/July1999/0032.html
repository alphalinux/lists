Do a search for multia heat death on the BSD mailing lists. There is a
chip that needs to be replaced on the multias. This chip is apperntly the
souce of the "death" There are more modern replacements for said chip
that don't gerneate nearly as much heat.

I've not done this yet, but plan to. People on the BSD mailing list seem
have had good results. If you have a bunch of multias in a rack, it seems
like replaceing this chip in all of them would be the ticket.
