Hallo!

Yesterday I have debugged kmail because it crashed every time
I tried to open a mail composer window. I am running RedHat 6.0
Linux/Alpha and KDE 1.1.1 from the RedHat updates.
My debugging ended in the KDE mimelib. It has problems with
the 64 bit time_t in Linux/Alpha.
If you have the same problem look at

http://ivs.cs.uni-magdeburg.de/~olaf/alpha.html

for another problem description and a downloadable corrected
libmimelib.so.1.

I hope this hint is usefull for you.
