> Hi, Todd,
> I met similar problems on my DS20 yesterday except that
> my PC code is different from yours. Do your system also
> halt when detecting the SCSI devices?
> The only way out I found is to restart the computer and
> enter the same boot command again and again. Sometimes it
> just worked. I have not found the reason yet.
> You can stick to the boot command:
> boot dva0 -fi vmlinux.gz -fl "root=/dev/sda1".

> Another clue is that my problem happened after I messed the
> partition table. You might need to format your disk in
> another system or zero the partition table using the following
> command:
> dd if=/dev/zero of=/dev/sda bs=512 count=2
