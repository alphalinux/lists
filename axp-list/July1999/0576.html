  Hi, the final solution to ncr53c8xx problem is on the way. Thanks to
Jay++. But you have to wait.
   I met the same problem on my DS20 sometimes.
   You have to use generic-smp-223 as boot disk if you have two CPU,
or up-223 if you have only one CPU. If you use boot image from RedHat,
definitely you will meet trouble with ncr53c8xx. and you should use
your CD-ROM as root, the boot command is like,
  P00>>>boot dva0 -fi vmlinux.gz -fl "root=/dev/scd0 load_ramdisk=1".
 If you still fails, you may need enter P00>>>init
 Halt the system and start again. Sometimes it took me several times to
boot successfully. I have not figured out the reason. However, most of
the time, it works pretty well.
  After installation, don't forget to copy the vmlinux.gz from floppy to
your root, because the generic image from RedHat can not boot your system.

  Take care.
