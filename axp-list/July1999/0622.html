Well, in reality I have my 164SX running 2.2.10 and it seems fine. Until I
go and compile a large package that is. Kernel compiles usually seg fault
5 or 6 times before completing, and I've seen this behavior on other
machines also, but for everyday use it seems fine. However, if I go back
to 2.2.7 then all the compiling problems disappear, so something isn't
right.

It could be, as you mentioned, a particular option, but I haven't spent
any time looking into that.
