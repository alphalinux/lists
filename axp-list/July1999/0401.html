Debian is available for Alpha now.. has been for awhile, actually.

FWIW, I _NEVER_ upgrade my important machines until months after the
packages/hardware/whatever have been out and I have done the reading to
find out what sort of problems I'm going to run into -- if it looks buggy,
I wait.. and even then, I don't upgrade unless I need the new features.

As such, my x86 machine is running RH 5.2 with a 2.2.9 kernel, and my
Alpha are still running 5.1 with a 2.0.x kernel.
