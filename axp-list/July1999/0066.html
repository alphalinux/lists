System: Ruffian UX4, Diamond FireGL1000Pro, off the shelf RH6.0

Thanks to those who suggested skipping the symlink and going straight to the
_explicit_ kernel file name (either of /boot/vmlinu[zx]-2.2.5-16) on the
milo boot line. Both files work OK, I didn't bother trying the symlink
again to see if my failure had mysteriously disappeared.

Since then I've got inittab to get me into both 3 and 5 correctly, but have
several problems.

1) ping doesn't work - byte 8 seems to be a random number.
   _But_ rsh, rlogin, ftp, nfs etc are fine.
   I remember previous messages about ping and packing/alignment, but am
   unable to http/ftp to the outside world atm. Do I need a patched version
   of ping? (*)

2) Gnome gives me 2 core files every time I log on (both my PC and my Alpha
   do this), and I don't like the ammount of memory it eats, so I'd rather
   go back to plain simple fvwm2. To do this, I have a .xsession file
   containing xterm&; xearth&; fvwm2 and no more. On the PC, this is
   fine and I get. On the PC everything's OK, on the Alpha I get an xearth,
   but no other windows, and no menus on my mouse. Nothing I run from the
   text-mode console ever appears on the screen. i.e. it's useless.
   Any suggestions? I'm confused.

3) When I C-A-backspace out of X, or I've killed certain X programs
   (e.g. the window manager) (i.e. when the above problem has left me no
   option) x respawns in a rapid loop, failing each time with a message
   about xdm being murdered mysteriously. This sends my monitor into fits,
   until x respawning is disabled by init. What should I do to exit a deaf,
   mute and blind X session?

4) Sometimes I try `telinit -t 1 3` to go from 5 to 3. This disappears up
   its own arse. I can sometimes go to 1, and then to 3. Erm?

So I'm happy as I now have 1600x1200x32 @ 84 Hz on my big monitor, but I'm
still not the fluffiest bunny in the field.
