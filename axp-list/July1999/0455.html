The 4x00 can support a large variety of disk, memory and video options,
many more than Linux can currently support on ALPHA, A major problem when
installing Linux on ALPHA boxes which had VMS, Unix or NT on them, then
trying to install Linux ,is a piece of hardware in the box not being
supported causing the PCI Bus probe to fail.
