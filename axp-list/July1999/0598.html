"Wes Bauske" <wsb@paralleldata.com> writes:

> Just a programming style hint. Never do "if(fpval1 == fpval2)".
> Alway do "if(ABS(fpval1-fpval2) < epsilon)". Pick an epsilon that
> makes sense for your algorithm and the machine it's running on.

This isn't the place to get into a discussion about this. Let me just
say that in general when you have higher precision registers then 1)
using tolerances don't always help and 2) reordering & different
optimization levels can significantly change results. There was a
long discussoin of this on the egcs mailing list.
