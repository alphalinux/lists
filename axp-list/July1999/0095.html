Check out the ADTX IDE to SCSI adapter. It piggybacks your 2.5 in IDE drive
with a SCSI controller.. It supports all EIDE up to PIO 4. I have two for my
multias and they are the best. Who needs IDE?

http://www.adtx.co.jp/english/prod/value/scsiide.html

>From website:

SCSI-IDE Converter Card
---------------------------------------------------------------------------- 

----

This product was developed in order to allow IDE HDDs to interface with SCSI
devices. The capacity of Bus Transfer Speed (non-synchronized mode)
increases up to x4.

 Main Features
Conforming to DOS/V, UNIX, and Apple Macintosh.
Card substrate and size conforming to 2.5-inch HDD.
By coupling with 12.5mm IDE HDD the height will be19mm (attachment
necessary).
Active terminator built in. SCSI ID address and terminator can be
set/altered wither with the ID switch on the card or pin on the J2 connector
(setting can also be made from the host system).
Standard length (max. 3m) SCSI cable supported (48mA driver built in).
Bus: Single-ended.
