I dont see how u can sugg this. the n/m/d/c have to be compiled into each float
instruction. changing the fpcr does nothing if none of the instructions have a
/d switch. If libm was compiled with the '\d' for dynamic rounding ( as per
the settings in the fpcr ), then you may get different results.

What u do have is a mix of rounding - compiled as part of the standard
libraries, and that of the executable.
