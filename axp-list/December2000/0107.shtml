>
> At the SRM prompt:
>
> set bootdef_dev dka0
> set boot_osflags 0
>
> You can then just type "boot" at the prompt to boot, or you can also
> set:
>
> set auto_action boot
>
> and your machine will automatically boot into linux without waiting at
> the SRM prompt.
>
> See the manual:
> http://www.redhat.com/support/manuals/RHL-7-Manual/alpha-install-guide/s1-gu
> imode-post-installation.html
