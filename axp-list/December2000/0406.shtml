> I could see what they HELL was going on. You see the RH installer won't
> work through the serial connection. Once the machine jumps to bootstrap
> code, thats it, no more serial output.

Ah...you're on the wrong serial port. aboot will only output everything on
serial port one. You must also tell the kernel you're using a serial
console. AAdd console=ttyS0 to the boot flags. RH 6.2 will install over
the serial port.
