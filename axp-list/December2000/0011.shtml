last I heard, the chipsets used w/ alphas could only address a 256MB DIMM
per slot (so on the LX/SX 512MB per bank).

The cache on the SX is L2 vs. the L3 on the LX boards. The additional cache
speed makes up for the fact that there's only 1MB of it. (if I remember the
LX has something like 96k of L2). You could also get an LX board w/ 8MB of
on board cache through Microway.
