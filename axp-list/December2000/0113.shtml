Job Title:
Systems Manager

  • Package Neg $$$

  • Excellent HPC Facility

    •

    Description:

    The Victorian Partnership for Advanced Computing (VPAC) offers a unique opportunity for a person to manage their High Performance Computing (HPC) Facility.

    VPAC is a consortium of six universities that aims to improve the effectiveness and competitiveness of Victorian universities, research institutes and industry through the provision, support and effective use of advanced computing facilities and technologies across all fields of research and industry sectors.

    The founding members of VPAC are La Trobe University, Monash University, RMIT University, Swinburne University of Technology, the University of Ballarat and the University of Melbourne. VPAC has recently been successful in receiving Victorian Government funding through the Science, Technology and Innovation Initiative and is a founding partner of the Australian Partnership for Advanced Computing (APAC).

    VPAC will be operating a 128-processor Compaq AlphaServer SC System running True-64 UNIX that will be the largest super computer installed within Australia to date.

    Reporting to the Chief Executive Officer, the System Manager's responsibilities will include

    · Managing the access to and services provided by VPAC's HPC Facility
    · Implementation of practices and procedures within the Facility. eg. for security
    · Develop performance measures and targets for activities and services
    · Management of budget submissions for the provision of facilities and services
    · Develop and maintain relationships with Members, Vendors and Industry Users.
    · Develop and maintain an overall strategic facilities plan
    · Manage the selection and acquisition of computer hardware and software and associated licensing.

    You will need a relevant tertiary qualification and/or equivalent training and experience within this field. An attractive remuneration package will be negotiated with the successful applicant.

    Intending applicants can obtain a copy of the position description from www.vicpac.org. Applications should be forwarded to the attention of Karen Iatrou, Victorian Partnership for Advanced Computing, P O Box, 201, Carlton South, Vic., 3053 or email to: karen@vicpac.org.

    Applications close 8th December, 2000.
