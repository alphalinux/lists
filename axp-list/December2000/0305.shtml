you should not have the .iso image on the cd, you should only have the
contents of the .iso image burned to the CD.

I assume you mounted the .iso as a loop device. that makes the .iso look
like a normal CD. what you see is what you should have been burned to your
CD, the contents of the iso, not the iso it's self.

I use Adaptec ezcd on w95 to burn my iso's it has a mode "create CD from
iso
disk image" which is the type of burn you need to perform.

JJ
