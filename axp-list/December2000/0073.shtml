bob@bob.usuhs.mil said:
> SRM disks must have BSD labels to enable booting.

Not strictly true, AFAIK. SRM uses the last 16 bytes of the first sector, I
believe. So you could put the necessary magic in for SRM booting if you only
have three primary partitions in an MSDOS-style partition table.

You may need to have aboot to get it to grok msdos partitions though - I'm
not sure.
