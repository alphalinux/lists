Fascinating...How did you get to the alpha bios on an XP1000?

On my XP1000/667 typing "arc" or "alphabios" results in:
        "ARC not supported in this console."

        [SRM V8.5-3 Aug 9 2000.]

Also, don't forget... you can't type "help show bios." If you do, you
get "help show" scroll off the screen, and a line "bios doesn't match any
topic..." You have to type "help show | more" and you will see the help
(such as it is) for "show bios".
