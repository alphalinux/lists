It turns out the peecee will keep the 10gb HD. There was a BIOS setting
buring inthere for "large disk access mode DOS/OTHER" and I picked OTHER
and now it works. The BIOS is Phoenix 4.057 in a Pentium100 Multia, and
there is no later upgrade - Phoenix is notoriously bad about that kind of
thing.

Anyway, I don't have the AXP Multia powered up right now (I got a
McMaster-Carr fan upgrade and I need to get that in there before I play
with the Multia any more, I feel like its liviing on borrowed time), but
whatever kernel comes from the stock RH6.2 install is the one I am using.
