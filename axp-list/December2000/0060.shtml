I experienced very bad performance out of DE600 in full duplex mode with
the standard Linux driver. Forcing eia0_mode to half duplex solved the
problem for me. It's somehow a pity I can't get full duplex which it is
supposed to provide, but for our applications it doesn't really matter
too much.
