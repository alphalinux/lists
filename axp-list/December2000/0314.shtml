I took a look at Compaq Alpha web site, and there is no mentioning of VX1
anywhere, even under systems and options lists for XP1000 or DS20E, or on
the powerstorm graphics or Tru64 pages. Strange...

Since VX1 itself is also becoming obsolete, I'd personally prefer to see
Compaq support Nvidia on the Alpha (both Tru64 and Linux). Once AGP Alphas
are out, you could essentially have the same graphics card on both Alpha
and Intel systems from Compaq (Geforce 3 / Quadro 3 by that time). Plus,
that could enable Alpha to return to the 3-D workstation market.
