This is very very very bad. Usually the sign of a hacked box. Have you
tried to re-install syslog and see if it starts working? (I assume you
checked /etc/syslog.conf to make sure it wasn't futzed with) If it does
start working after you re-install, it's probably time to do some serious
updating as your system may have been compromised. Of course if your
system is sitting off in the corner wiht no outside world contact, then
ignore:) I'm paranoid, but that doesn't mean they aren't out to get me!

As an aside, I have a RH 5.1 system that was updted to 5.2 years ago. It
deals with a lot of trffic daily, and is still logging :)
