I disconnected my HD to see if the problem still existed. Same problem,
hmmm, well lets see. I'll try NOT using the fwupdate kludge to get my
video to work. Guess what, it loads the ramdisk, and away we COULD go if
I could see what they HELL was going on. You see the RH installer won't
work through the serial connection. Once the machine jumps to bootstrap

Thanks to everyone that tried to help, while I would dearly love to use

keep this cheap, I'm not rushing out to buy another one. So it's back to
arc and Milo.

few cards. So far of the 3 cards I have available to me, SRM doesn't run
any of them (ATI, 3dFx and a creative). They are all good cards and work

is surely a simple fix.

puter sat to close to the british car and Lucas Gremlins have escaped from
