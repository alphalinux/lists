I noticed the saem thing yerterday. I just downloaded the latest driver
for this chipset (V1.11 I think) from:
http://www.scyld.com/network/eepro100.html

and this is a big improvement. I just transfered a 500Meg file w/o to much
trouble, whereas before it would just stop after about 100Meg, and this
time no timeout messages.

Make sure you also get the pci-scan.h/.c and kern-compat.h files, and
modify the Makefile if you're builing a monolithic kernel.

--rdp
