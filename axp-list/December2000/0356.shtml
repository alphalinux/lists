Hello Geerten!

The reason for that is probably the blocksize of your new CD. In the
old days blocksizes defaulted to 512 bytes and all was swell. Nowadays
cd-rom tend to use 2048 bytes. SRM (if I recall correctly) only supports
working from devices using 512 byte blocks, at least on ancient machines
like my jensen. Some cd-drives can work with both blocksizes (probably by
jumpers). But as intel machines usually do not mind 2048 blocks I would be
amazed if newer drives would still support 512 byte blocks. (I hope I am
wronmg here...)
