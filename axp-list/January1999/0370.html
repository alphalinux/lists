Then you do not have modules support. In other words, you either
have to recompile your kernel before you will able to insert any
module (you have to have also 'modutils' package installed) or
you have to recompile your kernel to put tulip as an integrated
driver.

If you have to pass some options to tulip in order to force
a media detection (happens) then it is easier to do that for a module.
