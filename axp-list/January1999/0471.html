> Anyone have a working DHCPCD for 2.2.0preXX?

First of all you have to turn it on by doing

echo 1 > /proc/sys/net/ipv4/ip_bootp_agent

somewhere in your startup files. The second you likely need another
client (and, possibly, also ipchains). Clients from

ftp://204.210.112.66/pub/dhcpcd
ftp://ftp.isc.org/isc/dhcp/test/
dhcpcd-1.3.16.tar.gz (don't know where)
maybe something from ftp://ftp.inr.ac.ru/ip-routing/

reportedly work but I do not know if anybody tried them on Alpha.

In general 2.1/2.2 kernels require various changes and expecting
that everything will work without spending some time reading
documentation has to end in failures.
