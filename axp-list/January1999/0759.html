Hm, this "MUST" does not mesh with my experience. A few days ago I was
experimenting with 'pretty-stable' Mozilla from K. Goto site. I have
no idea what I did, beyond having a correct network setup, but I did
not have any problems with DNS. All links were working "by name" like
they should. No, I do not have a proxy.

This Mozilla on Alpha still appeared to have problems with frames;
in particular LWN site looked pretty weird. www.alphalinux.com somewhat
better but still not correct. Otherwise not very long trial seemed
to indicate that this is indeed 'pretty-stable'.

Some accounts mentioned a need of setting up MOZILLA_NO_ASYNC_DNS
environment variable. I did not but maybe I have faster responses from
a DNS server? Try that one.
