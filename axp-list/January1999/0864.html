        Thinking about a PCI TV tuner card for a 500a Miata and heard that
the Hauppaugge (or however you spell it) WinTV card works fine. Looking
at a vendor's page (www.mcglen.com), I see four different WinTV packages:
   1. WINTV 125 CHANNEL TV TUNER & DIGITAL DIGITIZER WIN 98 COMPATIBLE 404
   2. WINTV 125 CHANNEL TV TUNER W/ VIDEO CAPT./CONF. & W/DBX STEREO 400
   3. WINTV DBX TV STEREO 125CHNL TV TUNER W/FM STEREO RADIO PCI 401
   4. WINTV WITH COLOR CAMERA BUNDLED WITH STEREO WINTVCARD 447
