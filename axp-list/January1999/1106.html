You need to purchase an SRM license from DEC. DEC's price is $75 and the
part# is QR-21B02-03. It is called an EBSDK kit. We sell it, but it
actually comes out cheaper when you buy it from DEC. Once you get the kit,
there should be a floppy in there that has SRM on it. Load your system and
then install the upgrade from DEC's site and you should be up and running.

Good Luck
