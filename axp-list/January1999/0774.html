>>> Arnold Sutter said:
>
> I'm looking for a HiRes (1280x1024x64k Color) graphics for
> a Personal Worksataion 433au that works with both Linux
> and Digital UNIX. I have a Powerstorm 3D30, a 4D40 and
> a ZLXp-E2.
>
> The TGA is not supported on the dpws (no picture in console
> mode and other oddities) and both Powerstorms
> are not supported by Linux.
>
> Does anybody know of work underway to get Powerstorm
> support into Linux/XFreeAXP?

Well, there *is* some Powerstorm support in the latest XFree86 (3.3.3.1);
seems that the 4D10T is a Permedia-2-based board, and there's an XF86_3DLabs
server available. It's not as fast, IMHO, as the Millenniums with the
XF86_SVGA server, but then, that code has had more than a year to
mature. But, it's not supported under DU.

In addition, the 3D10 is an S3-Trio64-based board which will run under DU
and LINUX. It's old/slow, a bit problematic under LINUX, only 8-bit under
DU, but workable.

> What other graphics option would satisfy my request?

At one time, ATI Mach64 was somewhat supported under DU, but I don't know if
that's still true.

I think that you should also do a bit more investigation, now that there are
two commercial Xserver packages out for Linux/Alpha (Metrolink and
XiGraphics). It's possible that they might support one (or more) of the
options that are available under DU. Unlikely, but possible.

Good luck.
