frts@matef.sch.bme.hu writes:

> I successfully compiled 2.2.0 on my jensen.
> But.. I cannot boot with the new kernel.
> Of sourse I use aboot via SRM and when I try to boot
>
> aboot> 2/vmlinux.gz root=/dev/sda2
>
> it sais "compress block size not valid" or somethig like this
> (I'm at work now, not at the console, sorry)
>
> I used make config, make dep;make clean; make boot, as usually.
>
> Any suggestions?

I had exactly the same experience - an author of aboot helped me. The
point is that your boot loader is too old as it was for me (RedHat 5.2
- upgraded from 5.1). Even though I had a new aboot package (0.5-7),
the boot loader was actually still old. This was fixed by reinstalling
the boot loader:

        /sbin/swriteboot /dev/sda /boot/bootlx
