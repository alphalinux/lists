John,

I STRONGLY suggest that you use the SRPM or get the source as a tar.gz.
and compile it yourself. The MAJORITY of the prebuilt RPMS for ALPHA
have been compiled/built incorrectly in rawhide, in my experience at
least. Well... let's say 12 out of 14 rpms I've used from rawhide have
been broken.... egcs being one of them... I don't know if release 4 is
any better. The rawhide stuff seems to be much better and usable for
Intel, but for Alpha, I have no clue what the people that are making
those rawhide RPMs are doing....

In case you screw up your system by using the RPMs and render yourself
without a working compiler system, and RPM refuses to uninstall, and
also hassbles you when you try to downgrade to a previously working
compiler system, send me a message. I had to deal with that situation
not so long ago.
