You probably misunderstood my response, as I have successfully installed a
second functioning Tulip card in my computer. My system is the DPWS 500a,
it has always used AlphaBIOS 5.65 and above, the firmware always recognized
both chips. The firmware update process seemed painless to me. I had a
little trouble getting Linux to use both properly, but a Linux guy helped
me and we found that the de4x5 driver does the trick.

If you still have trouble, please email me personally if you want. If your
firmware upgrade simply barfs, maybe you should contact your vendor or DEC
itself.
