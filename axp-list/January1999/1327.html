On Mon, 25 Jan 1999, Roman Martonak wrote:
>After compiling qt-1.40, kdesupport-1.0 and kdelibs-1.0 (on UDB with
>egcs-1.0.3a), I tried to compile kdebase-1.0, and had a problem already

This is probably a compiler problem - I got around it with either gcc-2.8.1 or
a newer egcs, say 1.1.1. While you are at it, you might try qt-1.42 and
kde-1.1alpha1 - works for me and is more configurable, and kfm doesn't
crash when I open a web page, making kde unusable.

OTOH it might be a problem locating the kde dynamic libraries; is your
LD_LIBRARY_PATH set correctly? (Or better, enter into ld.so.conf - saves
some microseconds at every program start.)

MfG, Ulrich

PS: Oh, I just remembered - I installed RH5.2 last week, so I must have
