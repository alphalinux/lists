No, it means that people need to realize what _rawhide_ is all about.
Rawhide is (for lack of a better phrase) bleeding edge devel/test
stuff that is in no way guaranteed to work. It's great if it does work
for you, but if I were to grab any rawhide rpms (for alpha,
especially), I'd expect it _not_ to work, and be surprized if it did.

> After 5.0 had C++ totally broken and never fixed, it's important for
> those that know to explain exactly how much trouble we are in...

In the case of egcs, my suggestion is to grab the 1.1.1 pristine
source and roll yer own. `It worked for me' (tm)
