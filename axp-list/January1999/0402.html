Hooman> 1) How do I install a browser on the system, preferably Netscape.

Have you got a Digital Unix license? If you have, you can run the DU
Netscr^Hape on Linux using some of the libraries from your copy of
Digital Unix. If you haven't apparently you can run the i386 version
under emulation, otherwise HotJava and Lynx are reasonable choices. I
hear that the KDE browser works, too.

Hooman> 2) How do I connect to other network drives in our office, preferably my
Hooman> Win98 Laptop to exchange documents.

Have a look at Samba (probably somewhere on your RedHat CD ROMs,
otherwise find it here <http://samba.anu.edu.au/>).
