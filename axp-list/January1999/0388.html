>> But, fwiw, I never did get the original RH image to work on an

>
>Actually, AS800 is known internally as CORELLE and comes only as 5/xxx.
>NORTIAKE is AS1000A, and can be 4/xxx or 5/xxx (PRIMO). Also part of the
>NORITAKE family is AS600A, known as ALCOR-PRIMO which is also 5/xxx only.
>All have EISA bus, IIRC, and differing numbers of PCI slots, builtin
>devices, etc.
>
>And something that might be worth trying: the dp264-s.gz and rawhide-s.gz
>kernels on the 5.2 CD are based on 2.1.120, IIRC, and are actually
>"generic", so they (are supposed to) run on any platform, regardless of SRM
>or ARC/AlphaBIOS console.
