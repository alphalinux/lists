> I am looking for the following items for my AXPpci33 board:
> 2 x Internal Serial cables (the ones that connect to J35 & J37)
You can use nearly ordinary PC cables here, but they must be wired in the
old style. Self made cables can use the crimp connectors for both the 9 pin
SUB-D and the J35 & J37 connectors.

> 1 x Internal Parallel cable (the one that connects to J36)
Absolutely normal PC parallel port connector. Any PC dealer shuld have this.

> 1 x LED display (the one that connects to J6)
Don't know if you can get this anywhere. Either try asking Compaq or build
it yourself following the instructions from the handbook.
