With regards to your message at 03:25 PM 01-18-99 +1100, Daniel Potts.
Where you stated:
>Hi,
>
>I am wondering why DS/Ruffian systems have there own loader (ldmilo.exe).
>Why doesn't linload.exe work? I am trying to get the evalutation board
>debug monitor to run on this board - i know it does via linload.
>
>Thanks in advance,
This is the result, as I see it, of 2 things:
1) Some people at Desktation wrote ldmilo when they designed this board.
The linload program is a result of work by people at Digital.
2) The reason they wrote their own is that the files and tools used for
linload were not publically available outside of DEC at that time.

Since then the needed information and files were released ( thanks to Jim
Paradis' efforts) and some work has gone into making them more "parallel"
in function ( thanks to Jim and Jay++).

At least that is how I understand it..
