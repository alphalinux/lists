On 11-Jan-99 Ross A. Knepper wrote:
> hey folks,
>
> i've got a problem with my udb. one day after a crash, i powered it back
> up to the following error message (this is from SRM):
>
> Multia SRM Console BL5 V3.0-1, built on Jun 22 1995 at 18:10:45
> *** Warning: NVR/TOY battery is low. System may not boot ***
> Testing Memory from 8 to 88 meg...
> Wrong QSNVR version - Expected: V1.0, Received: V42949676295.42949676295
> Initializing QSNVR...
> unexpected exception/interrupt through vector 420
>
> [followed by a register dump and halt]
>
> can anybody advise me on how to fix this? i'm not quite sure what NVR
> is. will a battery replacement do the trick? if so, what kind and where
> might i find one?

Well NVR is Non-Volatile Ram, which requires a battery (sometimes
self-contained) to maintain its contents. Almost certainly a battery
replacement will cure your problems but you will have to properly restore the
(former) contents of the nvr.

Hopefully somebody else here will know where to find a battery.
