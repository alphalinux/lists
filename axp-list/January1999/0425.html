Neil Carlson wrote:
>
> Does linux (alpha and intel) provide IEEE standard library routines
> for controlling IEEE fp exceptions (e.g., set_ieee_exception) and
> fp arithmetic (e.g., set_ieee_rounding_mode)?

Look at /usr/include/asm/fpu.h. You will find there
ieee_set_fp_control(). I am afraid that this header is the whole
documentation there is (or I missed something?).

AFAIK you will have to use -mieee compiler flag before any of that
will have any effect.
