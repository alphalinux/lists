> Hello.

> Well, does anyone know where I can get a binary for alpha/linux
> netscape, or should I risk www.mozilla.org, or should I simply wait for
> Mozilla/Netscape Communicator/Navigator 5.0, or www.w3c.org/Amaya

Right now, here are (most of) your options for graphical browsing, in no
particular order:

* KFM ... fairly fast, but has problems with some HTML
* Netscape for x86 Linux under em86 ... slow, but you get the "full"
           Netscape. Java's iffy.
* Mozilla for Alpha ... fairly fast, but unstable. Does a better job
           than KFM for most browsing. Javascript (sometimes :)), no
           Java. Precompiled binaries linked against lesstif and
           motif available.
* Amaya ... Personally, I don't really like this for browsing.

There are a few others I've probably missed (Does xemacs work on
the Alpha?)
