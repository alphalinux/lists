That means you may need to get a new compiler. But before you do that
get the latest binutils from rawhide and rebuild Mesa. If that doesn't
help then grab egcs-1.1.1 from rawhide and install. You'll also need cpp
and libstc++ to install and I HIGHLY recommend that you build from an
srpm. Lately alot of the stuff from rawhide has been really well raw.
