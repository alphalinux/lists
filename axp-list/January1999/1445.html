> And along those lines, I'd like to know of anyone's experiences
>when upgrading a Miata (433a or 500a) using non-DEC dimms. Might upgrade
>from 128M to 384M using two 128M dimms. Found several places offering
>"unbuffered 128mb 60ns EDO 3.3v ECC sdram" for around $190/each (eg
>www.memoryx.com). Do these sound acceptable, or could I even get away
>with "PC-66 10ns SDRAM" which cost ~$20 less per unit? And before you
>flame, no I do not have an owner's manual available and yes I have tried

If you decide that you don't want to spend an extra 10% that's fine, check
to see if the company will let you return the RAM if it doesn't work
properly. Given the small price difference, I'd go with the more expensive
stuff, for the piece of mind. I have no clue if non-ecc can be used in a
Miata, but I think if just some of it isn't ecc, it won't use any of it.

I wasn't given a manual either. I am considering carping to both the
company that sold the unit and DEC / Compaq.
