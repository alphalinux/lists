On Wed, 27 Jan 1999, Robert Fridman wrote:

> Speaking of aboot, is there a planned update to v0.5? The reason I'm asking is
> that aboot on my Miata GL seems to mess up SRM. After halting, instead of
> going to the SRM prompt, the machine freezes. This makes rebooting impossible.

 Do you have CONFIG_ALPHA_SRM_SETUP enabled in your kernel configuration?
If no, then please do enable it. Aboot has nothing to do with it.

> I would love to use MILO, but I have no idea about how to make it work under
> SRM.

 I don't know why you would love to use MILO as it adds another level of
complexity to the Alpha/Linux booting process. I find aboot clean and
straightforward.
