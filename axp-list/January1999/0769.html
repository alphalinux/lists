> However, when I reboot the Multia I need to press enter when it comes
> to the "Boot Linux" prompt of the ARC Console.
>
> Is there any way I can get this to automatically go without pressing enter?

Isn't there a "will boot in the next 10 seconds unless you press enter"?
I seem to remember a timeout in the ARC bios (if autoboot is setup) that
will boot your choice if no key is pressed. This is what I have done.

Also, if you do a shutdown -r now in Linux, I seem to recall that it only
reboots to MILO--not all the way back to ARC. Thus, it just lets MILO
bootstrap the OS again. I don't know how to get a restart with ARC short
of powering the machine off, which, if you do that, you might as well
press <enter> to "Boot Linux". :)
