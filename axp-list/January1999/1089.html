On Wed, 20 Jan 1999 20:16:07 -0500 (EST), Peter Petrakis <peterpan@wn.net> said:
> I allready have the CDR_FIFOSIZE=4000 as an env variable in mt

That ought to be 4000k, I guess...

> Now my plextor is supposed to have a 2 meg buffer but cdrecord doesn't
> notice.

Well, the built-in cache isn't visible to the software anyway, I
guess. The 'write buffer' cache concerns the software cache, because
4000 is < 96k, I assume.

> When I tried a second time I got " Sorry no cd recorder or
> unsupported cd recoder found". Here's the second process output.

It's common that CD-recorders get screwed up if you interrupt them.
Try the -reset command, but some (like my) need power cycling
thereafter.

If your problems persist, try to contact the author of cdrecord (Joerg
Schilling) . There is a mailinglist (cdwrite@other.debian.com, I
believe, also available as <news:linux.apps.cdwrite>) where he
actively participates.
