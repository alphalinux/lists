I use xntpd to synchronize time on a large bunch of routers. My server
couldn't care less who's asking. It would appear your problem is on the
client side. If you run a properly configured copy of xntpd on the
client (as in NO multicasting) pointing at the server, he'll have no
problem getting time from it.
