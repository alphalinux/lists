An interesting disscussion went on today at the alphant mailing list
that somehow got on the topic of DU. Turns out that DEC/Compaq is
planning to release a C/C++ compiler for alphalinux. Can anyone else
verify this? This would solve our mozzila problems in one shot. It would
also make porting DU applications like the JDK to alphalinux easy. See
below.
