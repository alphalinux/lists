You may change from AlphaBIOS to SRM on the SX164, but not with the SRM off
of DEC's site. The image located on their web sight requires that you have
a previous version loaded. I.E V5.1-4 will load from a floppy and then you
update it with the image from DEC's sight. This is to make sure you have a
license for it.
