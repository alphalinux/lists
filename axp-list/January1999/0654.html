On Tue, Jan 12, 1999 at 09:06:54PM +0000, Wes Bauske wrote:
> I agree with your description except for one point.
> What I've read about other systems is that it is the
> virtual address that drives your cache location, not
> your physical page address. Are you saying under Linux
> or in general it is the physical page address?

Whether the virtual address or physical address is used in the
on-chip cache is an implementation specific detail. Off-chip
caches will of course always use the physical address.

The EV5 Icache is virtually tagged, but the Dcache is physically
tagged, as is the Scache.
