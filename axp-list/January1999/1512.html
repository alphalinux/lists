Please do not mix-up "vesafb" and matroxfb".
vesafb is really Intel only while matroxfb is (in theory; there are
still "wrong" byte-order problems in it) platform independent.
(The byte-order problems are caused because the access should be in
natural (host) byteorder, but fbcon uses macros that force intel-byteorder.
;-} Anyway, no problem on AXP.
