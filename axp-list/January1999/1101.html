ctrl-right_mouse_button opens the VT-Fonts menu. Maybe you
have the same colors for fore- and background in your menus.
My .Xdefaults settings look like this:

XTerm*background: OldLace
XTerm*foreground: black
XTerm*SimpleMenu.background: OldLace
XTerm*SimpleMenu.foreground: black
