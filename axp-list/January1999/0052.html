> Well just to round things out... I have had some trouble using oss --

> have -- when I tried to install the new ncurses it busted me up pretty

That's not really an OSS problem...Redhat all of a sudden started putting
the term stuff in the right place, broke everyone's programs who followed
the broken method... a symlink fixes it. And really, I wouldn't call that
