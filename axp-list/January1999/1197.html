In my case (UDB, setserial /dev/modem spd_vhi), I am always getting 3.4 KB/s
for compressed files and occasionally more than 10 KB/s for uncompressed ones
which is consistent with the serial port running at 115200 and the modem
running at 38400 (remember modems do compress the data). If I try to
set the serial port directly to more than 38400, setserial or not, it doesn't
work, just like described by others. But setserial simply works.
