Sorry, I should have posted a notice that I had fixed my problem. The
problem was the result of misidentification of sound chip. For some
reason unknown to me at this point, the kernel was identifying my
ESS1688 as a ESS1888. To get around this, I had to recompile sound as a
module, and pass it the parameter esstype=1688. Once that was done, all
is well... 2.2.0 is now running without a problem. However, I'm still
not sure where/how to set the MPU_IRQ? In previous kernels, the sound
system occupied MPU_IRQ=9 and DMA 0. What is this stuff anyway?
Clueless here...
