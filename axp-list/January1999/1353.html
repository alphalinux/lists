> Which reminds me, has anyone performed a comparison of resulting
> binary using the DU compilers and the latest/greatest EGCS/GNU suite? If
> the nbench software is any indication, fp and memory ops seem to be at
> least 2-3x faster under DU when compiled with the Digital compilers.

In this case, Goto's GEMM routines are written in assembler, and yes,
they are faster than the DU GEMM routines, which are also written in
assembler. Only a few programs see 2x or 3x slowdown for g77+fast math
verses DU; most see a much smaller slowdown, and one SPECfp benchmark
is 7% faster with egcs-1.0.3...
