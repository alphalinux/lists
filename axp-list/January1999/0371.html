What My Cabriolet does...

You should see the video bios identify your particular card..then the
blue screen that says Initializing Firmware...PS/2 mouse detected
then...Initializing device drivers and that will reflect your scsi host
card. It will continue to boot even if you host adapter is not
recognized and take you to boot/supplementary menus.

>
> I don't get anything. Blank screen. I can hear
> the power supply and I hear the drives spin up.

More than likely the video card is not recognized by this board. The
board's design is a few years old and pretty proprietary. I tried
Trident PCI and Cirrus Logic PCI cards and neither worked. I used an S3
Virge originally, then changed to a Matrox Mill. Both of those work.
