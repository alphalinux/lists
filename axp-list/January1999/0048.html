>
> I'm also having some trouble compiling the
> dandelion.com driver into the kernel. Is there anything special I
> should know about this?

DAC960 driver is included in the current version 2.0.37pre... No, I did
not try to compile it on any platform but I would be surprised if it would
give you real troubles. Take 2.0.36 (release), alpply to it alpha-patches
for 2.0.35 from gatekeeper.dec.com (you will have to resolve some minor
patch rejects "by hand") and patch with the current 2.0.37 prepatch.
Or you may try starting with Red Hat variant of 2.0.36 sources
(I rather would not, but this is just me).
