I am having trouble with my AS200. It hangs while running X, usually
within a few minutes of starting X. I haven't been able to narrow the
problem down much. It only seems to hang while X is running. It
locks up hard - seems like interrupts are disabled. After it hangs
the magic sysrq keys don't do anything, and the machine doesn't ping.
I believe that it is due to a hardware problem of some sort. It seems
to be independent of which kernel I'm using (stock 2.0.x, 2.1.x, and
compiled from source). I have also tried various X servers, and even
distributions (Redhat 5.1, 5.2, Debian). I have also tried switching
video cards. In every case the machine still hangs eventually under X.

So, I am looking for hardware diagnostic tools or suggestions. The
machine has 80 MB of memory, 64 plus the stock 16 MB. I have tried
removing the two 8 MB simms, but it still hangs. I have not tried
swapping the 64 MB yet.

TIA,
