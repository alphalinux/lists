On 17-Jan-99 Larry Snyder wrote:

> One other thing that bit me was the instructions to dd
> milo to the hd. What I ended up doing was to put the msdos fs on sda1,
> mount it as such, then copy (NOT dd) linload.exe and the milo image to
> it. Once I pointed arc at it, it worked just fine.

What you *can* do is is dd the entire MILO floppy to the MSDOS partition. This
works as well for booting the Multia as formatting--as-msdos-then-copying. I
suppose that's what they mean by dd'ing MILO to the msdos partition.
