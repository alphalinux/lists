Flashpoint is only supported on Intel for the 2.1 series kernels. From
linux/drivers/scsi/BusLogic.h:

/*
  FlashPoint support is only available for the Intel x86 Architecture with
  CONFIG_PCI set.
*/

However, check out linux/drivers/scsi/README.FlashPoint. It details a
FlashPoint trade-in program circa 1996 for FlashPoint adapters and Linux
users. It's been a couple of years, but maybe you could trade yours in? I
have a BT-958 on my alpha and am very happy with it.
