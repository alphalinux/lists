Hi Joe,

I had the same problem when I installed my hard drive in my Multia and I
solved it by changing the flag -m21164 to -m21064 in
the kernel top level Makefile. I don't know why this was not working
before, but it worked with this. My Multia is the standard box from
CpuMicromart with 32 megs of RAM and one IDE 2.5' drive, no SCSI, nothing
else.

Unfortunately, I do not have the .config/Makefile files around here (I am
at work) but this manipulation should be easy to do if you have been
compiling kernels since 0.99pl15 ;-).

Hope this helps,
Nicolas.
