On Sat, 2 Jan 1999, Jonathan Vafai wrote:

> Anyone get the latest postgreSQL to compile? Anyone know of
> patches that might get it to work?

        Last I tried (with 6.4.0 I think) it was compiling fine as long as
you had a fixed egcs or made sure that nothing higher than -O1 was used
for optimizer flags. Releases previous to that back down to about 6.3.2, I
think, did not work as some assembly code inlines had been broken. I got
that fixed (see the email archives for this list for that patch), and sent
the patches to the pgsql maintainers who put them into atleast the 6.4.0
release.
        As for the more recent 6.4.{1,2}, I do not know. I have been on
vacation for the past three weeks, away from my Alphas :(, so I have not
had a chance to check. I should be back to them in a week and a half, and
I will check them out then.
        I do know there are still some lingering issues on date handling
on the alpha that need to be resolved, so don't expect everything to be
perfect if you can get it to compile. TTYL.
