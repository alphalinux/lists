On Sat, Jan 09, 1999 at 09:49:17PM -0600, Robb Romans wrote:
> I have been unable to see anything after milo loads the kernel with
> 2.1.132 through 2.2.0pre6. 2.1.131 is fine. The screen is completely
> blank, though the system boots ok and I get a login prompt on a serial
> console.

I fixed a compiler bug in egcs 1.1 that had this symptom. I didn't
think the bug was present in egcs 1.0, but I could be mistaken there.

Quick workaround is to change the types of vga_video_port_reg and
vga_video_port_val from u16 to int.
