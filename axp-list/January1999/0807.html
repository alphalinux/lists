No. But I'll tell you how I got mine working.

Make a directory for all the files.. something like /usr/local/DU40.

Make three subdirectories: /usr/local/DU40/etc
                                /usr/local/DU40/sbin
                                /usr/local/DU40/shlib

Mount your DU CD, somewhere convenient.. /mnt/cdrom comes to mind.

Copy /mnt/cdrom/etc/svc.conf (which is actually
/mnt/cdrom/etc/.new..svc.conf but that's shouldn't matter) into
/usr/local/DU40/etc/

Copy /mnt/cdrom/sbin/loader (which is actually
/mnt/cdrom/etc/.upd..loader but again, it shouldn't matter) into
/usr/local/DU40/sbin/

Copy /mnt/cdrom/usr/shlib into /usr/local/DU40/ (better safe than
sorry). Be sure to copy the full files and not just the symlinks.

Just in case, fix the permissions on everything. This should do a
sufficient job:

chmod -R ugo+r /usr/local/DU40/
find /usr/local/DU40/ -type d -exec chmod ugo+x {} \;
chmod 555 /usr/local/DU40/sbin/loader

Now start making symlinks:

ln -s /usr/local/DU40/etc/svc.conf /etc/svc.conf
ln -s /usr/local/DU40/sbin/loader /sbin/loader
ln -s /usr/local/DU40/shlib /usr/shlib

This set-up worked for me on a pair of AS255-300's which our company
inherited.

Corrections or comments are welcome.
