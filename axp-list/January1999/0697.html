David R. Gavin wrote:
>
>
> An even slicker way to migrate to a new filesystem that may not be the same
> size:
>
> cd dir_to_move
> tar cf - . | (cd target_directory ; tar xf -)

As we are talking about Linux then 'cp -a <source> <target>' works just
dandy. Use 'cp -xdpR' if you want to be a bit more selective. Works
on a contents of /dev as well. Try and see for yourself. :-)

As this is a properly of GNU 'cp' do not try that on a system where
this variant of 'cp' is not available.

Yet another method is --pass option to 'cpio'. See 'man cpio' for more.
