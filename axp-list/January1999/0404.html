All I can say is: you are not alone having problems with threads
on Linux/Alpha. My own multithreaded code, which compiles cleanly
and runs fine on Linux/Intel and IRIX, segfaults using RedHat 5.1
with glibc-2.0.7-19 and egcs 1.1.1 (in my case I'm getting SIGSEGVs).
All threaded examples that come with glibc run fine on my box.
