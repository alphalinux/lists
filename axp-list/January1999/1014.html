        I adminster a system of 3 Alphas and 3 Intel boxen, and my general
approach is to put as much energy/effort into securing the Intel's as I do
the Alpha's (redhat updates, monitoring bugtraq, etc..).

        It is my experience, having made equal efffort to secure both my
Alpha and Intel boxes, that the Alpha boxes are just that bit more secure
(in general terms) than the Intel boxes.

        ****NO MACHINE ON THE PLANET IS INVULNERABLE TO A WELL INFORMED
AND DETERMINED ATTACKER**** is my motto (be it true or untrue).

        My $.02
