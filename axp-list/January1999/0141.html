On Fri, 1 Jan 1999, Peter Petrakis wrote:

> I swear I can't get a booting kernel to save my life. What are you using
> to build it , egcs version and from where? Also what ver of binutils are
> you using? I've been using egcs 1.1.1 built from scratch and I still
> can't get pass "jumping to kernel". Thanks.

Are you sure you've got a recent milo? I was having similar problems a
while back when I switched from 2.0.xx to 2.1.124. It turns out my
milo (for my PC164) was quite old, and recent(?) 2.1 kernels require a
newer milo than what I had. I installed a more recent one, and have
had no problems since (Thanks, rth).
