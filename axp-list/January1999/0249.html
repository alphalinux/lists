> Thanks for the quick response! I got sh from DU and executed setld and

Of course for actually extracting the file, you'd need:

    # compress -dc <OSFJAVA425 | tar xvf -

This should work without problems. Note that the path in the TAR archive
is relative to ".", meaning that the stuff won't show up under /usr but
under `pwd`/usr. Just cd to / before extracting to have it in the
proper directories.
