Designer 7 writes:
> Hi Jaap,
>
> Ok.. so your tip worked great,.. I just had to recompile libffm as a
> shared library... however, I noticed that libffm gets loaded ALL the
> time, even if a program doesn't need libm/libffm... is this a good
> thing? or is the penalty of loading an extra lib all the time
> insignificant?

It isn't a BIG library, so I don't think that would be a problem but
have no actual fact to prove this. I now added the LD_PRELOAD var to
my /etc/profile and /etc/csh.cshrc on my system. Now all programs use
libffm. Even X seems to be faster (a bit). However, it does mean that
if i delete libffm all programs stop working.. I better move it from
/usr/local/lib to /lib to reflect it's new status .....
