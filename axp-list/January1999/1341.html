I think you might want to check things... Cause my milo and as400
and as300 for that matter all work fine with 3. I think what you did wrong was
to use the wrong image for the second disk. There are 2 images,
one for milo to read from a FAT floppy and one for milo to read
from a ex2fs floppy. I think that is where you went wrong. You used
the wrong image for the dd copy.

You can tell which is which by the names --

kernel.img is for FAT
kernel-s.img is for dd

in otherwords, avanti.img = FAT and avanti-s.img = dd.

hope this helps save the life of a floppy...
