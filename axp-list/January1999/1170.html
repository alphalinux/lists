I'm not familiar enough with DU to know quite what this does... perhaps
/usr/shlib/libc.so contains a non-kernel-based pthread implementation.
DU threads are generally based on Mach threads, which is very different
from Linux's clone() system call, and not easy to emulate. I'd expect
getting the JDK to run under Linux will not be easy since it relies
extensively on DU threads (although I'd be happy to be proven wrong).

Jeff
