>To make a long story short, I'd like to know what you folks out there who are

After owning a 8 bit computer (Z80 based) and a 16/32 bit computer (MC
68000) based, the next logical step was a 64 bit computer. The only
affordable 64 bit computer was the Alpha, a 300MHz XL turbo.

Wat am I doing with the machine?

Playing with algoritms, working on a compression program, still want to

anyhow). Text editing is done with a simple editor and LaTeX.
