I compiled the new kernel 2.20 on my AS 200 and it at first impression the
programs seem to start faster.
However, I have a problem and a half. My full problem is that I cannot make
it work and retrieve an IP through DHCP. Under 2.035 at boot time I get
something like:
using dhtp for eth0... eth0: media is TP
done.

Under the new kernel I get:
using dhtp for eth0...
failed.

My half-problem is with sound support. I get sound support but the sound
volume is lower than the sound volume under 2.035.

I had problems configuring certain options under make config under both
networking and sound support, because they are different than they were
under 2.035. Therefore, I decided to try a make oldconfig, using my 2.035's
config file. That was the worst experiment for the kernel panicked. I tried
to fool around with the card support and did two more compiles, both of
which had working sound but no DHTP boot up.

Remarkably, I could not find these options:

CONFIG_NET_PCI (and I assumed that it has been replaced by CONFIG_NET_EISA)
CONFIG_MSS (and I assumed that it has been replaced by CONFIG_SOUND_MSS)
CONFIG_AUDIO (and I assumed that it has been replaced by CONFIG_SOUND_OSS)
DSP_BUFFSIZE=65536 (no assumptions about this one)

Does anybody know what the problem with my compilation may be?
Does anybody have a .config that works for a stock AS 200 (with a Matrox
Millennium Card)?
