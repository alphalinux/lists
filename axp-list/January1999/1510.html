I'll just chip in that if anyone here at API told you "unofficially" not to
use the UX, it was probably only to persuade you to buy something more flash
:-) Linux in general (including the UX) is fully supported by API and we do
our best to keep everyone happy. Since we got off the ground last year, we
have produced the following for the UX:

1) A milo that network-boots (for clusters, etc.)
2) A UX chapter in the FAQ (albeit still in first draft)

There are more developments in the pipeline that will also be beneficial to
the UX. By no means is it about to become "unsupported" - it just wont be
the fastest thing out there for much longer :-)
