Ron Farrer wrote:
>
> Okay I did 'make config' and after answering lots of questions I did
> 'make dep ; make clean' and then 'make boot', but when the kernel boots it
> says
>
> "
> Partition check:
> VFS: Cannot open root device 08:05
> Kernel panic: VFS: Unable to mount root fs on 08:05
> "
>
> How do I fix this?
>
> TIA,
>
I'm not sure what you use to boot with but it sounds like after you
built the kernel that you didn't pass on the new kernel's name or
location.

On a Cabriolet I pass on these arguements thru OSLOADOPTIONS in the ARC
Console. Mine reads:
boot sdb2:<kernel name> root=/dev/sdb2 (assuming that is your drive
configuration). This can be typed in at the MILO prompt too.
