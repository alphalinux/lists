3 things:
What is your video card? ( make, model, amount of memory)
Do you know if the board has SRM or ARC firmware on it?
If SRM, it might be going to the console port - try hooking up to a serial
terminal or terminal program on another machine from the "funny" RJ45 on

prompt from there we know what to do next..
