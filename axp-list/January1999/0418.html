You motherboard could also be in the "debug" mode. It's a special mode
that the motherboard can get into in which the signals are send to a
serial port, and not to a monitor.

However I still say you should try a different video card. You memory
could be a problem, the only way to test is to try different memory, or
try yours on different system.
