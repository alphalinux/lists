You may wanna change your vid card. Matrox, S3, trident, and the older
number 9 boards are support by Xfree86. Metrolink & Xi graphics sell
Xservers for Alphas too. Here's metrolink's supported hardware list
http://www.metrolink.com/metrox431-alpha/cardlist4.3.1.html . I don't
know if all of the cards supported by Xi intel are supported on Alpha
but you can download a demo of the server and try it out. www.xig.com
