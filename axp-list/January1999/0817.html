Yes, you've caught a real standards conformance bug. This was fixed
early in the glibc 2.1 development cycle; glibc 2.1 is going through
test releases prior to final right now.

Binary compatibility (against libc at least) is handled with ELF
symbol versioning on the related functions.

> That is already required since I don't think that tv_sec is supposed
> to be a time_t... a bug in the standard. But we have to follow the
> bugs, too.

tv_sec is required to be a time_t. I don't recall off-hand what
POSIX sez for tv_usec -- probably `long'. I was thinking one of
Single Unix or UNIX98 defined a useconds_t or some such.
