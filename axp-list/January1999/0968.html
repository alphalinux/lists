Hi, All:

First, don't flame me for this email, as I'm not really trying to sell my alpha through this post... I'm mostly venting. I think I got into the alpha world without doing enough research. I prevailed through an install of an AS200 with 32MB, CDROM, and a 2GB hard drive. Got my redhat 5.2 installed, X working, etc, etc, only to find out that I have an extremely reliable system for which there are no decent browsers available (IHMO) and no Frontpage extensions, and seems half of the apps I want to run aren't ported to alpha yet. I'm not a heavy duty coder, so I'm unlikely to do my own porting any time soon.

To make a long story short, I'd like to know what you folks out there who are HAPPY with your Alphas do with them. I'll probably sell mine to that sector, and buy myself an Intel.
