On Mon, Jan 11, 1999 at 04:18:28PM +0100, Jaap Schellekens wrote:

> My XL300 already has 2 SCSI controllers (build in 810 and an 875). The
> problem was the 25 pin D-connector cable that came with the scanner
> (Mac type SCSI?). Also the scanner does not support disconnect so i
> did not want it on the other SCSI busses.

I also hate SCSI-devices without disconnect support, but I hate ISA
card much more. Normally a Scanner is only a target (it never plays
arbitration-game) so it blocks only when somebody active requests something
from it.
The NCR driver handles disconnect/reconnect on a target basis,
so you can drive all other devices with disconnect/reconnect enabled.
Summary: The scanner does not affect the bus performance if it's not
scanning (assuming that INQUIRY without discon is not too slow).
