> I have a netgear fa310 10/100 pci ethernet card. It's based on the dec
> chipset. I went to buy another one, and it has a different chip on it. Is
> this new chip 100% compatible and/or will it work?

This gets discussed all the time on the beowulf and tulip mailing
lists. No, the chip isn't 100% compatible. If you upgrade to the
newest tulip driver, you will get about 85% of peak performance out of
the new card. I believe that we know how to get the other 15%
out of it in a future driver.
