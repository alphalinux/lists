To get past that part, you need to comment out two include directives in
/usr/src/linux/ipc/utils.c

I can't exactly remember which,... but I think they were the 2nd and 3rd
from the bottom of the list of include directives at the top of the
utils.c file. They should be

#include <asm/xxx>
#include <asm/xxx>

or something like that. To be sure... see 2.2pre3's utils.c in /ipc/

However, I still had problems with ip_masq code even though I got around
the problem above.... but that was with ac patch 1. I am running 2.2pre4
right now without the ac patches on a Miata.
