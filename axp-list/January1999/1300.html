        You may want to check out the Fortran77 and Fortran90 compiled
code execution benchmarks at http://www.polyhedron.com/compare.html.
Unfortunately, I believe they only compare benchmarks on Windows.
        Something to keep in mind as well - the 21164a can execute two
floating point instructions per clock cycle (one FADD, one FMUL). If you
can control resulting asm to a fine degree with a good compiler, floating
point operations should in theory be twice as fast as on the P2.
