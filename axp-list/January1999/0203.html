>dude...why not just get an SCA -> 50 pin/power adapter?

Do the math:
        The adapters are $25 ea. (and it's 1 per drive!)

($25ea. You have to have them made!)

        It works out to around $150 to put the drives in!
        So before I did that, I thought I'd check to see if anyone else had a
system that could use them, and wanted to trade. If this turns up dry, then
I'll bite the bullet, and get the adapters.
