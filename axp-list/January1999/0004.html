>I had the same problem for some time, until finally it worked. I think
>it was because I took out support for all SCSI devices besides the
>ncr53c7/8xx.

 Thanks, that was it.

I thought the following was a section header for all NCR53Cxxx drivers instead of a driver itself.
>Generic NCR5380/53c400 SCSI support (CONFIG_SCSI_GENERIC_NCR5380) [N/y/m/?]

Once I just had this line then I was good to go.
>NCR53c7,8xx SCSI support (CONFIG_SCSI_NCR53C7xx) [Y/m/n/?]
