On Tue, 5 Jan 1999, Peter Petrakis wrote:

> [> [root@alphagen java]# setld -I /usr/shlib/java
> bash: /sbin/setld: No such file or directory
>
> I made sure it was executable and that I had the right permissions with
> no success.

"setld" is a shell script which has #!/sbin/sh in the first line.
Make sure that you've got an /sbin/sh.

Apart from that setld installable kits basically are just compressed tar
files. So in the case of the JDK 1.1.7 for DU you can just do a:

# compress -dc <OSFJAVA425 | tar tvf -

However I don't know if the JDK works under Linux/Axp at all.
