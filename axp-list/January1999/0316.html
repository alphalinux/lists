My Multia crashes every now and then but mostly I keep it on 24 hours a day
(highest uptime was 65+ days, and that was ended by a thunderstorm-induced
power outage). But when it does crash, it's cranky and hard to reboot.
Mostly, it doesn't recognize the external scsi devices at all. I often have
to power down all the external devices, reseat all the cables, power them up,
wait, wave a dead chicken over them, ritually sacrifice a goat, then power up
the Multia while holding my mouth just so. And even then it takes three
tries. It does see the scsi controller (at least I get that NCR5810....OK
message), but if you go to the hardware menu, it doesn't report any external
scsi devices at all.

Is this likely to be a problem with the external hardware, or the multia
itself? Should I buy a "new" Multia from cpumicromart?
