> Executable found (ls --color) yet when
> executed--nothing: command not found.

You should get "file not found" - which will be something which
a dynamic loader tries to load and which does not exist (really)
on your machine. Try 'file' command on your netscape executables.

You may run Linux/Intel binaries on Alpha provided:
  - you have em86 support in your kernel
  - if this is module than you have a proper alias in your
    module configuration file (consult /var/log/messages for informative
    errors)
  - they are in ECOFF and not ELF format as, so far, nobody
    extended em86 to ELF binaries; Jim Paradis mentions from time
    to time that maybe he we will do it someday but somehow he
    cannot find time.
