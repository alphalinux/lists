>
> It's time for me to make faxing work on my alpha. Does anyone know if
> internal fax modems (ISA variety, no PCI please) can be made to work
> with alpha and linux 2.2.x? What are people using in/on their alpha?

        3Com/USR 56k V.90/X2/ITU data/fax/voice modem - model number 5685.
Costs around $120, maybe less if you find a discount vendor.
Pre-configured to use plug-n-pray, but the jumpers work just fine for
manual configuration of com port/irq.
