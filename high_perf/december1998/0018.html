Hi,

Adam asked me about some missing files in the lapack stuff on our ftp
server, more specifically, the 981113 and 981122 directories as well
as the blas_patch.gz (which you need *only* to correct the typo in
axpy.S in the 981122 file).

Well, they're not missing, I just locked them so you don't see them. I
reworked some of the stuff (as is noted on the lapack.html page) to
make things simpler. There are two source rpms, for lapack and
fastmath (which has been upgraded to libffm-0.28), and the BLAS are in
a separate tar file.

The reason for this is that lapack itself will most probably not need
to be changed anymore - once you have it, that's it. The BLAS and
fastmath, OTOH, are still evolving, so it's best to have them
separately.

So, this is what things looks like now:

ftp://ftp.neuro.uni-oldenburg.de/pub

alpha: contains lapack.html and blas-alpha.tar
alpha/SRPMS: contains source rpms for lapack, fastmath, and the
lapack.rpmrc

In case of problems, please contact me!
