> Naohiko Shimizu wrote:

>But I wonder that if 21164A uses
>same process rule, it will mark higher clock rating. And the easy
>optimizable routine may have higher performance with (enhanced) 21164A.

Interesting question - but very hypothetical. The 21264 is a significantly
larger beast (about 15M transistors, I believe) than the 21164A, and a
radically different internal micro-architecture. I don't believe that
a (further) enhanced 21164A is on the Alpha CPU roadmap; all of the plans
I've seen indicate that the focus is on improving the performance of the
21264 CPUs.
