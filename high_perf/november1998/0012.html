Hi,

some time a go I assembled a compilation of the links I found on the net
to free Alpha processor and assembly documentation. Maybe the following
might be helpful for some people on this list !

Adam, If you like it, you might probably put a link to this list on the
high_perf page on www.alphalinux.org !??

Here it is, hopefully not yet outdated:

Look at

http://www.unix.digital.com/faqs/publications/base_doc/DOCUMENTATION/V40D_PDF/

I especially recommend:

APS31DTE.PDF Assembly Language Programmer's Guide

and also useful might be:

APY8ACTE.PDF Digital UNIX Calling Standard for Alpha Systems
and
APUBXCTK.PDF Digital Portable Mathematics Library

Also very helpful might be, on

http://gatekeeper.dec.com/pub/digital/DECinfo/semiconductor/literature/

alphahb2.pdf Alpha Architecture Handbook Version 3
and
21164hrm.pdf 21164 Hardware Reference Manual

You will find further references on pages like

http://sunsite.doc.ic.ac.uk/public/packages//NetBSD/misc/dec-docs/

http://www.partner.digital.com/www-swdev/pages/Home/TECH/documents/alpha_cookbook/biblio.htm

http://www.unix.digital.com/faqs/publications/base_doc/POSTSCRIPT/V40/00-READ-ME-FIRST

http://www.partner.digital.com/www-swdev/pages/Home/TECH/documents/osf1-docs-v3.0.html#PROGRAMMER

http://www.azstarnet.com/~axplinux/FAQ-6.html

but often those file are more outdated than the ones I cited above.
